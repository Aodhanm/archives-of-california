# ★ MEZCAL PROHIBITION DEBATE — Gov. Diego de Borica (1798) requested ban on aguardiente mezcal imports via San Blas ships

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n312–314** · 1803 Oct 20 · Savage abstract

★ MEZCAL PROHIBITION DEBATE — Gov. Diego de Borica (1798) requested ban on aguardiente mezcal imports via San Blas ships due to harm and scandals in new establishments; antecedents of prohibition not found; since then ships permitted to carry goods freely including aguardiente de caña (cane spirit); asks Gov. to report whether cane spirit causes same problems as mezcal. pp. 764–5
