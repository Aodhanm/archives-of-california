# ★ COMPLETE ARMS INVENTORY — FORT SAN JOAQUÍN + YERBA BUENA, 1802. 3-page detailed inventory of artillery and war matérie

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n225–228** · 1802 Jun 30 · Savage abstract

★ COMPLETE ARMS INVENTORY — FORT SAN JOAQUÍN + YERBA BUENA, 1802. 3-page detailed inventory of artillery and war matériel with condition ratings (good/medium/useless). Key items: 3 mounted 24-lb cannons (good), 2 mounted 12-lb cannons, 3 dismounted cannons, 5 caliber-8 cannons; ~1,374 iron balls (cal. 24), ~354 (cal. 12), ~704 (cal. 8); ~1,205 powder charges; 12 quintales gunpowder; 550 powder bags; 4 barrels tar; combat lanterns, slow match, tools. Signed Eugenio Martínez, approved Argüello. Cross-ref p. 353 of these extracts
