# ★ ROYAL ORDER: CALIFORNIA DIVIDED INTO TWO GOVERNMENTS — Junta de Fortificaciones de Indias resolved Peninsula divided i

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n178–180** · 1804 Aug 29 · Savage abstract

★ ROYAL ORDER: CALIFORNIA DIVIDED INTO TWO GOVERNMENTS — Junta de Fortificaciones de Indias resolved Peninsula divided into Nueva and Antigua California with existing boundaries; Governor of Nueva Cal. earns $4,000/yr, Antigua $3,000/yr; Arrillaga named Governor of Nueva California for his services; Viceroy to name interim for Antigua and propose permanent candidates; until interim named, Arrillaga to govern both, transferring to Monterey when convenient
