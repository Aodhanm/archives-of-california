# ★ HABILITADO AUDIT — SAN DIEGO & MONTEREY — Real Tribunal de Cuentas + Fiscal report on habilitados' failure to account 

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n295–302** · 1803 Mar 23–Jun 13 · Savage abstract

★ HABILITADO AUDIT — SAN DIEGO & MONTEREY — Real Tribunal de Cuentas + Fiscal report on habilitados' failure to account for supplies delivered by bergantín *Activo* (1798). SD habilitado never reported; excused with "rare pretext"; supplies from 1798 valued at 215,000 reales ¾ mark of silver. Monterey habilitado's response also unsatisfactory (claims 6 arrobas wax + cedar box never arrived). Fiscal demands formal accounts of ALL supplies received from ships; orders interim Gov. of California to enforce; certified copies required; both habilitados charged; Marina de San Blas notified. Signed Monteverde, Lacunza, Arnaiz (Contaduría Mayor), Apr 4, 1803; Fiscal Borbón Apr 20; Iturrigaray Jun 12. pp. 719–26
