# ★ FORNICATION SCANDAL AT SAN JOSÉ — Antonio Sandoval accuses Potenciana Ramírez re: her daughter; Potenciana alleged *ma

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n377–380** · 1804 Oct 27 · Savage abstract

★ FORNICATION SCANDAL AT SAN JOSÉ — Antonio Sandoval accuses Potenciana Ramírez re: her daughter; Potenciana alleged *mala amistad* (sexual relationship) with D. José Estudillo and Da. Gertrudis Orcasitas with Fr. José Vinales (who had 3 children from her); Castro tried to investigate but Potenciana and Gertrudis insulted him; Comisionado Sargento Macario de Castro (Potenciana's husband) refused to cooperate; Castro brings witnesses (Regidor Manuel Butrón, Poblador Tiburcio Vásquez, vecino Antonio Sánchez) but Macario says he's not competent; Castro renounces the case and files civil/criminal complaint against the Comisionado. pp. 943–4
