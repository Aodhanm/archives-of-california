# ★★ REPORT ON ILLEGAL COMMERCE FROM AMERICAN FRIGATE — Viceroy's order re: entry/commerce of frigate at Monterey and SF; 

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n407–409** · 1804 Jul 30 · Savage abstract

★★ REPORT ON ILLEGAL COMMERCE FROM AMERICAN FRIGATE — Viceroy's order re: entry/commerce of frigate at Monterey and SF; Castro reports frigate anchored at Puerto de SF and Yerba Buena in May; stayed several days; considerable commerce; Cadete José Ma. Estudillo told him ship carried $500 and returned with cargo; invalid Luis Romero seen carrying goods outside pueblo; Mayordomo of Mission del Carmelo went; beach crowded with people trading; "everyone got some little thing; to this day foreign goods are still circulating" ("hta. el día corren géneros que parecen extranjeros"). pp. 1057–60
