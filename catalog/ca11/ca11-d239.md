# ★ MURDER OF PADRE SURECA AT SANTO TOMÁS — deportation of accused Indians. By royal order, will embark the guilty: Lázaro

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n232–234** · 1803 Oct 20 · Savage abstract

★ MURDER OF PADRE SURECA AT SANTO TOMÁS — deportation of accused Indians. By royal order, will embark the guilty: Lázaro, Alejandro, Bárcena, and Juan Miguel (husband of the Indian woman whose testimony alone condemns him). Murdered Padre Fr. Eduardo Sureca at Mission Santo Tomás [Baja California]. Ruiz resolved to ship them all together; "if there was error, it is easy to return them." [continues]
