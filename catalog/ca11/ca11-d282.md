# ★ MERCURY (AZOGUE) SUPPLY TO BAJA CALIFORNIA — writes Intendente de Guadalajara; only sure method is appointing revenue 

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n285–287** · 1803 Jan 21 · Savage abstract

★ MERCURY (AZOGUE) SUPPLY TO BAJA CALIFORNIA — writes Intendente de Guadalajara; only sure method is appointing revenue administrators; Tribunal de Cuentas proposes buying gold/silver at 10 pesos/onza (gold) and 7/mark (silver); resolved that mercury be sent from capital to California on the supply ship every 1–2 years, consigned to Habilitado as formerly from Tesorería de los Álamos. pp. 682–4
