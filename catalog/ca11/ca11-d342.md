# ★ KNIFE PROHIBITION AT PUEBLO DE LOS ÁNGELES — prohibits carrying belduque in boot/waist within the pueblo; repeated sta

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n357–359** · 1804 Jan 1 · Savage abstract

★ KNIFE PROHIBITION AT PUEBLO DE LOS ÁNGELES — prohibits carrying belduque in boot/waist within the pueblo; repeated stabbings in quarrels; "prohibited in all places of the kingdom with much rigor"; persons of *color quebrado* (mixed race) publicly whipped; orders Alcalde of Pueblo de la Reina de Los Ángeles to enforce; knives only permitted going to the field for work. p. 859
