# ★ DAILY DIARY — FEBRUARY 1802 EPIDEMIC at Monterey/Carmel/Soledad. Day-by-day log: Day 1 transferred command; Day 3 thre

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n186–188** · 1802 Feb 28 · Savage abstract

★ DAILY DIARY — FEBRUARY 1802 EPIDEMIC at Monterey/Carmel/Soledad. Day-by-day log: Day 1 transferred command; Day 3 three soldiers sick with pleurisy; Day 5 Mission Soledad Indians fled — pages, cooks, shepherds abandoned mission out of fear of epidemic; Day 6 son of Moraga died; Day 8 Carmel: 7 sick at dawn, 1 Indian dead; Day 10 wife of Sargento Moraga died + invalid Montaña died + gentile cowboy baptized dying; Day 11 Soledad: 5–6 more sick, padres baptizing dying gentiles; Day 12 Indian died; Day 15 Indian man + woman died; Day 16 wife of [Moraga?] died, buried at Mission; Days 17–18 6 Indians died; Day 20 Indian woman + child died; Day 21 two Indians died; Day 25 Indian woman + small child died; Day 28 news from Soledad: 5 Christians and 3 gentiles murdered + 3 more at nearby ranchería (massacre, not epidemic). 12+ epidemic deaths + 8 murdered in single month
