# [cont.] Murder of Padre Sureca — boy Juan de Dios told Indian Bárbara that Lázaro and Juan Miguel killed the padre; Láza

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n234–235** · 1803 Oct 20 · Savage abstract

[cont.] Murder of Padre Sureca — boy Juan de Dios told Indian Bárbara that Lázaro and Juan Miguel killed the padre; Lázaro entered padre's room at night, took key, opened door, brought Juan Miguel; also brought Mariano; the three killed the padre; Lázaro re-entered and buried body in the field. Ruiz decides to ship Lázaro + Juan Miguel; Mariano stays; notes Bárbara has "diabolical spirit"; Indian Encarnación knows the truth. pp. 557–8
