# ★ FRIGATE *ALEXANDER* AT SAN DIEGO — arrived night of Feb 26, 1803; from Boston Jul 19, 1802; 110 tons, 19 crew + Capt. 

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n247–249** · 1803 Mar 10 · Savage abstract

★ FRIGATE *ALEXANDER* AT SAN DIEGO — arrived night of Feb 26, 1803; from Boston Jul 19, 1802; 110 tons, 19 crew + Capt. Brown + 2 pilots; scurvy + lacking provisions; supplied 40 barrels water, beef, salt; sick disembarked far from port; tried to trade furs; Sargento sent with 6 soldiers; despite precautions, large quantity of furs smuggled aboard night of the 3rd; searched vessel and confiscated 491 fur pieces; ordered to set sail immediately without the 8-day stay requested; left Todos Santos on the 7th at night, went ashore with pretext of needing firewood. pp. 617–19
