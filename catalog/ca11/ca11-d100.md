# Soldiers refuse to saddle officers' horses — asks whether escort soldiers are obligated to saddle the horse of the offic

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n162–163** · 1802 May 4 · Savage abstract

Soldiers refuse to saddle officers' horses — asks whether escort soldiers are obligated to saddle the horse of the officer or padre they escort; common opinion among troops that it is not their duty and they frequently resist; Carrillo thinks they should do it for padres (who can't bring a servant on short notice) and for officers on urgent marches
