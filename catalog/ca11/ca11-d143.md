# ★ Flax vs. hemp economics — flax harvested same way as hemp; in Spain flax costs 4 rs/lb, hemp 10 rs/lb because used for

**C-A 11 (Prov. St. Pap. XVIII), scan n185–186** · 1805 Aug 24 · Savage abstract

★ Flax vs. hemp economics — flax harvested same way as hemp; in Spain flax costs 4 rs/lb, hemp 10 rs/lb because used for rope/sacking; hemp processing (washing, drying, combing, spinning) produces large waste (tow/estopa) and raises costs; two-thirds of cost far from profit; nobody will undertake it; hemp farmers can cover costs themselves without Royal Treasury subsidy
