# CONSTRUCTION DIARY at Mission SJ: casas de padres (8 pieces, 5 varas each), Guardia (8×5 varas), Almacén (5 varas sq), 2

**C-A 8 (Provincial State Papers), scan n363–370** · 1797-08-17 · Savage abstract

CONSTRUCTION DIARY at Mission SJ: casas de padres (8 pieces, 5 varas each), Guardia (8×5 varas), Almacén (5 varas sq), 20 casas de soldados, corral (30+ varas). Bueyes exhausted from hauling. Day-by-day diary: punished 2 soldiers (Sánchez struck Indian, Duarte drunk); 3 gentiles came wanting Christianity but feared Sacelanes retaliation; Fr. Muñoz left for SF; Padre Barcanilla to Sta Clara
