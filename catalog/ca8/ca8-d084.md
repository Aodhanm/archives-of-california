# VERBATIM: Declarations re Sta Cruz incident. Soldier José Yguera: escorted Fr. Manuel Fernandez to 5 rancherías "con int

**C-A 8 (Provincial State Papers), scan n50–53** · 1796-04-11 · verbatim transcription

VERBATIM: Declarations re Sta Cruz incident. Soldier José Yguera: escorted Fr. Manuel Fernandez to 5 rancherías "con intrepidez"; Indian aimed arrow at padre; padre fled into tular; "No te metas, padre, que te atascará." Many cimarrones/gentiles came to be baptized after. Then mayordomo Juan Francisco Arias's matching declaration at Monterey, May 14 1796. Witnesses: Sal, Pedro Amador, Francisco Gonzalez, Manuel Rodríguez, Juan Ortega. Document incomplete.
