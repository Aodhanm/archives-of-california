# ⭐ Información vs Juan [John Rogers] Cooper, captain of the frigate "Rover," for throwing papers overboard: on his 2nd vo

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n291** · tomo p. LXIII.554–81 · 1826-12-23 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Información vs Juan [John Rogers] Cooper, captain of the frigate "Rover," for throwing papers overboard: on his 2nd voyage to Canton ("Catón"), at Guam a drunk sailor told the governor the *Rover* was not American but Mexican, belonging to Cal. Gov. Luis Argüello; fearing the same at Manila (Spanish), Cooper threw overboard the Spanish-language papers — the contract/sale of the schooner made with Argüello, invoices — and the national flag; escribano José Ant° Gaviola; no outcome recorded
