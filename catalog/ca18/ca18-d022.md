# Causa de conspiración = the 1835 Apalátegui–Torres revolt against Gov. Figueroa (first 2 folios missing). Deposition of 

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n78–n87** · tomo p. 840–47 · 1835-03-15 · unspecified · 

Causa de conspiración = the 1835 Apalátegui–Torres revolt against Gov. Figueroa (first 2 folios missing). Deposition of American Mr. Job Day via interpreter John B.R. Cooper; confession of Don Miguel Hidalgo detailing meetings w/ Antonio Apalátegui & Francisco Torres, letters smuggled by brigantine to Fernando Escobosa in Guaymas (Sonora), Englishman Lewis Burton, Martin Cooper, Benito Díaz; plotters "counted on the Monterey troops, minus their officers." Savage: "evidently a copy." ⚠ Date jumps to 1835
