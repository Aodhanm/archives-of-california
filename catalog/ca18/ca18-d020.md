# Mission SF Indians Cosme, Felipe, Emiliano, Sempero prosecuted for killing Hermón Esteban & Fidel (asleep) night of 2 Ma

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n76–n77** · tomo p. 740–94 · 1822-05-03 · unspecified · 

Mission SF Indians Cosme, Felipe, Emiliano, Sempero prosecuted for killing Hermón Esteban & Fidel (asleep) night of 2 May. Original has drawings of the weapons (knife, hammer, pike). Fiscal: treacherous murders → death by hanging for Sempero, Emiliano & Adriano "en nombre de la Soberana Junta de la Regencia del imperio mexicano"; Felipe de Venecia 10 yrs presidio + expatriation from mission, to publicly witness the others' execution
