# Causa criminal: soldier José Sánchez shot & killed fellow presidial soldier Luis González (night of the 23rd) after a dr

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n4–n6** · tomo p. 465–577 · 1822-07-13 · unspecified · 

Causa criminal: soldier José Sánchez shot & killed fellow presidial soldier Luis González (night of the 23rd) after a drunken altercation (hair-pulling, scuffle, lance taken twice). Fiscal: Sánchez provoked → asks 10 yrs presidio (not death); corporal punished per R.O. 26 Sept 1776 for abandoning guard
