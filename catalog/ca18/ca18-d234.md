# Investigation of Comisario [José Mª] Herrera for misappropriation of public funds + forgery of signatures. Witnesses Jos

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n247–n250** · tomo p. LXI.754–828 · 1830-08-05 (events fr. 1827) · unspecified · ⭐⭐

Investigation of Comisario [José Mª] Herrera for misappropriation of public funds + forgery of signatures. Witnesses José de la Guerra, Ignacio Maitorena, Juan Bandini, Romualdo Pacheco, Mariano G. Vallejo, Rodrigo del Pliego. Herrera allegedly mismanaged ~$2,000 + México funds, didn't pay duties on goods sold to an American brig (~$600 lost). The forgery scheme (by Luis Bringas) faked the signature of Capt. William Gale (American frigate *Brooklyn*) on a $2,000 libranza (a Guaymas draft); Gale testified it was false ("he signs *Gale* not *Galle*"). Escribiente Romualdo Pacheco
