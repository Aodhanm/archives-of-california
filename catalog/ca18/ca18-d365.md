# ⭐⭐⭐ Hoja de servicios documenting the capture of the famous fugitive-outlaw Pomponio: 3 campaigns into the Tulares vs ci

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n418–n419** · tomo p. LXVI.962–3 · 1828-12-31 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ Hoja de servicios documenting the capture of the famous fugitive-outlaw Pomponio: 3 campaigns into the Tulares vs cimarrón Christian/gentile Indians (killed a cabecilla); then ~13 leagues north of SF he captured the cimarrón Christian José/Pomponio at the cañada de Novato after a firefight (with only a cabo + 2 soldiers, one comrade wounded) — Pomponio guilty of "innumerables robos, homicidios (este de un soldado)"; Pomponio was fusilado (shot by firing squad) at Monterey by government order; 29+ yrs total service (entered 1799)
