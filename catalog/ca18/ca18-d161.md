# ⭐⭐⭐ THE 1824 CHUMASH REVOLT TRIAL & SENTENCES: depositions of captured rebels of Missions La Purísima & Santa Inés, then

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n188–n189** · tomo p. LVII.624–44 · 1824-03-23 · unspecified · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ THE 1824 CHUMASH REVOLT TRIAL & SENTENCES: depositions of captured rebels of Missions La Purísima & Santa Inés, then the *fallo* — 7 La Purísima neophytes (Baltasar, Pacífico, Esteban, Cines, Antonino, Felipe, José Andrés) sentenced to death (último suplicio) as accomplices in the murder of José Dolores Sepúlveda + 3 companions (night of 21 Feb 1824); Mariano, Pacomio, Benito & Bernabé to 10 yrs presidio + exile as "agentes principales de la revolución" (cf. Doc 46); 7 more to 8 yrs; 5 acquitted. (Antonino, in the chapel before execution, named Martiniano & Camilo as further accomplices.)
