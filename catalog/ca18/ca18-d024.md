**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n89–n90** · tomo p. 875–76 · 1830-08-31 · Savage abstract · 

Hoja de servicios of Capt. José María Estudillo (entered service 23 Jul 1796; total 44 yrs 4 mo; served Loreto/Monterey/San Diego). Campaigns: against Indians at Monterey Bay (killed/captured/wounded), the Tulares, Sierra Nevada, and a road-finding expedition to Sonora via the Colorado. Swore to Independence 9 Oct 1822. Died 8 Apr 1830
