⭐ Certified copy of the 1787 filiación (service record) of his father, sargento Ignacio Vallejo [⚠ Savage's header reads "Vicente Vallejo" — but the subject enlisted Monterey 8 Jun 1787, signs "Ygn° Vallejo," made cabo 1 Nov 1789, sargento 24 Sept 1805: this is Ignacio Vallejo]: son of Gerónimo & Antonia Gómez, native of Ntra Sra de las Cañadas (Guad.ª), campista, age 33, 5'5", witnesses sarg. Mariano Verdugo & cabo Manuel de Vargas; approved by Fages; certified by Mariano G. Vallejo, Alf. of the SF presidial company
