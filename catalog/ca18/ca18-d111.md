⭐ Hoja de servicios: age 44, b. Loreto, noble, married; entered service 1 Oct 1797, total 28 yr 3 mo. Campaigns: the Nov 1818 Bouchard defense of Monterey; promoted Tte, then led "la reducción de los rebeldes de la Misión de San Luis y la Purísima" [1824 Chumash Revolt] as jefe; Santa Catarina frontier (Río Colorado); 1822 independence oath; 1825 commissioned to negotiate the capitulation of the Spanish warships *Asia* & *Constante* to the Mexican republic
