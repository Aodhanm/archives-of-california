# Cluster of minor sumarias: militiaman José Romero (imprisoned from 5 Mar 1828 over an incest/amancebamiento matter w/ hi

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n386–n390** · tomo p. LXVI.~360–390 · 1828–1829 · Savage abstract · 

Cluster of minor sumarias: militiaman José Romero (imprisoned from 5 Mar 1828 over an incest/amancebamiento matter w/ his stepdaughter María Josefa/Rafaela — the women said voluntarily) freed by Echeandía 4 Jun 1829 for time served + ordered to amend; and Eugenio's scandalous amancebamiento with the gentile Petra Paula (wife of a wounded man) → 2 months public works + paying the victim's cure; process passed to the Jefe Político at San Diego 26 Sept
