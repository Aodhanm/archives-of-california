# ⭐⭐⭐ Arrival of the Russian frigate "Nicolás" [Nikolai] from Sitka carrying Gov. of Russian America Ivan Kupreanov, 6 off

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n134** · tomo p. LV.274–6 · 1840-10-28 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ Arrival of the Russian frigate "Nicolás" [Nikolai] from Sitka carrying Gov. of Russian America Ivan Kupreanov, 6 officers, 61 men (war-grade crew/armament; the ship itself not a warship). Kupreanov comes to discuss the last Anglo-American ship at Bodega + settle matters with the Governor of Ross, then on to Valparaíso; tonnage-charge query; told to go to Monterey, he awaited Gen. Mariano G. Vallejo or his reply
