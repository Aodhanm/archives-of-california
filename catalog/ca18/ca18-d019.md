# Child homicide. Body of neophyte shepherd boy Juan found near Mission SF, rawhide cord at neck. Four neophyte boys — Mar

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n73–n75** · tomo p. 721–36 · 1822-11-12 (events Jan 1822) · unspecified · 

Child homicide. Body of neophyte shepherd boy Juan found near Mission SF, rawhide cord at neck. Four neophyte boys — Marcelo (11), Ventura & Ildefonso (9), Vicente Francisco (10) — strangled him with cord, sand in mouth, kicking, over a quarrel about fetching tuna. Full confession; baptism records signed by Fray José Altimira
