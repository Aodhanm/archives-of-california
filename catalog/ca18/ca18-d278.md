# PROVINCE-WIDE ARTILLERY INVENTORY (all 4 presidios): Monterey 20 (6×8, 7×6, 7×4; 7 bronze/13 iron); San Francisco 14 (3×

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n275** · tomo p. LXII.696–7 · 1830-12-07 · unspecified · ⭐⭐⭐

PROVINCE-WIDE ARTILLERY INVENTORY (all 4 presidios): Monterey 20 (6×8, 7×6, 7×4; 7 bronze/13 iron); San Francisco 14 (3×24, 2×12, 8×8, 1×4; 6 bronze/8 iron); Santa Bárbara 7 (1×8, 5×6, 1×3; 2 bronze/5 iron); San Diego 13 (3×8, 7×6, 3×4; 8 bronze/5 iron) = 54 pieces. But the bronze 4-pdrs are dismounted, the naval & battle carriages useless, and the gun-platforms (except SF's) so bad "casi no se puede maniobrar en ellas." All belong to the federation
