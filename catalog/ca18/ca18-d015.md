# Neophyte Fabio stabbed & killed his wife Joviniana (2 neck, 1 back, 1 arm wounds). Defender pleads adultery + provocatio

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n65–n68** · tomo p. 644–70 · 1822-06-02 · unspecified · 

Neophyte Fabio stabbed & killed his wife Joviniana (2 neck, 1 back, 1 arm wounds). Defender pleads adultery + provocation (she attacked him w/ knife) and that Fabio is barely Christianized. Fiscal's dictamen treats minimal Christianization as mitigation — baptized 6–7 yrs, never confessed, "sin ninguna emoción en las cosas de Nuestra Sagrada religión," ignorant he could appeal to justice → asks 5 yrs presidio public works, removed from area (not death)
