⭐⭐ Order to investigate the contraband of the American frigate "Franklin" (Capt. John Bradshaw) at Santa Catalina Island / Baja California — supercargo G. Simpson; involves Bradshaw's other vessel the *Lascar* and a brig's boat; the famous 1828 Bradshaw/*Franklin* smuggling affair (the *Franklin* later defied San Diego and ran out under fire); the averiguaciones are a copy of the originals; escribano Francisco de Araujo
