# ⭐⭐ Commissioned to investigate possible *sublevación* in the San Luis, San Miguel, San Antonio & Soledad missions during

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n121–n123** · tomo p. LV.1–3 · 1824-04-14 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Commissioned to investigate possible *sublevación* in the San Luis, San Miguel, San Antonio & Soledad missions during the 1824 Chumash Revolt; Fr. Luis Martínez & the escort cabo assure him no sign of disloyalty — the San Luis Indians were solicited by the "Purísimeños" [the Purísima rebels] but refused. Embeds Vallejo's address to the neophytes (praises their steadfastness, threatens "providencias con fuego y sangre" if they revolt) + his confiscation of all Indian weapons and order that no Indian keep arms at home
