**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n310–n311** · tomo p. LXIV.472–85 · 1827-09-02 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Información sumaria vs Joaquín Solís for smuggling Virginia tobacco — 2 tercios in a cart driven by an Indian; the tobacco was handed to the rentas encargado José Mª Herrera; Solís jailed, Franc° Pacheco made responsible to deliver it to the Govt on demand. The future 1829-Revolt leader (Solís) and his co-conspirator (Herrera) already linked here in a 1827 contraband case — two years before the revolt
