# ⭐ "Sobre entrega de mercancías detenidas": the 3 trunks of American Charles (Carlos) Laing detained ~6 months at the SD 

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n92–n94** · tomo p. 983–1031 · 1828-10-14 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ "Sobre entrega de mercancías detenidas": the 3 trunks of American Charles (Carlos) Laing detained ~6 months at the SD custom-house. Recaps the 1826 contract Laing & Henry D. Bolt made for sea-otter/seal hunting (pesca de lobos marinos), provisioning the ship, payable in effects at Canton/Manila prices; the duties dispute (Domingo Carrillo held no duties owed since payment was in ship's provisions; Laing disagreed); admin. Juan Bandini & Tte. Ibarra inventory the trunks; per the law of 4 Sept 1823 declared confiscated by the Comandancia Gen. court; referred to the Comisario of Sonora
