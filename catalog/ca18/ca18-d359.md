# ⭐⭐ Causa de estupro vs artillero Ignacio Higuera for attempting to rape the ~5-yr-old girl Francisca Valencia (14 Sept 1

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n407–n410+** · tomo p. LXVI.~808 ff · 1828-09-14 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Causa de estupro vs artillero Ignacio Higuera for attempting to rape the ~5-yr-old girl Francisca Valencia (14 Sept 1828); Argüello's cover-note calls it "el tercer acontecimiento en pocos días en este presidio" (the third such case in days at SF) — "exigen el remedio… los excesos de la maldad humana"; graphic child testimony (he dragged her to a second kitchen, gagged her with a roof-tile shard); inspection by a "mujer perita" found blood on her shift; witnesses Antonino Buelna, Concepción Valenzuela, Mariano Uriarte. Higuera (18, native of SF) confessed; defense by Alférez Francisco de Haro (25 Oct 1828) argued the only proof is the confused child's statement, pleading "la miseria del hombre"; fiscal Estrada left it to the consejo de guerra; remitted via Argüello to Echeandía — no final outcome recorded (ends n415). [a cluster of child sexual assaults at the SF presidio, 1828]
