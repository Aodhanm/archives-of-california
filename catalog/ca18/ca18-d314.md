# ⭐ Standing orders for the comandante of the Guard at the fortaleza de San Joaquín on sighting ships: sentinel signals wi

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n302** · tomo p. LXIV.107–8 · 1827-04-21 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Standing orders for the comandante of the Guard at the fortaleza de San Joaquín on sighting ships: sentinel signals with a cannon shot, all artillerymen gather at the fort; no foreign-vessel boat permitted to land; if the ship is a warship, raise the flag + fire a blank cannon; if merchant, omit the blank — a Mexican-period coast-defense / foreign-vessel protocol
