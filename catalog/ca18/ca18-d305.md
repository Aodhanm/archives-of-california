# Criminal vs four S. Blas infantry soldiers (Antonio García, Francisco Arellano, Anacleto Balcazal, José Mª Romero) for r

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n292–n293** · tomo p. LXIII.628–38 · 1826-10-23 · Savage abstract · 

Criminal vs four S. Blas infantry soldiers (Antonio García, Francisco Arellano, Anacleto Balcazal, José Mª Romero) for robbing the houses of officers Mariano Estrada & Subtte de art.ª José Ramírez (chocolate, tallow balls); fiscal: García broke the lock & induced the others, and for this alone "debía ser ahorcado, según ordenanzas" (hanged); the first two petition the Comisario Gen. (SD, 1 Jan 1827) for release, their 4-yr presidio term done
