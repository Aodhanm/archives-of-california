# Incest sumaria (causa por incesto en 1er grado). Father (surnamed Pinto/"Quinto") accused of repeated rape of daughters 

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n7–n65** · tomo p. 537–647 · 1822-06-12 · unspecified · 

Incest sumaria (causa por incesto en 1er grado). Father (surnamed Pinto/"Quinto") accused of repeated rape of daughters M.ª Gertrudis & M.ª Concepción (tied victim by neck with cord; threatened to kill them if they reported); allegedly brought Manuel Mendoza + an unnamed Indian to assault them to shift blame. Reported by brother-in-law Juan Germán. ~58 pp. of testimony (Gertrudis, Mendoza, Encarnación Ramírez, Gabriela Higuera, Villareal, careos). Gov. Solá's signature absent from copy
