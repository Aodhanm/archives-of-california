# Desertion case vs 4 Sta Bárbara soldiers (cabo José García, Tomás García, Rafael López, José Mª Ayala) who abandoned the

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n434–n435** · tomo p. LXVIII.6–55 · 1829-04-29 · Savage abstract · 

Desertion case vs 4 Sta Bárbara soldiers (cabo José García, Tomás García, Rafael López, José Mª Ayala) who abandoned the San Luis Obispo mission detachment under Alf. Rodrigo del Pliego; escribano José Mª Arana; the 3 (already doing guard) freed to resume service, but cabo Gabriel García, as the ringleader (cabecilla), condemned to 2 yrs presidio (public works) at San Francisco
