# ⭐ Homicide: cavalry soldier Víctor Linares killed vecino Juan Germán (night of 28 Apr); defender Tte Agustín Zamorano ar

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n210–n212** · tomo p. LIX.140–262 · 1826-04-29 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Homicide: cavalry soldier Víctor Linares killed vecino Juan Germán (night of 28 Apr); defender Tte Agustín Zamorano argued self-defense (Linares on sentry, Germán attacked him armed). Court-martial (Rocha, Valle, Ibarra, Portilla, Romualdo Pacheco + Echeandía) unanimously ACQUITTED him, honor & promotions intact, returned to service (14 Jul)
