# ⭐⭐ Calumny case against a missionary: José Mª Pérez (Branciforte) accused of calumniating Padre Fr. Luis Taboada (Missio

**C-A 18 (Dep. St. Pap. Benicia Mil., 1822–28), scan n178–n183** · tomo p. LVII.573–87 · 1824-06-05 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Calumny case against a missionary: José Mª Pérez (Branciforte) accused of calumniating Padre Fr. Luis Taboada (Mission Santa Cruz); testimony (son-in-law Gervasio Soto, Margarita Rodríguez, Bruno Casal) circles a scandalous rumor — whether the Padre said "the Indians fornicate with the village women" or that the village "alcahuetea" Indian women for the friars' service. The Padre, confronted, said he had no complaint; the accused held in prison, case passed to the alcalde
