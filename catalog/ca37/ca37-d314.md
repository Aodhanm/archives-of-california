# THE MARSH–GANTT CONTRACT (complete, 6 articles): 'Contrato… pª persecución de los indios ladrones del interior qe contin

**C-A 37 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n141–n143** · Sav p. 140–142 · tomo p. 707–708 · 1845-03-27 · Savage abstract · 

THE MARSH–GANTT CONTRACT (complete, 6 articles): 'Contrato… pª persecución de los indios ladrones del interior qe continuamte atacan las propiedades de los ranchos' — the raiders strike 'desde esta ciudad inclusive hasta Sonoma'; 1º Marsh & Gantt raise their own expedition; 2º under Comandancia instructions; 3º pay = 'QUINIENTAS RESES' when the country is freed 'á satisfacción del Gtno.' + 'LA MITAD DE LA CABALLADA QE QUITEN'; 4º volunteers keep 'el dominio esclusivo' of their own recovered property; 5º the division at the RANCHO DE LOS POCITOS (between Marsh's and José Mª Amador's ranchos), w/ four impartial umpire-judges; 6º duplicate originals. Signed 'Pico = Juan Bandini, Srío. = Juan Marsh = Juan Gault'; certified by Bandini 'Marzo (quizá pretendió decir Abril) 7 de 1845' [Savage's note] — anti-raiding warfare privatized to Anglo-American contractors, paid in cattle and plunder shares
