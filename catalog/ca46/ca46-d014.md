# 'Misión de Sta Cruz': forwards the representations of the Ministros of Missions San Francisco de Asís (Fr. José Altimira

**C-A 46 (Departmental Records), scan n12** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 8–9 · 1823-04-10 · Savage abstract · 

'Misión de Sta Cruz': forwards the representations of the Ministros of Missions San Francisco de Asís (Fr. José Altimira) & Santa Cruz (Fr. Luis Gil de Taboada) to suppress/relocate Mission Santa Cruz ('carece ya de conquista,' mountainous, no progress, would harm neighboring pueblos) & consolidate — elevated to H.M. for approval; *the paper trail behind Altimira's founding of Mission San Francisco Solano (Sonoma) on 4 Jul 1823*
