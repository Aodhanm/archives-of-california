# 1824 CHUMASH REVOLT suppression order: a 'fatiga militar' Monterey→San Luis Obispo to investigate the 'alarma' of La Pur

**C-A 46 (Departmental Records), scan n104** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 74–75 · 1824-04-06 · Savage abstract · 

1824 CHUMASH REVOLT suppression order: a 'fatiga militar' Monterey→San Luis Obispo to investigate the 'alarma' of La Purísima, Sta Cruz[?] y Santa Inés; imprison the ringleaders & bring them to Monterey, publicly burn their weapons (bows kept for hunters via the padres), apply 50 azotes to secondary participants, warn of harsher punishment for renewed 'tumulto/sedición' *(⚠ 'Sta Cruz' anomalous — the canonical revolt trio is Purísima/Santa Inés/Santa Bárbara; verbatim re-read flagged)*
