# THE SONOMA FOUNDING DOSSIER: recounts that commissioner Fr. Agustín Fernández de San Vicente, the late Prefect Payeras &

**C-A 46 (Departmental Records), scan n32–n33** · Sav p.  · tomo p. ≈21–22 · 1823-08-04 · Savage abstract · 

THE SONOMA FOUNDING DOSSIER: recounts that commissioner Fr. Agustín Fernández de San Vicente, the late Prefect Payeras & the Ministers of Mission San Francisco resolved to move Mission SF to a site 'en la Contra Costa del Norte, en la frontera de la Gentilidad'; Fr. José Altimira presented himself before the Exma. Diputación, which resolved the mission be aggregated & 'trasportase en masa' to a convenient site — Sonoma, which Argüello approved; orders Altimira to proceed. → the primary paper trail of Mission San Francisco Solano (Sonoma), founded by CIVIL authority (Diputación + jefe político), 1823
