# 'Escasez de efectos' — since arriving at this presidio in January, 'no han sido más qe puras lamentacⁿes' over the soap 

**C-A 46 (Departmental Records), scan n28–n29** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 19–20 · 1823-03-01 · Savage abstract · 

'Escasez de efectos' — since arriving at this presidio in January, 'no han sido más qe puras lamentacⁿes' over the soap shortage afflicting 'todos los individᵒˢ de esta provᵃ'; the nearest mission, assigned 1,200 [lbs], is persuaded it can only manage the 300 it had pledged; Argüello asks the other missions' Padres to supply the rest 'á proporción de sus posibilidades' — even in his own house 'han faltado 3 semanᵃˢ pª lavar la tropa' *(✓ read vs scans n28–n29, 2026-07-02 verify pass)*
