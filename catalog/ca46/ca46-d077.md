# THE CONSTITUTIONAL-STATUS DOCUMENT: recounts the installation of the Exma. Diputación on 9 Nov 1822 (names the vocales: 

**C-A 46 (Departmental Records), scan n119–n120** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 84–85 · 1825-05-02 · Savage abstract · 

THE CONSTITUTIONAL-STATUS DOCUMENT: recounts the installation of the Exma. Diputación on 9 Nov 1822 (names the vocales: José Antonio Carrillo, Francisco Ortega, José Palomares[?], Carlos Castro…); the Spanish Constitution 'ya fenecido,' replaced by the sworn federal charter; suspends the Diputación pending the Sup. Gobierno because 'esta [prov.] no se puede reputar por Estado, si no un Territorio unido á Méjico' — it lacks the required population; California must follow the constitution & government 'particular del Estado Méjico'
