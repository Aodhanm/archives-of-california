# Límites de Misión San Francisco [de Asís]: in Solá's govt (1814) limits were fixed w/ the presidio officers + Fr. Ramón 

**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n73** · Sav p. 74 · tomo p. 1 · 1828-01-08 · Savage abstract · 

Límites de Misión San Francisco [de Asís]: in Solá's govt (1814) limits were fixed w/ the presidio officers + Fr. Ramón Abella (then administrator); from the misión E to the estero de los Laureles; S across the sierra a rancho San Pedro (sembríos + ganado mayor); lands *de temporal* (arroyos insufficient for riego, but ciénegas/pozos); S lands wooded w/ robles, pinabete y alerce
