# THE 1773 ECHEVESTE REGLAMENTO (full, w/ accompanying fiscal dictamen) — «Reglamento é instrucción… para el auxilio, resg

**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n250** · Sav p. 228–~263 · tomo p. 1–~38 · 1773 · Savage abstract · 

THE 1773 ECHEVESTE REGLAMENTO (full, w/ accompanying fiscal dictamen) — «Reglamento é instrucción… para el auxilio, resguardo y conservación de los antiguos y nuevos establecimientos de las Californias con el Departamento de San Blas», by Juan José de Echeveste — the FIRST regulatory & salary code for the Californias (superseded by Neve's 1781 reglamento). Contents: detailed presidio budgets — Monterey (3,000-p governor, sgt/cabos, 102 soldiers @ 365 p, carpenter/smiths, guarda-almacén w/ accounting duties), San Diego (Tte, 22 soldiers…), the California antigua/Baja presidios (total 16,450 p); the San Blas naval Department (comisario, arsenal, the new frigate + packetboats crews & rations); then the 'puntos instructivos' narrative articles (the 1769–70 founding of SD & Monterey via the packetboat *El Príncipe*, supply/economy rules for the comisario & San Blas/Loreto/SD/Monterey), a fianza requirement, grand total ~24,406 pesos, signed Echeveste. → [[project_californio_article]] + [[project_sal_papers]] («Habilitado's Empire» — the fiscal-administrative origin of the situado/habilitación system). ⚠ well-known published document; value = Savage's copy is HERE in C-A 62.
