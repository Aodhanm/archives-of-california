# Derecho de anclaje on the WHALING fleet: since 1822, when buques balleneros began putting in at the Santa Bárbara roadst

**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n319** · Sav p. 297 · tomo p. 364–365 · 1826-11-18 · Savage abstract · 

Derecho de anclaje on the WHALING fleet: since 1822, when buques balleneros began putting in at the Santa Bárbara roadstead, they have paid 10 pesos anchorage (per practice in other South-American ports); de la Guerra asks whether the duty pertains to ship-entry or has another object.
