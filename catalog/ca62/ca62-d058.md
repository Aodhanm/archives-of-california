**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n77–n78** · Sav p. 78–79 · tomo p. 5–6 · 1828 (sin fecha) · Savage abstract · 

Límites de Misión Santa Clara: 8 leguas N-S × 6 (3 more anchо) E-O; río Guadalupe + sierra; ⭐ Gov. Solá granted 'el Cañón de Los Bolbones' to Capⁿ Luis Argüello, w/ the arroyo San Francisquito as the mission/rancho boundary; 25 fanegas de sembradura de regadío; note that the mission's cattle brand 'se figura al margen (que no aparece)' + the ear-crop mark Solá ordered used
