THE 1769 FIRST-LAND-DIVISION DIARY (Sacred Expedition) VERBATIM: Rivera's company marches Velicatá→San Diego (121 leguas), arrives 14 May 1769, sights the packetboats *San Carlos* & *El Príncipe*, finds all the maritime officers sick with scurvy; helps unload the *Príncipe* (17 May–28 Jun); ⭐ Gov. Portolá arrives 29 Jun and decides to push north by land despite the ships' delay, leaving an 8-soldier escort at the nascent Mission San Diego; diary closed 3 Jul 1769 — a founding-of-Alta-California primary source
