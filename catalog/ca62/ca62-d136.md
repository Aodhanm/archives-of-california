# 1833 elections under Gov. Figueroa (per the electoral law of 12 Jul 1830): electors Juan Bandini (chosen presiding elect

**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n357–n364** · Sav p. 334–341 · tomo p. 12–19 · 1833-03-21+ / (Nov session) · Savage abstract · 

1833 elections under Gov. Figueroa (per the electoral law of 12 Jul 1830): electors Juan Bandini (chosen presiding elector, 6 votes), José Joaquín Estudillo, Anastasio Carrillo, José Antonio Estudillo, José Antonio Carrillo, Ignacio Martínez, José Mariano Estrada; extended per-district credential review (San Luis Rey, San Juan Capistrano, LA, Sta Bárbara, San José, San Francisco, Monterey, San Diego — San José faults «hijos de falta de experiencia» dispensed; a Santa Bárbara election ruled null then the elector admitted). Territorial Deputation result: vocales Carlos Antonio Carrillo, Pío Pico, [de Haro], José Antonio Carrillo, José Antonio Estudillo, José Castro; suplentes Santiago Estrada, Carlos Castro, José Pérez. Commissioners incl. Ortega & Vallejo. → [[project_californio_article]].
