# APPENDIX of actual FIERRO/SEÑAL DRAWINGS — the visual brands the text entries said «se figuran al margen», = 3 physical 

**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n120–n146** · Sav p. 121–125 · tomo p. 48–53 · 1828–34 · Savage abstract · 

APPENDIX of actual FIERRO/SEÑAL DRAWINGS — the visual brands the text entries said «se figuran al margen», = 3 physical leaves: (1) the 'Pobladores de Branciforte' list-with-brands (José Antⁿ Robles fundador, Joaquín Castro, Luis García, José Mª Pérez, Franᶜᵒ Suárez, Franᶜᵒ Rodríguez, José Mª Salazar, Joaquín Buelna, Franᶜᵒ González, Juan Pinto, Martín Vela, José Antⁿ Boronda + Mariano & Feliciano Soberanes — matches Doc 74); (2) a N-district brand grid-table (missions SF de Asís/Solano/San Rafael/San José/San Carlos/San Juan Bautista/Soledad/San Antonio/San Miguel + grantees Francisco de Haro, Mariano G. Vallejo, Rancho San Pablo, Bárbara Sánchez, each fierro + venta/señal); (3) multiple S-district brand grid-tables — S-1 (Simí/Pico, Rómulo/Los Alamitos, missions San Diego/San Luis Rey/San Juan Capistrano, Refugio-Ortega); S-2 (San Gabriel, Tía Juana/Santiago Argüello, Jamul/Pío Pico); an LA/SB-foreign grid (David Spence, John B.R. Cooper, Norberto Cantúa, Misión San Fernando, El Conejo/de la Guerra, Ortega/Refugio); and a Monterey-ranchos grid (Bolsa de San Cayetano/I. Vallejo, Vega del Pájaro, Corralitos/Amésti, Francisco de Haro, Juan Malarín, Bolsa del Moro Cojo). ⚠⚠ HEAVY IA duplicate-render drift: n120–n146 = only ~5–6 physical brand-leaves scanned ~26× with dark/illegible verso frames interspersed (n120/124/129/133/137/142 = dark). Cleanest frames: n123 (Branciforte), n128 (N grid), n131 (S-1), n136 (S-2/San Gabriel), n141 (LA/SB-foreign), n145 (Monterey ranchos). Nearly every fierro is paired w/ a *señal* (ear-mark) drawing. ⭐ scale-4 capture worth it for the registry/map collection. ⚠ do NOT count these as separate DB records — 1 appendix.
