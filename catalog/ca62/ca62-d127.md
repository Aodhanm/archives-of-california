# Markers — convict-transportation to Alta California + a fiscal credit: (a) 15 Oct, Sta Bárbara, a company credit of 7,03

**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n331–n332** · Sav p. 309–310 · tomo p. 577 / 584 / 586–587 / 679 · 1829 · Savage abstract · 

Markers — convict-transportation to Alta California + a fiscal credit: (a) 15 Oct, Sta Bárbara, a company credit of 7,036 p vs. a frigate; (b–d) several Mexican convicts sentenced to serve in Alta-California presidios — José Mª Mendoza &c. (3 yrs), José de León Salinas (4 yrs), José de la Cruz García (Veracruz deserter, 4 yrs), another (10 years), 1827–29 for robbery/desertion. → presidio convict-labor.
