Rancho San Rafael grant (Arroyo Hondo, 1½ lg from Mis. San Gabriel, camino de Monterey) — one of California's EARLIEST private land grants (1784, Fages); expanded 1797–98 (sitio de la Zanja) by Gov. Borica; carries an Indian-treatment clause (treat cristianos & gentiles 'con el amor y caridad recomendado por las leyes,' don't injure them, but live with due caution) → land-tenure origins + [[indian-raiding-article]]
