# The 1772 FRANCISCAN–DOMINICAN DIVISION of the Californias VERBATIM (full dossier): per the Reales Cédulas of 4 Nov 1768 

**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n13–n18** · Sav p. 14–18 · tomo p. 348–373 · 1772-04-07 · verbatim transcription · 

The 1772 FRANCISCAN–DOMINICAN DIVISION of the Californias VERBATIM (full dossier): per the Reales Cédulas of 4 Nov 1768 + 8 Abr 1770 the orders divide the peninsula — Dominicans ← the old missions + the Velicatá frontier down to the arroyo de San Juan ~5 leguas past San Diego mission's confines, then ENE to the Gulf/Colorado; Franciscans ← San Diego northward to Monterey, SF «y mas adelante»; over Croix's & Gálvez's objections; the Guardian cedes the Baja missions, asks cattle for the new missions (memorial 27 Oct 1771); confirmed by Iriarte (Apr 1772)
