# «Estado de las misiones de Baja California»: exorbitant sums on ~30 missionaries/missions for only 4,442 souls; poor yie

**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n42–n44** · Sav p. 43–45 · tomo p. 748–752 · 1792 · verbatim transcription · 

«Estado de las misiones de Baja California»: exorbitant sums on ~30 missionaries/missions for only 4,442 souls; poor yields; argues to reduce/suppress missions lacking land/water (to 300 families each) & relocate the Indians; no need to send for more missionaries from Spain; «el falso concepto que hicieron formar de la Cal. los interesados»
