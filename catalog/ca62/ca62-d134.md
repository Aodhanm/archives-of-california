**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n351–n353** · Sav p. 328–330 · tomo p. 6–8 · 1828-10-03/06 · Savage abstract · 

1828 elections. Electors: José Tiburcio Castro, Francisco Atanasio Cota, Manuel Domínguez, Miguel González de Alava, Zamorano (proceeding without SF's elector, per art. 6 «bastan 5 electores»). Territorial Deputation (6 Oct, bando 30 Jul 1828): propietarios Carlos Antonio Carrillo, Pío Pico, Vicente Sánchez, José Tiburcio Castro; suplentes Manuel Domínguez, Silvio Pacheco, Carlos Castro (síndico of San José) — with recorded vote tallies. → [[project_californio_article]].
