# CONVICT-TRANSPORTATION REGISTER to the Californias: a multi-page «Relación de los reos… destinados a Californias» (Nombr

**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n333–n338** · Sav p. 311–316 · tomo p. 589–605 · 1827–1829 · Savage abstract · 

CONVICT-TRANSPORTATION REGISTER to the Californias: a multi-page «Relación de los reos… destinados a Californias» (Nombres · Fecha de la sentencia · Tiempo de condena · Destino) plus the underlying individual sentencing orders — dozens of named Mexican convicts sentenced 1827–29 (terms 2–10 yrs, mostly robbery/desertion/homicide) to serve in the Alta-California presidios («Al Presidio» / «Reos de California» / «Servir en Californias»). Names incl. José Mª Blanquel, Ignacio Carrillo, Pedro Juan Salinas, Miguel Álvarez, Crescencio Araujo, José Gpe. Salgado, Mariano Kildon, Ignacio Olvera, Miguel Sánchez &c. A rare named roster of the deported-convict ('cholo') population sent to California on the eve of the 1830s. → presidio-population / social history + [[project_californio_article]] (the convict-soldier stigma).
