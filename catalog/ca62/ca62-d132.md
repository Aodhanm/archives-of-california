# «CUADERNO DE ACTAS DE ELECCIONES DE DIPUTADOS AL CONGRESO GENERAL Y Á LA DIPUTACIÓN TERRITORIAL, 1827–34» — the election

**C-A 62 (Archives of California, Miscellaneous), scan n345–n348** · Sav p. 322–325 · tomo p. 1–3 · 1827-02-16/18 · Savage abstract · 

«CUADERNO DE ACTAS DE ELECCIONES DE DIPUTADOS AL CONGRESO GENERAL Y Á LA DIPUTACIÓN TERRITORIAL, 1827–34» — the election-minutes book (title leaf @n345); runs continuously n345–n366 (internal fol. 1–35). [a] 1827 congressional election at the capital San Diego: the *junta de electores de partido* (Vicente Sánchez, Francisco de Haro, Carlos Antonio Carrillo, Estevan Munrás, Zamorano) convened 16 Feb under the bando of 4 Nov 1826; 18 Feb (after Mass & *rogativas*) they first named Pablo Vicente de Solá propietario, but a challenge to Solá's current residency (art. 19 §2; convocatoria 17 Jun 1823 / decreto 19 Jul 1824) triggered «una larga discusión»; on re-vote they unanimously elected Capt. José de la Guerra y Noriega *diputado propietario* + Gervasio Argüello *suplente* to Congress. ⭐ Prime evidence of Californio electoral self-governance — full procedure, residency dispute, substitution. → [[project_californio_article]] 'Repertoire of Self-Rule'.
