# Crueldades contra Indios — has learned that after alfz Guadalupe Sánchez's campaign vs the malévolos, 3 men & 3 women ca

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n213–n214** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.739–40 · 1829-08-05 · Savage abstract · 

Crueldades contra Indios — has learned that after alfz Guadalupe Sánchez's campaign vs the malévolos, 3 men & 3 women captured out of combat were killed by gunfire, lance & arrow and executed, and 3 more captured women were about to be killed — witnessed by christians of S. Carlos; names witness-soldiers (Nicolás Berreyeza, Ignacio Torres, Gabriel Alviso, Pedro Higuera +2); orders a careful investigation
