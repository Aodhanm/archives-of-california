# long entry (3 leaves): on suspending & reconstituting the Junta [Diputación] with candid character-appraisals of the Cal

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n3–n5** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.21–25 · 1829-04-13 · Savage abstract · 

long entry (3 leaves): on suspending & reconstituting the Junta [Diputación] with candid character-appraisals of the Californio political class — Juan Bandini ('buenos principios é ilustración… recta capacidad'), Antonio Buelna, Carlos Antonio Carrillo, Vicente Sánchez ('veterano… inquieto'), José Mª Herrera (Tesorería), Anastasio Carrillo, Cruz Castro (San José); notes the Junta was suspended (packed w/ soldiers), members won't leave homes/interests to serve; ⭐ explains he governs from San Diego because Monterey's cold, damp climate harms his health
