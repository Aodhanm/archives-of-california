# Baja California mission land-grab: the San Antonio ayuntamiento (jefe político subalterno José Mª Mata) + the Baja diput

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n35–n36** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.204–209 · 1829-08-31 · Savage abstract · 

Baja California mission land-grab: the San Antonio ayuntamiento (jefe político subalterno José Mª Mata) + the Baja diputación voted to extinguish Missions Todos Santos & San José del Cabo and distribute their lands to vecinos, excluding the padres (Feb–Mar 1829); Echeandía protests it all as *illegal* & asks the supreme govt to fix the authority question
