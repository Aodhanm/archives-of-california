# Comercio extrangero — the frigate 'Brooklyn' (sobrecargo Wm. Gale) may land part of its cargo at Sta Bárbara under condi

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n99** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.425–7 · 1829-03-06 · Savage abstract · 

Comercio extrangero — the frigate 'Brooklyn' (sobrecargo Wm. Gale) may land part of its cargo at Sta Bárbara under conditions (guía from Monterey/SD, no re-embarking); reaffirms only Monterey is habilitado per the 16 Nov 1827 law; the 30 Sept measures are ad interim, not a law, and give no free-traffic guarantee for the 3–4 yrs the ship will trade the coast (SF/SB/S. Pedro/SD)
