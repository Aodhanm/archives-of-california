# Proclama á los Sublevados de Monterey — Echeandía's proclamation to the rebel soldiers following Solís's plan; charges t

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n272–n274** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.5–8 · 1830-01-07 · Savage abstract · 

Proclama á los Sublevados de Monterey — Echeandía's proclamation to the rebel soldiers following Solís's plan; charges that comisario Pedro María Herrera deceived them into an unjust partido; ties the rising to the Spanish reconquest attempt on the Veracruz coast ('guerras de hermanos'); calls them to lay down arms & surrender the revoltosos, promising their lives, property & liberty will be respected and their error forgiven
