# Alzamiento de Joaquín Solís — Solís (sentenced to 10 yrs presidio) has declared himself among the soldiers under protest

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n256–n257** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.878–84 · 1829-11-25 · Savage abstract · 

Alzamiento de Joaquín Solís — Solís (sentenced to 10 yrs presidio) has declared himself among the soldiers under protest of 'mal gobierno' and headed a mutiny at Monterey for subversion of order; convoke a garrison junta to opine; his adherents who do not lay down arms and free the authorities will be declared traitors and accomplices of the Spaniards who have invaded the playa de Veracruz (the Barradas invasion)
