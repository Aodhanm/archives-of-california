# friar-loyalty / naturalization census — roster of the Spanish Franciscans w/ ages, health & constitutional-oath position

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n25–n28** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.149–156 · 1829-06-26 · Savage abstract · 

friar-loyalty / naturalization census — roster of the Spanish Franciscans w/ ages, health & constitutional-oath positions: Boscana (willing), Oliva (refused since '26), F. Martín, Peyri (sickly), Zalvidea, Salazar, José Sánchez, González de Ibarra, Liñán (blind), Blas Ordaz, M.A. de Vitoria, Arroyo de la Cuesta ('swore but keeping fidelity to the King of Spain'), Fortuni, Abella, Rivas, Pedro & Juan Cabot (refused, sworn to Fernando VII), J.B. Sancho, Amorós (fidelity to Mexico), Esténaga (couldn't per conscience), Catalá/Martín, Viader, Narciso Durán ('no juró'), Juan Moreno — most refuse the oath per conscience/loyalty to Spain but promise civil obedience; + a list of lay Spaniards
