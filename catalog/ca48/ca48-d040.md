**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n30–n31** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.157–167 · 1829-06-30 · Savage abstract · 

'Causas para no expulsar los PP. Españoles': the 21 missions depend on the padres — without them the neophytes fall into 'cuadrillas de pillaje,' revert to 'vida salvaje,' the missions collapse, no spiritual care; don't expel the non-jurors 'hasta que se puedan reemplazar por P.P. mejicanos'; Baja's decline blamed on lack of religious; notes Fr. Luis Antonio Martínez's passport request
