# Echeandía's reply — refuses to hand over the political & military command demanded in Solís's name ('José Solís reducido

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n274–n278** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.8–14 · 1830-01-08 · Savage abstract · 

Echeandía's reply — refuses to hand over the political & military command demanded in Solís's name ('José Solís reducido á subteniente'); traces the revolt to the Monterey & SF garrisons' grievances over unpaid haberes and comisario José Mª Herrera's fiscal failures; will cede command only to a Supreme-Government appointee (the Diputación has no legal power to substitute him) and accepts only the rebels' unarmed surrender to comandante en jefe Romualdo Pacheco
