# Eclesiásticos españoles á desterrar — per the ley de 20 Marzo: the Spanish missionary friars who by their last declarati

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n190–n191** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.665–6 · 1829-07-06 · Savage abstract · 

Eclesiásticos españoles á desterrar — per the ley de 20 Marzo: the Spanish missionary friars who by their last declarations refused all oath/protest in favor of the system, and indifferent ecclesiastics, are to be given passports at once; those who swore per the 20 Dec 1827 law must submit exemption documents within a month (art. 4 of the 20 Mar 1829 law)
