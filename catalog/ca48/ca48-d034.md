# cornerstone governance report — ayuntamientos, justice, jails, Baja-frontier division: council composition of each town 

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n20–n23** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.113–125 · 1829-06-25 · Savage abstract · 

cornerstone governance report — ayuntamientos, justice, jails, Baja-frontier division: council composition of each town — Los Ángeles (alcalde + 4 regidores + síndico), San José (alcalde + 2 regidores + síndico), Villa de Branciforte (alcalde under the Monterey commander), Monterey & San Diego neighborhoods; Baja partido seats Loreto / mineral de San Antonio / San José del Cabo / Santo Tomás (each a full ayuntamiento); the civil-military justice split (alcaldes: civil suits ≤100 pesos + minor offenses; serious/criminal → presidio military committee or Sonora's comandancia); 'el estado de la Justicia es lamentable' for lack of a *letrado*; foreigners settled for the fur/hide trade, some married in; Baja partidos 'apenas están al alcance del gobierno' for distance/roads
