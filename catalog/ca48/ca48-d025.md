# broad civic/economic report: ⭐ acute shortage of primary-school teachers (proposes a 1,000$/yr presidio schoolmaster); t

**C-A 48 (Departmental Records), scan n13–n15** · Sav p.  · tomo p. p.67–70 · 1829-05-19 · Savage abstract · 

broad civic/economic report: ⭐ acute shortage of primary-school teachers (proposes a 1,000$/yr presidio schoolmaster); the 3 gente-de-razón pueblos (San José, Los Ángeles, Branciforte) insolvent & subsistence-only; local crafts (serapes, blankets, tanning, saddlery); flax & hemp grow well but abandoned 'por falta de instrucción'; vines & olives abundant SD→San Buenaventura; ⭐ commerce = foreign (US/Europe) ships extracting hides & tallow, paying in imported goods
