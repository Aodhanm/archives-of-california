Indagación on Smith's whereabouts: a guide ('un tal Manuel') who presented at the Mission testifies he saw Capt. Smith two weeks earlier at the boca del Cajón de Musupiabit and guided the party (5 men on horseback) toward the interior rancherías; nothing can be done to detain the Americans; reported to the Comandante Gral
