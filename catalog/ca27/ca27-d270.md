# (survey Find #12) Circular publishing the Ley of 20 May 1829: the Spanish ecclesiastic missionaries who in their latest 

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n256–n257** · Sav p. II:92 · tomo p. 334–336 · 1829-07-06 · Savage abstract · 

(survey Find #12) Circular publishing the Ley of 20 May 1829: the Spanish ecclesiastic missionaries who in their latest declarations refused all oath/protest in favor of the System get their passport [to leave]; all ecclesiastics & laymen (whether or not sworn per the 20 Dec 1827 formula) must within a month remit documents proving their exception, for the Supreme Govt to grant a permanence document (Arts 4–5, Ley Marzo 1829)
