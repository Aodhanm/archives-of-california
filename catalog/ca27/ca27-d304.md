# Circular: informed that enemies of the Constitution plot reactions & coalitions against the govt pronounced by the Plan 

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n295–n296** · Sav p. II:131 · tomo p. 433–434 · 1830-02-07 · Savage abstract · 

Circular: informed that enemies of the Constitution plot reactions & coalitions against the govt pronounced by the Plan de Jalapa — threatening civil war and new invasions — urges vigilance against the 'seducción' of the incautious soldier; whether as head of the pronounced army or Supreme Magistrate his aim is only internal peace & external security
