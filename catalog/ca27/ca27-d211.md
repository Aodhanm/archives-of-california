# Description of his Rancho Nuestra Señora de Altagracia (El Conejo): granted by Gov. Solá in 1822; boundaries (Ojo de Agu

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n209** · Sav p. II:49 · tomo p. 104–106 · 1827-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

Description of his Rancho Nuestra Señora de Altagracia (El Conejo): granted by Gov. Solá in 1822; boundaries (Ojo de Agua Amarga E–W, the portezuelo de Simí to the Sierra de Malibú N–S); ~3 leagues by 2; neighbors Missions San Fernando & San Buenaventura and ranchos Simí & Santa Mónica; pasture-rich, timbered, two arroyos; grazed by cattle & sheep with small maize/bean plots
