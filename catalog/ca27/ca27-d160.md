# On Argüello's refusal to hand over the archive without a direct order: Estudillo crated the archive (1–4 Nov) and loaded

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n153–n154** · Sav p. 163–164 · tomo p. 583–587 · 1826-11-08 · Savage abstract · 

On Argüello's refusal to hand over the archive without a direct order: Estudillo crated the archive (1–4 Nov) and loaded the cajón on a mule to depart, but Argüello appeared and ordered it unloaded, insisting it would not leave his house without an express delivery order; Alférez Rodrigo del Pliego pursued him 2½ leagues to order it back — 'un escándalo nunca visto en Monterey' (pairs with doc 159)
