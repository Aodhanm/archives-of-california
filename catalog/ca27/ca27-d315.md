# Formal act organizing the Maritime Customs House (Aduana) & Comisaría of the Port of Monterey: per the Comisario Gral de

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n308–n310** · Sav p. II:145 · tomo p. 453–455 · 1830-10-21 · verbatim transcription · 

Formal act organizing the Maritime Customs House (Aduana) & Comisaría of the Port of Monterey: per the Comisario Gral de Occidente's 2 May recommendation & the 30 Sept 1824 Supreme-Govt dispositions, they agree provisionally on its structure & staffing — an Administrator w/ subaltern-comisario functions (1,000 p./yr), a Contador/vista (800), a resguardo commander w/ alcaide functions (811), a guard-clerk (422), a provisions servant, & two sailors for the port-captaincy boat (connects to the 1828 Aduana-establishment docs 219–221)
