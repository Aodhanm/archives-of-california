# On the arrest of Cadete Romualdo Vallejo (of Ten. Estrada's company) for removing a posted proclamation; Estrada shields

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n171** · Sav p. II:9 · tomo p. 14–15 · 1827-07-19 · Savage abstract · 

On the arrest of Cadete Romualdo Vallejo (of Ten. Estrada's company) for removing a posted proclamation; Estrada shields him and at his house mocks González's orders; asks his own request to be relieved be made public so it is not thought he is removed for a crime (copy by Zamorano)
