**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n2** · Sav p. 3 · tomo p. 3–6 · 1822-03-29 · Savage abstract · 

Defense vs. Lord Cochrane's squadron raiding Baja Calif.; quotes Gov. Capt. José Darío Argüello (Baja) via mission cordillera — enemy ships in the Gulf of Cortés seeking provisions, attack on Mission San José del Cabo 1 Feb, defenders take ~12 prisoners; invokes the 8 Oct 1818 (Bouchard-era) defense circular; withdraw Indian aid, guard livestock; ack. De la Guerra (SB 31 Mar), Fran.co Mª Ruiz (SD 2 Apr)
