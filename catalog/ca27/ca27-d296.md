# Interrogation of the Solís-revolt participants who abandoned the party: Cabo Manuel Peña + soldiers Miguel Alvarado, Ign

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n283–n285** · Sav p. II:119 · tomo p. 407–408 · 1830-01-18 · verbatim transcription · 

Interrogation of the Solís-revolt participants who abandoned the party: Cabo Manuel Peña + soldiers Miguel Alvarado, Ignacio Romero, Fulgencio Higuera, Antonio Hernández (SF company) and Francisco Soto, José de Jesús Río, Noberto García, Lugardo Botiller (Monterey company) — all disavow the pay grievances Solís's plan cited; shown the SF/Monterey income-expense states, they acknowledge being paid ~two-thirds of their wages; signed Echeandía, Domingo Carrillo & Agustín V. Zamorano (Echeandía's formal rebuttal of the revolt's pretext)
