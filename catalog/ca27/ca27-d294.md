# Post-revolt arms & provisions distribution: alférez José Mª Ramírez to return with more cavalry and bring 'reos de consi

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n281–n282** · Sav p. II:117 · tomo p. 402–403 · 1830-02-06 · Savage abstract · 

Post-revolt arms & provisions distribution: alférez José Mª Ramírez to return with more cavalry and bring 'reos de consideración' (the captured Solís rebels); distribute the remaining soldiers to the Department's missions except San Luis Obispo, keeping the presidios' mounted service, the trusted men given better arms; SF soldiers to their presidio; ⭐ request 100 fanegas wheat + maize from Mission San Antonio — incl. some the padre had prepared at Santa Cruz for Solís
