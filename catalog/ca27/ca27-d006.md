# The McCulloch, Hartnell & Co. contract (margin: '(McCulloch)'): commissioners Luis Nicolás(?) & Guillermo (William) Hart

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n7** · Sav p. 8 · tomo p. 18–20 · 1822-06-12 · Savage abstract · 

The McCulloch, Hartnell & Co. contract (margin: '(McCulloch)'): commissioners Luis Nicolás(?) & Guillermo (William) Hartnell ('Comp.ª Inglesa') authorized to trade 2 years with the missions & vecinos; fixed prices — *sebo* (tallow) 2 p., *jabón* (soap), *manteca de res* (lard) 3 p., *carne en salmuera* (salt meat) 4 p., etc.; vecinos/rancheros free to join
