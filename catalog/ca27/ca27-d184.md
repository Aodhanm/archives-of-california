# Certificate for Capt. Jedediah S. Smith: at his request they attest there is no doubt he was forced into Alta California

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n181–n182** · Sav p. II:19 · tomo p. 35–37 · 1826-12-20 · verbatim transcription · 

Certificate for Capt. Jedediah S. Smith: at his request they attest there is no doubt he was forced into Alta California by want of provisions & water (his horses perished, the barren country lat. 33–42 N); they examined his passports — granted by the U.S. Superintendent of Indian Affairs — and deem them legal & conforming to U.S. law; he intends to leave toward the head of the Columbia
