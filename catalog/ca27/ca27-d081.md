# Argüello embarked on an Anglo-American schooner (2 Oct 1825) without giving notice or naming who would hold command; an 

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n78** · Sav p. ~81 · tomo p. 310–311 · 1825-11-03 · Savage abstract · 

Argüello embarked on an Anglo-American schooner (2 Oct 1825) without giving notice or naming who would hold command; an officers' junta (2 Nov 1825, per the fuller acta re-copied at n105–n106) named Estudillo (senior officer) garrison commandant. Junta members: José Estrada (Lt. cav.), José Ramírez (Lt. arty. & arm cmdt), Antonio Valle, José Mª Ramírez (alférez), Rodrigo del Pliego, Juan Rocha (grenadiers)
