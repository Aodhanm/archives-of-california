# Rumored uprising at San Juan Capistrano proves false — the Indians denied any revolt; they had reacted to the escort cor

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n122** · Sav p. ~127 · tomo p. 463–464 · 1826-01-23 · Savage abstract · 

Rumored uprising at San Juan Capistrano proves false — the Indians denied any revolt; they had reacted to the escort corporal wheeling out the cannon; they only wanted a *fiesta* to welcome him; reprimanded & dispersed
