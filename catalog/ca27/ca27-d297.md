# Índice of the Supreme Government decrees received in Jan 1830 (Relaciones 1–19, Hacienda 1–15, Guerra 1+): the expulsion

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n286–n290** · Sav p. II:122 · tomo p. 409–415 · 1830-01 · reference · 

Índice of the Supreme Government decrees received in Jan 1830 (Relaciones 1–19, Hacienda 1–15, Guerra 1+): the expulsion-of-Spaniards deadline; cabinet changes (Espinosa de los Monteros & Bernardo González out, José Mª Herrera named Srio de Justicia); Vicente Guerrero's entry as President; ⭐ abolition of slavery in the Republic; emergency powers to preserve Independence; the Plan de Montaño amnesty; press-responsibility law; ⭐ occupying the properties of those 'en país enemigo'; 600,000-peso copper coinage; 2% on foreign goods; Texas paper-money liquidation; amnesty for officers married without permit & deserters
