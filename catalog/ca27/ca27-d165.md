# Punitive-expedition & incident report: a man's escopeta burst (skull fractured, brain matter exposed), treated by the tw

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n157–n159** · Sav p. 167–169 · tomo p. 601–609 · 1826 · Savage abstract ·
