# (survey Find #9) The RAC's long wheat/provisions trade with California; lacking salt at the frequented ports it has had 

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n230–n232** · Sav p. II:65 · tomo p. 193–201 · 1828-10-13 · verbatim transcription · 

(survey Find #9) The RAC's long wheat/provisions trade with California; lacking salt at the frequented ports it has had to source it from the Sandwich Islands — requests permission to gather salt at San Quintín; bearer Adolf Etholén (cmdr of the RAC brig *Baykal*) empowered on the salt price/duties AND commissioned to propose the renewal of the mutual otter-hunting contract granted in 1826; ~12 Aleuts & 6 skin canoes aboard, but under strict orders not to hunt otters without the governor's permission (aboard mainly to load salt at San Quintín)
