# His defense re not participating in the Plan of Solís: Solís was about to attack SF and he prepared to resist, but seein

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n293** · Sav p. II:129 · tomo p. 421–427 · 1830-02-12 · Savage abstract · 

His defense re not participating in the Plan of Solís: Solís was about to attack SF and he prepared to resist, but seeing the troops demoralized and intending to defect to Solís, he had to remain neutral awaiting results; on the 30th he was ordered to hand over the arms-command to José Sánchez and the habilitación to Francisco Sánchez, and to remain in his house as a prisoner
