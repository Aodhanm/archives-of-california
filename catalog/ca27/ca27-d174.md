# Resignation letter No. 35: submits his command amid complaints against him; a bitter defense against the three presidial

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n165–n170** · Sav p. II:4 · tomo p. 4–9 · 1827-02-22 · Savage abstract · 

Resignation letter No. 35: submits his command amid complaints against him; a bitter defense against the three presidial Cavalry officers he brands insubordinate, the arrest of Cadete Romualdo Vallejo & the withheld 100-peso company fund, the estupro Real orden of 30 Oct 1796 cited as precedent; ends refusing to recognize any authority in the Ilustre Ayuntamiento, answerable only to the Supreme Govt (copy signed Zamorano, Monterey 2 Aug 1827)
