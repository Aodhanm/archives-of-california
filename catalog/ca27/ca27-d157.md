# Cover report on the Sta Bárbara/Sta Inés/La Purísima revolt: the Indians have still not resubmitted to their old reducci

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n151** · Sav p. 161 · tomo p. 577–578 · 1826-10-06 · Savage abstract · 

Cover report on the Sta Bárbara/Sta Inés/La Purísima revolt: the Indians have still not resubmitted to their old reducción despite harsh punishment; frames their aim as 'acabar con toda la gente civilizada para volver á su anterior gentilismo'; warns of the weak military force & scant white population sustaining the Territory
