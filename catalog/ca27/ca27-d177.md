# Defense of his administration of justice: he offered to hear any vecino's proposals for good government (none came), but

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n172–n173** · Sav p. II:10 · tomo p. 10–17 · 1827-02-24 · Savage abstract · 

Defense of his administration of justice: he offered to hear any vecino's proposals for good government (none came), but cleared a backlog of justice-claims up to 10 years old; only the amancebados, estupradores & insolent would complain of him; has asked (23rd) to be relieved of the command (copy by Zamorano, 19 Jul 1827)
