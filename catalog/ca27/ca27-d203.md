# 'Sobre Smith' (survey Find #8 Doc C): the escort corporal reported Smith near San Bernardino; the sick man stayed there;

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n202** · Sav p. II:42 · tomo p. 84–86 · 1827-10-04 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre Smith' (survey Find #8 Doc C): the escort corporal reported Smith near San Bernardino; the sick man stayed there; the Indians took the party's horses, packs & some firearms at the river crossing (the 8 carried only 5 arms), with the killings the corporal reported; they came seeking provisions (killed 4 reses); Smith brought a letter for Echeandía whose envelope could not be read
