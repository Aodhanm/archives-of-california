# Romero's expedition narrative: left Mission San Gabriel 15 Dec 1823 with Lt. Estudillo & 40 men via San Bernardino, haul

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n24** · Sav p. 25 · tomo p. 73–78 · 1824-02-02 · verbatim transcription · 

Romero's expedition narrative: left Mission San Gabriel 15 Dec 1823 with Lt. Estudillo & 40 men via San Bernardino, hauling the cannon, guided by a San Juan Capistrano Christian Indian, through the Cajuenche nation; on the return the party got lost, the *caballada* collapsed, six days without water, reaching San Gabriel the last day of January (refers to Estudillo's fuller diary)
