# Formal averiguación on Capt. Smith & his companions (survey Find #8 Doc A): Smith found at Rancho San Bernardino (escort

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n196–n198** · Sav p. II:35 · tomo p. 35–36, 71–73 · 1827-02-08 · Savage abstract · 

Formal averiguación on Capt. Smith & his companions (survey Find #8 Doc A): Smith found at Rancho San Bernardino (escort corporal & mayordomo), a sick man left behind, no verified passport → detain-if-presented order (nothing done, Smith withdrew on the 2nd). Encloses Smith's translated letter to Padre José Sánchez: he hadn't expected to return; above San Francisco friendly Indians turned, and at the 'río de los Cajones Santos' killed 8 of his men & seized the whole caballada, survivors left near-naked; the party carried 5 firearms
