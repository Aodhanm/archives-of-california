# Inocente García (Rancho Natividad) beat the maimed artilleryman Atilano Soto, resisted arrest claiming only the Alcalde 

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n190** · Sav p. II:29 · tomo p. 56–57 · 1827-03-07 · Savage abstract · 

Inocente García (Rancho Natividad) beat the maimed artilleryman Atilano Soto, resisted arrest claiming only the Alcalde could arrest him, falsely alleged Soto's adultery (disproved by the husband); González ordered 50 lashes & jail, but García escaped 'protegido, se cree, por el mismo Alcalde' — a civil-military jurisdiction clash (copy Zamorano, 2 Aug)
