# (survey Find #11) Petition of the San José vecinos opposing the expulsion of the Spanish missionary friars: after long r

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n262–n264** · Sav p. II:98 · tomo p. 361–362 · 1829-08-25 · Savage abstract · 

(survey Find #11) Petition of the San José vecinos opposing the expulsion of the Spanish missionary friars: after long reflection they cannot silence the pueblo's discontent at the 20 March 1829 decree expelling all Spaniards incl. priests; without their pastors they would be left 'como gentiles… abejas errantes, hijos huérfanos,' deprived of spiritual succor at the hour of death — they beg the Supreme Govt to except the Spanish missionary ministers from the general law; the Ayuntamiento pleads in the name of the whole pueblo, 'leales hijos de la Iglesia'
