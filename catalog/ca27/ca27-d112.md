# ⚠️ Savage re-copies the 1825 Herrera/comisario, *Morelos*/*Nieves* cargo, officers'-junta, *Asia*/*Constante* arrival & 

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n103** · Sav p. ~106–~125 · tomo p. 413–~460 · 1825 (copied 1827) · reference · 

⚠️ Savage re-copies the 1825 Herrera/comisario, *Morelos*/*Nieves* cargo, officers'-junta, *Asia*/*Constante* arrival & Figueroa→Romero material here as 1827 copies — n105/n115 explicitly note 'Es copia… de José Mª Ybarra en 24 de Feb.º de 1827 y de Zamorano Ag.º 2' and 'este documento es la repetición del primero.' Not re-cataloged doc-by-doc. Added value captured: (a) the officers'-junta acta names the Monterey garrison officers — see doc 81; (b) the *Asia* arrival detail (74 guns, 600-man garrison, anchored Loreto 27 Apr 1825) — see doc 68; (c) Agustín V. Zamorano appears as 1827 copyist
