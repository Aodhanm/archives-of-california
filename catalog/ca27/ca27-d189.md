# Artillery-salute protocol conflict: won't fire the 45-gun salva for the installation of the Diputación Provincial withou

**C-A 27 (Departmental State Papers), scan n185–n186** · Sav p. II:24 · tomo p. 48–50 · 1827 · Savage abstract · 

Artillery-salute protocol conflict: won't fire the 45-gun salva for the installation of the Diputación Provincial without authorization (cites the Supreme Govt circular of 4 Jan 1826), but will for the Corpus Christi procession if the padres hold it — 'la sufriré gustoso antes de dejar de cumplir con lo que… se me ordena'
