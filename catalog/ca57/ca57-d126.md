# THE GRAHAM AFFAIR — official resolution (Mexico-City side): on the «sublevación intentada por varios extranjeros residen

**C-A 57 (Superior Government State Papers), scan n140–n142** · Sav p. 141–143 · tomo p. 112–114 · 1840-09-23 · Savage abstract · 

THE GRAHAM AFFAIR — official resolution (Mexico-City side): on the «sublevación intentada por varios extranjeros residentes» (comms of 22 Abr), the President rules on the prisoners taken to TEPIC by Capⁿ José CASTRO aboard the barca nacional 'Guipuzcoana' (May 1840): the ringleaders Isaac GRAHAM, Alberto (Albert) MORRIS, Guillermo (William) CHARD, Jorge J. (George) BOWLES stay jailed under Tepic's court; the non-naturalized / not-married-to-mexicana to be EXPELLED from the Republic + barred from returning to California; those married to mexicanas freed on bail; José Castro held in Mexico to answer the deportees' charges re his conduct as escort commander; warns the Gobⁿ to send full justificatory docs in future «para no dar lugar á reclamaciones» (the British/US diplomatic protests → release + compensation)
