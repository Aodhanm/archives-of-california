# FIGUEROA'S GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS (13–14 arts., the charter of his governorship): §1 repeat Echeandía's unfulfilled instru

**C-A 57 (Superior Government State Papers), scan n84–n91** · Sav p. 85–92 · tomo p. 272–281 · 1832-05-17 · Savage abstract · 

FIGUEROA'S GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS (13–14 arts., the charter of his governorship): §1 repeat Echeandía's unfulfilled instructions; §2 restore tranquility after the revolt; §3 territorial statistics (mission lands property-vs-baldías, gentile tribes, coastal islands); §4 ⭐ distribute cultivable mission parcels to capable neophytes so «se vaya sustituyendo al Sistema de Misiones… disminuya el influjo de los misioneros… queden secularizados los ministros»; §5 send able mission youth to Mexico City (literary + artisan education); §6 promote commerce — export surplus peltry/seeds + missions build cabotage ships; §7 ⭐⭐⭐⭐ colonization per the 1824 law/1828 reglamento but «que no se introduzca más que un corto número de familias Rusos ó de los E.U. del Norte»; fortify the new San Rafael presidio; found settlements where Russians or Americans might «usurpar»; límite = paralelo 42; §8–9 ⭐ named grants — Ignacio Martínez, José Ramírez, Henry Fitch (Salina de SF), + approve Henry Virmond's establishment in SONOMA; §10 peaceful reduction of «indios bárbaros»; §11 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ «Informará qué fuerzas mantienen los Rusos en el Puerto de la BODEGA» + Russian/US positions & designs; §12 ⭐⭐⭐ counter the unlicensed American otter «costeo» by making it a native «industria nacional»; §13–14 mines + «el estado de la pesquería» (sea-otter fishery) + stray cattle
