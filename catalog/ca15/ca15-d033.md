# ⭐⭐ Loreto Real Almacén inventory — incl. the Real Quinto on Baja California pearls. Inventory/avalúo of effects under co

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n30–n31** · orig. p. 449–465 · Sav p. 31–32 · 1779-03-23 · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Loreto Real Almacén inventory — incl. the Real Quinto on Baja California pearls. Inventory/avalúo of effects under comisario Álvarez Osorio = $24,968; adds powder, cartridges, and pearls (perlas de toda mar) from the diving (buceo) seasons, owed as the Real Quinto: 2¾ "libras largas" from the 1775 buceo handed to Gov. Felipe de Neve; 3 adarmes (1776, Misión Santiago de las Coras, "en el Sur"); 10½ adarmes (1777, Mulegé); 7½ (1778, dived "en balsa" by the Santiago Indians). Unpriced effects valued by the Tte. Gobernador → total $25,715. ⭐ Rare pearl-fishery / royal-tribute source. Orig. pp.449–465.
