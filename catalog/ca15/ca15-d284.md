# Loreto Departamento de Marina pay & shipbuilding: the 5 Oct 1789 decree set wages — 1st arráez $20/mo; calafate, herrero

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n207** · orig. p. 612–613 · Sav p. 202 · s.f. (decree 5 Oct 1789) · unspecified · 

Loreto Departamento de Marina pay & shipbuilding: the 5 Oct 1789 decree set wages — 1st arráez $20/mo; calafate, herrero, carpintero $20 each; sailors $10 each; + armada ration. Then to supply the presidio with smaller craft: keep a goleta + a small 74-ft-keel goleta + an existing lancha; a new 71-ft-keel goleta is being built for víveres, crewed at 12–15 plazas. Orig. pp.612–613.
