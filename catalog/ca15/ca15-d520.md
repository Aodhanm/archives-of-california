# ⭐⭐⭐ Abandonment of the Bodega Bay establishment, 1794. Sal reports that the establishment of the puerto de Bodega will n

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n354** · orig. p. 332–337 · Sav p. 348 · 1794-07-25 · unspecified · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ Abandonment of the Bodega Bay establishment, 1794. Sal reports that the establishment of the puerto de Bodega will not be carried out, and that he has reassigned to the San Francisco presidio various articles that had been destined for Bodega. Documents the Spanish decision *not* to occupy/fortify Bodega — the direct counterpart to Doc 515. Orig. pp.332–337.
