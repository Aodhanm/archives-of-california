# ⭐⭐ Officer uniform, armament & mount regulation. *Grand uniform:* blue casaca (royal cloth), red cuffs/lapel/collar with

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n136–n138** · orig. p. 3–7 · Sav p. 134–136 · 1780-09-21 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Officer uniform, armament & mount regulation. *Grand uniform:* blue casaca (royal cloth), red cuffs/lapel/collar with 2 gold galones (3-thread), matching breeches, laced chupa, gilt buttons, 3-cornered laced hat — worn only inside the presidio on feast days, royal birthdays & accessions, and outside when on commission where the Governor, Inspector or any jefe is. *Small uniform:* short blue tripe/paño chupa with the same divisas + charretera. *Arms:* same calibers/sizes as the troops but finer quality. *Mount:* silla brida or "mestiza," or vaquera. (Companion to the troop reg, Doc 172.) Orig. pp.3–7.
