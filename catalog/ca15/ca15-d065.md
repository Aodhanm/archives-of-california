# ⭐⭐ Los Ángeles pobladores pay-settlement — contract terms & enlistment dates. Ajuste in favor of the settlers $2,202; co

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n60** · orig. p. 13–14 · Sav p. 59 · 1781-12-31 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Los Ángeles pobladores pay-settlement — contract terms & enlistment dates. Ajuste in favor of the settlers $2,202; contracted for 3 years at $10/month + $1/day ration for 10 years. Enlistment dates: Félix Villavicencio (30 May 1780), Antonio Mesa (11 Jun), José Lara (24 Jun), José Vanegas (11 Aug), Pablo Rodríguez (13 Aug), Manuel Camero (17 Aug), Antonio Navarro (21 Aug), José Moreno (Sep), Basilio Rosas (7 Nov), Alejandro Rosas, Antonio Rodríguez, Luis Quintero (5 Feb 1781). Ration began on arrival at San Gabriel. Foundational LA-founding genealogy. Orig. pp.13–14.
