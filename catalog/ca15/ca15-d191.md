⭐ Monterey presidial company distribution & defense, 1782. 1 Tte, 1 Alférez, 1 cirujano, 1 sargto, 5 cabos, 46 soldados, 1 carpintero, 2 herreros = 57. Postings: caballada 3; ganado 4; 2 cabos + 14 soldados detached at missions; 1 sargto + 9 soldados on the Colorado expedition (Yuma); pueblo San José 2; presidio Santa Bárbara 2; free for service the officers + 2 cabos + 10 sold. Caballada 95 horses, 113 mules; recua 27; arms reserve 44 pairs pistols, 30 lance-heads; "armamento… en mediano estado de defensa"; troop well clothed & provisioned. Orig. pp.537–563.
