# ⭐ Factura of effects bought for California by order of Viceroy Bucareli — 44 tercios, 22 cajas, 17 cajones: víveres, sad

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n20–n21** · orig. p. ~276+ · Sav p. 21–22 · 1772-02-21 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Factura of effects bought for California by order of Viceroy Bucareli — 44 tercios, 22 cajas, 17 cajones: víveres, saddles, knives, cloth (paño, frazadas, bayeta, seda), spices, firearms, gold/silver braid, needles, crystal, cooking oil, aguardiente, white wine. Cost $8,853.2.7; muleteer Juan Manuel Barros carried 670 arrobas 21 lbs from Guadalajara (freight $587), per the Sonora-expedition treasurer Juan José de Echeasta[?]; Ruiz's receipt to deliver at La Paz. Orig. pp.~276+.
