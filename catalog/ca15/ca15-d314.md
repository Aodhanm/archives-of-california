**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n225** · orig. p. 137–467 (scattered) · Sav p. 220 · 1787-12-01 · unspecified · 

SB presidial company, 1787: Tte. Goycoechea; Alférez vacant until June (José Argüello joins); Sargtos Pablo Cota, José Olivera, Raymundo Carrillo; cabos Pedro Amador & Ignacio Olivera; ~53 soldados, total ~60–61 (Oct 56, Dec 53). In July cabo Pedro Amador was promoted to sargento of the San Francisco company. Orig. pp.137–467 (scattered).
