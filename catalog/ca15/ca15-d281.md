# ⭐⭐⭐ Livestock delivered to the Lapérouse expedition at Monterey, 1786. In the Monterey Real Hacienda livestock account (

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n206** · orig. p. 440–445 · Sav p. 201 · 1786-12-31 · unspecified · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ Livestock delivered to the Lapérouse expedition at Monterey, 1786. In the Monterey Real Hacienda livestock account (caballar 35, burral 15, vacuno 1,882 [+377 born], etc.; total 2,566 head $1,622), among the cattle sold this year: 27 steers @ $5, 2 calved cows @ $6, 1 heifer @ $3, "entregadas á las fragatas del Rey de Francia *Boussole* y *Astrolabe*" (Savage notes the original wrote "Brújula y Astrolavio"). The only known C-A documentary trace of the French *Lapérouse* expedition's Sept 1786 call at Monterey — the first foreign (non-Spanish) expedition to visit Alta California. Orig. pp.440–445.
