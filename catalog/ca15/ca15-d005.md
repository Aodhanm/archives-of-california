# ⭐ Criminal causa — death of an India at San Gabriel. Ortega (24 Sep 1779) ordered Sargto Mariano Carrillo, with 2nd alca

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n13–n14** · orig. p. 211–221 · Sav p. 14–15 · 1779-10-22 · verbatim transcription · ⭐

⭐ Criminal causa — death of an India at San Gabriel. Ortega (24 Sep 1779) ordered Sargto Mariano Carrillo, with 2nd alcalde José Mª Verdugo & Salvador Caroaga[?], to San Gabriel to take testimony on the death of the india Gregoria, found beaten/whipped to death two days before; soldado Ignacio Alvarado was accused (by Ignacio María) of killing her, and cabo José Ignacio Olivera jailed him. Witnesses (4–6 Oct): cabo Olivera, alcalde Juan Diego Verdugo, alférez-indio Nicolás, indio Matías, indio Ignacio María (accuser), Avelia[?] (the accused's mother). Nothing appeared against the accused — the accuser had borne false witness; Ortega ordered the false accuser jailed & given 35 lashes over 3 days, with a warning to be far slower to accuse, "y mucho más en asuntos criminales." Orig. pp.211–221.
