# Invoice of 45 tercios + 7 cajones to the Monterey habilitado: effects $7,476.4½; libranzas (Mission San Carlos to the Co

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n229–n230** · orig. p. 703–709 · Sav p. 224–225 · 1787-04-21 · unspecified · 

Invoice of 45 tercios + 7 cajones to the Monterey habilitado: effects $7,476.4½; libranzas (Mission San Carlos to the Colegio de San Fernando $3,500 + $1,000; Mission San Antonio $500); specie $301.2; freight $499.1½; total $10,980.3. Note: one box held $2,008 cash from a libranza drawn by Col. Fages against his salary for the Monterey presidio; Ortega deducts $40.4 for shortages → $10,939.7.4. Orig. pp.703–709.
