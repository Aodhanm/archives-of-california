**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n307–n309** · orig. p. 603–611 · Sav p. 301–303 · 1796-12-31 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Net product of Real Hacienda branches, 1792–1796: diezmos $1,138; toros alzados (wild bulls) $2,691; salt $737; correos $567; sea-freight $92; reconocimientos $75; tobacco $17,431 (by far the largest); quintos $73. A five-year royal-revenue summary — tobacco dominates California crown income. Orig. pp.603–611.
