# Sentence of deserter Lepis: rather than ship him to San Blas (for the Viceroy), given the shortage of supplies & labor f

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n49–n50** · orig. p. 926–929 · Sav p. 50–51 · 1776-05-20 · unspecified · 

Sentence of deserter Lepis: rather than ship him to San Blas (for the Viceroy), given the shortage of supplies & labor for the Monterey/SD works, condemned to 1 year forced labor, on ration & without pay; the robbed mission animals paid from his alcances. Orig. pp.926–929.
