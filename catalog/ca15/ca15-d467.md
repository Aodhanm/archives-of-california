⭐⭐⭐ SF company & fortifications 1798. Tte. Argüello; Sargto Pedro Amador; 4 cabos, 31 soldados; + 25 men of the 1st Catalan Volunteer company — 5 sold & 1 cabo manning the Batería de San Joaquín (Castillo), an alférez & 1 cabo at the Yerba Buena battery, relieved every 8 days; the artillery troop keeps 1 cabo & 5 soldiers at San Joaquín, the sergeant in the presidio. Artillery: 3 iron 24-pdrs + 1 of 12 in the batteries; 8 bronze half-culverins of 8; 2 bronze pedreros at Santa Cruz & Santa Clara with unserviceable carriages. Invalids: Alférez Ramón Lasso, cabos Gabriel Peralta & Miguel Pacheco. Orig. pp.525–630.
