# ⭐⭐ Sal's habilitación accounts, 1 Jan 1782 – 12 Sep 1787 (as Tte./Alférez Habilitado of the Monterey presidial company):

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n111** · orig. p. 109–140, 141–167 · Sav p. 110 · 1788-04-30 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Sal's habilitación accounts, 1 Jan 1782 – 12 Sep 1787 (as Tte./Alférez Habilitado of the Monterey presidial company): received effects valued $76,472.5; disbursed leaving $44,450.4 in the company's favor; net $654 in his favor. Savage's note (verbatim): "Sal had been suspended & arrested as a defaulter & proved triumphantly that he was innocent of the charge maliciously preferred against him by the Ayudante Inspector [Nicolás] Soler." Diligencias presented 11 Aug 1788. (Material later than the "1782" tomo label — volume not strictly chronological.) Orig. pp.109–140, 141–167.
