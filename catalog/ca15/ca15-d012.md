# Effects for Calif. presidios/missions shipped on the paquebot *Príncipe*: 10 tercios raw tobacco (1990 lbs) for Monterey

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n17–n18** · orig. p. 256–261 · Sav p. 18–19 · 1772-04-03 · unspecified · 

Effects for Calif. presidios/missions shipped on the paquebot *Príncipe*: 10 tercios raw tobacco (1990 lbs) for Monterey presidio, 21 for San Carlos missions, 1 for San Diego; smith's tools, cloth, 10 boar + 30 hens for breeding. Orig. pp.256–261.
