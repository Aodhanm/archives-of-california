# ⭐ Detailed condition report of the Monterey company's armament: 1st-class escopetas (cal. 14, 6 serving since 1776, 17 s

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n222–n223** · orig. p. 627–628 · Sav p. 217–218 · 1787-12-31 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Detailed condition report of the Monterey company's armament: 1st-class escopetas (cal. 14, 6 serving since 1776, 17 since 1781, defects in their lías); 2nd-class (11, short caliber, since 1774–75, bad locks/barrels); 3rd-class (4, bad); short 16 to complete the company. Pistols (7 pairs since 1781, rest since 1779) with the defect that the safeties are internal — they fire inadvertently; "la falta de armero causa graves perjuicios." Espadas of good temper (no reserve); lance-heads bad. Munitions: 53 arr. powder (= 780 ball cartridges of both calibers), 10 arr. lead, 20 arr. 23 lb balls, 1,400 gunflints. Orig. pp.627–628.
