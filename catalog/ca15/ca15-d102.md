# Internal Reglamento/carta instructiva for the presidios — 16 numbered articles (1º–16º) on presidial accounting procedur

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n83–n91** · orig. p. 129–141 · Sav p. 82–90 · 1782-03-01 · unspecified · 

Internal Reglamento/carta instructiva for the presidios — 16 numbered articles (1º–16º) on presidial accounting procedures: applying the new Reglamento, charge/credit of the companies' situado, the gratification fund ($1,245 in 1779), Arsenal/Marina dependencies, distribution of effects to the troop, reintegration to the Habilitado & Real Plata ($226), víveres for mission cattle, the still-pending liquidation of the troop/arriero/vaquero accounts Rivera y Moncada formed at SD & Monterey (charge vs. Real Plata $25,017, tying back to the Fages accounts), the inventory/handover of the Departamento de Marina stores (art. 12º–15º: the *balandra y lanchas*, jarcia, remos, anclas, delivered per the Alférez José de Velázquez), carpentry/blacksmith/masonry tools, and the rule that the Habilitado's handover be made within "4 á 5 meses." Closes (art. 16º) noting the great distance prevents prompt delivery. Long administrative text. Orig. pp.129–141.
