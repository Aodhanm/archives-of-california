**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n45** · orig. p. 765–767 · Sav p. 46 · 1778-04-12 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Report of the execution. Ortega reports having shot the 4 Pamó ringleaders, explaining the "poderosas razones" for not delaying the punishment, and awaits the Governor's resolution; the complicit rancherías sued for peace/pardon and were told the only way was to settle and live in peace. Orig. pp.765–767.
