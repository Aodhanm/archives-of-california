⭐ Officer appointments for the Californias presidios. Made alféreces: Sargto Mariano Carrillo, Manuel García Ruiz, Ramón Lasso de la Vega; made tenientes: Alonso Velázquez[?] & Diego Gonzáles — all to serve in the Californias. 5 officers now provided of the increase Gov. Neve proposed; they replace the comisarios, whose posts are abolished. Orig. pp.147–150.
