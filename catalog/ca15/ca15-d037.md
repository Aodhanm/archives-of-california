# ⭐ SD presidio + dependent-missions pay-settlement roster (ajuste de sueldos y raciones), 1777 — every man with balance o

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n33–n38** · orig. p. 534–689 · Sav p. 34–39 · 1778-03-10 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐ SD presidio + dependent-missions pay-settlement roster (ajuste de sueldos y raciones), 1777 — every man with balance owed/in-favor: Tte. Ortega, guarda-almacén Pedro y Gil, Sargto Mariano Carrillo, cabos, 48 soldados rasos, carpintero, herrero (Felipe Romero), arrieros & mission sirvientes; notes 7 soldiers transferred to Monterey 26 Nov 1777 by order of Gov. Neve, men supplied at SD for the Loreto troop, and ⭐ extraordinary 1777 provisions for the troops Anza brought (Tte.-Col. Juan Bautista de Anza) + Indians who drove cattle to Monterey + presidio-construction workers. Totals: 1 Tte, 1 guarda-almacén, 1 sargto, 6 cabos, 48 soldados. Genealogically central (parallels Doc 29). Orig. pp.534–689.
