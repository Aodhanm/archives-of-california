⭐⭐ Casta & literacy breakdown of the SF company, 1791: of 4 cabos, one a mestizo (illiterate), the other 3 Spaniards/whites who could write; of 28 soldados — 4 mulatos, 9 mestizos, 12 Spaniards/whites, 1 coyote, 2 Indians; only 2 could write. A rare casta/literacy snapshot of the presidial soldiery. Orig. p.127.
