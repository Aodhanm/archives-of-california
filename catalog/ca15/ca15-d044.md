# ⭐ Troop hardship. The troops got only ~1½ almud maíz + ¾ almud menestra, so most of the week they lived on "pura tortill

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n41** · orig. p. 720–721 · Sav p. 42 · 1777-01-22 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Troop hardship. The troops got only ~1½ almud maíz + ¾ almud menestra, so most of the week they lived on "pura tortilla"; Ortega authorizes ½ lb powder + 2 rounds per man to hunt the abundant ducks & geese (presidio + San Juan Capistrano & San Gabriel escorts). Vivid daily-life/subsistence detail. Orig. pp.720–721.
