# ⭐⭐ 1790 interior-Chumash ambush of a Santa Bárbara patrol. Sargto Olivera + 8 men sent (24 Aug) after a deserter Indian 

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n214–n216** · orig. p. 435–437 · Sav p. 209–211 · 1790-09-02 · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ 1790 interior-Chumash ambush of a Santa Bárbara patrol. Sargto Olivera + 8 men sent (24 Aug) after a deserter Indian to the ranchería de Tinoqui, also to prospect for metal veins (vetas de metales); joined by 9 Indians of Napalayagua (enemies of Tinoqui). After detaching men to gather ore, the camp (real) was attacked from the rear by 60+ Indians; the sergeant fought back wounding several but could not reach camp, found it in ruins; soldiers Gabriel Espinosa & Ignacio Carlón were killed (Carlón slain asleep; Espinosa made a vigorous defense), weapons (espadas, mangas, escopetas) seized. The survivors regrouped near San Buenaventura & returned to SB 1 Oct. Goycoechea requests mission reinforcements to punish the hostile Indians. Orig. pp.435–437.
