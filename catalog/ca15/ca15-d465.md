**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n316** · orig. p. 429–523 · Sav p. 310 · 1798-12-31 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Monterey company 1798: Ttes. Argüello & Sal; subtte. Raymundo Carrillo; cirujano; 2 sargtos (Macario Castro), 8 cabos, 1 drummer; soldiers varied 72–77 (76 at year-end) + invalids (Sargto Manuel Vargas, 5 cabos, 14 sold). Artillery: 5 mounted bronze 8-pdrs, 3 of 6, 2 of 3; a pedrero at the pueblo de San José & one at Mission San Luis; 1 unserviceable in the presidio. Catalan volunteers & San Blas troops also present. Orig. pp.429–523.
