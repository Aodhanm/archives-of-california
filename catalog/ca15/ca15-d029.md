# ⭐⭐ San Diego presidio garrison roster + missionaries, March 1775 (8 months before the revolt). Ministers: Fr. Vicente Fu

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n24–n28** · orig. p. ~344–424 · Sav p. 25–29 · 1775-03-14 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ San Diego presidio garrison roster + missionaries, March 1775 (8 months before the revolt). Ministers: Fr. Vicente Fuster (SD), Fr. Antonio Paterna (San Gabriel); cirujano José Dávila (left for Monterey Oct 1774). Comte. Tte. Ortega; guarda-almacén Rafael de Pedro y Gil; Sargto Mariano Carrillo; cabos Guillermo Carrillo, Mariano de la Luz Verdugo, Juan José Robles (San Gabriel escort), José Manuel de León (in mission SD); 31 soldados named (incl. Juan Esteban Rocha, Alférez Antonio Gonzáles, Francisco Mª Ruiz, José Raymundo Carrillo, Mariano Cordero, José Mª Ortega, Anastasio Camacho…) + inválidos (incl. Ignacio Vallejo, Ignacio Alvarado); carpintero, 2 herreros (Felipe Romero, José Manuel Arroyo — both later revolt casualties), arrieros. Pay (Tte $700, guarda-almacén $1000, sargto $450, cabo $400, soldado $365). Notes men transferred to Monterey early 1775 + Monterey-company men provisionally at SD in 1774 with date ranges; soldiers Fco. Cordero, Ignacio Olivera, Julián Murillo, Matías I. Soto resident in Baja Cal. Founding-generation SD garrison roster — genealogically central. Orig. pp.~344–424.
