# ⭐ Livestock bought for the Peninsula by Capt. Rivera y Moncada. Per a note signed by Rivera y Moncada: bought at Real de

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n114** · orig. p. 313–314 · Sav p. 113 · 1782-05-26 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Livestock bought for the Peninsula by Capt. Rivera y Moncada. Per a note signed by Rivera y Moncada: bought at Real de los Álamos for the Peninsula & received at Isla de Lobos 200 mules, 100 horses; died at sea 32 mules, 8 horses; received at Loreto 165 mules, 79 horses; distributed to presidio troops; died en route/on the frontier 3 mules, 16 horses; remitted to Nueva California 90 mules, 57 horses. (The remount herd of Rivera's fatal 1781 expedition.) Orig. pp.313–314.
