# SB supply invoice — víveres/efectos received on the frigate *Favorita* (Capt. José Tobar) from San Blas, total $2,474, w

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n205** · orig. p. 424 · Sav p. 200 · 1786-11-20 · unspecified · 

SB supply invoice — víveres/efectos received on the frigate *Favorita* (Capt. José Tobar) from San Blas, total $2,474, with itemized prices (maíz $4½/fanega, manteca $7½/arroba, arroz, panocha, azúcar $13/arroba, Mazatlán ankle-boots 3½ rls/pair, Aguacate saddles $13…; incl. 15% markup). Orig. p.424.
