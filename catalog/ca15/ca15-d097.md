# ⭐⭐⭐ Aftermath of the July 1781 Yuma (Colorado River) Revolt — Cayetano Limón's expedition. Fragmentary, hard hand: Alfér

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n77** · orig. p. 115–116 · Sav p. 76 · 1781-10 · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ Aftermath of the July 1781 Yuma (Colorado River) Revolt — Cayetano Limón's expedition. Fragmentary, hard hand: Alférez Cayetano Limón, on an *expediente*, "encontró la Concepción en ruinas, puesta la plaza de gente muerta á palos y quemada" (found [Misión Purísima] Concepción in ruins, the place full of people clubbed to death & burned); was attacked by the Indians; mentions "su hijo el soldado Cayetano Limón." ⚠️ *I'm inferring* this concerns the July 1781 Yuma Revolt (destruction of the Colorado missions & killing of Rivera y Moncada + the colonists) and Limón's relief/ransom expedition — to verify against the fuller text. Orig. pp.115–116.
