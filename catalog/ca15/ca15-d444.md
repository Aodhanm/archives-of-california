# ⭐⭐ Hoja de servicios of Capt. José Joaquín de Arrillaga (future Governor of California): age 41, native of Aya, Guipúzco

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n303** · orig. p. 537 · Sav p. 297 · 1791-12-31 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Hoja de servicios of Capt. José Joaquín de Arrillaga (future Governor of California): age 41, native of Aya, Guipúzcoa, of noble birth. Volunteer from 25 May 1777, alférez 1778, teniente 1780, captain 12 Jun 1783; served in the cuera company of San Miguel de Horcasitas, then San Sabás & La Bahía, then captain of the Loreto presidio; 3 campaigns under Col. Juan Bautista de Anza + 2 actions vs. the Seris & Pimas. Orig. p.537.
