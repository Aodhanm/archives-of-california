# Criminal causa — aiding a deserter's escape. Declarations vs. soldado Manuel Antonio de Robles, accused of letting the S

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n15** · orig. p. 224–233 · Sav p. 16 · 1779-03-27 · unspecified · 

Criminal causa — aiding a deserter's escape. Declarations vs. soldado Manuel Antonio de Robles, accused of letting the San Francisco deserter Felipe Ochoa (a prisoner, who had taken church asylum) escape. Cabo Vicente Briones commanded the San Luis escort; Sargto Mariano Carrillo acted as escribano (26–27 Mar). Witnesses: Guillermo Carrillo (cabo de plaza, who handed Ochoa over at San Luis), Ignacio de Castro, José Mª Verdugo, José Antonio Peña, & Robles. No collusion by Robles found in Ochoa's flight. Orig. pp.224–233.
