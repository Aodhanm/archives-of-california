⭐ Livestock driven from Baja missions to Alta California, per Visitor-General José de Gálvez's reglamento & viceregal order: stock from Misiones San José de Comondú, Guadalupe, Purísima, Sta Gertrudis, San Borja, San Fernando, San Vicente… for Monterey — 290 head total (mares $4, horses/colts $9, pack/saddle mules $20, cargo-machos $19, cows, bulls, goats, sheep…); plus 11½ mares, 1 stud, burros the King held at Loreto; total value of the missions' contributions $1,238.
