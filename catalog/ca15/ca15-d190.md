**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n160** · orig. p. 494–495 · Sav p. 157 · 1794-01-30 · unspecified · 

Ajuste of the Monterey company for 1782: Tte. Diego Gonzáles $550; 1 sargto $262.4; 5 cabos $1,125; 45 soldados $9,787.4; gratificación $460; $12,185; abonos to Alférez Mariano Carrillo $27.4½, Hermenegildo Sal $133.5, cirujano José Dávila $161.4, & various soldiers ($560.14); $12,745; less 1 soldier's unserved days $63; + 1 carpenter & 2 smiths $540; $13,222; the company ended owing $2,857.
