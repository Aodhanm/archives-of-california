# ⭐⭐ Service record (hoja de servicio) of Sargento Mariano de la Luz Verdugo, age 42. Enlisted as a soldier 15 Dec 1766; c

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n200–n201** · orig. p. 335 · Sav p. 195–196 · 1786-12-31 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Service record (hoja de servicio) of Sargento Mariano de la Luz Verdugo, age 42. Enlisted as a soldier 15 Dec 1766; cabo 1 Dec 1774; sargento 5 May 1781; served at Loreto (7+ yrs), San Diego (7 yrs), Monterey. Campaigns: the 1769 party under Capt. Fernando de Rivera y Moncada that intercepted gentiles at the paraje de la Ciénega (8 killed); the suppression of the 1775 San Diego revolt (sierra sweeps to seize the ringleaders "de la sublevación de cristianos y gentiles que destruyeron la misión de San Diego en 1775"); and the 1782 Río Colorado expedition under Cmdt.-Inspector Felipe de Neve (post-Yuma campaign). Orig. p.335.
