# ⭐ On masses for the souls of dead soldiers. Encloses an auditor de guerra's ruling on the missionaries' refusal to say s

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n213** · orig. p. 248–255 · Sav p. 208 · 1787-12-01 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ On masses for the souls of dead soldiers. Encloses an auditor de guerra's ruling on the missionaries' refusal to say sufragio masses at the prices set by Gov. Neve. Directs that for soldiers dying intestate without known heirs, their alcances & small pay be applied to masses for their souls: ¼ to the presidio chaplain ($1/mass), ¾ said by sacristans & religious accepting the most moderate alms, preferring former presidio chaplains. Orig. pp.248–255.
