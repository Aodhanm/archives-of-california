# ⭐ Detailed livestock census of the Monterey presidio herds, 1782. *Horses/mules:* 274 mares/colts existing end-1781 + 52

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n127–n129** · orig. p. 630–636 · Sav p. 125–127 · 1782-12-31 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Detailed livestock census of the Monterey presidio herds, 1782. *Horses/mules:* 274 mares/colts existing end-1781 + 52 colts + 32 fillies + 1 mule = 357; distributed to troops, comisos, deaths (98, $221); 261 remaining. *Cattle:* 938 existing end-1781 + 373 calves (182 male/191 female) = 1,311; distributed/sold (vacas, toros, novillos), matanza (10 arr. manteca); 1,129 remaining. *Sheep (lana):* 336, all sold/dead, none left. *Goats (pelo):* 115, all sold. *Pigs (cerda):* 18 → 12 left. Resumen: caballar 261, vacuno 1,129, cerda 12. Sale proceeds $1,099.7; less herders' pay & costs $120.6; net to Real Hacienda $979.1. Orig. pp.630–636.
