# ⭐⭐ Santa Bárbara presidial company distribution & defense, 1 Dec 1782. Postings: presidio guard 1 sargto/1 cabo/6 sold =

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n123** · orig. p. 561 · Sav p. 121 · 1782-12-01 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Santa Bárbara presidial company distribution & defense, 1 Dec 1782. Postings: presidio guard 1 sargto/1 cabo/6 sold = 8; caballada 1+6 = 7; Mission San Buenaventura 1 sargto + 14 = 15; pueblo de Los Ángeles 2; "francos para el servicio" officers + sargtos. Total 56 hombres (1 Tte, 1 Alférez, 2 sargtos, 2 cabos, 50 sold); short 1 sargto & 4 soldados. Caballada 89 horses, 144 mules; arms reserve 13 pairs pistols (short 2 escopetas, 36 espadas, 26 cueras for full company). Presidio guarded by 2 cannon (4-pounders) with 140 ball cartridges; munitions reserve 5,000 musket cartridges, 18 arr. powder, 127 arr. lead, 10 arr. balls. Orig. p.561.
