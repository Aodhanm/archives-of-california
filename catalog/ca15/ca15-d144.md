# ⭐ Detailed uniform, armament & mount regulation for the Baja California presidial troop & officers. *Troop:* chupa corta

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n118–n119** · orig. p. 141–145 · Sav p. 116–117 · 1781 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Detailed uniform, armament & mount regulation for the Baja California presidial troop & officers. *Troop:* chupa corta de tripe/paño with red cuff & collar, gilt button, blue tripe breeches, matching cape, cartuchera, cuera, bandolera embroidered with the presidio's name, corbatín, black hat, shoes, buckskin boots. *Divisas (rank lace):* sargento = galón of 14 threads on the cuffs; cabo = half-galón. *Armamento:* espada ancha, lanza, escopeta, pistolas, adarga. *Montura:* silla vaquera with mochila, coraza, anqueras, cojinillos, wooden stirrups, freno, espuelas, bozal. *Officers (dress):* blue casaca & breeches, chupín, red collar double-laced (5-thread galón), 3-cornered hat with gold lace; *(campaign):* short chupa, blue cape & breeches, round white hat. Orig. pp.141–145.
