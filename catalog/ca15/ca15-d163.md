# ⭐⭐ Padrón of neophyte Indians at Mission San Carlos, by Serra. Adult males (>18) by ranchería: San Carlos 23 married/1 w

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n130** · orig. p. 648–653 · Sav p. 128 · 1781-12-22 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Padrón of neophyte Indians at Mission San Carlos, by Serra. Adult males (>18) by ranchería: San Carlos 23 married/1 widower; San José 14; Santa Teresa 21; San Miguel 15 (1 widower, 1 single); San Francisco 18; total 96. Plus long-time apostates "de la misión y de la religión cristiana" whose recovery had required troops (4 @ $50). The 96 neophytes each contributed to the donativo gracioso for the war = $96; settler Ignacio Vallejo gave $10. 659 Indians baptized since the mission's founding; 243 dead; 109 married/widowed, the rest women & children. Orig. pp.648–653.
