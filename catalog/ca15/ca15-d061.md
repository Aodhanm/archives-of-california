⭐⭐ Los Ángeles founders (pobladores) — supply list. The settlers Rivera y Moncada recruited in Sonora & brought to Calif.: José Lara, Antonio Navarro, Basilio Rosas, Antonio Mesa, Antonio Villavicencio, José Vanegas, Alejandro Rosas, Pablo Rodríguez, Luis Quintero, Manuel Camero, José Moreno, Antonio Miranda Rodríguez, Miguel Villa (deserter) — among the 11 founding families of El Pueblo de Nuestra Señora la Reina de los Ángeles (founded 4 Sep 1781); supplied $2,546. Orig. p.9.
