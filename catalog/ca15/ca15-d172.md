# ⭐⭐ Troop uniform, armament & mount regulation (companion to Doc 169). Troop dress per the Reglamento: short blue chupa &

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n145–n146** · orig. p. 49–52 · Sav p. 143–144 · 1780-09-21 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Troop uniform, armament & mount regulation (companion to Doc 169). Troop dress per the Reglamento: short blue chupa & breeches, red cuff/lapel/collar, gilt button, gamuza chupa, black short-brimmed hat looped with a presilla on the left side "para manejar el fusil con desembarazo," red wool cap, blue cape (sarape/manga permitted); cuera-troops vs. light-troops distinguished by the white hat; silla vaquera, espuelas vaqueras. Expressly forbids the large iron stirrups (estriberas grandes de hierro) under penalty: officers suspension of office, sargentos loss of seniority, soldiers 4 years' presidio at ration without pay "con un grillete al pie" (shackled). Orig. pp.49–52.
