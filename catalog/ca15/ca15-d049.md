# ⭐ Demand for Pedro Fages's overdue accounts. Orders Neve to make Capt. Pedro Fages present the Monterey víveres/géneros 

**C-A 15 (Prov. St. Pap. Benicia Mil.), scan n46–n47** · orig. p. 843–850 · Sav p. 47–48 · 1778-02-17 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Demand for Pedro Fages's overdue accounts. Orders Neve to make Capt. Pedro Fages present the Monterey víveres/géneros accounts he held 11 Sep 1768 – 18 Jul 1774, with a fine for not rendering them despite repeated demands. Neve (rec'd 14 Oct 1778) ordered Tte. de Gobernador Rivera y Moncada at Loreto to make comisario Álvarez Osorio (who held Fages's books) formalize the SD & Monterey troop/artisan ajustes. Orig. pp.843–850.
