# 'tendencias revolucionarias' — ~200 Indians of different nations gathering to raid Mission SLR's rancho de San Jacinto; 

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n192–194** · Sav p. 193–195 · tomo p. XII·408–411 · 1835-02-14 · Savage abstract · 

'tendencias revolucionarias' — ~200 Indians of different nations gathering to raid Mission SLR's rancho de San Jacinto; ⭐ agitators ('enemigos del orden') trying to recruit the mission neophytes to revolt (promising 'una vida de muchas comodidades'); loyalist Indian captains Julio, Cristóbal de San Felipe & José María keep the rancherías (Lagunas → Valle de San José) faithful; Portilla shorted by Santiago Argüello (asked 12 men, sent 3 — 1 sick, 1 unarmed)
