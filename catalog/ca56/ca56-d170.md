# the formal sedition process against 8 named SF presidial soldiers (Juan & Jesús Berreyesa, Domingo & Manuel Sáez, José R

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n165–168** · Sav p. 166–169 · tomo p. XI·(388–603) · 1833-10–12 · Savage abstract · 

the formal sedition process against 8 named SF presidial soldiers (Juan & Jesús Berreyesa, Domingo & Manuel Sáez, José Romero, Manuel Villa, Blas Alviso, José Antonio Galindo) for the 25–26 Oct tumult — grievances: kept 'desnudos enteramente,' underfed, over-punished; fiscal admits 'es bien notorio el estado de miseria… el erario nacional está muy escaso'; ⭐⭐ the War Council ACQUITS all 8 ('plena libertad') & merely redistributes them across the presidios (Figueroa, 5 Dec 1833)
