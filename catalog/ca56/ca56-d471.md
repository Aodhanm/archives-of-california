# orders published (with rebuttal) the Pope's [Leo XII] encyclical to the Archbishops & Bishops of America — which, 'duped

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n415–416** · Sav p. 416–417 · tomo p. XIX·526–528 · 1825-02-06 · Savage abstract · 

orders published (with rebuttal) the Pope's [Leo XII] encyclical to the Archbishops & Bishops of America — which, 'duped by reports of the Spanish Government,' painted the independent American nations in false colors & urged submission to 'el tirano Fernando 7º'; the Mexican govt, per the Council of Government, counters it with the force of truth; the encyclical not to be printed except with the accompanying rebuttal
