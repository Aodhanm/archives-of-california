# '1838 Enero 5, San José de Guadᵖᵉ, Antᵒ Mª Pico, alcalde constⁱ — Bando de policía. De acuerdo con el ayuntamiento que p

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n137–143** · Sav p. 138–144 · tomo p. XI·(to ~315) · 1838-01-05 · Savage abstract · 

'1838 Enero 5, San José de Guadᵖᵉ, Antᵒ Mª Pico, alcalde constⁱ — Bando de policía. De acuerdo con el ayuntamiento que presido, acordé el sigᵗᵉ bando' (provisionally approved by the Gefe Político at Monterey, 16 Jan). arts. 1–3 confirmed: stray-animal roundup (art.1), twice-weekly street-cleaning + refuse-burning (art.2, 4 rs), ⭐ art.3 the absolute ban on the customary 'prender fuego á los montes… bajo la pena de doce pesos por primera vez.' *(arts. 4–21 on n138–143, not re-read.)* the 21-article municipal ordinance — stray-animal roundup, weekly street-cleaning, ⭐ ban on burning the hills/brush (12-peso fine), fandango-dance permits, 9 p.m. curfew, ban on edged weapons in town, rancho-trespass = *abigeato*, branding only on set days, livestock protection, no liquor before Mass, gambling ban, cattle/hide-trade control, carcass disposal 300 varas out, communal-labor obligation, no night wood-cutting, no refusing to aid officials, no scandalous assembly, no firing guns in town, well/pit safety, no damaging bridges/acequias
