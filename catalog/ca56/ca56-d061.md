# reports the ship-of-the-line *Asia* & brig *Constante* anchored at Monterey with ~700 Spaniards (defeated in México); th

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n32–33** · Sav p. 33–34 · tomo p. 404 · 1825-06-27 · Savage abstract · 

reports the ship-of-the-line *Asia* & brig *Constante* anchored at Monterey with ~700 Spaniards (defeated in México); though Monterey's governor accepts their capitulation, Echeandía 'no puedo persuadirme de su buena fe' and warns Sonora to guard against them
