# ✓ VERIFIED on scan n327, 2026-07-05 verify pass. One of a run of *borradores* on the leaf (also Ago 25 = Arrillaga's Lor

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n327–328** · Sav p. 328–329 · tomo p. XVII·25–26 · 1795-09-02 · Savage abstract · 

✓ VERIFIED on scan n327, 2026-07-05 verify pass. One of a run of *borradores* on the leaf (also Ago 25 = Arrillaga's Loreto-captaincy despatch; Ago 29 = the despatches of teniente & alférez for Hermᵈᵒ Sal & Raymundo Carrillo — a [[sal-papers]] corroboration of Sal's 1795 teniente promotion, received 29 Aug). The Sept 2 entry: the English ship *'Fénix' Capitán 'Mor' (sic)* [Savage's own '(sic)'; the row's earlier 'Moore[?]' was an interpretation] arrived Sta Bárbara 28 Aug; Goycoechea 'le franqueó agua, leña y algunos víveres'; Borica approved but, once resupplied, ordered it politely required to sail — 'á fin de no faltar al pacto hecho con la Corte de Londres en el año de 90' (✓ quote verbatim; the 1790 Nootka Convention)
