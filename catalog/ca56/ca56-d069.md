# reports the 1828 pay-mutiny: the Monterey mission-escolta soldiers abandoned their posts, joined the presidio garrison, 

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n36–38** · Sav p. 37–39 · tomo p. 414–419 · 1828-10-20 · Savage abstract · 

reports the 1828 pay-mutiny: the Monterey mission-escolta soldiers abandoned their posts, joined the presidio garrison, and 'se han presentado en pelotón con sus armas pidiendo que se les pague sus sueldos… de lo contrario no harán servicio'; blames possible agitation 'por algún comisionado de Sonora ó Misioneros'; the artillery reinforcement on the National brig *Ester* (22 Oct) to quell it
