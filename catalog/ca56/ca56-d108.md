# Pomponio-pursuit narrative — the SF mission escolta learns from a captive India that the outlaw Pomponio & 2 companions 

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n98–99** · Sav p. 99–100 · tomo p. (from p.621) · 1823-07-03 · Savage abstract · 

Pomponio-pursuit narrative — the SF mission escolta learns from a captive India that the outlaw Pomponio & 2 companions raided from the Sierra; soldiers recognize Pomponio, fire on him as he flees into the zacatal, surround it but can't seize him (nightfall), recover 4 stolen horses; a party under cabo Reyes finds Pomponio's 'madriguera' in the Sierra (2 lances, a machete made from a barrel-hoop, 2 knives, a hammer & chisel); Pomponio ambushes & disarms Reyes's son; cabo Reyes returns with 8 bow-armed Indian auxiliaries from Mission Santa Clara but can't catch him (pp.621–628)
