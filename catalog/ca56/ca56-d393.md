# the 10-article electoral instructions for provincial Deputies per the Soberana Junta Provisional: mission Alcaldes & Reg

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n341–344** · Sav p. 342–345 · tomo p. XVIII·11–18 · 1822-04-17 · Savage abstract · 

the 10-article electoral instructions for provincial Deputies per the Soberana Junta Provisional: mission Alcaldes & Regidores, with the padres' counsel, each name a district elector 'á ejemplo de los ayuntamientos que no hay' (missions standing in for absent ayuntamientos); San José & LA name 1 elector each; illiterate signers mark a cross, mission credentials authorized by the Padres; 5 electoral districts (cabeceras: San Francisco / Monterey / Santa Bárbara / Los Ángeles / San Diego); electors of Province meet at the presidio 15–20 [May]; electors must be pro-Independence, not on active military or religious duty (but alternates may be military); procedure otherwise per the Spanish Constitution
