# circular: the President orders strict instructions to every authority of the territory that, wherever 'Dⁿ Lorenzo Zavala

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n316** · Sav p. 317 · tomo p. XVI·521–523 · 1830-10-07 ✎ inferred *(MS '1823')* · Savage abstract · 

circular: the President orders strict instructions to every authority of the territory that, wherever 'Dⁿ Lorenzo Zavala, Gobⁿ qᵉ era del estado de Mégico' is found, he be arrested immediately and brought under secure guard to the capital (verbatim, n316). ✎ DATE CORRECTED '1823'→1830 (2026-07-06 verify pass): the manuscript header reads '1823 Obre 7,' but three facts make 1823 impossible — the Estado de México was created in 1824; Zavala governed it 1827–29; and his persecution (with J. de Dios Cañedo as Minister of Relaciones) came under the Bustamante regime in 1830.
