# warns about the surrendered Spanish warships Asia & Constante anchored at Monterey ('gente mala… la Canalla vil'), fears

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n10–11** · Sav p. 11–12 · tomo p. 236–237 · 1825-05-12 · Savage abstract · 

warns about the surrendered Spanish warships Asia & Constante anchored at Monterey ('gente mala… la Canalla vil'), fears harm to the province & disruption of the merchant ships; recommends dispatching the American frigate *Tártaro* (Capt. Morel) at SF to carry the news to Acapulco/the Mexican govt for $1,500
