# 8-doc letterbook — American schooner *Spy* (*Espía*) + frigate *Sachem* (fr. Monterey) + schooner *Tártaro*; ⭐⭐ asks per

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n258–260** · Sav p. 259–261 · tomo p. XIV·668–682 · 1825-04-22 · Savage abstract · 

8-doc letterbook — American schooner *Spy* (*Espía*) + frigate *Sachem* (fr. Monterey) + schooner *Tártaro*; ⭐⭐ asks permission to take provisions BY FORCE from the missions (the friars won't supply the escolta); ⭐ acks & will publish the 1824 Federal Constitution; ⭐ publishes the order suspending the Diputación Provincial; ⭐ requests return of the light 3-pdr cannon sent against the La Purísima [Chumash] rebels; Fr. Narciso Durán made *vicario foráneo*; collect copies of the Spanish Constitution
