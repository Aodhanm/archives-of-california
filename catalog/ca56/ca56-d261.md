# at a Dominican Baja mission ('San Regis'[?] / S. Fco de Borja), neophytes apprehended 5 deserters from Alta California; 

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n233–234** · Sav p. 234–235 · tomo p. XIV·96–97 · 1829-11-06 · Savage abstract · 

at a Dominican Baja mission ('San Regis'[?] / S. Fco de Borja), neophytes apprehended 5 deserters from Alta California; the resisting one — an Englishman — drew a *beldoque* (dagger) & stabbed the neophyte Ricardo three times; another Indian seized a musket and shot the Englishman dead; the other 4 deserters held awaiting resolution
