# long report (against building roadside inns/missions) w/ a province-wide census of both Californias: Alta Cal. (SD→SF, >

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n329–332** · Sav p. 330–333 · tomo p. XVII·88–91 · 1795-03-12 · verbatim transcription · 

long report (against building roadside inns/missions) w/ a province-wide census of both Californias: Alta Cal. (SD→SF, >200 leagues) = 4 presidios + 2 pueblos, 1,275 gente de razón (SD 231, SB 253, Monterey 255, SF 153, LA 186, San José 182) + 907 military-family = only 368 vecinos; Antigua/Baja Cal. = 1,032 souls (Loreto 481 + Real de Sta Ana 551, per the 1794 padrón); settlers too poor to use inns (travel on tortillas & roast meat)
