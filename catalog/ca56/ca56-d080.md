# force composition exact — Pacheco, art. under José Fernández del Campo, 30 Mazatlán, 30 SB-Permanente under Rodrigo del 

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n56–57** · Sav p. 57–58 · tomo p. (from p.471) · 1830-01-26 · Savage abstract · 

force composition exact — Pacheco, art. under José Fernández del Campo, 30 Mazatlán, 30 SB-Permanente under Rodrigo del Pliego, ~20 SD-Fija under José Ramírez. the Solís-Revolt suppression narrative — the 'plan de Solís' uprising at the SB presidio (after Monterey's), restored in 2 days; Echeandía marches from SD (18 Dec) with a force under tte Romualdo Pacheco (artillery under alférez José Fernández del Campo; 30 of the Mazatlán Squadron; 30 of the SB Permanent Co. under Rodrigo del Pliego; 20 of the SD Fixed Co. under José Ramírez; + ~100 more); camps near the oldest mission, exchanges letters with 'el faccioso Fr. J. [=José Mª] Solís,' confronts ~100 rebels near Santa Inés (13 leguas); ⭐⭐ the rebels flee on the 15th at 11 o'clock, abandon their cannon, and 26 surrender under the *indulto*; Monterey restored; Echeandía warns the territory could fall to 'los Borbonistas y Criminales' or be seized by 'los Españoles… los aventureros Anglo-Americanos ó los Rusos de la Bodega'
