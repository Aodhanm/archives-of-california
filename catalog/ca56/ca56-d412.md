# has suspended his departure for the southern presidios owing to the imminent arrival of Canónigo [Agustín] Fernández [de

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n358** · Sav p. 359 · tomo p. XVIII·49–50 · 1822-08-08 · Savage abstract · 

has suspended his departure for the southern presidios owing to the imminent arrival of Canónigo [Agustín] Fernández [de San Vicente]; sends the printed oficio of José de Herrera (Minister of Relations of the Empire) inserting the Junta Soberana's call for all inhabitants to present plans for framing the Constitution ('ayude con sus luces á esta grande obra') + the Soberano Decreto of 1 Mar on installing the Congreso Constituyente
