# [2nd copy of Doc 97, in Spanish] Wrangel rebuts Capt. Beechey's published charge (in his *Blossom* voyage, 1826–27) that

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n402–406** · Sav p. 403–407 · tomo p. XIX·114–120 · 1833-07-24 · Savage abstract · 

[2nd copy of Doc 97, in Spanish] Wrangel rebuts Capt. Beechey's published charge (in his *Blossom* voyage, 1826–27) that the Russians poach otter off California as 'sin sombra de verdad'; blames other (Anglo-American) foreigners for exterminating the sea-mammals of the Channel Islands & Farallones; welcomes friendly correspondence. ⭐ Savage notes the Spanish text is a translation of a RUSSIAN-language original bound at tomo pp. 117–120 (a rare Cyrillic doc). Julian date 12 Jul / Gregorian 24 Jul
