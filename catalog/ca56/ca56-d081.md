# reports the capture of Joaquín [José María] Solís and his 7 companions (Monterey & SB restored); the investigation confi

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n61–63** · Sav p. 62–64 · tomo p. 483–485 · 1830-02-23 · Savage abstract · 

reports the capture of Joaquín [José María] Solís and his 7 companions (Monterey & SB restored); the investigation confirms Solís & others 'se pronunciaron por aquel Gobierno [Español]' — the revolt's pro-Spanish character; the friars persuade the people 'que la República Mexicana se halla impotente'; begs for 200 men at the Altar presidio + a ship to remove the convicts
