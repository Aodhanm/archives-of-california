# verbatim English letter (translated from the Russian by Ivan Petroff) rebutting Capt. Beechey's (HMS *Blossom*, 1826–27)

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n83–87** · Sav p. 84–88 · tomo p. 506–509 · 1833-07-24 · verbatim transcription · 

verbatim English letter (translated from the Russian by Ivan Petroff) rebutting Capt. Beechey's (HMS *Blossom*, 1826–27) accusations that the Russians seized islands off Santa Bárbara & poached sea-otter depriving the natives — Wrangel calls it 'untrue and fabricated,' insists the RAC obeyed Mexico's otter-hunting prohibition, and pins the poaching on 'other foreigners' (Anglo-Americans) using ~40 canoes of 'our northern savages' (Aleut hunters) who 'exterminated… on those islands and on the Farallones all the amphibious animals'; seeks to restore Russo-Californian friendship
