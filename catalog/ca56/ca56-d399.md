# church-state clash — Fr. Vicente de Sarría preached at Mass that, Independence having been sworn, the troops no longer e

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n348–349** · Sav p. 349–350 · tomo p. XVIII·22–23 · 1822-05-20 · Savage abstract · 

church-state clash — Fr. Vicente de Sarría preached at Mass that, Independence having been sworn, the troops no longer enjoyed the papal meat-eating dispensation once granted the 'Rey Católico'; the Gov., finding it of grave import, ordered Sarría to abstain from saying Mass & preaching on feast days until the Regency decides whether Apostolic-See rulings on the military pass to the provincial governors
