# Figueroa's reply — affirms satisfaction with the Russians' 'honored and delicate conduct' & rebuts Beechey's otter charg

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n407–410** · Sav p. 408–411 · tomo p. XIX·121–122 · 1833-12-23 · Savage abstract · 

Figueroa's reply — affirms satisfaction with the Russians' 'honored and delicate conduct' & rebuts Beechey's otter charges as false; blames Anglo-American 'adventurers' for violating the law of nations & threatens indemnification; ⭐ questions the right by which the Ross establishment is founded, invoking the 1819 Adams-Onís boundary & Mexican prior possession, while deferring the decision to government-level negotiation
