# communication on the occurrences in the department & Gen. Micheltorena's departure — the national govt acks the 'movimie

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n314–316** · Sav p. 315–317 · tomo p. XVI·(from) · 1845-05-29 · verbatim transcription · 

communication on the occurrences in the department & Gen. Micheltorena's departure — the national govt acks the 'movimiento nacional del 6 Dic. 1844' (the anti-Micheltorena revolt), names José María Híjar commissioner to CA, forwards the presidential-election matter to the Asamblea, & plans a new military division ('no oprimir al ciudadano… sino proteger… y defender el país de cualquiera agresión')
