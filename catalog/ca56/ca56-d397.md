# relays Lt. José Manuel Ruiz (frontier cdr.)'s report of Lord Cochrane's insurgent squadron ravaging Baja California (Feb

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n346–349** · Sav p. 347–350 · tomo p. XVIII·20–21 · 1822-05-04 · Savage abstract · 

relays Lt. José Manuel Ruiz (frontier cdr.)'s report of Lord Cochrane's insurgent squadron ravaging Baja California (Feb–Mar 1822): the corvette *La Independencia* + a brig sacked Loreto, San José del Cabo & Real de San Antonio — pillage, violence against women, seizing a newly-launched schooner, desecrating the church (breaking the *custodia*, spilling the holy oils), capturing the ex-San Blas commandant & the Pitic treasury agent, entrenching with 3 cannon; eyewitness José S. de Leoba; Argüello ill, soldiers scattered/terrified; the Gov. warns the Padres to hide all gold, silver & valuables near the beach
