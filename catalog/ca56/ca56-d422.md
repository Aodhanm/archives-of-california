# founding report of Mission San Fernando Rey de España — on 8 Sep 1797 (Nativity of Mary) at the native site 'Achois Comi

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n363–364** · Sav p. 364–365 · tomo p. 389–390 · 1797-09-08 · Savage abstract · 

founding report of Mission San Fernando Rey de España — on 8 Sep 1797 (Nativity of Mary) at the native site 'Achois Comihavit' between Missions San Buenaventura & San Gabriel, with Fr. Francisco Dumetz assisting & the assigned garrison; many gentiles present; blessed water/place/cross, sang the Litany/Mass/Te Deum; gentiles offered 5 children of each sex for baptism; dedicated to San Fernando, King of Spain (per Viceroy Branciforte; copy by Borica)
