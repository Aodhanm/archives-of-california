# character-census of the resident Spaniards petitioning for exemption — each w/ age, years in CA, family, property, condu

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n49–51** · Sav p. 50–52 · tomo p. 466–... · 1829-11-03 · Savage abstract · 

character-census of the resident Spaniards petitioning for exemption — each w/ age, years in CA, family, property, conduct, loyalty oath: Manuel Gutiérrez (LA capitalist, 82, 20 yrs); a 2nd Manuel Gutiérrez (LA farmer, 45, huerta of 3,000 vines); Manuel Fabregat (ex-Mazatlán officer, >60, swore independence 1822); Salvador García (San José, ex-crew of the *Asia*); Antonio Suñol (San José, 35); José Joaquín de la Torre (Monterey, 44, 8 children); José Amesti (Monterey merchant, 36); Esteban Munras (Monterey, 39); + Manuel Jacinto Roqué & Luis Castro (ex-marines of the *Asia*/brig *Aquiles* who deserted at SB), José Joaquín Fernández (SF), Francisco García (SB invalid, 70) — all 'casados con hija del país' & 'jurado la Independencia' (~15 named; ends p.470)
