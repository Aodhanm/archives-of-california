# 'venida de un General Guamariocpa' — 8 Yuma Indians at Mission San Gabriel, their 'General' Guamariocpa bearing passport

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n379–380** · Sav p. 380–381 · tomo p. 473–475 · 1821-03-08 · Savage abstract · 

'venida de un General Guamariocpa' — 8 Yuma Indians at Mission San Gabriel, their 'General' Guamariocpa bearing passports from the Juez de Tubac recommending them as friends, come to trade; the Gov. wants to regulate their entry lest 'otro desastre semejante al de San Buenaventura' (1819) recur; instructs Gabriel Moraga to reconnoiter the Mojave rancherías (possibly w/ Lt. Antonio del Valle); Fr. Luis Martínez urges reinforcing San Miguel against a surprise from the tulares
