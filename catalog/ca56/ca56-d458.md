# Durán's canonical opinion (as Vicario Forense) on the duel between cabo Camilo Berenda[?] & Melitón Sota[?] near Montere

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n396–400** · Sav p. 397–401 · tomo p. XIX·44–52 · 1825-04-15 · Savage abstract · 

Durán's canonical opinion (as Vicario Forense) on the duel between cabo Camilo Berenda[?] & Melitón Sota[?] near Monterey Presidio — cites the Council of Trent (Session 25) condemning duels as 'detestable, introduced by the Devil'; rules it a 'simple and private' duel, both duelists incurring excommunication ipso facto (papally reserved), needing absolution + public satisfaction for the scandal
