# full acta of the Junta de Guerra y Real Hacienda on the campaign against the Yumas of the Colorado River (aftermath of t

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n273–278** · Sav p. 274–279 · tomo p. XV·78–84 · 1781-11-15 · Savage abstract · 

full acta of the Junta de Guerra y Real Hacienda on the campaign against the Yumas of the Colorado River (aftermath of the 1781 Yuma Revolt) — Croix presiding + Pedro Corbalán (intendant/gov. of Sonora) + auditor Pedro Galindo Navarro; reads Neve's & Fages's letters (Fages had freed the Yuma captives, sent them to the Presidio del Altar); 9 numbered resolutions — reward the friendly-nation auxiliary Indians; the Gov. of Cal. to reconnoiter the Colorado for a new establishment site securing free communication; report the presidial force & settler numbers needed; aid the widows/children/relatives of the killed Río-Colorado settlers (rations, resettlement); + Croix's confirmation ordering the Presidio de Sta Gertrudis (Andrés Arias Caballero) to disburse the *situado* funds
