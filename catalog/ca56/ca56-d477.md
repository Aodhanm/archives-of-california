# arrival of the first American overland party — an 'Inglés ó Americano' [Jedediah Smith] with 14 men of his nation reache

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n429–430** · Sav p. 430–431 · tomo p. XIX·659–663 · 1826-12-30 · Savage abstract · 

arrival of the first American overland party — an 'Inglés ó Americano' [Jedediah Smith] with 14 men of his nation reached Mission San Gabriel from the Great Salt Lake (Lago Salado), guided through the Mojave (Amajava) rancherías by runaway neophytes, carrying 40 beaver pelts & many traps; Smith presented his diary & itinerary; Echeandía finds 'no bad faith,' judging him culpable only for unauthorized entry through the desert, & holds him pending a reply
