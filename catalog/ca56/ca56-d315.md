# Serra's clemency letter after the 1775 SD Mission revolt — pleads against punishing the Indians ('sobre que se imponga [

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n282–283** · Sav p. 283–284 · tomo p. XV·153–154 · 1776-10-05 · verbatim transcription · 

Serra's clemency letter after the 1775 SD Mission revolt — pleads against punishing the Indians ('sobre que se imponga [poco] castigo á indios'): the murdered Fr. Jayme is already 'gozando de Dios,' and the guilty who remain alive should be cared for 'como las niñas de los ojos de Dios' & forgiven, showing them the law of 'volver bien por mal' → see Entry 3
