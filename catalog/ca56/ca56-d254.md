# SF letterbook extract, items 5–17 (cont. of Doc 253) — ⭐⭐ the Russian-Aleut otter hunt from Fort Ross: the *cayucos* (ba

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n226–230** · Sav p. 227–231 · tomo p. XIV·14–48 · 1824–1825 · Savage abstract · 

SF letterbook extract, items 5–17 (cont. of Doc 253) — ⭐⭐ the Russian-Aleut otter hunt from Fort Ross: the *cayucos* (baidarkas) hunting under a 14-month contract into San Pablo Bay (up to 22/day, ~66 taken) — Martínez left 'ni un solo pelo de nutria'; a later hunt found not one otter (depletion), returning to Bodega; ⭐⭐ the Russian war-frigates *Apolo* & *Ladoga* + brig *Rurik* sail for St. Petersburg; duties on the Russian brig *Kníaz*[?]; ⭐ SF soldiers mutiny over wages; ⭐⭐ the battle at Mission La Purísima vs. the 1824 Chumash rebels (division cmdr Mariano Estrada); the Sonoma escolta (Antonio Soto); cabo José Rodríguez court-martial; English merchantman *Tomasa Ann* (Lima)
