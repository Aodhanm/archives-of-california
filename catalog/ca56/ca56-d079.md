# first report on the 1829 Solís Revolt ('la pequeña revolución que se ha formado en este Territorio') — blames Sub-Comisa

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n53–55** · Sav p. 54–56 · tomo p. 470–475 · 1829-11-27 · Savage abstract · 

first report on the 1829 Solís Revolt ('la pequeña revolución que se ha formado en este Territorio') — blames Sub-Comisario José María Herrera for inciting 'el desterrado Joaquín [=José María] Solís' and other deported criminals (to escape his own fraud liability); wonders if it favors Spain or 'aventureros Norte-Americanos'; ties it to the pro-Fernando-VII missionaries, the mission-síndicos' unrest, and the total lack of *situados*; forms a *junta de guerra*, marches to SB, begs 30 men from Sonora across the Colorado
