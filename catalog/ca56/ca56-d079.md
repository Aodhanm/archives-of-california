# ✓ opening VERIFIED vs scan n53 (2026-07-06): the key quote 'la pequeña revolución que se ha formado en este Territorio' 

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n53–55** · Sav p. 54–56 · tomo p. 470–475 · 1829-11-27 · Savage abstract · 

✓ opening VERIFIED vs scan n53 (2026-07-06): the key quote 'la pequeña revolución que se ha formado en este Territorio' is verbatim; the SB comandante's 20 Nov express, the *junta de guerra* formed + circular issued, and the plan to march to Sta Bárbara 'dentro de 2 ó 3 [días]… reuniré una fuerza de 60 á 70 hombres… en socorro de los militares y vecinos honrados de Monterey' all confirmed. *(The Herrera-blame + Spain-vs-Norteamericanos speculation are on the n54–55 continuation, not re-read this pass.)* first report on the 1829 Solís Revolt — blames Sub-Comisario José María Herrera for inciting 'el desterrado Joaquín [=José María] Solís' and other deported criminals (to escape his own fraud liability); wonders if it favors Spain or 'aventureros Norte-Americanos'; ties it to the pro-Fernando-VII missionaries, the mission-síndicos' unrest, and the total lack of *situados*; forms a *junta de guerra*, marches to SB, begs 30 men from Sonora across the Colorado
