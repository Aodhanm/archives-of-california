# the 6% *reintegro* contribution must be paid by missions and vecinos on goods sold to nationals & foreigners; warns lest

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n352** · Sav p. 353 · tomo p. XVIII·28–29 · 1822-06-13 · Savage abstract · 

the 6% *reintegro* contribution must be paid by missions and vecinos on goods sold to nationals & foreigners; warns lest the Baja California catastrophe recur — where the garrison refused to obey its chief while the insurgents attacked, because the men were nearly naked & unpaid
