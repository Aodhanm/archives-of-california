# approves & returns the 'Plan de la contrata' ajusted with the Englishmen 'Luis Macala' [McCulloch] & 'Guillermo Arnel' [

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n353–354** · Sav p. 354–355 · tomo p. XVIII·(from) · 1822-06-22 · Savage abstract · 

approves & returns the 'Plan de la contrata' ajusted with the Englishmen 'Luis Macala' [McCulloch] & 'Guillermo Arnel' [Hartnell] for trade with the missions; a sharp church-state exchange over the 6% troop tax — rebuts the Prefect's protest that it strips the 'miserable' Indians of even necessary clothing with the caustic line that '4 pelones indios' should not have to sustain a whole military province, & resents being treated as one 'accustomed to dealing only with Indians'
