# fuller report — the unrest reached San Diego too (a soldier prosecuted for heading his company against interim cmdr Sant

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n39–41** · Sav p. 40–42 · tomo p. 416–424 · 1828-10-20 · Savage abstract · 

fuller report — the unrest reached San Diego too (a soldier prosecuted for heading his company against interim cmdr Santiago Argüello; comrades plotting to seize the guard & take up arms); ⭐ of the 14 officers in the 3 presidial companies of both Californias, only 7 fit for service; the friars 'desafectos á nuestras instituciones,' kept by force under the expulsion law, constantly demanding passports; ⭐ 'los Neófitos pugnan en las más Misiones para que sean convertidos en pueblos' (secularization pressure); begs for an aide, Juez Letrado, Comisario, 10 line companies + cash (½ the annual pay) to avoid 'un Cabal trastorno' — reinforcements could come overland from Sonora across the Colorado
