# (3 items) approves Fr. José Sánchez as minister of San Gabriel replacing Fr. Joaquín Pascual Nuez (d. 30 Dec); approves 

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n338** · Sav p. 339 · tomo p. XVIII·7 · 1822-01-09 · Savage abstract · 

(3 items) approves Fr. José Sánchez as minister of San Gabriel replacing Fr. Joaquín Pascual Nuez (d. 30 Dec); approves La Purísima neophytes building the 100-vara parapet; ⭐⭐ notes no public primary school has been established in the missions (no suitable teachers found; 'las gentes sensatas' hold that schooling is harmful rather than useful to the Indians; a respectable widow teaches at S. Carlos)
