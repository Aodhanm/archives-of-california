# arrival of a party of 74 armed men 'naturales del Canadá' (a Hudson's Bay Co./Canadian trapper brigade) near the Río San

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n411–412** · Sav p. 412–413 · tomo p. XIX·165 · 1843-01-06 · Savage abstract · 

arrival of a party of 74 armed men 'naturales del Canadá' (a Hudson's Bay Co./Canadian trapper brigade) near the Río San Joaquín, trading goods for horses; ties it to the Indios Cimarrones raiding the district's ranchos, which the vecinos can't pursue for lack of arms
