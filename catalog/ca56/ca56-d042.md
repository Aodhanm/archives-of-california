# register of convicts (*presos*) shipped from Mexico on the brig *Morelos*, 27 Jul 1825 (Capt. Ramón Sagasti) — named, w/

**C-A 56 (State Papers, Sacramento), scan n20–22** · Sav p. 21–23 · tomo p. 336–339 · (n.d., 1825) · Savage abstract · 

register of convicts (*presos*) shipped from Mexico on the brig *Morelos*, 27 Jul 1825 (Capt. Ramón Sagasti) — named, w/ crimes & sentences: José María Solís (alias 'Puerco'; a 'Gómez-gang' captain — the future leader of the 1829 Solís Revolt), Antonio Mesta, Fernando Corral 'Cara-de-toro,' Ignacio Solís, Pedro Lorenzo, José Mª Sosa, Francisco de Badillo (10 yrs presidio at San Rafael + fortifications, hardest labor, shoot-on-sight if he leaves his post); + a second group (Nicolás, Andrés León, Santiago Cárdenas, Ignacio García, Luis Beltrán, Pedro de Salas…) 'no tiene manifestación de sus delitos, sólo consta en la lista de entrega del comandante del bergantín' (~20 convicts total) — the California-as-penal-colony register
