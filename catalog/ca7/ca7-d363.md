# The Auditor de Guerra endorses Borica's escort order (the troops escorting the missionaries must sleep at the presidio, 

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n260–n261** · Sav p. (42)–(43) · tomo p. xiii.100–101 · 1795-10-05 · Savage abstract · 

The Auditor de Guerra endorses Borica's escort order (the troops escorting the missionaries must sleep at the presidio, not the mission; no troop *permuta* harmful to discipline & 'la moral cristiana') over the Monterey friars' objections (raised by Fr. Lorenzo Renalta[?]) — the metropole siding with the governor in the church–state escort dispute [cf. E11/E13]
