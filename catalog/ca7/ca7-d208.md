# The San José / Santa Clara affair (see E10): forwards Cabo Gabriel Moraga's report of a rumored gentile uprising round P

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n128–n136** · Sav p. 125–133 · tomo p. 336–353 · 1794-11-04 · Savage abstract · 

The San José / Santa Clara affair (see E10): forwards Cabo Gabriel Moraga's report of a rumored gentile uprising round Pueblo S. José caused by Fr. Manuel Fernández's violent forced-conversion sweeps; Argüello sent Hermenegildo Sal to reconnoiter (Sal found the Indians quieted, the plot rumor untrue, but confirmed the friar punished 'el indio Macho,' which caused the village abandonment; Sal withheld the Santa Clara ministers' letter ('la carta del Rdo P. Sánchez')); Borica's marginal note (1 Dec) records he agreed w/ Pe Pres. Lasuén to 'moderar el celo' of Fr. Fernández. Enclosures: Nº1 Moraga→Argüello (S. José, 30 Oct), Nº2 Argüello→Sal (31 Oct, w/ PS to deny the friar an escort if he won't leave), Nº3 Sal→Argüello (S. José, 2 Nov)
