# Genealogical deposition / heir-claim to the *alcances* of his late brother Sgt. Juan José Robles — names parents José An

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n242–n243** · Sav p. (24)–(25) · tomo p. xiii.58–61 · 1794-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

Genealogical deposition / heir-claim to the *alcances* of his late brother Sgt. Juan José Robles — names parents José Antonio de Robles & María Loreto Rodríguez (she buried at this presidio's church, he at Valladolid cathedral), brothers José María, Esteban Pantaleón, Juan José, Manuel Mariano, & a son Bachiller José Antº Robles (newly graduated, Univ. of México, seeking ordination); the family 'se crió en esa península de Californias'
