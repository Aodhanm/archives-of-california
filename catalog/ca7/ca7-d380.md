# Church–state escort compromise: the Pe Guardián of San Fernando protested Borica's order (escort soldiers not to sleep o

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n283–n284** · Sav p. (65)–(66) · tomo p. xiii.147–149 · 1795-11-07 · Savage abstract ·
