# Reconnaissance *diario* of the country N of Santa Bárbara (commissioned by Goycoechea): canyons & acequias of the Santa 

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n246–n248** · Sav p. (28)–(30) · tomo p. xiii.62–69 · 1795-06 (16–28) · Savage abstract · 

Reconnaissance *diario* of the country N of Santa Bárbara (commissioned by Goycoechea): canyons & acequias of the Santa Rosa river, farmland (≈1½ fanega maize), three more *acequias* for irrigation, a great piñería (the presidio's pine source) ~4 leguas out, oaks/willows, drought-thinned pasture, & 8 friendly gentile rancherías; also inspected the *caballada* & visited Mission San Buenaventura — handed the diary to Goycoechea
