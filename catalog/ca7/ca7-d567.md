# Armed clash at the ranchería 'Sochem' (Sierra behind Mission San Miguel): Gentiles killed a Christian India; an Alférez 

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n441–n442** · Sav p. (222)–(223) · tomo p. xiii.507–509 · 1795-05-13 · Savage abstract · 

Armed clash at the ranchería 'Sochem' (Sierra behind Mission San Miguel): Gentiles killed a Christian India; an Alférez + 5 soldiers seized 3 of the killers (17–18 Apr); on the return ~300 armed Indians attacked — soldiers killed 1 & wounded 2, forcing withdrawal; the 3 captives escaped; Grajera fortifies vs. a feared assault on the mission → E29
