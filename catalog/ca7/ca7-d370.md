# War-defense of California ('circunstancias actuales de la guerra'): men, artillery & munitions from San Blas to guard th

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n271–n272** · Sav p. (53)–(54) · tomo p. xiii.118–121 · 1795-07-28 · Savage abstract · 

War-defense of California ('circunstancias actuales de la guerra'): men, artillery & munitions from San Blas to guard the SF, Monterey & San Diego posts against a corsair or small landing — but the Erario cannot fund defense against a formal conquest by a major power, the immense Baja coast offering many landing-spots; prepare aid from the Sonora militia commander; and an engineer (Córdoba, Doc 366) is embarked for fortification work
