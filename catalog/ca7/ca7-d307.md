# 5 oficios: (1) chaplain replacement José Gómez (ex-*Concepción*); (2) ordered medicines/effects for Nootka on the *Aranz

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n213–n214** · Sav p. 209–210 · tomo p. 522–523 · 1794-07-15 · Savage abstract · 

5 oficios: (1) chaplain replacement José Gómez (ex-*Concepción*); (2) ordered medicines/effects for Nootka on the *Aranzazu*; (3) the Matute San-Blas troops already given to the *Concepción*; (4) ⭐⭐ re the 40-gun frigate — no cause for suspicion: 'las diferencias entre España é Inglaterra están terminadas… las dos Cortes han concluido un tratado de recíproca defensa y amistad'; (5) Viceroy's order to put the presidios in best defense (sent artillery) + the *Aranzazu* to carry SD-fort lumber
