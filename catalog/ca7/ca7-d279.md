# Order on the presidio works (per Junta Superior, 28 Dec 1793): carry them to completion; the sums advanced by the compan

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n185–n186** · Sav p. 181–182 · tomo p. 474–477 · 1794-01-22 · Savage abstract · 

Order on the presidio works (per Junta Superior, 28 Dec 1793): carry them to completion; the sums advanced by the companies to be reimbursed by the Real Hacienda (paid from Tobacco revenue, as *reintegro*); remaining work to be separately estimated & finished 'con solidez y economía'; the presidial soldiers who worked & still work on the fortifications *sin salario* to be recompensed somehow. Copy authorized Monterey 23 Jul 1794 (Arrillaga)
