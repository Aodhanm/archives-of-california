# Reglamento on troop accounts & supply (7 articles): no reales but every 2 yrs; no payment until alcances received; bans 

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n101–n102** · Sav p. 98–99 · tomo p. 258–259 · 1794-10-24 · Savage abstract · 

Reglamento on troop accounts & supply (7 articles): no reales but every 2 yrs; no payment until alcances received; bans direct/indirect trade with troops & families — 'es esto escandaloso abuso'; habilitado signs/posts tariffs; book to specify all *especies*; no officer to sell horses to troops; presidio cattle limited (4 cows/plaza de prest, 8/officer); halt *gastos de fábrica* except to finish the church
