# Execution of the resolution: the Indian accusers Plácido Ortiz & Antonio (in *grillos*), before the assembled Santa Clar

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n319–n321** · Sav p. (101)–(103) · tomo p. xiii.229–231 · 1795-09-15/20 · Savage abstract · 

Execution of the resolution: the Indian accusers Plácido Ortiz & Antonio (in *grillos*), before the assembled Santa Clara Christian community, escort & pueblo, publicly knelt 'en pública Palestra, en voz alta y clara' and begged forgiveness of the current ministers Fr. Miguel Francisco Sánchez (= the 'P. Sánchez' of E10) & Fr. Magín Catalá, then were released — the ceremony run by cabo-comisionado Gabriel Moraga (of E10), witnessed by Alf. ret. Ramón Lasso de la Vega & cabo Luis Peralta
