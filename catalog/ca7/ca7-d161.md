# Forwards a personal letter from George Vancouver (read it before the Pe Presidente; contents merely greetings, asks afte

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n100** · Sav p. 97 · tomo p. 256–257 · 1794-11-17 · Savage abstract · 

Forwards a personal letter from George Vancouver (read it before the Pe Presidente; contents merely greetings, asks after Goycoechea's health & requests *Gazetas nuevas*); Vancouver writes he is bedridden with head/body pain — 'no sé si habrá algún empacho, ó será resfrío del estómago, porq[ue] éste no resiste arriba de 3 ó 4 bocados, y aventado todo el lado del hígado'
