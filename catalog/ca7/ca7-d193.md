# Pearl-fishery revenue evasion at Mulegé: the King's canoe (*arráez* Ventura Mabu, *armador* Manuel Salazar) dove the Mul

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n118–n120** · Sav p. 115–117 · tomo p. 309–314 · 1794-11-14 · Savage abstract · 

Pearl-fishery revenue evasion at Mulegé: the King's canoe (*arráez* Ventura Mabu, *armador* Manuel Salazar) dove the Mulegé *placeres* & crossed back to the other shore without paying the royal *quinto* or showing a concealed pearl; the diver pleads 'las cosas del soberano son delicadas' & gives his word; on Rea's arrival (23 Aug) he confronts him, the pearl & its fifth are surrendered, Buenavista's alférez notified; encloses the diver's reply of 30 Oct (Dupdo)
