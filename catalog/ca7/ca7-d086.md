# 'Crítica situación, débiles escoltas' (→ E5): Santa Clara cattle-theft pursuit (Noboa's denunciation); an Indian kept in

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n49–n53** · Sav p. 50–54 · tomo p. 129–133 · 1794-03-15 · Savage abstract · 

'Crítica situación, débiles escoltas' (→ E5): Santa Clara cattle-theft pursuit (Noboa's denunciation); an Indian kept in the *cepo* 10 days though declared innocent; Christian Indians steal horses/cattle; Sgt. Pedro Amador's round-up (15 men + pueblo vecinos, gentiles captured) & recruitment of gentiles for Castillo labor; captives applied to the fort works in irons by day; encloses Fr. Diego de Noboa's 14 Mar letter warning of 'un rompimiento muy semejante al de Sonora' [= prior mil-hist #43, at Sav p.53]; only '2 useful men' to resist invasion
