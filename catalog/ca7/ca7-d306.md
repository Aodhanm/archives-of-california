# Intelligence (from an English merchant-frigate captain): an English 40-gun warship was to sail (last Oct.) to Nootka 'á 

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n212** · Sav p. 208 · tomo p. 521 · 1794-06-15 · Savage abstract · 

Intelligence (from an English merchant-frigate captain): an English 40-gun warship was to sail (last Oct.) to Nootka 'á tratar varios asuntos… y relevar á Dn George Wancouver,' now 'muy inmediata á esta costa' — warns Borica to watch; thinks Vancouver's stated motive (his commission ending this year) 'frívolo' (see E18)
