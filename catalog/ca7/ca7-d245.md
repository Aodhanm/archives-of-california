# The chaplain tangle: per Nootka cmdt Ramón Saavedra's instructions he must transfer a chaplain to his ship; the *Concepc

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n162** · Sav p. 158 · tomo p. 417–418 · 1794-07-06 · Savage abstract · 

The chaplain tangle: per Nootka cmdt Ramón Saavedra's instructions he must transfer a chaplain to his ship; the *Concepción* (Menéndez) is ordered to Acapulco w/ the sick friars; he needs a chaplain + *colegial* lest a man die at sea 'sin los sacramentos'
