# San Luis sedition (cont.): new declarations from the imprisoned La Purísima gentiles — they were *convidados* by the Ind

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n106–n107** · Sav p. 103–104 · tomo p. 268–269 · 1794-09-12 · Savage abstract · 

San Luis sedition (cont.): new declarations from the imprisoned La Purísima gentiles — they were *convidados* by the Indian Miguel of S. Luis, urged against the Christians, paid in *avalorios* (beads); only the gentiles seem culpable (the two factions of S. Luis Christians were to fight); meanwhile he keeps the prisoners 'divertidos en la necesidad de esta obra' (set to forced labor on the works)
