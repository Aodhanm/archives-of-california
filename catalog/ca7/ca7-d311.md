# Urgent request for medicines & provisions for the next Nootka winter ('muchos enfermos… de distintos achaques'); sent th

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n217–n218** · Sav p. 213–214 · tomo p. 528–529 · 1794-06-15 · Savage abstract · 

Urgent request for medicines & provisions for the next Nootka winter ('muchos enfermos… de distintos achaques'); sent the *Aranzazu* under 2nd pilot Dn Juan Kendrick to carry them, w/ his surgeon's lists; all charged at San Blas — the ship carries only enough to return, so furnish aid for the crew's stay at Nootka. ⟵ END OF TOMO XII (n218 verso blank)
