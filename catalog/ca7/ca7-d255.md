# The foreign-ship policy in his own hand (see E14): the London convention bars foreign ships from Spanish establishments,

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n168** · Sav p. 164 · tomo p. 429 · 1793-01-12 · Savage abstract · 

The foreign-ship policy in his own hand (see E14): the London convention bars foreign ships from Spanish establishments, but one that arrives by accident 'sin órden de S.E., ó permiso de S.E.' — 'como se ha verificado por esta vez, con el Comandante Vancouver' — is to get 'los precisos auxilios que manda la Hospitalidad, sin permitirles se demoren'
