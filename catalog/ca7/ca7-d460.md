# Roundup of the *cimarrón* (runaway) Indians of Mission San Borja (Baja) by Sgt. Javier Aguilar — with a named *Relación*

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n361–n364** · Sav p. (142)–(145) · tomo p. xiii.327–332 · 1795-05-31 · Savage abstract · 

Roundup of the *cimarrón* (runaway) Indians of Mission San Borja (Baja) by Sgt. Javier Aguilar — with a named *Relación* categorizing ~15 ('Cimarrones': Estanislao 60, 'cimarrón perpetuo,' caught 3×; Vicente; Basilio; Pablo of S. Fernando 70; José Antonio 80 — 'Por Ladrones': cattle-thieves; 'Remitidos á la Frontera': Sixto, the ringleader, wife-stealer & flight-inciter; 'Cogidos en la Contra Costa') + a total of ~60 from San Borja, 41 from San Fernando & more (see E23)
