# Arrillaga's command ends (→ deal w/ Borica); discontent of San Blas sailors at Mission Santa Cruz w/ manual labor (adobe

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n40–n41** · Sav p. 41–42 · tomo p. 96–102 · 1794-11-01 · Savage abstract · 

Arrillaga's command ends (→ deal w/ Borica); discontent of San Blas sailors at Mission Santa Cruz w/ manual labor (adobes etc.) — proposes paying them to work at presidio; + cabo Miguel Pacheco's report: a Padre badly beat (*aporreó malamente*) a gentile who refused baptism; the soldier-escort reprimanded for allowing it; Pérez laments actions 'diametralmente opuestas al servicio de Dios y del Rey' undoing his work to curb Indian 'altanería'
