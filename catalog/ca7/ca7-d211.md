**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n137–n138** · Sav p. 134–135 · tomo p. 357–361 · 1794-12-03 · Savage abstract · 

'Cinco oficios': (1) French-prisoner internment method; (2) recognizes Branciforte as Viceroy; (3) soldiers' *cédulas* (cabo Pablo w/ Andrés Ramírez; one suspended pending a replacement for Cantúa, needed on the R. Hacda ranch); (4) a Gremios Mayores de Madrid ship at SF & the Gibraltar→San Blas/Californias commercial expedition (effects shipped at Cádiz); (5) ⭐ intercept all foreign-addressed mail & forward to the Viceroy [cf. Docs 8/68]
