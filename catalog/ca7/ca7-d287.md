# 'Estado de las Californias' / Papel de Puntos — his 32-point handover state-of-the-province report (⚠ *trunco*; see E17)

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n192–n202** · Sav p. 188–198 · tomo p. 491–498 · 1794 (no date) · Savage abstract · 

'Estado de las Californias' / Papel de Puntos — his 32-point handover state-of-the-province report (⚠ *trunco*; see E17). Highlights: Indian-fire *bandos* (pt.2); 1792 pueblo unrest + San José land-*suerte* distribution in property, inalienable (pts.4–5); Monterey-river settlers (pt.6); soldier cattle-limits 3–4 cows SD/SB/Monterey (pt.9); ⭐⭐ SF baluarte 'ya puede hallarse al concluir' while SD & Monterey defenses suspended (pt.13); San Blas artisans + the mason for the *establecimto de Bodega 'que no se verificó'* → the Castillo (pt.14, = Doc 250); SF presidio & troop quarters 'en infeliz estado', the SF troops did most of the fort work (pt.16); artisan-instruction program placing craftsmen to teach Indians at Sta Cruz/S. Carlos/Monterey/S. Luis (pts.17–21); unbuilt gristmill (pt.19); ⭐⭐ indigenous-resistance catalog (pts.22–25, see E17): San Luis conspiracy ringleaders Miguel & Morillo → SF fort forced labor w/ Ildefonso/Constantino/Pablo; Charquín (SF mission) jailed for harboring runaways; a plot to burn Mission San Miguel & kill the escort; ⭐⭐ high child/adult mortality at Missions SF & Sta Clara (pt.29); muster-readiness gaps — weekly musket drill, night sentinels asleep (pts.26–28); Tte León Parrilla deemed 'insuficiente' (pt.31, cf. E11)
