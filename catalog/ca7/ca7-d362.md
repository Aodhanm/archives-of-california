# The 1795 artisan-instruction contracts (habilitado-gen. Manuel Cárcaba, per Viceroy's order 19 Jun) — full text w/ each 

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n254–n260** · Sav p. (36)–(42) · tomo p. xiii.86–99 · 1795-10-03 · Savage abstract · 

The 1795 artisan-instruction contracts (habilitado-gen. Manuel Cárcaba, per Viceroy's order 19 Jun) — full text w/ each master's biography (see E20): Sastre (tailor) Joaquín Botello (San Felipe, Valencia; 32; $1,000/yr + ⅛ of output), Listonero (ribbon) Manuel Muñoz (Puebla; 30; single; $500), Alfarero/Lozero (potter) Mariano Tapia (Puebla; 38; wife Leonarda Benites $10/mo; $500), Tejedor de lo ancho Mariano José Mendoza (México; 40; wife Antolina Soto $15/mo; $600) — 5-yr terms, ⅛ of output, to teach the *naturales*, travel at Crown cost w/ the troop under Pedro Alberni. Signed 6 Aug 1795 (witnesses Cadet Manuel Rodríguez & Ramón Pérez); ratified by Bonilla 3 Oct. ⚠ Botello = tailor here (cf. C-A 16/54 'mason Botello' — reconcile)
