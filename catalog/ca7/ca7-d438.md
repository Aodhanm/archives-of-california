# Consolidated war-defense order (11+ pts): fortify the SF & Monterey batteries + the new SD battery; the Catalonian Volun

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n333–n337** · Sav p. (115)–(119) · tomo p. xiii.252–259 · 1795-07-28 · Savage abstract · 

Consolidated war-defense order (11+ pts): fortify the SF & Monterey batteries + the new SD battery; the Catalonian Volunteers (72, Alberni) + artillery (18) + 3 armorers; a 6-monthly coastal patrol by two small ships; and the full foreign-ship R.O. chronology — 23 Mar 1793 (shelter English), 2 Feb 1794 (no trade), 6 Dec 1794 (good armony, no reconnaissance), 28 Jan 1795 — plus the motive: European nations covet Cal. ports 'que facilitan el comercio… con la Asia.' (consolidates Docs 370/392–394/386–388/427)
