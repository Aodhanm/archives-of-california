# Vancouver-deserter cost dossier: encloses Viceroy Revillagigedo's order (Mexico, 22 Jan 1794) to return the 5 English de

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n172–n174** · Sav p. 168–170 · tomo p. 437–441 · 1794-09-12 · Savage abstract · 

Vancouver-deserter cost dossier: encloses Viceroy Revillagigedo's order (Mexico, 22 Jan 1794) to return the 5 English deserters (carried on the *Concepción* to Nootka) to Vancouver & recover their keep; when handing them over, also reclaim the *three Spanish sailors* Marine Cmdt Juan de la Bodega [y Quadra] gave Vancouver in their place; cost $298.3½ rl; w/ Francisco Hijosa's San Blas account (26 Apr 1794) & Menéndez's Nº3. ⚠ numbers vary (6 held / 5 ordered returned / 3 claimed British)
