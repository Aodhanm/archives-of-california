# THE 1795 MISSION DOLORES MASS-FLIGHT (see E24): reports (via Fr. Martín Landaeta) that 'cerca de 200 cristianos de ambos

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n366–n368** · Sav p. (147)–(149) · tomo p. xiii.362–367 · 1795-09-15 · Savage abstract · 

THE 1795 MISSION DOLORES MASS-FLIGHT (see E24): reports (via Fr. Martín Landaeta) that 'cerca de 200 cristianos de ambos sexos abandonaron la Misión de San Franco,' fleeing 'poco á poco y por diferentes rumbos' to the *Otra banda* (East Bay) — joining SF/S. Mateo/S. Pablo runaways of earlier years; a fugitive José Miguel reports a *cimarrón* Enrique killed there; 7 old Christians + 15 women & families sheltered in the ranchería of the Pachines (capitanejo Ayala Orila); recovery attempted via the gentile Alonso & gifts
