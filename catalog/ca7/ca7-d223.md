# Vancouver's frigate *Discovery* anchored at Monterey 5 Nov (see E12): came only to collect deserters & to forward Court 

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n148–n149** · Sav p. 145–146 · tomo p. 380–381 · 1794-11-07 · Savage abstract · 

Vancouver's frigate *Discovery* anchored at Monterey 5 Nov (see E12): came only to collect deserters & to forward Court *pliegos* he expected in Cal.; finding none, begged a courier toward San Diego (had charged Tte. Grajera) & that a recommended letter be forwarded to the first English ship there. Vancouver asked if there'd be 'the same retribución as last year' — Argüello answered NO, he now had orders to give *abrigo* to ships of Vancouver's nation, warships & merchantmen, under the Alliance — which Vancouver & the Viceroy's letter 'celebró infinito'
