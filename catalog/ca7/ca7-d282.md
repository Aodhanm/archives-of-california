# Detailed annual supply-*memoria* instruction: weigh the state of troop & family clothing, arms, mounts & warehouse stock

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n187–n188** · Sav p. 183–184 · tomo p. 480–481 · 1794-12-11 · Savage abstract · 

Detailed annual supply-*memoria* instruction: weigh the state of troop & family clothing, arms, mounts & warehouse stocks; exclude luxury goods from the troop *memorias* (velvet skirts, fine lace, silk taffeta, etc. — officers order those in their own *memorias particulares*); buy soap, blankets, *jerga* in the Peninsula where cheaper than San Blas; request the balance in coin; the Márquez / Gremios Mayores trade
