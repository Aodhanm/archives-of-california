# Sal's own report on the Castillo/baluarte labor force: 33 gentiles from San José ('muy contentos, se les trata bien'); 7

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n77–n78** · Sav p. 74–75 · tomo p. 187–190 · 1794-04-30 · Savage abstract · 

Sal's own report on the Castillo/baluarte labor force: 33 gentiles from San José ('muy contentos, se les trata bien'); 78 men total on the works = 30 from the SF mission + punished *cimarrones* + 8 from Sta Clara + 37 gentiles, under 1 sgt/1 cabo/2 soldiers; mission Padres demand a blanket/trousers monthly on top of pay (refused); 1,500 adobes/day; cut 52 *cuartones* for the esplanade; gentiles fled leaving their *prendas* — wages/belongings left for them at Sta Clara; the 4 Christian captives (from E5) to go to Mission Santa Clara → folds into E5
