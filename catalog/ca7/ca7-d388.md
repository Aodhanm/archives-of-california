**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n289–n291** · Sav p. (71)–(72) · tomo p. xiii.160–161 · 1795-11-14 · Savage abstract · 

Reservada R.O. (3 Jul): the KING resolves the Vancouver-provisioning accounts — Salvador Hidalgo supplied Vancouver at Nootka (~$700) + ~$500 worth at San Francisco; S.M. resolves not to reclaim the sums, but henceforth take a receipt from any vessel so supplied [royal resolution of the E7 gift-audit]
