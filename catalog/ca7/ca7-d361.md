# Fiscal/Junta dossier responding to Borica's 27 Jun consulta on the SF presidio (see E19): the SF presidio 'muy arruinada

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n250–n254** · Sav p. (32)–(36) · tomo p. xiii.82–86 · 1795-12-04 · Savage abstract · 

Fiscal/Junta dossier responding to Borica's 27 Jun consulta on the SF presidio (see E19): the SF presidio 'muy arruinada,' the proposal to relocate it closer to the port; doubts on the new *fábrica*'s solidity (begun 1776); the fort-works cost ($11,716.3.8; Contador José María Beltrán); + Tribunal proposals — schools for men & women, take 14-yr-olds from the foundling house (*casa de expósitos*) to work the fort, a nuns' monastery (deemed unnecessary), artisans at low pay; endorses proceeding w/ the new SF presidio *fábrica*
