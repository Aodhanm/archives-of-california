# The 6 English deserters (brought by the *Concepción*): three superior orders (20 Aug) to deliver them to Vancouver or th

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n152–n153** · Sav p. 149–150 · tomo p. 389–391 · 1794-09-30 · Savage abstract · 

The 6 English deserters (brought by the *Concepción*): three superior orders (20 Aug) to deliver them to Vancouver or the first British ship, recovering costs amicably; the resolution kept reserved to stop them making 'esfuerzos extraordinarios pª huirse'; the men, idle on rations, expected repatriation (some sought baptism, others enlistment — denied); if no English ship comes, to be sent to Nootka w/ the charge-document
