# Fiscal's review of the artisan-remission expediente (the earlier stage of E20/Doc 362): ⚠ resolves the 'Botello mason' p

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n274–n278** · Sav p. (56)–(60) · tomo p. xiii.125–133 · 1795-06-19 · Savage abstract · 

Fiscal's review of the artisan-remission expediente (the earlier stage of E20/Doc 362): ⚠ resolves the 'Botello mason' puzzle — the *Cantero/Albañil* (mason) is *Manuel Ruíz* ($365/yr), Botello is the *Sastre* (tailor); on armorers (*maestros armeros*) — Pedro González García (left at Nootka off the *Concepción*, July 94) to be sent to Monterey via Álava, & an aging SD armorer; mill-stones & carding-combs from San Blas; weavers/ribbon-makers/potters wanting; schoolmasters/*labradores*/*herreros* deferred; the program's stated aim = 'la educación política de los Indios'
