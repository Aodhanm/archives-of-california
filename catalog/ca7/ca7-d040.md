# Márquez trade license + free-trade extension: King's grant to Ramón/Román Márquez (comercio de Indias) for a Cádiz→San B

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n21–n22** · Sav p. 22–23 · tomo p. 51–52 · 1794-08-09 · Savage abstract · 

Márquez trade license + free-trade extension: King's grant to Ramón/Román Márquez (comercio de Indias) for a Cádiz→San Blas→California expedition (South-Sea stops), all aid w/o burden to the Treasury; the franchises extended generally — absolute freedom from Spanish export duties, *alcabala* on first sale & municipal duties on national fruits/manufactures bound to Cal. ports, foreign goods pay 7% (as at Veracruz)
