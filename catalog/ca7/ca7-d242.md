# Verbatim letter on the deserters: of the 6, only three are British subjects — Diego Acheson & Diego Lanson (Landon) (des

**C-A 7 (Provincial State Papers), scan n159–n160** · Sav p. 155–156 · tomo p. 410–411 (dup 407–409) · 1794-11-16 · Savage abstract · 

Verbatim letter on the deserters: of the 6, only three are British subjects — Diego Acheson & Diego Lanson (Landon) (deserted the *Chatham* at Monterey 6 Jan 1793) and Tomás Smith (deserted the urca *Dédalo/Daedalus* at San Diego 7 Dec 1793; my earlier '[Owen?]' → corrected to Smith per Doc 264); he does not claim the other three (deserters of the *Dédalo*, Dec 1792); cannot pay the costs himself but will lay an itemized account before the Lords Commissioners of the Admiralty in London. (Spanish translation tied to Brig. Álava.)
