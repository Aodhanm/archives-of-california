# S.M. approves Manuel Cárcaba as *habilitado general* (Capt. Inf. Puebla → graded Capt. Cav., 1,200 p/yr); future habilit

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n137–n138** · Sav p. 136–137 · tomo p. 465–468 · 1791-10-03 / 08-11 · Savage abstract · 

S.M. approves Manuel Cárcaba as *habilitado general* (Capt. Inf. Puebla → graded Capt. Cav., 1,200 p/yr); future habilitados to be elected by the presidio officers w/ a 5,000-peso bond (pp.465–466); + ⭐⭐ a carpenter contracted at 10 r/day 'para enseñar á lo menos á 12 indios' of the razón/missions — same terms as the artisans dispatched March 1791 via the Guadalajara intendant (pp.467–468)
