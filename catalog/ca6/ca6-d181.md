# The goleta *Santa Saturnina* (alias 'La Orcasitas') at Loreto, May 1793 — to stay in the port warehouses; crewed by the 

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n250–n251** · Sav p. XI:66–67 / 211–212 · 1793-06 · Savage abstract · 

The goleta *Santa Saturnina* (alias 'La Orcasitas') at Loreto, May 1793 — to stay in the port warehouses; crewed by the *práctico* Joaquín Robles + 2 artillerymen + 5 sailors; came empty (no timber); carried 12 cases of *azogue* (mercury) from Álamos; arrived 29 May (the same Límites-expedition schooner of the 1792 SF friendly-fire & Docs 122/127)
