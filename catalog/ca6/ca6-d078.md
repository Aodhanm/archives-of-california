# R.O. via the innocent-fisherman case: at Alicante, José Porras (*pescador*) + his son were jailed for having 'captured' 

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n71** · Sav p. 70 · tomo p. 254–255 · 1791-08-22 (R.O. 5 May 1788 ← 6 Sept 1770) · Savage abstract · 

R.O. via the innocent-fisherman case: at Alicante, José Porras (*pescador*) + his son were jailed for having 'captured' 6 deserting Flandes-infantry soldiers (1770) — the soldiers had seized his boat by force; the Consejo de Guerra & King order the boat returned and both freed *'en libertad libremente y sin costos.'* ⭐ Copyist's note: the innocents spent 5–6 months in jail, and the 1770 order reached the Gov. of California in 1791 — '21 años después.'
