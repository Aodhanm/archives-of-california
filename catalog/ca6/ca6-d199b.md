# Vancouver's letter cont. — the shore operations he needs: examine the rigging, unload the supply ship's cargo onto the *

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n290–n291** · Sav p. XI:106–107 / 269–278 · 1793-12-02 · Savage abstract · 

Vancouver's letter cont. — the shore operations he needs: examine the rigging, unload the supply ship's cargo onto the *Discovery* & *Chatham* (requiring part on land), casks + a guard ashore; his commission includes Astronomy & Natural History, with shore observatories; names the officer Mr. Whidbey [Joseph Whidbey], pilot of the *Discovery*, + 2 midshipmen; and ⭐ 'Mr. Menzes [Archibald Menzies],' the naturalist, needing to come ashore to fulfill his commission; asks that the officers be allowed to walk/ride as on his prior visit
