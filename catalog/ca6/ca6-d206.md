# THE VICEROY APPROVES ARRILLAGA'S WHOLE CONDUCT WITH VANCOUVER (per the Gov.'s 14 Apr notes on Vancouver at San Francisco

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n346–n347** · Sav p. XI:162–163 · tomo p. 380–382 · 1794-02-28 · Savage abstract · 

THE VICEROY APPROVES ARRILLAGA'S WHOLE CONDUCT WITH VANCOUVER (per the Gov.'s 14 Apr notes on Vancouver at San Francisco & Monterey): the timely warnings to the commanders were 'acertadas,' 'todo lo ha aprobado,' and orders him to continue admitting & franqueando auxilios á embarcaciones inglesas (R.O. 25 Mar 93); if Vancouver returns, 'le atiendan y socorran,' limiting the officers' walks + the observatory to near the presidio, the men aboard at night, effects in the warehouse/barracks under the Spanish commander — 'y sobre todo, conserve con Vancouver la mejor armonía' [the official seal on the Open Door; → THESIS-CRITICAL #7]
