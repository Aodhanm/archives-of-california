# Forwards + transcribes the verbatim Royal Decree regulating the *gracia de inválidos* (disability/retirement for sgts & 

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n54–n58** · Sav p. 53–57 / 214–218 · 1791-11-15 (R.O. Madrid 24 Jun 1791 / 22 Nov 1790) · Savage abstract · 

Forwards + transcribes the verbatim Royal Decree regulating the *gracia de inválidos* (disability/retirement for sgts & soldiers): the King, noting excessive invalid proposals, fixes 5 points — (I) 18 yrs good service + real ailment; (II) 18 yrs even if only 'tired' (to the Jefes' discretion); (III) under 18 yrs but disabled in action of war / march / garrison / escort / aid to justice / pursuit of contrabandists & malefactors, certified by the immediate Jefe + regiment surgeon; (IV) involuntary illness under 18 yrs; (V) bad-conduct/own-fault → absolute discharge per ordenanza
