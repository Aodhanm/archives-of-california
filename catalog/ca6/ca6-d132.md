# The King accepts Coronel Pedro Fages's resignation as Gov. of California and confirms Tte. Cnl. José Antonio Romeu (sarg

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n140** · Sav p. 139 · tomo p. 473 · 1791-05-18 (copy cert. José Argüello) · Savage abstract · 

The King accepts Coronel Pedro Fages's resignation as Gov. of California and confirms Tte. Cnl. José Antonio Romeu (sargento mayor, Dragones de España) as Governor in propriety. ⚠ Note: this copy was made at the request of Doña Josefa Sandoval, *widow* of the late Gov. Romeu — i.e. Romeu had since died (1792)
