# The SF presidio CONSTRUCTION HISTORY (1776–92): begun 27 Jul 1776; by end-1778 the commandant's house was adobe + partia

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n236–n237** · Sav p. XI:52–53 · tomo p. 175–179 · 1792-03-11 · Savage abstract · 

The SF presidio CONSTRUCTION HISTORY (1776–92): begun 27 Jul 1776; by end-1778 the commandant's house was adobe + partial walls + a stone *casa mata* + palisade-and-earth warehouses/church/quarters; the Jan 1779 rains collapsed 2 warehouses, the casa mata, church, commandant's house, 6 troop houses & most of the 4 wall-curtains; by 1780 nothing of the 1778 work remained; the adobe is bad ('con solo la humedad se deshace'); timber 10+ leagues off; the presidio built three times; in 1787 & 1791 the troops reverted to palisade houses to shelter their families. Signed Sal
