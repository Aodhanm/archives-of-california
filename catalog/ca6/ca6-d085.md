# Carta de aviso re a bando against disorders in the sale/barter of bestias (pp.275–276); + the bando itself — re-publishe

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n78–n79** · Sav p. 77–78 · tomo p. 272–276+ · 1791-09-28 / 09-27 · Savage abstract · 

Carta de aviso re a bando against disorders in the sale/barter of bestias (pp.275–276); + the bando itself — re-publishes the Caballero de Croix bando of 22 Mar 1778 barring anyone from buying/selling/pawning troop animals, arms, monturas, vestuario; animals already bought to be presented & branded before the inspector de presidios Tte. Cnl. *Diego de Borica* (Sonora: Roque de Medina); term 4 mo. (>100 leagues) / 2 mo. (less) [⭐ Borica before he was CA governor] (cont. n80)
