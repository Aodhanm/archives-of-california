# Ortega is ALIVE & commanding Loreto in 1793 (no novelty, p.205; Alférez Ildefonso Bernal's sueldo, p.206); ⭐ the Indian 

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n249–n250** · Sav p. XI:65–66 · tomo p. 205–210 · 1793-04 to 06 · Savage abstract · 

Ortega is ALIVE & commanding Loreto in 1793 (no novelty, p.205; Alférez Ildefonso Bernal's sueldo, p.206); ⭐ the Indian Juan Tarpio asks exemption from pueblo *recogimiento* for 15+ yrs' service in the marina (Ortega consults, pp.207–208); inventory via Sgt. Carrillo (pp.209–210). ⚠️ RE-CORRECTION: proves my Doc 159 reading of 'Ortega's death' (n208) was WRONG — Ortega lived (the famous J. F. de Ortega d. 1798); the death Sal witnessed at Monterey was someone else / a misread. *Do not cite Doc 159's Ortega-death.*
