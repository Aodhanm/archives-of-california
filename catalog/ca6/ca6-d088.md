**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n82–n84** · Sav p. 81–83 / 286–294 · 1791-08-17 / 1787-12-21 · Savage abstract · 

Forwards + transcribes the R. Cédula of 21 Dec 1787 on jurisdiction over *causas de concubinato* (mixed fuero church vs. royal courts; cites the 1771/1777 cédulas + Obispo de Plasencia). ⭐ Circulation-compliance certs name the four presidio commanders, 1792: Hermenegildo Sal (SF, 26 Feb), José Argüello (Monterey, 28 Jul), Felipe Goycoechea (Sta Bárbara, 8 Aug), Juan Pablo Grijalva (San Diego, 14 Aug); + the printed cédula (pp.292–294)
