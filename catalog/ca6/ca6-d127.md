# Acks the founding of Mission Santo Tomás de Aquino — ⚠️ here dated *24 April* (vs Doc 117's 26 April; flag) (p.456); + ⭐

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n135** · Sav p. 134 · tomo p. 456–459 · 1791-11-26 / 11-28 / 10-04 · Savage abstract · 

Acks the founding of Mission Santo Tomás de Aquino — ⚠️ here dated *24 April* (vs Doc 117's 26 April; flag) (p.456); + ⭐⭐⭐ acks that the Gov., arriving the 13th, found the goleta *Saturnina* 'procedente de Nuca [Nootka]' heading to San Blas (p.457; the *Saturnina* = the Límites-expedition schooner, later the 1792 SF Cantil-Blanco friendly-fire ship — Bodega/SF-defense thread); + a printed cédula on the *indulto del delito de bebidas prohibidas* (dup of p.111, pp.458–459)
