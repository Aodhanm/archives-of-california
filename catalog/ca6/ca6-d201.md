# The Viceroy congratulates Vancouver that (per Vancouver's 13 Jan letter) the Spanish subjects of New Orleans received hi

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n296–n297** · Sav p. XI:112–113 · tomo p. 279 · 1793-02-18 (copy Monterey 2 Nov 1793) · Savage abstract · 

The Viceroy congratulates Vancouver that (per Vancouver's 13 Jan letter) the Spanish subjects of New Orleans received him 'con la mayor hospitalidad y amistad,' conforming to the King's wish to keep 'la mejor correspondencia con el Rey y la Nación Británica'; he'll please Vancouver 'en cuanto pueda ofrecérsele en estos Reinos' — signed 'El Conde de Revilla Gigedo. Sr. Jorge Vancouver'
