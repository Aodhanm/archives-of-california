# Branciforte to the incoming governor Diego de Borica re his journey to Monterey — Borica reported (8 Oct) leaving Missio

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n391** · Sav p. XI:207 · tomo p. 491–92 · 1794-12-17 · Savage abstract · 

Branciforte to the incoming governor Diego de Borica re his journey to Monterey — Borica reported (8 Oct) leaving Mission San Juan Capistrano the next day for Monterey, where interim gov. Arrillaga would be relieved to finish his commission (the proprietary-governorship handover begins); + (Dec 30) supply: increase of various articles in the presidio *memorias* per the Tribunal de Cuentas, as Arrillaga requested 11 Jul
