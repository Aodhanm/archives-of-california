# Artisans' wage-balances (*alcances de artesanos*) of $10,329..4 against the Royal Treasury surfaced in habilitado Argüel

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n392–393** · Sav p. XI:208–09 · tomo p. 498 · 1794-12-20 · Savage abstract · 

Artisans' wage-balances (*alcances de artesanos*) of $10,329..4 against the Royal Treasury surfaced in habilitado Argüello's regulation of the 1794 *situado* — Branciforte demands to know its origin since their *haberes* were already settled & paid in prior accounts (the 1791 artisan-instruction program's fiscal tail)
