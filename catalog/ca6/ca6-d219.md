# The Viceroy declines & returns the $740 war-donation the vecinos of Loreto, Sta Bárbara & San Diego gave for the war wit

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n364–n365** · Sav p. XI:180–181 / 426–429 · 1794-06-26/28 · Savage abstract · 

The Viceroy declines & returns the $740 war-donation the vecinos of Loreto, Sta Bárbara & San Diego gave for the war with France (thanks them in the King's name, p.426); + the order (Conde de Alange, 9 Dec 93) on the intimation to French prisoners (bilingual French/Spanish; barring them from raising 'cuestiones ó disputas… de los negocios públicos,' pp.427–29)
