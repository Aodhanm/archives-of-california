# The detailed Loreto-presidio SITUADO report (Tribunal de Cuentas): the annual situado of Loreto + its Marina dept. = $14

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n202–n204** · Sav p. XI:18–20 / 63–76 · 1792-02-06 · Savage abstract · 

The detailed Loreto-presidio SITUADO report (Tribunal de Cuentas): the annual situado of Loreto + its Marina dept. = $14,818 4 3/8 + $4,130 6r augment; the double-accounting confusion (Caja de San Blas vs. habilitación) harming the Real Hacienda; order to hand the $14,632 integrally to habilitado Manuel Cárcaba (¼ reserved for víveres/memorias); the regulation of the troop/marina pay, pearls & cattle as Real-Hacienda income; approved by Fiscal Campo Marín (20 Dec 1792)
