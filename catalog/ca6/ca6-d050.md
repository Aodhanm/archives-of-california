# The 1791 artisan-instruction program: by govt order, workers go to San Francisco to teach the *naturales* their trades —

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n42** · Sav p. 42 · tomo p. 160–162, 153 · 1791-08-31 / 04-17 · Savage abstract · 

The 1791 artisan-instruction program: by govt order, workers go to San Francisco to teach the *naturales* their trades — 1 albañil, 1 cantero + 2 oficiales de cantero, 1 maestro sastre + 2 oficiales de sastre, 3 herreros (the last a blacksmith condemned to 10 yrs presidio, unpaid); their wages + portions to wives stated (pp.160–162). + Romeu takes possession of the governorship, relieving Coronel Pedro Fages; Pedro de Nava takes the Provincias Internas (p.153)
