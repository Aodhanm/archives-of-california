# The Christian Indian's death (cf. Doc 143): he left his ranchería w/ 3 Gentiles, met 6 more, they fought (cause unknown)

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n176** · Sav p. 175 · tomo p. 564–567 · 1791-11-29 · Savage abstract · 

The Christian Indian's death (cf. Doc 143): he left his ranchería w/ 3 Gentiles, met 6 more, they fought (cause unknown), 1 of the 3 left for dead; + ⭐⭐ 'corre el ruido que los gentiles quieren ir á matar á la gente y llevarse la caballada' (the Gentiles rumored to plan attacking the people & stealing the horses) — echoes Fages's Apache/horse warning (Doc 139); + a welcome to Romeu (pp.566–567)
