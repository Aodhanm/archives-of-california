# NOOTKA/OPEN DOOR (corroborates THESIS-CRITICAL #3): by his 25 May letter, Floridablanca acknowledged the Gov.'s complian

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n127** · Sav p. 126 / 439–440 · 1791-09 (s. día) · Savage abstract · 

NOOTKA/OPEN DOOR (corroborates THESIS-CRITICAL #3): by his 25 May letter, Floridablanca acknowledged the Gov.'s compliance with the order that 'no se hiciesen hostilidades á las embarcaciones de pesca inglesas ú otras, ni á establecimientos que se hallasen en costas desamparadas' — the post-Nootka fishing-vessel rule applied & confirmed at the CA level (pp.439–440); + Goycoechea's April/May extractos de revista (p.441)
