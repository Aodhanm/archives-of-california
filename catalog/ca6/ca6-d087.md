# NOOTKA / OPEN DOOR (heading reads *'Sobre prisiones'* — ⚠ mismatch; content is the fishing-vessel order): transmits Flor

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n81** · Sav p. 80 · tomo p. 284–285 · 1791-02-22 (copy cert. Saltillo 2 Apr 1791) · Savage abstract · 

NOOTKA / OPEN DOOR (heading reads *'Sobre prisiones'* — ⚠ mismatch; content is the fishing-vessel order): transmits Floridablanca's R.O. of 25 Sept [1790] *'sobre que no se hagan hostilidades á embarcaciones de pesca inglesas ú otras'* (no hostilities to English or other fishing vessels — the post-Nootka-Convention free-navigation rule), with the added caveat that this does NOT extend to those 'haciendo contrabando, ni introducir á los establecimientos que se intenten formar en el mar del Norte ó en el del Sur ó Pacífico á la parte interior de las costas.' ✅ verified scale 2. → THESIS-CRITICAL #3
