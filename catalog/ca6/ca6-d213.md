# The 5 English deserters to be HANDED OVER TO VANCOUVER (per the 22 Nov order) embarked on the fragata *Concepción*; the 

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n355–n356** · Sav p. XI:171–172 / 405–406 · 1794-05-16 · Savage abstract · 

The 5 English deserters to be HANDED OVER TO VANCOUVER (per the 22 Nov order) embarked on the fragata *Concepción*; the $388 3r in San Blas costs to be recovered from the establishments when Vancouver arrives, else sent to Nootka with the charges, or given to any English ship — recovering it 'con prudencia y sin alteraciones que turben la armonía'; + notice that another Commissioner will come from London this year to settle the Nootka affairs
