# Pedro Amador — Expediente de Servicios: his petition to credit the 8 yrs 4 mos he served at Loreto (soldado/cabo/sargent

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n85–n87** · Sav p. 84–86 / 295–300 · 1773-12-11 / 1791-05-15 · Savage abstract · 

Pedro Amador — Expediente de Servicios: his petition to credit the 8 yrs 4 mos he served at Loreto (soldado/cabo/sargento; separated 11 Dec 1773 for having married); Fages's margin-deferral (SF 13 May 1791, 'vaya á donde le convenga'); the 1773 Loreto passport from Felipe Barry (sargento mayor de Caballería, Intendente RH y Comandante de la Península de California); and his ⭐⭐ 1791 filiación (cert. Sal): native of *Tecuala*, son of Juan Amador & María Josefa de Carpio, enlisted Apr 1764, separated from Loreto 1784 (order of Ayudante Inspector Nicolás Soler), ascended sargento in the SF company 20 Jul 1787
