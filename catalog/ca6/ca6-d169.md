# Floridablanca no-hostility order, THIRD copy (reinforces THESIS-CRITICAL #3): no hostilities to English fishing/other ve

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n223–n224** · Sav p. XI:39–40 / 159–160 · 1791-09-06 / 09-07 · Savage abstract · 

Floridablanca no-hostility order, THIRD copy (reinforces THESIS-CRITICAL #3): no hostilities to English fishing/other vessels; but if establishments are found, recognize them and 'sujetarlos á la última convención' [the Nootka Convention], maintaining precautions on the sites & establishments (pp.159–160); + ⭐⭐ Tte. de Fragata Francisco Antonio Mourelle, commanding the goleta *Mexicana*, on 'una comisión interesante del servicio en la Costa de Californias' — give him aid (the 1792 Sutil y Mexicana expedition, p.160)
