# Labor-coercion dossier (Baja): 3 workers report (via Tte. Diego González) that, transferred to work in the houses of the

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n51–n52** · Sav p. 51–52 / 205–209 · 1789-01-01 / 02-14 · Savage abstract · 

Labor-coercion dossier (Baja): 3 workers report (via Tte. Diego González) that, transferred to work in the houses of the families assisting the troop, they were imprisoned — they argue working for the soldiers 'no presta al Rey ningún servicio' (p.205); + Arrillaga on the custom that soldiers build new missions (defense works, guard, stores, church, friar's house), and the founding of Mission San Miguel: begun 1787 at the paraje del Encino, moved ~3 leagues to the Arroyo de San Juan Bautista where it stands; asks what to do re the soldiers imprisoned by the Comte. de San Diego for refusing to work in the escort-families' private houses (pp.206–209)
