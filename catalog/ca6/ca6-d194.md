# The King relieves the Conde de Aranda of the interim first Secretaryship of the Despacho (giving him propietario honors 

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n278** · Sav p. XI:94 · tomo p. (1792-11-24) · 1792-11-24 · Savage abstract · 

The King relieves the Conde de Aranda of the interim first Secretaryship of the Despacho (giving him propietario honors + Dean of the Consejo de Estado) and names the Duque de Alcudia [Manuel Godoy] to succeed him — the rise of Godoy (cont.)
