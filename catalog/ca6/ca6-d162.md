# LAND-GRANT POLICY: transmits the order (Cmdte. Gral. → Romeu) that Presidio Captains may henceforth grant *solares y tie

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n211–n212** · Sav p. XI:27–28 / 110–111 · 1792-06-03 · Savage abstract · 

LAND-GRANT POLICY: transmits the order (Cmdte. Gral. → Romeu) that Presidio Captains may henceforth grant *solares y tierras* to soldiers & vecinos who ask to settle (despite Art. 81 of the Ordenanza de Intendentes) — within 4 *leguas comunes* measured from the center of the presidio plaza, and no more; for the new poblaciones to be founded. [Californio land-tenure origins] + a Castilla-titles cédula begun (DUP, cont.)
