# ⚠ BOUNDED Ortega 1793 Loreto routine (Sept–Dec): ⭐ the Sgt. Luis López $2,511 3r embezzlement of Real Hacienda (cigars/a

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n306–n344** · Sav p. XI:122–160 · tomo p. 282–387 · 1793-09 to 12 · Savage abstract · 

⚠ BOUNDED Ortega 1793 Loreto routine (Sept–Dec): ⭐ the Sgt. Luis López $2,511 3r embezzlement of Real Hacienda (cigars/aguardiente exchanges; he 'se decidió en no llevar apuntes'; sworn declaration before Ortega, n310); ⭐ Baja food shortages (the supply barco not arriving; ~200 fanegas owed to the missions; the *Guaymón*/*Loretana* runs, n320); the Real-quinto grace to *armadores* suspended pending order (n335); ⭐ timber from San Blas + a skilled ribera-carpenter to repair the wrecked 'Goleta perdida' — the copyist notes '= la Princesa' (so Doc 187's shipwreck = the *Princesa*; n335)
