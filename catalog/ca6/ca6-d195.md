# = THESIS-CRITICAL #1 (verified verbatim in the audit): (a) the 1793-08-11 order to HAND the 5 deserters over to Vancouve

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n279–n281** · Sav p. XI:95–97 · tomo p. 260–263 · 1793-08-11 + s.f. · Savage abstract · 

= THESIS-CRITICAL #1 (verified verbatim in the audit): (a) the 1793-08-11 order to HAND the 5 deserters over to Vancouver 'en uso de la buena armonía,' charged to the *expedición de Límites* (n279–280, refs Bodega y Quadra); (b) the 'Reservadísima' (sin fecha; copyist attributes it to Revillagigedo→Arrillaga ~Aug 1793) — foreign ships excluded, but aid OFFERED to Vancouver at the main port and DENIED only at the Baja pueblos (the Vancouver paragraph probably appended by Arrillaga); names Vancouver, Peter Puget, la 'Descubierta' [Discovery] & 'Chatam' [Chatham]
