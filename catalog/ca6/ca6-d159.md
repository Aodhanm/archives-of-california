# Sal went to Monterey to rectify the 1790 tobacco accounts associated with 'Dn José Fran. de Ortega'; my first reading to

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n208** · Sav p. XI:24 · tomo p. 96–97 · 1792-05-01 / 07-09 · Savage abstract · 

Sal went to Monterey to rectify the 1790 tobacco accounts associated with 'Dn José Fran. de Ortega'; my first reading took 'cuya muerte tuvo la desgracia de presenciar' as Ortega's death — ⚠️ NOW KNOWN WRONG (Doc 180): Ortega was alive & commanding Loreto in 1793. The death Sal witnessed was someone else / a misread antecedent; do not cite an Ortega death here (p.96); + Argüello forwards San Blas-Comisario oficios opened by José Sandoval (p.97)
