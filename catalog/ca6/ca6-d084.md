# R.O. on habilitado insolvency (*quiebra*): arrest the defaulting habilitado (2 r/day), purify the shortfall and charge i

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n77** · Sav p. 76 / 272–274 · 1791-09-20 · Savage abstract · 

R.O. on habilitado insolvency (*quiebra*): arrest the defaulting habilitado (2 r/day), purify the shortfall and charge it *prorrata* among officers/chaplain/troop, embargo his goods → Caja del fondo de gratificación; publish before the companies every 3 months
