# Will publish the Royal Order that frees the *Comercio de pieles de Nutria* (sea-otter pelts) to private individuals (p.1

**C-A 6 (Provincial State Papers), scan n38** · Sav p. 38 · tomo p. 140–143 · 1791-03-21 · Savage abstract · 

Will publish the Royal Order that frees the *Comercio de pieles de Nutria* (sea-otter pelts) to private individuals (p.141) — a 1791 liberalization of the otter trade (cf. the 1787 Vasadre monopoly *bando*); + received the order on the uniform for retired officers (p.143)
