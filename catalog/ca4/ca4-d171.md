# 'Precios de granos, arancel nuevo' — Fages, informed of each pueblo's & mission's grain harvest, for the common benefit 

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n171–n173** · Sav p. 172–174 · tomo p. 535+ · 1787-01-02 (San Gabriel) · Savage abstract · 

'Precios de granos, arancel nuevo' — Fages, informed of each pueblo's & mission's grain harvest, for the common benefit of growers & consumers varies the 1781 price *arancel* effective 1 Jan 1787 (a 1787 price arancel, distinct from the 1788 arancel @n220). Orig. p.535+.
