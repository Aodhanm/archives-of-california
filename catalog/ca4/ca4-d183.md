# 'Reglamento de precios' — Fages (Gob. y Comandante Inspector), for the economic good of the establishments of Monterey, 

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n220–n221** · Sav p. 220–221 · tomo p. 33, 36, 103–106 · 1788-01-02 · Savage abstract · 

'Reglamento de precios' — Fages (Gob. y Comandante Inspector), for the economic good of the establishments of Monterey, San Diego, San Francisco & Sta Bárbara & their pueblos/missions, alters the price *arancel* hitherto in force for livestock, produce, seeds & fruits. ✎ the actual arancel price table (@n221): toro 2 yr 3 p, toro 3–4 yr / vaca de rodeo / novillo 4 p, buey de yugo / vaca ochichigua 5 p, ternero 1 p 4 r; arroba tasajo 6 r, sebo machacado 1 p 2 r, manteca de vaca 2 p 4 r, velas de sebo 2 p 4 r, manteca de cerdo 3 p; cerdo 2 yr 3 p; carnero >2 yr 2 p; arroba lana sucia 1 p 2 r / limpia 2 p. (Preceded @n220 by a note that Sal is prevented from concluding 2 account operations reclaimed by the Tribunal de Cuentas.) Orig. pp.33, 36.
