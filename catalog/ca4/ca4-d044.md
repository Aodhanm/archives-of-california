# The founding of Mission San Miguel [de la Frontera] (28 Mar 1787, at 'el Príncipe') — the 4th mission to fill the gap be

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n40** · Sav p. 41 · tomo p. 93 · 1787-06-20 · Savage abstract · 

The founding of Mission San Miguel [de la Frontera] (28 Mar 1787, at 'el Príncipe') — the 4th mission to fill the gap between the *antigua California* (Baja) frontier and the San Diego presidio; orders the Viceroy to release the 1,000 pesos the Reglamento assigns each new mission. Orig. p.93.
