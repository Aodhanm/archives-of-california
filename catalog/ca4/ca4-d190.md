# Purísima construction (Cota, 24 Ago): the mission *cuadro* 'cerrado' + a rebellín, houses done by mid-Sept, 5 families a

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n272** · Sav p. 272 · tomo p. 256–260 · 1788-08-24 / 10-01 · Savage abstract · 

Purísima construction (Cota, 24 Ago): the mission *cuadro* 'cerrado' + a rebellín, houses done by mid-Sept, 5 families arrived + 2 more coming — 'La Misión ya tiene 79 cristianos.' ⭐⭐ Goycoechea (1 Oct) reports the return of Esteban Martínez from 'la Expedición del Norte' on the fragata *Princesa* (the 1788 Martínez–Haro Alaska voyage — the one that found the Russians, precursor to the 1789 Nootka crisis); + a fragata anchored at SF on the 13th under Jph de Guiriarza[?] w/ the presidio *memorias*, mission *sínodos* & 8 servants. ✎ the volume's one NW-coast datapoint (Russia-driver/Bodega-precursor; no Nootka-*crisis* material — that = C-A 5). Orig. pp.256–260.
