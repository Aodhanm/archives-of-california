# 'Justicia en San José' (@n134, 17 Oct): Soler went to San José to hear the pobladores' complaints against the escort cab

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n131–n133** · Sav p. 132–134 · tomo p. 391–401 · 1787-07→12 · Savage abstract · 

'Justicia en San José' (@n134, 17 Oct): Soler went to San José to hear the pobladores' complaints against the escort cabo Ignacio Vallejo (M.G. Vallejo's father) — found he kept 'relación criminal con una mujer casada' & ran 'un mando insípido,' and proposes he be put to *trabajos personales*. (Same leaf: an undated homicide *diligencia* vs. Rafael Morrón for killing Manuel Duarte → Asesor Galindo Navarro.) + routine: remisión de oficios; the 3,500-p. imposición for Soler's wife; Hacienda accounts, alférez Laso, Argüello. Orig. pp.391–411.
