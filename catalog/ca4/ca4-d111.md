# *Cristiano huido* — a fugitive Christian (neophyte) damaging the sierra, sheltered in a ranchería 3+ days from Mission S

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n73** · Sav p. 74 · tomo p. 210–211 · 1787-09-10 · Savage abstract · 

*Cristiano huido* — a fugitive Christian (neophyte) damaging the sierra, sheltered in a ranchería 3+ days from Mission San Buenaventura; Goycoechea hasn't sent to seize him (that ranchería was the one sargento Cota visited during the *registro* two years back) and fears the Indians will defend him; asks instructions. Orig. pp.210–211.
