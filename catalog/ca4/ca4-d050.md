The founding of Mission Santa Bárbara (Dec 1786): acknowledges Fages's oficios (1 & 16 Dec) that he left Monterey for San Diego, found no trouble at Sta Bárbara, and verified the founding of Mission Santa Bárbara. Orig. p.106.
