# The 8 July 1787 royal decree dividing the Ministry of the Indies into two secretarías — (1) Gracia y Justicia + ecclesia

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n16–n29** · Sav p. 17–30 · tomo p. 45–58 · 1787-07-08 · Savage abstract · 

The 8 July 1787 royal decree dividing the Ministry of the Indies into two secretarías — (1) Gracia y Justicia + ecclesiastical matters (graces, titles, tribunals of civil/criminal justice, church affairs — *misiones, doctrinas, regulares,* Jesuit *temporalidades,* dioceses, the Patronato universal de Indias) under Antonio Porlier (+ Filipinas); (2) Guerra, Hacienda, Comercio y Navegación (military, fortification, defense, appointment of viceroys/governors/intendentes, consulados, contaduría, mints, contraband per the 30 Jan 1786 Reglamento, superintendencia de Hacienda + Almadén/minas/azogues, the cannon-foundry at La Cabada, the Colegios de San Telmo & pilots' schools, the Matrículas de Indias/guarda-costas, the Junta de Estado) under Antonio Valdés (also Marina, interim). Includes the clarifying articles routing ship Patentes Reales & merchant-vessel construction/navigation through Marina. A major Bourbon administrative reform, printed at the Imprenta de S. Martín; ends n29 'Es copia de los originales. Valdés, Porlier.' Orig. pp.45–58.
