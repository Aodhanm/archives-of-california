# Requests a report on measures to supply the Peninsula with salt — per Viceroy Gálvez's 6 Sep 1785 order, San Blas supply

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n11–n12** · Sav p. 12–13 · tomo p. 32–36 · 1787-01-11 · Savage abstract · 

Requests a report on measures to supply the Peninsula with salt — per Viceroy Gálvez's 6 Sep 1785 order, San Blas supply-ships to Monterey & Loreto to return loaded with salt to replace the shortfall from the Zapotillo salinas (whose use was harming the mining interest). Orig. pp.32–36.
