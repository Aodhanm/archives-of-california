# Sal defends himself against a charged *quiebra* (shortfall) in his habilitado accounts — Ayte. Insp. Soler's audit (Jan 

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n60–n61** · Sav p. 61–62 · tomo p. 168–170 · 1787-08-07 · verbatim transcription · 

Sal defends himself against a charged *quiebra* (shortfall) in his habilitado accounts — Ayte. Insp. Soler's audit (Jan 1782–Dec 1786) finds him short 2,633 p. 4 r. 6 g. in the Gratificación & Retención funds; Sal disputes the accounting method, insists the retention was properly carried, and offers a general account 1782–87 to clarify. Orig. pp.168–170.
