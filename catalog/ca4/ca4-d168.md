# Fages's comprehensive ~17-article comisionado/pueblo INSTRUCTION for the escort-cabo of the pueblo de Los Ángeles — dire

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n144–n159** · Sav p. 145–156 · tomo p. 148–156, 481–485 · 1787-01-13 (San Gabriel) · verbatim transcription · 

Fages's comprehensive ~17-article comisionado/pueblo INSTRUCTION for the escort-cabo of the pueblo de Los Ángeles — directing the Alcalde y Regidores: art.1 art.24 of the *Reglamento* on the pueblo, art.2 Feliz to execute the works/faenas, arts. on the *repartición de solares* (art.7), everyone to have house & arms per the Reglamento (art.8), the caballada-guard custodias (art.9), and ⭐ art. 16 'Disposiciones para seguridad contra los Indios' — rancherías near the pueblo may not be permitted within the town's territory; the comisionado & alcalde to keep strict watch over the Indians; art.17 the comisionado's & alcalde's responsibility for observance. (⚠ an ink-blot obscures part of n150.) Orig. pp.148–156. Companion to Doc 169.
