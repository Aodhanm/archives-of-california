# 'Cristianos huidos en la Ranchería de Calandarraca. Paso prudente para la captura del capitanejo de allí.' Over damage t

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n141** · Sav p. 142 · tomo p. 434–436 · 1787-04-10 · Savage abstract · 

'Cristianos huidos en la Ranchería de Calandarraca. Paso prudente para la captura del capitanejo de allí.' Over damage to livestock by gentiles + 'otra banda del río,' the SF-company cabo held a rodeo (took 3–4 prisoners); then Soler dispatched the SF-company cabo with 8 men to Ranchería Calandarraca (guided by the imprisoned Indians) to seize the capitanejo — instructed to warn, not harm, unless the fugitive Christians resisted; the party returned augmented by 7 Carmel-mission Indians + 5 gentiles; 5 Christians presented themselves voluntarily at the mission. Orig. pp.434–436.
