# Royal order on the California missions. The Bishop of Sonora Fr. Antonio de los Reyes & Cmdt-Gen Felipe de Neve having p

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n12–n14** · Sav p. 13–15 · tomo p. 24–25 · 1787-09-09 (Pardo 20 Mar 1787) · Savage abstract · 

Royal order on the California missions. The Bishop of Sonora Fr. Antonio de los Reyes & Cmdt-Gen Felipe de Neve having presented (26 Jan & 3 Apr) papers on erecting a custodia & arranging the California missions: the King orders that, the San Fernando (Mexico) Franciscans sufficing for the current Monterey missions, new ones be staffed likewise (or drawn from the Michoacán province of the same order pending the custodio); and that governors report every 2–3 years on the missions' spiritual progress. A document of the California-custodia controversy. Orig. pp.24–25.
