# ⏹ LAST LEAF OF THE VOLUME (n321, orig p.410). 'Aviso de una deuda. Libranzas' — Fr. José Lafuente (missioner of Mis. San

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n321** · Sav p. 321 · tomo p. 410 · 1788-07-29 · Savage abstract · 

⏹ LAST LEAF OF THE VOLUME (n321, orig p.410). 'Aviso de una deuda. Libranzas' — Fr. José Lafuente (missioner of Mis. San José del Cabo) notifies that Sgt. Pedro Amador owes 18 p 5 r for various supplies; encloses the adjunct *libranzas* of soldier Salvador Higuera, asking Fages influence their collection. ⚠️ NO 'Fin del tomo VIII' colophon — the volume ends mid-run here; Tomo VIII is mis-bound out of chronological order (Dec 1788 material appears earlier @n310). Orig. p.410.
