# Fuller Lapérouse-hospitality record (Fages's oficio #354, 28 Sep): the French frigates *Brújula* (Boussole) & *Astrolabi

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n42** · Sav p. 43 · tomo p. 104–105 · 1787-04-12 · Savage abstract · 

Fuller Lapérouse-hospitality record (Fages's oficio #354, 28 Sep): the French frigates *Brújula* (Boussole) & *Astrolabio* (Astrolabe), 'destinadas á investigar el globo' under la Pérouse, took port at Monterey the 15th [Sept 1786], departed the 24th after Fages supplied provisions per the royal order la Pérouse carried to the Viceroy; forwarded to S.M.; Loyola commends Fages's attention to the officers. Orig. pp.104–105.
