# The cédula on divorce-case jurisdiction: to the viceroys/audiencias/governors of the Indies — ecclesiastical judges are 

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n34** · Sav p. 35 · tomo p. 72–77 · 1787-03-22 · Savage abstract · 

The cédula on divorce-case jurisdiction: to the viceroys/audiencias/governors of the Indies — ecclesiastical judges are to hear only the divorce itself, while secular magistrates handle the temporal aspects (alimony, litis-expenses, dowry). Cert. copy Arizpe 13 Sep 1787 (Garcés & Durán). Orig. pp.72–77.
