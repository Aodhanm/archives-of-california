# Mission Santa Bárbara construction + the peaceful Chumash. Fr. Cristóbal's travel to relieve the SLO friars; timber-cutt

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n58–n59** · Sav p. 59–60 · tomo p. 164–165 · 1787-06-27 · Savage abstract · 

Mission Santa Bárbara construction + the peaceful Chumash. Fr. Cristóbal's travel to relieve the SLO friars; timber-cutting & plastering the walls ('6 frente… para su mayor resguardo'); 27 baptized; ⚠️ they stopped soliciting gentiles ('no solicitamos ya Gentiles, solo se reciben los que voluntariamente se ofrecen al bautismo… no hay proporción para mantenerlos'); and 'la Gentilidad se halla muy sosegada.' A rich founding-era mission-construction + Indian-relations letter from the Bodega article's key officer. Orig. pp.164–165.
