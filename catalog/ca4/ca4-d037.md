# Anti-gambling bando (16 articles). Prohibits the games albures, banca, quince, treinta, dados & other *envite/suerte/aza

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n34–n38** · Sav p. 35–39 · tomo p. 76–81 · 1773-02-15 (renewed 1787-07-18) · Savage abstract · 

Anti-gambling bando (16 articles). Prohibits the games albures, banca, quince, treinta, dados & other *envite/suerte/azar* games; penalties escalate by offense — nobles/public officials 200 p. fine, commoners jail → 5 yrs presidio/frontier for the incorrigible; permits only naipes de Castilla, truco, villar where no *envite* (capped at 1 real/person/day); bars gaming in public & private houses; enforced by the Real Sala del Crimen. A vivid social-history / law-and-morals document. Orig. pp.76–81.
