# Reforms the deserter-capture reward — abolishes the abused '2 years' service credit per denunciation/capture,' substitut

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n52–n53** · Sav p. 53–54 · tomo p. 139–142 · 1787-01-30 → 1787-06-19 · Savage abstract · 

Reforms the deserter-capture reward — abolishes the abused '2 years' service credit per denunciation/capture,' substituting a silver premium; retirement now requires 20 actual years' service (28 for alférez). Orig. pp.139–142.
