# Clarifies that horse-herd guards ('Postas') are true sentinels (guarding against surprise/enemy), unlike soldiers merely

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n51–n52** · Sav p. 52–53 · tomo p. 137–138 · 1787-05-26 · Savage abstract · 

Clarifies that horse-herd guards ('Postas') are true sentinels (guarding against surprise/enemy), unlike soldiers merely rounding/tending the *caballada*. Orig. pp.137–138.
