# 'Lujo en los soldados' — a general order reprehending soldiers' luxury, specifically 'ropas de la China y pieles de Nutr

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n112** · Sav p. 112 · tomo p. 310–312 · 1787-03-03 · verbatim transcription · 

'Lujo en los soldados' — a general order reprehending soldiers' luxury, specifically 'ropas de la China y pieles de Nutria' (China cloth & otter skins); the soldier should keep his family, arms & powder instead. A garrison-second-economy datapoint (soldiers keeping the otter pelts owed the monopoly). Orig. pp.310–312.
