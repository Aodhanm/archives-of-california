# Ruling that the *alcances/haberes* of deceased soldiers be applied to masses for their souls via the company chaplain — 

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n232** · Sav p. 232 · tomo p. 139–141 · 1787-11-26 · Savage abstract · 

Ruling that the *alcances/haberes* of deceased soldiers be applied to masses for their souls via the company chaplain — 'la limosna de un peso tasada en los aranceles de esta diócesis,' the surplus masses distributed among presidio chaplains at moderate alms. Orig. pp.139–141.
