# The Lapérouse visit. Acknowledges Fages's report that the French frigates *Brújula* (Boussole) & *Astrolabio* (Astrolabe

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n6** · Sav p. 7 · tomo p. 16–17 · 1787-01-16 · Savage abstract · 

The Lapérouse visit. Acknowledges Fages's report that the French frigates *Brújula* (Boussole) & *Astrolabio* (Astrolabe), under the Conde de la Pérouse, anchored at Monterey — Fages 'le ha franqueado los recursos necesarios' (gave them the needed supplies); the viceroy received Fages's account of their voyage, commission & personnel. The first foreign expedition to Alta California (Sept 1786) from the viceregal side. Orig. pp.16–17.
