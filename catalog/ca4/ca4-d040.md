# Renews the prohibition on buying goods from the Filipinos (Manila-galleon China-goods); on the asesor's dictamen, keeps 

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n38–n39** · Sav p. 39–40 · tomo p. 86–87 · 1787-06-23 · Savage abstract · 

Renews the prohibition on buying goods from the Filipinos (Manila-galleon China-goods); on the asesor's dictamen, keeps in force the prior bandos barring purchase from the *Nao de Filipinas* — over Arrillaga (Loreto)'s request for a permit for the troops/settlers. Orig. pp.86–87.
