# Two docs: (a) 19 Jul, distributed by lot the 10 potros received for the company remount (5 p each; 1 drowned, 1 injured)

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n320** · Sav p. 320 · tomo p. 135, 408–409 · 1788-07-19 / 10-15 · Savage abstract · 

Two docs: (a) 19 Jul, distributed by lot the 10 potros received for the company remount (5 p each; 1 drowned, 1 injured); (b) 15 Oct, 'sobre deudas de soldados' — will charge Sgt. Pedro Amador 18 p 5 r to satisfy his debt at San José del Cabo, likewise soldier Salvador Higuera 16 p per his libranza, returning Capt. Arrillaga's oficio. Orig. pp.408–409.
