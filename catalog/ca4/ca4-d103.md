# 'Crueldad contra Indios.' At the Ranchería de la Cepeda[?], a soldier party (the presidiario González's) — over soldier 

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n70** · Sav p. 71 · tomo p. 199 · 1787-10-30 · verbatim transcription · 

'Crueldad contra Indios.' At the Ranchería de la Cepeda[?], a soldier party (the presidiario González's) — over soldier Diez[?]/Gutiérrez's pursuit of an India — wounded her in two or three places and injured an Indian man by throwing him off a cliff (*desbarrancándolo*); Goycoechea sent to bring in those gentiles. Orig. p.199.
