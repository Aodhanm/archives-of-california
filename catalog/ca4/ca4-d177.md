# 'Acopio de Pieles de Nutria' — Ugarte acknowledges the establishment in California of 'el acopio de Pieles de Nutria de 

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n192** · Sav p. 192 · tomo p. 28 · 1788-01-14 · Savage abstract · 

'Acopio de Pieles de Nutria' — Ugarte acknowledges the establishment in California of 'el acopio de Pieles de Nutria de cuenta de S.M.' (per the R. Audiencia's 8 Mar order + the Intendant of Army & Hacienda's providencia + Fages's compliance). + ⭐⭐ 1788-08-09 Chihuahua, 'Condena de un oficial por desfalco' — Ugarte, per the Auditor de Guerra, condemns alférez Ramón Laso de la Vega to loss of employment, unfit for royal service, arrested on 2 r/day (the officer-embezzlement conviction). Orig. p.28+.
