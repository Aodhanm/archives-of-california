# Annual supply-list for the Baja frontier troops (what the Baja-Cal. governor requests via Tte. Col. Pablo Vicente [de So

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n97–n98** · Sav p. 98–99 · tomo p. 267 · 1787 · Savage abstract · 

Annual supply-list for the Baja frontier troops (what the Baja-Cal. governor requests via Tte. Col. Pablo Vicente [de Solá?]): 12 saddles, 12 pairs riding-arms, 12 shotgun-cases, 30 *frazadas cameras* + 30 half + 90 *pastoras*, 300 p. of soap, 100 mules, 10 pack-saddles, 10 loads of *vaqueta* sacks — shippable via San Diego. A vivid frontier-logistics inventory. Orig. p.267.
