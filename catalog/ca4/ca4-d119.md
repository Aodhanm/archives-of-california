# Fort-building + anti-Indian stratagems (Baja/San Miguel frontier). Ortega reports (from San Miguel, to 29 May) building 

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n78–n80** · Sav p. 79–81 · tomo p. 224–226 · 1787-06-05 · verbatim transcription · 

Fort-building + anti-Indian stratagems (Baja/San Miguel frontier). Ortega reports (from San Miguel, to 29 May) building a 60×50-vara adobe stockade (4 bastioned gates) against a reported large Indian gathering 'para atacar la tropa'; daily patrols; captives sent to warn that peace is refused — the troop 'decidida á poblarles el campo… con Indios muertos para que Coyotes y Pájaros los coman'; raids on the Ensenada/Tigre/Carmen rancherías dispersed the threat; 2 children baptized. Orig. pp.224–226.
