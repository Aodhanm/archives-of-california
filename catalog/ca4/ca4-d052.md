# Command-structure change: on the death of the Viceroy Conde de Gálvez, the Comandancia General de Provincias Internas is

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n43–n45** · Sav p. 44–46 · tomo p. 108–110 · 1787-02-10 · Savage abstract · 

Command-structure change: on the death of the Viceroy Conde de Gálvez, the Comandancia General de Provincias Internas is reintegrated (independent again) — its temporary subordination to the Viceroyalty of Mexico had been *personal* to Gálvez (as it stood under de Croix & Neve); Fages to report to the Comandancia again, omitting consultas on gubernativo/militar/económico matters covered by the Reglamento. Orig. pp.108–110.
