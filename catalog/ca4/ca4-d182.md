# 'Instrucciones que debe seguir durante su ausencia' (a 2nd Fages governance instruction, for while he tours the southern

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n218** · Sav p. 218 · tomo p. 33 · 1788-03-16 · verbatim transcription · 

'Instrucciones que debe seguir durante su ausencia' (a 2nd Fages governance instruction, for while he tours the southern presidios): monthly correspondence by the 4th; ⭐ Indian-punishment provision — Christian or Gentile Indians caught in excesses are sentenced to 'corte de tule' (tule-cutting), time graduated to the culpa, unless grave enough to need Fages's review; the off-guard soldiers share the tule-cutting; cattle/hide/tallow management (economize the *untos*, store hides folded). Orig. p.33.
