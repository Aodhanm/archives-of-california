# The new Viceroy [Manuel Antonio] Flores retains the same authority over the Comandancia General de Provincias Internas a

**C-A 4 (Provincial State Papers), scan n31** · Sav p. 32 · tomo p. 66 · 1787-09-17 · Savage abstract · 

The new Viceroy [Manuel Antonio] Flores retains the same authority over the Comandancia General de Provincias Internas as his predecessor (per the Marqués de Sonora / Gálvez communication 20 Mar 1787; the King approved Gálvez's *Instrucción* for the frontier's defense). Orig. p.66.
