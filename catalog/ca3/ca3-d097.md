# The 1784 Santa Bárbara presidio armament & munitions inventory: Artillery = 2 bronze 4-pounders on carriages; 46 muskets

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n96–n98** · Sav p. 97–99 · tomo p. v.197–198 · 1784-04-27 · Savage abstract · 

The 1784 Santa Bárbara presidio armament & munitions inventory: Artillery = 2 bronze 4-pounders on carriages; 46 muskets (from 1780) + 12 short (1775); 40+17 pairs of pistols; 46 swords; defensive: 40 *cueras*, 60 *adargas*, 60 cartridge-boxes, 30 shields; munitions (cannon/musket cartridges, 8 arrobas powder, 173 arrobas lead, ~2,400 flints); 89-man company. → artillery register
