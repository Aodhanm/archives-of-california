# The documents a military officer must present to obtain permission to marry: (1) his commission copy (authorized by the 

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n412–n414** · Sav p. 406–408 · tomo p. vi.307–309 · 1786-09-04 · Savage abstract · 

The documents a military officer must present to obtain permission to marry: (1) his commission copy (authorized by the Contaduría); (2–3) both parties' baptism certificates; (4–5) paternal consent of both; (6) an instrument proving the bride's nobility/hidalguía/*calidad*; (7–8) dowry documentation (land/property or cash in judicial deposit); + Notas (legalized certs; the Cmdt-Gen may dispense *calidad*/dowry; all in triplicate)
