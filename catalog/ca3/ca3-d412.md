# Approves founding the Mission of La Concepción (= La Purísima Concepción): Fages having reconnoitered the Santa Bárbara 

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n372–n374** · Sav p. 369–371 · tomo p. vi.244–245 · 1786-03-24 · Savage abstract · 

Approves founding the Mission of La Concepción (= La Purísima Concepción): Fages having reconnoitered the Santa Bárbara Channel (Of. 2 Jun) found no better site than Santa Rosa de la Gaviota (a 10–12-league saving to the presidio); Rengel orders him to proceed to found it after re-examining Sta Rosa de la Gaviota & neighbors, then request the 1,000-peso Reglamento *fomento*
