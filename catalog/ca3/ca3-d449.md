# Instructions for the *repartimiento de tierras* (land distribution) at the Pueblo of Los Ángeles + tithe collection: Arg

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n416–n418** · Sav p. 409–411 · tomo p. vi.310–312 · 1786-08-14 · Savage abstract · 

Instructions for the *repartimiento de tierras* (land distribution) at the Pueblo of Los Ángeles + tithe collection: Argüello commissioned (by an attached *Mandamiento*) to distribute the LA lands per the plan he drew when the pueblo was erected, following the San José precedent (⚠ Savage writes 'á lo practicado en el Presidio de San José' [sic — San José was a *pueblo*; full-res crop, 2026-07-02 verify pass]), sending 'testimonio separado de cada una de las diligencias relativas á cada individuo… para servir de título á cada interesado' (✓ verbatim RE-verified on scan n416 at full res, 2026-07-02 verify pass — each settler's approved testimony to serve as his land TITLE); + open a tithe register per vecino (the *diezmo* formula on grain/cattle/wool/cheese after the 5-yr exemption; Cabo Vicente Félix collecting; the 'nuevo diezmo' of maize per poblador to the Real Hacienda)
