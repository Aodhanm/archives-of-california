# ✓ VERIFIED vs scans n468–n469 (2026-07-06). Viáticos for the San Fernando College Missionaries bound for the [Baja] miss

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n468–n469** · Sav p. 461–462 · tomo p. vi.421–422 · 1786-12-07 · Savage abstract · 

✓ VERIFIED vs scans n468–n469 (2026-07-06). Viáticos for the San Fernando College Missionaries bound for the [Baja] missions — 200 pesos each overland to San Blas + 7⅗ reales/day by sea (from the Pious Fund); no friars sent/recalled without viceregal leave. ⭐ Note (verbatim, n469): 'No habiendo podido firmar el Exmo Señor Virrey Conde de Galvez por su grave enfermedad, y vecina muerte, esta carta… lo [suple] esta Real Audiencia en quien reside este Superior Gobierno' — signed by the three Oidores Simón Antonio de Mirafuentes, Eusebio Bentura Beleña, [Cosme] de Mier y Trespalacios: a datable primary trace of Bernardo de Gálvez's fatal illness (d. 30 Nov 1786; the correspondence to Fages shifts Viceroy→Audiencia here)
