# Viáticos for the San Fernando College Missionaries bound for the [Baja] missions — 200 pesos each overland to San Blas +

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n468–n469** · Sav p. 461–462 · tomo p. vi.421–422 · 1786-12-07 · Savage abstract · 

Viáticos for the San Fernando College Missionaries bound for the [Baja] missions — 200 pesos each overland to San Blas + 7⅗ reales/day by sea (from the Pious Fund); no friars sent/recalled without viceregal leave. ⚠ Note: Viceroy Conde de Gálvez [Bernardo de Gálvez] could not sign 'por su grave enfermedad y vecina muerte' — the Real Audiencia signed (Oidores Mirafuentes, Bentura Beleña, Mier y Trespalacios): documents Gálvez's fatal illness (d. 30 Nov 1786)
