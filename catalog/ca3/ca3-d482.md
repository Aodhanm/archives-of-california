# Keep the Chinese goods (*efectos de China*) stored & enforce the Royal prohibition on trading any merchandise with the t

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n448–n449** · Sav p. 441–442 · tomo p. vi.368–370 · 1786-06-01 · Savage abstract · 

Keep the Chinese goods (*efectos de China*) stored & enforce the Royal prohibition on trading any merchandise with the troop — noting the abuse that soldiers barter away their own rations/provisions to get otter pelts from the Indians (turning them into gala/painted goods), 'quebrantando… la orden Real que prohíbe este tráfico' (otter-coercion + the Chinese-goods thread, Docs 437/476)
