# On the pearl-fishery (*buceo de perlas*): acks the Asesor's 1777 pearl-lease dictamen; encloses a *quinto* (royal-fifth)

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n66–n68** · Sav p. 67–69 · tomo p. v.127–128 · 1784-01-08 · Savage abstract · 

On the pearl-fishery (*buceo de perlas*): acks the Asesor's 1777 pearl-lease dictamen; encloses a *quinto* (royal-fifth) production table for Loreto, 1774–1783 — by year & named armador (Antonio de Orio/Osio, Miguel Alvarado, Rafael Marrón, Luis & Manuel Padilla, Misión de Santiago, Gaspar Pisón, Joaquín Días Sayas, Juan Avilés, Juan Rodríguez), w/ canoe-counts & payments (1st quinquenio $920 + 2nd $1,159.4 = $2,079.4, plus pearl-in-kind: *aljófar* & *perla prieta*)
