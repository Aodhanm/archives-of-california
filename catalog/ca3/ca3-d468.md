# Ortega's full service record (seeking relief/retirement for age & 'grosura de cuerpo'): 30 yrs 8 mo in California since 

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n434–n439** · Sav p. 427–432 · tomo p. vi.344–350 · 1786-06-08 · Savage abstract · 

Ortega's full service record (seeking relief/retirement for age & 'grosura de cuerpo'): 30 yrs 8 mo in California since 1 Oct 1755 (soldier 10 mo → Cabo 2½ yr → Sergeant 14 yr 6 mo → Lt-Commandant 12 yr 9 mo); at San Diego founded Mission San Juan Capistrano & rebuilt the SD presidio; as Comandante/Habilitado of the Sta Bárbara Channel presidio under Neve founded the SB presidio & Mission San Buenaventura; then Comandante of the Baja frontiers at Loreto. ⭐⭐⭐ A first-person account of the 1769 Portolá 'Sacred Expedition' — Ortega the sergeant-explorer who guided the road (naming Alf. Pedro Fages ahead & Fernando de Rivera y Moncada, 25 soldiers, dep. 20 Mar 1769, reached SD 14 May), through 'miles de Gentiles' (held off 'á fuego de armas' at El Encino & the Puerto de San Diego); ~580 leagues of unknown country reckoned
