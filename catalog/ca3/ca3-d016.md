# Graduated penalty-scale for soldier theft (moderating Art. 70 of the military ordinances): death by hanging for theft ≥2

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n41–n43** · Sav p. 42–44 · tomo p. v.55–58 · 1784-06-07 · Savage abstract · 

Graduated penalty-scale for soldier theft (moderating Art. 70 of the military ordinances): death by hanging for theft ≥200 rs in barracks/officer's house; hanging for break-in/weapons (even <1 real); presidio/public-works for lesser sums; aggravated for highway/campaign theft
