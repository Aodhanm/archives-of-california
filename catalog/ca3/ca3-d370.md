# Certifies that the SB presidio wheat (from predecessor Ortega + the year's harvest) was used only as the gratification p

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n327–n328** · Sav p. 325–326 · tomo p. vi.142 · 1786-07-19 · Savage abstract · 

Certifies that the SB presidio wheat (from predecessor Ortega + the year's harvest) was used only as the gratification paid to the gentiles who helped with the presidio works — i.e., Indians were paid in wheat for their labor; signed also by Raymundo Carrillo & José Samaniego[?] (the *apoderados*)
