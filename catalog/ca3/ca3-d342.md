# The frigate *La Princesa* (Capt. *Esteban Martínez*) anchored at Santa Bárbara (17th), delivering 58 *varas* & 12 beams 

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n310** · Sav p. 308 · tomo p. vi.97–100 · 1786-10-21 · Savage abstract · 

The frigate *La Princesa* (Capt. *Esteban Martínez*) anchored at Santa Bárbara (17th), delivering 58 *varas* & 12 beams for the presidio; only 40 planks & 5 heavy beams could be landed by launch, the rest Monday, Capt. Martínez helping make adobes; the officers' houses being built (roofs already tiled)
