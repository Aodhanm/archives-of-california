# The wreck of the Macao ship 'San Francisco de Paula alias El Hombre' at the Bahía de San José del Cabo (3 Nov 1784): fro

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n134–n135** · Sav p. 135–136 · tomo p. v.277–278 · 1785-05-04 · Savage abstract · 

The wreck of the Macao ship 'San Francisco de Paula alias El Hombre' at the Bahía de San José del Cabo (3 Nov 1784): from Macao (East India) under the 'Conde de San Denis,' bound Macao→Callao (Lima), ran aground (broken rudder, 14 in. water/hr, small crew); given Spanish aid (*socorro*); the count then armed it as a corsair for the Lima merchants, sailed the Philippine coasts → E2
