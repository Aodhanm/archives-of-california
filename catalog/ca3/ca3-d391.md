# On punishing Christian & Gentile Indians 'with prudence': re Sgt. Ignacio Olivera's arrest of the instigators of the *Al

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n351–n353** · Sav p. 349–351 · tomo p. vi.195–197 · 1786-08-08 · Savage abstract · 

On punishing Christian & Gentile Indians 'with prudence': re Sgt. Ignacio Olivera's arrest of the instigators of the *Alboroto* at the Missions of San Gabriel (cf. Doc 321); invokes Teodoro de Croix's *Instrucción reservada* (Máximas 4–5) — punish crimes but avoid 'vueltas funestas'; weighs whipping/prison for the la Grulla & San Francisco Solano rancherías, the San Diego neophytes 'robando y causando daños,' and a la-Grulla gentile who killed a Christian of Mission San Vicente
