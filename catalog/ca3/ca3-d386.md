# Transmits a royal treatise (*Disertación*) on preserving the pueblos from SMALLPOX (*viruelas*) (ordered published via t

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n340–n343** · Sav p. 338–341 · tomo p. vi.173–175 · 1786-09-15 · Savage abstract · 

Transmits a royal treatise (*Disertación*) on preserving the pueblos from SMALLPOX (*viruelas*) (ordered published via the Marqués de Sonora, 8 Sept prior yr): a quarantine method — on an outbreak, remove the infected to a designated *ermita*/casa de campo away from the population (spread being 'por el contacto' only), per the Louisiana precedent; distribute printed copies via the parish priests & physicians; 30 printed *Ejemplares* enclosed — report results to the King
