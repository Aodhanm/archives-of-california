# THE FOUNDING VICEREGAL ORDER of the Vasadre sea-otter monopoly: per R.O. 2 Jun 1785, the Superior Gov. examined Vicente 

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n470–n472** · Sav p. 463–465 · tomo p. vi.426–427 · 1786-01-23 · Savage abstract · 

THE FOUNDING VICEREGAL ORDER of the Vasadre sea-otter monopoly: per R.O. 2 Jun 1785, the Superior Gov. examined Vicente Vasadre y Vega's project for 'un comercio recíproco con las Chinas cambiando Pieles de Nutrias y Lobos Marinos… por Azogues de la Asia' — and, per the Tribunal de Minería & Fernando Mangino (Superintendent-General of Azogues), resolved to establish it: Vasadre to sail to California on one of the San Blas presidio-supply ships 'que deben salir en Marzo' (✎ CORRECTED from 'May' — full-res crop, 2026-07-02 verify pass; the founding-order quote 'un comercio recíproco con las Chinas cambiando Pieles de Nutrias y Lobos Marinos… por Azogues de la Asia' ✓ re-verified verbatim on n471 the same pass), make sufficient pelt-*acopio*, then return; the pelts Fages obtains in the interim to go via the *Nao de Filipinas* (touching California late that year) to the Ministry of Acapulco; Fages to aid & protect Vasadre.
