# Point-by-point commentary on Adjutant Nicolás Soler's inspection report (Doc 4): troop reform (2 officers per presidio),

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n3–n11** · Sav p. 3–12 · tomo p. v.5–14 · 1787-11-08 · Savage abstract · 

Point-by-point commentary on Adjutant Nicolás Soler's inspection report (Doc 4): troop reform (2 officers per presidio), the *situado*-arrears problem, suppressing the Loreto captaincy, removing Tte Diego González from Monterey, reducing the SF company = 'dejar abandonadas á las Misiones'; the cattle-slaughter labor (⭐ must request Indians from Mission Carmelo for the SD/SB provisioning), the tithe on cattle, land-*mercedes* harming the Gentiles' reduced herds, discharged-soldiers (*licenciados*) settled in the pueblos with *solares*/*tierras*/livestock. Ends 'prescindiendo de la pasión que me pueda ocultar el Ayud.te Inspector Soler.'
