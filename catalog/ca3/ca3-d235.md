# DIARY of Gov. Fages's 1785 expedition — reconnaissance + PUNITIVE campaign — from San Diego across the Sierra to the mou

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n191–n212** · Sav p. 189–210 · tomo p. v.401–417 · 1785-04-27 · Savage abstract · 

DIARY of Gov. Fages's 1785 expedition — reconnaissance + PUNITIVE campaign — from San Diego across the Sierra to the mouth of the Colorado & back (~21 days; via the Anza-route landmarks Sta Olaya / Cerritos de San Pedro y San Pablo / the Yumas), reconnoitering the desert nations (Cocopás, Camillares, Guycomaes, Bagiopas, Cajenches, Yumas) — AND a firefight killing a desert Indian captain + interrogation of survivors re the killing of a soldier (who had pursued the runaway Hermenegildo Flores, Doc 64) + 3 Christians & an India of Mission San Diego → E3
