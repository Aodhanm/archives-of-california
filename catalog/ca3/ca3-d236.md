# Audit (*reparos*) of the Loreto presidio accounts (1781–82, habilitado Alférez José Mª Estrada) by the Real Tribunal de 

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n211–n220** · Sav p. 211–218 · tomo p. v.419–423 · 1785-06-08 · Savage abstract · 

Audit (*reparos*) of the Loreto presidio accounts (1781–82, habilitado Alférez José Mª Estrada) by the Real Tribunal de Cuentas (contadores Abad/Alegría/Campo Marín/Guerrero/Fagle; dir. Manuel Saviñón); the general inventory (Comisario Francisco Álvarez de Osío) valued $22,916 → $11,656 at cost (old-Reglamento 20%-uplift reduced to actual-Reglamento cost prices); Estrada's line-by-line reply (incl. *carenas de lanchas*); acknowledged by Fages at Monterey 28 Jan 1785, remitted via Arrillaga (San Vicente→Loreto) to habilitado Estrada; certified 'pp. 419 á 423' Loreto 8 Jun 1785 (witnesses Manuel de Vargas, José Estrella, Ortega, Osuna, A. Martínez, J. Castro). *Genre matches C-A 7's Branciforte-era Contaduría reviews.*
