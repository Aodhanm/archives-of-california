# The official sea-otter-pelt PRICE SCHEDULE (of the King's account; 'Tarifa de los precios á que por ahora han de satisfa

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n408** · Sav p. 403 · tomo p. vi.303 · 1786-08-31 · Savage abstract · 

The official sea-otter-pelt PRICE SCHEDULE (of the King's account; 'Tarifa de los precios á que por ahora han de satisfacerse de cuenta de S.M. las pieles de nutria'): 3 classes (1ª = piel de 1¼ vara; 2ª = de á vara; 3ª = de ¾) × negra/color × curtida/cruda, 4 price points each — 1ª: 10 / 9 / 8 / 7 pesos; 2ª: 8 / 7 / 6 / 5 pesos; 3ª: 5 / 4 / 3½ / 2½ pesos per pelt (order: negra-curtida, color-curtida, negra-cruda, color-cruda; the ½ = 4 reales) — the monopoly's paid prices (invaluable economic data; consistent w/ Canton resale ~$18¾, C-A 41 Cooper contract) → E6. ✎ CORRECTED 2026-07-02 verify pass (full-res table read): the old row read the 'Pesos' column as cents ($0.10…$0.02½ — off ×100) and '1¼ vara' as '≥1½'
