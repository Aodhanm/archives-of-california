# The otter-monopoly Comisionado's own letter: encloses 2 viceregal oficios; frames his commission as supplying quicksilve

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n401–n402** · Sav p. 397–398 · tomo p. vi.295–297 · 1786-08-27 · Savage abstract · 

The otter-monopoly Comisionado's own letter: encloses 2 viceregal oficios; frames his commission as supplying quicksilver (*Azogues*) to New Spain — 'Ramo Principal de la subsistencia de España' — via the pelt trade; fair advance prices to sellers, funds from the San Blas treasury; writes 'de la Fragata *Princesa* al ancla en la Bahía de Monterey' (confirms Doc 345)
