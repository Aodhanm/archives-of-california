# Approves founding the 4th Baja mission at *El Encino* — the terrain (per Fages's Of. 228, 4 May, w/ the Velásquez/Ortega

**C-A 3 (Provincial State Papers), scan n370–n372** · Sav p. 367–369 · tomo p. vi.241–243 · 1786-05-24 · Savage abstract · 

Approves founding the 4th Baja mission at *El Encino* — the terrain (per Fages's Of. 228, 4 May, w/ the Velásquez/Ortega docs 243–244) suitable for the 4th of 5 missions to link the Loreto→San Diego road; Fages already agreed with P. Presidente Fr. Miguel Hidalgo; +12 plazas to the SD presidio for its escort; the 5th mission to be at San Francisco Solano; request the 1,000-peso *fomento* from Mexico
