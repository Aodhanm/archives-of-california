TROOP DISTRIBUTION, San Diego. Between Presidio and Mission: Ten.te José Fran.co de Ortega, Sarg.to Mariano Carrillo, Cabo Mariano Verdugo. Soldiers: Juan Estevan Rocha, Joseph Raymundo Carrillo, Joseph Camacho + others. 23 men continued to Monterey (names include Ign.o Vallejo, Luis Lugo, Joseph Dominguez, Ign.o Alvarado, Nicolas Beltran, etc.).
