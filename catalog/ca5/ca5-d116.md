# San Diego fugitive-Indian / cattle-raid case: two fugitive Indians apprehended; confessed petty thefts in the gentile ra

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n214–n219** · Sav p. 212–217 / 367–377 · 1790-06-30 · Savage abstract · 

San Diego fugitive-Indian / cattle-raid case: two fugitive Indians apprehended; confessed petty thefts in the gentile rancherías + killing 5 cattle of Mission San Diego + taking 4 of the mission's cavalry horses; a party of 3 under Sgt. Raimundo Carrillo dispatched to keep the herds together & pursue; the two Indians manacled (one with a broken leg), held under the Santa Clara escort
