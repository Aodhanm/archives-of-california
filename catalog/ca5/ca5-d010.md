# 'Sobre nombramientos de sargentos' — the Capitán de Loreto may make sergeant-appointments in his company & present them 

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n52–n54** · Sav p. 52–54 / 54–55 (DUP 56–59) · 1789-07-11 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre nombramientos de sargentos' — the Capitán de Loreto may make sergeant-appointments in his company & present them to the Inspector; Ayudantes Inspectores verify aptitude first; for captain-less companies the Tenientes form the proposals. ⚠ Savage: this doc is REPEATED in the tomo — once cert. by Gasiot (Chihuahua 11 Jul 89), once by Fages (Monterey 7 Nov 89), pp.56–59
