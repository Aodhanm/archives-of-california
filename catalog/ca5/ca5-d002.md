# Instructions to observe during Fages's absence (travelling to Sta Bárbara): continue the livestock *rodeos*; ration the 

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n1–n2** · Sav p. 1–3 / 1–4 · 1789-04-14 [yr 9/2 MS-ambiguous] · Savage abstract · 

Instructions to observe during Fages's absence (travelling to Sta Bárbara): continue the livestock *rodeos*; ration the troop with meat from the Pueblos, noting values/qualities for the *matanza* (2–3,000 head); twice-weekly target practice (tiro al blanco) at the presidio chapel; keep clothing/arms inspected; dispatch to San Francisco promptly esp. when a ship arrives; livestock to the corral; no soldier, boy, or woman to take animals without consent
