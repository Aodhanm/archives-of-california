# Transmits a Viceroy's oficio of 20 Nov 1788 w/ orders re *subalterns' representations to the Superior Govt*: per the Con

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n2–n3** · Sav p. 3–4 · tomo p. 4–7 · 1789-03-18 · Savage abstract · 

Transmits a Viceroy's oficio of 20 Nov 1788 w/ orders re *subalterns' representations to the Superior Govt*: per the Conde de Gálvez's Decreto of 14 Jun 1788, Viceroy Flores orders all subalterns be made to understand that pretensions/intentions not routed through proper superior channels will be denied ('sin curso ni efecto'), under penalty; certified copy Monterey 8 Jul [1789] by Fages (half-signature)
