# 'Testimonio de las diligencias formadas á instancia del Defensor de Temporalidades D. José Mª Estrada' — a legal interro

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n7–n50** · Sav p. 7–50 · tomo p. **17–51** · 1789-08/09 (cert. 25 Sep) · Savage abstract · 

'Testimonio de las diligencias formadas á instancia del Defensor de Temporalidades D. José Mª Estrada' — a legal interrogatorio of witnesses re the *Fiscal de Hacienda*'s demand over the situado pesos for the Presidio de Loreto + the *Escuadra del Sur* + the King's communication ship (*barco del Rey*). The questions reconstruct Baja's presidial-fiscal history: Loreto's 30-plaza *dotación*; the ~1768 hand-over of mission direction to the Regulars [post-Jesuit]; the Escuadra del Sur at San José del Cabo under Tte. Pedro de la Riva; its partial transfer north for the Alta-CA conquista/new missions, merged with Loreto's under Fernando de Rivera y Moncada & Tte. Blas Fernández de Somera; pay rates (Loreto 450 p / Sur 402 vs. 400 assigned); the Yaqui/Mayo-river maize/meat/frijol purchases; the King's ship & its mixed-caste crew (some Filipinos, none Californian); the King's blacksmith & carpenter on the Almacén fund. A rich Baja-California presidial/fiscal-history source — the Crown's lawsuit over the Jesuit Temporalidades (confiscated 1768); commission cert. by Viceroy Manuel Antonio Flórez (Mexico, 30 Jun / 8 Jul 1788); witnesses incl. the Invalid Sgt Francisco Aguilar (~60, native of Guadalajara) + others, each answering all 24 points; *apoderado del Real Fisco* Juan Fco. Rosales (Contador, San Blas). ENDS n50 'Es copia fiel… Loreto 25 Sep 89. Arrillaga. pp.17–51'
