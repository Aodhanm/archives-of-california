# The Fages→Romeu handover named: Tte. Coronel José Antonio Romeu has been appointed to relieve Fages of the California go

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n309** · Sav p. 307 · tomo p. 309 · 1790 · Savage abstract · 

The Fages→Romeu handover named: Tte. Coronel José Antonio Romeu has been appointed to relieve Fages of the California governorship — Romeu to enter via San Diego, Fages to hand over the command (or via the SD captain if ill) and form a complete *instrucción* of the state of the Province for his successor + order all presidio commanders accordingly. Dates the 1790 gubernatorial-transition setup (Romeu formally succeeded 1791)
