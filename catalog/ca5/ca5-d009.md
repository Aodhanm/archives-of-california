# 'Sobre goce de sueldos de militares' — transmits Viceroy Manuel Antonio Flórez's rule (via Antonio Valdés, R.O. 1 Oct): 

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n50–n52** · Sav p. 50–52 · tomo p. 51–53 · 1789-04-14 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre goce de sueldos de militares' — transmits Viceroy Manuel Antonio Flórez's rule (via Antonio Valdés, R.O. 1 Oct): an officer who has completed his term in a military govt and is replaced *not* by his own fault keeps the sueldo of his grade in his current destination if the Superior Jefe finds him useful (placed in a post per his graduation/merit); if no vacancy in that Kingdom, he's remitted to Spain with 12 months' pay advanced (per the R.O. of 29 Feb 1764)
