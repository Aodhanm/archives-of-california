# THE CLOSING DOCUMENT — the Manila-Galleon / China-trade question: whether to lift the prohibition and let the *Nao de Ch

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n356–n362** · Sav p. 354–360 · tomo p. 356–641 · 1790-10-30 (cert.; refs 1787) · Savage abstract · 

THE CLOSING DOCUMENT — the Manila-Galleon / China-trade question: whether to lift the prohibition and let the *Nao de China* (Manila Galleon) unload & sell goods at Monterey — per Fages's 'instrucción Reservada' Art. 17 'plan de comercio.' The reasoned conclusion: opening Monterey to the China trade would yield no real utility to the troops or *familias pobladoras* and would harm Spanish commerce / risk contraband; the *géneros* better routed to the Acapulco/Manila companies. A California foreign-commerce / trade-liberalization debate (echoes the Open-Door §④ economy + the otter/China-trade nexus)
