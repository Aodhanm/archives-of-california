# Sexual-assault case: the poblador Félix Antonio Villavicencio found imprisoned at the pueblo for having 'violentado á un

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n109–n110** · Sav p. 107–108 · tomo p. 182–186 · 1789-06-26 · Savage abstract · 

Sexual-assault case: the poblador Félix Antonio Villavicencio found imprisoned at the pueblo for having 'violentado á una India Gentil' (a young Indian he had set to irrigate, said to be Mission-connected [?]); fearing he'd abandon his *siembra*, Goycoechea freed him after 20 days' suffering; + presidio construction (2 *lienzos* done, 3rd rising) + *traslado de familias* (Sgt. Olivera, Cabo Aguirre) to the pueblo
