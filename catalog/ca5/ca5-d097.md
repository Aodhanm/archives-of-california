# Church-vs-state cluster around the census & a mission-lands grant: (a) Fr. Sitjar objects to the order that *missionarie

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n155–n157** · Sav p. 154–167 · tomo p. 280–89+ · 1790-07 (Jul 13/26) · Savage abstract · 

Church-vs-state cluster around the census & a mission-lands grant: (a) Fr. Sitjar objects to the order that *missionaries* help form the civil *padrones* — resents census categories ('Barrachos,' 'amantes de cura'), insists 'nuestros Indios son unos pobres é miserables Huérfanos' not the state's to enumerate; (b) invalid Cabo Francisco Cayuelas (married to a Mission San Antonio woman) petitions for *solar y tierras* at the paraje de Santa Margarita *within the mission's lands*; the friars oppose the grant as prejudicial to the mission Indians' *naturales* rights — an early land-grant-vs-mission & missionary-autonomy conflict (Arrillaga forwarding, the Padre resisting)
