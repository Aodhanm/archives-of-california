# 'Sobre aprehensión de gentiles': the pueblo *alcalde* reported gentile Indians killed 2 cattle (*reses*) + took/forced a

**C-A 5 (Provincial State Papers), scan n101–n102** · Sav p. 99 · tomo p. 158–159 · 1789-09-19 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre aprehensión de gentiles': the pueblo *alcalde* reported gentile Indians killed 2 cattle (*reses*) + took/forced another; Goycoechea ordered an inquiry, then a *partida* to seize them; the *comisionado* reports the gentiles imprisoned — one recognized his [brand] + the wounded horse; a 'Dios' (idol/leader) said to have come to the field; the gentile *Capitanes* themselves turned in two other gentiles. A 1789 SB/LA-area cattle-raiding + indigenous-justice incident
