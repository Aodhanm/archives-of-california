# 'Sobre dar azotes á los indios' — an extended friar's defense of whipping neophytes at Mission San Francisco: they flee 

**C-A 39 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n133–n135** · Sav p. 133–135 · tomo p. 12–14 · 1833-06-16 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre dar azotes á los indios' — an extended friar's defense of whipping neophytes at Mission San Francisco: they flee to the mountains 15–16+ days without notice, refuse work; debates whether corporal punishment is licit; cites the decreto of 17 Aug 1813 & suprema orden of 2 Aug 1822 (which he says apply only to civilized pueblos of the Republic, not these neophytes who 'comienzan ahora en la infancia política y religiosa'); says if the govt orders azotes abolished absolutely he'll obey but asks the Rev. P. Comisario's opinion first — a vivid document on mission labor discipline & the emancipation debate
