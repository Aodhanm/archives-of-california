# 'Sobre pocas existencias en la Misión' — the mission has very few/no existencias for its fomento; the seeds insufficient

**C-A 39 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n162–n164** · Sav p. 162–164 · tomo p. 1083–1084 · 1843-05-6 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre pocas existencias en la Misión' — the mission has very few/no existencias for its fomento; the seeds insufficient for even 2 months; lack of tools (aradores, palas, hachas, herramienta de carpintería y fragua); the few neophytes at work; the ranchos 'en el estado de desnudez'; asks direction; Micheltorena's marginal reply (15 May) authorizing selling caldos, buying tools on credit, distributing to the S. Luis & Pala Indians, sending a nominal relation of quantities distributed — a vivid mission-decay/secularization-administration document
