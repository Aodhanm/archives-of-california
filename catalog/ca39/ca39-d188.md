# Representation in the name of the pronounced force: invited by Antonio Apalátegui & Francisco Torres to cooperate for th

**C-A 39 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n142** · Sav p. 142 · tomo p. 451 · 1835-03-7 · Savage abstract · 

Representation in the name of the pronounced force: invited by Antonio Apalátegui & Francisco Torres to cooperate for the territory's good per the plan they presented; the plan not adopted, having no other means to avoid a rompimiento they present themselves as promoters, that if their empresa is just it be judged by law; unanimously beg to be absolved
