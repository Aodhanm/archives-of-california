# Names an interim Comandante de Celadores (1,000 pesos/yr); ⭐⭐ customs-payment reform — the practice of not requiring for

**C-A 39 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n246** · Sav p. 246 · tomo p. 67–72 · 1839-04-8 · Savage abstract · 

Names an interim Comandante de Celadores (1,000 pesos/yr); ⭐⭐ customs-payment reform — the practice of not requiring foreign ships to pay duties in silver (only effects) has harmed the erario & exhausted coin; henceforth foreign ships pay: 1–3,000 pesos all in silver; 3–6,000, ⅔ silver; 6–12,000, ½ silver; over 12,000, ⅓ in silver
