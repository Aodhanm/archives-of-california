# ⭐⭐ Supply-crisis complaints: urgent letters to the Viceroy (6 & 24 Sep) for money; the *San Carlos* & *Nueva Reyna* fail

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n229–n230** · Sav p. 230–231 · 1819-09-28 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Supply-crisis complaints: urgent letters to the Viceroy (6 & 24 Sep) for money; the *San Carlos* & *Nueva Reyna* failed to bring the promised 400 muskets & 500 swords; only 5 six-pdrs + four-pdrs received; the 100 reinforcement men were "sacados de las cárceles y cogidos en leva" (jailbirds & press-ganged) — "de ellos no se espera sino dolores y resultas"; the 2-ship expedition cost the Real Erario "muy cerca de $60,000"; the 400 saber-blades came without hilts. Vivid on the dysfunction of the post-Bouchard reinforcement. Orig. pp.412, 425
