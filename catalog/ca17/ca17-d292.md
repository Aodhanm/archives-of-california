# ⭐⭐ "Molina y su castigo": the *paisano* Isidro Molina [Doc 271 has "Pedro Molina" — name variance per Savage] for joinin

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n233–n234** · Sav p. 234–235 · 1818-12-11 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ "Molina y su castigo": the *paisano* Isidro Molina [Doc 271 has "Pedro Molina" — name variance per Savage] for joining the insurgents at the Monterey presidio during the attack of 22–25 Nov 1818 — sentenced to 6 yrs presidio at Sta Bárbara in public works (ración, no pay). Confirms Bouchard's multi-day occupation of Monterey, 22–25 Nov 1818. Orig. p.435
