# ⭐ Habilitado-general crisis. Solá resolves to send Capt. de la Guerra y Noriega to México to attend to the *situados* (s

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n325–n326** · Sav p. 325–326 · 1819-10-17 · unspecified · 

⭐ Habilitado-general crisis. Solá resolves to send Capt. de la Guerra y Noriega to México to attend to the *situados* (subsidy) accounts of California & demand accounts from the interim habilitado José Antonio [Ormart?] and the current one, Alfz Gervasio Argüello; needs a monthly 100-peso assignment for the 4 presidios to cover de la Guerra's extraordinary travel costs. Notes he failed to get Gervasio Argüello to go to México to exercise the Habilitado General post — Argüello has been ~3 years stuck in Guadalajara. Orig. pp.518–521.
