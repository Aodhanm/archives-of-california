# ⭐⭐ "Preparativos de defensa" — the "150 machetes" order. With Fr. Prefect Mariano Payeras, Solá ordered 150 machetes + 1

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n300–n302+** · Sav p. 301–303+ · 1819-03-07 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ "Preparativos de defensa" — the "150 machetes" order. With Fr. Prefect Mariano Payeras, Solá ordered 150 machetes + 150 cartridge-boxes made to arm the Monterey troops & some vecinos, for the shortage of swords; 2 model machetes made at Mission Soledad sent to the missions as patterns (each mission to make a share). PLUS a horse-relay defense plan: each named *paraje* between the missions (San Antonio–San Miguel, San Luis Obispo–Purísima, San Buenaventura–San Fernando, San Gabriel–San Juan Capistrano) to keep ≥150 horses for rapid troop remounts against the "traitors" (Bouchard). Order grew to 200 machetes; 40 skilled Indian bowmen to be named at each mission (30 San Luis Obispo, 16 Soledad/San Carlos/Sta Cruz, 20 San Juan Bautista) as auxiliaries, each mission to elect an Indian "capitán." Includes a mission-by-mission machete/cartridge-box production quota (e.g., SF 10, Sta Clara 10, San Gabriel 24… = 150 + 150). Orig. pp.301–15
