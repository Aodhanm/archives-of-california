**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n48–n49** · Sav p. 49–50 · 1815-05-05 (data: end of 1814) · unspecified · 

⭐⭐ Province-wide LIVESTOCK census, end of 1814 (*ganado mayor* & *menor* by establishment, totals) + NOTAS: ⭐ 1,207 baptized vs. 1,327 buried in the missions that year (net decline; 575 baptisms were of gentiles); presidio-branded stock; reference to an earlier cattle epidemic; smallpox (viruela) epidemic & cattle losses. Strong demographic/epidemic evidence. Orig. p.52
