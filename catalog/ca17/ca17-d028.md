# ⭐ Causa vs. two Cuerpo de Artillería soldiers — Severo Silvas & Tomás Carrillo[?] — for robbing Patricio Pico (28 June):

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n30–n31** · Sav p. 31–32 · 1810-07-24 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Causa vs. two Cuerpo de Artillería soldiers — Severo Silvas & Tomás Carrillo[?] — for robbing Patricio Pico (28 June): 5 shirts, cloth, a vara of gold thread, a muslin shawl, *manta* & *indiana*. Sentenced (1st) 8 yrs presidio San Diego w/ *grillos*, public works, 1½ ration; (2nd) 6 yrs. The prisoners scaled the Sta Bárbara jail walls & took sanctuary in the chapel, then were re-seized. Orig. pp.84–161
