# ⭐⭐ The Mexican Independence war (Morelos) disrupts California's supply line: explains the delays in the *memorias* — shi

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n86–n87** · Sav p. 87–88 · 1812-11-07 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐ The Mexican Independence war (Morelos) disrupts California's supply line: explains the delays in the *memorias* — shipped from Acapulco in a chartered vessel but had to be returned to the capital lest the cargo fall "en manos de las fuerzas de Morelos"; asks recompense for the exorbitant freight; will send a well-escorted convoy.
