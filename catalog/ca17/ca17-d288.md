# ⭐⭐ "Campaña contra indios": approves the province making its expedition "en los términos de su demarcación"; San Diego t

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n231–n232** · Sav p. 232–233 · 1819-10-07 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐ "Campaña contra indios": approves the province making its expedition "en los términos de su demarcación"; San Diego to send a force visiting the points Moraga designates; once the 45 Mazatlán men reach Sta Bárbara, Moraga sets out (command of SB arms left to a Tte. Argüello). ⭐⭐ The Monterey party under Tte. José Mª Estudillo (guide Sgt. José Dolores Pico as *práctico*) to set out 10–15 Oct, enter via San Miguel, scout the laguna de Ugarra[?], Telame, and the San Gabriel river, then return. A multi-pronged interior (Tulares) sweep. Orig. pp.425–30
