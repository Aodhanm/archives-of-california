**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n299** · Sav p. 300 · 1819-07-06 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐ "Aprobación del Virrey": the Viceroy Conde del Venadito (Apodaca) (oficio 22 Mar), pleased by Solá's 13-Dec report on the insurgent (Buenos Aires) frigates' attack, thanks the California troops & vecinos for their valor, honor & fidelity in the Bouchard defense in the King's & his own name; published in the Gaceta extraordinaria de México (24 Mar). Official recognition of the Bouchard defense. Orig. p.305
