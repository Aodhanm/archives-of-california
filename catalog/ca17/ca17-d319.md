# ⭐⭐ "Ataque sobre buques; estado indefenso de las provincias": will keep precaution with every approaching ship lest it c

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n261–n262** · Sav p. 262–263 · 1818-04-23 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐ "Ataque sobre buques; estado indefenso de las provincias": will keep precaution with every approaching ship lest it carry the expedition prepared by Lord Cochrane & Sir Robert Wilson against the King's American dominions; has only 25–30 men; stresses the presidios' defenseless state on "esta abierta y dilatada costa"; from 1 May the militia-artillerymen to rotate to the presidio monthly. Orig. pp.853–5
