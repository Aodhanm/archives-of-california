# ⭐⭐ Causa criminal — the neophyte José Mª (Mission San Diego) flogged to death a gentile woman ("Cuasislpi"), his lover, 

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n195–n197** · Sav p. 196–198 · 1811-11-09 (events Apr 1811) · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Causa criminal — the neophyte José Mª (Mission San Diego) flogged to death a gentile woman ("Cuasislpi"), his lover, for squandering his goods & being unfaithful (1 Apr 1811). Fiscal: he "took justice into his own hands," a recent convert (1807) — 50 lashes + the 5 months already served; a gentile witness who beat her more harshly to get 50 lashes + 6 months. Indigenous-on-indigenous violence in the mission orbit. Orig. pp.131–56
