# ⭐⭐ Population census of the San Diego district 1818 — presidio + Missions San Luis Rey/San Diego/San Juan Capistrano/San

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n270** · Sav p. 271 · 1818-12-31 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐ Population census of the San Diego district 1818 — presidio + Missions San Luis Rey/San Diego/San Juan Capistrano/San Gabriel, by sex & age; totals 2,793/2,310/1,011/960 (gran total ≈ 7,074); broken down by caste (españoles, indios 2,645+, mestizos, color quebrado) & marital status (8 ecclesiastics; casados 1,344; viudos; solteros). Each mission had 2 missionaries; sínodos $400 each = $3,200, paid from the ex-Jesuit "temporalidades" (Pious Fund). Valuable demography. Orig. pp.1002–5
