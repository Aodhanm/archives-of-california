# ⭐⭐ Causa criminal — a mission Indian *alcalde* killed a neophyte (threw a stick ~30 paces that struck his head, for not 

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n174** · Sav p. 175 · 1813-12-07 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Causa criminal — a mission Indian *alcalde* killed a neophyte (threw a stick ~30 paces that struck his head, for not working as he should). Ruled accidental, no culpability; but the fiscal urges a punishment "para que… conozcan los demás alcaldes é indiada el que de ningún modo deben castigar… á sus súbditos" beyond what humanity allows. Notable on mission Indian-alcalde authority & violence. Orig. pp.88–96
