# ⭐ Bando licensing a tavern (taberna) at Monterey presidio — 8 conditions: cuartillo de aguardiente 6 reales, de vino 3 r

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n335–n336** · Sav p. 336–337 · 1820-02-16 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Bando licensing a tavern (taberna) at Monterey presidio — 8 conditions: cuartillo de aguardiente 6 reales, de vino 3 reales; no liquor to any Indian; sales only with the Comandante's boleta; closed on holidays, open 7am–7pm (–6pm Nov 1–Apr 30); arrivals with liquor to present boleta to the Habilitado, paying $1/barrel aguardiente, $6/barrel vino; only one tavern permitted, clandestine sale = 2 months' prison + grillete on public works; liquor seized held in deposit. Orig. pp.366–367.
