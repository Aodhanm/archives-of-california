# ⭐⭐⭐ "Sobre canje de prisioneros con Bouchard; pena á Molina; desagrado con el Tte. Ignacio Martínez." Of the supposed 3 

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n210–n212** · Sav p. 211–213 · 1818-12-11 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐⭐ "Sobre canje de prisioneros con Bouchard; pena á Molina; desagrado con el Tte. Ignacio Martínez." Of the supposed 3 prisoners the enemy claimed, only one (Molina) was actually exchanged; suspecting Molina may have gone over to Bouchard voluntarily, Solá sentences him to 6 yrs presidio in chains (ración, no pay) + 100 public lashes, the company & vecindario assembled, the sentence read aloud "para escarmiento." Orders the 20 Monterey soldiers back; voices displeasure with Tte. Ignacio Martínez for his tardiness; lookouts to track the enemy's course. Orig. pp.384
