# Sta Bárbara muster 1819: Capt. de la Guerra, Tte. Gabriel Moraga, Alf. Maitorena, 3 sargtos, 6 cabos, 1 cadete (de la Gu

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n297** · Sav p. 298 · 1819-12-01 · verbatim transcription · 

Sta Bárbara muster 1819: Capt. de la Guerra, Tte. Gabriel Moraga, Alf. Maitorena, 3 sargtos, 6 cabos, 1 cadete (de la Guerra), 56 soldados = 69 + Inválidos 27. ⭐⭐ Embeds a certified burial record (Fr. José Senán): Mariano Cota (Sta Bárbara soldier, on sentinel duty) atrociously killed during High Mass on Pentecost 1819 by Amajahua/Mojave gentiles from the Río Colorado (20–21 then held in the guardhouse), and cabo Rufino Leyva, killed the same day — at San Buenaventura. The Mojave attack that triggered Moraga's 1819 campaign (cf. Doc 273). Orig. pp.227–74
