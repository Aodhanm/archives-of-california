# ⭐⭐ Multiple: the Colorado raid (of 21 Indians, 11 killed, 10 fled per Guerra; "10 attackers" per Señán); the Viceroy, af

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n220–n221** · Sav p. 221–222 · 1819-06-21 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐ Multiple: the Colorado raid (of 21 Indians, 11 killed, 10 fled per Guerra; "10 attackers" per Señán); the Viceroy, after the Monterey attack, named Solá Coronel de Milicias Provinciales; reinforcements coming (Fr. Carlos w/ troops/artillery/money from San Blas; the brig *Cossack* w/ 100 men from Mazatlán); ⭐⭐ Alféreces Ignacio Martínez & Gabriel Moraga promoted to Teniente (Martínez → SF company, Moraga → Sta Bárbara company); promotions of José Estrada & Manuel Gómez. Solá expects relief from his governorship. Orig. pp.401–3
