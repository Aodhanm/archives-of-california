# ⭐ Labor/discipline order re gentiles: 9 days' prison/cepo for gentiles who arrive at a *punta* and don't present to thei

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n140** · Sav p. 141 · 1815-05-29 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Labor/discipline order re gentiles: 9 days' prison/cepo for gentiles who arrive at a *punta* and don't present to their padres; 15 days for *amos* who take on *agregados* without licence or don't report them to the ministers — born of the difficulty of forming *padrones*. Argüello accepts. Orig. pp.476–7
