# ⭐⭐ Capture of two Americans from a smuggling schooner (CLOSED). Diligencias on an accusation against "un tal Maliga[?],"

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n351–n352** · Sav p. 352–353 · 1821-09-14 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Capture of two Americans from a smuggling schooner (CLOSED). Diligencias on an accusation against "un tal Maliga[?]," by Antonio Briones, Ignacio Valencia, Mariano Mariné[?], Desiderio Olvera, Paulino Pico et al., in the apprehension of two Americans from a schooner on the coast (~Nov 7): seeing the contrabandista vessel, Briones signaled it to send a launch, which came with pilot & interpreter, bringing goods to trade; Briones — in the name of Capt. de la Guerra y Noriega — seized them and some effects. Outcome: seized goods' value assigned to repair of the pueblo church; Briones 6 months, [companion] 4 months presidio with grillete on Sta Bárbara public works; helpers 1 month arrest in the Guard. Orig. pp.698–718.
