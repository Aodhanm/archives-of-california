# ⭐⭐⭐⭐ SECOND *borrador* register of Solá's outgoing orders (1815–17) — the volume's richest Russian/foreign-relations str

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n143–n152** · Sav p. 144–153 · 1815–1817 (borradores; orig pp.510–684) · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐⭐⭐ SECOND *borrador* register of Solá's outgoing orders (1815–17) — the volume's richest Russian/foreign-relations stretch. Indigenous/campaign: ⭐⭐ Moraga's 1815 Palomas expedition (Solá orders Comte. Luis Argüello to mount 50 men—35 presidial + 15 inválidos/vecinos—and Gabriel Moraga to direct from Santa Cruz the campaign to the Ranchería de las Palomas); ⭐⭐⭐ Solá's Russian-trade policy query ("cuántas veces los Rusos han entrado… qué se les ha suministrado, cómo lo han pagado," whether to continue/cut communication "con otros Rusos, Anglo-Americanos ú otra Nación extranjera"); the Quintana assassins causa remitted to Gómez (Lino, Antonio, Vicente, Julián, Ambrosio, Andrés, Secundino, Fulgencio); the Juan Eliot de Castro chest sequestered (RAC agent, cf. C-A 12); Sgt. Soto's expedition. Russian/foreign maritime (1816–17): ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Kotzebue's *Rurik* at SF (Oct 1816) — "franquee todos los auxilios que necesite el buque de Nueva Rusia, su Comandante Kossebue [Kotzebue]… que ancló ayer"; ⭐⭐⭐ the Russian ship *Suvorov* ("Serraroff," Lazarev) at SF (21 Aug); ⭐⭐⭐ two Russians fled from "el establecimiento situado arriba del Puerto de Bodega" (Fort Ross), declarations taken (Nov 1816); ⭐⭐⭐ the Russian war-chief Podushkin + an "almost-resolved" decision to hand him a packet from St. Petersburg (Mar 1817); ⭐⭐ an anchored Russian ship's request to leave canoes/baidarkas at Sausalito to hunt (denied); ⭐⭐ the French corvette *Bordelais* (Roquefeuil), 200 t, for wood & water (Aug 1817, took deserters); ⭐⭐ an English NWC brig from the Columbia River (Aug 1816); ⭐⭐ defensive measures vs. the "insurgentes de Buenos Aires" (pre-Bouchard, 1816: spike the battery guns if taken, missions to ready bows & arrows); ⭐ Fort San Joaquín works concluded (Gómez). Converges w/ C-A 12 (*Suvorov*, Elliot de Castro) & C-A 13 (Podushkin/*Chirikov*, Roquefeuil/*Bordelais*). Orig. pp.510–684
