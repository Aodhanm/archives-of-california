# ⭐⭐ "Castigo por adulterio": re the scandal of the widow Anastasia Zúñiga (Los Ángeles) with the San-Gabriel-mission carp

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n204–n205** · Sav p. 205–206 · 1818-10-03 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐ "Castigo por adulterio": re the scandal of the widow Anastasia Zúñiga (Los Ángeles) with the San-Gabriel-mission carpenter José Antonio Ramírez — Ramírez to acknowledge his obligations to mother & daughter & marry if possible (else his pay garnished for the child); jailed a month. The widow to be publicly shamed: head shaved, one eyebrow scraped, displayed bareface at Mass at San Gabriel, then 6 months' servitude & a "religious life." Brutal gendered moral discipline. Orig. pp.374–5
