# ⭐⭐ Causa criminal — the neophyte Antonio/Rafael Chales flogged his wife María Gabriela to death (Mission San Diego). The

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n271–n272+** · Sav p. 272–273+ · 1818-08-14 (death 3 Aug) · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Causa criminal — the neophyte Antonio/Rafael Chales flogged his wife María Gabriela to death (Mission San Diego). The escort cabo Pedro Lobo found her body whip-marked, "de la cintura abajo, en los muslos, toda desollada, enseñando la carne viva." Chales's confession: he beat her with a "real doblado" + 25 lashes for a paper given by a gentile Indian she'd slept with 3 nights; she fell ill, he tried the native sweat-cure (heated hole + green herb, *frazada*) — she died Monday morning. Fiscal: among the Diéguinos, fear of punishment leads them to kill rather than leave a victim alive, then flee or take sanctuary; flogging-abuse "se va haciendo común entre ellos." Orig. pp.1077–1104
