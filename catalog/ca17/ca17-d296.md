# ⭐⭐⭐ Order for the Amajaba (Mojave) campaign: the ranchería de Amajaba threatened to attack Missions San Gabriel & San Fe

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n235–n237** · Sav p. 236–238 · 1819-11-03 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐⭐ Order for the Amajaba (Mojave) campaign: the ranchería de Amajaba threatened to attack Missions San Gabriel & San Fernando; Moraga to take 50 men (35 cavalry from both presidios + 15 Mazatlán under Fabregat) + the armament Tte. Francisco Mª Ruiz brought + 4 artillerymen + a cannon (metralla y bala), march at once to the Mojave ranchería, make the *capitanejos* surrender the fugitive Christian Indians, then leave garrisons of 15 men at Missions San Gabriel & San Fernando. Orig. pp.442–3
