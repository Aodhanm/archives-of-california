# ⭐ Causa criminal vs. various Angelenos for "excesos del juego, bebida, y pendencias." Embeds Sgt. Javier Alvarado's 21 J

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n25–n26** · Sav p. 26–27 · 1809-07-05 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Causa criminal vs. various Angelenos for "excesos del juego, bebida, y pendencias." Embeds Sgt. Javier Alvarado's 21 June report: rampant drunkenness, the jail/guardhouse full, men named — Ignacio Valencia (jailed), José Antº Nieto, Capitán Varela, Manuel Moreno, Molina. Measures: suspend wine & aguardiente sales except from Mission San Gabriel; after 8 pm no riding through the pueblo unless working. Finding: Valencia resisted justice, struck alcalde Guillermo Soto, and wounded Capitán Varela with a spur — all from drink; fiscal asks the Gob. for an exemplary punishment. Rich pueblo social-history (alcohol, disorder, civil-military authority). Orig. pp.586–603
