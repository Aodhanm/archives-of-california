# ⭐⭐⭐ "Artillería y pertrechos — Existentes en Cal." — a complete province-wide artillery inventory (1818): 58 pieces by c

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n265–n266** · Sav p. 266–267 · 1818-09-05 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐⭐ "Artillería y pertrechos — Existentes en Cal." — a complete province-wide artillery inventory (1818): 58 pieces by caliber (24/12/8/6/4/3/2/1-pdr) & metal (bronze/iron), located at the Castillo de San Joaquín (SF) (3×24, 8×8 bronze, etc.), Castillo de San Carlos (Monterey), Santa Bárbara presidio & baluarte, San Diego, Los Ángeles, Pueblo San José, and the missions (San Diego, Sta Inés, San Fernando, San Luis Rey). 3 useless, several *desgranados*; the 24-pdrs & SF iron 12-pdr corroded; gun-carriages (cureñas — campaña/plaza/marina) in poor repair "por falta de herraje." A comprehensive ordnance census — major for presidio-defense/decay. Orig. p.915
