# ⭐⭐⭐ "Boris" the Russian & Aleut otter-hunters at San Pedro: sighted in the ensenada de San Pedro 8 canoes manned by 17 m

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n114–n115** · Sav p. 115–116 · 1815-09-20 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐⭐ "Boris" the Russian & Aleut otter-hunters at San Pedro: sighted in the ensenada de San Pedro 8 canoes manned by 17 men under "Boris, ruso," hunting sea otters; ordered the Los Ángeles comisionado to let them leave and not return. Guillermo Cota (17 Sep) then seized some of the Indians incl. Boris, took their otter skins, holding guarded effects on the beach; awaits orders. Orig. pp.104–6
