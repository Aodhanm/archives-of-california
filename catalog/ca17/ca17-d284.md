# ⭐⭐⭐ Instruction letter to Moraga for the 1819 expedition against the "indios prófugos": "deseoso de dar una castigada ge

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n227–n228** · Sav p. 228–229 · 1819-09-24 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐⭐ Instruction letter to Moraga for the 1819 expedition against the "indios prófugos": "deseoso de dar una castigada general á los gentiles por… abrigar en sus rancherías á los cristianos huidos de las misiones," forms an expedition of 60 men under Moraga (30 Sta Bárbara + 30 San Diego), asks Moraga's written plan; coordinates another party of 25 under another officer, a SF party of 20–25 under Sgt. Sánchez via San José, and a launch with a cannon by water — a multi-pronged interior campaign. Asks where the SD & SB parties should rendezvous. Orig. pp.421–2
