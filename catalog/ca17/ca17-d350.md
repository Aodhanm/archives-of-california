# ⭐ Muster — San Blas infantry company in California. Compañía de Infantería de San Blas at Monterey: Capn de Cab. grad. N

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n323–n324** · Sav p. 323–324 · 1819-12-31 · unspecified · 

⭐ Muster — San Blas infantry company in California. Compañía de Infantería de San Blas at Monterey: Capn de Cab. grad. Navarrete (Comte.), Tte. de inf. Antonio Valle, Subtte. Francisco de Haro, 1 sargto 1º, 2 sargtos 2º, 1 tambor, 5 cabos 1º, 5 cabos 2º, 86 solds = Total 105. Arrival narrative: this veteran infantry co. (aggregated to the fija of the Apostadero de San Blas) came to Monterey in 1819 — capitán + 50 men embarked on the *San Carlos* (?) 7 Jun 1819; the Tte., Subtte. + 50 men on the frigate *Reyna de los Ángeles* same 7 Jun; *San Carlos* contingent disembarked Monterey 28 Aug, frigate's 25 Jun; by Oct the Tte. + 1 sargto + 4 cabos + 35 solds detached to presidio San Francisco. Orig. pp.458–473.
