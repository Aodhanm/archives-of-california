# Full company account (cargo/data). *Cargo:* carryover deficit from Dec 1808 $10,655; *memoria* from México $22,222 + San

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n15–n17** · Sav p. 16–18 · 1809-12-31 · unspecified · 

Full company account (cargo/data). *Cargo:* carryover deficit from Dec 1808 $10,655; *memoria* from México $22,222 + San Blas $1,442; company débitos $5,010; derechos (inválidos/montepío/retenciones) $443; rentas $1,654; habilitados' debts to the missions (SF $1,173, Sta Clara $1,258, Sta Cruz $530, San José $287, San Luis Obispo $309) + Monterey presidio $497; total cargo $46,218. *Data:* haberes $18,809; ⭐ "suministro de víveres á los Americanos y Rusos" $289 (detained American & Russian otter-hunters — pre-Farallones; cf. the 1812–13 Russian-prisoner accounts); etc.; balance $104 in the habilitado's favor. Orig. pp.373–6
