# ⭐⭐⭐ "Sobre los buques de Bouchard": approves de la Guerra's going to San Pedro with 25 men, Bouchard's two ships having 

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n212** · Sav p. 213 · 1818-12-16 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐⭐ "Sobre los buques de Bouchard": approves de la Guerra's going to San Pedro with 25 men, Bouchard's two ships having put in there on 13 Dec — with that force + the San Diego company + armed *vecinos* + the San Gabriel escort, ready to fire on the enemy if they landed; the San Diego commander prepared the battery with hot-shot ("bala roja") to receive them. The San Pedro/southern phase of the raid. Orig. p.385
