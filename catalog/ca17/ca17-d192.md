# ⭐⭐ "Respuestas de Boris" — the interrogation of the captured Russian otter-hunter. He is Boris Tarasov, sailor, Russian 

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n153–n154** · Sav p. 154–155 · 1815-10-15 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ "Respuestas de Boris" — the interrogation of the captured Russian otter-hunter. He is Boris Tarasov, sailor, Russian of Novgorod; taken by comisionado Guillermo Cota (7 Oct); came from the "islas fronterizas" to Sta Bárbara 7 days before capture; 3 times in the ensenada de San Pedro hunting otters, sheltering from storms; carried silk goods of his own. Questioned in Spanish (by Solá) and English (interpreter). ⭐⭐ Names Juan Elliot de Castro as captain of the *Ilmena*/"Hermina" (the RAC ship, cf. C-A 12) and John Cann; hadn't seen the *Ilmena* in 7 months. Refused to sign; signed a scrap of paper. ["Fin del tomo 46."] Orig. pp.720–2
