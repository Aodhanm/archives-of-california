# ⭐⭐⭐ "Comisión… Expedición contra indios": commissions de la Guerra to go to México to represent the province's crisis (a

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n224–n225** · Sav p. 225–226 · 1819-09-18 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐⭐ "Comisión… Expedición contra indios": commissions de la Guerra to go to México to represent the province's crisis (a general power from all companies to audit the Habilitado Gral.; $100/mo.); orders Teniente Gabriel Moraga to lead the expedition against the [Colorado/Mojave] Indians, taking 100+ armed mission Indians from San Buenaventura, San Fernando & San Gabriel; 20–25 men from San Diego to combine w/ Sta Bárbara's. Orig. pp.416–7
