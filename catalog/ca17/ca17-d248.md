# ⭐⭐ Causa criminal vs. the inválido soldier Ramón Sotelo for "blasphemous propositions about the Sacrament of Penance" (1

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n198–n200** · Sav p. 199–201 · 1817-12-05 (events Oct 1817) · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Causa criminal vs. the inválido soldier Ramón Sotelo for "blasphemous propositions about the Sacrament of Penance" (10 Oct). Friars Joaquín Pascual Nuez & Ramón Olbés testified (with P. Pres. Vicente Francisco de Sarría's licence) that his statements about confession were such that "ni aún el protestante más calificado podría proferirlas en lo público." Fiscal: his "disparates" stem from ignorance of doctrine & frivolous *charlatanería* — sentence to public works at the SD presidio w/ grillete + public satisfaction to the pueblo of Los Ángeles + must learn doctrine & take Penance/Eucharist publicly. ⭐ A striking case of religious dissent/skepticism in 1817 California. Orig. pp.223–45
