# Causa vs. Benhamito[?] & Simona, Christian Indians of San José, for the killing of [Santa] María, a Christian Indian of 

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n346–n347** · Sav p. 347–348 · 1821-05-16 · unspecified · 

Causa vs. Benhamito[?] & Simona, Christian Indians of San José, for the killing of [Santa] María, a Christian Indian of the mission — a fight in which Benhamito beat her and Simona finished the killing when he left her alone. Conflicting recommendations: Haro asks the capital penalty *not* be applied to Benhamito; Tte. Ignacio Martínez (juez fiscal) asks Benhamito be hanged and Simona 8 years' confinement. Escribano José Berreyesa. "No viene la resolución del Gobierno." Orig. pp.285–357.
