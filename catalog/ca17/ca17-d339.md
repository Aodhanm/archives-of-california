**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n296–n297** · Sav p. 297–298 · 1819-03-27 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Causa criminal (by Solá's order): Tte. Moraga investigates the theft of Fr. Olbés's trunk by José Antº Robles & son Nieves (& Martín Ortega). Nieves Robles confesses he broke the trunk on Ortega's & his father's orders ("echando en una frazada lo que pudo"), hid it at Las Espuelas. (Moraga as juez fiscal.) Orig. pp.~
