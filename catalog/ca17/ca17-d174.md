# ⭐⭐ Boris declared he had hunted 950 otter skins that year; couldn't leave for want of favorable winds. The Russian/Aleut

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n115–n116** · Sav p. 116–117 · 1815-09-22 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Boris declared he had hunted 950 otter skins that year; couldn't leave for want of favorable winds. The Russian/Aleut men jailed (natives in the cárcel, Boris in the guardhouse then house arrest); Cota let him bathe his sailors daily but denied the request to attend Mass at the nearest mission; the embargoed canoe-effects guarded. Orig. pp.107–9
