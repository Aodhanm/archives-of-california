# ⭐⭐ "Indios malhechores castigados": praises Antonio Ignacio Ávila (regidor of Los Ángeles) & vecino Juan Nepomuceno Alva

**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n241** · Sav p. 242 · 1820-03-25 · unspecified · 

⭐⭐ "Indios malhechores castigados": praises Antonio Ignacio Ávila (regidor of Los Ángeles) & vecino Juan Nepomuceno Alvarado for killing the principal ringleader of the "diéguino" raiders who hid much of the game/stock of Simí (3 others wounded & disarmed); orders monthly patrols by 4–5 vecinos "para su total exterminio." Settler-Indian violence near Simí/LA. Orig. pp.464–5
