**C-A 17 (Ben. Mil. XL–LII, 1809–22), scan n32–n33** · Sav p. 33–34 · 1810-05-15 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Causa vs. soldiers José Mª Franco & Marcelo Bojórquez and neophytes Respira[?], Simón & Francisco Reyes for robbing the warehouse (night of 26 May): *manta, lienzos, seda* & other goods, via two holes (roof & floor). The two soldiers found innocent & freed; the fiscal leaves the neophytes' penalty to the Jefe Superior, the robbery being "una costumbre difícil de desarraigar." Orig. pp.181–296
