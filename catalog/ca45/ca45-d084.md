# US CONQUEST (English verbatim): on the Santa Clara & San José mission buildings — 'All persons occupying any of the Miss

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n360–n362** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 340 · 1847-06-24 · verbatim transcription · 

US CONQUEST (English verbatim): on the Santa Clara & San José mission buildings — 'All persons occupying any of the Mission buildings at Santa Clara and San José without the permission of the Catholic Priest — Padre Real — are hereby required to vacate immediately'; per the emancipation decree of 22 Mar 1847; the San José Alcalde to enforce it; 'If required, a Military force will be sent to the Pueblo to enforce the judicial acts of the Alcalde' → conquest / secularization aftermath
