# the foreigner-registration circular — each foreigner files a signed declaration (name, age, status, whether wife is Mexi

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n80–n86** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 293–323 · 1828–1829 · Savage abstract · 

the foreigner-registration circular — each foreigner files a signed declaration (name, age, status, whether wife is Mexican & children by her, place/country of *naturaleza*); categories by date (pre-1 Mar 1824 / pre-Independence, married to a *hija del país*), the SJ & LA alcaldes remitting via the military commanders; + fuero-militar vs. civil jurisdiction note (criminal cases to the arm's Comandante via the Sta Clara escolta); + the 20 Mar 1829 expulsion-of-Spaniards decree signed by Pres. Guadalupe Victoria ⚠ mostly gubernatorial/dup
