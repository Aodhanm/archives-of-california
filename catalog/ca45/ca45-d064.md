# completes the 1st & 2nd civic-militia companies to aid the government in need (Jun 1837, p.199); ⭐ combats a FALSE RUMOR

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n221–n222** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 199–203 · 1837-03→06 · Savage abstract · 

completes the 1st & 2nd civic-militia companies to aid the government in need (Jun 1837, p.199); ⭐ combats a FALSE RUMOR of an invasion of California 'por Opatas y Sonorenses' — will punish the author of 'tan alarmante noticia' (Mar 1837, pp.200–1); + the 1837 southern campaign — Alvarado present at Sta Bárbara/San Gabriel with northern vecinos under Castro's command 'para rechazar la supuesta invasión' (enforcing the Alvarado govt against the south, pp.202–3)
