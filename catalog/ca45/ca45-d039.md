# the American-trapper attribution + the MONTHLY-EXPEDITION system: Figueroa, 'enterado de los robos de caballada que moti

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n138** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 566–568 · 1833-11-18→22 · Savage abstract · 

the American-trapper attribution + the MONTHLY-EXPEDITION system: Figueroa, 'enterado de los robos de caballada que motivan los americanos del Norte que á título de tramperos se introducen entre la gentilidad,' orders the SF military commander to mount an expedition each month to the points where the stolen horses are, the vecindario supplying horses to mount the troop (Diezmo to be paid regardless); + orders that 'salga mensualmente una expedición militar en seguimiento de la caballada robada por los gentiles,' vecinos joining voluntarily / named by the ayunt. — the institutionalized anti-raiding campaign, explicitly blaming the American trappers → [[indian-raiding-article]] KEYSTONE (pairs w/ DeLay/Blackhawk's American-arms-into-the-raiding-economy)
