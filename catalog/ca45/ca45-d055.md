# an anti-raiding military expedition order: raids have brought 'ruina total de varias familias establecidas en los Rancho

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n203–n204** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 127–129 · ~1839 · Savage abstract · 

an anti-raiding military expedition order: raids have brought 'ruina total de varias familias establecidas en los Ranchos,' so on the 1st of next month a punitive expedition 'con objeto de escarmentar á sus tribus enemigas' marches; every citizen is obligated to serve — enlist a 30-man force of the most patriotic, joined to Capt. D. Antonio Buelna's force, to march the last day of the month; the enlisted to arm & mount themselves (merchants/others excused for personal service) — the Figueroa-era campaign continuing into the Alvarado period → [[indian-raiding-article]]
