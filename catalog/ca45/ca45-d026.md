# the Guillermo (William) Willis / 'Laguna de los Bolbones' cattle-grazing dispute (San-José-specific): the Englishman Wil

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n78** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 280 · 1828 · Savage abstract · 

the Guillermo (William) Willis / 'Laguna de los Bolbones' cattle-grazing dispute (San-José-specific): the Englishman Willis grazed cattle at Laguna de los Bolbones, having gone to the SF Comandancia (which improperly gave him a 1-year privilege, 'no teniendo facultad'); the alcalde is to summon Willis, fine him 50 pesos, and remove his cattle to the San José pueblo *ejidos* — a foreigner-land/cattle case; + a list of debtors of Juan José Bernal
