**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n385–n386** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 701–725 · 1839-07-22 / 1839-01-10 · Savage abstract · 

the volume's last content leaves: a criminal causa against José J. Castro for a fatal stabbing of Ignacio Cortez (who died upon giving his declaration) in a mill-room over a *juego de naipes* quarrel — burial certified by Fr. Jesús Mª Vásquez at Mission Sta Clara — 'No hay más trámites' (pp.701–25); + a note of the 'Libro de Juicios verbales… (sin interés)' for 1839 (pp.1027–73). ⚠ No 'Fin' colophon — the volume simply ends at n386 (Sav. p.383, leaf 18); it is NON-CHRONOLOGICAL, ending on 1839–40 San José juzgado material, NOT at the conquest (which sits earlier at n356–366).
