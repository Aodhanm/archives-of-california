# the Alta-California ELECTORAL LAW (reglamento de elecciones), full text (arts. 1–57) — the three-tier junta system: Junt

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n58–n72** · Sav p.  · tomo p. ~56–72 · ~1828 · Savage abstract · 

the Alta-California ELECTORAL LAW (reglamento de elecciones), full text (arts. 1–57) — the three-tier junta system: Juntas Primarias (voters = citizens 18+ resident in the partidos; exclusions for debt/crime/incapacity); Juntas Segundas/de Partido (municipal electors 28+/21-if-married, literate, no arms); Junta Tercera/de Territorio (all partido electors congregate at San Diego to name the Diputado + suplente to the General Congress; art. 47 = Diputado qualifications, 25+/2 yrs vecindad). The constitutional machinery of Californio elections → [[californio-article]]. ⚠ general reglamento — likely duplicates C-A 59 (Leg. Rec.); skim
