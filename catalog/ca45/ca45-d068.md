# a bando on elections (p.229); ⭐ the 1839 REGLAMENTO PROVISIONAL for mission administrators (secularization governance, p

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n229–n234** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 227–233+ · 1839-01-01 · verbatim transcription · 

a bando on elections (p.229); ⭐ the 1839 REGLAMENTO PROVISIONAL for mission administrators (secularization governance, pairs w/ Hartnell's visitorship Doc 58) — abstract at pp.230–1, then Savage transcribes the reglamento's ~14 articles in full (pp.231–233): admins to render management accounts to the govt for inspection; hand over archive documents to successors; remit records to end of Dec; a detailed notice of assets & liabilities per epoch; no contracting credit against mission property 'por ningún pretexto'; may invest manufactures in the finca; present a padrón of all [the neophytes] with their ages 'á fin de formar una estadística general'; the *indios* still belong 'en la comunidad' — the disentailment/audit rules on the missions → secularization
