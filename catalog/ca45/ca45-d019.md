**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n42–n48** · Sav p.  · tomo p. ~180–191 · ~1825 · Savage abstract · 

liquor-tax reglamento (*contribución sobre licores*), full text (Caps 1–4): provincial duties on liquor — 5 pesos/barrel aguardiente & 20 r/barrel wine at Monterey & SF; 10 & 5 at Sta Bárbara/LA/SD; collected by the Síndicos (or the Habilitado where there's no ayunt., e.g. San Diego); *guías*/boletas procedure; the *propios y arbitrios* fund the *instituto*.
