# Juan Anzar named prefecto interino of the 1st district (Castro had to march off, 'por orden del gobierno, [en] servicios

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n205–n206** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 133–136 · 1839-05-23 / 1839-06-15 · Savage abstract · 

Juan Anzar named prefecto interino of the 1st district (Castro had to march off, 'por orden del gobierno, [en] servicios convenientes á la seguridad pública' — i.e. the military campaign); then ⭐ the DISSOLUTION of the San José ayuntamiento by the Governor's order — the body doesn't correspond to the number of inhabitants; the elected Juez de Paz to take charge of all *existencias y archivos* (the 1839 municipal downgrade)
