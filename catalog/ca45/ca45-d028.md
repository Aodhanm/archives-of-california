# the SOLÍS-REVOLT aftermath (San-José-relevant self-rule keystone): (a) a 1829 war-tax *prorateo* apportioning a forced c

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n90** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 344–347 · 1830-02-05 (re 1829–30) · Savage abstract · 

the SOLÍS-REVOLT aftermath (San-José-relevant self-rule keystone): (a) a 1829 war-tax *prorateo* apportioning a forced contribution — Monterey $304, Sta Bárbara $300, Los Ángeles $200, San Diego $100 — covered from *propios y arbitrios* + 'los individuos mejor acomodados'; (b) to pacify the Territory 'alterado por la facción de Joaquín Solís,' the Diputación met 19–21 Jan 1830 in 5 extraordinary sessions; the armed faction ended, it resolved to advise better troop discipline and to petition the Supreme Govt NOT to send 'tropas viciadas, presidiarios, ú otra clase de gente desmoralizada' — the Californio legislature answering the convict-soldier revolt & indicting the convict-soldier system → [[californio-article]] (self-rule) + the Solís dossier (cf. C-A 19)
