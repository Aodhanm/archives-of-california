# the RESOLUTION of the Alvarado revolt — México legitimizes his government: transcribes Capt. Andrés Castillero's relay o

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n225–n226** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 215–216 · 1838-12-10 · Savage abstract · 

the RESOLUTION of the Alvarado revolt — México legitimizes his government: transcribes Capt. Andrés Castillero's relay of the Ministro del Interior's 20-Jul-1838 order to Carlos A. Carrillo — the President, 'impuesto con satisfacción que la Alta Cal. ha resuelto el orden constitucional,' applies the amnesty law ('cubre un denso velo sobre todas las ocurrencias políticas'), has the 1st vocal of the territorial junta assume gubernatorial functions, orders election of the junta departamental & a *terna* to México — i.e. Mexico legalizing Alvarado's regime (1838)
