# Agustín del Carmen embarked on the bergantín María Ester (may not disembark to another ship at sail); recognizes Mariano

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n34** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 122–129 · 1826 / 1836 · Savage abstract · 

Agustín del Carmen embarked on the bergantín María Ester (may not disembark to another ship at sail); recognizes Mariano Castro as Alcalde Constitucional; ⭐ Gov. Echeandía succeeds Argüello (1826) — name 5 useful men for arms-service; + 1836 SCJ referral of the *proceso* vs Juan Beforje for killing the militia-artilleryman José Cibrián
