**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n280** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 279 · ~1837 · verbatim transcription · 

reglamento on departmental municipal government (per the 20-Mar-1837 centralist law), articles 8–15 transcribed: *ayuntamientos* only at the capital & Monterey (art. 8); juntas municipales (jueces de paz + 2 vecinos 'de notoria honradez') in the other *cabeceras de partido* (arts. 9–10); officeholders must be ≥25 & hold 'un capital físico ó moral de 500 pesos' (art. 12); where no ayunt., jueces de paz (art. 13); appointments per the 20-Mar-1837 law (art. 14) — the centralist local-government structure that downgraded San José (cf. Doc 56) → [[californio-article]]
