# the 1836 ALVARADO REVOLT, from the sovereign body itself — four decrees of the 'Congreso de California' (the reconstitut

**C-A 45 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n200** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 111–115 · 1836-12-07→09 · Savage abstract · 

the 1836 ALVARADO REVOLT, from the sovereign body itself — four decrees of the 'Congreso de California' (the reconstituted Diputación): (1) 7 Dec naming Juan B. Alvarado gobernador interino (p.111); (2) 7 Dec naming Alvarado coronel de las fuerzas cívicas (p.112); (3) 9 Dec dividing the Territory into two cantones (pp.113–4); (4) 9 Dec a decreto sobre comercio y derechos (customs — the free-trade dimension of the rising, p.115) — the November-1836 pronunciamiento that ousted Gutiérrez, in its own legislative voice → californio self-rule keystone (pairs with the anti-Victoria manifesto Doc 30 & Pico-1832 C-A 59)
