# bundle of short items.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n63–n64** · orig. pp. 291–306 · Sav 64–65 · 1802, México · detail Abstracts

bundle of short items.

> [!note] (Jan 12) sends 4 *cédulas de inválidos*; (Jul 13) 3-yr retirement *abono* via San Blas; ⭐ (Jun 24) encloses royal dispatches naming Raymundo Antonio Carrillo, José de la Guerra y Noriega, and Francisco M.ª Ruiz as tenientes/alféreces of the Monterey & Sta Bárbara companies; (Jul 12) on muster extracts; (Feb 5) acknowledges the gov.'s delegation to Lt.-Col. Pedro Alberni of authority to grant missionaries retirement permits; (Jan 15) rules on artillery cpl. Francisco Aguilera at Punta de Guijarros [the San Diego harbor battery] filling a sergeant vacancy.
