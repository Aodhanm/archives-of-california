# his retirement petition.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n356** · orig. pp. 484 · Sav 357 · 1800, Mar 28, Monterey · detail Transcription

his retirement petition.

> [!note] ⭐ Sal petitions the King (in quintuplicate) for retirement with the rank of Captain, unable to continue "á causa de mis gravísimas enfermedades, avanzada y cansada edad" [his tuberculosis]; begs the governor's favorable report, "esperando ha de mirarme con la caridad á que mi infeliz estado me ha conducido." He died 8 months later. → [[sal-papers-project]] (the retirement petition; cf. the bio's "Mar 18 1800" — this copy dated Mar 28).
