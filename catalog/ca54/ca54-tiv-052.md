Doc IV-52 — Branciforte (relaying Godoy) → Borica — "Desconfianza contra los Ingleses."

> [!note] ⭐ A Royal Order from the Príncipe de la Paz (Godoy), 6 May 1796: re the provisioning of English captains "Moore" and "Loke" (English ships) at the Santa Barbara Channel & Bahía de Todos Santos (they left cloth "en demostración de gratitud") — the King approves the precautions but orders the governor to "prohíba absolutamente semejante tráfico, en caso de que la sagacidad de los Ingleses proyectara abrir comercio." ⭐ Post-Nootka royal distrust of the English on the CA coast.
