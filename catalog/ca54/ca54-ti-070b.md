# Nava → Gov. of Cal. — "Llegada de hombres científicos á Loreto," 10 May 1792

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n103** · orig. p. 465 · Sav 104 · 1792-05-10, San Diego · Savage abstract

Acknowledges receipt of the governor's oficio of **20 January** announcing the arrival at Loreto, aboard the frigate ***Princesa***, of "el Naturalista Dⁿ Joseph Longinos con el botánico Don Jaime Sensebe [Senseve]" — the **Royal Scientific Expedition to New Spain** reaching the Californias (Longinos Martínez's 1792 journey). Pairs with record 1070d, the Viceroy's fuller statement of the expedition's purpose.

Record recovered in the 2026-07-02 verify pass: the old Doc 70 open bound (~n105) concealed this abstract, which sits directly under the Neve *Instrucción*'s closing signature on n103.
