# resolution of the Ávila affair.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n150–n151** · orig. pp. 69–76 · Sav 151–152 · 1792, Sep–Dec · detail Abstracts

resolution of the Ávila affair.

> [!note] (Manuel de Vargas → Argüello, Sep 23) many vecinos attest Ávila's bad conduct — "consideran como un bien á la humanidad el hacerle salir de ese lugar"; (Arrillaga → Argüello, Dec 14) orders Ávila embarked on a San Blas ship — i.e. deported from California. Also two short notes (widow Josefa Gandiaga's accounts; deserter José Pedraza remitted to the naval commander per Nava's order).
