# Otter deserters + foreign-trade ban + Fort San Joaquín rain damage.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n393–n394** · orig. pp. 721–24 · Sav 394–395 · 1796, Dec 6, Monterey · detail Abstracts

Otter deserters + foreign-trade ban + Fort San Joaquín rain damage.

> [!note] The 11 English & American men Capt. Door left near Monterey to be shipped to San Blas; acknowledgment of the R.O. prohibiting admitting foreign ships / paying them in goods; ⭐⭐ the damage the rains caused to the *fuerte de San Joaquín* (SF), with measures so future rains don't ruin the artillery/carriages. → [[presidio-decay-project]] (Fort SJ rain decay, 1796), [[bay-area-borderlands project]].
