# delegation of powers on the death of Lt.-Col. Pedro de Alberni.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n58–n59** · orig. pp. 260 · Sav 59–60 · 1802, Jul, México · detail Abstract

delegation of powers on the death of Lt.-Col. Pedro de Alberni.

> [!note] On the death of Lt.-Col. Pedro de Alberni (Catalan Volunteers cmdr.), his delegated authority (granting missionary retirement licenses) is transferred to Capt. Goycoechea, cmdt. Sta Bárbara. ⭐ Dates Alberni's death to 1802.
