# return of the unfit stonemason Manuel Dorotea.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n141–n143** · orig. pp. 18–20 · Sav 142–143 · 1792–93, Monterey · detail Abstract

return of the unfit stonemason Manuel Dorotea.

> [!note] (1793 Dec 30) Argüello returned the apprentice stonemason Manuel Dorotea to San Blas as incapable; (1792 Nov 18) surgeon Pablo Soler had certified the incapacity of "Manuel Dorotea Ruiz" as *oficial cantero*.
