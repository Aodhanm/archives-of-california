# slavery policy (2 docs).

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n332** · orig. pp. 59–62 · Sav 333 · 1785, May 19, Chihuahua · detail Abstracts

slavery policy (2 docs).

> [!note] ⭐ Historically notable: the King resolved to facilitate the entry of black slaves into the American dominions to develop agriculture, lowering import duty to ~9 pesos/head "sin diferencia de edad, sexo ni clase"; + the R.O. of 4 Nov 1781 abolishing the branding of slaves (face/back) at the ports of the Indies. (Promulgated in CA though slavery was not practiced there.) → social/economic history.
