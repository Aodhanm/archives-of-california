# arrival of a King's ship.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n52** · orig. pp. 237 · Sav 53 · 1794, Nov 1, San Francisco · detail Abstract

arrival of a King's ship.

> [!note] On 29 Oct the San Blas frigate "Nuestra Señora del Rosario" (a) "La Princesa," from Manila returning to San Blas, anchored; her commander Francisco Maurelle reported sighting the nao de Filipinas (Manila galleon) on the 21st, likely to anchor at Monterey. ⚠️ Savage names the commandant "Joseph Moraga" — but founder José Joaquín Moraga died 1785; flag as a likely Savage slip (the SF commandant in late 1794 was Argüello/Sal).
