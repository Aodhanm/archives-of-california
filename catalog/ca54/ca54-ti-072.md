# service record (*hoja de servicios*) of Sgt./Alférez Mariano Carrillo.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n108–n109** · orig. pp. 108 · Sav 109 · 1781, Dec 31, Monterey · detail Transcription

service record (*hoja de servicios*) of Sgt./Alférez Mariano Carrillo.

> [!note] Began service 24 Jul 1756; ~25 yrs by end of 1781. ⭐ Present at the 1769–70 founding expedition to the ports of Monterey & San Diego — held a post with 7 men and attended the scurvy-stricken crew of the packet *San Carlos*; assaulted by gentiles, defended with 5 *soldados de cuera* + 3 volunteers; commanded squads at Monterey & San Diego; in the 1775 San Diego mission revolt ("la sublevación de los indios cristianos de la cristiandad de San Diego, y gentiles de la Sierra, el año de 75") made repeated *entradas*, "castigó y apresó toda la cabilla." → [[indian-raiding-article]] (1775 SD revolt), founding-era prosopography.
