# "Sobre buques que han pasado frente al puerto."

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n120** · orig. pp. 660 · Sav 121 · 1792, Oct 31, San Francisco · detail Abstract

"Sobre buques que han pasado frente al puerto."

> [!note] A second Sal letter of 31 Oct 1792 (companion to the boundary letter i.119): on the 21st a frigate passed the harbor mouth and saluted with 4 cannon (answered in kind), thought to be the *"Concepción"[?]*; on the 24th two frigates + a sloop/brig seen near the mouth heading apparently north, judged to be Vancouver's ships en route to Monterey. ⭐ Sal's running ship-movement intelligence during the Vancouver visit. → [[vancouver-voyage-discovery-1798]], [[bodega-failure-1793]], [[sal-papers-project]].
