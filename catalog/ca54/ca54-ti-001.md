# Cuenta de la Compañía presidial de Monterey. Full double-entry account.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n1–n4** · orig. pp. 12–18 · Sav 1–4 · 1794 (year-end, covering 1793–94) · detail Transcription (Savage copied the ledger in detail)

Cuenta de la Compañía presidial de Monterey. Full double-entry account.

> [!note] Cargo (income), total $68,215: existencias fin-1793 $16,950; *memoria* from México $32,072; from San Blas $1,725; tobacco-admin revenue $2,125; *diezmos* $461; Guadalajara settler-family support $245; artisan balances (Manuel Ruiz, Joaquín & Salvador Rivera, Pedro González García) $832; mission debts owed — San Diego $1,914, San Francisco $1,891, San Carlos $838, Soledad $69, San Antonio $1,184, San Luis $1,851, Santa Clara $331, Santa Cruz $831 (= $7,413). Data (expenditure), total $48,569: troop/friar/artisan wages $15,524; the 4-presidio *donativo* to the Crown $7,410; supplies to ships anchoring at Monterey 1792–3 $3,022 + 1794 $3,228; ⭐⭐ "Socorros hechos á los desertores de la embarcación inglesa del mando del Capitán George Vancouver — $392"; 1793 mission debts paid in 1794 $7,818. Closing balance $1,571 in the habilitado's favor, 31 Dec 1794.
