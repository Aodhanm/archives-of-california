# supply/*situado* *memorias* for the 4 presidios, the Catalan Volunteers, and the artillery *piquete*.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n165–n170** · orig. pp. 130–80 · Sav 166–171 · 1797–98, Jan 31, México · detail Abstracts (fiscal)

supply/*situado* *memorias* for the 4 presidios, the Catalan Volunteers, and the artillery *piquete*.

> [!note] ⭐ 1797 presidio fiscal balances: Monterey déficit $6,850; San Francisco déficit $1,431; Santa Barbara existencia $3,152; San Diego existencia $3,097. Compañía de Voluntarios de Cataluña *situado* $11,315 (split into *piquetes* at SF $5,941 / Monterey $1,057 / San Diego $4,314). Plus a separate artillery *piquete* (piquete de Artillería destacado en la Nueva California) account. → [[presidio-decay-project]] (Monterey & SF running annual deficits against the *situado*).
