# Antonia Victoria Carrillo, widow of Capt. (graded) José Francisco de Ortega — pension petition to the King.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n121** · orig. pp. 697–719 · Sav 122 · 1799, Apr 15, Santa Barbara · detail Abstract

Antonia Victoria Carrillo, widow of Capt. (graded) José Francisco de Ortega — pension petition to the King.

> [!note] Details her husband's services in Alta & Baja Cal. ⭐ Ortega died 3 Feb 1798 at Santa Barbara, retired for infirmities "en clase de disperso," left no will and insufficient estate for his family. Founding-era prosopography (death date of the famous first SD/SB commandant).
