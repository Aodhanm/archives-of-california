# Revilla Gigedo → Gov. Romeu — three dispatches, 29 May 1792

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n105** · orig. pp. 472–77 · Sav 106 · 1792-05-29, México · Savage abstract ("3 doc.")

**(1)** Habilitado **José de Zúñiga** (San Diego), now promoted ("ascendido á Capᵃⁿ"), is granted leave to come to México to settle "sus bienes patrimoniales"; Comandante-General **Nava** will designate "un oficial idóneo y honrado que se encargue no solo de la habilitᵒⁿ de la Compᵃ sino de otros intereses y ramos de Rl Hacdᵃ"; Zúñiga must remain at San Diego until relieved, then may pass to México "ántes de pasar á ocupar su puesto en **Tucson (Sonora)**."

**(2)** Approves the governor having sent **Tte. José Francisco de Ortega** to Loreto to replace **Alfz. Joseph Mª Estrada**, habilitado of that presidio, who died; measures taken to fill the vacancy Ortega leaves.

**(3)** Orders that **Tte. de dragones José Fernández Loredo** be kept in mind for vacancies in California.

Record recovered in the 2026-07-02 verify pass (old Doc 70 fuzzy bound concealed leaves n103–n105).
