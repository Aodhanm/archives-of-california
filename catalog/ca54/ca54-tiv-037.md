# leave, the Russia war, and succession.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n397** · orig. pp. 838–9 · Sav 398 · 1799, Dec 31, México · detail Transcription

leave, the Russia war, and succession.

> [!note] On Borica's 9-month leave to Mexico — questions whether his presence is needed "considerando que España está en guerra con la Rusia"; if he goes, Arrillaga (Loreto) takes the government and Pedro Alberni transfers to Monterey "por ser el punto mas importante… para mandar las armas." → [[russian-california]] (Spain-Russia war reaches CA, 1799), governance.
