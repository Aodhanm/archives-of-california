# Alberni: smallpox quarantine + Branciforte water.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n208** · orig. pp. 428–33 · Sav 209 · 1798 + 1797, SF / Santa Cruz · detail Abstracts

Alberni: smallpox quarantine + Branciforte water.

> [!note] (Alberni → Borica, 30 Apr 1798) ships wintering at SF: if anyone aboard is infected with smallpox, no landing permitted; (1797) the Branciforte settlers may draw water from an arroyo ~200 *varas* off — the ordered *saca de agua* unnecessary.
