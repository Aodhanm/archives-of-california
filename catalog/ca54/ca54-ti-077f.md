# Sal → Arrillaga — departure for the San José sumaria, 1 Nov 1792

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n117** · orig. p. 622 · Sav 118 · 1792-11-01, San Francisco · Savage abstract

"Avisa que por órden del Tenᵗᵉ José Argüello pasará á San José á hacer una sumaria con motivo de heridas que recibió el vecino **Claudio Alires [Alvires]**, dejando encargado del presidio al Sargᵗᵒ **Pedro Amador**."

The document that explains the unsigned Vancouver-arrival dispatch's attribution problem (see 1077e): if Sal was still at San José on 13–14 Nov, the presidio — and the arrival report — were Amador's; the pencil note in the copy nonetheless credits Sal. For the [[sal-papers]] chronology this fortnight matters.

Recovered in the 2026-07-05 verify pass (n115–n118 gap).
