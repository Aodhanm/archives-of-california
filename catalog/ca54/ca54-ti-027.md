# founding new missions in Baja CA.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n26–n28** · orig. pp. 103–104 · Sav 27–29 · 1791, Oct 4 · detail Transcription

founding new missions in Baja CA.

> [!note] Dominican mission Santo Tomás de Aquino (at San Solano) founded; 3 more proposed in the sierras between Rosario & Santo Domingo; $1,000/foundation; over Gov. Pedro Fages's objection; framed as facilitating Alta–Baja CA communication; proposed presidio at Laguna de Santa Olaya. (Addressed to Gov. Romeu — d. Apr 1792, cf. the Sal/Argüello command shuffle.)
