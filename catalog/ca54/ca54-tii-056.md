# Solá *borradores*, pt. 4 (artillery & campaigns).

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n238–n245** · orig. pp. 547–73 · Sav 239–246 · 1819, Jul–Dec, San Juan Bautista / Rancho del Rey / Monterey · detail Bulk (terse)

Solá *borradores*, pt. 4 (artillery & campaigns).

> [!note] ⭐ 74 militia artillerymen province-wide to be paid as veterans "por todo el tiempo que estén sobre las armas" (→ [[presidio-garrison-artillery-register]]); ⭐ Lt. Manuel Gómez commissioned to emplace up to 3 cannons at Mission San Juan Bautista "para impedir el desembarco de algunos invasores" (post-Bouchard coastal defense, 1819 → register, [[bay-area-borderlands project]]); ⭐ repair of the batteries + Maestranza (ordnance shop); 40 reinforcements arrived from San Blas; Indian campaigns — Estudillo's 30-man *piquete* after horse-raiders, and the successful campaign vs. the Ranchería de los Muquelemnes (Mokelumne) reported to the Viceroy (→ [[indian-raiding-article]]; = mil-history register #33); Padre Narciso Durán named. Routine: militia pay, licenses, Sgt. José Sánchez → comisionado of Pueblo San José, Martínez transfer request.
