# Doc III-18 — 1793 armament report + musters.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo III), scan n317–n322** · orig. pp. 622–718, · Sav 318–323 · 1780, 1793, 1835 · detail Transcription/abstracts

Doc III-18 — 1793 armament report + musters.

> [!note] ⭐⭐⭐ Goycoechea → Arrillaga, 9 Jul 1793 — province armament (the 1793 Nootka-crisis defense assessment): Monterey = 2 six-pounder cannon + 1 small field gun (3 guns), 16-man garrison, 90 pairs of pistols needing repair, 28@ powder, 200 flints — "should be reinforced with troops + 2 small cannon"; Santa Barbara = ~50 escopetas (23 good), short 27 swords, lances complete. → [[bodega-failure-1793]], [[presidio-garrison-artillery-register]]. Also: Santa Barbara 1793 muster = 61; the Mazatlán Squadron at San Luis Rey, 1835 = 33 men (Portilla/Ibarra; Mexican-era); 1780 Croix regs (officers marrying for widow-pensions; war-tax bando vs. the English).
