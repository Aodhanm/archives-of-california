# diary, expedition against "indios agresores."

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n30–n31** · orig. pp. 117–120 · Sav 31–32 · 1795, Feb 14 · detail Transcription (diary)

diary, expedition against "indios agresores."

> [!note] Out of Mission Santa Cruz, 9 men + 1 cpl., chasing fugitive Christian Indians + horse thieves; raid on Rancho San Benito; aggressors had fled to the Sierra; horse-thief Indian caught by mission Indians; a gentile captured; some horses recovered; returned the 13th. → [[indian-raiding-article]] (fugitive-neophyte/horse-theft punitive expedition).
