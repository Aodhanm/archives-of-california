# Royal Order banning officers' *repartimientos*.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n181–n182** · orig. pp. 50, · Sav 182–183 · 1788, Sep 4, Monterey · detail Transcription

Royal Order banning officers' *repartimientos*.

> [!note] Transmits a Royal Order (via the Cmdt.-Gen., from José de Gálvez) prohibiting captains from making "repartimientos y habilitaciones" to the soldiers of their own companies (the company-supply profiteering abuse, per the army *ordenanzas*, tomo 1, trat. 11, tít. 10) — to be enforced in presidios, *compañías volantes*, and all troops of New Spain. ⭐ Addressed to Sal at Monterey, 1788 (then storekeeper/alférez). → [[otter-corruption-project]], [[sal-papers-project]].
