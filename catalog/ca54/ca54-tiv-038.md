# CA–New Mexico frontier/exploration report (cont.).

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n398–n401** · orig. pp. 75, · Sav 399–402 · ~1796–99 · detail Abstract

CA–New Mexico frontier/exploration report (cont.).

> [!note] On the feasibility of opening overland communication between California & New Mexico: trading bulls for *abalorios* (beads) with the Colorado Indians (8 bulls = 48 pesos); the "tierra blanca" Indians who treated the deserters well; the war-skilled enemies in between. → [[bay-area-borderlands project]] (the CA–NM road).
