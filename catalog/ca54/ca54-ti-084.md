# the presidio commandants' joint petition on inadequate pay (with Borica's endorsement).

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n123–n124** · orig. pp. 757–9 · Sav 124–125 · 1799, Dec 15, Santa Barbara · detail Transcription

the presidio commandants' joint petition on inadequate pay (with Borica's endorsement).

> [!note] The Alta CA presidio commandants jointly petition the viceroy over the penury of their limited salaries (memorial carried by Capt. Grajera). ⭐⭐ Gov. Borica's endorsement (29 Dec): it is all true — their short pay keeps them "en las mayores estrecheces," not enough "para comer y vestir ni medianamente"; their children often go barefoot; their wives wash other people's laundry, sew, and bake bread to help support the family; same for the alféreces of the 5 presidios and the Loreto teniente; recommends +$150/year. Closes: *"Conozco las graves atenciones del Erario, pero me juzgaría culpable si no expresase ingenuamente mi sentir."* ⭐ Vivid evidence of the material poverty of the Spanish officer corps in California. → [[presidio-decay-project]], [[sal-papers-project]], [[bay-area-borderlands project]].
