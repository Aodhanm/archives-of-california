# labor for the San Diego fort.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n62** · orig. pp. 291 · Sav 63 · 1793, Aug 22, Monterey · detail Abstract

labor for the San Diego fort.

> [!note] Preceded by the timber bill-of-materials for the SD fort (80 posts of 5 *varas*, 80 beams of 7 *varas*, 600 roofing *morillos*). Argüello requests 12 yoke of oxen, fully harnessed, + 12 Indians who know how to drive them from Mission Carmel to haul the timber to the *embarcadero*. ⭐ Mission-Indian labor + oxen mobilized for the San Diego coastal-fort works. → [[presidio-decay-project]] (Indian construction labor).
