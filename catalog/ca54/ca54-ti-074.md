San Francisco presidial company muster. *(Bouchard-raid year)*

> [!note] Capt. Luis Argüello; Tente Gabriel Moraga; Alférez Santiago Argüello; cadet José Joaquín de Estudillo; 2 sgts., 8 cpls., 56 soldiers. ⭐ Artillery: Sgt. Manuel Gómez (graded alférez) + 1 soldier at Monterey; only 2 soldiers at the fuerte de San Joaquín. Invalids: 1 alférez, 1 sgt., 3 cpls., 6 sol. Same in August. ⭐ SF garrison in 1818 (year of the [[bouchard-raid-1818]]) — thin artillery manning of Fort San Joaquín. → [[bouchard-raid-1818]], [[presidio-decay-project]].
