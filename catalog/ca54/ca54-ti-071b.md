# Arrillaga → Argüello — "Sobre entrega de marineros desertores ingleses," 27 Mar 1793

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n107** · orig. p. 482 · Sav 108 · 1793-03-27, San Diego · Savage abstract

"Dispone que **no entregue los 5 desertores ingleses á ningún buque de esa nación que no sea del mando de Vancouver, y á éste solo cuando pague sus gastos**."

⭐⭐⭐ Interim Governor Arrillaga's standing order to the Monterey commandant on the **five English deserter sailors** — they are to be handed to no British vessel except one under **Vancouver's own command**, and to him only against payment of the expenses of their keep. Falls between Vancouver's first (Nov 1792) and second (Oct–Nov 1793) California calls, in the season of Arrillaga's hard line on the second visit — pairs with the C-A 6 Vancouver↔Arrillaga dossier and the C-A 54 Sal/Bodega corpus.
