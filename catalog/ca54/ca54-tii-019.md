# "Relación de la Artillería, pertrechos y municiones que existen en la batería de San Joaquín."

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n155–n158** · orig. pp. 112–16 · Sav 156–159 · 1796, Dec 31, San Francisco · detail Abstract + partial figures

"Relación de la Artillería, pertrechos y municiones que existen en la batería de San Joaquín."

> [!note] ⭐⭐ Inventory of the artillery, equipment & munitions of the Castillo de San Joaquín, 31 Dec 1796 (two years after the 1794 build), signed Lt.-Col. Pedro de Alberni. The figures do continue onto n158 (I earlier thought only the title was copied): includes 2 mounted field cannon ("2 cañones de Campaña montados"), 600 gunflints, 12 arrobas of powder, 18 of balls, 4 of munitions (4-pounder shot). ⚠️ The full gun list may be abbreviated by Savage — for the complete inventory cite the original St. Pap., Sac., MS., ii. 112–16. ⭐ A datapoint for the SF battery's armament late 1796 (cf. the 1820 "8 cannon, 2 broken" in [[presidio-decay-project]]). → [[presidio-decay-project]], [[bodega-failure-1793]], [[bay-area-borderlands project]].
