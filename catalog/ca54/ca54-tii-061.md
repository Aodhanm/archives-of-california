# soldier Flores's illicit cattle-trade with the gentiles.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n257–n258** · orig. pp. 126, · Sav 258–259 · 1800, Apr 4, Monterey · detail Transcription

soldier Flores's illicit cattle-trade with the gentiles.

> [!note] Soldier Bernardino [/Hermenegildo ⚠️] Flores spent "13, 20 días y hasta un mes" slaughtering cattle on the Monterey plain "para comerciar ilícitamente con los Gentiles" — recommends his banishment to San Blas in irons; accomplice Rafael Gómez (less culpable, "inducido de Flores") released with admonishment. → [[sal-papers-project]] (late Sal letter), [[otter-corruption-project]] (illicit frontier trade).
