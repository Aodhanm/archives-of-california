# "Sobre inconveniencia de construir fortalezas."

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n380–n381** · orig. pp. 56, · Sav 381–382 · 1796, Sep 21, Monterey · detail Transcription

"Sobre inconveniencia de construir fortalezas."

> [!note] ⭐⭐ With engineer Córdova's plans of the SF port + Santa Cruz ensenada: building fortifications capable of covering the Peninsula's main points "erogaría crecidos gastos" — better to increase the cavalry and keep 2–3 ships always at San Francisco; "2 fragatas de guerra en San Fran.co bastarán" against single ships, and "en aumentando la población todo hombre será miliciano." ⭐ The governor himself declining to fortify — Spain's deliberate choice not to harden the coast. → [[presidio-decay-project]], [[bay-area-borderlands project]], [[bodega-failure-1793]].
