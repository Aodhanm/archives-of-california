muster + equipment inventory, SF presidial company. *(out-of-sequence 1787 doc)*

> [!note] Argüello (promoted Lt. 18 Jun 1787, "prev. alférez of Santa Barbara"); Alférez Ramón Luis de la Vega; Sgt. Juan Pablo Grijalva; 32 men incl. Gabriel Moraga, Alfz Miranda, the founding families. ⭐ Equipment: SF presidio had "2 cañones montados" in 1787 (vs. one burst cannon by 1792 — decline baseline for [[bodega-failure-1793]]); 174 horses/mules; mostly broken small arms; 84@ powder, 332 gunflints.
