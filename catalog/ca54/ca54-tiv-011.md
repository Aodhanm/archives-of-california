# roster of all California missionaries (1797).

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n338–n341** · orig. pp. 15, · Sav 339–342 · 1797, Oct 20 / Nov 9, San Diego · detail Transcription

roster of all California missionaries (1797).

> [!note] The complete 1797 list of friars serving the 13 old missions (2 each), for the Colegio de San Fernando — e.g. San Carlos: Lasuén, Mariano Payeras, Francisco Pujol; San Gabriel: Cruzado, M. Sánchez; SJ Capistrano: Fuster, Santiago; San Diego: Mariner, Panella; with the names of friars retiring (Diego García, J. M.ª Fernández, etc.) + Borica's certification. → mission prosopography.
