# mission-escort inspection + card-game ban.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n130–n131** · orig. pp. 830–1 · Sav 131–132 · 1793, Oct 3, Monterey · detail Abstract

mission-escort inspection + card-game ban.

> [!note] Orders Parrilla to inspect the escorts of his district — Mission Soledad → San Antonio → San Luis [Obispo] — reviewing the troops' mounts, arms, and clothing, and to promulgate the prohibition of card games (*juegos de naipes*) (bando of 23 Sep), with a 15–20-day deadline. ← End of Tomo I (n0–n131).
