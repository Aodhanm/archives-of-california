# royal approval of Neve's *Reglamento*.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n355–n356** · orig. pp. 31, · Sav 356–357 · ~1782 (royal) · detail Abstract

royal approval of Neve's *Reglamento*.

> [!note] The King approves Gov. Felipe de Neve's proposals & the new *Reglamento* for California — incl. the Santa Barbara presidio (Canal de Sta Bárbara) and a Spanish pueblo on the Colorado; the Viceroy to assist. The foundational 1781 governance code. → governance, borderlands.
