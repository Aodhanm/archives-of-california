# requests retirement.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n106–~n107** · orig. pp. 480–1 · Sav 107 · 1796, May 1, San Diego · detail Transcription

requests retirement.

> [!note] Service record: 33 years' service at the Terrenate (Sonora) presidio + San Diego; 9 campaigns vs. Apaches & Seris (wounded twice); 11 *mariscadas*; in Cal. 10 *salidas*, 8 as commander to punish "indios presidiales" & pursue fugitives; age 51, leg wound. ⭐ Gov. Borica's endorsement (Monterey, May 1796): grant retirement at half-pay of alférez + grade of teniente, for his "celo, exactitud y valor." → [[indian-raiding-article]], prosopography (Grijalva = Anza-expedition veteran).
