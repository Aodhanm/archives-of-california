# a fault committed; English frigate "Discovery".

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n51** · orig. pp. 228–9 · Sav 52 · 1792, Dec 31, San Francisco · detail Transcription/abstract

a fault committed; English frigate "Discovery".

> [!note] NEW Vancouver-fraternization doc. Sal admits he did wrong in permitting Alférez [José] Pérez to travel to Monterey aboard Vancouver's English frigate "Descubierta" [*Discovery*], and submits to whatever punishment is imposed "por haber hecho lo que no debió." Regrets that [Pérez] wrote to the gov. from Monterey but dated the letter from San Francisco — "probablemente creyó que él (Sal) no daría parte de su ida á aquel presidio." ⭐ Shows the degree of Spanish–British fraternization during Vancouver's 1792 visit and the command anxiety it caused. → [[vancouver-voyage-discovery-1798]], [[bodega-failure-1793]], [[sal-papers-project]]. ⚠️ Verify verbatim wording before quoting.
