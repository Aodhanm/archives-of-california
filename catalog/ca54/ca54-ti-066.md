**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n64** · orig. pp. 293–5, · Sav 65 · 1802, Jul 13, México · detail Abstract

"Sobre obras de fortificación en San Francisco."

> [!note] Approves and closes the accounts of the SF habilitados Raymundo Carrillo & José Argüello for the works of *renovación del fuerte de San Joaquín* AND *la batería de Yerba Buena*. ⭐⭐ Confirms (a) the Castillo de San Joaquín was renovated c. 1800–02 and (b) a second SF battery at Yerba Buena (the cove) existed by then — SF coastal-defense chronology after the 1792 "one burst cannon." → [[presidio-decay-project]], [[bodega-failure-1793]], [[bay-area-borderlands project]]. *(Followed at p. 305–6 by a Feb 1 1802 order paying a small balance owed the SF presidio.)*
