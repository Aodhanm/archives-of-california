# convicts to CA + Goycoechea on troop pay arrears.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo V), scan n434** · orig. pp. 119 · Sav 435 · 1796, México / Santa Barbara · detail Abstracts

convicts to CA + Goycoechea on troop pay arrears.

> [!note] Branciforte sends convicts (*presidiarios*) to the CA presidios for 9-yr terms; ⭐⭐ Goycoechea (Sta Bárbara): troops paid 1½+ years in arrears per the Reglamento → "muchos soldados han muerto careciendo de sus alcances" (died without their back-pay) and others never enjoyed it; recommends the Real Hacienda advance half the *situado*. → [[presidio-decay-project]] (the pay/*situado* crisis).
