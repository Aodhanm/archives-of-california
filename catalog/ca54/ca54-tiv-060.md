# the Bouchard deserters' *causa* + a Russian brig.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n457–n460** · orig. pp. 255, · Sav 458–460 · 1820, Monterey / Sta Bárbara · detail Transcription/abstract

the Bouchard deserters' *causa* + a Russian brig.

> [!note] ⭐⭐ Viceroy Conde del Venadito orders a *causa* formed against the 4 men "que se pasaron… de los buques insurgentes de Buenos Aires" (Bouchard's ships) — named Nicolás Echeverría, Mateo José Pascual, Pedro Caldívar, Juan de la Rosa — Estudillo to prosecute (→ [[bouchard-raid-1818]]); ⭐ a "Bergantín Ruso" set sail from the Sta Bárbara roadstead, 21 Sep 1820 (→ [[russian-california]]); a customs note.
