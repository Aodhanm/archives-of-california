# Habil.do José Pérez Fernández → Gov. Borica (2 docs).

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n51–n52** · orig. pp. 234–7 · Sav 52–53 · 1795, Jan 31, San Francisco · detail Transcription/abstract

Habil.do José Pérez Fernández → Gov. Borica (2 docs).

> [!note] (1) Accounts: the Habil.do Gen.l will owe the presidio only $1,490 after deductions (from $9,349 less $1,572 tobacco, $53 mail, $4,246 *libranzas*); asks the gov. to adopt measures for the relief of soldiers & families. (2) ⭐ The cost of the Castillo/fuerte de San Joaquín [the new SF Fort Point battery at Cantil Blanco] amounts to $1,503.4½, advanced by the habilitación. → [[presidio-decay-project]], [[bodega-failure-1793]] (construction cost of the battery built 1794).
