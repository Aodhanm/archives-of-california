# muster roll, San Francisco presidial company.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n7–n9** · orig. pp. 25–26 · Sav 8–10 · 1795, Dec 31 · detail Transcription (full roster)

muster roll, San Francisco presidial company.

> [!note] Lt. José Argüello; Sgt. Pedro Amador; Cpls. Miguel Pacheco, Luis Peralta, Manuel Boronda; soldiers = the founding SF/San José families (Bernal, Higuera, Alviso, Galindo, Bojorques, Sánchez, Aceves, Valencia, Castillo). Retired alférez Ramón Lasso (de la Vega); invalids Gabriel Peralta, Ignacio Linares, Justo Altamirano, Ignacio Soto. Balance $5,751 in the company's favor.
