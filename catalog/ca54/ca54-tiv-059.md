# Solá *borradores* (1819, cont.).

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo V), scan n453–n456** · orig. pp. 202–28 · Sav 454–457 · 1819, Monterey · detail Abstracts

Solá *borradores* (1819, cont.).

> [!note] Gov. Argüello (Baja) sends an express having heard "los insurgentes habían atacado á Monterey" (the Bouchard news reaching Baja); Solá presses for the hot-shot furnaces (*hornillos para bala roja*) + roofing finished before the rains (→ [[presidio-decay-project]]); fiscal cost-distribution; troops arriving at the Bahía de los Ángeles.
