# Monterey presidio repair / church / artisans.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n325–n326** · orig. pp. 1–20 · Sav 326–327 · 1792–93 · detail Abstracts

Monterey presidio repair / church / artisans.

> [!note] Nava on the repair of the Monterey presidio (refs the 1789 fire) + the artisan program; Revilla Gigedo suspends the Monterey church works until Fages's plan is "reformed and reduced to simplicity"; the tanner Miguel Sangrador ($400/yr) + Joaquín de Abalos contracted to teach trades mission-to-mission.
