# bundle of orders.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n60–n61** · orig. pp. 284–9 · Sav 61–62 · 1793, Aug, San Francisco · detail Transcription/abstract

bundle of orders.

> [!note] (Aug 11) recognize Lt. León Parrilla at Monterey with his commission + back-pay from the *despacho* date. (Aug 14, 2 docs) receipt of letters; apprehension of an Indian and suspicion that many others are "inquietos," awaiting Cpl. Valdés's report. (Aug 28) acknowledges the 25 Aug arrival at Monterey of the frigate "Aranzazú" (pilot Salvador de Menchaca), warns against disorder while she stays, no landing of wine barrels. (Aug 18) orders timber cut immediately for an esplanade, *tinglado*, etc. for the defense of the San Diego fort, to be shipped on the frigate "Princesa." ⭐ Post-Nootka San Diego coastal-defense works, 1793. → [[presidio-decay-project]], [[indian-raiding-article]].
