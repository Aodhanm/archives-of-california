# list of the annual San Blas supply ships to California.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo V), scan n507–n508** · orig. pp. 106, · Sav 508–509 · 1783–1796 · detail Abstract (table)

list of the annual San Blas supply ships to California.

> [!note] The yearly San Blas → California supply vessels & pilots, 1783–96 — frigates *Favorita*, *Princesa*, *Concepción*, *Aránzazu*; packets *San Carlos*, *Aranzazú*; pilots incl. Esteban Martínez (the 1788 Nootka commander), José Cañizares, Francisco de Eliza (1792), Juan Matute (1790/95, the Bodega-expedition commander), Bertodano. → maritime history / the supply line.
