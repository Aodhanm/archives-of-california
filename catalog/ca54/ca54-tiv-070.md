Romeu: the Malaspina & Longinos Martínez expeditions + governance.

> [!note] Romeu's chest-pain illness ("no se ha rendido"; he died Apr 1792) + his confirmation as governor (R.O. 18 Feb 1790); ⭐ arrival of the naturalist José Longinos Martínez & botanist Jaime Senseve (royal natural-history commission, on the *Princesa*, 1791); ⭐⭐ the corvettes *Descubierta* & *Atrevida* of Alejandro Malaspina's round-the-world expedition at Monterey (Sept 1791, w/ José Bustamante); death of Alférez José M.ª Estrada (Loreto). → scientific voyages, [[bay-area-borderlands project]], governance.
