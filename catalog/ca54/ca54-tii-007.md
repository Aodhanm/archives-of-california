# bundle of short admin notes.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n142–n144** · orig. pp. 24–36 · Sav 143–144 · 1792, Dec, Monterey · detail Abstracts

bundle of short admin notes.

> [!note] Tanner Miguel Sangrador's posting; receipt of the Guadalajara Intendant's order on artisan *ascensos*; Lasuén's artisan distribution; embarkation of the deserter José Antonio Pedraza; duplicate official correspondence; a memorial re the settler Marcos Chaboya's inability to march to San Blas.
