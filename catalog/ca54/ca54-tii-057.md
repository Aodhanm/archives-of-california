# Solá *borradores*, pt. 5.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n246–n253** · orig. pp. 573–89 · Sav 247–254 · 1819 Dec – 1820 Mar, Monterey · detail Bulk (terse)

Solá *borradores*, pt. 5.

> [!note] ⭐⭐ Viceregal order (relayed): the SF presidio company advanced $3,881 in rations to relieve "varios prisioneros Rusos y Anglo-Americanos" held 1808–1815, to be reimbursed via the *situado* (→ [[bay-area-borderlands project]], [[russian-california]] — Russian/Aleut otter-hunters + Anglo-American prisoners at SF). ⭐ 1820 political news: the restoration of the Cortes + the great Cádiz expedition to Chile & Buenos Aires + a royal *indulto* (→ Cádiz-Constitution thread). ⭐ Register: SF Cavalry Company short 9 men; militia artilleryman Briones died; artillery numbers to be reported to the Brigada de Artillería de N.E. Routine: convict Robles's family, timber-cutting, an artilleryman reassigned to SF (affair with a married woman), a foggy ship-sighting.
