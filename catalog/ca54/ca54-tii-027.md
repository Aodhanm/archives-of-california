**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n176–n178** · orig. pp. 209–13 · Sav 177–179 · 1785, May 3, San Gabriel · detail Transcription

Gov. Fages (transmitting the Mexico Audiencia / Rengel) — governance succession.

> [!note] On the deaths of Viceroy Matías de Gálvez and Cmdt.-Gen. Felipe de Neve (both 1784): the Mexico *Audiencia* assumes the Superior Gobierno; José Antonio Rengel acting Cmdt.-Gen.; Fages continues in interim political-military command of the Provincias Internas / Californias pending the King's decision. ⭐ Dates Neve's death (the same Neve whose *Instrucción* is Tomo I Doc 70). → governance, prosopography.
