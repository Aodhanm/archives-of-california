# Instructions for the Mission Santa Cruz escort (25 articles).

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n273–n280** · orig. pp. 143–50, · Sav 274–281 · 1791, Sep 21, San Francisco · detail Transcription

Instructions for the Mission Santa Cruz escort (25 articles).

> [!note] A detailed *instrucción* for the escort commander: rations for soldiers & the 6 mission servants; build a communal *rancho*; tobacco/soap/chocolate allowances; daily Rosary; monthly courier to Santa Clara; weekly arms/munitions/clothing review. ⭐ A Neve-style conciliatory Indian-policy code — disarm Indians before they enter the mission; "buen trato" with the gentiles; if forced to use arms, suspend hostilities the instant they make peace and conciliate "con la mas posible suavidad"; troops not to enter rancherías or harm Indians; watch for pasture-burning; escort the padres; no gambling. ⭐ Parallels the Carrillo Santa Inés *instrucción* (C-A 11). → [[sal-papers-project]], indigenous relations.
