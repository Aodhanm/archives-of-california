# cattle management + Monterey construction. *(out-of-sequence 1788)*

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n178–n179** · orig. pp. 214–17 · Sav 179–180 · 1788, Feb 25, Monterey · detail Abstract

cattle management + Monterey construction. *(out-of-sequence 1788)*

> [!note] No selling cattle on the hoof; slaughter only as needed (hides to Real Hacienda; troops eat meat "á precio cómodo"). Repair of the block of houses beside the Monterey church using convicts (*presidarios*) + off-duty soldiers + a craftsman; soldiers from Sta Barbara & San Diego to cut timber for the works. → [[presidio-decay-project]] (convict/soldier construction labor).
