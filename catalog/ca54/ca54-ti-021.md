# *Obsequio á caballero inglés*.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n20** · orig. pp. 85 · Sav 21 · 1792, Dec 20 · detail Abstract

*Obsequio á caballero inglés*.

> [!note] Provisioning of Vancouver's officers at Monterey: Alférez Josef Bravo Fernández commissioned to gift/supply "los caballeros ingleses"; Mission Santa Cruz gave 100 fanegas of maize, carried on the sloop *Horcasitas*; returned 23 Dec. Spanish corroboration of Vancouver's Monterey provisioning ([[vancouver-voyage-discovery-1798]]). Cite: St. Pap., Sac., MS., i. 20 (scan n20).
