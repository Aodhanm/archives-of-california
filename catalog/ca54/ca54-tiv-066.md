# officer-promotion *ternas* (incl. Sal's).

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n477–n481** · orig. pp. 76–79, · Sav 478–482 · 1791 + 1795 + 1796 · detail Transcription

officer-promotion *ternas* (incl. Sal's).

> [!note] Romeu 1791 (SF alférez: Carrillo, Pérez Fernández, Amador — "los 3 son dignos, pero Carrillo es mas inteligente en papeles"); Arrillaga 1796 (Loreto teniente: Cota, Pérez Fernández, Bernal); ⭐⭐ Borica, 1 Feb 1795 — terna for the Monterey teniente post (vacant by León Parrilla's "incapacidad absoluta"): proposes Hermenegildo Sal first (alférez, "12 años 10 meses 18 días… los 2 meses y 10 días de Sargento"), then Pablo Grijalva. The terna behind Sal's 27 Oct 1795 teniente commission. → [[sal-papers-project]] (⚠️ "2 mo 10 days as sgt" implies his alférez date was ~late Jul 1782, not 29 May — reconcile with Doc III-2).
