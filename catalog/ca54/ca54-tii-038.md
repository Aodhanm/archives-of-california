# Real Tribunal de Cuentas / Fiscal de Real Hacienda — expediente on the SF presidio's 1801 construction accounts.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n192–n194** · orig. pp. 305–7 · Sav 193–195 · 1803, Feb 27, México · detail Transcription

Real Tribunal de Cuentas / Fiscal de Real Hacienda — expediente on the SF presidio's 1801 construction accounts.

> [!note] The SF habilitado's accounts for construction in 1801 — the guard house, Cuartel (barracks), Almacenes (warehouses), quarters for the Volunteers detachment, and 4 houses for cavalry-troop families — total $2,143; the Tribunal faulted it for lacking a prior estimate/budget/permit. (+ Arrillaga's 1803 orders to continue necessary presidio works.) → [[presidio-decay-project]] (SF building program, 1801).
