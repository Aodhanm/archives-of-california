# Solá *borradores*, pt. 2.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n222–n229** · orig. pp. 511–33 · Sav 223–230 · 1818 May – 1819 Feb, Monterey · detail Bulk (terse)

Solá *borradores*, pt. 2.

> [!note] Cadet appointments (Joaquín Estudillo → Monterey company; José Guadalupe Moraga → San Diego company); cattle payments to Mission San Juan Bautista; discharges; missionary aid; promotion of Ignacio Martínez (Sta Bárbara). ⭐ Militia-artillery allocation (24 Jul 1817): 23 artilleros milicianos for San Francisco, 20 Monterey, 12 Santa Barbara, 16 San Diego (→ [[presidio-garrison-artillery-register]]). ⭐⭐ French merchant corvette *Bordelais* under Camille de Roquefeuil (Sept–Nov 1818) — same ship as the prior year, bound for China, extending the NW-coast fur trade (*peletería*); allowed only strict necessities, no crew–shore contact, must pay duties (→ [[bay-area-borderlands project]]). ⭐ Foreign "rebel" ships off Baja (San Quintín / San Vicente, Feb 1819) — 3 vessels watching the coast, judged "los rebeldes… ya tienen otro buque de armamento… acaso á alguna parte van á dar el golpe"; hot-shot (*hornillos de bala roja*) ordered (→ [[bay-area-borderlands project]], military).
