# Arrillaga → commandants of San Diego & San Francisco — bar foreign ships from the ports.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n189–n190** · orig. pp. 294–303 · Sav 190–191 · 1803, Jun 13, Loreto · detail Transcription

Arrillaga → commandants of San Diego & San Francisco — bar foreign ships from the ports.

> [!note] Transmits the Viceroy's order (14 Apr) + the Royal Order of 10 Aug 1802: foreign vessels in the Mar del Sur, "so color de la pesca de ballena" (under pretext of whaling), are touching at the California coast and smuggling — they are to be denied entry to the ports per the standing Reales Prevenciones. ⭐ Early-1800s foreign-whaler/contraband problem on the CA coast. → [[bay-area-borderlands project]], [[presidio-decay-project]].
