# Solá *borradores*, pt. 3.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n230–n237** · orig. pp. 531–43 · Sav 231–238 · 1819, Feb–Jun, Monterey/San Antonio · detail Bulk (terse)

Solá *borradores*, pt. 3.

> [!note] Promotions: Gervasio Argüello → alférez (SF), Gabriel Moraga → teniente (Sta Bárbara), Ignacio Martínez subrogated; royal *despachos* & *cédulas* forwarded (Cabo Ignacio Alviso → sgt. 2°, etc.); militia gunners given 4 months' planting leave; a theft *sumaria* (José Antonio & Nieves Robles, father & son → 2 and 4 yrs presidio). ⭐⭐ Aftermath of the Bouchard insurgent attack on Monterey (draft to Alférez Manuel Gómez, re "el encuentro de los insurgentes en Monterey") — names the militia artilleryman José de Jesús Vallejo and the two Monterey cannons (→ [[bouchard-raid-1818]], [[presidio-garrison-artillery-register]]). ⭐ Mass neophyte flight from Mission Santa Cruz (Padre Ramón Olbés: "se fugó la indiada… solo quedaron tres"; Lt. Estudillo + Alférez Estrada sent to recover them) (→ [[indian-raiding-article]]).
