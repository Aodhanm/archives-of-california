# the war-with-France *donativo*.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo V), scan n499–n505** · orig. pp. 99, · Sav 500–506 · 1795–96 · detail Transcription

the war-with-France *donativo*.

> [!note] Branciforte's appeal (17 Jun 1795) for voluntary contributions to the war against France (the Viceroy gave $15,000); a voluntary capitation ($2 Spaniards / $4 castas; Indians give what they can); + the California donation lists — Gov. Borica $4,000; province total $3,881 (SF $707, Monterey/San José $554, Sta Bárb/LA $980, San Diego $634); per-person breakdowns (e.g. Goycoechea $137, Pérez Fernández $100 = ¼ of his salary; "los indios no dieron nada"). → governance, borderlands (wartime fundraising).
