# the chronic construction-failure report.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo V), scan n510–n512** · orig. pp. 100 · Sav 511–513 · n.d. (report) · detail Transcription

the chronic construction-failure report.

> [!note] ⭐ The presidio buildings "han caído á viejas," ruined since 1776, nothing finished — blamed on the lack of skilled labor (*facultativos*), scarce & poor materials (timber 10 leagues off, lime 6), and only Indian/gentile labor "cuyo número varía, como también sus salarios." → [[presidio-decay-project]] (the construction never completed).
