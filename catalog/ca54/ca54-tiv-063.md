# ambush near San Vicente / Santa Catalina.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n468–n470** · orig. pp. 346–9 · Sav 469–471 · ~1800–05, Baja frontier · detail Transcription

ambush near San Vicente / Santa Catalina.

> [!note] ⭐⭐ An escort transporting 5 captured Indians was ambushed: 2 soldiers killed (Juan Camacho + 1), beaten on the head with stones, muskets found loaded but broken; the prisoners escaped; bodies buried at San Vicente. → [[indian-raiding-article]]; logged to the military register as engagement #88 (distinct from the 1804 Santa Catalina victory, #37). ⚠️ Pin the year.
