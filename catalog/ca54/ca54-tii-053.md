# Gov. Pablo Vicente de Solá's outgoing draft letters (*borradores*), pt. 1.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n215–n221** · orig. pp. 493–511 · Sav 216–222 · 1817 Dec – 1818 May, Monterey · detail Bulk (terse)

Gov. Pablo Vicente de Solá's outgoing draft letters (*borradores*), pt. 1.

> [!note] ⭐ Savage's structural note: orig. pp. 493–597 are *borradores* of the governor at Monterey — short outgoing drafts to Luis Argüello / Gabriel Moraga / Manuel Gómez / Francisco Castro. Routine: soldier licenses, planting labor ("emplee gente de razón y no gentiles"), a soldier's marriage (*limpieza de sangre* check), discipline (soldier Gerónimo Soto — shackle + 1 yr public works + jail), company vacancies. ⭐ Notable: Mission San Rafael (founded 1817) "no necesita igual guarnición á la de las otras Misiones"; ⭐⭐ foreign-ship protocol, 18 Apr 1818 — no foreign (European *or American*) officers/crew may land; the ship's commander sends one officer ashore to state his business; if only watering, "un novillo á la playa y el agua diaria" (→ [[bay-area-borderlands project]], Bouchard year); ⭐ the Santa Cruz friar-poisoning case — neophytes accused of poisoning Padres Jayme Escudé & Marcelino Marquínez, investigated by Lt. Moraga (→ [[indian-raiding-article]]).
