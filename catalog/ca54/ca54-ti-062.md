# Viceroy Marquina → acting Gov. (2 items).

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n60** · orig. pp. 281–3 · Sav 61 · 1802, México · detail Abstract

Viceroy Marquina → acting Gov. (2 items).

> [!note] (Jul 12) Peace with England having been made [Treaty of Amiens], withdraw the Baja Cal. militia company unless the gov. finds it convenient to retain it. (Jun 16) Approves $3,395 to build 2 schooners (*goletas*) for the Loreto presidio.
