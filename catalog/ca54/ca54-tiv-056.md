# Krusenstern aid order + SB fiscal.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n443–n444** · orig. pp. 155–60 · Sav 444 · 1803 + 1818 + 1787 · detail Abstracts

Krusenstern aid order + SB fiscal.

> [!note] ⭐⭐ Viceroy Iturrigaray (9 Dec 1803) transmits a R.O. to aid, in case of forced arrival, "los buques de la expedición Americana Rusa bajo órdenes del capitán Krusenstern" — the first Russian circumnavigation (Russian-American Company, 1803–06) (→ [[russian-california]], borderlands); + the Sta Bárbara company account (de la Guerra & Martínez, 1818, *alcance* $25,463); a Fages→Sal note (1787).
