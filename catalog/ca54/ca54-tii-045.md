# Real Orden on San Blas ship freight & commerce regulation.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n204–n207** · orig. pp. 73, · Sav 205–208 · 1803, Jun 20, Loreto · detail Abstract

Real Orden on San Blas ship freight & commerce regulation.

> [!note] On collecting freight (*fletes*) per the *arancel* for goods the commandants, chaplains, officers & missions imported via the brig *Activo* & frigate *Concepción* on the return from California; bars Marina employees/commandants from commerce; registers of cargo + passenger lists at each port. Refs José de Gálvez's 1769 visitation + the Reales Ordenanzas de la Armada. → [[otter-corruption-project]] (officers' private trade on supply ships).
