# Argüello → Arrillaga — "Sobre servicio de correos," 27 Jun 1792

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n115** · orig. p. 606 · Sav 116 · 1792-06-27, Monterey · Savage abstract

Acknowledges the order of 7 May and has passed it to the Comandante of San Francisco "para que despache el día 1º de todos los meses," Argüello himself keeping his correspondence ready "para que sin demora se dé tránsito y se halle en Sta Bárbᵃ del 7 al 8 del mes."

Recovered in the 2026-07-05 verify pass — leaf n115 fell in the uncataloged n115–n118 gap flagged 2026-07-02.
