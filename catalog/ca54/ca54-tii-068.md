"Diario del Reconocimiento de la Misión de Santa Cruz."

> [!note] Sal's expedition diary siting Mission Santa Cruz (via Santa Clara; Padres Salazar & López; 30 head of cattle). Sac.* ii.137 (confirms the scan for the [[sal-papers-project]] bio reference). → indigenous-intelligence (Sugert as broker).
