# Doc IV-69 — Romeu: Eliza's Nootka dispatches + the *Saturnina* from Nootka.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo V), scan n489–n492** · orig. pp. 800, · Sav 490–492 · 1791, San Fernando / Monterey · detail Transcription/abstracts

Doc IV-69 — Romeu: Eliza's Nootka dispatches + the *Saturnina* from Nootka.

> [!note] ⭐⭐ Romeu forwards (July 1791) the dispatches of Francisco de Eliza, Comandante del Presidio de Santa Cruz de Nutka [Nootka], relayed via Loreto to the Viceroy "en la mayor prontitud"; ⭐⭐ announces the schooner *Saturnina* arrived at Monterey "de regreso de Nutka," 14 Oct 1791 (again confirming the *Saturnina* as the NW-coast schooner named in the boundary letter i.119). + ornaments for the new Mission Santa Cruz; the founding of Mission Santo Tomás de Aquino (Baja, Apr 1791). → [[bodega-failure-1793]] (Nootka/Saturnina), [[ca54-bodega-vancouver-deep-read]].
