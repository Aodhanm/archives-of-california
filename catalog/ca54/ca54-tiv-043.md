# annual goods list for the San José & Los Ángeles settlers.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo V), scan n408–n410** · orig. pp. 32–33 · Sav 409–411 · 1796, Dec 19, Monterey · detail Abstract (list)

annual goods list for the San José & Los Ángeles settlers.

> [!note] A detailed *memoria* of effects for the pueblos — textiles (*manta*, *rebozos*, *sayal*, silks, *bombasí de China*), thread/ribbon, and tools (hoes, axes, hammers, augers, carpenter/blacksmith tools, *puntas de arar*). Settler material culture/economy. → [[pueblo-papers-project]].
