# muster roll + arms/munitions report, Monterey presidial company.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo I), scan n4–n7** · orig. pp. 19–21 · Sav 4–8 · 1797, Apr · detail Transcription (full roster + inventory)

muster roll + arms/munitions report, Monterey presidial company.

> [!note] Lt. Cmdt. Goycoechea (alférez post *vacante*); Sgts. Pablo Cota, José Olivera, Raymundo Carrillo; Cpls. Pedro Amador, Ignacio Olivera; ~51 soldiers named (incl. Luis Peña, Martín Reyes, Manuel Boronda, Mariano Cota, José Pico). Totals: 1 lt., 3 sgts., 2 cpls., 51 sol. (4 plazas vacantes); 124 horses, 109 mules. Arms shortfall: short 13 muskets, 6 *cueras*, 19 sword-guards; "armamento en uso… en mediano estado." Munitions: 4@ 17 lb powder, cannon cartridges (bala de á 4), grapeshot, 3,600 musket cartridges, 113@ lead plate, 1,900 gunflints. ⭐ Build note: "se halla concluido el 3.er lienzo de este Presidio [Monterey] con Iglesia, casas de oficiales, casa de capellán, y 5 casas de soldados" (3rd curtain-wall complete, 1797).
