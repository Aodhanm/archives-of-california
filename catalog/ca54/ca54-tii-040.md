# Santa Barbara & San Francisco munitions/arms inventories.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n195–n197** · orig. pp. 342–5 · Sav 196–198 · 1794 + 1797 · detail Abstracts

Santa Barbara & San Francisco munitions/arms inventories.

> [!note] Santa Barbara (Goycoechea, Dec 1794): 128 cannonballs, 100 grapeshot cartridges, "118 cartuchos con bala de á 6," powder, lead plates — *the source of the "118 cartridges" SB figure cited elsewhere.* San Francisco (Argüello, Dec 1797): 1,441 cannonballs (8–24 lb), 245 arrobas powder, 9,814 musket cartridges. ⭐ SF presidial company Dec 1797 = 36 men (1 sgt, 4 cpls, 31 soldiers), each with escopeta, sword, lance, cartridge-box, *cuera* & *adarga*. → [[presidio-decay-project]] (arms/munitions/garrison strength).
