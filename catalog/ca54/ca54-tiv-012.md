# Doc IV-12 — Sal → Arrillaga — fort-builders arrive.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n341–n342** · orig. pp. 164–6 · Sav 342–343 · 1793, Jul 25, San Francisco · detail Transcription

Doc IV-12 — Sal → Arrillaga — fort-builders arrive.

> [!note] ⭐ Sal reports the arrival of the Condestable (master gunner) [Saraicheta?] + the master blacksmith of the San Blas Dept., Francisco Gómez, + 3 artisans, "con la comisión de hacer las fuertes en San Francisco, Monterey y San Diego." The post-Nootka coastal-fort building program (the Castillo de San Joaquín etc.) staffed from San Blas, 1793 — the construction response to the boundary crisis. → [[bodega-failure-1793]], [[presidio-decay-project]].
