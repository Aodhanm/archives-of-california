# Arrillaga's skirmish with the Colorado Indians.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo IV), scan n389–n390** · orig. pp. 65 · Sav 390–391 · 1796, Nov 19, Monterey · detail Abstract

Arrillaga's skirmish with the Colorado Indians.

> [!note] Forwards Arrillaga's report of his Colorado-River reconnaissance & skirmish: a numerous *indiada* attacked his party; "prudencia y humanidad… hasta el caso forzoso de repeler la fuerza con la fuerza" — soldiers' gear hit by arrows, 1 mule killed, 8 horses wounded, a musket & lance lost; 2 enemy dead on the road + 3 in the lagoon. On opening CA–Sonora communication, "ninguno como Arrillaga puede consultar lo que convenga." → [[indian-raiding-article]], borderlands.
