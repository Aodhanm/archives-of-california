# English ship at Guaymas + natural-history request.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo III), scan n322–n323** · orig. pp. 724–26 · Sav 323–324 · 1802, México · detail Abstracts

English ship at Guaymas + natural-history request.

> [!note] ⭐ Viceroy Marquina: an English ship anchored at Guaymas and traded/plundered "más de 300,000 pesos" in gold, silver & coin for overpriced goods (→ [[bay-area-borderlands project]], foreign incursion 1802); + remit natural-history specimens + fine pearls/emeralds for the Queen. ← End of Tomo III (n323, "Fin").
