# maritime + moral-regulation orders.

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n180–n181** · orig. pp. 222–5 · Sav 181–182 · 1785, Jul 18 / Aug 5, Monterey · detail Abstracts

maritime + moral-regulation orders.

> [!note] (1) Don't detain the San Blas ships & the Manila galleon (*nao de Filipinas*) at Monterey longer than necessary. (2) Heads of families to "scrupulously correct" their children's "excesos y desórdenes," held responsible for resulting disturbances.
