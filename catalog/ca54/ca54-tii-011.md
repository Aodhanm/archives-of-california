# José Antonio Rengel (acting Cmdt.-Gen.) → Gov. Pedro Fages — "Prosperidad de Establecimientos."

**C-A 54 (St. Pap. Sacramento, Tomo II), scan n146–n147** · orig. pp. 15–16 · Sav 147–148 · 1785, Apr 6, Chihuahua · detail Transcription

José Antonio Rengel (acting Cmdt.-Gen.) → Gov. Pedro Fages — "Prosperidad de Establecimientos."

> [!note] Commends the flourishing pueblos of San José and Los Ángeles (settler-built houses, abundant harvests in pueblos & missions, livestock increase); approves the order that habilitados not request seed from San Blas (local harvest suffices); commends the Los Ángeles pueblo head Hermenegildo Vicente Feliz; on land grants (*registro de sitios*) for grazing with the Asesor's prior opinion. → [[pueblo-papers-project]] (early LA/San José prosperity, 1785).
