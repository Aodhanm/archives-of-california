# instructions to buy arms at Fort Ross — Vallejo to go to Ross as an envoy of the Comandancia Gen., offer friendship & co

**C-A 20 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n93–95** · Sav p. 94–96 · tomo p. 355–9 · 1833-04-11 · Savage abstract · 

instructions to buy arms at Fort Ross — Vallejo to go to Ross as an envoy of the Comandancia Gen., offer friendship & commerce to the Russian commander and the Governor of Sitka, and negotiate 200 cavalry carbines/tercerolas (or fusiles, cal. 18), 150 sabres, 200 saddles, 100 more arms, 30 infantry shakos, 10 quintales of lead, paid in barter goods at Monterey — *expanded below*
