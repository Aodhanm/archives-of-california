# A statement on INDIAN PROPERTY RIGHTS & the oppression of secularization (✅ author/date pinned 2026-07-02, scan n265). A

**C-A 20 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n264–n265** · Sav p. 264–265 / 55–56 · tomo p. **1835-12-28** · Fray Jesús Mª Vázquez del Mercado (Mission San Antonio de Padua) → via Gefe Político José Castro → Diputación · verbatim transcription · 

A statement on INDIAN PROPERTY RIGHTS & the oppression of secularization (✅ author/date pinned 2026-07-02, scan n265). Author = Fray Jesús María Vázquez del Mercado (✅ name corrected 2026-07-02 against BOTH copies — my first read 'Juan Mª del Prado' was a mis-transcription), of the regular observance of San Francisco, *individuo del Colegio de Ntra. Sra. de Guadalupe de Zacatecas*, and *actual ministro* of Mission San Antonio de Padua; dated San Antonio, 28 Dic 1835; forwarded the same day with a companion representation to Gefe Político José Castro, who elevated it to the Exma Diputación. ⭑ DUPLICATE across series: this is the SAME representation as [[ca61-legislative-records-iii-iv-survey]] Docs 9–10 (n9+, orig pp.34–40) — Savage copied it into both the Benicia-Military (C-A 20) and Legislative-Records (C-A 61) series; C-A 61 preserves the fuller text (the mayordomo/administrator José M. Ramírez's *sevicia*, neophytes fleeing 20 leguas to the rancho de los Alisos). Argues the indigenous are the rightful proprietors of the mission property (goods, lands, tools) they built over 'once lustros' (~55 yrs) of labor, now 'despojados de un derecho que las mismas leyes llaman sagrado'; denounces (1) 'la injusta opresión que sufren los indígenas,' (2) the *salvo conducto* (impunity) for all that 'destruye la sana moral del Evangelio,' (3) the 'suma indigencia' of the old/sick/widows/orphans/wards denied even charity, (4) that if unaddressed it completes 'la ruina del esqueleto que aún resta.' Closes vowing that if the secularized missions could be returned to their old system he would be 'el 1º' to oppose it. A powerful pro-Indian (Zacatecano-friar) critique of secularization's despoliation → [[indian-raiding-article]] / mission-critique / [[californio-article]].
