# A statement on INDIAN PROPERTY RIGHTS & the oppression of secularization: argues the indigenous are the rightful proprie

**C-A 20 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n264–n265** · Sav p. 264–265 / 55 · tomo p. 1836[?] · (a friar/official) · Savage abstract · 

A statement on INDIAN PROPERTY RIGHTS & the oppression of secularization: argues the indigenous are the rightful proprietors of the mission property (goods, lands, tools) they built over 'once lustros' (~55 yrs) of labor, now 'despojados de un derecho que las mismas leyes llaman sagrado'; denounces (1) 'la injusta opresión que sufren los indígenas,' (2) the impunity of those who destroy Gospel morals, (3) the 'suma indigencia' of the old/sick/widows/orphans/wards denied even charity.
