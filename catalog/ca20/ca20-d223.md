# 'Nota analítica sobre la formación de la estadística' — the full 1834 geographic/economic survey of the Sta Bárbara dist

**C-A 20 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n146–n179** · Sav p. 147–180 · tomo p. 1119–34 · 1834-07-25 · Savage abstract · 

'Nota analítica sobre la formación de la estadística' — the full 1834 geographic/economic survey of the Sta Bárbara district / Alta CA for the Mexican national statistics, as numbered answers to a questionnaire: coordinates, extent (55 leguas E–W × 25 N–S), the 5 missions & inter-mission distances, mission by mission (San Fernando, San Gabriel, Santa Inés…) — terrain (Sierra Madre, llanuras, passes), routes/water, buildings (adobe/tile), orchards/vineyards, aguardiente (~60 barrels; San Fernando 60 bbl, the Tujunga acequia), wheat/barley harvests; climate (No.10–11: no thermometer, summers ~30°, fog/rain Oct–Mar); rural/fabril/comercial industry (No.24–29: the *gente*'s trades — shoes, blankets, coarse leather/timber goods); admin/missions arts 48–56 ('son Misⁿᵉˢ de neófitos… se nombran sus prelados con el títº de Presidᵗᵉ y Vicario foráneo sujeto á la mitra de Sonora'); lands & waters. A rich economic-geography snapshot on the eve of secularization. Ends n179 with 'pp 1119–34' and 'Fin del tomo nº 79' *(✓ end pinned vs scan n179, 2026-07-02 verify pass; an earlier duplicate pass logged Savage pages as scans, hence its 'n147–n180')*
