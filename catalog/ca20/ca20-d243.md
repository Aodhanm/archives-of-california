# Mission-flogging testimony: neophytes Marcial Mora & José Granados testify that the mayordomo Ramírez flogged (*apaleó*)

**C-A 20 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n272–n281** · Sav p. 272–281 / 63+ · tomo p. 1836[?] · (a *sumaria*) · Savage abstract · 

Mission-flogging testimony: neophytes Marcial Mora & José Granados testify that the mayordomo Ramírez flogged (*apaleó*) & shackled (*grillos/cepo*) them for leaving the mission without license (to visit family) — beatings that drew blood, the *cepo de la pozolera*, 15 palos; carpenters Hugo Mier & Gaspar Vicente likewise (alcalde Norberto Cantúa). Neophyte testimony to mission coercion driving flight → [[indian-raiding-article]] (cf. C-A 16 Doc 429, C-A 10 Docs 52/122)
