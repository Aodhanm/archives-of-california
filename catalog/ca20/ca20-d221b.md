# 'Nota analítica sobre la formación de la estadística' — a detailed 1834 geographic/economic survey of the Sta Bárbara di

**C-A 20 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n147–n180** · Sav p. 86–119 / 1117+ · tomo p. 1834-07-25 · Santa Bárbara (José Mª García, alcalde) · Savage abstract · 

'Nota analítica sobre la formación de la estadística' — a detailed 1834 geographic/economic survey of the Sta Bárbara district / Alta CA for the Mexican national statistics, as numbered answers to a questionnaire: mission by mission (San Fernando, San Gabriel, Santa Inés…) — terrain (Sierra Madre, llanuras, passes), buildings (adobe/tile), orchards/vineyards, aguardiente (~60 barrels), wheat/barley harvests, water; + climate (No.10–11: no thermometer, summers ~30°, fog/rain Oct–Mar), rural/fabril/comercial industry (No.24–29: the *gente*'s trades — shoes, blankets, coarse leather/timber goods), lands & waters. A rich economic-geography snapshot on the eve of secularization. ENDS ~n180
