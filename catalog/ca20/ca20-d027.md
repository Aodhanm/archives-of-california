# fuero-militar clash — fined Sgt [de la] Torre for gambling at cards; the sgt refused to pay, claiming the alcalde had no

**C-A 20 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n45** · Sav p. 46 · tomo p. 805–6 · 1834-05-19 · Savage abstract · 

fuero-militar clash — fined Sgt [de la] Torre for gambling at cards; the sgt refused to pay, claiming the alcalde had no jurisdiction over him or his soldiers; also accuses the sgt of killing 5 bulls of the San José mission-pueblo — defers to Figueroa since the offender is *'solo del fuero militar'*
