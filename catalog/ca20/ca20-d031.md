# petition to restore his citizenship rights — deposed/jailed/marched to San Gabriel in irons as LA alcalde after the 6 De

**C-A 20 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n48–57** · Sav p. 49–58 · tomo p. 1055–79 (compiled) · 1835-05-25 → 08 · Savage abstract · 

petition to restore his citizenship rights — deposed/jailed/marched to San Gabriel in irons as LA alcalde after the 6 Dec 1831 revolt against Gov. Manuel Victoria, persecuted by the victorious José Mª Echeandía faction (forced $100 repayment; a trivial 'robbery of 2 chests' = a family prank; a gambling charge). OUTCOME: judge Castillo Negrete finds the case rooted in *'el intruso Gefe Político'* Echeandía's illegitimate authority ('indudablemⁿ vicioso'), Sánchez jailed & fined without charges or a hearing; ex-asesor Rafael Gómez confirms he'd ruled the 1831 *sumaria* null; Figueroa restores Sánchez to full 'ciudadano Mejicano' rights (may vote & be elected) — *expandable*
