# instruction (8 points) for a 9-man expedition to rediscover the 'Paso del Río Colorado' (Colorado River crossing), guide

**C-A 21 (Provincial State Papers, Presidios), scan n111–115** · Sav p. 112–116 · tomo p. II·36–59[?] · 1796[?] · Savage abstract · 

instruction (8 points) for a 9-man expedition to rediscover the 'Paso del Río Colorado' (Colorado River crossing), guided by an Indian informant — detailed Indian-diplomacy protocol: win over the 'capitanejo,' treat Indians humanely w/ no force, exchange gifts/horses, send couriers to the Yuma/Quíquima/Cajuenche pueblos & to San Vicente, keep a duplicate diary. ⚠ Savage dates 1796 but Fages's governorship was 1782–91 — likely misdated/misfiled
