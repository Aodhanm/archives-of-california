# artillery munitions & stores of the Castillo de San Joaquín de Punta de Guijarros (= San Diego's Fort Guijarros, Ballast

**C-A 21 (Provincial State Papers, Presidios), scan n57–59** · Sav p. 58–60 · tomo p. 354 · 1818-09-05 · Savage abstract · 

artillery munitions & stores of the Castillo de San Joaquín de Punta de Guijarros (= San Diego's Fort Guijarros, Ballast Point — *distinct* from SF's Castillo de San Joaquín, Docs 63–64) — iron balls: 101×12-pdr, 619×8-pdr, 408×6-pdr, 9×4-pdr, 25×2-pdr + 15 bronze 4-pdr; grapeshot 9@; cartridge/powder/ball bags; full tool set (rammers, sponges, worms, handspikes, slow-match, powder-horns); Gómez inventoried both San-Joaquín forts the same week (cf. Doc 64)
