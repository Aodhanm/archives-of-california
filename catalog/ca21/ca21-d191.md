# statistical 'Estado' of Indians congregated in the southern missions (neophytes by sex/age + gentiles, ganado mayor, har

**C-A 21 (Provincial State Papers, Presidios), scan n97–99** · Sav p. 98–100 · tomo p. 879–882 · 1828-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

statistical 'Estado' of Indians congregated in the southern missions (neophytes by sex/age + gentiles, ganado mayor, harvested seed) — Presidio/jurisdiction of San Diego, Missions San Diego, San Luis Rey, San Juan Capistrano, San Gabriel + their asistencias/ranchos (Temecula, Sta Margarita, S. Antonio de Pala, Las Pulgas, S. Jacinto, Agua Caliente, La Puente, Jurupa, S. Bernardino…); 1828 vitals 141 marriages, 350 baptisms, 391 deaths; industries (wool blankets/*jerga*, cotton, leather-tanning/shoemaking, carpenters/smiths/masons, irrigation, orchards); ⭐ notes an 1828 measles epidemic killing some adults, 'mostly children and Indians'
