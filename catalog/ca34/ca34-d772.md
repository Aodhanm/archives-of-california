# Antº Reyes claims an 'indito' donated to him by the New Mexican Franco Pacheco (the child also wanted by Don Carlos Carr

**C-A 34 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n330** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 331 · 1839-04-22 · Savage abstract · 

Antº Reyes claims an 'indito' donated to him by the New Mexican Franco Pacheco (the child also wanted by Don Carlos Carrillo); the judicial inquiry finds the transfer contravened the anti-slavery law of 13 July 1824 → the child left in Botello's care — see E24 (doc pp.648-63)
