# remits the itemized list of the Indian Rafael's crimes: prowling houses/horses, breaking jail by fire, abducting two wom

**C-A 34 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n203–n205** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 204–206 · 1836-01-28 · Savage abstract · 

remits the itemized list of the Indian Rafael's crimes: prowling houses/horses, breaking jail by fire, abducting two women repeatedly (breaking through A. I. Ávila's wall to re-take them), raiding toward the Sta Bárbara presidio where vaqueros recovered the women but couldn't take him — see E18 (doc p.138)
