# Machado's son clubbed the soldier José Ignacio Aguilar; the quarrel arose from Machado disparaging the Mexican troops ('

**C-A 34 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n259–n260** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 260–261 · 1837-02-11 · Savage abstract · 

Machado's son clubbed the soldier José Ignacio Aguilar; the quarrel arose from Machado disparaging the Mexican troops ('el soldado Mejicano no valía nada… las tropas… unos ladrones que venían tan solo a robar') and 'los que actualmente gobiernan en California' (the Alvarado regime); the wound was examined by the foreigners Jonathan Temple & Alexandre Levi[?]; Machado released with a warning (doc pp.358-62)
