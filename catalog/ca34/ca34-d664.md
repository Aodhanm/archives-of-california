# 3 docs: (1) the danger to Los Angeles from 'la facción de Monterey'; (2) requests 200 pesos from deposit to supply the '

**C-A 34 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n272–n274** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 273–275 · 1837-01-17 · Savage abstract · 

3 docs: (1) the danger to Los Angeles from 'la facción de Monterey'; (2) requests 200 pesos from deposit to supply the 'División de San Fernando' force; (3) a full-text proclamation by Alcalde 2º José Sepúlveda (S. Fernando, 17 Jan 1837) to the 'Angelinos patriotas' denouncing Alvarado's 'Estado libre y Soberano' and Art. 3 of 'el Plan de los Montereyeses' for undermining the Catholic religion, calling to defend Mexico — see E21 (doc pp.406-11)
