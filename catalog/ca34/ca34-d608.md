# formal written PROTEST against the acts of the 'titulado Congreso/Diputación de California' (the 1836 Alvarado revolutio

**C-A 34 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n238–n241** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 239–242 · 1836-12-06 · Savage abstract · 

formal written PROTEST against the acts of the 'titulado Congreso/Diputación de California' (the 1836 Alvarado revolution): the plan 'published the 7th' by 'only four deputies without legal function' declaring California a 'Estado Soberano' with a federal constitution is illegal, unpatriotic, an armed rebellion endangering the Republic's unity; declares his oath involuntary; unpaid 20+ months, he emigrates with his family — see E20 (doc pp.273-76)
