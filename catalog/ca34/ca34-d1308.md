# 3 docs — a 36-man vecino party under José Antº Carrillo sets out (pp.461-2, May 19); the released prisoners (Brígido Mor

**C-A 34 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n533–n534** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 533–534 · 1840-05 · Savage abstract · 

3 docs — a 36-man vecino party under José Antº Carrillo sets out (pp.461-2, May 19); the released prisoners (Brígido Morillo, Hilario García, José Antº Abitia[?]) placed under Comte Carrillo's command & responsibility (pp.463-4, May 19); ⭐⭐⭐⭐ the campaign report (May 30): the expedition returned the night of the 28th; on the 25th they found the raiders at 'Agua de Vermón[?]' ~100 leagues off, who fled on sighting the camp — abandoning gear, some tamed horses, & ~1,500 beasts driven to death; the band = '20 men, citizens of the United States' driving 1,000+ head stolen in partial raids; the raiders gained little (lost over half the herd in waterless flight); Leandri carries a list of the principal robbers' names (pp.465-469, May 30)
