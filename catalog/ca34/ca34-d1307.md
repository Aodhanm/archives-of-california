# an express from Rancho San José (3 a.m.) relaying Palomares: at 8 p.m. 'le ganó la campaña' — attacked the thieves beyon

**C-A 34 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n532** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 532 · 1840-05-19 · Savage abstract · 

an express from Rancho San José (3 a.m.) relaying Palomares: at 8 p.m. 'le ganó la campaña' — attacked the thieves beyond the Matanceras & recovered the caballadas; casualties — José Mª Almenara wounded, 'el Chino Véjar' unaccounted; the rest returned this side of the river with exhausted herds; ordered Comte 'Chino' Gallardo to hold a secure post & rest (doc pp.458-460)
