# full-text account of the 7 Mar 1835 pronunciamiento: an armed party seized the ayuntamiento house at dawn, took the cann

**C-A 34 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n174–n175** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 175–176 · 1835-03-07 · Savage abstract · 

full-text account of the 7 Mar 1835 pronunciamiento: an armed party seized the ayuntamiento house at dawn, took the cannons/arms (prepared for anti-raiding use), and presented Juan Gallardo a 'plan' to hand political & military command to José Noriega (and P. de la Portilla); the ayuntamiento refused to pronounce, urged calm, closed '¡Viva la Constitución! ¡Vivan las leyes! ¡Viva la Alta California!' — see E16 (+ carátulas pp.902-903) (pp.896-97)
