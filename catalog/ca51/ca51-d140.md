# BOUNDED — ⭐ Juan Bandini resigns as administrator of San Gabriel (Mar 1839, dimisión accepted → do the inventory); on ta

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n259–260** · Sav p. X·1–2 · tomo p. orig pp.12–57 · 1839–1842 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED — ⭐ Juan Bandini resigns as administrator of San Gabriel (Mar 1839, dimisión accepted → do the inventory); on taking it he had found 'todo en muy mal estado… los indígenas en una desnudez casi total, y algunos débitos de consideración' (neophyte destitution → declension datapoint), then claims he remedied it; Juan Pérez 1841 mayordomía accounts (wine-dominant economy: 100+ barriles vino; 9 docs bounded); + Santa Bárbara habilitado's prorrateo supply to La Purísima's mayordomo Diego Fernández
