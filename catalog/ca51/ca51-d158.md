# BOUNDED — Pío Pico (1 Jul 1836) rebuts Bishop Francisco García Diego y Moreno's charges of 'mal gobierno' against Figuer

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n331–333** · Sav p. XI·54–56 · tomo p. orig pp.706–712 · 1836 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED — Pío Pico (1 Jul 1836) rebuts Bishop Francisco García Diego y Moreno's charges of 'mal gobierno' against Figueroa & the mission comisionados/administradores: argues the missions' decline is not due to secularization (an 'imperativa' measure) but to the friars; is 'altamente conmovido' at García Diego's letter to Congress (via José Antonio Carrillo) blaming the administrators' 'dilapidación'; ⭐ eulogizes the late Figueroa — 'su desinterés… su amor por los Californios y bien público' → [[californio-article]] secularization debate + Californio-identity
