# BOUNDED — a marquee Indian-agency document. Hartnell's report on his San Diego-district visitation (26 Aug 1840): (1) at

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n285–289** · Sav p. XI·8–12 · tomo p. orig pp.127–173 · 1840–1842 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED — a marquee Indian-agency document. Hartnell's report on his San Diego-district visitation (26 Aug 1840): (1) at San Luis Rey, after distributing 168 blankets, Pío Pico personally told the Indians the Gov. had given him Temecula and 'por más que ellos se opusieran siempre había de obtenerlo el Gob.,' demanding they name 4 apoderados to argue it at Monterey — 'le contestaron los indios que no querían nombrar á nadie'; (2) ⭐⭐⭐ the Indians of Las Flores complained bitterly that Pico's cattle 'les hacía mucho daño en sus milpas, pues ocupa todo el terreno de ellos y ya va cegando su aguaje,' begging removal 'pues de lo contrario no podrán subsistir,' and that houses occupied 'contra la voluntad de los indios' be vacated; (3) comisionado Carlos Castro's inventory fraud (never went to San Jacinto, 'apuntó lo que le pareció'; much less cattle than the prior count); (4) Santiago Argüello demands satisfaction for his son Ramón being 'despojado violentamente' of the SJC mayordomía; (5) at San Buenaventura the Indians presented themselves 'por medio de sus Alcaldes' asking that Santa Paula not be taken from them. + Pico's Feb-1842 SLR handover items (field-goods to the indígena José Manuel, encargado del Pueblo de las Flores). ⚠ transcribe n287–288 verbatim; corroborates Doc 141
