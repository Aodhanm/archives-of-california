# the consolidated record of Pico's mission alienations. Outright sales/auctions: La Purísima → Juan (John) Temple ($10,11

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n341–346** · Sav p. XI·64–69 · tomo p. orig pp.907–1003 · 1839–1846 · verbatim transcription · 

the consolidated record of Pico's mission alienations. Outright sales/auctions: La Purísima → Juan (John) Temple ($10,110, Dec 1845); San Fernando → Eulogio Célis ($14,000, Jun 1846, 'para sostener la integridad del Territorio'); San Luis Obispo → Scott, Wilson & James McKinley (Dec 1845); San Juan Capistrano → John Forster & James McKinley ($710, Dec 1845); La Soledad → Feliciano Soberanes ($800, Jun 1846). ⭐⭐⭐ The master 9-year LEASE auction (5 Dec 1845): San Buenaventura → Narciso Botello & José Arnaz ($1,630/yr); San Fernando → Andrés Pico & Juan Manso ($1,120/yr); Santa Inés → José M. Covarrubias & Joaquín Carrillo ($580/yr); Santa Bárbara → Nicolás A. Den & Daniel Hill ($1,260/yr). + a note that San Diego/San Luis Rey/San Gabriel were 'no… licitables' for rent (creditors); + Andrés Pico/Manso's SB & Sta Inés alcances 'en donación… á los neófitos'; closes with a minor 1839 Bandini/San Gabriel grist-mill note (orig pp.1002-03). ⭐ CONTENT ENDS n346; n347–357 = blank. → [[californio-article]] the terminal mission alienation on the eve of US conquest
