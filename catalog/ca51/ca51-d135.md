# BOUNDED BLOCK — 3 strong finds: (1) ⭐⭐ Anastasio Carrillo → Figueroa on the HÍJAR-PADRÉS COLONISTS as suspected revoluti

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n203–212** · Sav p. IX·26–35 · tomo p. — · 1834–1839 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED BLOCK — 3 strong finds: (1) ⭐⭐ Anastasio Carrillo → Figueroa on the HÍJAR-PADRÉS COLONISTS as suspected revolutionaries (Oct 1834 — 'síntomas de revolución… queriendo trastornar el orden entre los indígenas'; surveilled the colonist Araujo, forbade colonists talking to the natives so they had 'no time to sow the bad seed') → the 1834 colony conflict (ties Doc 123 funding) → [[californio-article]]; (2) ⭐ license to erect Mission Santa Bárbara into a pueblo + preceptor Bonilla $1,000 (the mission→pueblo conversion); (3) ⭐⭐⭐ José Joaquín Ortega, San Diego 1839 — mission Indians FLEEING TO FORM ROBBER BANDS: Figueroa's order (obey the administrator; 'cada uno con una mujer') was defied — they fought over women and 'por temor del castigo se van á los campos y forman partidas de ladrones' = a direct secularization → neophyte-flight → raiding link → [[indian-raiding-article]] core thesis
