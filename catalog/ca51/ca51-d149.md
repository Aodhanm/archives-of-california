# KEYSTONE — see Expanded Entry. Hartnell's 14 May 1840 report to Jimeno Casarín on his San Rafael visitation (Indian libe

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n289–294** · Sav p. XI·12–17 · tomo p. orig pp.177–185 · 1840 · Savage abstract · 

KEYSTONE — see Expanded Entry. Hartnell's 14 May 1840 report to Jimeno Casarín on his San Rafael visitation (Indian liberty + property-distribution under the new Reglamento): Gen. M.G. Vallejo had repeatedly promised the Indians 'su libertad' + their goods; the Indians 'difieron con mucha resolución y que no querían ya misión, que demasiadas veces se les había engañado,' said 'se les habían quitado todos sus terrenos,' refused the named mayordomo (Gregorio Briones), and navigated shrewdly between Vallejo & the Gov.; ⭐⭐⭐ Hartnell warns forcing them under the Reglamento 'puede ser causa de un levantamiento.' Then ⭐⭐⭐ at Yerba Buena (8 May) Vallejo intercepts and ARRESTS Hartnell — seizes his boat ('Lleve V. á Hartnell y entréguelo al cabo'), shoves it out 'con sus propias manos… con mucha cólera,' detains him aboard a launch; reconciled at San Rafael, agreeing the Indians get liberty + some goods (reserving mission debts + a pueblo/worship fund), releases him with a passport
