# BOUNDED BLOCK — the SLO secularization admin sequence (Santiago Moreno → Juan P. Ayala → Vicente Canet → Mariano Bonilla

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n251–257** · Sav p. IX·74–80 · tomo p. orig pp.742–789 · 1838–1844 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED BLOCK — the SLO secularization admin sequence (Santiago Moreno → Juan P. Ayala → Vicente Canet → Mariano Bonilla, 1838–44): ⭐⭐ admin Santiago Moreno (1838) reports open neophyte/alcalde insubordination — the alcaldes 'han tirado las varas [staffs of office] con la mayor insolencia' before him, obey no one, and he fears a 'sublevación… que no podría contener por que se encuentra sin tropa,' begging for troops (after he tried to stop neophytes taking wheat without order + oversaw the Sta Margarita harvest) → [[indian-raiding-article]] neophyte-resistance; handovers + inventories (finca 'en mal estado… nada útil'); receipt of circulars (⭐ confirms Hartnell as Visitador + José Castro & Lic. Cosme Peña named Prefects, 1839); Vicente Canet town-site proposal on mission baldíos (1 league sq.); ⭐ neophyte census = 62 (named alcaldes Leopoldo [Cristiano] & Donato [Tulareño]; Cristiano/Tulareño split); ⭐ the neófito Leopoldo's house granted to J.M. Camacho for $225 'repartido entre los neófitos más menesterosos' → Indian property under secularization
