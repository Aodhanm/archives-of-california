# BOUNDED — Hartnell's sharp 7 Jul 1841 letter to Pío Pico ('suma sorpresa… la extraordinaria carta de Ud.'): refuses Pico

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n282–284** · Sav p. XI·5–7 · tomo p. orig pp.57–69 · 1840–1841 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED — Hartnell's sharp 7 Jul 1841 letter to Pío Pico ('suma sorpresa… la extraordinaria carta de Ud.'): refuses Pico's request to leave SLR untouched and meet him in San Diego; asserts he still holds 'el carácter de visitador… cumpliré… sin parcialidad alguna'; had already named José Antonio Estudillo mayordomo; the indiada 'muy contenta' with the appointments; announces he leaves for Temecula in company with D. Andrés [Pico] (→ ties directly to the Temecula land dispute, Docs 141/148); + Estudillo's acknowledgment of the mayordomía. ⚠ re-copies the 1840–41 Pico correspondence
