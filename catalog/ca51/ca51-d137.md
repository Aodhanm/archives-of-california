# BOUNDED BLOCK — mostly routine mission accounts, w/ 3 notable items: (1) ⭐⭐ San Buenaventura admin Rafael González advis

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n223–244** · Sav p. IX·46–67 · tomo p. — · 1835–1841 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED BLOCK — mostly routine mission accounts, w/ 3 notable items: (1) ⭐⭐ San Buenaventura admin Rafael González advises AGAINST settling *gente de razón* at the mission (1841) — 'pretenderán vivir á costa de los indios, y esto introducirá la desmoralización y el desorden' (protecting neophytes from settler exploitation) → [[californio-article]]/[[indian-raiding-article]]; (2) ⭐ a friar-neglect complaint — a friar refusing to promptly confess a dying Indian near San Mateo (Santa Clara), dismissing him as not that sick (admin Gumesindo Flores sent to press him) → neophyte-welfare; (3) a curiosity — an editorial note on a star-clock diagram (nighttime hours by the Big Dipper vs Polaris, 'Padre Gil'); + Ignacio del Valle completing Santa Cruz's secularization + preceptor appointments
