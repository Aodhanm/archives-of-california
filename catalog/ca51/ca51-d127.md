# BOUNDED BLOCK — San Buenaventura ex-mission accounts (Rafael González: warehouse inventory + income/expense, existencia 

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n132–142** · Sav p. VIII·7–17 · tomo p. — · 1838–1839 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED BLOCK — San Buenaventura ex-mission accounts (Rafael González: warehouse inventory + income/expense, existencia ~$2,965) + San Miguel salary list (admin Vicente García $800; friar Fr. Juan Moreno unpaid; Rancho de San Simeón) + ⭐ another instance of HARTNELL'S ADMINISTRATOR INSTRUCTIONS (San Miguel, 2 Aug 1839 — Indians to attend church 'conforme se acostumbraba antes de la llamada secularización'; the friar minister may punish non-complying alcaldes/Indians 'por su propia autoridad') — he re-issued them per mission on his 1839 visitation (cf. Docs 109/111)
