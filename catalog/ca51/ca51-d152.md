# BOUNDED (low-marquee) — San Antonio secularization admin: mayordomo handovers (José Ramírez, Manuel Crespo → proposes Jo

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n308–315** · Sav p. XI·31–38 · tomo p. orig pp.404–565 · 1822–1835 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED (low-marquee) — San Antonio secularization admin: mayordomo handovers (José Ramírez, Manuel Crespo → proposes José Tiburcio Castro); an expediente on the conflict between the fund-administrator and the ayuntamiento regidor (Juan Parma Cruz, pp.487–528); ⭐ teacher Florencio Serrano reports his primary school 'enteramente falta de alumnos' — neither kindness nor 'azotes y encierros' work; parents put the children to hard labor and 'los esconden cuando él va á buscarlos'; and a long friar jeremiad (Vásquez del Mercado?) to Figueroa on the moral/religious decline under secular administration — gentiles keeping heathen custom, 'la rapiña, la embriaguez y la lascivia,' even 'antiguos cristianos… solo se distinguen de los gentiles en el nombre,' blaming the Indian alcalde & regidores and 'apóstoles de la impiedad'; + Fr. Esteban Tapis's 1822 independence oath (p.565)
