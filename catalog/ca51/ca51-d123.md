# BOUNDED BLOCK — San Fernando ex-mission payments 1834–37, showing the mission drained by every claimant: friar stipends 

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n111–120** · Sav p. 112–121 · tomo p. — · 1834–1837 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED BLOCK — San Fernando ex-mission payments 1834–37, showing the mission drained by every claimant: friar stipends 'por cuenta de' Fr. Narciso Durán (via mayordomos Antonio José Cot, Miguel Hidalgo, Fr. Francisco González de Ibarra; + $200 to Fr. Juan Cabot); military supply (Gov. José Castro orders seeds to the San Gabriel garrison; Pablo de la Portilla draws wheat/beans/soap; a $100 division loan); ⭐⭐ the HÍJAR-PADRÉS COLONY funded from San Fernando's accounts — $500 via a Gov. Figueroa draft 'en favor de D.n José Mª Híjar para gastos de la colonia,' + $1,000 to Francisco Torres (the colony's co-director) → colonization/secularization. Prosopography: Figueroa, Castro, Nicolás Gutiérrez, del Valle, Villavicencio, Torres, Híjar
