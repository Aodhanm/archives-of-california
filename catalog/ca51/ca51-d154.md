# BOUNDED — S. Argüello relays the Visitador's energetic complaints re general Indian desertion + cattle theft (1839): des

**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n320–323** · Sav p. XI·43–46 · tomo p. orig pp.590–635 · 1839 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED — S. Argüello relays the Visitador's energetic complaints re general Indian desertion + cattle theft (1839): deserters (incl. the caporal over the cattle + the ox-keeper) fled when the gente de razón raised a cattle-theft alarm at Santa Ana, 'dando á entender ser cómplices'; work paralyzed, the mission facing ruin 'por la falta de brazos'; ⭐⭐ the Prefecture's order that no administrator may move Indians found in Los Angeles without Juzgado/Prefectura approval effectively shelters runaways at the ranchos — a family fled at night led by Severiano Ríos → [[indian-raiding-article]] mission-flight/labor structure (cf. Doc 135)
