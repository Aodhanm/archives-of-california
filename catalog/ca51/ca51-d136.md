**C-A 51 (State Papers, Missions), scan n213–222** · Sav p. IX·36–45 · tomo p. — · 1839–1841 · Savage abstract · 

BOUNDED BLOCK — ⭐ Santa Bárbara admin Manuel Cota reports the ex-mission subsisted 'desde tiempo inmemorial… mas bien de la industria fabril de zapatería, carpintería, herrería, teja' than of rural goods (scarce pasture/water) — a mission-craft-economy note; + a mission-administration conflict (a friar/admin dispute over policía/moral/orden público); + Santa Clara 1841 accounts (admin Ignacio Alviso; minister Fr. Vázquez del Mercado): cattle remission (50 cows for want of steers), branding 716 head, matanzas/esquilmos destined to pay for the church tower (matanzas by Alviso, Soledad Ortega, Dolores Pacheco)
