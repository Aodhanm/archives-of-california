# Fiscal-system reform: Herrera brought a $22,000+ cargo/cash from Mexico to distribute among the five presidios of both C

**C-A 40 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia (Commissary & Treasury)), scan n3–n5** · Sav p. 4–6 · tomo p. 19–20 · 1825-12-06 · Savage abstract · 

Fiscal-system reform: Herrera brought a $22,000+ cargo/cash from Mexico to distribute among the five presidios of both Californias to cover arrears (*alcances*), part diverted to Loreto for Echeandía; missions to settle supply reimbursements through the *Comisaría* (not directly with presidios); equitable distribution of scarce funds
