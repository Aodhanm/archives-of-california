# Herrera disapproved account items: the Comandante's clerks' salaries can't be paid from the Treasury; only Padrés's secr

**C-A 40 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia (Commissary & Treasury)), scan n54** · Sav p. 55 · tomo p. 337–338 · 1826-03-31 · Savage abstract · 

Herrera disapproved account items: the Comandante's clerks' salaries can't be paid from the Treasury; only Padrés's secretary gratification is authorized — and if Padrés draws it double while Zamorano does the work, Zamorano gets nothing
