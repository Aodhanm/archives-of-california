# Spanish-restoration conspiracy + anti-friar: warns of plots to overthrow the Republic & restore Spain; the paper *El Sol

**C-A 40 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia (Commissary & Treasury)), scan n36–n38** · Sav p. 37–39 · tomo p. 264–265 · 1826-08-16 · Savage abstract · 

Spanish-restoration conspiracy + anti-friar: warns of plots to overthrow the Republic & restore Spain; the paper *El Sol*'s writers 'salaried by the King of Spain'; California friars disaffected, refusing to swear the Federal Constitution (per their own letters seen at Mazatlán); if a Padre wishes to die a martyr for religion & the King, give him a passport to Madrid — but leave with only a friar's satchel & staff, 'leaving the Mission's interests to the Mission itself'
