# Utterly lacks resources to maintain troops/sailors; threatens to evacuate the territory within a month, leaving it *acéf

**C-A 40 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia (Commissary & Treasury)), scan n17–n18** · Sav p. 18–19 · tomo p. — · 1826-12-25 · Savage abstract · 

Utterly lacks resources to maintain troops/sailors; threatens to evacuate the territory within a month, leaving it *acéfalo*; near-physical impossibility of provisioning because the Yaqui uprising ('anexión de los indígenas del Yaquí') drove off the coastal settlers/suppliers; must negotiate ~$1000 owed — *the fiscal collapse of the Baja frontier*
