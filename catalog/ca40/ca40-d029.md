# Customs-jurisdiction + politics: Echeandía suspended (by circular) the *derecho de internación* & *avería* on foreign go

**C-A 40 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia (Commissary & Treasury)), scan n26–n29** · Sav p. 27–30 · tomo p. 198–202 / 210–214 · 1826-09-11 · Savage abstract · 

Customs-jurisdiction + politics: Echeandía suspended (by circular) the *derecho de internación* & *avería* on foreign goods; Riesgo defends collection citing soberano decreto Nº 70 & the *instrucción de comisarios* — import 25% / internación 15% + ¼ avería (art. 41); frames foreign traders as 'smugglers... prohibited by law, only tolerated by circumstance'; attacks the mission friars as merchants 'attached to the old rights of Spain, our declared enemies' for refusing to swear the Constitution; only the President can waive the duties
