# war-contingency defense instructions for San Diego (full text): save all possible powder & ball-cartridges for guerrilla

**C-A 24 (Provincial Records), scan n172** · Sav p. 173 · tomo p. 443–444 · 1797-06-08 · Savage abstract · 

war-contingency defense instructions for San Diego (full text): save all possible powder & ball-cartridges for guerrilla actions & as a last resort; if forced to evacuate, hand over the sacred vessels, church ornaments, archives & most precious effects and destroy the provisions but never burn the presidio (lest the enemy do it); keep his few forces united; accept two 4-pdr cañoncitos w/ carriages; as fort *gefe* he issues orders per his own zeal — Capt. graduado Pedro Font fittest to command the battery
