⚠ BOUNDED FILL (gap-sampled n432/438/450/460/480/500/520/540/560/568) — the 1798–1800 index tail, NON-chronological (a future pass can itemize): ⭐ Borica's linguistic-diversity note — 'en la distancia de 12 leguas se hallan tres ó cuatro [lenguas] tan distintos que no se entienden unas rancherías con otras' (n432); ⭐ Indian uxoricide sentenced to 8 yrs grillete + public works at SD; recurring malhechor apprehensions; ⭐ new-mission-site proposal at Calchuenas (Sta Bárbara area, ~1,500 Indians, per Goycoechea/Tapis/Cota; = future Sta Inés recon?); ⭐⭐ the American fur-ship *Eliza* (denied license beyond SF, feared contraband, left for Boston with pelts + 12 cannons, 135 tons, 1800 → [[bodega-primary-sources]]); the SJC 'india que parió una perra' case (SD-commander inquiry, cf.
