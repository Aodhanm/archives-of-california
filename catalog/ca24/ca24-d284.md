**C-A 24 (Provincial Records), scan n203** · Sav p. 204 · tomo p. 529–530 · 1799-03-08→11 · Savage abstract · 

estados de servicio & padrones received; ⭐⭐⭐ the 'monstrous birth' investigation — per Grajera's private letter (18 Feb) from S. Juan Capistrano, an india of the mission reportedly 'parió una perra prieta' (gave birth to a black she-dog) on the 3rd, which the Indians killed & buried in the camposanto until the friars had it exhumed & marveled at it; Borica, treating it 'jurídicamente,' orders Alférez Manuel Rodríguez to SJC to take formal declarations from the friars & reliable persons (continues n204) [rough reading — transcribe verbatim before citing]
