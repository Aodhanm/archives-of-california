# congratulates Arrillaga on his 3rd frontier sortie (the reconnaissance diary; Sta Catalina/Catarina proposed for a settl

**C-A 24 (Provincial Records), scan n267** · Sav p. 268 · tomo p. 673–675 · 1796-11-27 · Savage abstract · 

congratulates Arrillaga on his 3rd frontier sortie (the reconnaissance diary; Sta Catalina/Catarina proposed for a settlement); ⭐ Borica 'ya no quiere hablar de castigo, y el mal método de esas reducciones' (weary of the punishment method in the reductions); ⭐⭐⭐ an American frigate 'llena de necesidades' from Bahía Botánica [Botany Bay] with 1,000 pelts, bound for China then its own country (maritime fur trade); ⭐⭐ awaiting final orders to found the Villa de Branciforte (the friars readying supplies); quadruplicate the Teniente propuesta
