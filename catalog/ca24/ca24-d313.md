# military engagement near the Río Colorado — the commander, meeting insults from 'una colonia numerosa' of Indians on all

**C-A 24 (Provincial Records), scan n270** · Sav p. 271 · tomo p. 697 · 1796-11-17 · Savage abstract · 

military engagement near the Río Colorado — the commander, meeting insults from 'una colonia numerosa' of Indians on all sides in bad terrain, 'se vió precisado á atacar la fuerza con la fuerza'; losses: a mule + 7[–8] horses (Spanish), 7 gandules (Indian); reports to the Viceroy the difficulty of the Sonora communication & that no establishment is safe without substantial troops (1 sgt/1 cabo/12 soldados)
