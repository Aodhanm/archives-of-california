# *negativa de auxilio de Soldados á los Misioneros* — DENIES the SF friars 2–3 escort soldiers for a spiritual-conquest p

**C-A 24 (Provincial Records), scan n31–n32** · Sav p. 32–33 · tomo p. 32–33 · 1794-12-03 · Savage abstract · 

*negativa de auxilio de Soldados á los Misioneros* — DENIES the SF friars 2–3 escort soldiers for a spiritual-conquest push among the gentiles; soldiers not to overnight outside their escorts (even for confesiones/bautismos); the friars to stop sending Christian Indians 'por mar á Catequizar' (conquistas/empresas)
