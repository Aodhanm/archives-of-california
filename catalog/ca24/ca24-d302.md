**C-A 24 (Provincial Records), scan n221–259** · Sav p. 222–260 · tomo p. ~565–657 · 1795 & 1798 · reference · 

⚠ BOUNDED FILL of the n221–259 Baja/index block (gap-sampled n221/226/236/246/256/261 — dead 'municipal-arbitrios re-copy' stretch, NON-chronological: 1795 Monterey letters interleaved w/ 1798 San Diego index entries; a future pass can itemize). Content: 1795 Borica↔Baja-frontier correspondence — ship & mail logistics, friar-movement control ('no… ningún religioso sin orden superior,' per Conde de Gálvez), Baja mission-founding (San Pedro Mártir bimonthly progress reports; new-mission site reconnaissance via Arrillaga; Santa Catarina), troop rosters. ⭐ the English establishing at Nootka + Broughton's frigate (n235–236 → [[bodega-primary-sources]]; Savage marks the rest 'sin importancia'). ⭐ Borica's presidio primary-schools program (Manuel Boronda; teachers 2 rl/child at Monterey/San José/Sta Bárbara/SD, from soldier-subscription/gratification funds, n256). 1798 San Diego index-style entries: ⭐ an English frigate sighted off Isla del Espíritu Santo / S. José (→ bodega); war-donation libranzas 'para gastos de la presente guerra'; ⭐ recurring 'Yndio Malhechor' apprehension cases (→ [[indian-raiding-article]]); coast-vigilance orders
