# *Proclama* assuming the governorship & the Diputación's disavowal of Micheltorena — denounces his force ('adventurers, d

**C-A 36 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles (Decrees & Despatches)), scan n115–n117** · Sav p. 116–118 · tomo p. 242–244 · 1845-02-15 · Savage abstract · 

*Proclama* assuming the governorship & the Diputación's disavowal of Micheltorena — denounces his force ('adventurers, demoralized bachelors, undocile Indians') and Sutter as instigator of the civil war 'para manchar el honor de los Californios' (Sutter's ~140 foreign men per his 18 Oct 1844 letter)
