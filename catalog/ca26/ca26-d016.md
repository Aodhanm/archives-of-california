# Arrillaga á Condestable Sánchez — the hemp program wound down

**C-A 26 (Provincial Records (Tomo XI)), scan n11** · Sav p. 12 · tomo leaf 11 · orig pp. 48–49 · 1811-02-22 · Savage abstract ·

'Feb.º 22 (1 Doc), p. 48: q.e prevenga á los cosecheros q.e no se empeñen en el cultivo del cáñamo y beneficio de dicho ramo, fiando en q.e se les ha de recibir — growers must not stake themselves on hemp expecting government purchase; anyone may grow for his own use or other ends; sufficient quantity exists to judge the utility of the first experiments; outlet to be sought for what is already harvested. + A Com.te de S.ta Bárb.a, Mzo. 30 (2 Docs, p. 48); A Condestable Sánchez, Mzo. 30 (1 Doc, pp. 48–9): conservación de la semilla de cáñamo. [= Bancroft Hist. Cal. II, the hemp-culture note: Feb. 22, 1811, Arrillaga to Sanchez, Prov. Rec., MS., xi. 11 — his 11 = this leaf number]
