**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. I), scan n43–n44** · tomo p. 62–63 · Sav p. 43–44 · ~1776-03/05 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ State & progress of the frontier missions (per Alférez José Velázquez's reports of 10/18/20 Mar): Santo Domingo only 2 gentiles baptized — P. García won't baptize 33 women who came wanting to be Christians "hasta que estén civilizadas é instruidas" (6 fan. wheat, 233 cattle); Ntra Sra del Rosario (Viñadaco) 557 baptized; San Fernando [Velicatá] 1,466 Christian souls in 11 rancherías. *(Savage: doc "trunco y tachado")*
