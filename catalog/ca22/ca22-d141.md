# ⭐ *Sobre ganados* — livestock census: at end of Dec 1778, 156 mares + 507 head cattle + 124 at SF = 631 head of royal *g

**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. I), scan n125–n126** · tomo p. 264–267 · Sav p. 125–126 · 1779-04-03 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐ *Sobre ganados* — livestock census: at end of Dec 1778, 156 mares + 507 head cattle + 124 at SF = 631 head of royal *ganado mayor* (excl. this year's calves); the presidio's *yeguada* suffices, with the 13 San José settlers supplied; bring 140 brood mares (80 tamed) + donkeys to stock the new pueblo & each presidio; ⭐ requests 40 settler-farmers (some who can make plows) + a mason, each bringing 2 mules & 1 horse
