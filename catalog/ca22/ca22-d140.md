**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. I), scan n123–n124** · tomo p. 259–264 · Sav p. 123–124 · 1779-04-03 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Approval of the founding of the Santa Barbara Channel Presidio & 3 Missions and the two pueblos (on the Guadalupe & Porciúncula rivers) — confirms the 1777 Channel plan (Entry 10); 2 religiosos per Channel mission; only 1 cajón of ornaments exists (for San Buenaventura) — detailed church-inventory (cáliz, copón, vinajeras, Santos Óleos vessels, plata para bautizar, 5 casullas of the 5 colors, 2 campanas…); the 3 Channel missions to be paid $3,000 in Mexico to the Síndico of the Colegio de San Fernando; San Luis & San Gabriel can supply 1,000 fanegas maize
