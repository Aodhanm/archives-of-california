# ⭐ Cost & delivery of livestock from Álamos: 200 mules + 100 horses bought, only 163 mules & 75 horses arrived (rest lost

**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. I), scan n87** · tomo p. 137–139 · Sav p. 87 · 1778-08-14 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Cost & delivery of livestock from Álamos: 200 mules + 100 horses bought, only 163 mules & 75 horses arrived (rest lost/died); cost rose $22→$34/mule, $8→$20/horse w/ expenses; 79 mules + 10 horses to these presidios (+45 lately to SD, mounting 25 recruits); troops charged 150% recargo = $70/mule, $51/horse "insoportable" — recommends charging only $25/mule, $12/horse
