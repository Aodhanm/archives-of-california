# ⭐ Judicial aftermath of the SD revolt: Lt. José de Ortega's inquiry (4 Mar) proves a second *convocación* of 21 rancherí

**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. I), scan n61–n62** · tomo p. 91–94 · Sav p. 61–62 · 1777-06-03 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Judicial aftermath of the SD revolt: Lt. José de Ortega's inquiry (4 Mar) proves a second *convocación* of 21 rancherías by Manuel, Christian captain of the Soledad ranchería (1 legua from Mission SD), + 3 accomplices — arrested & confessed; prisoners told they deserved death but pardoned in the King's name (prison deemed sufficient), warned reoffense = death; troops & prisoners acclaimed the King, two cannon fired ("tienen mucho miedo los Indios"); released to the padre to be admonished, fed & sent home — see Entry 9
