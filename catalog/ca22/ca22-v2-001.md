# ⭐⭐ "Documento trunco" = the fiscal's opinion in the Horra expediente: weighing the mistreatment charges

**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. II), scan n219–n221** · tomo p. II:1–4, 30–33 · Sav p. 218–220 · c.1801–1805 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

"Documento trunco sobre trato á Indios de Mis.s" (Savage's heading, which he prefaced "1783" — his guess; see the note below). The writer reports long holding "el expediente de la denuncia hecha por Fr. Ant.º de la Concepción sobre varios desórdenes que notó en las Misiones de la Alta California y mal trato que se da á los Neófitos por los R.s P.s que las administran"; given the contradictory declarations, "el Fiscal quiere que se rectifiquen los informes" from others with knowledge; he pleads "la enorme distancia en que reside de aquellos parages," "la adulteración de las noticias," and the lack of anyone to commission "en asunto tan delicado." Even if a fact or two proved true, "de esto no se deduce una consecuencia gral." Second part (n220–n221): the structural analysis — "el manejo de intereses ofrece entre los Com.s y Misioneros bastante oposición"; "el comercio de la tropa con las Indias que dan lugar á diarias disensiones"; the P.-vs-Com. friction over castigos; and the verdict "abonándose el carácter y circunstancias del Indio, se verá no ser tan cruel ni tirano el trato que se da á los Neófitos y aun á los cristianos viejos." Ends "pp. 1-4 y 30-33."

> [!note] Re-dated + re-identified 2026-07-14 (Session G, register F39). Savage's "1783" heading is a copyist's guess on an undated trunco; the content is the **Horra expediente** (Fr. Antonio de la Concepción Horra's 1798 denuncia and the investigation it triggered). Bancroft cites this exact fragment — "A fragment of the fiscal's opinion is also extant. Prov. Rec., MS., ii. 1-3. He advises that there be no sweeping decision… There is a natural conflict of interests between…" (HoC I, p. 597 n.) — in his account of the fiscal's review preceding the viceroy's April 19, 1805 exoneration of the padres. Year 1804 is a placement within c.1801–1805, inferred, not documentary. Original pages leaf-verified: the fragment occupies tomo II pp. 1–4 y 30–33 (bound apart); the previous record wrongly gave scan n219–n220 and no orig pages, which mis-attached Bancroft's ii.1-3 cite to d2002.
