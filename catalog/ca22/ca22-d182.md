# *Misiones &c.*: reconnaissance (May–June) of the 5 Baja missions + the Real de Sta Ana — too poor to maintain their nati

**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. I), scan n148–n150** · tomo p. 347–349 · Sav p. 148–150 · 1775-12-30 · Savage abstract · 

*Misiones &c.*: reconnaissance (May–June) of the 5 Baja missions + the Real de Sta Ana — too poor to maintain their natives (except Todos Santos); failure to settle the Indians in pueblos per Gálvez's 1769 decree; no operarios; recommends a Padre Presidente "separado del espíritu de facción, comercio y tráfico"; the Indians cannot be made "vasallos útiles" — *closely parallels the 1779 version, Doc 148*
