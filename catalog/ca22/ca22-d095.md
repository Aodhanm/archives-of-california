# ⭐⭐ "Sobre la situación de las Rancherías de Gentiles" — the Santa Barbara Channel establishment plan: Canal runs ~24–26 

**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. I), scan n71–n76** · tomo p. 106–110, 120 · Sav p. 71–76 · 1777-06-03 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ "Sobre la situación de las Rancherías de Gentiles" — the Santa Barbara Channel establishment plan: Canal runs ~24–26 leguas, 8,000–10,000 gentiles; proposes central Fuerte + 3rd Mission "Santa Bárbara" plus Missions San Buenaventura & Purísima Concepción; total 1 Tte, 3 sgts, 2 cabos, 62 soldados; SF fort garrison set at 1 Tte, 1 Alférez, 2 sgts, 4 cabos, 34 soldados; detachments from Monterey/Santiago/SF companies; best fort site near Mescaltitán; harbor recon (ref. Tte. de Fragata Diego Choquet[?], anchored near the 3 Channel towns in 1776) — see Entry 10
