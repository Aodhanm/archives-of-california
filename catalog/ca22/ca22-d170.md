# ⭐ *Fundación de Misiones*: on arrival, Mission San Diego already rebuilt & San Juan Capistrano's founding concluded (bot

**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. I), scan n141–n143** · tomo p. 322–323 · Sav p. 141–143 · 1777-02-25 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ *Fundación de Misiones*: on arrival, Mission San Diego already rebuilt & San Juan Capistrano's founding concluded (both *terrado*-roofed after the 1775 burning); the Presidio of San Francisco & Mission Dolores occupied 27 Jul [1776] (mission ½ legua from the fort by the Laguna de los Dolores); Mission Santa Clara sited 4 Jan, 15 leguas SE on the Guadalupe river; SF presidio still "barracas é chozas" w/ no defense but the adobe commander's house & warehouse (nearly collapsed) — to be walled per the Reglamento
