# ⭐⭐ *Expedición al Norte* of the frigates *Favorita* & *Princesa* (Lt. Ignacio Arteaga cmdg.): reached 61°4′, could not m

**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. I), scan n133–n135** · tomo p. 291–293 · Sav p. 133–135 · 1779-10-07 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ *Expedición al Norte* of the frigates *Favorita* & *Princesa* (Lt. Ignacio Arteaga cmdg.): reached 61°4′, could not make 70° as wished, retreated to 55° (long. W of San Blas); the Russians place the passage at 43–44°; explored Puerto de Bucareli (discovered at 55° on the prior expedition by Juan de la Bodega y Quadra), an archipelago "lleno de buenos puertos"; brought back 3 boys & 2 girls + native artifacts; >7 months' navigation, crews scurvy-stricken — see Entry 15
