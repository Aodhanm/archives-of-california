# ⭐⭐ *Inventarios de Misiones* — the missionaries refuse to give the governor mission inventories: Serra (1775) excused hi

**C-A 22 (Provincial Records Vol. I), scan n128–n129** · tomo p. 270–271 · Sav p. 128–129 · 1779-06-04 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ *Inventarios de Misiones* — the missionaries refuse to give the governor mission inventories: Serra (1775) excused himself saying he sent them direct, and later told Neve in person he had a *precepto de obediencia* from his Guardián "de no dar informe ni noticia alguna de las Misiones al Gobernador"; Neve reads in this the friars' "espíritu… de mantener las Misiones y sus naturales con total inhibición del conocimiento" of government — see Entry 14
