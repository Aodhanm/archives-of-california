# ⭐⭐⭐ On the Reglamento governing this Peninsula (genesis: RO 21 May 1775 to revise the defective Reglamento) + full compa

⭐⭐⭐ On the Reglamento governing this Peninsula (genesis: RO 21 May 1775 to revise the defective Reglamento) + full comparative establishment/budget for all 4 presidios ("Conforme al Reglamento" vs "Actual pié"), mission escoltas, the Loreto Departamento de Marina (balandra *Pilar*, launches *Sta Lucía* & *San Juan Nepomuceno*; ~$16,393), mission *sínodos*, the Resumen General (4 presidios $41,793 vs $55,385; GRAND TOTAL $62,520 vs $76,105 as-is), the 150%/100% recargo critique, a per-soldier cost ($439.7.1¾) + ration schedules, and a discursive Informe (frontier service; "solo 3 que casaron con Indias naturales" count as settlers); ends "propone varias otras modificaciones al reglamento… pp.194–225" — the budget core of the Reglamento de 1779 — see Entry 13
