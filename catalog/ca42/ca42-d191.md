# Full transcription of the Lambruston affair (spans n51–n56): the 13 Jan 1846 ball-room clash where Prefect Castro jailed

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n51–n56** · Sav p. 52–57 · tomo p. ii.10–36 · 1846-01-29/30 · verbatim transcription · 

Full transcription of the Lambruston affair (spans n51–n56): the 13 Jan 1846 ball-room clash where Prefect Castro jailed the foreigner Henrique Lambruston; port captain Pedro Narváez & French agent Gasquet contest Castro's authority (he lacks the legal age of 30) & demand 100 pesos/day indemnity; Castro fines Lambruston 100 pesos to municipal funds; declarations of Narváez, Antonio Chávez, Bernardino & Jesús Soto; Lambruston freed by alcalde 2º Ignº Escamilla — Expanded Entry 11
