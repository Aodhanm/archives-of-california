# Battle report: ~100+ hostile Indians attacked Santa Isabel; Ibarra routed them 4 leguas to the San Felipe valley (the Sa

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n312–n314** · Sav p. 81–83 · tomo p. iii.683–684 · 1826-04-05 · verbatim transcription · 

Battle report: ~100+ hostile Indians attacked Santa Isabel; Ibarra routed them 4 leguas to the San Felipe valley (the San Felipe & Agua Caliente indiada auxiliaries joining), 28 enemy killed (incl. the 'capitancillo' of Jaquilla), 1 soldier + 14 Indians + 1 gentile wounded; 'Remito á V. 20 pares de orejas que mandé quitar' (ears cut from the dead) and the captured Indian prisoner 'lo mandé matar' — Expanded Entry 26
