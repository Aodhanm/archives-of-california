# the Tribunal's dictamen on the Osio–Berry–García lands (Bolinas/Point Reyes, Marin): García keeps his 2 sitios in the ca

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n400–n401** · Sav p. 50–51 · tomo p. iv.485–487 · 1844-02-29 · Savage abstract · 

the Tribunal's dictamen on the Osio–Berry–García lands (Bolinas/Point Reyes, Marin): García keeps his 2 sitios in the cañada de Baulines measured to the punta; Berry's 8 sitios measured exactly by the agrimensor; Osio gets only the *sobrante*; Berry left 'en pacífica posesión'; ⚠ the *diseño* disputed — the tract runs onto 'una parte de la Misión de San Rafael' (ex-mission land)
