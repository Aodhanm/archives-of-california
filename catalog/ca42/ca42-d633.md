# Received the Defenders-corps reglamento & the hides ('arreglo de cueros') regulation; ⭐ measures to repel the 180 armed 

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n226–n228** · Sav p. 227–228 · tomo p. ii.1075–1082 · 1845-11-20 · Savage abstract · 

Received the Defenders-corps reglamento & the hides ('arreglo de cueros') regulation; ⭐ measures to repel the 180 armed Yutas (Utes) appearing near San Bernardino — so delicate 'el Gobernador debe salir en persona'; José Carrillo/José Lugo/Jacobo Sánchez arrived with Comandancia-Gral officer passports (not remitted by the Monterey Prefect as ordered) — couldn't be arrested for lack of force (vecinos busy with harvest), sent to the capital [4 docs]
