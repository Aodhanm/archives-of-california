# FULL TRANSCRIPTION — Stearns protests his EXPULSION order: ordered to leave the Territory for México as 'peligroso… inme

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n499–n500** · Sav p. vi.2 · tomo p. vi.17–21 · 1836-07-08 · Savage abstract · 

FULL TRANSCRIPTION — Stearns protests his EXPULSION order: ordered to leave the Territory for México as 'peligroso… inmediato en la Revolución del Sur (Victoria)'; pleads his 'multitud de asuntos con los… buques que tocan en esta costa' can't be settled in a month, that he can't even notify his creditors; formally protests & holds the government responsible for his losses — 'se desecha su solicitud'
