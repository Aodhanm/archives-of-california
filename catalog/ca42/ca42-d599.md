# Full-text dictamen: brands Castro's Comandancia Militar as pretending to impose martial law / 'un dictador absoluto' str

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n213–n216** · Sav p. 214–216 · tomo p. ii.983–986 · 1846-06-22 · verbatim transcription · 

Full-text dictamen: brands Castro's Comandancia Militar as pretending to impose martial law / 'un dictador absoluto' stripping citizens of their legal guarantees; 4 resolutions — impede the effects of Castro's communications; put the District in a state of defense to bar 'esos malhechores' (Castro & company) by 'el sagrado derecho de propia defensa'; the Sub-Prefect to take defense measures while professing respect to the Governor & Assembly; copies to the Governor & the pueblos 'que este Distrito se halle dispuesto á defender sus derechos con la energía necesaria' — Expanded Entry 23
