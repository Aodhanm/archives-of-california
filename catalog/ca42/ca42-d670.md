# 'Arresto de Carrillo': his arrest lifted (per Comte. José Castro); admonished not to publish sensitive govt orders and r

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n236–n239** · Sav p. 6–9 · tomo p. iii.75–78 · 1840-08-28 · verbatim transcription · 

'Arresto de Carrillo': his arrest lifted (per Comte. José Castro); admonished not to publish sensitive govt orders and reproached that he did not proceed with enough vigor 'contra los extranjeros ilegalmente introducidos al país'; blames Leandro González's intrigue (via Fr. Antonio Jimeno & Dr. Nicholas Den, cf. Doc 374) and asks satisfaction (Graham Affair)
