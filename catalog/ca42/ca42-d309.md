# Deserters from the US whaler *Hopewell* (Capt. George Littlefield): 7 named sailors (José Johnson, Luis Hector, Guillerm

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n115–n116** · Sav p. 116–117 · tomo p. ii.314–315 · 1845-04-16 · verbatim transcription · 

Deserters from the US whaler *Hopewell* (Capt. George Littlefield): 7 named sailors (José Johnson, Luis Hector, Guillermo Marshal, Juan Castle, Eduardo Malloy, Ricardo Reed, Felipe Cronstead + 1) fled all armed with rifles/muskets/pistols/daggers, carrying a map, headed for San Bernardino — vecinos (Juan Machado) didn't dare pursue. Also: 11 Americans landed from a Hamburg frigate unknown to the sub-prefecture for 11 days; port captain let 2 go inland despite laws barring foreigner entry
