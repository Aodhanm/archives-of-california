# Encloses a copy of the parish priest's instancia & his declaration: predecessor Joaquín Estrada failed to comply re the 

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n112–n113** · Sav p. 113–114 · tomo p. ii.300–302 · 1846-01-04 · verbatim transcription · 

Encloses a copy of the parish priest's instancia & his declaration: predecessor Joaquín Estrada failed to comply re the surplus ('sobrante') of houses, because the apoderado of the buyers (compradores), José de Jesús Pico, was absent; detailed building survey — casa cural/consistorial/escuela, one lienzo ~102 varas × 8, stone-and-mortar walls in good state but the roof (madres podridas) failing, patio & corredor 18 varas [Pico-era mission-property sale]
