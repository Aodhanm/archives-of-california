# Graham-Affair inquiry: to clarify Sub-Prefect Raimundo Carrillo's statement to the American Consul re the order to appre

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n144–n146** · Sav p. 145–147 · tomo p. ii.478–488 · 1840-08-05 · verbatim transcription · 

Graham-Affair inquiry: to clarify Sub-Prefect Raimundo Carrillo's statement to the American Consul re the order to apprehend the foreigners; depositions (Leandro González, via Dr. Nicholas Den & supercargo Tomás Ochoa, & Fr. Antonio Jimeno): the American prisoners José Castro deported to Acapulco were disembarked and set free, and Castro himself was arrested there as 'el revolucionario de California'; the govt order = ship out every foreigner lacking citizenship papers & $300 capital; the Consul presented a copy to the US warships at Mazatlán
