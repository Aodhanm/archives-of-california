# Asks what penalties to impose for the frequent offenses — verbatim list: scandals, drunkenness ('por el aguardiente'), s

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n19** · Sav p. 20 · tomo p. 272–273 · 1833-03-29 · Savage abstract · 

Asks what penalties to impose for the frequent offenses — verbatim list: scandals, drunkenness ('por el aguardiente'), stealing others' horses, breaking into others' houses at night, 'ultrajar á las casadas y doncellas' (assaulting married women & maidens), concubinage, etc.
