# The 'Reglamento sobre carreras de Caballos' (horse-racing regulation): bets must be by a written legal obligation with n

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n339–n342** · Sav p. 108–111 · tomo p. iii.908–910 · 1841-07-27 · verbatim transcription · 

The 'Reglamento sobre carreras de Caballos' (horse-racing regulation): bets must be by a written legal obligation with no appeal; a 6% municipal tax on winnings; livestock bets appraised by peritos before the Juez; all wagers registered — signed Argüello, Palomares, V. Sánchez, Basilio Valdez, Juan Gallardo, Sepúlveda
