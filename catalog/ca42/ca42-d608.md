# The night of the 29th [Nov 1845]: summoned via Francisco Díaz & Antonio Torres, he found Felipe Figueroa at the head of 

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n218** · Sav p. 219 · tomo p. ii.1000 · 1845-12-02 · Savage abstract · 

The night of the 29th [Nov 1845]: summoned via Francisco Díaz & Antonio Torres, he found Felipe Figueroa at the head of 20 armed men, who ordered him arrested with the 1st Alcalde & Antonio Rodríguez, disavowing the civil authorities & subjecting them to the Comandancia General (a Sta Bárbara armed coup, echoing the LA 'night of the 29th' conspiracy)
