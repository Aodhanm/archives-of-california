# 'Sobre movimientos de Frémont': the Gavilán (Hawk's) Peak standoff — Frémont, breaking January's good-faith promise, int

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n139–n140** · Sav p. 139–141 · tomo p. ii.441–443 · 1846-03-14 · verbatim transcription · 

'Sobre movimientos de Frémont': the Gavilán (Hawk's) Peak standoff — Frémont, breaking January's good-faith promise, introduced armed force near Monterey, fortified an eminence of the Sierra del Gavilán at 37° latitude & hoisted the US flag with 'insultos y desafíos'; Castro mustered the militia at San Juan [de Castro] as Cuartel Gral; Frémont fled north by night; cites his correspondence with US Consul Thomas O. Larkin — Expanded Entry 22
