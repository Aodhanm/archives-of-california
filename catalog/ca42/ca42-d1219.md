# 'Muerte de indios ladrones' — Salazar reports 21 armed Indians presented at the Rancho de San Francisco (Del Valle, N of

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n412** · Sav p. 62 · tomo p. iv.546–550 · 1846-03-26 · Savage abstract · 

'Muerte de indios ladrones' — Salazar reports 21 armed Indians presented at the Rancho de San Francisco (Del Valle, N of LA); disarmed 'mañosamente,' they revolted with stones & knives and he 'se vió precisado á hacerles fuego' → 18 dead, 2 wounded, 1 escaped unharmed + no provisioning to those bringing *parejas de caballo*
