# Responds to the Governor's note on 'los acontecimientos ocurridos en Sonoma' (Bear Flag): the seizure was by 'aventurero

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n128–n129** · Sav p. 129–130 · tomo p. ii.402–404 · 1846-07-02 · verbatim transcription · 

Responds to the Governor's note on 'los acontecimientos ocurridos en Sonoma' (Bear Flag): the seizure was by 'aventureros Norteamericanos'; since war may not be declared, they are an 'atentado' outside their nation's protection — and having hoisted 'otro [pabellón] distinto' (a different, unknown flag) they are to be deemed pirates; urges the Govt to 'sofocar' them 'con energía' — a Californio legal-diplomatic reading of the Revolt (see Expanded Entry 21)
