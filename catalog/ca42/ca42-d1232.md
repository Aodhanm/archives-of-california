# the *Bando* continued — arts. 15–20 + additional: no sale of <20 unbranded hides, no *feriado* work w/o permit, dance-pe

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n426** · Sav p. v.10 · tomo p. v.130–134 · 1839-05-31 · Savage abstract · 

the *Bando* continued — arts. 15–20 + additional: no sale of <20 unbranded hides, no *feriado* work w/o permit, dance-permits (licence from the Juez, $2 fine on host & musicians; a 3-family / 10 p.m. exception), clean *solares* by 8 a.m., fines to public funds; no night shooting in town; riding-in-town exceptions; no *bronco* cattle driven through town — a complete municipal ordinance
