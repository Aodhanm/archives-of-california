# 'Desorganización del gobierno en el tribunal de S. José' — 9-point grievance memorial: (1) Contra Costa jueces must send

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n56–n59** · Sav p. 57–60 · tomo p. ii.39–44 · 1843-04-18 · verbatim transcription · 

'Desorganización del gobierno en el tribunal de S. José' — 9-point grievance memorial: (1) Contra Costa jueces must send all cases to S. José; (2) landless vagrants live by theft & join the Tulare horse-thieves as guides; (3) foreigners trafficking without cartas de naturaleza, entering via the Sacramento River — the 1826 foreigner-internment decree 'no effect'; (4) mission Indians, land & labor, desertion to the Tulares; (5) military-*fuero* abuses; (6) cross-jurisdiction lawsuits dragging 8 years; (7) unjust juzgado boundaries; (9) mail-service failures — Expanded Entry 12
