# Session of 21 Sep 1835: read Figueroa's circular leaving the government to the 3rd Vocal José Castro 'á causa de sus mal

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n265** · Sav p. 34 · tomo p. iii.281–282 · 1835-09-21 · Savage abstract · 

Session of 21 Sep 1835: read Figueroa's circular leaving the government to the 3rd Vocal José Castro 'á causa de sus males'; Osuna argues the succession should legally fall to the 2nd Vocal Antonio Estudillo (since 1st Vocal J.A. Carrillo was elected deputy to Mexico), not the 3rd (Castro) — the Figueroa-death succession dispute
