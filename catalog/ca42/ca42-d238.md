# 'Matrimonios con protestantes': denounces Sutter for authorizing marriages of Protestants with Mexican Catholic women (n

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n84–n86** · Sav p. 85–87 · tomo p. ii.167–169 · 1845-11-22 · Savage abstract · 

'Matrimonios con protestantes': denounces Sutter for authorizing marriages of Protestants with Mexican Catholic women (no *tolerancia de cultos* in Mexico); those married claim faculties from the Governor & the Bishop of the Californias; marriage is a Sacrament not mere contract; Sutter 'se burla y ve con menosprecio las leyes soberanas de la Nación… profanando nuestra Religión Católica' — Expanded Entry 17
