# Intelligence via the neophyte Antonio de San José Guadalupe: at a rancho north of the Estero de Sacramento, a foreign ra

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n135** · Sav p. 136 · tomo p. ii.435–437 · 1846-05-06 · verbatim transcription · 

Intelligence via the neophyte Antonio de San José Guadalupe: at a rancho north of the Estero de Sacramento, a foreign ranchero hosted a gathering by Capt. J. C. Frémont (US Army) of 200 armed foreigners incl. ex-Micheltorena-battalion soldiers, purposed to go to Monterey and rob the rancheros; Frémont then left with 80 men toward the Columbia to recruit Indians to swell his ranks and carry out 'la toma de Monterey' — Expanded Entry 22
