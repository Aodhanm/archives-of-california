# Cota certifies the Rancho de los Nietos bounds — seen in 1812 when Arrillaga distributed the late M. Nieto's cattle: bet

**C-A 42 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n525** · Sav p. vi.28 · tomo p. vi.214–215 · 1828-07 · Savage abstract · 

Cota certifies the Rancho de los Nietos bounds — seen in 1812 when Arrillaga distributed the late M. Nieto's cattle: between the San Diego *camino real*, the San Gabriel & Santa Ana rivers 'hasta contra la mar'; the river has since shifted its old channel (Alvarado & Talamantes concur)
