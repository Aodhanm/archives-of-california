# "Captura de fragata *Mercurio*": Arrillaga's order to investigate Noé's prize — reconnoiter the captured Anglo-American 

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n358–n359** · tomo 1179–80 · 1813-06-08 Monterey · Savage abstract · 

"Captura de fragata *Mercurio*": Arrillaga's order to investigate Noé's prize — reconnoiter the captured Anglo-American frigate *Mercurio* ("del mando del que llaman Jorge, que tantos años anda contrabandeando por estas costas"), make an exact inventory, take declarations from Capt. Jorge & the crew, establish their purpose on the coast & whether other company ships are about, and report by duplicate.
