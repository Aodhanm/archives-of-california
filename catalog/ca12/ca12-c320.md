# ⭐⭐ Arrillaga's oath of office as Governor: at Mission San Carlos (Carmel), 5 pm, kneeling before a crucifix with one han

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n265–n266** · tomo 702–704 · 1809-08-10 Mission San Carlos · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Arrillaga's oath of office as Governor: at Mission San Carlos (Carmel), 5 pm, kneeling before a crucifix with one hand on the Gospels & the other on his sword-cross, Arrillaga swore fidelity to Fernando VII & to defend the Crown's rights — before padres Tapis, Amorós, Sarría, surgeon Quijano & cadete J.R. Estrada. (Dup.)
