# Letter (English translation, Iván Pisteff transl.): received the French-language letter via Moraga & Gervasio Argüello b

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n376–n377** · tomo 1218–20 · 1814-06-08(20) [Fort Ross] · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐

Letter (English translation, Iván Pisteff transl.): received the French-language letter via Moraga & Gervasio Argüello but had no interpreter; sends the RAC confidential agent Slobodchikov to San Francisco with a boat & 3 bidarkas to settle the balance due, offering goods per enclosed invoice or 2 oxen; again asks return of the Kodiak men — if Arrillaga has truly ordered it, forward them; else "it is my duty to inform my superior officers and the Home Government"; forwards the tent & enclosed letter to the Monterey commander.
