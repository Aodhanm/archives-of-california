# ⭐⭐ Indictment of the Baja Cal. governor (Goycoechea): tyranny & corruption — contraband with foreign ships, selling fore

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n323–n326** · tomo 1037–1040 · 1812-05-20 San Vicente · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ Indictment of the Baja Cal. governor (Goycoechea): tyranny & corruption — contraband with foreign ships, selling foreign goods at his private Loreto stores (profiting off the royal warehouse), forcing troops to buy there, taking the soldiers' rations & cigarettes to resell, selling the troops' munitions to traders, gambling, troops & families in misery, demoralization; only *gachupines* employed; the Padre Presidente indifferent.
