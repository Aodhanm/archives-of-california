# ⭐⭐ Peralta's punitive expedition (19–27 Jan 1805) vs. the gentiles who attacked Padre Cuevas near Sta Clara. Left SF 19 

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n33–n34** · tomo 64–65 · 1805-01-30 SF · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Peralta's punitive expedition (19–27 Jan 1805) vs. the gentiles who attacked Padre Cuevas near Sta Clara. Left SF 19 Jan w/ inválidos, vecinos & 18 troops; tracked into the sierra (rains hid the trail), seized 2 gentile guides; 23 Jan battle — "fueron haciendo armas y nos comenzaron á tirar… dimos sobre ellos": ~5 gandules killed incl. captains; pursued into the woods; took 25 women + 11 Indians (2 wounded) captive; released 1 on 27 Jan to prove innocence.
