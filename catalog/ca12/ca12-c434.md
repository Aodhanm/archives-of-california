# Aid to HMS *Racoon*: gave Capt. Black 42 arrobas of gunpowder (to be restored if unconsumed at a Spanish port); the *Rac

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n378** · tomo 1221–3 · 1814-04-29 San Francisco · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

Aid to HMS *Racoon*: gave Capt. Black 42 arrobas of gunpowder (to be restored if unconsumed at a Spanish port); the *Racoon* left the 19th for Monterey, then "de allí en derechura á las islas de Sandwich en persecución de los Anglo-Américanos."
