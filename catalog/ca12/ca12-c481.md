# FINAL DOC — NWC schooner *Columbia* (2nd visit): Capt. John Jennings, supercargo Agustín Belhune[?]; the same ship that 

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n408–n410** · tomo 1417–20 · 1815-11-03 (expediente) · Savage abstract · ⭐

FINAL DOC — NWC schooner *Columbia* (2nd visit): Capt. John Jennings, supercargo Agustín Belhune[?]; the same ship that a year before advanced the Habilitado goods for provisions; now reclaiming deserters (7 delivered); Argüello gave the promised provisions, told her not to return without royal permission, freed the English deserters (among the captives 4 Galicians, 2 Portuguese, 2 Maltese); forbade trade between Jennings's ship and "la gente del Ross," posted a wharf guard; ordered the reclaimed prisoners delivered on condition they be absolved of any crime committed. — "FIN DEL TOM. XIX."
