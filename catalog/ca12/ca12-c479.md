# Passport/proclamation of the ship *Suvorov* — addressed "á nuestros buenos amigos y vecinos, los nobles y honrados Españ

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n403–n406+** · tomo ~1405– · 1811-07-12 St. Petersburg · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐⭐

Passport/proclamation of the ship *Suvorov* — addressed "á nuestros buenos amigos y vecinos, los nobles y honrados Españoles habitantes de Cal." The RAC under Tsar Alexander I invokes the long Russo-Spanish friendship, the 1812 Veliky Luki treaty, the Battle of Vitoria (21 Jun 1813) (Jourdan's baton, 130 cannon taken), Napoleon's catastrophe in Russia, the Spanish-prisoner "Regimiento Imperial Alejandro," Wellington crossing the Pyrenees; recounts Rezanov's 1806 SF visit & the bid to open commerce between California and the RAC colonies at Kodiak & Sitka; references the 15 May 1810 RAC proclamation [= C329]; names Baranov at Sitka dispatching a brig of goods. Concl. (n407–408, tomo 1405–10): they now send the *Suvorov* under Capt. [Lazarev] with supercargo Herman Mollers[?] and a cargo, hoping California will trade; signed by the RAC directors under Alexander I. [Fuller companion to C329.]
