**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n185–n188** · tomo 504–506 · 1806-06-18 San Diego · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Written instructions for the exploration expedition (transcribing the Gov.'s 19 May order): befriend the gentiles so they "lose their fear," gather cimarrones, keep a daily diary of routes; strict orders the troop not to abuse the gentiles, take nothing, no contact with their women; 20–25 day expedition.
