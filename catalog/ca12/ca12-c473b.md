# Ingraham concl.: Baranov ("Coscoff/Beranoff") styles himself Governor of Sitka; Ingraham knows Benjamin Tarasoff & John 

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n395–n397** · tomo 1368–73 · 1815-10-02 Monterey · verbatim transcription · 

Ingraham concl.: Baranov ("Coscoff/Beranoff") styles himself Governor of Sitka; Ingraham knows Benjamin Tarasoff & John Elliot de Castro (interpreter, English, paid $50/mo.); the brig (the *Ilmena*) worked San Luis, rancho de las Bodegas, San Pedro, San Juan, San Simón for otter pelts & provisions (5–6× at the Ranchos). ② "El Segundo": a sailor under Patrón Boris [Tarasov] on Baranov's merchant ship; 2 yrs aboard the American brig (Capt. Walter Whitmarck); names Pedro Camú (American); Boris Tarasoff = shoemaker/carpenter; Elliot de Castro sold meat & goods at the Spanish establishments; the rancho del Refugio (Sgt. [José F.] Ortega's) used for provisioning.
