# ⭐⭐ On commerce with the Californias: trade goods carried back from Calif. on the brig Activo & frigate Concepción by the

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n82–n86** · tomo 223–226 · 1803-04-30 México · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ On commerce with the Californias: trade goods carried back from Calif. on the brig Activo & frigate Concepción by their commanders, chaplains, officers — and the missions — without paying the established fletes (per Gálvez's 1769 Arancel) is ruled an abuse. Orders a bando at San Blas / Tepic / the Calif. presidios: every ship registered outbound & inbound, only moderate departmental freight, no sailors smuggling. (Legalized copy Méx 16 Feb 1806, Velázquez de León.)
