# KUSKOV'S OWN LETTER (English translation by Iván Pisteff). As "Commercial Councillor" of the Honorable Russian-American 

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n372–n373** · tomo 1213–4 · 1814-06-08(20) [Fort Ross] · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐⭐

KUSKOV'S OWN LETTER (English translation by Iván Pisteff). As "Commercial Councillor" of the Honorable Russian-American Company, under special protection of His Majesty the Emperor of all the Russias: received the Gov.'s April letter via Moraga but couldn't act (no interpreter, can't read Spanish); offers the Gov. a present (a tent his men left at SF); asks the return of the Kodiak men captured at San Francisco (they "did no wrong," only saved themselves from the surf) + 2 men & 2 women Kodiaks detained in 1813.
