# ⭐⭐ A missionary suspected of contraband. Fr. Pedro González (Mission San Miguel) vanished for hours; soldiers presumed h

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n147–n148** · tomo 377–384 · 1806-04-01 San Diego · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ A missionary suspected of contraband. Fr. Pedro González (Mission San Miguel) vanished for hours; soldiers presumed he was "comiendo con el inglés" at a place called Carmen; a soldier saw him with a foreign-made leather case locked away. Sgt. José Pico searched & found the case holding 4 books of writing paper "en forma de remesas."
