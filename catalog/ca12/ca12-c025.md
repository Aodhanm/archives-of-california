# ⭐⭐ The Cuevas attack itself. Padre Pedro Cuevas requested an escort (soldiers Ignacio Alviso & Joaquín Higuera) to confe

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n34–n35** · tomo 66–67 · 1805-01-16 Mission San José · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ The Cuevas attack itself. Padre Pedro Cuevas requested an escort (soldiers Ignacio Alviso & Joaquín Higuera) to confess the sick at a Christian ranchería; in heavy fog the guide led them to a gentile ranchería claiming it Christian; arrows + great shouting, more came from nearby rancherías — Ignacio Higuera killed (= the *mayordomo*, per C26), Padre Cuevas wounded in the face, Joaquín Higuera in the thigh; saddle & spare horses killed; party besieged midday→dusk, losing all but their *cueras*, *adargas* & muskets.
