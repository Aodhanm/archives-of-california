# ⭐⭐ Naval council on the best means to drive foreign ships off the California coasts & where to station the patrol (resol

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n93–n100** · tomo 232–237 · 1805-09-20 San Blas · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Naval council on the best means to drive foreign ships off the California coasts & where to station the patrol (resolving the Arrillaga–Vivero dispute). Officers (pilot Salvador Menéndez, Alférez Manuel Murga, Braulio Otalora, Francisco Taraga[?]): the frigate to leave San Blas in Jan/Feb for Monterey, anchor ~1 month, then cruise the coast checking every cove down to Cabo San Lucas for English/American smugglers; also notes foreigners whaling off the coast. Signed by all 5 officers.
