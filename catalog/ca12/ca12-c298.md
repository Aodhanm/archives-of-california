# ⭐⭐⭐ Indians from the Russian establishments: two men + a woman from the Russian establishments, sent by the SF commander

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n252–n253** · tomo 742–744 · 1809-10-05 San Antonio · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐⭐ Indians from the Russian establishments: two men + a woman from the Russian establishments, sent by the SF commander to Monterey — keep the men in the barracks, the india as convenient; pay 1½ reales daily but have them work with the convicts (axe/earth) as Vallejo directs. [First Russian-establishment doc in the systematic read — ties to the Kuskov/Bodega 1808–09 presence.]
