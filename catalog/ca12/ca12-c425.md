# ⭐ Earthquakes — "Sufrimiento de la guarnición. Condición del presidio": the 21 Dec tremors still continuing; the garriso

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n367** · tomo 1193–6 · 1813-01-14 Sta Bárbara · Savage abstract · 

⭐ Earthquakes — "Sufrimiento de la guarnición. Condición del presidio": the 21 Dec tremors still continuing; the garrison suffering immense labor in the weather; asks the Government to help rebuild the presidio (or build anew); describes the buildings' bad state; proposes using the indios presos at Monterey + a *gratificación* (~$4,000), the troop employed throughout.
