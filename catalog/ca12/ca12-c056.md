# Loreto supply crisis (no ships): the San Blas Apostadero has only the frigate Princesa & brig Activo, both assigned to A

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n62–n63** · tomo 145–148 · 1805-05-30 México · Savage abstract · 

Loreto supply crisis (no ships): the San Blas Apostadero has only the frigate Princesa & brig Activo, both assigned to Alta California — so Loreto (needing maize & cigars) can't be supplied until their October return, unless its two goletas are used.
