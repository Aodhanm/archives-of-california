# ⭐ Medical dictamen on California's climate & diseases (from José Benítez's tour of Monterey & missions): climate judged 

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n63–n65** · tomo 152–155 · 1805-05-10 México · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Medical dictamen on California's climate & diseases (from José Benítez's tour of Monterey & missions): climate judged *healthy*, no endemic disease; illness comes from cold/exposure/bad water/poor diet and "carnal disorders," contact with the infected, and natives' reliance on empirical "national doctors" over rational medicine; syphilis (gálico) frequent & severe. Proposes an infirmary for the gentiles — esp. vs. syphilis, which "without aid ravages the populations."
