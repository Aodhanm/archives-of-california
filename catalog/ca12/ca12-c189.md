# ⭐⭐⭐ Interrogation of the 4 captured Peacock crewmen. 1st pilot Tomás Kilven (Thomas Kilby?) of Boston; contramaestre/int

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n176–n177** · tomo 470–473 · 1806-05-04 San Diego · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐⭐ Interrogation of the 4 captured Peacock crewmen. 1st pilot Tomás Kilven (Thomas Kilby?) of Boston; contramaestre/interpreter Juan Pedro (Jean-Pierre) of Bordeaux; sailors Blas Sincam[?] & Blas Sama of Boston. The brig Peacock: 10 R tons, 6×4-pdr + 4 pedreros, 18 crew (14 left). Left Boston 14 Apr 1805 for the NW Coast to trade furs with the natives, thence to Canton; reached the Sandwich Islands 12 Feb (sweet potatoes/dried meat); the San Gabriel coast 19 May. Cargo: 6 bales of batiste, painting supplies, a box of haberdashery. Fed maize/beans/lard since 12 Apr.
