# ⭐⭐ "Buque corsario contra los contrabandistas": Capt. Nicolás Noé will leave the *Flora* at San Pedro and prizes at San 

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n357–n358** · tomo 1176–8 · 1813-06-26 Sta Bárbara · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ "Buque corsario contra los contrabandistas": Capt. Nicolás Noé will leave the *Flora* at San Pedro and prizes at San Diego with the *Mercurio*, then scour the coast to Monterey; 3 ships reported in the [Channel] islands off rancho Refugio + others off the presidio; lent Noé 2 presidio cannon; recommends moving "Capitán Jorge" + the most suspect sailors to Monterey lest they league with the island gentiles & the "Americanos" who keep raiding the islands.
