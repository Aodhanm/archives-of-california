# ⭐ Legal ruling on the *Mercurio* seizure: the *Flora* could not lawfully seize her as a corsair — exercising the *derech

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n371** · tomo 1211–2 · 1814-04-24 México · Savage abstract · 

⭐ Legal ruling on the *Mercurio* seizure: the *Flora* could not lawfully seize her as a corsair — exercising the *derecho de corso* requires a declared state of war between two states; the real question is "el comercio ilícito que el Capt. de la *Mercurio* protegió en costas de Cal."; the San Blas marine command has no jurisdiction here.
