# Cluster: (May 8) query re the late Arrillaga & the six-presidio Habilitación accounts; (May 9) approved licensing the Am

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n393** · tomo 1354–62 · 1815 México · Savage abstract · ⭐

Cluster: (May 8) query re the late Arrillaga & the six-presidio Habilitación accounts; (May 9) approved licensing the American brig *Pedlar* — captured at San Luis Obispo by José Cavenecia (captain/owner of the frigate *Tagle*) — with its cargo; (Jun 27) requires Eayrs's answer to Arrillaga's letter on his purpose; orders vigilance over all foreigners' movements.
