# Provisioning costs of the *Mercurio* prisoners: per the Consejo de Presidios order (18 Sep 1813), the Habilitación suppo

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n369–n370** · tomo 1203–4 · 1814-10-30 Sta Bárbara · Savage abstract · 

Provisioning costs of the *Mercurio* prisoners: per the Consejo de Presidios order (18 Sep 1813), the Habilitación supported Eayrs & crew to embarkation — the Captain $406, the others $285.4 r.; Eayrs credited $1/day from 2 June (his capture) to 21 [Oct], when he embarked for San Blas; the 4 sailors at 1½ r./day from 30 Sep (arrival at Sta Bárbara) to the 17th, when received aboard the *Tagle*[?].
