# On the Habilitación General: Goycoechea given a new post (gov. of Baja Cal.) and ceasing as Habilitado Gen.; Pablo Vicen

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n11–n12** · tomo 28 · 1805-09-20 San Vicente · verbatim transcription · 

On the Habilitación General: Goycoechea given a new post (gov. of Baja Cal.) and ceasing as Habilitado Gen.; Pablo Vicente de Solá (capitán de milicias), proposed by Arrillaga in 3rd place, to substitute in case of death/illness (serving without salary until Goycoechea departs) — quotes the Viceroy's order noting Solá has his sureties ready. Ends p.28. ⭐ Early appearance of Solá — future CA governor 1815–22.
