# ⭐ Orders compensation to Mission San Juan Bautista + 15 Indians for aid given in 1800 to Monterey when sails believed en

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n6** · tomo 11–15 · 1804-11-15 México · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Orders compensation to Mission San Juan Bautista + 15 Indians for aid given in 1800 to Monterey when sails believed enemy were sighted (aid = 3 carretas, 9 horses, 15 Indians). Viceroy + Tribunal de Cuentas order payment for carts/teams/horses to the community and personally to the 15 Indians — "so they won't hold back from giving aid in future, and do so with greater zeal." Legalized copy by Arrillaga.
