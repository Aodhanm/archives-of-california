**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n30** · tomo 52–53 · 1805-05-10 Mission San José · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ Plot to burn Mission Santa Clara & kill the padres. A Christian Indian caught scouting (with a gentile from the ranchería) a planned night attack to burn the mission and kill the friars; gentiles massed in great number at the rancherías of Pelbones[?] & Seunenes; the prisoner declared all — 5 Christian Indians implicated & arrested.
