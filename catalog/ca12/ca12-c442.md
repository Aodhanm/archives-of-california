# Per Viceroy Calleja's 26 Jan order, presidios are henceforth to request their *memorias* from Guadalajara (can't be supp

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n381** · tomo 1249–53 · 1815-10-15 Monterey · Savage abstract · 

Per Viceroy Calleja's 26 Jan order, presidios are henceforth to request their *memorias* from Guadalajara (can't be supplied, "por causa de los sucesos políticos"); Ormaechea unable to relocate — appoint a new Habilitado General. Ackn.: Estudillo (Monterey 10 Oct), de la Guerra (Sta Bárbara 13), Francisco Mª Ruiz (San Diego 17).
