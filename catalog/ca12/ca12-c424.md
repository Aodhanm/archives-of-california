# ⭐⭐⭐⭐ The *Mercurio* / Eayrs depositions (19 Jun 1813)

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scans n360–n364** · Sav 353–357 · tomo 1181– · 1813-06-19, Santa Bárbara · Savage abstract

The sworn interrogation of the *Mercurio*'s Anglo-American crew: Capt. **George W. Eayrs** — eight years trading with the Russians and Northwest-Coast Indians, ~8 American ships buying otter on the coast, and **provisions carried to Fort Ross on Russian commission, delivered to Kuskov in May 1813** — the documentary heart of the Russo-American provisioning network.
