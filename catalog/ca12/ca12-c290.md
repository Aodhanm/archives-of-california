# ⭐ Certified act of the Junta General de Autoridades (México, 9 Aug 1808, presided by Iturrigaray): recognizes Fernando V

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n246** · tomo 708–716 · 1808-08-20 México · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Certified act of the Junta General de Autoridades (México, 9 Aug 1808, presided by Iturrigaray): recognizes Fernando VII's authority, nullifies acts of his "representatives" (Napoleon's regime), names the Viceroy first representative. [Prelude to the Sept 1808 coup.]
