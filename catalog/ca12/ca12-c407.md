# ⭐ British warships in the Pacific: relays Regency order (25th) — the English Ambassador announced Britain will send 1–2 

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n343–n344** · tomo ~1121 · 1813-06-28 México · Savage abstract · 

⭐ British warships in the Pacific: relays Regency order (25th) — the English Ambassador announced Britain will send 1–2 warships to the Pacific to protect its whalers in the war with the U.S.; Spanish ports to give them good reception, attend the officers/crew, help curb desertion of British sailors, but watch their conduct & prevent contact with "los descontentos." (Estudillo copy.)
