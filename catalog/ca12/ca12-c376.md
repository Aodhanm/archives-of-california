# ⭐ Colorado River Indian movement + Moraga's measures: gave Gabriel Moraga troops/munitions (San Gabriel/San Juan Cap./Sa

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n310–n311** · tomo 979–982 · 1811-01-21 San Diego · Savage abstract · 

⭐ Colorado River Indian movement + Moraga's measures: gave Gabriel Moraga troops/munitions (San Gabriel/San Juan Cap./San Luis Rey companies); lookouts watch the "indios del Colorado". Moraga's expedition brought in 2 Indians (one a "capitán de recoletura") + 11 horses + 61 from rancho de los Nietos + 2 cimarrones.
