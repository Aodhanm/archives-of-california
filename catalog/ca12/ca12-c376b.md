# ⭐⭐ Argüello: the Russian-Aleut otter fleet inside San Francisco Bay (30 May 1811)

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n311–n313** · tomo 983–986 · 1811-05-30 San Francisco · Savage abstract ·

"1811 Mayo 30. Sⁿ Franᶜᵒ. Luis Argüello á Arrillaga — Sobre pesca de nutrias." The otter canoes (the RAC's Aleut baidarka fleet) remain fishing within the port, up to 50 together; ~130 counted between Punta Boneta and San Antonio; the detained neophyte lobo-fishers released ("no por espías nuestros"); a sergeant's 3-day patrol down the coast toward San Pedro y los Pilares found only beach traces. Scale of the fleet: the Indians "regulan y comparan qᵉ excede á toda la gente" of the mission.
