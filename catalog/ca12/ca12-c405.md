# ⭐⭐ "Sobre los rusos en Cal." A foreign nation, apparently Russian, has established at the puerto de la Bodega — a fortif

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n336–n337** · tomo 1093–6; **Dup. 1112–4; Trip. 1147–?** · 1813-08-03 México · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ "Sobre los rusos en Cal." A foreign nation, apparently Russian, has established at the puerto de la Bodega — a fortification with artillery and ~100 individuals, mostly Russians from the establishments at Onalaska & Kodiak, agents of the Russian [American] Company, with the aim of opening commerce with the provinces; cites the treaty of alliance signed at Veliky Luki, 8(20) July 1812. Establishing in Spanish territory (the northern coast up to the entrance of Juca [Juan de Fuca]) violates the territory & treaties. Orders relayed to the Comandantes Generales of Nueva Galicia & the Provincias Internas (Baja Cal & Sonora) and the Comandante of San Blas.
