# "Comercio con los rusos": among the Ross boats arriving from Bodega came a 130-ton schooner that supplied goods to the t

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n386** · tomo 1281–9 · 1815-09-30 San Francisco · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

"Comercio con los rusos": among the Ross boats arriving from Bodega came a 130-ton schooner that supplied goods to the troops, payable with interest — cloth, tools, iron in exchange for wheat & flour; the schooner returned to Ross; the itemized list sent to Argüello (not enclosed, the Habilitado being absent). [Direct Russo-Californian provision trade.]
