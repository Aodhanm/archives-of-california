# ⭐⭐ Decree (1 Apr 1805) to increase the presidial companies' force — withdrawal of the 1st Catalan Volunteers + new missi

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n46–n49** · tomo →120 · 1805-04-03 México · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Decree (1 Apr 1805) to increase the presidial companies' force — withdrawal of the 1st Catalan Volunteers + new mission foundations + "frecuentes arribadas de buques Americanos á los puertos" leave too few troops. Quotes Arrillaga's 1803 plan (Nº 435) for exact augmentations: SF +1 capt, 1 sgt, 4 cabos, 29 soldiers; Monterey +1 sgt, 5 cabos, 19 sol.; Sta Bárbara +1 capt, 4 cabos, 3 sol.; San Diego +1 capt, 1 sgt, 3 cabos, 14 sol.; Loreto +1 alférez, 1 sgt, 4 cabos, 10 sol.
