# On deserter punishment: abolishes the penalty of *baquetas* (running the gauntlet) throughout the Indies; 2nd-time deser

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n4–n5** · tomo 5–6 · 1805 México (header "Set. 23"; Rl orden 14 Feb via Caballero) · verbatim transcription · 

On deserter punishment: abolishes the penalty of *baquetas* (running the gauntlet) throughout the Indies; 2nd-time deserters "con iglesia" instead get 6 months in shackles (cleaning duty) then 10 years presidio, without formal trial, whether or not they hold sanctuary.
