# ⭐ Self-defense ruling: the 3 Indians (Francisco Esteban, Pío, Francisco Zerpa) killed Luis in self-defense — he attacked

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n169–n170** · tomo 453–456 · 1806-05-04 San Diego · verbatim transcription · 

⭐ Self-defense ruling: the 3 Indians (Francisco Esteban, Pío, Francisco Zerpa) killed Luis in self-defense — he attacked with a bow; a stone by Estevan struck his stomach, killing him; body hidden in a cliff (4–5 months). Judged not liable (cf. C163).
