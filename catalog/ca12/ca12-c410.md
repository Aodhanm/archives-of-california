# ⭐⭐ 1812–13 earthquakes: the 8 March shock was the last felt; cracks oozing chapapote (tar) in the Sierra de San Bernardo

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n345–n346** · tomo 1133–5 · 1813-03-19 Sta Bárbara · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ 1812–13 earthquakes: the 8 March shock was the last felt; cracks oozing chapapote (tar) in the Sierra de San Bernardo, at Lomas, and in the Tulares; a coastal volcano gained new vents + another reported behind the Sierra de los Pinos — Argüello infers subterranean tar "boiling with sulfur" at the quake's center; the presidio chapel just finished (palisade, tile-roofed, 18 × 6 varas); the alférez's & his own houses still need roofing & plastering.
