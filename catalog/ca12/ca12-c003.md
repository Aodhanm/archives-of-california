# On the order of formation/precedence of artillery troops and dismounted dragoons. Transmits royal order (via Min. José A

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n3** · tomo 4 · 1805-09-20 México (header "Set. 20"; conveys Rl orden 23 Nov 1804) · verbatim transcription · 

On the order of formation/precedence of artillery troops and dismounted dragoons. Transmits royal order (via Min. José Antonio Caballero), 4 articles: dismounted dragoons serving as infantry rank after artillery & before dragoons; militia artillerymen attached to artillery form with veterans; dismounted-dragoon companies per Rl Orden 8 Jul 1799; militia infantry regiments in the Indies.
