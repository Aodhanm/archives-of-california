# ⭐⭐ "Preceptos Generales" — Arrillaga's general instructions after his first year as governor. Each commander responsible

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n111–n115** · tomo 272–283, sup 570–6 · 1806-12-22 San Diego · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ "Preceptos Generales" — Arrillaga's general instructions after his first year as governor. Each commander responsible for the pueblo entrusted to him: peace/harmony, a disciplined company, Indians "dóciles y subordinados" (prevent fugitives), due regard for invalids/retirees/vecinos; escort sizes 3–6 soldiers; pacify Indians by few visits → "conquista ó reducción"; peace & harmony with the missionaries (extinguish the "notorias etiquetas"). Ends with a foreign-trade prohibition — notes maize was sold at the ensenada of San José del Cabo (bought in Alta Cal.) and mares taken to the Sandwich Islands from this coast; "muchas personas caracterizadas" involved. Registered at the presidios 1807.
