# ⭐⭐⭐ "Conducción de presos rusos": the Englishman Don Juan Eliot [John Elliot de Castro] and six more foreigners, capture

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n368–n369** · tomo 1200–1 · 1814-10-13 Sta Bárbara · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐⭐ "Conducción de presos rusos": the Englishman Don Juan Eliot [John Elliot de Castro] and six more foreigners, captured aboard the Russian corvette *Ilmena* in the ensenada del Refugio, are to be conducted to Monterey with all speed by the Governor's order; asks the missions to furnish *bagajes* & aid to cabo Anastasio Carrillo who leads the escort.
