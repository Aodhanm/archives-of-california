# "Toma de posesión de gobierno político y militar": Solá notifies his appointment by Viceroy Calleja (31 Dec 1814) as Gov

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n382** · tomo 1254–7 · 1815-10-05 Monterey · Savage abstract · ⭐

"Toma de posesión de gobierno político y militar": Solá notifies his appointment by Viceroy Calleja (31 Dec 1814) as Governor & Inspector of Cal. troops; swore before Comdt.-Gen. of Nueva Galicia José de la Cruz; arrived Monterey 30 Aug 1815 aboard the frigate *Paz y Religión* (sent by interim Gov. Capt. José Argüello). Recognized by all presidios (de la Guerra had the troops recognize him 2 Oct; Ruiz at San Diego 8 Oct).
