# ⭐ Prize law: how prizes taken from contrabandists are distributed under the Mar del Sur regulation — contraband ship + c

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n355–n356** · tomo 1170–1 · 1813-06-27 Sta Bárbara · Savage abstract · 

⭐ Prize law: how prizes taken from contrabandists are distributed under the Mar del Sur regulation — contraband ship + cargo appraised, sold at public auction, a share to the corsair's *armadores*, the rest to the crew by class; prisoners to the castillo; captains/pilots lodged by the armadores, crews maintained by the King; after declarations, repeat-offender "pirates" shipped out via Cabo de Hornos / Europe, sometimes via Panamá.
