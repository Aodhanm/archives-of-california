# ⭐⭐ On the prohibition of foreign commerce with the Californias: the three oficios written in three languages (1810) by t

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n338** · tomo 1097–9 · 1812-03-26 San Vicente · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ On the prohibition of foreign commerce with the Californias: the three oficios written in three languages (1810) by the Russian Emperor Alexander I; in reply, the governments of Alta & Baja Cal would permit no new dominion nor commerce with any power, being forbidden by law — only Fernando VII (now the Real Junta de Regencia) could grant it; "siempre que S.M. lo permita… obedeceremos sus órdenes como buenos vasallos."
