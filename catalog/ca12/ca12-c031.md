# ⭐ Second Peralta expedition vs. the Cuevas attackers — "felices resultados": most near-Christians returned to missions S

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n39–n40** · tomo 82–84 · 1805-02-28 SF · Savage abstract · 

⭐ Second Peralta expedition vs. the Cuevas attackers — "felices resultados": most near-Christians returned to missions Sta Clara/San José, some gentiles sought baptism; a capitanejo of the big ranchería "el Pescadero" (SF rivers) assured Peralta his people weren't involved; Peralta returned 22nd with 2 Christian accomplices (held + 4 gentiles), recommends exiling the Christians to a distant presidio.
