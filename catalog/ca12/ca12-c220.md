**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n205** · tomo 543–545 · 1807-10-27 México · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Abolition of the 1805 anti-contraband bounty decrees (C54/C59): since neutral ships are now permitted to trade legally, clandestine trade will cease; revert to the 12 Jul 1802 instruction; only intercepted silver/gold bullion goes to captors.
