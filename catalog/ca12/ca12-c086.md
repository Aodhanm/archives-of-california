# ⭐⭐ Anti-smuggling patrol strategy: how to stop foreigners trading with the coastal natives. Settles the Arrillaga vs. Be

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n87–n92** · tomo 227–231 · 1805-09-20 San Blas · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Anti-smuggling patrol strategy: how to stop foreigners trading with the coastal natives. Settles the Arrillaga vs. Benito Vivero y Escaño dispute over where to station the patrol frigate. Dictamen: use the copper-bottomed frigate *Princesa* (faster), based at Monterey, cruising to Punta de la Concepción (34°32') and on to San Diego, checking all anchorages for English/American smugglers; build a small battery at Punta López de Haro (entrance to Pto San Quintín) to block foreign ships. (Legalized Méx 10 Mar 1806.)
