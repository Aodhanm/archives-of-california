# ⭐⭐⭐ "Visita á los rusos": Teniente Gabriel Moraga visited the Russians north of Bodega, returning 27 Jan without inciden

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n349–n351** · tomo 1151–2, 1156–7, 1153–5 · 1813-01-31 San Francisco · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐⭐ "Visita á los rusos": Teniente Gabriel Moraga visited the Russians north of Bodega, returning 27 Jan without incident; infers their object was trade in provisions on Rezanof's terms; quotes a paper Kuskov ("Coscof") handed Moraga; woodcutters across the port brought in a deserter (fled harsh punishment); Kuskov asked Moraga to return Russian deserters; vivid troop-nakedness report ("bastantes soldados no tienen camisa… ni chupa ni calzones… la desnudez y la miseria me la está experimentando"). Appended at pp. 343–4: Moraga's certified copy of the 15 Mar 1810 RAC letter to the inhabitants of the Californias ('Es copia á la letra… Nova Rossa… 25 de Enero de 1813. Gabriel Moraga'), with Savage's cross-note 'vide pp. 271–3 de este tomo' (= doc 329).
