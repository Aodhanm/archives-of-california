# ⭐⭐ Cuevas affair — umbrella report (ties together the C24 diary + C25/Sánchez & Alviso declarations). Confirms the mayor

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n36–n37** · tomo 68–74 · 1805-01-31 SF · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ Cuevas affair — umbrella report (ties together the C24 diary + C25/Sánchez & Alviso declarations). Confirms the mayordomo Ignacio Higuera killed; dispatched Peralta with 34 men (15 vecinos); complains Padre Cuevas, newly arrived from México, recklessly ignored the soldier's warnings; asks the Gov. to order Monterey to send help promptly for future *salidas* vs. hostile Indians; Peralta's caballada returned broken-down.
