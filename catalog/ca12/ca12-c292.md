# On the anchorage duty (derecho de anclaje): commerce ships from Europe/non-favored American ports pay 2 pesos at Veracru

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n247–n248** · tomo 723–726 · 1808-04-25 México · verbatim transcription · 

On the anchorage duty (derecho de anclaje): commerce ships from Europe/non-favored American ports pay 2 pesos at Veracruz; nothing charged at Acapulco/San Blas/the Californias — Tribunal recommends no change.
