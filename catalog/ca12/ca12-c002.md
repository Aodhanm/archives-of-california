# Decree declaring "desvanecidos" (unfounded) the charges Fray Antonio de la Concepción raised against abuses/mismanagemen

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n1–n2** · tomo 1–3 · 1805 [Savage header "Nbre 19"; decree body dated "19 de Abril de 1805" — discrepancy, flagged] · verbatim transcription · 

Decree declaring "desvanecidos" (unfounded) the charges Fray Antonio de la Concepción raised against abuses/mismanagement in the Nueva California missions run by the Colegio Apostólico de San Fernando; Fiscal de Real Hacienda found the accusations baseless; praises the missionaries' apostolic zeal; orders presidio commandants to keep "recíproca buena correspondencia" with the friars. Certified copy by Arrillaga (Loreto, 10 Jun 1805); registered at S. Diego (2 Jul, Manuel Rodríguez), Sta Bárbara (5 Jul, Luis Raymundo Carrillo), Monterey (8/12 Jul, José de la Guerra y Noriega), SF (Argüello).
