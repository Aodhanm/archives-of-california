# ⭐⭐ Prisoner-exchange resolution: O'Cain had proposed swapping cabo Osuna + 2 soldiers for the 4 detained Americans; now 

**C-A 12 (Prov. St. Pap. XIX), scan n165–n166** · tomo 438–441 · 1806-07-25 San Diego · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ Prisoner-exchange resolution: O'Cain had proposed swapping cabo Osuna + 2 soldiers for the 4 detained Americans; now Osuna is freed, no exchange needed. Osuna was imprudent (could have lost his whole party); more caution in future.
