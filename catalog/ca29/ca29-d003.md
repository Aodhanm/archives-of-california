**C-A 29 (Departmental State Papers), scan n7–n8** · Sav p. 8–9 · tomo p. 14–16 · 1835-03-03 · verbatim transcription · 

Conspiración contra el Gobierno — the March-1835 Apalátegui–Torres revolt intelligence: Gutiérrez reports a conspiracy afoot, with el Médico [Dr. Francisco] Torres (Híjar–Padrés colony figure) as a principal conspirator, kept under surveillance along with 'varios sonoricos' [Sonorans] as his proselytes; alerted the comandantes of San Luis & San Diego; will arrest Torres and form a sumaria if evidence warrants
