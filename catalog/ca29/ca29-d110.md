# The 1838 Alvarado–Carrillo civil-war campaign dossier: Castro congratulating on the toma de San Buenaventura (~300 men) 

**C-A 29 (Departmental State Papers), scan n195–n201** · Sav p. 198–204 · tomo p. 435–449 · 1838 (Apr–May) · verbatim transcription · 

The 1838 Alvarado–Carrillo civil-war campaign dossier: Castro congratulating on the toma de San Buenaventura (~300 men) [was GS-n195]; civil-war logistics (gunpowder, 4000 cartuchos, Sgt. Hipólito Sepúlveda to San Juan w/ 20 soldiers); Inocente García from SLO ('falso informe del Administrador'); ⭐⭐ Alvarado → Carrillo urging him to abandon 'la mala causa de los sublevados,' naming the rebel commander Pablo de la Portilla + a Castañeda, and his recourse to the national militia; Tobar's farewell (leaving for Mexico); Alvarado → Bandini on cattle + 'restablecer la paz' (with Anastasio & Pico)
