# The Feb-1835 San Bernardino Indian uprising: report on the *gentiles*' attack on the Rancho de San Bernardino (the mayor

**C-A 29 (Departmental State Papers), scan n1–n2** · Sav p. 2–3 · tomo p. — · 1835-02 (early) · verbatim transcription · 

The Feb-1835 San Bernardino Indian uprising: report on the *gentiles*' attack on the Rancho de San Bernardino (the mayordomo fled at the start of the refriega; 13 dead), the punitive *partida de tropa* (~50–80 armed & provisioned men) dispatched via the Alcalde of Los Angeles; the mayordomo's intelligence that the rebels were directed by 'un alcalde y otros,' and ⭐ that the gentiles conspired to ally with the Río Colorado (Yuma/Mojave) rancherías to form raids and attack Santa Ana and the pueblo de Los Ángeles
