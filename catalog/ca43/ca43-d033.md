# The Nicolás Fink murder case: José Castro publishes by bando a Sup. Govt decree dividing the Republic's departments into

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n180** · Sav p.  · tomo p. iii.235–237 (stamp 73) · 1840-01-01 / 1841-04-01 · Savage abstract · 

The Nicolás Fink murder case: José Castro publishes by bando a Sup. Govt decree dividing the Republic's departments into two sections + renewing half the Cámara de Diputados (p.235). Then Alvarado's 1841 proclama — no Superior Tribunal de Justicia nor Junta Departamental being convened, he was obliged to confirm the Juez de 1ª's death sentence on the homicides Atanacio Valencia, Santiago Linares & José Duarte for the murder of the German merchant Nicolás Fink (p.237). A capital-case foreign-resident record; Alvarado exercising judicial authority absent the tribunals.
