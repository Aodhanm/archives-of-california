# Publishes decrees (engineer corps + colegio militar; dissolving army corps acting against the Constitution); ⭐ verbatim:

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n72** · Sav p.  · tomo p. ii.167–174 · 1833–1835 · verbatim transcription · 

Publishes decrees (engineer corps + colegio militar; dissolving army corps acting against the Constitution); ⭐ verbatim: 'Tratado de amistad, comercio y navegación entre México y Perú,' ordered observed by Santa Anna 20 Nov 1833 (p.167–171); 1835-12-01 decree creating a third District diputado (p.174); 1836-02-04 on *jefes y oficiales que se pronuncian* (p.175).
