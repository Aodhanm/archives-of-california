# the complete 'Defensores de la Independencia y de las Leyes' militia *reglamento* (per the President's 4 Jun 1845 *Bases

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n204–n208** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 285 (stamps 114–118) · 1845-10-08 · Savage abstract · 

the complete 'Defensores de la Independencia y de las Leyes' militia *reglamento* (per the President's 4 Jun 1845 *Bases*): open a volunteer-inscription Register at each Sub-Prefectura / Juzgado (8 a.m.–noon); plural-vote election of sergeants; officers named by the Asamblea; ⭐ the Comandante Gral to solicit arms & munitions 'por no poder proporcionar el Departamento' (the arms-shortage confession on the eve of 1846); organized into squadrons — San José/Sta Bárbara/San Diego + Monterey/Yerba Buena. Publ. Monterey 20 Dec 1845 by Alcalde Escobar
