# Alvarado, ill, hands command to Manuel Jimeno Casarín (Jun 1839); then, named Gobernador Constitucional by the Sup. Govt

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n423** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 44–45 · 1839–41 · Savage abstract · 

Alvarado, ill, hands command to Manuel Jimeno Casarín (Jun 1839); then, named Gobernador Constitucional by the Sup. Govt., takes command (Nov 1839); 1841 — Bernal cattle damages, an arrest order (Felipe Baturín); ⭐ María Silveria Pacheco's grievance that Judge Dolores Pacheco unjustly evicted her and carted off her belongings.
