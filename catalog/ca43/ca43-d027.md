# The 1806 Rezanov / Khvostov *Juno* voyage to San Francisco (extract, embedded in the retrospective register): Argüello r

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n36** · Sav p.  · tomo p. i.334–335 · 1806-04-14 · Savage abstract · 

The 1806 Rezanov / Khvostov *Juno* voyage to San Francisco (extract, embedded in the retrospective register): Argüello reports what Capt. Khvostov ('Chrestov') of the frigate *Juno* told him — sailed to Japan as ambassador, carried a passport from 'Portugal, Inglaterra y Holanda,' needed provisions; wore 'varias cruzes é insignias y una llave de oro colgada' (verbatim). Followed by a copied French letter (SF, 21 Apr 1806, unsigned) in the name of the Emperor of Russia, the Conde de Rezanov ('Marcoff') requesting assistance for the ships of the Compañía Americana Rusa under Rezanov, 'que se hace conocido á la América occidental rusa.'
