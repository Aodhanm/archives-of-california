# The port-captain (foreign-ship / foreign-sailor) layer — closest thing to 'arrival logs': Narváez accuses an unnamed ind

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n300** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 288–293 (stamp 74) · 1842–1843 · Savage abstract · 

The port-captain (foreign-ship / foreign-sailor) layer — closest thing to 'arrival logs': Narváez accuses an unnamed individual of being a *contrabandista de la playa* who shelters foreign sailors deserted from the ships ('marineros estrangeros desertores de los buques,' p.288–9); orders Tomás Blanco's disembarkation withheld pending the Juzgado (p.292); the Juez asks the barca *Ntra Sra de Clarita* to carry a deportee to Sonora (p.293).
