# 1838 Alvarado-era civil-war cluster (brief abstracts): Castro reports a 160-man force garrisoning 'la plaza' abandoned i

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n420** · Sav p.  · tomo p. (tomo 41) · 1838 · Savage abstract · 

1838 Alvarado-era civil-war cluster (brief abstracts): Castro reports a 160-man force garrisoning 'la plaza' abandoned it by night after a 2-day continuous firefight, only 1 dead — to be pursued by cavalry; order to free all political prisoners (San Fernando); two bronze cannon at the Castillo transferred to José Estrada; Alvarado orders two deserters (Simplicio Valdez, Domingo Hernández) caught. → military-history register (skirmish).
