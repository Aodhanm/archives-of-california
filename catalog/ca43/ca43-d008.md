# Chico's governorship decrees: takes possession of command (3 May 1836); bando to form a 'noticia general de todos los ex

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n416** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 37–38 · 1836-05 · Savage abstract · 

Chico's governorship decrees: takes possession of command (3 May 1836); bando to form a 'noticia general de todos los extranjeros' (foreigner registry); 2 decrees creating the juntas departamentales & new form of govt (centralism); re-imprison Gertrudis Garibay & Juan García; 2 bandos protecting commerce; apply the 11 Jul 1833 secularization law (the Diputación being unable to convene); José M.ª Maldonado named Secretary of the Junta Departamental.
