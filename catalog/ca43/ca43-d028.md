# Figueroa's publication-bandos of federal legislation: publishes decrees authorizing the President to command in person; 

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n48–n54** · Sav p.  · tomo p. ii.49–55 · 1833-12-31 / 1834 · verbatim transcription · 

Figueroa's publication-bandos of federal legislation: publishes decrees authorizing the President to command in person; extinguishing the Colegio de Sta Mª de Todos Santos; Spanish retirees from the military-health corps; the Gómez Farías comisaría decree; a verbatim Gómez Farías decree on the *jueces de 1ª instancia* (22 Jul 1833, p.128) reorganizing the juzgados per the 1812 law (verbal justice under 100 pesos, penalties of reclusion/jail/public works).
