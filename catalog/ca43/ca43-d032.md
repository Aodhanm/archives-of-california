# Foreigner-registry bando (fuller text of Doc 8): all foreigners resident in the District must present themselves to auth

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n150** · Sav p.  · tomo p. iii.150 (stamp 64) · 1836-03-06 · Savage abstract · 

Foreigner-registry bando (fuller text of Doc 8): all foreigners resident in the District must present themselves to authorities within 10 days, 'bajo la pena de 25 [pesos?] de multa y ocho días de grilletes en obras públicas' (p.175–76); + federal decrees on the interim presidency (José Justo Corro substituting, 27 Feb 1836; Ortiz Monasterio) and a 22 Feb 1836 Congress decree.
