# 1834 cholera-*morbo* public-health measures (genuine CA record): the preventive/curative method of the military Sanidad 

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n80** · Sav p.  · tomo p. ii.80 (stamp 32) · 1834 · Savage abstract · 

1834 cholera-*morbo* public-health measures (genuine CA record): the preventive/curative method of the military Sanidad corps (Manl de Alva) circulated; sick without means moved to the hospital at Monterey; at Los Ángeles the ayuntamiento to distribute aid / set up a *lazareto* from municipal funds; where no ayuntamiento exists, the military commanders + friars to arrange it (p.336).
