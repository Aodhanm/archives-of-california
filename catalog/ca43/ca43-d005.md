# Foreign-resident grievance: the murder of a foreigner (son of a European; had left the schr. *Fly* at SF 'por haber camb

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n280** · Sav p.  · tomo p. (tomo 54) · ~1841–43 · Savage abstract · 

Foreign-resident grievance: the murder of a foreigner (son of a European; had left the schr. *Fly* at SF 'por haber cambiado su bandera,' en route to Monterey for another ship) killed by 'bárbaros'; the local Juez de Paz did nothing; foreign residents' rights 'claman altamente por satisfacción.' (Graham-Affair-era foreign-resident tension.)
