# Micheltorena takes over the governorship — Jimeno hands him the mando (1 Jan 1843); his oath; a church-tithe order; circ

**C-A 43 (Departmental State Papers, Monterey), scan n427** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 48–49 · 1843 · Savage abstract · 

Micheltorena takes over the governorship — Jimeno hands him the mando (1 Jan 1843); his oath; a church-tithe order; circulates the Bases de Tacubaya; Independence-Day salutes/fireworks/misa de gracia (15–16 Sep); proclaims the bases constitucionales de la República with artillery salutes; orders a robbery at Pablo de la Guerra's house investigated.
