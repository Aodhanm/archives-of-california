# Fages transmits Art. 116 of the Comandante General's Instruction (2 Dec 1786): Indians NOT to manage the horse-herd or r

**C-A 23 (Provincial Records), scan n64–66** · Sav p. 65–67 · tomo p. 110–113 · 1787–1788 · Savage abstract · 

Fages transmits Art. 116 of the Comandante General's Instruction (2 Dec 1786): Indians NOT to manage the horse-herd or ride except in exceptional cases — applies esp. at Mission San Luis, and Fages notes he 'aun pudiera prohibir del todo que cabalgaren los indios, como se practica en Misiones de Sonora' → [[indian-raiding-article]] (colonial control of Indian horsemanship — the pre-history of mounted raiding); + a new price *arancel* (formed on rising ganados/semillas) + mission-family placements (Sta Clara/San Antonio)
