# KEYSTONE — 'Concesión de terrenos': Fages's instruction to formalize the juridical possession of lands (regadío/secadal)

**C-A 23 (Provincial Records), scan n156** · Sav p. 155 · tomo p. 307– · 1782-12-12 · Savage abstract · 

KEYSTONE — 'Concesión de terrenos': Fages's instruction to formalize the juridical possession of lands (regadío/secadal) + solares to the vecinos of San José — with 2 witnesses, grant possession, record in the 'libro Maestro,' issue each vecino a título noting the solar's situation, suertes, number of *varas*, and boundaries 'por todos rumbos' (each title prefaced with a copy of the mandamiento). The founding land-distribution mechanism of Pueblo San José (1782) — the origin of Californio pueblo land title → [[californio-article]] land tenure. ⚠ transcribe verbatim
