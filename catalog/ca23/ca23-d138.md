# KEYSTONE — 'Instrucción para perseguir gentiles cristianizados': a field instruction for a *partida* to recover fugitive

**C-A 23 (Provincial Records), scan n154–155** · Sav p. 153–154 · tomo p. 305–307 · 1782-12-11 · Savage abstract · 

KEYSTONE — 'Instrucción para perseguir gentiles cristianizados': a field instruction for a *partida* to recover fugitive Christian Indians hiding in gentile rancherías (Ranchería Soledad, los Sanjones): Art.1 surround the ranchería undetected at dawn, intimate via interpreter that the party 'va de paz, á regalarlo,' demand the fugitives be handed over, use arms only if resisted; Arts.3–5 amonish the gentiles gently ('no se les quiere hacer mal sino que sean amigos'), give gift-diplomacy (abalorio/beads), make them 'bien inteligenciados de nuestro sistema,' and have them return future refugee Christians unharmed to a mission/presidio where 'el Sr Capitán Grande los regalará con maíz, ropa'; soldiers must not enter 'con alboroto ni amenazas.' A keystone on mission-flight/fugitive-recovery + the coercion-vs-conciliation balance → [[indian-raiding-article]]. ⚠ transcribe verbatim
