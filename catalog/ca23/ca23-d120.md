# 'Sobre crueles castigos á indios' — some naturales of the friar's mission told Fages that Indians were being flogged wit

**C-A 23 (Provincial Records), scan n51** · Sav p. 52 · tomo p. 80 · 1785-06-11 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre crueles castigos á indios' — some naturales of the friar's mission told Fages that Indians were being flogged with chains ('azotaba… con cadenas') for trivial faults ('faltas triviales'); skeptical, he investigated 'con escrupulosidad' and found 'con dolor que es cierto,' and so 'implora las leyes, la humanidad, y el nombre del Rey' to demand such punishments cease — grave crimes go to the escort corporal for lawful punishment. A 1785 governor's critique of mission cruelty → [[indian-raiding-article]] (pairs with C-A 24 Borica 'llegó á ser cruel' & C-A 51 Hartnell). ⚠ transcribe verbatim
