# 'Fénix' closed-door protocol: the English merchant frigate *Fénix* (from Bengala) anchored at Sta Bárbara — grant aid 'a

**C-A 23 (Provincial Records), scan n299–n300** · Sav p. 297–298 · tomo p. IV:26–27 · 1795-08-31 · verbatim transcription · 

'Fénix' closed-door protocol: the English merchant frigate *Fénix* (from Bengala) anchored at Sta Bárbara — grant aid 'arreglada á derecho de gentes' but require its departure, prohibit all coastal reconnaissance, forbid all contact, demand a manifest, and if it refuses to leave cite its contravention of the Convention of '90 (Nootka 1790) — to be reported to London; + Arrillaga's order not to sell breeding livestock to foreigners
