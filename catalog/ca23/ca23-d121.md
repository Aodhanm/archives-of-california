# ⚠ BOUNDED — 1785 Fages by-recipient correspondence (item-level-worthy but mostly personal/routine): ⭐ the Fages–Eulalia 

**C-A 23 (Provincial Records), scan n52–60** · Sav p. 53–61 · tomo p. 81–95 · 1785 · Savage abstract · 

⚠ BOUNDED — 1785 Fages by-recipient correspondence (item-level-worthy but mostly personal/routine): ⭐ the Fages–Eulalia Callis marital scandal — Fages has Alférez Hermenegildo Sal gather testimony to clear his name from the public 'crimen' his wife alleged (n52, → registry/Sal); ⭐ Fages–Franciscan friction — snubs/'desaires' from Frs. Palóu & Matías tied to a Fr. Piéras document (n60); reprimand of Ignacio Vallejo's church conduct (n54); ministers requested for the Santa Bárbara founding (1786); a planned 'relación circunstanciada del estado del país' to the Viceroy (n57); maize at 12 rl/fanega (Savage: 'cosas de ninguna significación')
