# *Sobre la misma rencilla* — Argüello mediates: regrets he can't remedy the friar's troubles, but the cabo is seen angry,

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n39** · Sav p. 77–79½ · 1797-10-26 · Savage abstract · 

*Sobre la misma rencilla* — Argüello mediates: regrets he can't remedy the friar's troubles, but the cabo is seen angry, scolding, *"echar pestes,"* shouting that it was the Indians who did all the mission's works; at the friar's request removed Higuera from the mission; now the cabo complains bitterly and only the Gov. can remove him; Alejo also asks relief — refer to the Chief
