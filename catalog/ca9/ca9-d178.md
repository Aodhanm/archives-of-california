# ⭐⭐ *Aprehensión de unos Indios* — the San Borja-detached soldier had five Indians imprisoned (named, with "crimes": Igna

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n109–n110** · Sav p. 318–320 · 1797-01-28 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ *Aprehensión de unos Indios* — the San Borja-detached soldier had five Indians imprisoned (named, with "crimes": Ignacio — sent to catch *Cimarrones*, instead wandered 7 months bringing none; Benito — lifelong runaway "but never did harm"; Cristóbal, Vicente, Alonso — cattle-destroyers / *Cimarrón*); Arrillaga sent them to the frontier as more useful for the new [Santa Catalina] founding *(coerced-labor transfer)*
