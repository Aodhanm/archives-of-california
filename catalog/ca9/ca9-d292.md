**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n171** · Sav p. 633–636 · 1797-06-20 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ *Remisión de una Causa criminal* — remits the case against Aurelio, Indian of Mission San Juan Capistrano, for killing his wife; thought no formal process needed given the *reo*'s sincere confession that he only meant to punish her with blows and she likely took a fatal head-blow falling *(possibly = the "Olerio" case, Doc 262)*
