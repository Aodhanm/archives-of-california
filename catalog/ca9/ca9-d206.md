# ⭐⭐ *(three oficios)* — (a) all royal servants incl. naval officers arriving at NE ports must obey the Viceroy; (b) recei

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n125–n126** · Sav p. 467–469 · 1797-04-29 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ *(three oficios)* — (a) all royal servants incl. naval officers arriving at NE ports must obey the Viceroy; (b) received a certified copy of Teniente José Argüello's report on the terrain he reconnoitered on the banks of the Colorado & Gila and the gentiles inhabiting them; (c) the pearls (*perla*) in the warehouse + last year's collection weren't sent to San Blas (the ship's commander left)
