# ⭐ *Detención de un albañil / Daño en los ganados / Falta de tacos y barriles / aumento á una escolta* (3 ofs) — detains 

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n250–n251** · Sav p. 949–954 · 1797-04-01 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *Detención de un albañil / Daño en los ganados / Falta de tacos y barriles / aumento á una escolta* (3 ofs) — detains mason Toribio Ruiz with soldier José Lobo (running the brick-works) to advance 2 houses & a small warehouse this month; Gentile Indians with some cimarrones are doing notable damage to the San Miguel cattle — will detach the sergeant & 8 men to seize them; short of cannon-wadding material, has resorted to burlap sacks (*guangochas*); needs ≥24 good barrels & wood for oars; adds one man to the San Juan escolta to keep a daily lookout on the beach
