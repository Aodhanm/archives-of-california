# ⭐ *Sobre Indios homicidas* — had ordered the Frontier Sgt. to send him the gentiles imprisoned for killing Christian Ind

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n148** · Sav p. 535–536 · 1797-11-09 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *Sobre Indios homicidas* — had ordered the Frontier Sgt. to send him the gentiles imprisoned for killing Christian Indians, but Borica's 10 Aug order was to seize all accomplices & send them to San Diego; unaware of that, he'd urged Loreto instead (if they fled from San Diego they'd reach their nearby rancherías & do more harm)
