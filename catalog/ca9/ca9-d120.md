# *Cargo á una Compañía* — encloses his company's *Lista de cargo*; the company carries a complete debit of 1,425 p 1 r 7⅜

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n81–n82** · Sav p. 223–224 · 1797-10-21 · Savage abstract · 

*Cargo á una Compañía* — encloses his company's *Lista de cargo*; the company carries a complete debit of 1,425 p 1 r 7⅜ g; to cover it he must withhold all supply, *"con sumo dolor suyo,"* even from the previous men's families, issuing only service essentials; knows the charge is interim pending retentions on his predecessor
