# ⭐⭐ *Llegada de Cristianos* — the friars' defense: today received the 79 returned Christian fugitives; took offense at Ar

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n89** · Sav p. 246 · 1797-07-26 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ *Llegada de Cristianos* — the friars' defense: today received the 79 returned Christian fugitives; took offense at Argüello's *oficio*, insisting that over the past two years "á cuantos han vuelto, lejos de castigarlos, han sido muy bien recibidos y vestidos" — yet this didn't suffice to hold them; any fitting punishment would be Argüello's to apply *(direct counter-claim to the fugitives' flogging testimonies — Docs 112–114; the cruelty question is contested within the dossier)*
