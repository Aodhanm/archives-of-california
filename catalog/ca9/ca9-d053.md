⭐ *Sobre cierta licencia* — the Mission SF minister asks two Christian Indians be allowed to go by sea to the *otra banda* to seek their children, since by land lies *peligro de la Indiada* and a 100+ league detour; if refused they'd likely flee. Asks guidance for such cases
