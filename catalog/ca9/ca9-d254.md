# ⭐ *(two oficios)* — (a) to examine with the Fr.-President whether divine service is better fixed at San Antonio or alter

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n153** · Sav p. 552–555 · 1797-07-17 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *(two oficios)* — (a) to examine with the Fr.-President whether divine service is better fixed at San Antonio or alternating with Santa Ana (& whether to repair the Santa Ana chapel); (b) ⭐ soldiers' uniform: may wear a dyed buckskin *chupa* with red collar & cuffs & gilt buttons, dyed buckskin breeches & red vest, but the rigorous *Reglamento* dress on feast & guard days
