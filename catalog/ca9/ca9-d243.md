# ⭐ *(three oficios)* — (a) the merchant *balandra* "La Merced" sailed 4 Aug for the Río Yaqui; the free-commerce goleta "

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n147–n148** · Sav p. 530–534 · 1797-09-09 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *(three oficios)* — (a) the merchant *balandra* "La Merced" sailed 4 Aug for the Río Yaqui; the free-commerce goleta "Nuestra Señora del Carmen" anchored here the 2nd from San Blas, left for the Yaqui the 5th; (b) ⭐ S.M. relieved Branciforte of the viceroyalty, naming in his place Miguel José de Azanza (Secretary of State & of the War Despacho); (c) the habilitado-gen.'s costs in the Soler suit charged to the *Fondo de Gratificación*
