# ⭐⭐ *Sobre armar inváls y vecinos* — per his office of 5 Apr., the frigate Concepción was to bring 200 short 16-cal muske

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n273–n274** · Sav p. 1052–1053 · [s.f., 1797] · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ *Sobre armar inváls y vecinos* — per his office of 5 Apr., the frigate Concepción was to bring 200 short 16-cal muskets, 200 covers & cartridge-boxes & 16,000 gunflints to arm ¼ of the Invalids & citizens of each presidio's jurisdiction; poor citizens not to receive arms until invasion (collected back after); invalids & citizens to be supplied cartridges with a good reserve at the escoltas; monthly diaries to specify the number & calibre of arms & cartridges held & those spent in target practice
