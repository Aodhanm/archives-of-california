# ⭐⭐⭐ *Castigos de criminales indios* — Borica's sentencing decree apportioning punishment (azotes + months of grillete at

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n77–n79** · Sav p. 212–213 · 1797-08-26 · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐⭐ *Castigos de criminales indios* — Borica's sentencing decree apportioning punishment (azotes + months of grillete at ration, no pay, then returned to the friars) to the Sacalan/Cuchillón participants per their Aug-9 declarations; the 60 neophytes sent to the *otra banda* to recover fugitives were the party attacked; closes ordering work to begin 1 Sept, rations so they "no padezcan hambre," King's-works only the daylight hours health permits, "tratándolos con mucha humanidad."
