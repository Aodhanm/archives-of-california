# ⭐ *(three oficios)* — (a) if the four surviving San Borja Indians (one died) show signs of fleeing to the *Gentilidad*, 

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n124–n125** · Sav p. 463–466 · 1797-04-29 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *(three oficios)* — (a) if the four surviving San Borja Indians (one died) show signs of fleeing to the *Gentilidad*, will send them to San Diego; (b) will observe the orders on prompt coastal warnings; (c) will publish the *bando* of S.M.'s General Indulto on the peace concluded with France [Basel 1795]
