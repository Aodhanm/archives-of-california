# ⭐⭐⭐ *Sobre comunicación con la Sonora* — major strategic memo (answering the R.Hac. fiscal's 1 May request) on opening f

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n137–n141** · Sav p. 510–513 · 1797-10-26 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐⭐ *Sobre comunicación con la Sonora* — major strategic memo (answering the R.Hac. fiscal's 1 May request) on opening free overland communication with Sonora across the Colorado: the numerous Colorado-river gentiles begin ~18–20 leagues from Santa Catalina; recalls "el sacrificio que se verificó en 81" (the 1781 Yuma Revolt); proposes two options — (1) revive the Santa Olalla presidio at the Colorado crossing (planned 1778–83, ~100 men split among Sonóitac, San Felipe, the presidio) or (2) found a new presidio near the river mouth on the Antigua-California side, 20–25 leagues from Santa Catalina (preferred); closes urging officers experienced in handling Indians
