# ⭐⭐⭐ *Avistamiento de 16 Buques* — the express by *cordillera* with news of 16 ships sighted off the coast of Mission San

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n133** · Sav p. 495–496 · 1797-11-09 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐⭐ *Avistamiento de 16 Buques* — the express by *cordillera* with news of 16 ships sighted off the coast of Mission San Miguel reached him 11 PM on the 2nd; by 2 AM he'd dictated providences to the Southern Sgt.; couldn't relay the original *cordillera* up the opposite coast (the "Guairo" stove-in, the "Loretana" kept out of port by NW winds), so sped it to Real de los Álamos to dispatch an extraordinary to S.E. via Rosario & Guadalajara for advance notice along the NE coast
