# ⭐ *Rencilla con un Cabo* — the friar's account: tried to engage the cabo's son to mind the cattle for 4 pesos/month; son

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n37** · Sav p. 69–71 · 1797-10-13 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *Rencilla con un Cabo* — the friar's account: tried to engage the cabo's son to mind the cattle for 4 pesos/month; son begged off (couldn't count), then friar asked him to make plows, refused; cabo angrily barred the friar — *and any Indian* — from his house; cabo gave only one soldier (not the three the friar wanted given gentile danger) to retrieve missing mules & cows; asks the cabo be replaced
