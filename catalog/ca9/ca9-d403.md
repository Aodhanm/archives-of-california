⭐ *Establecimiento de un telar de frazadas / Gastos de cierto Pleito* (2 ofs) — ordered not to request blankets from México since a blanket loom (telar de frazadas) has been set up at this presidio; will charge the *fondo de Gratificación* the costs of the Cárcaba lawsuit to recover what was advanced to Soler, pending resolution of repayment
