# ⭐⭐ *Sobre moneda falsa* — received Branciforte's order to investigate circulation of counterfeit pesos fuertes; found 13

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n247** · Sav p. 935–936 · 1797-02-04 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ *Sobre moneda falsa* — received Branciforte's order to investigate circulation of counterfeit pesos fuertes; found 131 p whose double "F" marks mean *fábrica falsa* & 6 more with reversed F & M of the worst quality; says ~5 million of this coin was struck at Puebla, & that when the maker was found the King spared his life but exiled him to a presidio for life for having stamped the truth of its falseness on the coins
