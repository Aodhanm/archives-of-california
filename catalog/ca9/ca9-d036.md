# ⭐⭐ *Recibo* — inventory of effects of the paquebot *San Carlos*, wrecked (*naufragados*) in the port of San Francisco, l

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n35–n36** · Sav p. 59–63 · 1797-08-30 *(copy)* · verbatim transcription · 

⭐⭐ *Recibo* — inventory of effects of the paquebot *San Carlos*, wrecked (*naufragados*) in the port of San Francisco, left in deposit, delivered by the ship's *contador* to Alf. de fragata Gonzalo López de Haro. Carrillo notes the heavy timber & plank lie outside, the iron-work (*pernería/clavazón*) is badly rusted by saltwater — *"no soy responsable de mermas"*; signed for the sick/absent contador José de Tobar
