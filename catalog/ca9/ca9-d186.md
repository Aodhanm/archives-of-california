**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n115–n116** · Sav p. 336–339 · 1797-05-22 · Savage abstract · 

*Sobre embarque de dos Religiosos* — on the Viceroy's 17 Feb order: Fr. Andrés Caballero has a habitual illness yet keeps advancing his mission (Naturales' instruction & temporal goods), and his brother Fr. Rafael Caballero (delicate, lively temper) is occasionally ailing; both have worked as hard as any; leaves their embarkation to the gov.
