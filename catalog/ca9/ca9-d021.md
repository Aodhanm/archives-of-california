# ⭐ *Inconvenientes para una fábrica de loza* — finished his pottery workshop (*obrador de alfarería*) at SF but can't pro

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n26** · Sav p. 42–43 · 1796-06-30 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *Inconvenientes para una fábrica de loza* — finished his pottery workshop (*obrador de alfarería*) at SF but can't produce fine glazed ware: the wind never stops, lacks very-fine white sand, and the *Reverendos Padres* haven't supplied all he needs; meanwhile makes ordinary ware and will train the apprentices as ordered. *(Artisan-instruction program in action — cf. the 1795 artisan accounts, Docs 4–6)*
