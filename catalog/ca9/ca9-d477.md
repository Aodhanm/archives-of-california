# ⭐⭐ *Prevenciones contra hostilidades* — explains to the 1st artillery corporal the standing orders for the Punta de Guij

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n270–n271** · Sav p. 1039–1042 · 1797-05-11 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ *Prevenciones contra hostilidades* — explains to the 1st artillery corporal the standing orders for the Punta de Guijarros battery: the corporal & 4 of his piquete (5th as cook) alternate the guard with the Volunteers every 24 h, keeping a live sentinel at the battery day & night watching the harbour-mouth (bocana del Estrecho); on sighting a sail, leather-jacket soldier rides with the report; if a ship nears at dawn without answering the signal, raise the flag & fire a cannon-shot; weekly drills; had 4 leather cases made to keep cannon-cartridges dry in the battery
