# ⭐⭐ *Sobre creación de una Escolta* — establishes an escolta at Mission San José del Cabo (4 men + cabo + 2 vecinos) for 

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n113–n114** · Sav p. 330–332 · 1797-05-24 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ *Sobre creación de una Escolta* — establishes an escolta at Mission San José del Cabo (4 men + cabo + 2 vecinos) for night-and-day coastal watch against enemies (the cape near San Lucas, 30 leagues from the Sgt. at Real de Santa Ana): to drive off livestock from raiders & keep anyone from approaching the coast to be seized; only 9 shotguns for 120+ able men in the South, so sent 40 shotguns + 2,000 cartridges; vecinos rotate guard every 15 days, fed cimarrón-cattle meat
