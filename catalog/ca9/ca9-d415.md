# *Pesquisa de malhechores* — set out on the 8th in search of the malefactors per the governor's order & could not find th

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n239–n240** · Sav p. 904–905 · 1797-01-14 · Savage abstract · 

*Pesquisa de malhechores* — set out on the 8th in search of the malefactors per the governor's order & could not find them; at a ranchería an old India told him the Gentiles of those parts had gathered near the headwaters (nacimiento) as if heading for the Tulares; he turned back for lack of provisions & intends to return on the 19th to quiet that gathering of Gentiles
