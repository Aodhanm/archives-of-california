# ⭐⭐ *Sobre cierta Representación* — reports on the settlers' petition to move the pueblo of San José out of flood-reach: 

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n24–n26** · Sav p. 38–39 · 1797-11-07 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ *Sobre cierta Representación* — reports on the settlers' petition to move the pueblo of San José out of flood-reach: found higher ground ¼-league off and told the *vecindario* to stake it, but they replied they'd only asked to place some little houses for refuge when floods threatened, not a total relocation; sees no problem with the water being a little farther. [cf. Doc 20, the underlying petition]
