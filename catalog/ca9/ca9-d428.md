# *Sobre cuentas de mulada, manteca y cueros / eliminación de fracciones de grano* — in reply to B.'s office of the 4th: t

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n245–n246** · Sav p. 931–933 · 1797-02-10 · Savage abstract · 

*Sobre cuentas de mulada, manteca y cueros / eliminación de fracciones de grano* — in reply to B.'s office of the 4th: this year all R. Hac. mule-stock (mulada) of *herradero* age & up inventoried on a separate account; individual accounts of lard & tallow; same for usable hides of dead cattle (those slaughtered for the troop uncollected for years); will pay the tithe (diezmo) to the Guadalajara cajas — 2 r per female slaughtered + 25-per-thousand on sheep; sworn inventory of all R. Hac. stock; will drop grain fractions from accounts
