# "Satisfacción" — point-by-point reply to the audit objections (*reparos*) against the liquidated judgment of the Loreto 

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n10–n19** · Sav p. 11–21 · (1797 audit; re yrs 1787–92) · Savage abstract · 

"Satisfacción" — point-by-point reply to the audit objections (*reparos*) against the liquidated judgment of the Loreto presidio Real Hacienda accounts 1787–92 (objections numbered through ~18, then a 7-point set): *plomo de tiradura*, weights, the habilitado being "recién venido," etc. ⭐ Objection 7 concerns the San Blas Comisaría's 1792–93 supplies to the ships *San Carlos*, *Concepción* & the goleta *Saturnina* (the Bodega/Vancouver-era vessels) — still unsettled. Ends "Omito el responder"
