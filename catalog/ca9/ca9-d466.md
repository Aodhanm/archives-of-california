# ⭐ *Sobre Indios tejedores* — gathered discreetly from master-weaver Mariano Mendoza that he made a plain loom & combs fo

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n263–n264** · Sav p. 1006–1008 · 1797-04-01 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *Sobre Indios tejedores* — gathered discreetly from master-weaver Mariano Mendoza that he made a plain loom & combs for wide weaving & wove blankets, half-size *cameras*, vaquero sleeves, 30 varas of fine wide cloth & 30 varas of baize in 4 days with 5–6 Indians; the Indians are apt & weave alone shepherd's blankets, *cordoncillo* & *jerga* of better quality than before; the Indian women spin & card wool & cotton well & make dyes (imperfect for want of materials but serviceable)
