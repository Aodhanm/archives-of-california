# ⭐ *Sobre un nombramiento* — notifies that Felipe de Goycoechea, governor-elect of Antigua (Baja) California, is to be sw

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n81** · Sav p. 220–222 · 1805-08-16 *(in 1797 vol.)* · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *Sobre un nombramiento* — notifies that Felipe de Goycoechea, governor-elect of Antigua (Baja) California, is to be sworn & given possession in the usual form, his salary paid, on condition he posts bond to present his *título de Gobernador Político* within the ordinary overseas term, else lose the salary & repay it *(anomalous 1805 insertion — Goycoechea's Baja governorship)*
