# ⭐⭐ *Sobre su trato á Indios y sobre evitar tratos ilícitos* — answering reflections on the death in a waterhole of the I

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n173** · Sav p. 641–642 · 1797-07-17 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐ *Sobre su trato á Indios y sobre evitar tratos ilícitos* — answering reflections on the death in a waterhole of the Indian Juan Nepomuceno: he treats Indians with piety but corrects them — moderate work, sufficient food, regular rest, care when sick; keeps the local natives from mixing much with the mission Indians to avoid the *ilícitos comercios* with the women *(a presidio counterpart to the SF Indian-treatment debate)*
