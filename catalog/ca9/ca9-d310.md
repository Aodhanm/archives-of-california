# ⭐ *(two oficios) Media firma / Vinos* — (a) Juan Manuel Álvarez may sign with surname only on non-royal papers; (b) ⭐ wi

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n181** · Sav p. 669–671 · 1797-12-15 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *(two oficios) Media firma / Vinos* — (a) Juan Manuel Álvarez may sign with surname only on non-royal papers; (b) ⭐ will permit the free wholesale & retail introduction & sale of California-made wines & grape-brandy (*vinos y aguardiente de uva*) at agreed prices, merchants barred from suing troops/Loreto sailors/artisans/pobladores for supplies (who'd lose pawned uniform/arms/saddle)
