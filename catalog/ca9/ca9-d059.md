**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n48–n49** · Sav p. 105 · 1797-09-27 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *Sobre una acusación* — none of the escort will lift a hand for mission tasks, even common ones "the poorest Indian wouldn't refuse"; blames Salvador Higuera for turning them against helping the Padres; asks him removed at once; notes the cabo "enteramente mudado," now wanting pay for any task — attributes the change to Higuera (the cabo's *compadre*)
