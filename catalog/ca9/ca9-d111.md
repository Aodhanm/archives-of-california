# ⭐⭐⭐ *Ataque á una Ranchería* — orders Amador with 20 invalids/vecinos + 2 soldiers to dawn-raid (*albazo*) the Sacalan r

**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n71–n72** · Sav p. 202–203 · 1797-07-10 · Savage abstract · 

⭐⭐⭐ *Ataque á una Ranchería* — orders Amador with 20 invalids/vecinos + 2 soldiers to dawn-raid (*albazo*) the Sacalan ranchería, seize the ringleaders & those who took part in the killing of the seven Christians of Mission SF in 1795, take them to San José with any fugitive Christians among them — "se ha de evitar toda efusión de sangre," firearms/lance only if attacked and only as much as needed to contain them
