**C-A 9 (Prov. St. Pap. XVI, 1797), scan n265–n266** · Sav p. 1013–1020 · 1797-05-09 · Savage abstract · 

⭐ *Alta de un Alfz / descuento / retención / revista de armas / economía / abono al soldº carpintero / remisión de balas* (7 ofs) — enrolls Alférez José Luján from the 17th, with pay-deductions for the halberdier-vs-current-grade difference & to recover the 115 p advanced him; weekly inspection of arms & munitions; will economize stores if the '98 Memorias fail; pays carpenter Salvador Vejar 28 p/month to keep the launch & carts in repair (+2 apprentices); Santa Bárbara to send 6,000 balls for the Ripoll muskets & others for invalids & citizens
