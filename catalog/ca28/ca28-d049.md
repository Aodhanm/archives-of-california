# On the non-recognition of Zamorano's authority: forwards the Sta Bárbara commander's 28-Feb oficio & acta; reports the S

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n44–n45** · Sav p. 45–46 · tomo p. iii.132–133 · 1832-03-20 · Savage abstract · 

On the non-recognition of Zamorano's authority: forwards the Sta Bárbara commander's 28-Feb oficio & acta; reports the San Diego forces (7, 8, 13 March) resolved to declare Monterey's acts illegal & null, refuse to recognize Capt. Agustín V. Zamorano, and oblige Echeandía to keep the Comandancia General (vocales present: Capt. Argüello & José María de la Portilla). See Entry 3 [cited: Sav p.45; p.46 gap]
