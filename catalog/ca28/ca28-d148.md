# Reports an Indian conspiracy: 2 Christian mission Indians apprehended (from Rancho Tecate) declared they were sent by To

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n118–n119** · Sav p. 117–118 · tomo p. iii.348–351 · 1833-06-15 · Savage abstract · 

Reports an Indian conspiracy: 2 Christian mission Indians apprehended (from Rancho Tecate) declared they were sent by Tomás Tajochi, the alcalde Ramón de Santa Mónica & the Captain of San Isidro with a paper to rally the *gentiles* across San Diego / San Luis Rey / San Juan up to San Gabriel; the *gentiles* to gather 20 June at El Cajón; ⭐ cause = 'los indios… no quieren obedecer á otro que no sea Echeandía ó el Capitán Barroso' (Indian loyalty to Echeandía vs Figueroa). See Entry 6 ⚠ UNCITED (Sav pp.117-118)
