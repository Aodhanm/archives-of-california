# *Inconvenientes de la conversión de la Misión de San Gabriel en Pueblo* — warns converting San Gabriel to a pueblo would

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n1–n3** · Sav p. 2–4 · tomo p. iii.7–14 · 1831-01-21 · Savage abstract · 

*Inconvenientes de la conversión de la Misión de San Gabriel en Pueblo* — warns converting San Gabriel to a pueblo would cripple military supply: quantifies the mission's annual support to the presidio — 730 fanegas maize, 300 beans, 900 lbs lard, 750 pesos silver; ties it to the 6 May 1826 presidial-companies law; closes with a barb that Echeandía's last-minute dispositions left the SD garrison 'mendigando' (begging)
