# Forwards 3 original letters (from customs admin. Ángel Ramírez, Fr. Blas Ordaz, Antonio María Lugo) with *indicios* of t

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n153–n154** · Sav p. 152–153 · tomo p. iii.613–617 · 1834-05-26 · Savage abstract · 

Forwards 3 original letters (from customs admin. Ángel Ramírez, Fr. Blas Ordaz, Antonio María Lugo) with *indicios* of the conspiracy plotted at Mission San Gabriel by 'los encarnizados enemigos de nuestra patria': Fr. Narciso Durán, Fr. Tomás Estenaga, Capt. José de la Guerra & a sergeant; Gutiérrez as fiscal to arrest & prosecute to sentencing; the conspirators allegedly extracted money from Mission San Gabriel (recover it); and an unknown courier delivered sealed dispatches (pliegos) addressed to Capt. de la Guerra y Noriega at San Gabriel (witnessed by soldiers Romero & Ayala). See Entry 8 [cited: Sav pp.152-153]
