# Continued observations on the actas of 26–27 Jan: spells out the constitutional procedure for suspending a governor and 

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n31–n37** · Sav p. 32–38 · tomo p. iii.103–110 · 1832-02-01 · verbatim transcription · 

Continued observations on the actas of 26–27 Jan: spells out the constitutional procedure for suspending a governor and naming a substitute (per the 6 May 1822 decree) — the *expediente* signed before the assembled ayuntamiento/electores, suspension or absolution by majority vote, immediate naming of the substitute vocal, all communicated to the public; plus the new Gefe's salary/powers and municipal funding, and the balance of powers across the ayuntamientos (S. Francisco, Los Ángeles, Monterey, San José, Branciforte) [cited: Sav pp.32-38, block 'iii. 27-38']
