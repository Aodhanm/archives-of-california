# Reports ~40 foreigners (Anglo-Americans, English & French) calling themselves hunters at Suisun; asks orders to remove t

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n112** · Sav p. 112 · tomo p. iii.325 · 1833-04-07 · Savage abstract · 

Reports ~40 foreigners (Anglo-Americans, English & French) calling themselves hunters at Suisun; asks orders to remove them as harmful — they appropriate others' property, 'sugieren malas ideas á los indios contra ellos (los blancos) y los impulsan á robar caballos y ganado, dándoles baratijas y trapos viejos en cambio de animales.' Extends Entry 5 ⚠ UNCITED (Sav p.112)
