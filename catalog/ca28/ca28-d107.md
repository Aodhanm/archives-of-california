# Praises Echeandía's subordinate conduct during 'la escisión' — the govt 'lejos de vituperar su conducta, reconocerá como

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n84–n85** · Sav p. 84–85 · tomo p. iii.253–254 · 1833-01-18 · verbatim transcription · 

Praises Echeandía's subordinate conduct during 'la escisión' — the govt 'lejos de vituperar su conducta, reconocerá como un buen servicio' his maintaining public tranquility; assures the Supreme Gov. 'atenderá á los reclamos de los Californios contra las arbitrariedades de Victoria'; pledges to 'vigilar la conducta de los enemigos de la independencia de México'; leaves Echeandía free to arrange his trip to México (borrador sin firma) [cited: Sav pp.84-85]
