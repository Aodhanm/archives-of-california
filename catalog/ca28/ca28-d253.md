# Reports his expedition vs the rebels: at dawn 21 Oct routed a party of 60 (4 killed); then, reinforced by travelers, met

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n178–n179** · Sav p. 177–178 · tomo p. iii.723–729 · 1834-10-23 · Savage abstract · 

Reports his expedition vs the rebels: at dawn 21 Oct routed a party of 60 (4 killed); then, reinforced by travelers, met ~200 well-armed Indians about to ambush who 'lo batieron enérgicamente hasta obligarlo á retroceder' (forced his retreat) — the largest Indian resistance in the volume.
