# Announces the imminent arrival of the new Gefe appointed by the Supreme Government to govern them; urges 'públicos regoc

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n63–n64** · Sav p. 64–65 · tomo p. iii.195–196 · 1832-07-28 · Savage abstract · 

Announces the imminent arrival of the new Gefe appointed by the Supreme Government to govern them; urges 'públicos regocijos,' good order, unity, submission to the laws, and obedience to the new chief; affirms Alta California 'parte integrante é inseparable' of the Mexican Republic [cited: Sav pp.64-65]
