# Acknowledges the 23-Oct report; formally attributes the gentile uprising to 'el 1er Teniente de la Armada Nacional D. Bu

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n184–n186** · Sav p. 183–185 · tomo p. iii.742–744 · 1834-10-31 · Savage abstract · 

Acknowledges the 23-Oct report; formally attributes the gentile uprising to 'el 1er Teniente de la Armada Nacional D. Buenaventura Araujo' (the Híjar–Padrés colony's officer); directs Gutiérrez to first offer the deceived Indians pardon & peace (force only if they resist), sends 15 men + munitions, and thanks Ramírez. See Entry 9 [cited: Sav pp.183-184]
