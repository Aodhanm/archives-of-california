# Digest of the year's garrison Plaza Orders, noting only the extraordinary: the San Felipe de Jesús feast (Feb, artillery

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n133–n135** · Sav p. 132–134 · tomo p. iii.459–476 · 1833 (Feb 1–Dec 31) · Savage abstract · 

Digest of the year's garrison Plaza Orders, noting only the extraordinary: the San Felipe de Jesús feast (Feb, artillery salvos), the Constitution-anniversary salute (Oct 3–4), and ⭐⭐ the anti-Cholera hygiene code for the barracks (Dec 19–20): cleanliness, no spirits/fruit sold nearby, food in clay vessels, a set diet (rice/noodles/beans/mutton/veal), warm bathing, latrines disinfected w/ lime or charcoal, quarters fumigated w/ vinegar-and-salt braziers, and (13th) spirits & 'la indulgencia en los placeres del amor' declared harmful [Sav p.132 cited; pp.133-134 gap]
