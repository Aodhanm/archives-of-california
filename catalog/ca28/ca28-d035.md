# Warm personal letter ('Muy apreciable amigo') on the amnesty decreed 25 April [1833]: the Supreme Gov. will 'cubrir con 

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n23–n26** · Sav p. 24–27 · tomo p. iii.91–92 · 1833-01-14 (borrador; date resolved via its fair copy Doc 101) · verbatim transcription · 

Warm personal letter ('Muy apreciable amigo') on the amnesty decreed 25 April [1833]: the Supreme Gov. will 'cubrir con un velo cuanto ha pasado en este Territorio' and forget the *emigraciones* Echeandía was caught in; the amnesty law aimed 'restablecer el orden constitucional sin sacrificar á los hombres'; Figueroa 'resuelto á no… perjudicar á persona alguna,' asking Echeandía to help pacify — the incoming governor's reconciliation policy after the 1831–32 upheavals [cited: Sav p.23–26, HoC v3 'iii. 23-6']. ⚠ n25 leaf dark at scale=2 — verify the closing lines
