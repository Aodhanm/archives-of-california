# Victoria's deportation aboard the American frigate *Pocahontas* back to Mexico; the Diputación Territorial opened its se

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n21–n22** · Sav p. 22–23 · tomo p. iii.88–89 · 1832-01-17 · verbatim transcription · 

Victoria's deportation aboard the American frigate *Pocahontas* back to Mexico; the Diputación Territorial opened its sessions 10 Jan 1832 at Los Ángeles, tasked with (1) the *expediente* for the case against Victoria and (2) substituting the Gefatura Política per the 6-May-1822 decree; congratulates the Diputación on its reinstallation. See Entry 2 [cited: Sav p.22–23, HoC v3]
