# As government commissioner to settle disputes between the neophytes & neighboring Indians at Mission San Rafael (caused 

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n138** · Sav p. 137 · tomo p. iii.532–533 · 1833-12-22 · Savage abstract · 

As government commissioner to settle disputes between the neophytes & neighboring Indians at Mission San Rafael (caused by the padre's predecessor), received complaints against Mayordomo José Molina and judges his expulsion from the mission necessary — Vallejo acting as the state's agent over mission affairs (prefigures secularization) [cited: Sav p.137]
