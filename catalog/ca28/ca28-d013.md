# *Ocurrencias políticas en Los Ángeles* — on hearing of the 'novedad,' he set out to prevent violence/conspiracy, but the

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n9–n10** · Sav p. 10–11 · tomo p. iii.35–36 · 1831-04-22 · verbatim transcription · 

*Ocurrencias políticas en Los Ángeles* — on hearing of the 'novedad,' he set out to prevent violence/conspiracy, but the pobladores' letters reported it was over: they had suspended the Alcalde from authority and installed the first Regidor interim, pending the governor's decision (no violence done to the alcalde). An early municipal flare of anti-Victoria unrest
