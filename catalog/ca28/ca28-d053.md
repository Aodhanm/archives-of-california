# Transcribes a War-Ministry note (via the Vice-President) putting at Figueroa's disposal (as Comandante General de Califo

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n47** · Sav p. 48 · tomo p. iii.142–144 · 1832-05-10 · Savage abstract · 

Transcribes a War-Ministry note (via the Vice-President) putting at Figueroa's disposal (as Comandante General de Californias) the troops who volunteer for California, offering them preference in the distribution of land ('serán preferidos en el reparto de tierras') [cited: Sav p.48]
