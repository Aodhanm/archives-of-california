# Complains his ship was fired upon by the San Diego fort by order of Cmdte. Rodríguez (22 March 1834) after arriving 16 M

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n206–n208** · Sav p. 205–207 · tomo p. iii.788–790 · 1834-04 · Savage abstract · 

Complains his ship was fired upon by the San Diego fort by order of Cmdte. Rodríguez (22 March 1834) after arriving 16 March for provisions & repairs, over a soldiers/contraband dispute: the fort's battery fired until the ship took considerable hull damage; the captain returned fire, then at 9:30 AM put the soldiers ashore and fled to San Quintín Bay (Baja Cal.) to repair; Rodríguez accuses him of disrespecting the flag. Savage: '(Traducción)' from English — an armed US-ship/San Diego-fort incident [cited: Sav pp.205-207]
