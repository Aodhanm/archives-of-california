# Recommends the Gefe Político suspend the Monterey & San José police bandos, keeping in force only articles emanating fro

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n167–n168** · Sav p. 166–167 · tomo p. iii.658–661 · 1834-06-19 · Savage abstract · 

Recommends the Gefe Político suspend the Monterey & San José police bandos, keeping in force only articles emanating from gubernatorial authority, and refer to the Cámaras de la Unión (Congress) to define the ayuntamientos'/alcaldes' administrative competence & jurisdiction [cited: Sav pp.166-167]
