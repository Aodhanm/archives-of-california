# Protests the illegality of Pico's election to substitute him in the political command; declares himself forced ('incurso

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n42–n43** · Sav p. 43–44 · tomo p. iii.126–127 · 1832-02-16 · verbatim transcription · 

Protests the illegality of Pico's election to substitute him in the political command; declares himself forced ('incurso por fuerza') to relinquish the supreme political command, holding Pico and the Diputación 'responsables á la Nación y al Territorio por los trastornos que á este sobrevengan á causa de los abusos cometidos en el ejercicio de sus atribuciones.' See Entry 3 [partially cited: Sav p.44 in set; p.43 gap]
