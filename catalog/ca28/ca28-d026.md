# Private letter ('Mi estimado Sánchez') on the forgery accusation against Carrillo: asks Sánchez not to sentence the alle

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n16** · Sav p. 17 · tomo p. iii.67–68 · 1826-06-07 · verbatim transcription · 

Private letter ('Mi estimado Sánchez') on the forgery accusation against Carrillo: asks Sánchez not to sentence the allegedly-forged letter until Echeandía 'tome yo conocimiento de todo'; and directs that Carrillo & 'todos los demás de su clase' not be named to the mission escorts (harm they cause), naming instead men 'que no tengan intereses conocidos.' ⚠ OUT OF ORDER (1826) [cited: Sav p.17, HoC v2 'iii. 17']
