# Informs the mission prelates of his appointment; asks them & their fellow religiosos to use their 'influjo respetable' o

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n86** · Sav p. 86 · tomo p. iii.258–259 · 1833-01-19 · Savage abstract · 

Informs the mission prelates of his appointment; asks them & their fellow religiosos to use their 'influjo respetable' over the inhabitants for order & obedience to the legitimate government; encloses the Amnesty Laws and asks the amnesty + proclamation be read in the churches on a feast day after Mass (enlists the friars pragmatically — cf. Doc 95's anti-Spanish tension) ⚠ UNCITED (Sav p.86)
