# Monterey municipal police / daily-life ordinance (14 arts.): round up wandering pigs; sweep house-fronts twice weekly; r

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n160–n162** · Sav p. 159–161 · tomo p. iii.641–645 · 1833-01-06 · Savage abstract · 

Monterey municipal police / daily-life ordinance (14 arts.): round up wandering pigs; sweep house-fronts twice weekly; relocate adobe-making; no fires in wooded areas (forest-fire prevention); fandangos need an Alcalde/Regidor license; 10 PM curfew (suspicious persons arrested); cattle-provenance checks before the Síndico; timber-cutting licenses (esp. wood sold to ships; free for residents); foreigners must show/obtain passports entering & leaving; no firing guns in town; no market-selling on holy days until after Mass; no public card games/drunkenness; ⭐ anyone employing a mission neophyte must notify the local authority (ex-mission Indian-labor tracking); weapons-law enforcement [cited: Sav pp.159-161]
