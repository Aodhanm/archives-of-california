# Reports the capture of Tomás Tajochi: the expedition returned with him prisoner; he confessed to authoring the uprising;

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n124** · Sav p. 123 · tomo p. iii.373–378 · 1833-07-01 · Savage abstract · 

Reports the capture of Tomás Tajochi: the expedition returned with him prisoner; he confessed to authoring the uprising; the Santa Ysabel Indian alcaldes themselves delivered him (with 4 accomplices) to Capt. Portilla, who remitted him for trial — all now pacified. Closes Entry 6 ⚠ UNCITED (Sav p.123)
