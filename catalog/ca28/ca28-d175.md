# Requests permission to found, with Rev. Fr. Patrick Short, a casa de educación (school) for youths ≥8 yrs — curriculum: 

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n132–n133** · Sav p. 131–132 · tomo p. iii.434–451 · 1833-11-18 · Savage abstract · 

Requests permission to found, with Rev. Fr. Patrick Short, a casa de educación (school) for youths ≥8 yrs — curriculum: Spanish / English / French / German / Latin grammar, Rhetoric, Syntax, Mathematics, Philosophy & Christian Doctrine, $200/yr; asks for garden land. Figueroa (per Gómez's opinion) rules no special permit needed — the founding of Hartnell's Colegio, California's first secondary school [cited: Sav pp.131-132]
