# Sent 4 trusted neophytes to scout; they found the Gentiles had withdrawn to their Sierra rancherías near San Gorgonio (3

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n191** · Sav p. 190 · tomo p. iii.758–761 · 1834-10-29 · Savage abstract · 

Sent 4 trusted neophytes to scout; they found the Gentiles had withdrawn to their Sierra rancherías near San Gorgonio (32 leagues out); requests instructions to attack them in their strongholds. Also: the gentiles robbed the sacred vessels/ornaments from Mission San Gabriel's San Bernardino chapel and the seed-stores meant to ration the neophytes. See Entry 9 ⚠ UNCITED (Sav p.190)
