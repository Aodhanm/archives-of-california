# Reports his fiscal reforms: (1) collect ship duties at ports lacking customs offices (posting a coastal guard); (2) coll

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n123** · Sav p. 122 · tomo p. iii.371–372 · 1833-06-24 · Savage abstract · 

Reports his fiscal reforms: (1) collect ship duties at ports lacking customs offices (posting a coastal guard); (2) collect the Alcabala — 'nunca se ha percibido antes de su llegada á California'; (3) collect Diezmos (tithes), never fully collected, previously handled by the presidial Habilitados; subject to Supreme-Gov. approval (copia) ⚠ UNCITED (Sav p.122)
