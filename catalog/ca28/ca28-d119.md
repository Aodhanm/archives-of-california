# Grants Teodoro González a license for otter hunting (pesca de nutrias) from Monterey to Santa Bárbara under the Decreto 

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n93–n94** · Sav p. 93–94 · tomo p. iii.282–283 · 1833-02-16 · Savage abstract · 

Grants Teodoro González a license for otter hunting (pesca de nutrias) from Monterey to Santa Bárbara under the Decreto de 16 Abril 1811 (deemed still in force in the Republic), with a prohibition on killing the pups 'para conservar la especie' (early conservation) and requiring the license be shown to the R.P. Ministros of the missions along the coast (borrador) ⚠ UNCITED (Sav p.94, gap) → otter flag
