# Public anti-cholera bando to all Californians: general cleanliness; Dr. Manuel Alba's printed curative; avoid meat broth

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n135–n138** · Sav p. 134–137 · tomo p. iii.~531 · 1833-12-19 · Savage abstract · 

Public anti-cholera bando to all Californians: general cleanliness; Dr. Manuel Alba's printed curative; avoid meat broths/fatty meats/coarse vegetables; build Campos-Santos (cemeteries) where none exist, graves 2½ varas deep; no burial before 24 hrs (against burying the apparently-dead); no funeral bell-tolling; 8 PM curfew (violators → 4 days public labor); liquor sales only 11 AM–3 PM (6-peso fine); the sick to the capital's sole physician/hospital; padres & military commanders to enforce where no ayuntamiento; informers get ⅓ of fines; fines fund epidemic costs. See Entry 7 [Sav p.137 cited; pp.134-136 mostly gap]
