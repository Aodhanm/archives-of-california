# Transmits 38 copies (assembled by Zamorano) documenting the whole revolution against Victoria, 'desde el 29 de Noviembre

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n105–n109** · Sav p. 105–109 · tomo p. iii.310–311, 314–315 (bdr) · 1833-03-23 · verbatim transcription · 

Transmits 38 copies (assembled by Zamorano) documenting the whole revolution against Victoria, 'desde el 29 de Noviembre de 1831 hasta el 14 de Enero' 1833; narrates the revolt, Zamorano's defense of the capital, and the armistice/convenio (Zamorano Los Ángeles→San Francisco, Echeandía →San Diego); recommends Zamorano, Lt. [Juan Mª] Ibarra & Alférez Ignacio Carrillo; ⭐ notes 'varios extranjeros radicados en la capital' formed an armed Company (acta of 24 Jan 1832, copy Nº 10) to sustain the Government — the Monterey foreign company; ⭐ confirms the SD pronunciamiento = 29 Nov 1831 (cf. Doc 95; corrects Doc 38's '29 Dic') (borrador) [Sav pp.105-108 gap; p.109 cited]
