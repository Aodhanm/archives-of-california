# Forwards for publication the Diputación's resolution that NEITHER the political command NOR the mission property be hand

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n178** · Sav p. 177 · tomo p. iii.720–722 · 1834-10-22 · Savage abstract · 

Forwards for publication the Diputación's resolution that NEITHER the political command NOR the mission property be handed to Híjar — the command because 'así lo ordena el Supremo Gobierno' (the countermand of Híjar's Gefe-Político appointment), the mission property because it is 'de la propiedad de los indios'; orders precautions against disturbance & to aid Gutiérrez. The crux of the Híjar–Padrés crisis. See Entry 9 ⚠ UNCITED (Sav p.177)
