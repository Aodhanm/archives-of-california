**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n27–n31** · Sav p. 28–32 · tomo p. iii.98–102 · 1832-01-31 · verbatim transcription · 

Point-by-point commentary on the Diputación's January actas — the suspension of Gov. Victoria per 'el Plan pronunciado el 29 de Noviembre' [1831] (✅ corrected — Docs 95 & 134 confirm 29 Nov; the earlier '29 Dic' was a misreading), forming the *expediente* against Victoria, and naming the new Gefe Político 'con arreglo á la Ley de 6 de Mayo de 1822.' Extends the Victoria-suspension mechanics [cited: Sav pp.28-32, block 'iii. 27-38']
