# Reports a rumor that the gentile Indians threatened to attack the pueblo of Los Ángeles from the Río Colorado and other 

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n206** · Sav p. 205 · tomo p. iii.785–787 · 1834-12-16 · Savage abstract · 

Reports a rumor that the gentile Indians threatened to attack the pueblo of Los Ángeles from the Río Colorado and other points (a Gentile Captain warned Serrano to leave Rancho del Temescal); sent commissioners to investigate — the late-1834 Indian threat continues (Entry 9) [cited: Sav p.205]
