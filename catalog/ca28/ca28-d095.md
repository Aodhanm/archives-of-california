# Political letter justifying his conduct: love of 'mi patria' and its republican institutions committed him to 'el pronun

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n75** · Sav p. 75 · tomo p. iii.227–228 · 1832-10-17 · verbatim transcription · 

Political letter justifying his conduct: love of 'mi patria' and its republican institutions committed him to 'el pronunciamiento del 29 de Noviembre' at San Diego; the Territory stays 'unido á la Nación… á pesar del poderoso influjo que en él tienen las máximas españolas de los misioneros, que excepto uno ú otro, todos son apologistas y súbditos de tal gobierno' — the anti-Spanish-missionary rationale for secularization ⚠ UNCITED gem (Sav p.75) ⚠ dates the SD pronunciamiento to 29 Nov [1831] — re-verify Doc 38's '29 Dic' reading
