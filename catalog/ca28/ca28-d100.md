# On the general amnesty: transcribes the supreme order (via the Secr. de Guerra y Marina, 6 June) and encloses 3 copies o

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n77–n79** · Sav p. 77–79 · tomo p. iii.236–240 · 1833-01-14 · verbatim transcription · 

On the general amnesty: transcribes the supreme order (via the Secr. de Guerra y Marina, 6 June) and encloses 3 copies of the Ley de 25 Abril 1832 (art. 5) amnestying all who took part in the 1831 movements against 'el Señor mi antecesor' (Victoria); asks Echeandía's cooperation to restore peace, unite the militias under Figueroa, and 'desterrar para siempre de este suelo el germen de discordia' [Sav pp.77-78 gap; p.79 cited]
