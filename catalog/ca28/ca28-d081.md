# Proposes a Colorado-River periodic mail (15-man parties from the Presidio del Norte & San Diego) and colonizing the Colo

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n62–n63** · Sav p. 63–64 · tomo p. iii.189–194 (bdr) · 1832-11-12 · Savage abstract · 

Proposes a Colorado-River periodic mail (15-man parties from the Presidio del Norte & San Diego) and colonizing the Colorado's banks — both to 'poner un dique' to the foreigners who 'impunemente extraen los castores de aquellos ríos' (American beaver trappers) and to open 'un nuevo teatro de comercio y de agricultura' for Sonorans; promises to distribute lands to colonists (borrador) [cited: Sav pp.63-64] → otter/beaver + Open-Door flag
