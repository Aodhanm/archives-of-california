# Transcribes Capt. Portilla's 9-Feb warning of an alleged Indian mobilization: the neophytes of Missions San Diego (rebel

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n90** · Sav p. 90 · tomo p. iii.273–275 · 1833-02-10 · Savage abstract · 

Transcribes Capt. Portilla's 9-Feb warning of an alleged Indian mobilization: the neophytes of Missions San Diego (rebellious), San Luis Rey, San Juan Capistrano & San Gabriel planned to gather to demand distribution of their lands & goods, proclaim Echeandía as Comandante General, and reject Figueroa — though Valle reports San Gabriel calm and downplays an 'imagined' sublevación. ⭐ Indian political agency + secularization/land + the succession [cited: Sav p.90]
