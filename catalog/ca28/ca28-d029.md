# Authorizes a copy of Manuel Gutiérrez's 26-Sep-1830 certification that José Antonio Carrillo is 'indigno' to be Elector 

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n18** · Sav p. 19 · tomo p. iii.78–80 · 1831-08-24 · Savage abstract · 

Authorizes a copy of Manuel Gutiérrez's 26-Sep-1830 certification that José Antonio Carrillo is 'indigno' to be Elector de Partido, citing commercial transactions in which Carrillo 'abusó… de la buena fe de Sánchez' (the feud's crux — disqualifying Carrillo from electoral office). ⚠ likely UNCITED (Sav p.19 sits in a gap in Bancroft's DSP-iii cited pages; ⚠ do NOT confuse with Bancroft's 'iii. 79-82' = Savage pp.79-82, a different leaf)
