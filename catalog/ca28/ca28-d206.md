**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n149–n150** · Sav p. 148–149 · tomo p. iii.597–599 · 1834-05-23 · Savage abstract · 

On public schools: only 3 boys' public schools in the Territory (Monterey, Santa Bárbara, Los Ángeles), 'mal dotadas y surtidas' (Monterey & LA paid from municipal funds, Sta Bárbara by subscription + the local Company); ⭐ no girls' school exists and educating 'al bello sexo' was never considered [Sav p.148 gap; p.149 cited]
