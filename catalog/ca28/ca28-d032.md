# THE ANTI-VICTORIA REVOLT — on the pronunciamiento of the Southern Department against Victoria, the clash with Victoria's

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n20–n21** · Sav p. 21–22 · tomo p. iii.86–87 · 1831-12-09 · verbatim transcription · 

THE ANTI-VICTORIA REVOLT — on the pronunciamiento of the Southern Department against Victoria, the clash with Victoria's troops, their defeat and Victoria left 'en estado de muerte' (wounded at Cahuenga, 5 Dec 1831), and the call to the Northern Dept's ayuntamientos/presidio commanders to interim-appoint a Gefe Político and a Comandante General per the 'plan del pronunciamiento'; urges Vallejo to Los Ángeles for the installation. See Entry 2 [cited: Sav p.21–22, HoC v3]
