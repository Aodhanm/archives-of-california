# Announces the convenio ending the Echeandía–Zamorano conflict, partitioning Alta California to avoid 'efusión de sangre'

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n47–n48** · Sav p. 48–49 · tomo p. iii.145–146 · 1832-05-22 · verbatim transcription · 

Announces the convenio ending the Echeandía–Zamorano conflict, partitioning Alta California to avoid 'efusión de sangre': (1) until the Supreme Government's appointed chief arrives, jurisdiction divided — from San Gabriel southward under Echeandía, from San Fernando northward under Zamorano; (2) mutual military aid if public peace is disturbed; (3) the ordinary mail leaves Monterey the 7th of each month for San Diego and San Diego the 22nd; accessory conditions pending. See Entry 3 [cited: Sav pp.48-49]
