# Council proceedings: approves & amends the police bando (Doc 227) article-by-article + adds arts. 15–18 (fines for tresp

**C-A 28 (Departmental State Papers), scan n162–n164** · Sav p. 161–163 · tomo p. iii.646–651 · 1833-01-11 · Savage abstract · 

Council proceedings: approves & amends the police bando (Doc 227) article-by-article + adds arts. 15–18 (fines for trespassing on ranchos, polluting water fountains, damaging bridges/roads/public works; the Alcalde arrests non-payers); orders a calzada between the wharf & the Presidio; to hold extraordinary sessions naming auxiliary alcaldes, justice comisarios & jueces de campo [cited: Sav pp.161-163]
