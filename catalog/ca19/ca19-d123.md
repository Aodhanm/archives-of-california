# 'Queja por caballada embargada.' Gilbert's complaint over his embargoed horse-herd: a buyer ('Colt[?]') bought ~400 head

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n138–n139** · Sav p. 135–136 · tomo p. 305–331 · 1830-06-09 · verbatim transcription · 

'Queja por caballada embargada.' Gilbert's complaint over his embargoed horse-herd: a buyer ('Colt[?]') bought ~400 head, was intercepted near San Juan Bautista; Romero, Tomás Linares, Sergio Escamilla merged their horses in; Gilbert's money vanished; Alf. José Fernández del Campo seized men/horses; some claimed the stock was stolen; Soto et al. embargoed Gilbert's *marked* herd. Fernández del Campo accuses Gilbert of buying stolen horses & funding their removal by San José people; Gilbert demands proof. Fiscal Rodrigo del Pliego, escribano Zamorano; no conclusion. (Cf. Doc 39, the 1829 Gilbert horse-theft causa.) Orig. pp.308–331.
