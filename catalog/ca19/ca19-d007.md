# Criminal causa vs. a soldier: sumaria against soldier Francisco Verdugo for losing a dispatch (pliego) he was carrying f

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n5** · Sav p. 5 · tomo p. 165–180 · 1829-10-02 · verbatim transcription · 

Criminal causa vs. a soldier: sumaria against soldier Francisco Verdugo for losing a dispatch (pliego) he was carrying for the Comandante General (20 Sep 1829); convicted & confessed (lost it through carelessness); the fiscal asks 25 lashes (palos) in his quarters + one month's plantón at the Castillo de San Diego. (A note suggests he was freed after the prison term.) Orig. pp.165–180.
