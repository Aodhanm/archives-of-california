# Notes promotion inequities: Santiago Argüello (1st post 15 Oct 1805) & Zamorano (1 May 1821, ~16 yrs later) both made Ca

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n460** · Sav p. 455 · tomo p. 25 · 1834 · Savage abstract · 

Notes promotion inequities: Santiago Argüello (1st post 15 Oct 1805) & Zamorano (1 May 1821, ~16 yrs later) both made Captain the same day, 23 May 1831; Ignacio Argüello (2 Apr 1804) & Ramírez del Pliego (21 Dec 1824, ~19 yrs later) both made Lieutenant the same day, 23 May 1831 — 'estas diferencias en los favores de la fortuna.' Orig. p.25.
