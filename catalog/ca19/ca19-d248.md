# Causa against Francisco Sánchez (vagrancy + fomenting insubordination among the SF troops, Jan 1834; fiscal Dámaso Rodrí

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n442–n443** · Sav p. 437–438 · tomo p. 510–511 · 1834 · Savage abstract · 

Causa against Francisco Sánchez (vagrancy + fomenting insubordination among the SF troops, Jan 1834; fiscal Dámaso Rodríguez; the accused recused him). ⭐ Records Asesor Rafael Gómez's resignation mid-1834 — 'que no se le remitan más sumarios, procesos y expedientes, por no ser ya Asesor ni ejercer la abogacía' — the end of the moderating asesor's tenure across this volume's criminal docket. Orig. pp.510–11.
