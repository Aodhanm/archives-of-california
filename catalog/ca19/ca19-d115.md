# Criminal vs. the Santa Bárbara neophyte Telapie[?] for killing another SB-mission neophyte, Gonzalo. Fiscal Juan Salazar

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n134** · Sav p. 132 · tomo p. 205–277 · 1830-09-27 · verbatim transcription · 

Criminal vs. the Santa Bárbara neophyte Telapie[?] for killing another SB-mission neophyte, Gonzalo. Fiscal Juan Salazar sought the death penalty (ordenanzas art.61 tít.10 trat.8); Asesor Gómez found the case procedurally deficient (no facultativo's wound report, no body examination, faulty depositions) and recommended 10 years public works; Echeandía agreed (counted from imprisonment, sent to SD presidio). (Orig. pp.256–77 blank but foliated.) Orig. pp.205–277.
