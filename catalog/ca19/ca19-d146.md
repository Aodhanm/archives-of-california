# Report corroborating the imprisoned subalterns' statements (cabo Mariano Vaquero, artilleryman Antonio Guerra, paisanos 

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n155** · Sav p. 152 · tomo p. (process) · 1830-04-15 · verbatim transcription · 

Report corroborating the imprisoned subalterns' statements (cabo Mariano Vaquero, artilleryman Antonio Guerra, paisanos Sergio Escamilla & Melitón Soto): Escamilla went as commissioner to SB and made the garrison revolt; names M.l Herrera as author of the Plan (per Solís's own declaration); Herrera induced soldier Pablo Vejar to rebel.
