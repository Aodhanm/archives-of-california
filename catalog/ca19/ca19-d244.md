# Province-wide artillery census — bronze & iron cannon by caliber at all four presidios: Monterey (bronze 8s/6/4 + iron 8

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n390–n391** · Sav p. 385–386 · tomo p. 1–2 · 1833-06-07 · Savage abstract · 

Province-wide artillery census — bronze & iron cannon by caliber at all four presidios: Monterey (bronze 8s/6/4 + iron 8/6×5/4/3), San Francisco (bronze 18 ×5/8/2½ + iron 24 ×3/12/4/2½), Santa Bárbara (bronze 6/4×2/3/2 + iron 4×2/3/2), San Diego (bronze 6 ×7 + pedreros + iron 8×2/6×2), each with carriages/munitions 'en suficiente cantidad.' A rare 1833 total-province ordnance snapshot for the [[presidio-garrison-artillery-register]]. Orig. pp.1–19 (LXXVI).
