# Deportation roster of the Solís-revolt prisoners shipped on the American barque *Volunteer* to San Blas 'á la disposició

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n145–n146** · Sav p. 142–143 · tomo p. 679–680 · 1830-05-07 · verbatim transcription · 

Deportation roster of the Solís-revolt prisoners shipped on the American barque *Volunteer* to San Blas 'á la disposición del Supremo Gob.no' — 15 individuals: José Mª Herrera (sub-comisario provisional, lodged 'en un camarote de proa de la cámara y recluido á él'), Joaquín Solís (paisano — the revolt's leader), soldiers of the Monterey cav. Raimundo de la Torre, Pablo Vejar, Gonzalo & Victorino Altamirano, Leonardo Acedo; perm. artillery Máximo & Antonio Guerra, García Lara, cabos Mariano Vaquero & Andrés de León (shackled '2 barras con un anillo cada uno'); paisanos Melecio Soto, Sergio Escamilla; and presidiario Inés/José Polanco (10-yr sentence from 1827). Received by John Coffin Jones, master of the *Volunteer*. The core Solís-deportation record (cf. Docs 91, 131). Orig. pp.679–680.
