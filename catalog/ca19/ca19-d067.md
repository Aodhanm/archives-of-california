# Cattle-roundup & land/livestock census order for the Monterey jurisdiction: holders of land (by governors' concession) &

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n61–n62** · Sav p. 61–62 · tomo p. 161–163 · 1830-08-17 · Savage abstract · 

Cattle-roundup & land/livestock census order for the Monterey jurisdiction: holders of land (by governors' concession) & cattle to present at the rodeo from Mon 23 Aug for the Comisionado Cadete Raymundo Estrada + 2 associates to count & record (rancho name, owner, head of cattle/horses, brand & mark per the bando of 7 Oct 1827), forming a general report to the Secretaría. (Rancho/brand census — useful for californio rancho economy.) Orig. pp.161–163.
