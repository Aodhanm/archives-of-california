# Resolution of the bigamy case vs. Alférez José Mª Ramírez: accused of marrying Dolores Palomares (daughter of retired so

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n33–n34** · Sav p. 33–34 · tomo p. 23–254 · 1830-06-21 · verbatim transcription · 

Resolution of the bigamy case vs. Alférez José Mª Ramírez: accused of marrying Dolores Palomares (daughter of retired soldier José Palomares) at Los Ángeles while married in México to Dolores Olvera; separated from his wife 2 yrs 3 mo during the case. Per Ramírez's confession, Olvera had been only his concubine (family by her) not legal wife. Echeandía, finding bigamy not proven, orders him to rejoin his legal wife & notifies the ecclesiastical curia. Orig. pp.23–254.
