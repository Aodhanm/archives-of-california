# 'Sobre sus facultades' — replies to the Comandante Militar of SD that, regarding the demanded payments, he cannot delibe

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n69** · Sav p. 69 · tomo p. 260–261 · 1830-03-17 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre sus facultades' — replies to the Comandante Militar of SD that, regarding the demanded payments, he cannot deliberate without prior mandate of the Encargado of the territory's principal Comisaría, and will not make the payments until an order comes through the proper channel. (Bandini defining his fiscal/jurisdictional limits.) Orig. pp.260–261.
