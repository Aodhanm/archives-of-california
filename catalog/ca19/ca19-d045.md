# SF artillery & ordnance inventory, July 1829: bronze cannon various calibers 7 serviceable + 1 useless; iron cannon 5 se

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n43–n44** · Sav p. 43–44 · tomo p. 495–497 · 1829-07-31 · Savage abstract · 

SF artillery & ordnance inventory, July 1829: bronze cannon various calibers 7 serviceable + 1 useless; iron cannon 5 serviceable + 5 useless; plaza guns ~3 (2 serviceable); 4 marine carriages; loading tools (atacadores, cucharas, sacatrapos…); 1,225 round shot, 525 tin-canister grapeshot; powder, parque supplies. (Mexican-period SF battery state — for the artillery register.) Orig. pp.495–497.
