# Causa vs. soldier Domingo Olivas for perjury (false oath in a case against the neophyte José Fidel[?]). Fiscal: per ordi

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n33** · Sav p. 33 · tomo p. 1–22 · 1829-04-27 · verbatim transcription · 

Causa vs. soldier Domingo Olivas for perjury (false oath in a case against the neophyte José Fidel[?]). Fiscal: per ordinance he merits being shot, but given the trivial matter & his ignorance of what swearing means, recommends 4 months' hard labor with a shackle. Echeandía absolves him (time served); sentence executed 13 Jul. Orig. pp.1–22.
