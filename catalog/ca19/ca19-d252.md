# Deposition re the Spanish corsair *Griego* (bound out of order): the American James Smith, cook of the schooner *Emilia*

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n469** · Sav p. 463 · tomo p. 1+ · 1828-09-17 · verbatim transcription · 

Deposition re the Spanish corsair *Griego* (bound out of order): the American James Smith, cook of the schooner *Emilia* of the Sandwich Islands, had crewed the Spanish privateer *Griego* which in 1827 captured patriot (Mexican & South American) ships — a sugar brig sent to Manila, a schooner off Acapulco, a sloop off Mazatlán. Smith deposed he was forced aboard at Cádiz, June 1827; taken via interpreter by order of Cmdt. José Estrada. A post-independence privateering datapoint. Orig. pp.1+ (LXXVII, misbound).
