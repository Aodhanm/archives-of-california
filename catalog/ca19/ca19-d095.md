# Causa vs. two neophytes Pedro & Feliciano (sent from Tecate[?] by Cabo Juan Mª Marrón) for horse-theft and because they 

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n117–n118** · Sav p. 117–118 · tomo p. 647–654 · 1830-05-18 · verbatim transcription · 

Causa vs. two neophytes Pedro & Feliciano (sent from Tecate[?] by Cabo Juan Mª Marrón) for horse-theft and because they 'no querían sujetarse á los trabajos de comunidad de su misión' (refusing mission community labor). Argüello — here styling himself Cmdt. Militar of SD 'y encargado de los asuntos políticos de este Departamento' — finds them liable under the vagrancy decree of 8 Mar 1828 and the supreme decree of 7 Sep 1829 (art.1 §6) on ladrones cuatreros. Echeandía's sentence (apparently in his own hand): Pedro → 2 yrs public works at the SF presidio; Feliciano → 1 yr at Monterey. Orig. pp.647–654.
