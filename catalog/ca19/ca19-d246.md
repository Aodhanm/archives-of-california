# Monterey presidio monthly ration/supply requirement: troops by unit (artillery 23, 2º Batallón 35, cavalry 30, Mazatlán 

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n406** · Sav p. 401 · tomo p. 166 · 1833-11-16 · Savage abstract · 

Monterey presidio monthly ration/supply requirement: troops by unit (artillery 23, 2º Batallón 35, cavalry 30, Mazatlán 5, plus presidiarios, prisoners, 'neófitos amanuenses' 8) + foodstuffs (maíz 91½ fanegas, frijol, manteca, carne, cecina, jabón, zapatos, velas) — with a note there was not one blanket left in the storehouse (200 needed). A vivid presidio-poverty/logistics datapoint. Orig. p.166.
