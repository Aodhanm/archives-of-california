# Causa vs. soldier Isaac Salgado for slapping a soldier's wife (in the house where he was visiting, not in front of Capt.

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n34–n35** · Sav p. 34–35 · tomo p. 255–273 · 1829-04-27 · verbatim transcription · 

Causa vs. soldier Isaac Salgado for slapping a soldier's wife (in the house where he was visiting, not in front of Capt. Miguel González as charged); Tte. Ibarra told González the woman deserved it for receiving visits she shouldn't. Fiscal leaves the penalty to the Superioridad. Orig. pp.255–273.
