# Service record of Sargento Ignacio Vallejo (father of M. G. Vallejo): 18 yrs as soldier + 24 yrs as sergeant = 42½ yrs, 

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n56** · Sav p. 56 · tomo p. 755–756 · 1829-10-31 · Savage abstract · 

Service record of Sargento Ignacio Vallejo (father of M. G. Vallejo): 18 yrs as soldier + 24 yrs as sergeant = 42½ yrs, entering the service 8 June 1787; campaigns vs. Indians; commissioned to the pueblo & Villa de Branciforte; swore the Independence 11 Apr 1822. (Cross-links his enlistment in [[provincial-state-papers-benicia-military-ca15]] Doc 289 — which dated the entry 1 Jul 1787; minor date discrepancy.) Orig. pp.755–756.
