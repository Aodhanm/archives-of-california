# SD presidial company annual return 1830: Capt. José Mª Estudillo (reviewed the first 4 months 'aunque siempre ausente');

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n74–n75** · Sav p. 74–75 · tomo p. 310–395 · 1830-12-01 · Savage abstract · 

SD presidial company annual return 1830: Capt. José Mª Estudillo (reviewed the first 4 months 'aunque siempre ausente'); Tte. Santiago Argüello; Alf. Gervasio Argüello (absent, his son acting as habilitado); Alf. Domingo Carrillo (attached Jan); 3 sargtos, 5 cabos, 149 soldados; present strength Jan–Mar 58, Apr–Jun 54, rest of year 53; invalids under Capt. José Mª Ruiz + sargto Juan Mariner. Reviews passed before José Antonio Estudillo ('se titulaba Comisario Subalterno Provisional') then Juan Bandini (interino). Orig. pp.310–395.
