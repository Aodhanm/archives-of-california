# Echeandía's secularization bando (Jan 1831). Encloses the reglamento for the handover of the missions' temporalities to 

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n63–n64** · Sav p. 63–64 · tomo p. 165 · 1831-01-05 · Savage abstract · 

Echeandía's secularization bando (Jan 1831). Encloses the reglamento for the handover of the missions' temporalities to Administrators, per the 13 Sep 1813 [Cortes] law, to be circulated to the Padres Ministros; the diocesan to convert the missions into curatos/parishes with canonically-provided párrocos (or leave them to the Religiosos administering them); copies to the federal Supremo Gobierno. (Borrador.) The famous 6 Jan 1831 Echeandía secularization decree. Orig. p.165.
