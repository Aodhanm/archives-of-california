**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n325** · Sav p. 320 · tomo p. 128–129 · 1834-01-16 · Savage abstract · 

Causa: a representation by Capt. Miguel González against José Joaquín Maitorena — never prosecuted (no officers available under Victoria, 1831); the Méx. Superioridad referred it to Gov.
