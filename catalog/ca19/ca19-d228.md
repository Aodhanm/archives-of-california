# 'Robos y otros excesos de aventureros de N. Méx.' — a denuncia that New Mexico vecinos were driving many horses & mules 

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n425–n430** · Sav p. 420–425 · tomo p. 301, 485 · 1833-02-18 · Savage abstract · 

'Robos y otros excesos de aventureros de N. Méx.' — a denuncia that New Mexico vecinos were driving many horses & mules toward Sonora without the required registro; 5 LA citizens pursued them 20 leagues, seized their partida (~200 bestias, incl. ~100 of a mission padre's); the thieves imprisoned. Orig. pp.301, 485.
