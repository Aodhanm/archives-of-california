# Criminal causa — soldier sedition. Artillery soldier Manuel Rojas charged with having 'vociferado palabras indecorosas c

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n24** · Sav p. 24 · tomo p. 629–652 · 1829-04-23 · verbatim transcription · 

Criminal causa — soldier sedition. Artillery soldier Manuel Rojas charged with having 'vociferado palabras indecorosas contra los oficiales'; after Alférez José Sánchez took depositions, Echeandía ordered the sumaria passed to artillery Cmdt. Alférez Fernández del Campo to be judged per ordinance. (Result not found.) An early-1829 sign of the troop unrest that fed the Solís revolt. Orig. pp.629–652.
