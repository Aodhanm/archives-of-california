# ⚠️ The Domingo Olivas causa (desertion + first-cousin concubinage — NOT a straightforward homicide; corrected in the com

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n402–n405** · Sav p. 397–400 · tomo p. 135–163 · 1833 · verbatim transcription · 

⚠️ The Domingo Olivas causa (desertion + first-cousin concubinage — NOT a straightforward homicide; corrected in the completeness pass). Soldier Domingo Olivas of the San Gabriel escort deserted and had by force taken Luisa Pérez — his first cousin, with whom he had lived *amancebado* for years (3 living daughters); her brother José Pérez complained. After 7+ months' jail the LA alcalde absolved him (to rejoin his company). ⚠️ A separate/entangled dictamen (n405) states Olivas 'infirió á Luisa Pérez la muerte del 5 de Feb' and refers *that* matter to the Superior Consejo de la Guerra — but the two leaves don't fully reconcile (the abduction complaint treats her as alive) and Savage's record is incomplete ('Facultado no aparece'). Do not assert a homicide as established — flagged for a careful re-read. Orig. pp.135–63.
