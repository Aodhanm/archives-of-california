# San Diego treasury statement, Sept 1829. In $26,631 (existence $25,342 + mission supplies + a payment re the fragata *Fr

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n29** · Sav p. 29 · tomo p. 807 · 1829-09-01 · Savage abstract · 

San Diego treasury statement, Sept 1829. In $26,631 (existence $25,342 + mission supplies + a payment re the fragata *Franklin* $826). Out $33,710 — incl. pay of the cavalry company $671, Mazatlán squadron $404, B. Cal. frontier support $247, artillery piquete $168, the engineer corps (Pacheco) $198, loans to Missions San Diego ($250) & San Miguel ($146). Existence $23,261. (Bandini's commissary accounts; the American *Franklin* in the ledger.) Orig. p.807.
