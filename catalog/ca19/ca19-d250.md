# Maritime assault: Isidro Núñez, cabin boy of the Mexican brig *Catalina*, 'hirió alevosamente' the pilot Guy Stone aboar

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n447** · Sav p. 442 · tomo p. 567–568 · 1833-05-01 · verbatim transcription · 

Maritime assault: Isidro Núñez, cabin boy of the Mexican brig *Catalina*, 'hirió alevosamente' the pilot Guy Stone aboard ship; on Asesor Gómez's advice (28 Feb 1833) the accused was remitted with a sealed record to the naval command at San Blas, embarked 1 May on the same vessel, Capt. Snook receipting custody. Foreign-crewed Mexican shipping + violent-crime-at-sea. Orig. pp.567–8.
