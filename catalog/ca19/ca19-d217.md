# Memorial: exiled to California since 1824 'por malos informes… en el 1er día de la independencia de México'; during Solí

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n383** · Sav p. 378 · tomo p. 675, 680–682 · 1833-03-29 · Savage abstract · 

Memorial: exiled to California since 1824 'por malos informes… en el 1er día de la independencia de México'; during Solís's 1829–30 revolt the Cmdt. de Armas Alf. Francisco Pacheco gave him command of 30 men to pursue Solís, and he captured Solís & his followers and delivered them. Certified by Alf. Francisco Pérez Pacheco (10 Feb 1830), 3er Tte. de Marina Juan Malarín (28 Feb 1830) & Tte. retirado José Estrada (26 Mar 1830). Orig. pp.675, 680–2.
