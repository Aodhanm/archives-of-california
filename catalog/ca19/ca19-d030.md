# San Francisco treasury statement, Dec 1829. Income $10,768 — incl. tobacco $195, mission supplies $4,543, Monterey/SD tr

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n30** · Sav p. 30 · tomo p. 811 · 1829-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

San Francisco treasury statement, Dec 1829. Income $10,768 — incl. tobacco $195, mission supplies $4,543, Monterey/SD treasury transfers, a $1,000 debt to the fragata *Franklin* (re Mission San José powder), foreigner-import duties $177, and whaling-ship anchorage fees $87. Paid $10,444. (Foreign-ship/whaler revenue at SF.) Orig. p.811.
