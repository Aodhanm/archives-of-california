# Certifies Echeandía ordered him (Jan 1830) to mobilize 30 Los Ángeles men under José Mª Cota to help defend SB against S

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n297** · Sav p. 292 · tomo p. 680 · 1831-07-04 · verbatim transcription · 

Certifies Echeandía ordered him (Jan 1830) to mobilize 30 Los Ángeles men under José Mª Cota to help defend SB against Solís; José Antonio Tapia among them (in LA under bond). (The 'LA citizens mobilized' material — the loyalist side of the Solís revolt.) Orig. p.680.
