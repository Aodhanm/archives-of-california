# Causa criminal vs. cavalry soldier Juan Antonio Arces for treacherously wounding the Santa Bárbara neófito Francisco Jav

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n238** · Sav p. 233 · tomo p. 21–106 · 1832-02-01 · verbatim transcription · 

Causa criminal vs. cavalry soldier Juan Antonio Arces for treacherously wounding the Santa Bárbara neófito Francisco Javier (12 Aug 1831). Asesor Lic. Rafael Gómez (7 Sep 1832): the wound was not grave (the victim recovered quickly, 'fué por causa de sus embriagueces') and had no aggravating circumstance — Arces had already purged the offense with ~6 months' prison; released to continue service. Orig. pp.21–106.
