# Recovery of stolen stock: the party captured some NM thieves and recovered many animals in the valle de los Tulares near

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n430** · Sav p. 425 · tomo p. 486–487 · 1833-02-18 · Savage abstract · 

Recovery of stolen stock: the party captured some NM thieves and recovered many animals in the valle de los Tulares near Arroyo Grande — of these 75 mules, 30 mares, 11 horses belong to Mission San Luis Obispo (may reclaim them); two SLO neophytes — Lucas & Coronado — appear as accomplices, whom Carrillo asked the Gefe Político to hand over for prosecution. Orig. pp.486–7.
