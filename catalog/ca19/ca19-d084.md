# Causa criminal: an averiguación opened on Vicente Ríos's complaint that the soldier [J.] Becerra had handed Ríos's wife 

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n73** · Sav p. 73 · tomo p. 294–302 · 1830-03-02 · verbatim transcription · 

Causa criminal: an averiguación opened on Vicente Ríos's complaint that the soldier [J.] Becerra had handed Ríos's wife 'unos papeles con polvos p[ar]a que lo echase en su alimento' (powders to put in Ríos's food) and boasted that the better clothes she wore he had given her. Escribano Sargto Dámaso[?] Rodríguez. Fiscal's dictamen: per the wife's declaration Becerra's conversations with her 'pasaron á solas'; to avert 'alg[un]a desgracia,' Becerra to be sent back to his company and held under arrest pending the Comandante General. 'Así se hizo.' Orig. pp.294–302.
