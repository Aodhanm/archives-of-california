# 'Sedición de Solís' — the SB guard's defense against Solís (8 soldiers, 1 artillery sargto + 6 men, 1 cannon) under Eche

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n296–n297** · Sav p. 291–292 · tomo p. 678–679 · 1830-07-16 · verbatim transcription · 

'Sedición de Solís' — the SB guard's defense against Solís (8 soldiers, 1 artillery sargto + 6 men, 1 cannon) under Echeandía; Manuel Cota arrived with 30 men from Los Ángeles (incl. José Antonio Tapia) to reinforce SB by Echeandía's order; served until Solís didn't come, then returned to LA. Orig. pp.678–679.
