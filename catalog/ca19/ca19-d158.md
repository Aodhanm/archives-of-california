# 'Sobre cambio en misiones' — secularization: per the 13 Sep 1813 law, the missions' temporal administration to change; a

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n289** · Sav p. 284 · tomo p. 494 · 1831-01-06 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre cambio en misiones' — secularization: per the 13 Sep 1813 law, the missions' temporal administration to change; asks the Bishop to resolve what is best for the 'nuevos pueblos' spiritually — convert them into curatos with canonically-provided párrocos, or leave them to the Religiosos. (The 6 Jan 1831 secularization measure addressed to the Bishop — parallel to Doc 69.) Orig. p.494.
