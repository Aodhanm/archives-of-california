# The fiscal's report in the Charles Laing contraband case (escribano Cadete Ignacio del Valle): Laing landed at San Quint

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n26–n27** · Sav p. 26–27 · tomo p. 699–763 · 1829-02-21 · verbatim transcription · 

The fiscal's report in the Charles Laing contraband case (escribano Cadete Ignacio del Valle): Laing landed at San Quintín & the ensenada de Todos Santos, introduced effects into the territory's pueblos without paying duties or licence, 'saltando en los puntos donde no es permitido' — convicted by his own declarations; the fiscal asks confiscation of all the goods introduced. Orig. pp.699–763.
