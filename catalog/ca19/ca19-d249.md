# Church-robbery causa: neophytes Verano & Cesáreo robbed the SLO mission church (~20 Jan 1833) — in via the carpentry sho

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n443–n447** · Sav p. 438–442 · tomo p. 543–545 · 1833-03 · verbatim transcription · 

Church-robbery causa: neophytes Verano & Cesáreo robbed the SLO mission church (~20 Jan 1833) — in via the carpentry shop → forge → up the bell tower → down the choir → sacristy, taking 18 valuable ornaments (chasubles, capes, surplices, altar cloths) + carpentry/forge tools; both confessed; ornaments returned under bond by Fray Luis Gil; Cabo Isidro Ibarra of the escort reported it. Orig. pp.543–5.
