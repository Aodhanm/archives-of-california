# Reglamento (8 articles) on the missions' temporal administration / livestock distribution: crops to complete each missio

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n368** · Sav p. 363 · tomo p. 971–972 · 1833-12-01 · Savage abstract · 

Reglamento (8 articles) on the missions' temporal administration / livestock distribution: crops to complete each mission's seed; cattle loaned to the national ranchos, to be repaid & integrated back to the missions within 6 years; rules on goods receipt (receipts via the subcomisaría), escoltas, etc. A secularization-era mission-management regulation (Figueroa, prelude to his 1834 secularization). Orig. pp.971–972.
