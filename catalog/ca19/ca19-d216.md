# Election of 9 electores by the mission vecindario for 1834 — the junta named secretaries Antonino Buelna & José Fernánde

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n382** · Sav p. 377 · tomo p. 645 · 1833-12-28 · Savage abstract · 

Election of 9 electores by the mission vecindario for 1834 — the junta named secretaries Antonino Buelna & José Fernández; elected 'los Ciudadanos': Raymundo (50 votes), Eduardo (48), Hilario (45), Severo (44), Norberto (39), Gerónimo (34), Máximo (29), Sebastián Aparicio (26), Pascual (26). Single-name electors ⇒ almost certainly neophytes voting as citizens — a striking secularization-era municipal-election record at a mission. Orig. p.645.
