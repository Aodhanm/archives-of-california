# SB sub-treasury account (cargo/data): revenue $6,710 incl. mission suministros to the presidio (La Purísima $578, Sta In

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n401** · Sav p. 396 · tomo p. 131–133 · 1833-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

SB sub-treasury account (cargo/data): revenue $6,710 incl. mission suministros to the presidio (La Purísima $578, Sta Inés $164, SB $150, San Buenaventura $984, San Fernando $454 = $2,270), tithes, sealed paper, the company rancho, a tonnage duty on the frigate *Guipuzcoana*; expenditure $6,725. Shows the missions still bankrolling the presidio in 1833 on the eve of secularization. Orig. pp.131–3.
