# The Solís revolt's 'Plan de la revolución' / 'Manifiesto al Público' — a long indictment of Gov. Echeandía (corruption, 

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n155–n165** · Sav p. 152–162 · tomo p. (Plan) · 1829-11-15 · verbatim transcription · 

The Solís revolt's 'Plan de la revolución' / 'Manifiesto al Público' — a long indictment of Gov. Echeandía (corruption, self-enrichment, favoritism, suspending the Diputación, paralyzing agriculture/commerce/schools, the suspended Hacienda, impunity of crimes, troop 'hambre y desnudez' and indiscipline) followed by 9 articles: (1) reconvene the Diputación; (2) Echeandía surrender all command to the Diputación, which names an interim chief until the new governor arrives; (3) ayuntamientos provisionally appoint Hacienda administrators; (4) discipline; (5–7) supply rules (missions requisitioned only via alcaldes w/ receipts); (8) property & persons respected, esp. 'los estrangeros que residen pacíficamente entre nosotros' — with a Nota adicional extending the same protection to the Spaniards (suspending the expulsion → why the revolt was framed as 'por España'). → verbatim in Expanded Entry D. Orig. pp.152–162.
