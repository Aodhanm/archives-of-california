# Review of the Mazatlán cavalry squadron at San Diego, Dec 1829. Capt. Pablo de la Portilla 'en campaña' (the Solís-revol

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n2** · Sav p. 2 · tomo p. 2–96 · 1829-12-03 · Savage abstract · 

Review of the Mazatlán cavalry squadron at San Diego, Dec 1829. Capt. Pablo de la Portilla 'en campaña' (the Solís-revolt campaign); Ttes Ibarra & Narciso Fabregat; 4 alféreces, 3 cabos, 39 soldados = 50 hombres; much of the unit 'en campaña' (Portilla, Sargtos Dámaso Tetary[?] & Matías Ramos, 2 cabos, 32 soldados) while Fabregat & Alf. Roberto Pardo[?] were at Santa Bárbara. Company costs ~$1,694; SD subaltern commission monthly pay Jan–Jun 1829 ($687→$781). Orig. pp.2–96.
