# 'Sedición de Solís' — Tapia petitions to be exempted from prison, noting he was forced to come to SB with 30 men 'para s

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n296** · Sav p. 291 · tomo p. 676–677 · 1831-08-22 · Savage abstract · 

'Sedición de Solís' — Tapia petitions to be exempted from prison, noting he was forced to come to SB with 30 men 'para sostener al Gob.no legítimo, y contra la revolución de Solís' (a loyalist who fought Solís seeking relief). Orig. pp.676–677.
