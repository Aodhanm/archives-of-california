# Diezmos (tithe) collection difficulty: no one will collect the tithes for the offered compensation (3% or even double) —

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n371–n372** · Sav p. 366–367 · tomo p. 21–22 · 1833-08-02 · Savage abstract · 

Diezmos (tithe) collection difficulty: no one will collect the tithes for the offered compensation (3% or even double) — SF to the last rancho of the district is 40 leagues; asks the tithe he was to collect be credited toward duties/salaries owed. Orig. pp.21–22.
