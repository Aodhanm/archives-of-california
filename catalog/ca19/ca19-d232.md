# Formal INSTRUCTIONS for a military expedition into the Tulares to 'perseguir y destruir la reunión de individuos del ter

**C-A 19 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n440** · Sav p. 435 · tomo p. 334+ · 1833 · Savage abstract · 

Formal INSTRUCTIONS for a military expedition into the Tulares to 'perseguir y destruir la reunión de individuos del territorio de Nuevo México denunciados de ladrones de ganado caballar y mular': 15 cavalry from the Santa Bárbara presidio to march to Los Ángeles, join the alcalde & armed vecinos, well-armed & munitioned. A rare organized 1833 punitive expedition vs. Old-Spanish-Trail / Chaguanoso horse-raiders in the San Joaquín Valley. Orig. p.334+.
