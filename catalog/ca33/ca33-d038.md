# Observaciones al bando de 12 de Enero de 1844 (verbatim, 10 leaves) — commentary requested by the ayuntamiento clarifyin

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n20–n29** · Sav p. 20–29 · tomo p. 85–94 · 1844-04-09 · verbatim transcription · 

Observaciones al bando de 12 de Enero de 1844 (verbatim, 10 leaves) — commentary requested by the ayuntamiento clarifying the anti-cattle-theft bando: reviews the 3-Mar-1837 predecessor bando, the Diputación Territorial decree (Art. 16, 7 Oct 1837) & the 3-Feb-1836 govt order; discusses hide-marking/branding, the embargo & recognition of cattle-ownership (fierro de dominio), the office of Comisionado/Agente de Policía (½ real per hide); dissects each article's enforceability (who pays for embarked hides — buyer/seller/bargeman); ⭐⭐⭐ closes on Indian & New Mexican horse-raiding: 'la grande extracción de caballada que hacen anualmente los especuladores de Nuevo Méjico, se unirá con el abandono [de] la persecución y escarmiento de los ladrones' → within few years Alta California stripped of horses; warns that confusing punishment of criminals with protection of persons ruins general interests. Testimoniado por Srio. Ignacio Coronel; appends bando Arts. 13/15/16 (cattle registro/fierro)
