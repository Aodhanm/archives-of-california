# Copies of two bandos sobre cueros: (1) the LA Ayuntamiento's bando of 3 Mar 1839 (Art 1: every merchant buying hides mus

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n30–n32** · Sav p. 30–32 · tomo p. 95–97 · 1844-04-09 · verbatim transcription · 

Copies of two bandos sobre cueros: (1) the LA Ayuntamiento's bando of 3 Mar 1839 (Art 1: every merchant buying hides must record in a bound book the seller's name, the marca and price; Art 2: a Comisión named by the alcaldes inspects all hides sold each week; Art 3–4: hides with false marks judged as thieves per the Sres. Alcaldes, buyer reimbursed, owner of marcas paid); (2) José María Echeandía's order as Jefe Superior Político de ambas Californias, 7 Oct 1827, published in Monterey — Art 16: on ranchos no cattle/hides bought without the owner's knowledge, a constancia given the buyer for transit
