# Reibo nota oficial + (Sept 28) in compliance with oficio of Aug 23 he assigned new house-lots (solares) to each of the v

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n10–n11** · Sav p. 10–11 · tomo p. 39–45 · 1840-05-21 / 09-28 · Savage abstract · 

Reibo nota oficial + (Sept 28) in compliance with oficio of Aug 23 he assigned new house-lots (solares) to each of the vecinos residing there; gives measured list: Prudencio Espinosa, Joaquín Buelna, Eleuterio Villa, Ramón Buelna, Manuel Espinosa, José Higuera, Tomás Cole (extranjero), Josecillo (neófito de San Carlos), Antonio (neófito de San Carlos), Esteban Espinosa — each with varas dimensions 'para casa y corral'
