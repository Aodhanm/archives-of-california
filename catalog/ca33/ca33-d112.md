# Reglamento para la venta ó entrega de cueros (7+ arts.): Art 1 every delivery of hides must be witnessed with the owner'

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n67** · Sav p. 67–? · tomo p. — · 1841-03-20 · verbatim transcription · 

Reglamento para la venta ó entrega de cueros (7+ arts.): Art 1 every delivery of hides must be witnessed with the owner's fierro de propiedad and carry the señal branded & marked, else penalty of arms; Art 2 hides taken from that jurisdiction need a paper from the authority of the jurisdiction to which they belong; Art 3 hides taken by exterior traders need a boleto with legal delivery, fierro & señal (fine $5); Art 4 whoever wants to extract cattle from the campo must notify the Juez de campo/authority, list fierros & marks, deliver on private terrain to the dueño; Art 5 persons judged of terrenos...; Art 6 property held in comunidad must be marked & registered; Art 7 must inform the Juez de Campo; Art 8 aserradores/traders must brand no señal without the authority's knowledge (fine); to be published & posted (tomo pp. 313–314)
