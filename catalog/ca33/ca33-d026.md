# Comercio-de-cabotaje regulation: to enforce the laws and correct abuses observed in permitting the coasting trade to for

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n15** · Sav p. 15 · tomo p. 65 · ~1840 (sin fecha, sin lugar, sin dirección) · verbatim transcription · 

Comercio-de-cabotaje regulation: to enforce the laws and correct abuses observed in permitting the coasting trade to foreign ships, this Pueblo is henceforth admitted as the único habilitado por la ley where foreign ships may unload their mercancías paying all duties in efectivo and completing the formalities of the Arsenal de Aduanas Marítimas; ships currently coasting given time to end their voyages
