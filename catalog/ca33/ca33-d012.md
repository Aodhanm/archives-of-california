# Instrucciones for the voyage: sail to San Diego; there Enrique (Henry) Fitch to load all cargo he can; carry to the Sand

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n8** · Sav p. 8 · tomo p. 35–37 · 1840-01-30 · verbatim transcription · 

Instrucciones for the voyage: sail to San Diego; there Enrique (Henry) Fitch to load all cargo he can; carry to the Sandwich Islands, leave cargo & repair the schooner; take aboard Enrique Fitch as sobrecargo who is to consign the cargo; Fitch & Cooper to agree on refit; proceeds from otter skins to be deposited in the Islands for the ship's repair; if opportunity to trade the vessel for a better one, Cooper may do so 'aun cuando haya que dar 4 á 6 mil pesos'; return to Monterey; misc. matters left to 'los conocimientos y experiencia del Capn. Juan Cooper' (15 arts.)
