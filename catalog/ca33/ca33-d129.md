# Declaraciones de atentados contra la vida de Sutter — Sutter's first-person narrative of three Indian assassination/upri

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n88–n93** · Sav p. 88–93 · tomo p. 415–418 · ~1844 (sin fecha ni lugar) · verbatim transcription · 

Declaraciones de atentados contra la vida de Sutter — Sutter's first-person narrative of three Indian assassination/uprising plots against him among the Sacramento tribes (Cozumnes / Cosolumnes); the first bramba [ambush?] by an Indio he calls Antonio (baptized by Francisco Javier), an Americano-influenced sujeto; the second (Mar 1) ~50 armed & feathered Indios headed by Capitán Antonio y Sixto planning to burn the houses and kill; the third (Mar 24) foiled; ⭐⭐⭐⭐ appends the Declaración de los Indios Magín, Camilo y Salvador naming the conspiracy's leaders — 'El Capitán Antonio, el Capitán Chupuchu, el Capitán de los Pescadores Anashe' — and ~18 named Indios (Salvador, Aráney, 'Ansh que mató al Indio Francisco Javier,' Geronimo, Julian, Manay, Berno, Cristano, Ense, Cosimo, Celso...); plot to gather 50 armed Indios, incendiary; ends 'End.' (= final leaf of Vol. XVII Juzgados)
