# Sutter / Nueva Helvecia militia + Hudson's Bay Co. + purchase of Russian Ross–Bodega (Constitutas / consultas; see Expan

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n83–n86** · Sav p. 83–86 · tomo p. 387–413 · 1844-03-19 → / 1841–1843 (retro docs) · Savage abstract · 

Sutter / Nueva Helvecia militia + Hudson's Bay Co. + purchase of Russian Ross–Bodega (Constitutas / consultas; see Expanded Entry 3). Mar 19/Apr 17: re Sr. Bidwell (John Bidwell), one of his empleados, whom he ordered to make a Carta topográfica mapping the terrain from the Bahía de San Francisco and the Río San Joaquín to the US frontier (to prevent foreigners penetrating), and re Jacob P. Leese de Sonoma; Apr 21: recomienda a Guillermo (William) Dickey para terrenos; Jul 23: has organized the Compañía Militar 'Nueva Helvecia' ready to defend the patria — recruits Sinclair (ex-Regt. de la Guardia Suiza), 40 Indios Cazadores, plans a Compañía de Indios Estrangeros; Vallejo has many govt horses in Sonoma; the Compañía 'Caravana' de la Columbia [Hudson's Bay Co.] arriving; Aug 6: extranjeros procedentes de Oregón, Columbia interrogated said they came to establish in the país, be made Mexicans, subject to the laws; ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (1843 Feb 1) the 'línea del Norte' opened by govt 1 Sept 1840, the Compañía de Hudson's Bay introducing itself into the country's lands 'con el objeto de pescar castores [otter]'; ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (1841 Dec 18, Sausalito) Sutter → Manuel Jimeno encargado del Gobierno en Monterey: that he has purchased the Russian establishments of Bodega y Ross ('ha comprado los Establecimientos rusos de Bodega y Ross')
