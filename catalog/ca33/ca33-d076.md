# (6 docs) Feb 14: acknowledges recognition as autoridad política, the Reglamento de Misiones, bando to populate the adjac

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n46–n47** · Sav p. 46–47 · tomo p. 176–184 · 1839-02-14 / 24 / 03-01 / 15 · Savage abstract · 

(6 docs) Feb 14: acknowledges recognition as autoridad política, the Reglamento de Misiones, bando to populate the adjacent islands, régimen de sus pobladores, instrucciones para causa criminal, receipt of the Constitución de 36. Feb 24: ⭐⭐ orders the Capitán de la Compañía de Cazadores instructions to proceed to take all the caballada found in the Rancherías de los Tulares to end the ladrones — 'desde 10 años para arriba,' take women & children prisoner, and any who resist be examined; then attack for the recovery of the horse-herd (an anti-raiding campaign order). Mar 1: ayto. swears the Prefecto Coronel José Castro; Mar 15 (6 docs, cont. n48): receives appointments of Inspectores (Castro 1st, Lic. Cosme Peña 2nd), correspondence index (tomo pp. 198–201), departure of the elector-poblador José Fernández; item 4 reclamando el bando re solicitudes de terreno baldío (nota of the 9th); items 5–6 index of correspondence Juzgado↔Secretaría
