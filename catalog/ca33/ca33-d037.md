# Municipal public-order bando: Art. 1 prohibits totally the sale of all kinds of liquors on feast days from 8 p.m. the pr

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n18–n19** · Sav p. 18–19 · tomo p. — · 1844-04-16 · verbatim transcription · 

Municipal public-order bando: Art. 1 prohibits totally the sale of all kinds of liquors on feast days from 8 p.m. the prior night to next morning (fines $10 / $25 / four months public works); Art. 2 stores stay closed, exceptions for necessity; Art. 3 penalizes gatherings making drunken uproar; Art. 4 the drunk causing scandal to be jailed / one month public works, if military handed to his cuartel
