# Inventario de Fincas: on delivering official communication to R.P. Fr. José María del Real, was told to inventory all th

**C-A 33 (Departmental State Papers (Juzgados / Naturalization)), scan n4** · Sav p. 5 · tomo p. — · 1839-03-11 · Savage abstract · 

Inventario de Fincas: on delivering official communication to R.P. Fr. José María del Real, was told to inventory all the mission buildings except the cuadra where he [Real] lives; gives the inventory of the buildings of San Carlos received into his charge, witnessed by Toribio Manjares y Mariano Romero (who cannot sign). Room-by-room list ('Cuadra que mira al Norte: 2 cuartos chicos...'; continues n6 pp.38–39, with a note that the Poniente rooms serving the church he did not receive because the padre says they belong to his living quarters)
