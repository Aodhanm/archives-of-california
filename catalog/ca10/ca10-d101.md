# A SECOND commandant's answers to the 15-question mission interrogatorio (the SOUTHERN missions): escorts used for punish

**C-A 10 (Provincial State Papers), scan n197–n202** · Sav p. 195–200 / 195–403 · 1798-12 (copy 7 Feb 99) · Savage abstract · 

A SECOND commandant's answers to the 15-question mission interrogatorio (the SOUTHERN missions): escorts used for punishments; the missions peg sales to the *arancel* but sell everything 'con exceso'; the padres buy extra mescal; the missions' cash held by the Síndicos of Mexico/Tepic (San Gabriel 2,897 p, San Juan Cap. 4,552, San Luis Rey newly founded, San Diego 5,810 = 10,960 p) — extends the Doc 52–53 mission-cruelty inquiry to a THIRD witness
