# THE AMERICAN FRIGATE *ELIZA* — per Borica's 1797 parts, the American merchant frigate *Eliza* arrived at [Monterey], cap

**C-A 10 (Provincial State Papers), scan n17** · Sav p. 17 · tomo p. 31–32 · 1798-01-03 · Savage abstract · 

THE AMERICAN FRIGATE *ELIZA* — per Borica's 1797 parts, the American merchant frigate *Eliza* arrived at [Monterey], captain [James Rowan?]; the Spanish gave it 187.3.1 in aid (the captain drawing a bill of £4,111.12 sterling against Boston); the *Eliza* abandoned 11 English people, including a woman — the Viceroy orders an *expediente* formed, informed the King, and decreed the English sent Veracruz → Havana → their countries (notifying the British envoy + enclosing the captain's bill to collect). A major early-American-contact / foreign-ship document
