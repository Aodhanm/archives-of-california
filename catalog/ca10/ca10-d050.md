# Neophyte murder case (Mission San Luis Obispo): the neophyte Silverio killed his wife Rebeca at the instigation of Rosa;

**C-A 10 (Provincial State Papers), scan n47–n48** · Sav p. 47–48 / 85–87 · 1798-08-21 · Savage abstract · 

Neophyte murder case (Mission San Luis Obispo): the neophyte Silverio killed his wife Rebeca at the instigation of Rosa; sentenced to the gallows (*horca*) — the Viceroy suspended the execution pending a ruling on how such crimes should henceforth be punished. A mission-Indian homicide + capital-sentence case (uxoricide + a love-triangle)
