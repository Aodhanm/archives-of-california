# THE CLOSING DOCUMENT ('Fin del tomo 17'): re the seizure (*apresamiento*) of the balandra under Bernardo Suárez and the 

**C-A 10 (Provincial State Papers), scan n346** · Sav p. 346 · tomo p. 932 · 1799-07-06 · Savage abstract · 

THE CLOSING DOCUMENT ('Fin del tomo 17'): re the seizure (*apresamiento*) of the balandra under Bernardo Suárez and the measures taken — in view of the circumstances, Borica is 'no haga uso del permiso q.e le concedió hasta que mude el semblante de las cosas' (not to use the license granted him until the aspect of things changes). Copied by Borica at Monterey, 'Sbre 20 de 99'; below the signature: 'el original llevó el q.e firma p.ª su resguardo.' ⭐ Cross-link (2026-07-05): the capture itself = C-A 15 Doc 392 (Loreto, 28 Jun 1799 — the *Mercedes*, Suárez Infanzón, taken by 4 English frigates near the Islas Marías; investigation dossier). *(Itemized from the bounded tail n288–n346 in the 2026-07-02 audit — verified on the scan; cited in [[borderlands-thesis-narrative]].)*
