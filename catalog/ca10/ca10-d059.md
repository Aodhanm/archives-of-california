# 'Informe sobre hostilidad de los indios': Amador sent Indians to the rancherías to covertly learn if they plan to attack

**C-A 10 (Provincial State Papers), scan n100** · Sav p. 100 / 156–161 · 1798-04-19 · Savage abstract · 

'Informe sobre hostilidad de los indios': Amador sent Indians to the rancherías to covertly learn if they plan to attack the Mission; found the Indians had gone to the river to hunt deer (their *comercio*) and fear the soldiers 'porque les matan sin motivo' (kill them without cause); the Mission alcalde requested men for an 8-league expedition to a distant ranchería, which Amador denied pending review. A key 1798 Bay-Area Indian-unrest document — with the Indians' own stated grievance
