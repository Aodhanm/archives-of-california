# 'Diario de su expedición á rancherías': Castro left the Mission (1 Jun) with 13 soldiers + a cabo + 13 men from Soledad 

**C-A 10 (Provincial State Papers), scan n331** · Sav p. 329 / — · 1799-06-26 · Savage abstract · 

'Diario de su expedición á rancherías': Castro left the Mission (1 Jun) with 13 soldiers + a cabo + 13 men from Soledad to recover fugitive Christian Indians — captured people at the ranchos, gave 25 lashes each to those who had hidden the Christians, marched 80 'Carmeleños' back to their Mission (with a soldier, an Indian alcalde & an interpreter), hunted the killers of a Christian Indian (one handed over, one fled to the hills), and negotiated with the *Capitanejos* (the ranchería of Orestay) to surrender the Christians. A detailed mission-flight-recovery / punitive-expedition diary — the flogging corroborates the Doc 52–53 mission-cruelty charges
