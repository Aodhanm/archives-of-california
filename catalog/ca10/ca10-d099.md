# 'Lista de reos transportados' — the transport manifest of the convict-colonists on the frigate *Concepción*: incl. alfér

**C-A 10 (Provincial State Papers), scan n182–n183** · Sav p. 182–183 · tomo p. 407 · 1798-06-06 · Savage abstract · 

'Lista de reos transportados' — the transport manifest of the convict-colonists on the frigate *Concepción*: incl. alférez José de los Reyes + his wife Mª Eduarda Camargo, Ana Pérez (wife of Cornelio Rocha), Mª Petra Aranda (a woman), + the *reos* (José Chávez, Pedro Osorno, José Rosas, José Ávila, José Velázquez, Antonio Ortega, José Calzada, Bárcena, Felipe Hernández, Casimiro Corujo, José Egaña, Juan Blanco, José Ramos…) + 2 sentenced to the ships. The actual ship-manifest of the 1798 colonization (cf. Docs 12/56); conducted by Matute (the Bodega-1793 officer)
