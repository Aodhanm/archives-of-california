# Bounded 1799 *correo*/courier-logistics cluster: how to route the California *correspondencia* — the *extraordinario* co

**C-A 10 (Provincial State Papers), scan n22–n30** · Sav p. 22–30 / 46–61 · 1799 (Jan–Jul) · Savage abstract · 

Bounded 1799 *correo*/courier-logistics cluster: how to route the California *correspondencia* — the *extraordinario* couriers, whether via Chihuahua vs Álamos/Tepic/Buenavista vs San Blas by ship; the chain of orders (Viceroy Azanza → Nava → the correos-administrators); reimbursement of courier costs from Real Hacienda. ⭐ Marks the viceregal transition Branciforte → Azanza (1798→99).
