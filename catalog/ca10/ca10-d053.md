# the diet answer verbatim — 'por comida dos cucharadas de pozole é trigo cocido… á la tarde por cena otra tanto atole; ca

**C-A 10 (Provincial State Papers), scan n71–n80** · Sav p. 71–80 · tomo p. 105–… · 1798-12-14 · Savage abstract · 

the diet answer verbatim — 'por comida dos cucharadas de pozole é trigo cocido… á la tarde por cena otra tanto atole; carne le da por semana para uno ó dos días… por insuficiente, para que con sólo esto se mantengan y puedan RECIBIR las fatigas á que se les obliga' ✎ *(the manuscript reads 'recibir,' not 'resistir')* — and the causal link explicit: this is 'la próxima causa de que muchos… se van pasando á robarla de la Misión, ó á las fugas.' GOYCOECHEA'S ANSWERS to the 15-question interrogatorio — a secular official corroborating the mission-cruelty charges 'con la verdad que solo bajo mi palabra de honor': doctrine taught in the native language then Castilian (1ª); ⭐ the ordinary diet — morning *atole*, midday *pozole* + wheat, evening *atole* w/ meat 1–2×/week — 'insuficiente para que con sólo esto se mantengan y puedan recibir las fatigas á que se les obliga' (the food question); ⭐ single women & single men locked in a room/*pozolera* within the Mission at night, released by the padres each morning (only Sta Bárbara has a recreation room; 8ª); labor hours/tasks, pregnant & nursing exempt (9ª); ⭐⭐⭐ 12ª — the missions PREVENT & PUNISH Indians who occupy themselves with soldiers (*gente de razón*), so the Indian is barred from earning wages & the food/clothing improvement he'd gain — 'por esto se retarda visiblemente la civilidad de estos indios'; the missionaries peg their sales to the *arancel* yet recompense poorly (13ª); the *temporalidades* run with hidden 'máximas' (14ª); + an attached table of the missions' debits with the SB habilitación (San Gabriel 3,311 p, San Juan Cap. 1,627, Sta Bárbara 1,696, Purísima 405, San Buenaventura 1,611, San Fernando 267…; n80). A presidio commandant testifying, on his honor, that the mission labor system coerces & impoverishes the neophytes — with Doc 52 a two-witness indictment.
