# Borrador register, leaf 126 (Oct 1810–Jan 1811) — the Mercury deserters on the Princesa

**C-A 25 (Provincial Records (Tomo IX)), scan n378** · Sav p. 375 · tomo leaf 126 · orig pp. 384–92 · Oct 1810–Jan 1811 · Savage abstract (register) ·

The three deserters of the American ship *Mercury* — "Juan Blanco, del Ferrol; Juan Bautista, natural de Manila; y Leo Sam[?], indio de las Islas de Sandwich" — shipped to San Blas on the frigate *Princesa* (Oct 26), with the December account of what their keep cost; Guerra y Noriega embarks as habilitado-general-elect; the hemp harvest (8,752 arrobas); the artillery detachment's threatened withdrawal. Bancroft's ix.126 cites (HoC II:95, 97, 187) land here.
