# Sola borradores, leaf 135 (Oct–Nov [1815]) — the Russian brig's deserters; the Quintana prisoners die

**C-A 25 (Provincial Records (Tomo IX)), scan n387** · Sav p. 384 · tomo leaf 135 · orig pp. 441–53 · Oct–Nov [1815] · Savage abstract (register) ·

Two Americans desert the Russian brig [Il'mena?] anchored at San Luis Obispo cove; the burial fees of Andrés and Secundino — two of Fr. Quintana's killers — who died in the calabozo (→ the causa continues at leaves 138–139); the British Northwest Company's *Columbia*; and the acknowledgment of the June-1814 restoration decrees. Bancroft's ix.135 cites land here.
