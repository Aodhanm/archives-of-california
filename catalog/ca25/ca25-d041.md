# Borrador register, leaf 127 (May–Sept 1811) — the insurgency-era indulto and the hemp instructor

**C-A 25 (Provincial Records (Tomo IX)), scan n379** · Sav p. 376 · tomo leaf 127 · orig pp. 392–401 · May–Sept 1811 · Savage abstract (register) ·

The Hidalgo-era indulto for places where "conmociones populares" had shown themselves (May 29); Condestable Joaquín Sánchez retired at two-thirds pay conditioned on teaching hemp culture; presidio supply notes and retirements; and the San Diego mission causas (Ramírez; the Indian Hermenegildo). Bancroft's ix.127 (HoC II:180) lands here.
