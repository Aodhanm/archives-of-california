# Borrador register, leaf 128 (1811–May 1812) — Hidalgo's seditious papers; the unpaid troops

**C-A 25 (Provincial Records (Tomo IX)), scan n380** · Sav p. 377 · tomo leaf 128 · orig pp. 402–6+ · 1811–1812 · Savage abstract (register) ·

The governor acknowledges the order for vigilance against "papeles sediciosos del Cura Hidalgo"; reports the troops' deplorable, two-years-unsupplied state (May 22, 1812); worries the mescal-wine permission "puede tener malos resultados en la Prov.ª"; and opens the Feb 4, 1812 report of a brig aground near San Francisco. Bancroft's ix.128 (HoC II:424) lands here.
