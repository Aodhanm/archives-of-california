# Sola borradores, leaf 137 (Dec [1815]–Feb 1816)

**C-A 25 (Provincial Records (Tomo IX)), scan n389** · Sav p. 386 · tomo leaf 137 · orig pp. 460–70 · 1815–16 · Savage abstract (register) ·

San Francisco's fortifications ordered repaired after the governor's visit; hospitality-only rations for the NW Company's *Columbia*; renewed vigilance against insurgent emissaries; and the San Diego mission Indians who killed mayordomo Miguel Álvarez sentenced to ten years. Bancroft's ix.137 cites land here.
