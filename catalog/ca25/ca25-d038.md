# Borrador register, leaf 101 — the Argüello marriage license without montepío rights

**C-A 25 (Provincial Records (Tomo IX)), scan n352** · Sav p. 350 · tomo leaf 101 · orig pp. 275–278 · c.1805 · Savage abstract (register) ·

Register-of-borradores leaf. Item 18 (orig p.277): cédulas de invalidez for cabo [Cayetano?] Espinosa and soldado Manuel Montero of the Monterey presidio, and "Licencia de matrimonio á Luis Ant.º Argüello, sin q.e esposa é hijos tengan derecho á Montepío Militar" — the marriage license Bancroft cites in Argüello's Pioneer-Register footnote as Prov. Rec., MS., ix. 101 (his leaf-number citation; register F38/F39 adjudication). Companion to the C-A 12 marriage-license docs at Prov. St. Pap. XIX (sav 40).
