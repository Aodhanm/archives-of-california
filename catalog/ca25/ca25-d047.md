# ⭐⭐⭐⭐ The Il'men dossier — Tarasov's baidarka party seized at San Pedro; Elliot de Castro taken at San Luis Obispo

**C-A 25 (Provincial Records (Tomo IX)), scan n388** · Sav p. 385 · tomo leaf 136 · orig pp. 453–61 · [1815–16] · Savage abstract (register) ·

The register's consolidated report of the Il'men captures: twelve Russian baidarkas and twenty-four [Kodiak] hunters seized in San Pedro cove with their patrón Boris Tarasov; the brig's Anglo-American supercargo Juan Eliot de Castro apprehended at San Luis Obispo with 4 Russians and 7 Indians (soldier Diego Olivera recommended an escudo for the capture); the Russian frigate *Suvorov* at San Francisco; then the prisoners' embarkation. The heart of the 1815 Russian-poaching crisis — Spanish enforcement finally reaching the RAC's California hunting system (→ bodega / Open-Door arc; Elliot de Castro later returns with Kotzebue's Rurik).
