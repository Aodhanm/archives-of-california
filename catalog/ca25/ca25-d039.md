# Borrador register, leaf 121 (Apr–May 1810) — Danish war news, deserters' account, the habilitado-general election

**C-A 25 (Provincial Records (Tomo IX)), scan n373** · Sav p. 370 · tomo leaf 121 · orig pp. 360–66 · Apr–May 1810 · Savage abstract (register) ·

The governor's borradores for late April–May 1810: the expense account "errogada por desertores Americanos y Rusos" (Apr 28); notice of the declaration of war with Denmark (May 22); penalties for deserters; and the May 31 officer-corps election — Capt. Manuel Rodríguez, with the most votes for habilitado general going to Lt. José de la Guerra y Noriega of Santa Bárbara (whose Oct 1810 embarkation is at leaf 126 = d40). Plus the 1807 hemp harvest at 6,000 arrobas credited to Condestable Sánchez. Bancroft cites this leaf at HoC II:187 ("Prov. Rec., MS., ix. 121, 126").
