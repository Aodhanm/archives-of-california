# Leaf 134 — the Sola borradores open (Sept 1815)

**C-A 25 (Provincial Records (Tomo IX)), scan n386** · Sav p. 383 · tomo leaf 134 · orig pp. 435–41 · 1813/1815 · Savage abstract ·

Transition leaf: Argüello's Feb 1813 squad appointment, an unsigned index, and the opening of Gov. Sola's letterbook to the viceroy — his Sept 5, 1815 arrival report on the frigate *Paz*, "estado de miseria en q.e se encuentran sus habitantes." Fixes the chronology of leaves 135–139 (all Sola-era, 1815–16).
