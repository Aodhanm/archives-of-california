# 'Sobre la Escuela' — ALL residents (soldiers, inválidos, pobladores, agregados) send their children to Ramón Lasso's sch

**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n50** · Sav p. 51 · tomo p. 270–372 · 1795-07-13→23 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre la Escuela' — ALL residents (soldiers, inválidos, pobladores, agregados) send their children to Ramón Lasso's school, 2½ reales/month each; report the annual seed the vecinos owe for community expenses; ⭐ collect the community grain (28 fanegas wheat + 34 maize) → the presidio habilitado; Claudio Alviso named to distribute the water; the Gentile capitancillo Soler delivered 10 prisoners; 'escribirá V. oficios en Castilla'
