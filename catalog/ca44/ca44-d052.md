# the HEMP / FLAX / NAVAL-CORDAGE dossier (2 chained México copies Borica assembled for the CA hemp project): (a) the 1793

**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n54–59** · Sav p. 55–59 · tomo p. 54–57 + 282–284 · 1793 → copied 1795-11-27 · Savage abstract · 

the HEMP / FLAX / NAVAL-CORDAGE dossier (2 chained México copies Borica assembled for the CA hemp project): (a) the 1793 royal hemp/flax cultivation instructions (México 14 Apr 1793, Director D. Luis Gálvez; retting, spadilla, tools 'como los del país' not Spain, the labradores weed with Indians 'de á dos reales,' an administrator + maestro carretero) — copied Parrilla/Bonilla → Borica; (b) the 1795 San Blas ARSENAL cordage report (Hijosa → Marqués de Branciforte, 21 May 1795: the arsenal spins pita/cáñamo into jarcias/cables/estopa for the ships, ~4,800 [arrobas] consumed/yr, the Veracruz *jarcia trozada*, oakum for caulking) — the Spanish naval-hemp initiative Borica pushed in Alta California. ⚠ n55 & n56 are the SAME leaf (Savage 56; n56 ink-blotted)
