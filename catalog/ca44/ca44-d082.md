# the San José & Villa de Branciforte Ayuntamiento (Alcaldes, Regidores, vecinos) swears the INDEPENDENCE of the province 

**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n100** · Sav p. 99 · tomo p. 483–490 · 1822-07-06 (+ oath) · Savage abstract · 

the San José & Villa de Branciforte Ayuntamiento (Alcaldes, Regidores, vecinos) swears the INDEPENDENCE of the province — no longer recognizing the King of Spain, now under the Regencia del Imperio Mexicano, per the Plan de Iguala & Tratados de Córdoba, read publicly, certified in duplicate; + 'Sobre sebos y juntos' (tallow): suspend the stray-cattle-tallow order this year — ⭐ two English merchants have contracted with the P.te de Misiones to take 25,000 arrobas
