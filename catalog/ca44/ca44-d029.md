# tithe-deposit room built (Argüello to inspect the herds for tithe); ⭐⭐⭐ 'Sobre Indios y otras cosas' — the GENTILE-INDIA

**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n26–27** · Sav p. 27–28 · tomo p. 174–178+ · 1789-12→1790-01-02 · Savage abstract · 

tithe-deposit room built (Argüello to inspect the herds for tithe); ⭐⭐⭐ 'Sobre Indios y otras cosas' — the GENTILE-INDIAN doctrine: when Gentiles flee others who 'furtively took their women,' persuade/admonish (via the Capitanes); but if the women have been held long & have children, 'dejarlos así'; ⭐⭐⭐ 'como… los Gentiles son libres, no puede precisárseles á que reguladamente trabajen con tal ó tal sujeto' — since the free Gentiles can't be compelled to work for a given person, distribute their labor (men & women) under Castro per the Instruction, paid justly with NO advance pay 'para que siempre estén libres á trabajar donde V. les destine'; move the rodeo toward 'el Roblar de San Juan Bautista'
