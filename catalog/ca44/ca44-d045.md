# 'Sobre adulterio' — the 'Indio Californio Matías' & the wife of vecino José Aguilar (both deny the charge); Borica sends

**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n47** · Sav p. 48 · tomo p. 262– · 1795-02-22→03-26 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre adulterio' — the 'Indio Californio Matías' & the wife of vecino José Aguilar (both deny the charge); Borica sends Matías to the presidio, insists Aguilar 'viva con su muger según Dios manda,' else prove the adultery — insisting always 'en la unión del Matrimonio'; + drought fears, sow maize, reassign uncultivated *suertes*
