# send another Indian gang 'cuando se acabe la luna'; ⭐ no one to the rancherías except for cattle, 'no en busca de mugere

**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n31** · Sav p. 32 · tomo p. 201–206 · 1790-05-01→07 · Savage abstract · 

send another Indian gang 'cuando se acabe la luna'; ⭐ no one to the rancherías except for cattle, 'no en busca de mugeres'; ⭐⭐ 'Sobre recoger herramientas de los Gentiles' — collect/ransom the axes the Gentiles hold; NO ONE to give the Indians 'hachas, arados, ú otras Armas ó herramientas' (controlling Indian access to metal tools/weapons); the fugitive & voluntary Indians at Monterey to relieve the others
