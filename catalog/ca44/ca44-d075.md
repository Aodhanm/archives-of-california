# assure the capitanejo Soler that providing Indians for the King's hemp 'no se molestará á su ranchería'; vecinos build *

**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n84** · Sav p. 84 · tomo p. 392–395 · 1797-08-20→31 · Savage abstract · 

assure the capitanejo Soler that providing Indians for the King's hemp 'no se molestará á su ranchería'; vecinos build *carretas* to mill wheat; 'Sobre corte de Cáñamo' — cabo Mateo Bello directs the cutting/benefit, vecinos & Indians to learn it; the cured hemp to San Blas (the *Concepción*)
