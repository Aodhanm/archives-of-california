**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n87** · Sav p. 87 · tomo p. 407–411 · 1797-09-21→23 · Savage abstract · 

⚠ overlaps C-A 24. cabo Mateo returns to direct the hemp; offer the Sta Clara friars 5–10 arrobas of hemp seed; ⭐⭐ 'Sobre dar trabajo á los Gentiles' — because of the GOOD TREATMENT of the Gentile Indians of Soler's ranchería who work the hemp, several more presented themselves for employment; use the necessary few, give the surplus to the vecindario 'con la condición de tratarlos bien y pagarlos según costumbre'
