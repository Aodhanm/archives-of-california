# 'Órdenes sobre diezmos, bebidas y castigo de indios' — sustain the tithe; no liquor sold; ⭐⭐ any Christian Indian arrivi

**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n22** · Sav p. 23 · tomo p. 142–147 · 1788-10-08→31 · Savage abstract · 

'Órdenes sobre diezmos, bebidas y castigo de indios' — sustain the tithe; no liquor sold; ⭐⭐ any Christian Indian arriving at the pueblo without his friars' license → 10 azotes (1st), 12 (2nd), 15 (3rd), then returned to the friars with a soldier (the pueblo-Indian pass/flogging regime); care for the cañón; protect the works so 'las aguas no destruyan lo trabajado' (flood risk)
