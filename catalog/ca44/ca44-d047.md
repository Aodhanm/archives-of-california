# inválidos (Ramón Bojórquez &c.) with lands to work them; pobladores who can't cultivate all their *suertes* must rent th

**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n49** · Sav p. 50 · tomo p. 267–269 · 1795-07-03 · Savage abstract · 

inválidos (Ramón Bojórquez &c.) with lands to work them; pobladores who can't cultivate all their *suertes* must rent the surplus; the vecinos can't be forced to give the soldiers water if unwilling; ⭐ reprimand to Narciso Saer — being Alcalde/Regidor 'no le liberta del cumplimiento de mis órdenes', must set the best example, or come to Monterey 'para arreglarle'
