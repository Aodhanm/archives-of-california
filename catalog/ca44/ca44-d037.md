# 6 imprisoned Indians presented to the friar; don't harass Sta Clara Indians; investigate whether Pedro Cayuelas & others

**C-A 44 (Departmental State Papers, San José), scan n38–40** · Sav p. 39–41 · tomo p. 222–229 · 1790-07-03→22 · Savage abstract · 

6 imprisoned Indians presented to the friar; don't harass Sta Clara Indians; investigate whether Pedro Cayuelas & others will settle at Santa Margarita; ⭐⭐ 'Sobre Indios para trabajar' — the RECRUITMENT METHOD: 24 Indians came (the old ones 'poco valen'); ⚠ an Indian was hurt 'por haber ido á sorprenderlos en su baile' — Fages rebukes it ('no es esto lo que se ha mandado'); recruit NOT by surprise but visit the Capitancillos and gently persuade each ranchería to send 'cuatro ó 6 ú 8… turnando' — 'de ninguna manera… sorpresas, y mucho menos estando ellos en sus fiestas'
