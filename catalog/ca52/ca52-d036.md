# plan for founding NEW FRONTIER ESTABLISHMENTS (the San Diego–Baja frontier: new missions/pueblos, incl. one near Mission

**C-A 52 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n326** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 328–~364 · ca.1790s · Savage abstract · 

plan for founding NEW FRONTIER ESTABLISHMENTS (the San Diego–Baja frontier: new missions/pueblos, incl. one near Mission San Miguel) — a numbered document reusing the full pueblo-founding legal apparatus: keeping 'la comunicación de los nuevos establecimientos con la antigua California… abierta' (n330); the Recopilación de Indias framework (Ley 15–16 Tít.3 Lib.6 @ n340; ⭐ Ley 1 & 10 Tít.12 Lib.4 — pobladores gain dominion of their solares, sellable 'como cosa suya propia' but NOT alienable to the Church/monasteries/manos muertas @ n350); the peonía/caballería/sitio measures; R.O. 15 May 1773. Reuses Neve's 1781 Instrucción apparatus (dedup-adjacent, but frontier-expansion-specific)
