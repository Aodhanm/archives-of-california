# Los Angeles 1786 possession dossier (roster ~COMPLETE, item-read) — the formalization of the LA founders' titles: per Fa

**C-A 52 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n280–305** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 285–307 · 1786-09-04 · Savage abstract · 

Los Angeles 1786 possession dossier (roster ~COMPLETE, item-read) — the formalization of the LA founders' titles: per Fages's mandate & Neve's Reglamento, the 'nueve Pobladores' each granted a solar (20×40 varas) + 4 suertes (200 v²) + a registered brand. ⭐ COMPLETE 9-poblador roster: Antonio Clemente (José proxy), Antonio Navarro (absent, Clemente proxy), José Vanegas, Pablo Rodríguez, José Moreno, Basilio Rosas, Alejandro Rosas, José Sinova, Manuel Camero (n288); witnesses Vicente Feliz & Roque de Cota; most illiterate (signed by cross). ⭐⭐ Ends with a 1786 PLANO/MAP of the pueblo (n302 — plaza+church-cross, grid of solares, suertes, Río de Porciúncula, Acequia Madre) → [[historical-map-collection]]. + land-measure policy (peonía/caballería/sitio; 4-legua grants, n320)
