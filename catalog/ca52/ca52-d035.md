# Los Angeles 1786 possession dossier — the formalization of the LA founders' titles: per Fages's mandate & Neve's Reglame

**C-A 52 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n280** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 285–~310 · 1786-09-04 · Savage abstract · 

Los Angeles 1786 possession dossier — the formalization of the LA founders' titles: per Fages's mandate & Neve's Reglamento, the 'nueve Pobladores' each granted a solar (20×40 varas) + suertes + a registered brand. Named grantees incl. Antonio Clemente (n290, son José standing in), José Sinova (n300); witnesses Vicente Feliz & Roque de Cota; most illiterate (signed by cross). + land-measure policy (peonía/caballería/sitio; 4-legua grants, n320)
