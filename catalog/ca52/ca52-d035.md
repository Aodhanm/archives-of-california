# Los Angeles 1786 possession dossier — the formalization of the LA founders' titles: per Fages's mandate & Neve's Reglame

**C-A 52 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n280** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 285–~310 · 1786-09-04 · Savage abstract · 

Los Angeles 1786 possession dossier — the formalization of the LA founders' titles: per Fages's mandate & Neve's Reglamento, the 'nueve Pobladores' each granted a solar (20×40 varas) + 4 suertes (200 v²) + a registered brand. Named grantees (item-read): Antonio Clemente (José standing in), José Vanegas, Pablo Rodríguez, José Sinova (+ the rest of the 9 in the n281–305 acts — most illiterate, signed by cross); witnesses Vicente Feliz & Roque de Cota. + land-measure policy (peonía/caballería/sitio; 4-legua grants, n320). ⚠ ~4 of the 9 named so far; remaining LA acts n292–305 to itemize.
