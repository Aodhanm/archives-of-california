# division of the Comandancia General de Provincias Internas into divisions (altering Croix's 26 Aug 1786 Instrucción, 3 f

**C-A 52 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n60–69** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 61–70 · 1787 · Savage abstract · 

division of the Comandancia General de Provincias Internas into divisions (altering Croix's 26 Aug 1786 Instrucción, 3 frontier divisions); the 2nd Comandancia's duty = war/peace with Indians + defense of settled lands + peace with the northern nations (cites the 1772 Reglamento de Presidios)
