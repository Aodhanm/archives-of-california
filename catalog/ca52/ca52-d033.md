# San José 1783 possession dossier (COMPLETE roster, item-read) — Fages's framing ordinance + Moraga's *autos*; for each p

**C-A 52 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n240–269** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 242–271 · 1783-05-09 · Savage abstract · 

San José 1783 possession dossier (COMPLETE roster, item-read) — Fages's framing ordinance + Moraga's *autos*; for each poblador 3 *diligencias*: (1) a 30-vara-square solar, (2) 4 *suertes de tierra* (200×200 varas, irrigated), (3) a cattle brand (fierro); dated 9 May 1783. ⭐ The 9 grantee-pobladores: Manuel González, José Tiburcio Vásquez, Manuel Amézquita, Antonio Romero, Sebastián Alvitre, Bernardo Rosales, Francisco Ábila, Claudio Alvires (Alviso), Juan Atanacio Vásquez (adjacency notes tie their solares/suertes together). Officials/witnesses: José Moraga (comisionado), Alcalde Ignacio Archuleta, Juan Pablo Grijalva, Juan José Peralta, Felipe Tapia, Felipe Saavedra, Antonio Butrón (most pobladores illiterate → signed by proxy)
