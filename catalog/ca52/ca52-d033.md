# San José 1783 possession dossier — for each poblador, 3 *diligencias*: (1) a 30-vara-square solar, (2) 4 *suertes de tie

**C-A 52 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n245–269** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 247–271 · 1783-05 · Savage abstract · 

San José 1783 possession dossier — for each poblador, 3 *diligencias*: (1) a 30-vara-square solar, (2) 4 *suertes de tierra* (200×200 varas, irrigated), (3) a cattle brand (fierro). Named grantees incl. Manuel González (n246), Antonio Romero (n254; neighbors José Tiburcio Vásquez & Francisco Ábila); Alcalde Ignacio Archuleta; witnesses José Moraga, Juan Pablo Grijalva, Felipe Tapia, Antonio Butrón, Juan José Peralta (most pobladores illiterate — signed by proxy)
