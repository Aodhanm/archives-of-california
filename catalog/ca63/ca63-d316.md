# In relation to imprisonment of Pinto (answering doc 315): says he received a copy of Pinto's letter, that Pinto was deta

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n218** · Sav p. 216 · 1847-03-13 · Savage abstract · 

In relation to imprisonment of Pinto (answering doc 315): says he received a copy of Pinto's letter, that Pinto was detained on his arrival at that place because he had notice that Pinto was organizing a force for the purpose of joining those already in revolt at the South; that he offered to furnish him the ration allowed by Gov. which he declined, preferring to be subsisted by his family; that upon arrival Frémont at that place, he informed him what he had done in the case, and was directed by him to retain him still as a prisoner
