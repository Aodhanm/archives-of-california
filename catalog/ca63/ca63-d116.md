# Indian depredations: gives notice that to keep the Indians who are constantly raiding on the ranchos & running off their

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n43–44** · Sav p. 43 · 1848-02-06 · Savage abstract · 

Indian depredations: gives notice that to keep the Indians who are constantly raiding on the ranchos & running off their horses in check, the Commanding officer has consented to keep an armed force under an experienced officer at Cajon Pass until the season for Indian depredation is past; owners of horses called upon to furnish the horses assigned to each for such service, under penalty of $5 for each failure
