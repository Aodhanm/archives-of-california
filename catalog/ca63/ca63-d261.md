# Reports a party to establish an independent govt & attack the prison of Monterey (fuller than doc 32): information of a 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n169–170** · Sav p. 169 · 1848-03-04 · Savage abstract · 

Reports a party to establish an independent govt & attack the prison of Monterey (fuller than doc 32): information of a company of 60 men organized & daily receiving recruits, holding constant communication with the malcontents in the service, being in view to soon attack the prison of Monterey & to loose prisoners; they also have formed the plan of establishing an independent govt in Cal.; they are well armed; 'the good people of the country stand in fear of exposing these people lest they be killed'; say before yesterday a party of 3 foreigners tried to induce a fourth to join them for stealing horses
