# On receiving gold ore as security for duties: accompany proceedings of a meeting at City Hall. Resolutions: 1. Gov. peti

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n136–137** · Sav p. 136 · 1848-07-22 · Savage abstract · 

On receiving gold ore as security for duties: accompany proceedings of a meeting at City Hall. Resolutions: 1. Gov. petitioned to appoint one or more assayers to test quality of the gold taken from the placers at the Sacramento; 2. Gov. asked to extend the time allowed for redemption of gold dust deposited as collateral security for payt of duties, to 6 months, to allow time for import of curnas [current] money into the country; 3. Gov. requested to appoint a competent person to superintend the conversion of gold into ingots of convenient weights (Not Carried); 4. Appointment of a committee to petition Congress to establish a mint in this town (petition to be circulated in the Sacramento valley & elsewhere) — committee: C.V. Gillespie, Jas. C. Ward, W.D.M. Howard & Capt. Joseph L. Folsom (U.S. Army)
