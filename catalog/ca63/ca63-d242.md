# On organizing a govt for Cal.: apologizes he did not convey his exact meaning in conversation, wished to convey that if 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n140–142** · Sav p. 140 · 1848-11-25 · Savage abstract · 

On organizing a govt for Cal.: apologizes he did not convey his exact meaning in conversation, wished to convey that if on receipt of intelligence that Congress had adjourned last summer without organizing a territorial govt for Cal., the people should organize a temporary govt for themselves; that this intelligence (adjournment of Congress) was expected to be rec'd by the St. Mary's now due & daily expected
