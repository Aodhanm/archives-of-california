# Bound jointly & severally in sum of $5,000 to U.S. Authorities in Cal.; conditions of the obligation: 'If said de la Tor

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n131–132** · Sav p. 131 · 1848-02-05 · Savage abstract · 

Bound jointly & severally in sum of $5,000 to U.S. Authorities in Cal.; conditions of the obligation: 'If said de la Torre abstain during present war between U.S. & Mex. from taking any part, either by counseling, aiding or abetting, directly or indirectly in any revolutionary or other measures calculated to disturb the pub. quiet of Cal., then this obligation to be null & void, otherwise to be of full force & effect in law.' Witness to the bond W.E.P. Hartnell (a loyalty/good-behavior bond on a prominent Californio during the conquest)
