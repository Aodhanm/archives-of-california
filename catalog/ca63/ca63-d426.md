# THE SACRAMENTO-DISTRICT CENSUS TABLE (Spanish version of doc 187, w/ the actual figures): 'Statistics of Population &c o

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n306–307** · Sav p. 306 · 1847-12-20 · verbatim transcription · 

THE SACRAMENTO-DISTRICT CENSUS TABLE (Spanish version of doc 187, w/ the actual figures): 'Statistics of Population &c of the district including the country East of the San Joaquín & Sacramento Rivers, Upper California Dec 20th 1847' — Whites 218 M / 71 F / 289; Tame Indians or 'Neófitos' 306 / 173 / 479; Wild Indians or 'Gentiles' 11,224 / 10,649 / 21,873; Half-breed Indian children 3/7/10; Sandwich Islanders 4/1/5; Negroes 1/0/1; Grand Totals 11,756 / 10,901 / 22,657. Stock: cattle 20,000, horses 2,500, mules 70, sheep 2,000, hogs 1,000. Mills/buildings: 2 water + 3 horse flouring mills, 1 water saw-mill, 1 tannery, 60 dwelling houses (Fort Sacramento & outbuildings), 0 school houses
