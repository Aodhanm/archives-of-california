# Held a meeting & resolved to address the Gov., recommending John H. Nash be continued as Alcalde of the dist.; state tha

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n116** · Sav p. 116 · 1847-05 · Savage abstract · 

Held a meeting & resolved to address the Gov., recommending John H. Nash be continued as Alcalde of the dist.; state that those who asked for his removal have misinformed the Gov., prompted by personal animosity & without consulting pub. interests; that Nash was elected by a large majority, all satisfied with his official conduct, discharged the duties discreetly & faithfully (the pro-Nash counter-petition; Ide = Bear Flag 'president')
