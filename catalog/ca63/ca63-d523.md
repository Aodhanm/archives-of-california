# (3 notes) PRISONER-OF-WAR RELEASE REQUESTS: Silva, being a prisoner of war & of advanced age not permitting him to work,

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n398** · Sav p. 398 · 1847 · Savage abstract · 

(3 notes) PRISONER-OF-WAR RELEASE REQUESTS: Silva, being a prisoner of war & of advanced age not permitting him to work, asks to embark from one of the ports of the Mexican Republic; Sánchez, a prisoner of war, having made representations asks to embark for Mazatlán or San Blas, requesting his liberty to provide what is needed to go to his patria, & the document of being freed from prisoner status; + Castro (San Luis Obispo) received his passport & presents himself — ⭐ Mexican POWs of the conquest seeking repatriation
