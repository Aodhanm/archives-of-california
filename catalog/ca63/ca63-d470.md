# (Doctº en inglés) That Francisco Rico created suspicion of his intention to organize a party (in the late revolution) fo

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n341–342** · Sav p. 339 · 1847-03-14 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) That Francisco Rico created suspicion of his intention to organize a party (in the late revolution) for the purpose of joining the forces in the south; that he knows him to be one of their worst enemies, because he knows Rico to have done all in his power against the American cause — a Californio (Rico) flagged as a conquest-resistance organizer
