# (Doctº en inglés) DISTURBANCES between whites & Indians alarmingly frequent; bands of whites daily forming to seize & ki

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n305** · Sav p. 305 · 1849-04-24 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) DISTURBANCES between whites & Indians alarmingly frequent; bands of whites daily forming to seize & kill every Indian they meet, innocent or guilty, peaceful or turbulent; the Indians living near their property in great alarm, some having escaped from assassins beg protection; the whites who commit these disturbances are newcomers mainly from Oregon; among the aggressors is a true fury to assassinate Indians; 'el grito ahora es: 'exterminate the Indians, let there be a war of extermination'' (the actual extermination-cry recorded); sees the imperious necessity of immediately sending a sufficient force to protect the Indians — ⭐ a naming of the extermination rhetoric at its origin
