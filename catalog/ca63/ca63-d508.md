# (a) Pico orders the Mission [San Diego] & all its pertenencias delivered to Santiago Argüello by inventory (24 Jul 1846)

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n378–379** · Sav p. 376 · 1846-07-24 / 1847-04-26 / 1845-12-06 · Savage abstract · 

(a) Pico orders the Mission [San Diego] & all its pertenencias delivered to Santiago Argüello by inventory (24 Jul 1846); (b) Oliva names Estudillo Administrador of Mission San Diego during the padre's absence (26 Apr 1847); (c) ⭐ Pico's 6-Dec-1845 bill-of-sale of Mission San Juan Capistrano — 'dio título de venta á Juan Forster y Santiago Machado por compra que estos hicieron de la parte del edificio principal de la Mis. de S. Juan Capistrano y 3 huertas y muebles de ella, pagando á plazo $710 en moneda, cueros y sebo' — another documented Pico mission sale (cf. the antedated SD sale, doc 190)
