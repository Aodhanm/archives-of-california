# Arrest of Indian-murderers: reports Gov's orders carried out — Armijo, Smith ('Growling Smith') & John Egger have been a

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n90** · Sav p. 90 · 1847-08-15 · Savage abstract · 

Arrest of Indian-murderers: reports Gov's orders carried out — Armijo, Smith ('Growling Smith') & John Egger have been arrested, one at Sutter's, one at Sonoma, & the other here; some expenses already incurred, & if the prisoners are brought to trial, many more must be for attendance of witnesses/travelling expenses which must be paid on the spot; someone should be empowered to settle such accts without referring them first to Hd Quarters
