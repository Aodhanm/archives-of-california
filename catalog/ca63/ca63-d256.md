**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n159** · Sav p. 159 · 1848-08-09 · Savage abstract · 

Re condition of San Diego Mission: unless willing to remain long in charge of the mission (if left in present state the property will soon disappear), he thinks it should be at once sold & proceeds paid into the Civil Fund; the San Indians who claim support from the mission-property — if claim is just they might receive a desertion from the post, or as arranged, might be made with some citizen of San Diego to take a few cattle & maintain them during life; property worth something now, in a few months there will be nothing left
