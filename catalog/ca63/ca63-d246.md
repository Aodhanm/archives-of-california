# On regulating weights for gold: gold now almost entire currency in Cal., no law to regulate weights, much confusion; sen

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n143** · Sav p. 143 · 1848-07-31 · Savage abstract · 

On regulating weights for gold: gold now almost entire currency in Cal., no law to regulate weights, much confusion; sent to the mines, Yerba Buena & Sonoma to get the true weights; the weight got from Sonoma, the ounce weighs 13/16 more than a gold ounce or 16-dollar piece; the weight from the mines 6/16 more; Gov. should stop 'this enormous swindling machine and protect the poor laborer'; says he is receiving gold at the regular weight of 16 [dwt] to the ounce, 'others are taking it by a weight almost double as heavy'
