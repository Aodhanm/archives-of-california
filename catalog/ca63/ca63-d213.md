# Mr. Reed has entered upon his duties as Sheriff; Nash the former alcalde refuses to deliver the town records — gave up a

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n118–119** · Sav p. 118 · 1847-06-29 · Savage abstract · 

Mr. Reed has entered upon his duties as Sheriff; Nash the former alcalde refuses to deliver the town records — gave up a few but the important ones (without which the Court cannot act) he has concealed; neither Mr. Boggs nor Capt. Brackett the Commandt can do any more in the premises; Gov. will have to adopt measures & make such an example of Nash that others will hereafter be deterred; 'Nash places the Govr & all others in authority at defiance'
