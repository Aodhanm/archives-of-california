# On appt of Sub-Indian Agent & need of keeping military force in dist.: understands Capt. J.D. Hunter intends resigning a

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n174–175** · Sav p. 174 · 1848-09-03 · Savage abstract · 

On appt of Sub-Indian Agent & need of keeping military force in dist.: understands Capt. J.D. Hunter intends resigning as Sub-Indian Agent; fears if the force stationed here is removed there will be disturbances & raids from the Indians; the appt of a new Sub-Indian Agent urgently pressed by inhabitants, who recommend J.J. Warner — 'his probity, knowledge & long experience of the diff. Indian tribes on the frontier render him the proper man'
