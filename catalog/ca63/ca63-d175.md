# About Alcalde Hyde: referring to the recommendation sent to the Gov. by Fourgeaud, Howard, Leidesdorff & Jones claiming 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n81** · Sav p. 80 · 1848-03-20 · Savage abstract · 

About Alcalde Hyde: referring to the recommendation sent to the Gov. by Fourgeaud, Howard, Leidesdorff & Jones claiming to represent the town council for the removal of Alcalde Geo. Hyde, says he (Clark) is a member of that body, that the communication of those gentlemen does not express his views & is not in accordance with facts as developed in the evidence rec'd; there was no meeting of that body to investigate the charges since last Dec; the action of those 4 members was not public; Clark was not present & not informed of the matter; 'unwilling to be responsible for the acts of those gentlemen'
