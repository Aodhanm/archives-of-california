# Instructions: to require all idle Indians or other persons who have no masters or employers to find a home or employment

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n85–86** · Sav p. 85 · 1848-02-04 · Savage abstract · 

Instructions: to require all idle Indians or other persons who have no masters or employers to find a home or employment, or they must be arrested & set to labor for the public, or for such persons as will give them employment for their food & season of clothing; to promptly punish according to laws & customs of the country all thefts & violations of the peace & good order in the dist.; to establish his appointment in the part of the country over which his jurisdiction extends (the Stevenson labor/vagrancy regime for ex-mission Indians)
