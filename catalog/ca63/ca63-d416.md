# (Doctº en inglés) Acks the 21 Dec 1847 circular enclosing the 29 Nov proclamation, will post copies where liquor or wine

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n296–297** · Sav p. 295 · 1848-01-08 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) Acks the 21 Dec 1847 circular enclosing the 29 Nov proclamation, will post copies where liquor or wine is sold; sends a statistical account of the district population; some citizens hire Indians, some refuse to work & others kill cattle; had the recommendation published in the 'California Star' (11 Dec 1847); the Indians of this valley are more industrious than the Rocky-mountain ones & with direction could make good farmers & mechanics; had told Kearny he would present the case to the govt; in Sep 1846 John Sinclair was elected alcalde for one year & continues; the white population men/women/children = 289, about 100 living outside
