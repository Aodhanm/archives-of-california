# (Doctº en inglés) Having found A.M. Armijo sufficiently recovered to move here, returned him to jail; the affairs of his

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n313** · Sav p. 313 · 1847-09-20 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) Having found A.M. Armijo sufficiently recovered to move here, returned him to jail; the affairs of his rancho are in great confusion; hopes to be given authority to release him under good bond to the commissioners for whom the cause was postponed; 'dicho Armijo es persona altamente estimada de todos y un buen amigo del país' (Armijo highly esteemed) — the Cosumnes-massacre defendant's local standing
