# (Docts en inglés) re the imprisonment of Armijo: Brackett reports Antonio M. Armijo, one of those accused in the Sacrame

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n297–298** · Sav p. 297 · 1847-09-03→07 · Savage abstract · 

(Docts en inglés) re the imprisonment of Armijo: Brackett reports Antonio M. Armijo, one of those accused in the Sacramento killings, is gravely ill, cannot be moved without danger; per M.G. Vallejo the Indian-agent examined those with Armijo when he was jailed — all in interrogation said no Indian had been killed, only 11 prisoners taken of whom 4 escaped; Armijo had the alcalde's license to pursue Indians employed & found dead; Armijo has a son & wife also ill; at Vallejo's instance fixed a $1,000 bond that Armijo present himself to answer the accusation when recovered; acted so believing Col. Mason would have done the same
