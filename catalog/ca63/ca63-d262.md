# State that Estudillo's term as Alcalde has expired; the country extending north from the pueblo including the Contra Cos

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n170** · Sav p. 170 · 1847-03-10 · Savage abstract · 

State that Estudillo's term as Alcalde has expired; the country extending north from the pueblo including the Contra Costa & that bordering on the South Side of the bay & straits to the Valley of San Joaquín is destitute of a civil officer; recommend Elam Brown as competent for the position, having performed similar duties in Illinois
