# Reports arrest of Lieut. Canfield, Barras, Sergt. Myers & a man named Rockwell, the 1st three of the Mormon Volunteers, 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n144** · Sav p. 144 · 1848-04-04 · Savage abstract · 

Reports arrest of Lieut. Canfield, Barras, Sergt. Myers & a man named Rockwell, the 1st three of the Mormon Volunteers, for circulating counterfeit coin; charges preferred agt them; asks for a military commission to try them. (The first three finally tried by a special court of Alcalde Foster & Abel Stearns & a jury — Canfield & Myers acquitted, Barras convicted, sentenced to 5 yrs hard labor but recommended to Gov's mercy — cf. doc 111)
