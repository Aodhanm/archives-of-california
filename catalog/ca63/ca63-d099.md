# Petition for removal of the Alcalde: allege Wm. Blackburn unworthy of public confidence & unfit; assumed power to set as

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n22–24** · Sav p. 22 · 1848-04-28 · Savage abstract · 

Petition for removal of the Alcalde: allege Wm. Blackburn unworthy of public confidence & unfit; assumed power to set aside verdicts of juries; attempted to control the town council; arrogating superior powers; squandering public money; denying individuals the right of fair trial by object'g to jurors; neglecting to bring the guilty to trial after complaint made; oppression. Petition for his removal & appointment of James G. T. Dunleavy in his place
