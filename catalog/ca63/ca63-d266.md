# Effects existing in the mission this day rec'd by him from E.L. Brown: a lot of crockery, glass & other utensils, all in

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n173–174** · Sav p. 173 · 1848-08-06 · Savage abstract · 

Effects existing in the mission this day rec'd by him from E.L. Brown: a lot of crockery, glass & other utensils, all in poor condition; Animals: 23 mares, 6 horses, 13 colts, 1 mule colt, 38 goats, 6 young goats, 28 sheep, 5 young sheep, 34 cows, 17 calves, 2 bullocks, 7 jennies, 1 jack, 5 young jacks & jennies = 186 head altogether. According to E.L. Brown's statement on 12 June 1848 the total number was 107 head. (Mission San Diego secularized-property inventory under US military govt — cf. the Argüello/Stevenson threads)
