# Execution of an Indian criminal: Juan Antonio, a neophyte of San Gabriel, charged with many murders, burglary, theft, ai

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n52** · Sav p. 52 · 1849-05-13 · Savage abstract · 

Execution of an Indian criminal: Juan Antonio, a neophyte of San Gabriel, charged with many murders, burglary, theft, aiding & abetting horse-thieves of the [Tulares?], captured several times but escaped each time — had been a terror to the community during 15 years — was captured, tried by him & a jury of 6, convicted & sentenced to be hanged; execution took place on 10th; consolations of religion afforded, the curate accompanying him to the scaffold
