# On jurisdiction of the office of Alcalde & judicial powers of Alcaldes in general: doesn't know how far his jurisdiction

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n110–111** · Sav p. 110 · 1847-05-27 · Savage abstract · 

On jurisdiction of the office of Alcalde & judicial powers of Alcaldes in general: doesn't know how far his jurisdiction extends — on twelve days it embraces all the territory on this side of the bay & this side of the Sacramento river; that Bodega, San Rafael & Napa are under or subordinate to the Alcalde at Sonoma; would like to know if grand jury at Monterey thrown light upon; understands judicial authority in acting for debt extends only to $99 3/4 — frequent applications for larger sums; believes large claims should await establishment of proper tribunals; criminal causes — should the Constitution of the U.S. be considered in force here? — it requires indictment by a Grand Jury; no prison to keep prisoners in safe keeping
