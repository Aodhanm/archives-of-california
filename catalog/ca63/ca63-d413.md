# Cannot send the statistical census asked, for want of data & the difficulty that most rancho owners do not understand Ca

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n292–294** · Sav p. 292 · 1847-09 · Savage abstract · 

Cannot send the statistical census asked, for want of data & the difficulty that most rancho owners do not understand Castilian; rumors of war stirred by the Indians 25 leagues N of Sonoma; unable to understand the Indians who do not speak Spanish, asks that the Sub-agente de indios be excused (he had accepted only to keep peace with the Indians in Kearny's time); in present circumstances the whites cannot buy lands until the govt settles all definitively
