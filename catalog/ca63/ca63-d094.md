# Necessity of a Port Physician in San Francisco: last 2 months several contagious diseases introduced by vessels from abr

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n20–21** · Sav p. 20 · 1848-12-21 · Savage abstract · 

Necessity of a Port Physician in San Francisco: last 2 months several contagious diseases introduced by vessels from abroad, prevalent & often fatal; immense influx of foreigners from Pacific countries brings peculiar diseases — yellow fever & smallpox — yellow fever may propagate in SF; impossible to establish complete quarantine regs, but precautions needed; vessels with contagion should moor at some convenient point in bay; thinks a port physician should be appointed & would like to be it
