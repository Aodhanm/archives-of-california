# On formation of a new district with an Alcalde: petition to form a new dist. not to extend within 12 miles of Sonoma, wi

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n127–128** · Sav p. 127 · 1847-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

On formation of a new district with an Alcalde: petition to form a new dist. not to extend within 12 miles of Sonoma, with the natural boundary Napa river; describe Benicia, say settlement & business rapidly increasing, need of an alcalde; dist. to embrace: beginning at the embarcation at head of tide water on the Napa river, down to its mouth, thence up the Straits of Carquinez & the Suisun bay to mouth of the Sacramento, thence up to the head of the present dist. of Sonoma, up the Sacramento to head of the mountain, & to place of beginning incl. rancho of Captain Emery on the Napa river
