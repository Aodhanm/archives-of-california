# (Doctº en inglés) Reports that twelve jurors sentenced Mathew Fracer[Frazer], Wm. Campbell and David A. Davis to be hung

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n341–342** · Sav p. 339 · 1848-12-15 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) Reports that twelve jurors sentenced Mathew Fracer[Frazer], Wm. Campbell and David A. Davis to be hung for highway robbery and attempt to murder, the foregoing sentence approved by him & carried into execution accordingly, the prisoners having confessed on the gallows their guilt & other crimes; also asking Sherman's approval of the sentence — a jury capital-punishment execution in the early American period
