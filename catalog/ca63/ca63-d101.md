# Complaint against Pablo de la Guerra for having wrongfully imprisoned him in the guard-house merely because he complaine

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n25** · Sav p. 25 · 1848-04 · Savage abstract · 

Complaint against Pablo de la Guerra for having wrongfully imprisoned him in the guard-house merely because he complained; denied his authority to act as Alcalde, his term of service having expired on 1st day of January; there were no alcaldes exercising functions of such the two that had been elected, prior to the time when they should be inducted into office, notifying the public of their unwillingness to serve & never took the oath of office
