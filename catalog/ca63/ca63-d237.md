# Arrival of Pío and Andrés Pico: announces arrival of Don Pío Pico at San Juan Capistrano on Sunday last, no communicatio

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n135** · Sav p. 135 · 1848-07-12 · Savage abstract · 

Arrival of Pío and Andrés Pico: announces arrival of Don Pío Pico at San Juan Capistrano on Sunday last, no communications for you. Don Andrés Pico has called on him with a letter (copy enclosed) from the Mexn Gen. commanding the 'South' Mexn Dept, addressed to the Military Commandt in Cal., sent to Andrés as the last-known Mexn Commandt in the country. Andrés will inform this officer that this country is actually & peaceably in the possession of the American authorities, & that altho' he was the last Commandt, he is now not only without a Command, but by the treaty, with Frémont, is upon his parole & cannot assume the least direction or control of milit. matters
