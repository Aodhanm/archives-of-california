# Foxen's crime & complaint against Col. Stevenson's interference: Don [Benjamin?] Foxen, English, arrested for murder of 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n63** · Sav p. 63 · 1848-03-24 · Savage abstract · 

Foxen's crime & complaint against Col. Stevenson's interference: Don [Benjamin?] Foxen, English, arrested for murder of Agustín Dávila, a Mexican, on 22 March near Mission Sta Inés; delivered himself up to said magistrate & was turned over to Capt. Lippitt, Commandt, for safe keeping; inquires if Foxen is to be tried under Mexn law & procedure, or to have a trial by jury under Amn law. Reports that Col. Stevenson, commandt at Los Angeles, sent an armed force which arrested Ml. Anguisola[?], one of the inhabitants of Sta Bárbara, carrying him off to Los Angeles without giving any notice to the magistrate; wants to know if Col. Stevenson has Gov's sanction to order him (Carrillo) & his people, for he has sent an order to have Franco Badillo forwarded to him & that Badillo has refused to go
