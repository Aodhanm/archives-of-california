# Meeting of citizens: notify Gov. that when he issued his proclamation for election of officers the population was quite 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n47–49** · Sav p. 47 · 1849-09-20 · Savage abstract · 

Meeting of citizens: notify Gov. that when he issued his proclamation for election of officers the population was quite small (dry season) & the necessity not felt, no election; but since then it has become numerous & daily increasing from overland travel, & for preservation of peace & order public officers deemed indispensable; prompted by Gov's order, hope he will approve the resolutions: 1º Presidte appoints judges of an election to fix time & place within 5 days, somewhere in vicinity, to elect 2 Alcaldes & a Sub-Prefect for the district, held subject to Gov's ratification; 2º judges to certify results. Expect population of district to swell to thousands this winter; ask Gov. address his answer to Sacramento City
