# Party in favor of independence of Cal. from U.S.: says the persons opposed to Alcalde Hyde are open advocates of Cal. be

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n68** · Sav p. 68 · 1847-05-30 · Savage abstract · 

Party in favor of independence of Cal. from U.S.: says the persons opposed to Alcalde Hyde are open advocates of Cal. being an independent govt & not a territory of the U.S.; they are anxious to get possession of the office to execute their designs; that party assisted by the Mormon influence; must be watched, & he, Leidesdorff, will inform the Gov.
