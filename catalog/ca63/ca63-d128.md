# Need of funds for civil expenses: citizens after town have already voluntarily contributed $1,900 for prosecution of cri

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n49** · Sav p. 49 · 1849-10-25 · Savage abstract · 

Need of funds for civil expenses: citizens after town have already voluntarily contributed $1,900 for prosecution of criminals, hope to be re-imbursed by Gov. at some future time; their treasury now exhausted; citizens now paying for municipal regulation, incl. a large hospital & purchase of a prison ship latterly alone at ~$4,000; requests order to pay the City of Stockton at least $6,000 from civil fund; a portion of the civil fund should be placed in the district; corroborated by Geo. G. Belt (Judge of 1st Instance) & Salisbury Haley (Secy)
