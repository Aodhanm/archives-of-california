# Antº Suñol, Dolores Pacheco, José Noriega, José Fernández, Salvio Hanks[?], Wm. Fisher, Isaac Branham & Félix Buelna con

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n114** · Sav p. 114 · 1847-06-05 · Savage abstract · 

Antº Suñol, Dolores Pacheco, José Noriega, José Fernández, Salvio Hanks[?], Wm. Fisher, Isaac Branham & Félix Buelna constituting a Junta of the Pueblo; petition Gov. Mason to appoint at once an Alcalde, the present incumbent John Burton unwilling to continue serving; that a small force be stationed to uphold supremacy of the laws amid the constant disputes between foreigners & natives about horses & other property 'taken during the late war'; a corporal's guard may be enough
