# (Doctº en francés) Delesque re the San Juan Bautista mission vineyard, title given by Gov. Pío Pico, occupied & taken by

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n288–290** · Sav p. 287 · 1847-05 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en francés) Delesque re the San Juan Bautista mission vineyard, title given by Gov. Pío Pico, occupied & taken by the magistrate; property obtained in payment of a sum owed the Monterey troop by order of Comte Gral José Castro; + the Pedro Atallan / Pierre Attisson pension case: a French customs-house employee at Monterey, gravely wounded by Mexican soldiers 15 Mar 1844, granted a $25/mo pension by Gov. Micheltorena confirmed by 2 governors & 2 administrations for 3 years, now cut off at the change of govt — begs it be restored so he & his family may live
