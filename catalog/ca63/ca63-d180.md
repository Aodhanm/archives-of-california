# On the nature of civil govt in Cal.: says he fully agrees with Alcalde Walter Colton that as a military officer he (Sher

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n83–84** · Sav p. 83 · 1847-04-29 · Savage abstract · 

On the nature of civil govt in Cal.: says he fully agrees with Alcalde Walter Colton that as a military officer he (Sherman) is not amenable to his court for any act he may do in this country; 'the President's Message, Secretary of War's Report, and facts as they exist in the territory all show that the nature of this Govt is military, and that civil law only exists so far as it is consistent [with military govt]'
