# (Doctº en inglés) DISTURBANCES BETWEEN INDIANS & WHITES alarmingly frequent in the district; from particular observation

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n295–296** · Sav p. 294 · 1849-04-30 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) DISTURBANCES BETWEEN INDIANS & WHITES alarmingly frequent in the district; from particular observation of Indian affairs, inclined to believe the Indians do not by themselves cause disturbances — in most cases the whites are the originators & aggressors; the weakness & inoffensive condition of the Indians, of small physical strength, means they can only fight with wiles & traps; when Indians have committed a cruelty it was in revenge; whites working in the vicinity kill & scalp every Indian they meet; an Indian agent is important & only with prudence & gifts can accomplish anything; latterly made a small outlay in gifts; constant disturbances, assassinations on one side & massacres on the other — ⭐ a rare pro-Indian assessment of frontier violence by Sutter
