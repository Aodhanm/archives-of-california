# THE SAN JUAN BAUTISTA ELECTION-FRAUD + VAIL/WAIT LAND-DESPOLIATION AFFAIR: (a) the Comisión de Elecciones' queja against

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n363–366** · Sav p. 361 · 1849-02-04 → 11-30 · Savage abstract · 

THE SAN JUAN BAUTISTA ELECTION-FRAUD + VAIL/WAIT LAND-DESPOLIATION AFFAIR: (a) the Comisión de Elecciones' queja against the Juzgado of San Juan Bautista — the Juez José María Sánchez refused to accept the Governor's order to verify the elections, having made the Junta de vecinos convened for the purpose fail; the querella adds (Lehiya[?]) that Sánchez was found 'muy borracho junto con otros individuos también borrachos, en un lugar por donde pasaban Lehiya y sus compañeros'; the Comisión asks the sentencias of not having verified the elections be sent to the Governor; (b) ⭐⭐ Antonio Mª Pico, Prefecto (30 Nov 1849): 'Despojos de terrenos cometidos por el Medir[?] Wait/Vail' — several vecinos have suffered attacks & despojos in their properties (casas y terrenos) because the ex-Juez Wait formed a bogus 'Junta del Pueblo' with other American citizens & divided the pueblo lots into blocks (manzanas) of more than one league square, whose lands he took, dispossessing the vecinos & the mission of its most valuable lands; at the first junta only 4 or 5 vecinos of the country (Californians) & the rest Americans attended, & the act was written in English, so no one gave Wait authorization to redistribute lands; the Ayuntamiento's efforts to remedy met opposition; Pico names Francisco Vaca[?] as comisionado of the Prefectura to report fully — ⭐⭐ a documented American land-grab dispossessing Californios & the mission at San Juan Bautista, prosecuted by the Californio prefect
