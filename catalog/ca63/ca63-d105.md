# Withdrawing his resignation: thanks Gov. for confidence, promises best abilities; had sent his resignation 20 Aug, which

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n33** · Sav p. 33 · 1848-09-06 · Savage abstract · 

Withdrawing his resignation: thanks Gov. for confidence, promises best abilities; had sent his resignation 20 Aug, which Gov. had conferred on him in Dec 1847; 'My reason for resigning is simply this: With the abandonment of this post ends my employment as Govt interpreter, the salary of which is my only reliance for support of myself & family, and I am about entering on other business incompatible with the duties of an office or arduous as that of Alcalde of a district like this'; will continue discharging duties till Oct 1st
