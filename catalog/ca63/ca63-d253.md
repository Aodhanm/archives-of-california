# Appointment of alcaldes & auxiliary justices: filled the blank in the office of Alcalde for San Bernardino with the nami

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n156–157** · Sav p. 156 · 1848-02-08 · Savage abstract · 

Appointment of alcaldes & auxiliary justices: filled the blank in the office of Alcalde for San Bernardino with the naming of Antº Prieto, a Lower-Cal. of excellent character & friendly to U.S.; another person named for San Juan, character/fitness not yet certain — when satisfied on those points will appoint him; has appointed some auxiliary justices with limited powers to arrest criminals, punish suasions & others, upon suppressing sale of liquor to Indians under Gov's proclamation
