# Represent that they have been with the Hon. T.M. Leavenworth, Alcalde of the district, during the past 18 mos, one of th

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n66–67** · Sav p. 66 · 1849-05-16 · Savage abstract · 

Represent that they have been with the Hon. T.M. Leavenworth, Alcalde of the district, during the past 18 mos, one of the most difficult & embarrassing periods in the history of a newly acquiring territory; that his general character for integrity, prudence, intelligence, energy & promptness in duties is good; his suspension from office has surprised them; petition for its removal; & that he be again called upon to discharge his duties as Alcalde, 'as the legitimate authority of the district'
