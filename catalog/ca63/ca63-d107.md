# Election of Alcalde & execution of criminals: reports White having resigned, he was elected on the 12th & took charge; i

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n34** · Sav p. 34 · 1848-12-18 · Savage abstract · 

Election of Alcalde & execution of criminals: reports White having resigned, he was elected on the 12th & took charge; immediately caused 3 men charged with robbery & wounding another man with intent to kill to be arrested; on Saturday tried, evidence positive, convicted, '& will be hung today'; will make full report to Gov. stating 'why the sentence was so speedily executed'
