# (Doctº en inglés) HORSE-RAID: the Indians are stealing horses more than ever; on the 29th they stole 110 horses from Cap

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n299** · Sav p. 298 · 1847-09-02 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) HORSE-RAID: the Indians are stealing horses more than ever; on the 29th they stole 110 horses from Capt. Fisher's estancia, 11 in the pueblo, & 2 bands formed of ~200 horses; they have stolen the few horses he had, leaving him ruined; with difficulty found 7 men & 12 horses in bad shape to go after Capt. Fisher who separated from them & is believed dead or nearly so; hopes to be aided or will be completely ruined
