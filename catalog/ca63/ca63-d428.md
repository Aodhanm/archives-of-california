# (Doctº en inglés) THE ENCLOSED KELSEY CLEAR-LAKE REPORT (✅ re-verified): sends 2 'bucaroes'; much to do with the cattle,

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n310** · Sav p. 308 · 1848-11-27 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) THE ENCLOSED KELSEY CLEAR-LAKE REPORT (✅ re-verified): sends 2 'bucaroes'; much to do with the cattle, could only find 100 head & 23 oxen; on penning them found several with arrow-wounds; an Indian tells him the Indians are killing the cattle; go see the Indian agent about it; also found some cattle killed a mile from the house — ⚑ the Andrés Kelsey ranch at Clear Lake (the same Kelsey killed by Pomo in 1849 → the Bloody Island massacre precursor) [[project_indian_raiding]]
