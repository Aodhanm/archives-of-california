# Members of town council petition Gov. Mason to remove George Hyde from office of Alcalde & appoint Dr. John Townsend & J

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n7** · Sav p. 7 · 1846-03-10 / 1848-03-18 · Savage abstract · 

Members of town council petition Gov. Mason to remove George Hyde from office of Alcalde & appoint Dr. John Townsend & John C. Buchanan. (Mar 18) E.P. Jones & W.A. Leidesdorff, two council members, ask for the removal of Ex-Alcalde Hyde from office on charges preferred agt him — Hyde on the 11th had applied for leave, speaking of secret proceedings agt him
