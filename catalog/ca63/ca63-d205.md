# Vallejo seems to be the only person competent for making titles to lots in the town of Sonoma, the site already granted 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n113** · Sav p. 113 · 1847-05[?] · Savage abstract · 

Vallejo seems to be the only person competent for making titles to lots in the town of Sonoma, the site already granted & conferred by the Mexn Govt to the inhabitants of the pueblo; Nash, as Alcalde, has told lots & rec'd money for them & is not censured; recommends a Receiver of pub. money of the town be named & placed under bond, & that Nash be made to designate the pub. funds
