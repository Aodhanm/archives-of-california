# Sentence of a reo: yesterday sentenced M. Guild for robbery committed at the casa-aduana to receive 30 lashes on the bar

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n35** · Sav p. 35 · 1848-12-09 · Savage abstract · 

Sentence of a reo: yesterday sentenced M. Guild for robbery committed at the casa-aduana to receive 30 lashes on the bare back; the reo begged commutation, denied for want of authority in the juez; then begged suspension of execution till today; various persons interceded, among them Col. Burton & Lieut. Ord, who assure Guild had good conduct before, cite his long imprisonment & loss of his small interests, and that Guild is not a thief by profession, his crime the effect of liquor; recommends him to the Gov., 'único tribunal en quien ha facultad pª absolverlo si lo creyere posible'
