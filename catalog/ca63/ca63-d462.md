# (Doctº en inglés) ORDERING that every rancho send one or two good men well mounted with spare horses to unite with the U

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n330–331** · Sav p. 328 · 1847-04-10 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) ORDERING that every rancho send one or two good men well mounted with spare horses to unite with the U.S. troops at this place under Lieut. Col. Cooke, the military commandant of this district, for the purpose of repelling & destroying the Indians who are now committing such extensive depredations — a mounted-Californio levy against Indian raiders under Cooke
