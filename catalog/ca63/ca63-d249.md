# On armed opposition in Sonoma & Northern Cal.: courts as at present constituted bring justice into disrepute; the old ma

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n145** · Sav p. 145 · 1847-03[?] · Savage abstract · 

On armed opposition in Sonoma & Northern Cal.: courts as at present constituted bring justice into disrepute; the old man now acting as Alcalde (Nash) ignorant, conceited & dogmatical, governed by whims, without regard to law; a runaway sailor by the name of Green has obtained a strong influence over him; the present unlimited & illegal powers assumed then the country by the diff. Alcaldes will give rise to much confusion & litigation; will continue acting on events & all matters connected with Gen. Kearny's administration in Cal.
