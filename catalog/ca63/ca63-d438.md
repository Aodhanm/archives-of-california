# (Doctº en inglés) Has learned of the departure of the Company destined to pursue the Indians who are stealing so much ca

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n316** · Sav p. 316 · 1847-05-04 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) Has learned of the departure of the Company destined to pursue the Indians who are stealing so much cattle & horses, alive & dead; Boggs, in charge of the alcaldía, still meets resistance from the one who should hand it over [Nash]; this latter has delivered no doc, having gone to Yerba Buena to embark by an express to Washington to obtain the nullification of his removal (by Gen. Kearny); awaits his return, & then 'quizás, entregará el archivo' (the archive-handover standoff)
