# Petition Gov. to order an election for two members of the Common Council to fill vacancies occasioned by resignation of 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n158** · Sav p. 158 · 1848-09-08 · Savage abstract · 

Petition Gov. to order an election for two members of the Common Council to fill vacancies occasioned by resignation of Glover & death of Leidesdorff; Council now reduced to 4, held no meeting for want of quorum; it would be well members could have authority to preside as Justices of the Peace; also necessary to appoint a harbor master; recommend appt of 3 Port-Wardens to choose Harbor masters & examine such persons as desire to serve as pilots — Collector of Port to be ex-officio member of the Board of Port Wardens
