# (Doctº en inglés) THE LEIDESDORFF-ESTATE ADMINISTRATION: requires the exhibition of inventories & appraisement directed 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n351–353** · Sav p. 349 · 1848-04-30 → 1848 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) THE LEIDESDORFF-ESTATE ADMINISTRATION: requires the exhibition of inventories & appraisement directed by the Governor (per instructions in the Alcalde's office), 'to put the Estate into the hands of competent & safe men who should be required to give bond & good security in at least double the amount of the estimated value... inventories & appraisements of every species of property of the deceased [made] under oath by two competent & respectable persons appointed by your court & deposited in your office' — the administration of the wealthy SF pioneer W.A. Leidesdorff's estate
