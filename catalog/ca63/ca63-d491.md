# THE MAJORS NATURALIZATION PROCEEDINGS: 'Naturalización de ciudadano americano y diligencias que se practicaban para obte

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n359** · Sav p. 357 · 1837-05-21 · Savage abstract · 

THE MAJORS NATURALIZATION PROCEEDINGS: 'Naturalización de ciudadano americano y diligencias que se practicaban para obtenerla en California' — in the case of Majors, native of the United States, the following diligencias are noted: (1) Petition of the interested party to the judge of his residence; (2) Providencia of the judge ordering the party present 3 witnesses declaring on the nation he is of, being Catholic, of good conduct, having an office or occupation producing 1,000 pesos monthly[sic]; (3) Declarations of 3 witnesses; (4) Providencia ordering the diligencias pass to the Governor; (5) Petition of the interested party to the Governor enclosing the diligencias — ⭐⭐ a documented template of pre-conquest Mexican naturalization procedure for an American settler (a stray 1837 doc in the loose collection) ⚠ CONTINUES next leaf
