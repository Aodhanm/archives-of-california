# Indian depredations: took a stroll by the Contra Costa of SF, having heard great complaints agt the Indians; conversed w

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n168–170** · Sav p. 168 · 1847-04-25 · Savage abstract · 

Indian depredations: took a stroll by the Contra Costa of SF, having heard great complaints agt the Indians; conversed with the Chief Magistrate of the dist., Don Joaquín Estudillo, found him anxious to do all in his power to check them; advised the Gov. on the best means of procuring Native-Cal. volunteers to accompany the troops; the wild Indians committing raids carrying off horses; sent after them, were seen, but succeeded in escaping — want of horses prevents them being pursued
