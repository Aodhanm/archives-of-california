# Sufferings & complaints: relates a long story of wrongs suffered at the hands of natives & even at the hands of the Amn 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n104** · Sav p. 104 · 1847-05-03 · Savage abstract · 

Sufferings & complaints: relates a long story of wrongs suffered at the hands of natives & even at the hands of the Amn authorities in return for reliable information furnished from time to time on the state of affairs in dist., & for other services of a mechanical nature; complains of Burton the alcalde, formerly a run-away sailor, who has by false decisions & false imprisonment made him suffer; concludes: 'I am clear of opinion now, if we had continued the Bear Flag we would now be in better condition in this vicinity'
