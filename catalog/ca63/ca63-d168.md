# Municipal revenue & expenses: amt collected 1st 4 months of the year $173 from duties imposed by a territorial law on st

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n71–72** · Sav p. 72 · 1848-05-17 · Savage abstract · 

Municipal revenue & expenses: amt collected 1st 4 months of the year $173 from duties imposed by a territorial law on stores, taverns, billiard tables & liquors; fines imposed by magistrates for petty offences ~$25/mo, total $868/yr[?]; expenses necessary ~$135/mo (rent of court rooms, salaries of jailor/alguacil/clerk/superintendent of prisoners in pub. works, paper, prison irons, repairs on jail), leaving a deficit of $67/mo which must be covered by some tax on people of district
