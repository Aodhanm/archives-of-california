# Election of Alcaldes: under instructions of Col. Mason, in absence of Sta Bárbara, met on 25 Sept for purpose of electin

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n123–124** · Sav p. 123 · 1847-05-26 · Savage abstract · 

Election of Alcaldes: under instructions of Col. Mason, in absence of Sta Bárbara, met on 25 Sept for purpose of electing persons to receive the votes & to be the judges of an election held in Sta Bárbara on the 2d May 1847 for the purpose of choosing 7 electors; the 7 electors chosen on 2 May, subsequent Sunday they elected Pablo de la Guerra (1º Alcalde) & Luis Carrillo (2º Alcalde); 'there has been considerable feeling manifested by the Alcaldes respecting their oath of office'; for want of a proper person to administer the oath, it has not been administered either as an oath to Am. or Mexn law; Guerra called the people of Sta Bárbara together and swore before them to administer justice according best of his ability, & required same oath from the 1º Alcalde; 'when I offer the appt to sign by you… they decline them, saying that by the Mexn laws the Alcaldes rec'd their appt from the [people]'
