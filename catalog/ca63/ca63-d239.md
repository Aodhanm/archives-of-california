# On divorces: 'Neither he nor any alcalde possess any authority to grant a divorce… If your husband has abandoned you and

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n137** · Sav p. 137 · 1847-12-08 · Savage abstract · 

On divorces: 'Neither he nor any alcalde possess any authority to grant a divorce… If your husband has abandoned you and left the country with no intention of returning and without providing you with any means or resources whatever, I am of opinion, there being no law in Cal. that I can either find or hear of on the subject of divorces, that he should be viewed as tho' he was dead so far as you are concerned'
