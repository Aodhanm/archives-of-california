# (Doctº en inglés) Received the letter saying the Indian Gerónimo is to be interrogated at Los Angeles; since the offense

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n312** · Sav p. 312 · 1848-05-30 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) Received the letter saying the Indian Gerónimo is to be interrogated at Los Angeles; since the offense happened in the San Diego district, believes the interrogation should be done there, so sent the prisoner to that alcalde's disposal; to examine him at LA would need transport costs for witnesses from San Diego, San Luis & Pala, 'y no hay en la tesorería ni un 'farthing'' (the empty-treasury line)
