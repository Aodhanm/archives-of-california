# A committee of 5 (H.A. Green, J.H. Nash, M.G. Vallejo, Capt. Grigsby, M.E. McIntosh) to lay before Kearny resolutions as

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n138–140** · Sav p. 138 · 1847-02-26 · Savage abstract · 

A committee of 5 (H.A. Green, J.H. Nash, M.G. Vallejo, Capt. Grigsby, M.E. McIntosh) to lay before Kearny resolutions asking that the North side of the Bay of SF be given a fair & equal representation in the proposed legislative Council (at least ⅕ of the whole white people of Cal. reside N. of the bay, & ⅔ of the volunteers engaged in the late contest); recommend the council be increased to 15 members & 5 be given the North side of the bay, with right of electing them; present names of Gen. M.G. Vallejo, L.W. Boggs, Capt. John Grigsby, Stephen Cooper, Capt. Richardson as suitable persons
