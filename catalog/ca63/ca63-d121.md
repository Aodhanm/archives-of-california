# Horse-stealing: reports horse-stealing going on strongly in his district during last week; Antº Suñol lost 50 horses, th

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n46–47** · Sav p. 46 · 1848-01-29 · Savage abstract · 

Horse-stealing: reports horse-stealing going on strongly in his district during last week; Antº Suñol lost 50 horses, the Bernals & Galindos as many more; suspected the robbers were attached to Indians, but couldn't be ascertained; has now more facts worthy of notice that two Bernals were attacked by 6 foreigners armed with rifles — the Bernals fled & were pursued
