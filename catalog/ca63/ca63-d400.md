# THE STA CLARA/SAN JOSÉ MISSION-SALE CONFLICT DOSSIER (running Spanish correspondence, ~9 docs): Padre Real → Gob. re the

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n270–276** · Sav p. 268 · 1848–1849 · Savage abstract · 

THE STA CLARA/SAN JOSÉ MISSION-SALE CONFLICT DOSSIER (running Spanish correspondence, ~9 docs): Padre Real → Gob. re the mission-property despoliation & his refusal to recognize the sale; Alcalde Antº Mª Pico forwarding the indígenas de Sta Clara's protest against the sale of Mission property (⭐ the Indian voice, 'reclamando la destrucción de sus intereses y salían se hallaban vendidas'); the Steinberger lease dispute; Fr. Antº Mª Pico → Fr. Real (July 23 1849) 'prohibiendo terminantemente cualquier contrato de venta relativa á los bienes de las Misiones de San José y Sta Clara'; Padre Real replies he is responsible for his acts before the exclaustration; ⭐ Forbes certification (n272): 6 certificados firmados por James Alex. Forbes, Talbot de la Cruz, José Tiña, Ygnº Alviso, Joaquín G. Castro y Cárlos Mª Osio para comprobar la residencia de las personas á cuyo favor fueron expedidos los títulos de las Huertas á las Misiones de San José y Sta Clara; the padre missionero de San Juan participating with Sta Cruz missions; all one continuous secularization-endgame dispute (already thematically captured in docs 167/382/386–388 — these are the fuller running texts)
