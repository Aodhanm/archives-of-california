# Pardon: by virtue of his power, 'and in the intention of contributing to the tranquillity of the territory by the return

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n133–134** · Sav p. 133 · 1847-02-26 · Savage abstract · 

Pardon: by virtue of his power, 'and in the intention of contributing to the tranquillity of the territory by the return of its citizens to their families and homes,' grants a full pardon to said Alvarado for any acts committed during the present hostilities agt the Govt of the U.S., & further assures him free passage & entire security of property & person
