# Protest agt the admission of James F. Reed and John Murphy as members of town council, on the plea that they were not el

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n120–121** · Sav p. 120 · 1847-11-16 · Savage abstract · 

Protest agt the admission of James F. Reed and John Murphy as members of town council, on the plea that they were not elected by any considerable vote, most voters having taken no part; that they are not residents, have no avocation except gambling; & Reed acknowledged killing a man, says self-defence which may or may not be true — he has not undergone a trial; that James W. Weekes, appointed Alcalde for the dist., is entirely unfit; abstain from bringing charges agt him unless not removed
