# (Doctº en inglés) PRIVATE GOLD-COIN MINT REQUEST: asking permission to issue gold coin of the denomination of $10 & $5; 

**C-A 63 (Miscellaneous / Unbound Documents), scan n326–327** · Sav p. 324 · 1849-08-04 · Savage abstract · 

(Doctº en inglés) PRIVATE GOLD-COIN MINT REQUEST: asking permission to issue gold coin of the denomination of $10 & $5; willing to give bonds to any amount required by the collector of this port, that their coin shall be of equal (if not more) value than the coin of the U.S. Mint; they want their coin to be receivable in payment of duties; object to afford a circulating medium for business purposes in this country — an early gold-rush private-coinage petition
