# major dispatch (topic-header: Tulares; fleet; Dept. commerce; American-adventurer & wild-Indian threats; the Alvarado–Va

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n351–n358** · Sav p. XIII·6–13 · tomo p. XIII·16–23 · 1842-01-11 · Savage abstract ·
