# modifies the *bando* for the expedition against the Indians — (1) instead of Mission San Fernando they assemble on the 1

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n482** · Sav p. XIV·37 · tomo p. XIV·131–133 · 1845-06-03 · Savage abstract · 

modifies the *bando* for the expedition against the Indians — (1) instead of Mission San Fernando they assemble on the 10th at Sta Bárbara before the judges; by accord with the Military Cmdr arrange the manner of the expedition and 'obligar á los comprometidos á su cumplimiento' [compel the enrolled to fulfill it]; the commander to be a man 'de carácter y representación'; make the 'descontentos con la expedición' understand the Govt's aim is not to abuse authority but 'por medios pasivos… evitar el perjuicio qe les causan los indios en sus propiedades'
