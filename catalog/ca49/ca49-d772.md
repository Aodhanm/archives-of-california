# 'Indios = Compañía de la Colombia' — (1) approves stopping the San José Indians from carrying off gentile women; no arme

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n284** · Sav p. XII·1 · tomo p. XII·2–4 · 1841-01-04 · Savage abstract · 

'Indios = Compañía de la Colombia' — (1) approves stopping the San José Indians from carrying off gentile women; no armed parties to leave San José without permission; (2) on the HBC brigade ('Compañía de Colombia') under Michel [Laframboise], use 'la mejor política sin hostilizarlos' to ease them out pending govt decision
