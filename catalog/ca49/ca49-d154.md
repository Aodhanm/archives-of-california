# Sharp personal rebuke replying to Bandini's 7 Aug oficio — dismisses his complaints as attacks on the governor's *decoro

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n43–n45** · Sav p. 44–46 · tomo p. 91–93 · 1831-09-07 · Savage abstract · 

Sharp personal rebuke replying to Bandini's 7 Aug oficio — dismisses his complaints as attacks on the governor's *decoro*; accuses him of self-interest in 'el Comercio federal' and of sustaining 'los escandalosos desórdenes en que ha estado la administración'; jabs that Bandini's *hermano político* profited from a 'protección pública' that produced only 'la ruina de abusos'; ties it to Bandini's conduct as administrator of the Aduana (customs) at San Diego, then under investigation; refuses further engagement (see Entry 2)
