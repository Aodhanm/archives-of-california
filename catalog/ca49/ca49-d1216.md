# the 1845 US-war scare & militia mobilization — the Comandante General's note (6 Aug) reports that, per an official dispa

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n490** · Sav p. XIV·45–46 · tomo p. XIV·154–156 · 1845-08-06 / 08-13 / 08-19 / 08-23 · Savage abstract · 

the 1845 US-war scare & militia mobilization — the Comandante General's note (6 Aug) reports that, per an official dispatch received via a warship that arrived at Monterey, war had probably been declared by Mexico on the United States 'á virtud de la agregación de Texas'; laid before the Assembly in extraordinary session, which approved (consultative) the articles of 13 Aug: Art. 1 the Governor to 'arreglar y poner sobre las armas las milicias auxiliares,' 'excitar á los Ciudadanos á la defensa de la patria… hasta el grado de poner á cubierto el país de toda clase de enemigos' (see the acta of 13 Aug); + (23 Aug) approval of the committee report on a note of José Antº Carrillo (1er Ministro)
