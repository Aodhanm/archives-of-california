# the Assembly's initiatives to Mexico — (14 Jul) asks Congress for 'leyes excepcionales para gobernar el Departamento'; (

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n489** · Sav p. XIV·44 · tomo p. XIV·152–154 · 1845-07-14 / 07-26 / 08-18 · Savage abstract · 

the Assembly's initiatives to Mexico — (14 Jul) asks Congress for 'leyes excepcionales para gobernar el Departamento'; (26 Jul, approved 25th under the Bases Orgánicas) seeks to adopt the *sistema Federal*, 'quitando á la Carta de 824 la ingerencia qe le daba al poder central en lo peculiar de los Estados' and to make reforms 'para afianzar las garantías individuales' — the Californios' constitutional-autonomy / federalist program; + indemnification for lands the authority took from Leonardo Cota
