# 'Incursión de aventureros' — ~200–300 persons entered Los Angeles from New Mexico (mostly armed men + 11 'hijos del país

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n361–n363** · Sav p. XIII·16–18 · tomo p. XIII·27–28 · 1842-06-02 · Savage abstract · 

'Incursión de aventureros' — ~200–300 persons entered Los Angeles from New Mexico (mostly armed men + 11 'hijos del país,' all with the NM governor's passports — the Rowland–Workman party, come to trade textiles for livestock via the Old Spanish Trail); ⭐ a 50-man American beaver-trapping party under 'Smith' on the Gila–Colorado; many intend to bring families & settle permanently
