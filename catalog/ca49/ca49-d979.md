# 'Abandono de Ross por los Rusos. Desavenencias entre el Gobernador y la Comandancia General' — recaps the Russian abando

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n363–n364** · Sav p. XIII·18–19 · tomo p. XIII·28–30 · 1842-06-03 · Savage abstract · 

'Abandono de Ross por los Rusos. Desavenencias entre el Gobernador y la Comandancia General' — recaps the Russian abandonment; his plan to place a troop detachment to fly the Mexican flag & assert the boundary to the 42nd parallel (long held by the Russians); the Vallejo rift (no reply from him); vows to bar anyone from acquiring rights in the Ross possessions (despite Sutter's purchase)
