# the mission-inventory commission's object is expressly 'que se proceda á su *arrendamiento*' (the LEASE of the Missions)

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n499** · Sav p. XIV·54 · tomo p. XIV·183–184 · 1845-07-01 · Savage abstract · 

the mission-inventory commission's object is expressly 'que se proceda á su *arrendamiento*' (the LEASE of the Missions) — it will begin at San Gabriel and continue with the others toward the North; the Govt 'no puede derogar las disposiciones… sobre arrendamiento de Misiones' and expects the friar-president's obedience
