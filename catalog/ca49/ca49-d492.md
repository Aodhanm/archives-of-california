# 1839 reports bundle — ⭐ 'la revolución de California se concluyó'; the division into 3 districts & prefect appointments 

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n187–n188** · Sav p. 32–33 · tomo p. X·169–191 · 1839 · Savage abstract · 

1839 reports bundle — ⭐ 'la revolución de California se concluyó'; the division into 3 districts & prefect appointments (José Castro 1st, Cosme Peña 2nd, Luis del Castillo Negrete 3rd/Baja); provisionally established the Alta California Sub-Comisaría; ⭐ asks the Pious Fund (fondos piadosos) be used for religious utility; ⭐ notes the 1st Bishop of California, Fr. Francisco García Diego, named (took possession 24 Nov); ⭐ the principal SF-port fortification to be on Angel Island (Isla de los Ángeles)
