# the Govt, unable to organize a suitable force to pursue the Indians in their frequent raids ('las frecuentes excursiones

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n475** · Sav p. XIV·31 · tomo p. XIV·113–116 · 1845-04-13/22 · Savage abstract · 

the Govt, unable to organize a suitable force to pursue the Indians in their frequent raids ('las frecuentes excursiones qe hacen'), resolves to solicit the opinion of the harassed *rancherías*' residents on whether they can lend assistance, and on that basis organize a formal expedition; + Antº Botiller's fine complaint; a matter suspended pending the BC-frontier note
