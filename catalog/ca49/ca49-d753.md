# the full Graham-Affair narrative to Mexico: a private denunciation → Castro ordered to act in the 1st District → a force

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n272–n273** · Sav p. XI·67–68 · tomo p. XI·222–223 · 1840-04-22 · Savage abstract · 

the full Graham-Affair narrative to Mexico: a private denunciation → Castro ordered to act in the 1st District → a force of trusted vecinos rounds up the (chiefly unmarried) illegally-entered foreigners → ringleaders apprehended, Tribunal investigation → ~118 foreigners embarked 22 Mar 1840 for San Blas (ship chartered $4,000; cf. #729), remitted to the Supreme Govt for lack of a consul/warship, guarded by José Castro
