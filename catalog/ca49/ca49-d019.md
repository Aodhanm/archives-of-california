# Horse-theft network (cont. n6): parties from New Mexico entering the territory trade with *gentiles*, fugitive Christian

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n5** · Sav p. 6 · tomo p. 12–14 · 1831-03-10 · Savage abstract · 

Horse-theft network (cont. n6): parties from New Mexico entering the territory trade with *gentiles*, fugitive Christians & mission neophytes in the mountains/Tulares, who steal mission & rancho horse-herds to sell to the New Mexicans who drive them off to their lands; recent repeated thefts via San Gabriel/Los Ángeles & at Mission Soledad
