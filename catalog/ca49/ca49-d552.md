# 'Conspiración de extranjeros' = the 1840 GRAHAM AFFAIR — the Governor's 8 Apr alarm that resident foreigners intend to c

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n214–n215** · Sav p. XI·9–10 · tomo p. XI·58–61 · 1840-04-07 · Savage abstract · 

'Conspiración de extranjeros' = the 1840 GRAHAM AFFAIR — the Governor's 8 Apr alarm that resident foreigners intend to conspire; José Castro to round up all illegally-introduced foreigners (except the married/settled/employed), the northern forces mobilized, deportees embarked on Aguirre's national ship, all 'con consulta y aprobación de la Diputación'; ⭐ Isaac Graham ('Capitán de los Rifleros') to be apprehended, 'con pérdida de 1 hombre'; Tapia (w/ Capt. Villa) to seize the foreigners of his district & hold them till the ship collects them
