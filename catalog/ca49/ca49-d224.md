# 'Ley sobre Administración de Misiones' — Echeandía's full secularization reglamento, arts. 1–33 (complete; see Entry 3):

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n65–n76** · Sav p. 66–77 · tomo p. 150–168 · 1831[?]-01-06 · verbatim transcription · 

'Ley sobre Administración de Misiones' — Echeandía's full secularization reglamento, arts. 1–33 (complete; see Entry 3): missions→pueblos per the 1813 Cortes law; San Gabriel & San Carlos first (elected *comisarios de policía*), then art. 24 extends it to all remaining missions; detailed land/livestock/tool *repartimiento* to neophytes, 5-yr inalienability, padrón, mission buildings→school/hospital/jail/ayuntamiento, per-pueblo administrators/mayordomos, friar stipends ($1,000 SG / $600 Carmelo); arts. 29–32 = two indigenous *pupilaje* training schools (San Gabriel for the southern missions, Carmelo for the northern; 4 natives >18 per mission; teacher stipends per pupil); signed Echeandía / por ausencia del secretario José Mª Padrés
