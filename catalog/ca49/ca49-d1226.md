# report on Híjar's arrival — reached Santa Bárbara 8 June, Los Angeles 21 June 1845 — as the Supreme Govt's Commissioner 

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n500** · Sav p. XIV·55–56 · tomo p. XIV·185–188 · 1845-07-01 · Savage abstract · 

report on Híjar's arrival — reached Santa Bárbara 8 June, Los Angeles 21 June 1845 — as the Supreme Govt's Commissioner 'para procurar el restablecimiento del orden constitucional'; his 11 Apr appointment publicized in the capital 'con salvas de Artillería, repiques de Campanas y otras demostraciones públicas,' the 'Californios oían la voz paternal del Supremo Magistrado de la República'; frames the disorder as caused by the 'conducta criminal de las tropas del Sr Gral Micheltorena'
