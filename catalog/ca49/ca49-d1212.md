# the constitutional prelude to the revolt — the 'desórdenes' obliged Pico to convene the Assembly, which met at LA on 28 

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n487** · Sav p. XIV·42 · tomo p. XIV·147–149 · 1845-02-01/05 · Savage abstract · 

the constitutional prelude to the revolt — the 'desórdenes' obliged Pico to convene the Assembly, which met at LA on 28 Jan and issued dispositions (copy enclosed) for Micheltorena to help fulfill, 'porque de otra manera el país será envuelto en grandes males,' as augured by the 'reuniones de gente qe de diversos puntos del país ocurre á reunirse en los Angeles, formando fuerzas armadas, cuyas intenciones no se pueden saber'; + a decree from the Assembly's last session
