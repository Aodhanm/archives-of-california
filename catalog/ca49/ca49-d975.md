# 'Incursión de aventureros armados' — the Missouri party's total = 60 (≈30 continued to the Columbia/Oregon, ≈30 stayed);

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n358–n360** · Sav p. XIII·13–15 · tomo p. XIII·23–25 · 1842-01-11 · Savage abstract · 

'Incursión de aventureros armados' — the Missouri party's total = 60 (≈30 continued to the Columbia/Oregon, ≈30 stayed); ⭐⭐⭐ a second party of 60–80 armed men approaching from New Mexico (the Rowland–Workman party via the Old Spanish Trail); orders Lt. Col. José Castro out with volunteers to intercept it; begs Mexico for 150–200 soldiers (dup. pp.33–35)
