# Victoria's connected narrative of the anti-Victoria conspiracy (opening on the secularization 'plan de reducir á pueblos

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n150–n153** · Sav p. 150–153 · tomo p. 382–385 · 1831-09-21 · Savage abstract · 

Victoria's connected narrative of the anti-Victoria conspiracy (opening on the secularization 'plan de reducir á pueblos las misiones') — casts José Mª Padrés as 'autor de todos los males,' scheming via clandestine juntas during Victoria's 20-day Santa Bárbara delay; 'sedujo' Alférez Mariano Guadalupe Vallejo & Sgt José Sánchez into insubordination; exploited news of Guerrero's return & the rumor Victoria would be removed by an 'intruso' govt to make 'la revolución'; 'flattered Echeandía with the [return of] command — esta es la verdadera causa de su detención en San Diego'; the plotters kept pushing to convene the Diputación
