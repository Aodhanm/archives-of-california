# [= Entry 1] report on the Russian establishments — refuses the Russian otter-hunting contract ('Ulbrikoff'[?]); praises 

**C-A 49 (Departmental Records), scan n120–n121** · Sav p. 121–122 · tomo p. 296–300 · 1831-04-13 · verbatim transcription · 

[= Entry 1] report on the Russian establishments — refuses the Russian otter-hunting contract ('Ulbrikoff'[?]); praises the Russian colonists as 'buena gente' who buy from the country, vs. the 'North Americans' whose beaver-hunting convoys 'han destruido completamente' the rivers; Bodega colony 'en regular fomento'; Ross surrounded by hostile tribes, dependent on its ships; declined the Ross governor's gift of 'old cups'
