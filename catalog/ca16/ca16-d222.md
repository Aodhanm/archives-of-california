# *Ejecución de una sentencia* — soldier José Antonio Rosas (age 18, of the Pueblo de Los Ángeles) executed for bestiality

**C-A 16 (Provincial State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n136** · Sav p. 137 · tomo p. 490–533 · 1801-02-11 · Savage abstract · 

*Ejecución de una sentencia* — soldier José Antonio Rosas (age 18, of the Pueblo de Los Ángeles) executed for bestiality (with a mule) committed 20 June 1800 at Mission San Buenaventura, seen by Indians; sentenced by the Auditor de Guerra to be hanged & his body burned with the mule — no hangman, so shot then burned; prosecuted by alférez Pablo de Cota (fiscal), defended by Sgt José Ortega; case sent to México; convicted & confessed (the original process fills these pages)
