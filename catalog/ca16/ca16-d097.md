# ⭐⭐⭐ *Sobre castigo de indios* — on the two San Francisco missionaries' opposed views; counsels moderation, that punishme

**C-A 16 (Ben. Mil.), scan n59–61** · tomo p. 325–6 · Sav p. 60–62 · 1796-08-11 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ *Sobre castigo de indios* — on the two San Francisco missionaries' opposed views; counsels moderation, that punishment or imprisonment require both friars' consent (never one alone), and orders a formal inquiry in which the company's officers, sgts, cabos & soldiers of the SF escort declare what they know of the Indians' treatment, labor, and the punishments given and why (see expanded entry)
