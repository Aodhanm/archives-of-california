**C-A 16 (Ben. Mil.), scan n125–126** · tomo p. 269–70 · Sav p. 126–127 · 1800-12-31 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ *Descripción del presidio* — physical condition report (see expanded entry): a 110-vara square of stone & tile-roofed adobe; N wall = main gate, guard-room, 2 large storerooms (two threaten collapse, a wall cracked); W wall = Governor's house (sala, 8 rooms, kitchen), barracks, officers' & surgeon's houses, all built on walls burned in the 1789 fire; S wall = 9 troop-family rooms + the church (in the middle, needs repair); E wall = 9 rooms + a forge; corrals in bad state; all roofs need re-tiling — warns the repair cost will double if delayed
