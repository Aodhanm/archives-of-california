# accepts that the master weaver Antonio Henríquez work at the mission for a time; the mission pays his salary and keeps ⅛

**C-A 16 (Provincial State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n83** · Sav p. 84 · tomo p. 379–81 · 1797-05-27 · Savage abstract · 

accepts that the master weaver Antonio Henríquez work at the mission for a time; the mission pays his salary and keeps ⅛ of his works' value, the other ⅞ to the Royal Treasury (declines the alternative of retaining him outright at $12–3/day)
