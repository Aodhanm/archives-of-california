# *Hoja de servicio del Sargento [Javier] Aguilar* — entered service 16 Oct 1768; 32 yr 3 mo total, always in the Loreto c

**C-A 16 (Provincial State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n135** · Sav p. 136 · tomo p. 486 · 1800-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

*Hoja de servicio del Sargento [Javier] Aguilar* — entered service 16 Oct 1768; 32 yr 3 mo total, always in the Loreto company; present at the exploration & founding of San Diego and Monterey and all expeditions there 1768–1771; many actions vs. gentiles (last 1785 in the San Diego sierra, 12 gentiles killed); entrusted with most *cimarrón* expeditions for 12 yr; ⭐ 24 June 1799 at Cabo San Lucas killed some of the English — though the two frigates fired nine cannon shots, they then withdrew; commands the southern district 5½ yr + the militia company 14 mo
