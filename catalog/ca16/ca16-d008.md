# *Interim-government orders* — take interim command after Gov. Romeu's death; faculty to open his communications; digest 

**C-A 16 (Provincial State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n3–n5** · Sav p. 4–6 · tomo p. — · 1792-07-01 · Savage abstract · 

*Interim-government orders* — take interim command after Gov. Romeu's death; faculty to open his communications; digest of 1792–93 resolutions: Romeu's papers sealed in a 2-key box & go to Monterey to continue his *ajustes* (Sbre 21); watch for English ships, arrange troop *ajustes*, report nobility/armament/strength (Oct 24); tools/memorias suspended pending accounting (1793); fortify Monterey, San Diego & San Francisco — order cannons, pertrechos & utensils (Fbº 16); relocate Mission Santo Tomás de Aquino to a better-ventilated *cañada* (mosquito plagues), found a new mission between Santo Domingo & San Rosario, +8 plazas to the Loreto presidio, $1,000 to the missions' Procurador (Mzo 27); Llagas[?]→San Diego to arrange *ajustes* (Jul 6)
