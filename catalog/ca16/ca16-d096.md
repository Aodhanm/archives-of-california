# troops content with available meat, scarce at the Rancho del Rey from frequent gentile thefts; ⭐⭐ on the Indians: by the

**C-A 16 (Provincial State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n58–59** · Sav p. 59–60 · tomo p. 325 · 1796-08-23 · Savage abstract · 

troops content with available meat, scarce at the Rancho del Rey from frequent gentile thefts; ⭐⭐ on the Indians: by the Laws of the Indies neophytes should leave mission tutelage at 10 years, but those of New California 'at this rate will not leave even in 10 centuries — why? God knows, and men know something of it.'
