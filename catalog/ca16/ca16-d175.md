# *Compañía presidial* (muster) — 1 tte, 1 alférez, 1 sgt, 5 cabos, 1 cadet, 50 soldados = 59; 6 gunners at the fort; inva

**C-A 16 (Ben. Mil.), scan n107** · tomo p. 868–917 · Sav p. 108 · 1800-12-01 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

*Compañía presidial* (muster) — 1 tte, 1 alférez, 1 sgt, 5 cabos, 1 cadet, 50 soldados = 59; 6 gunners at the fort; invalids 15. ⭐⭐ records that Capt. Antonio Grajera, lt.-commander of the San Diego company, died aboard the King's frigate *Concepción* en route to San Blas on 18 June [1800]; Manuel Rodríguez became interim commander, promoted teniente 11 Nov (Grajera retired same date)
