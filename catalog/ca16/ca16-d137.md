# *Cuenta con el Presidio* (master account) — total cargo $84,790, total data $85,088.5, balance $298.6 in habilitado's fa

**C-A 16 (Provincial State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n80–82** · Sav p. 81–83 · tomo p. 290–7 · 1797-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

*Cuenta con el Presidio* (master account) — total cargo $84,790, total data $85,088.5, balance $298.6 in habilitado's favor. Credits to presidios (Loreto $743, San Diego $530, Sta Bárbara $1,154.5, SF $1,192) and to the missions (San Carlos $1,383, Sta Cruz $582, Sta Clara $2,147.4, San Antonio $1,482.4, San Luis $1,131.2, Soledad $417.3 = $9,714.1); also artisan salaries $3,364, 9 settlers' wages $672, $7,183 remitted to the Treasury via Cárcaba; grain/effects/víveres on hand end-1797 $41,129
