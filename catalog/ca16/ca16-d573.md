⭐⭐ incest *causa* vs. Vicente Quijada (invalid soldier, *mayordomo* of Mission San Fernando, 47, native of Real de los Álamos, Sonora) — accused of repeated rape of his daughter Bibiana over ~3 yrs (Ranchos del Conejo & Simí, the San Buenaventura road, Río Sta Clara/La Mojonera), the child conceived at Mission San Fernando Christmas 1805; case built on the testimony of the wife Josefa Villavicencio & children Bibiana, Dolores, Francisco, Nazario; Quijada denies all; fiscal notes the crime "poco visto… careciendo de leyes pª casos de esta naturaleza" and refers sentencing to the superior/King — *expanded below*
