**C-A 16 (Ben. Mil.), scan n265–266** · tomo p. 183–4 · Sav p. 266–267 · 1805-01-29 *(Savage: "1804")* · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ *Expedición contra Indios* — Fr. Juan Martín of San Miguel visited the Cholán[?] rancheria (14 leagues off); the chief Guchapa[?] gave some youths to be Christianized but refused himself, saying "he was not afraid of the soldiers, who were cowards, and he knew for certain they died like everyone else"; de la Guerra sent a sgt + 13 soldiers (out 22 Dec, back 10 Jan), capturing Guchapa, his son, 2 chieftains & 2 Christians; Guchapa then agreed to bring in his rancheria's Christians, leaving his son as hostage, and was rewarded for good conduct, with a charge that they henceforth respect the troops & Padres
