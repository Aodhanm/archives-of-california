# *Compañía presidial* (muster + year-events) — 1 tte, 1 alférez, 1 surgeon, 1 cadet, 1→2 sgts, 5→8 cabos, 48→67–68 soldad

**C-A 16 (Ben. Mil.), scan n39–40** · tomo p. 2–95 · Sav p. 40–41 · 1796-12-31 · Savage abstract · ⭐

*Compañía presidial* (muster + year-events) — 1 tte, 1 alférez, 1 surgeon, 1 cadet, 1→2 sgts, 5→8 cabos, 48→67–68 soldados + a drummer = 83 men year-end; invalids 1 sgt, 3 cabos, 8 sol.; tte José Argüello left 11 Apr to command his SF co. ⭐ Year-events logged: May 7 packet *San Carlos* lands Catalonian volunteers for Alberni's co. (2 gunners, 2 artisans + families); May 15 frigate-lt. Ramón Saavedra knighted into the Order of Santiago (Borica as Grand Master, Lasuén godfather); June 11 English warship *Providence*, Capt. William Broughton, salutes Monterey w/ 11 guns, reprovisioned, departs 8 July
