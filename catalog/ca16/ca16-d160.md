# *Compañía presidial* (muster) — 1 capt, 1 tte, 1 cadet, 3 sgts, 5 cabos, 59 soldados = 70 men; invalids 1 sgt/2 sol. ⭐⭐ 

**C-A 16 (Provincial State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n98** · Sav p. 99 · tomo p. 355–519 · 1799-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

*Compañía presidial* (muster) — 1 capt, 1 tte, 1 cadet, 3 sgts, 5 cabos, 59 soldados = 70 men; invalids 1 sgt/2 sol. ⭐⭐ records that on 20 June [1799] two frigates anchored at Cabo San Lucas, their crews landing to take on water; the 21st the area sergeant-commander roused [his men]; though the ships fired nine howitzer rounds of grapeshot at him, the crews managed to re-embark, abandoning a water-store, and set sail at once
