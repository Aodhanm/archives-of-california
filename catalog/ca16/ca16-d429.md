# ⭐⭐⭐ forwards alférez Luis Argüello's expedition diary (see expanded entry) — a 32-day reconnaissance of the San José/San

**C-A 16 (Ben. Mil.), scan n250–251** · tomo p. 644–61 · Sav p. 251–252 · 1805-06-25 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ forwards alférez Luis Argüello's expedition diary (see expanded entry) — a 32-day reconnaissance of the San José/Santa Clara country to the rivers, plains & *tulares*; the threat to burn Mission San José proved a fabrication to escape the friars' continual floggings; razed the Tossori rancheria; brought back 22 captives (13 Christians, 9 gentiles), 6 implicated in killing the Christian "George," delivered to the San José/Santa Clara ministers with a plea to moderate their punishment
