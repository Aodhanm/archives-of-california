**C-A 16 (Ben. Mil.), scan n264** · tomo p. 114–81 · Sav p. 265 · 1805-05-18 · Savage abstract · ⭐

*Compañía presidial de Loreto en 1804* (muster) — capt. (lt.-col.) Arrillaga, tte José Pérez Fernández, alférez José Manuel Ruiz, 3 sgts, 5 cabos, cadet José Mª Estudillo, 57–59 soldados = 69 men; ⭐ note: Arrillaga was named proprietary Governor of Alta California by Royal despatch of 26 March [1804] (notified 12 July), ceasing as Loreto captain; the King set invalid pay (sgt $10/mo, cabos & sol. $8) on 24 Oct 1803
