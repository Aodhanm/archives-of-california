# ⭐⭐⭐ war alert: no fewer than 12 English/enemy ships cruise from Cabo Corrientes to Cabo San Lucas until January; the sou

**C-A 16 (Ben. Mil.), scan n121** · tomo p. 51–3 · Sav p. 122 · 1799-07-19 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ war alert: no fewer than 12 English/enemy ships cruise from Cabo Corrientes to Cabo San Lucas until January; the southern settlers had for 2 months abandoned their families in perpetual movement to prevent enemy landings; authorizes arming 50 men under veteran sgt Javier Aguilar (4 r/day); the San Diego commander to ready a 14-man mounted force on demand; Arrillaga empowered to act with full freedom in emergencies (cf. C-A 15 Doc 392 British-squadron-in-the-Gulf)
