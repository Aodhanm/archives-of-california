# the American frigate *Hazard* entered San Francisco 30 Jan 1804, badly damaged — 3 months out from the Sandwich Islands 

**C-A 16 (Provincial State Papers, Benicia (Military)), scan n262** · Sav p. 263 · tomo p. 73–4 · 1804-01-31 · Savage abstract · 

the American frigate *Hazard* entered San Francisco 30 Jan 1804, badly damaged — 3 months out from the Sandwich Islands bound for the NW coast; in storms it lost its 1st & 3rd pilots and 3 Hawaiian Islanders, 2 boats, a canoe & the mizzen topmast, the ship 'all in pieces'; asked aid. ⭐ The SF commander, with only a 4-man guard, feared the foreigners might come and seize the undermanned presidio, so summoned the Santa Clara & San José escorts
