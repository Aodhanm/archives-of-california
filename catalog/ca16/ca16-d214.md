# ⭐⭐ *Salida á contra Indios* — punitive expedition (8–15 May): from Santa Clara, found the relocated rancheria the 9th, I

**C-A 16 (Ben. Mil.), scan n129–131** · tomo p. 303–11 · Sav p. 130–132 · 1800-05-15 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ *Salida á contra Indios* — punitive expedition (8–15 May): from Santa Clara, found the relocated rancheria the 9th, Indians fled to the Sierra; used lance & sword, killed a captain, others fled; took 10 prisoners to the San José escort; on the 11th fell on 7 rancherías and recovered 21 Christian fugitives handed over by the captains (no gentile would convert); disarmed the gentiles (seized bows & arrows) as punishment "for daring to bear arms against us"; identified men who had helped kill [SF] Christians; one captain, run through by a lance, kept throwing stones and grasped the lance with his hands; reached San Francisco the 15th
