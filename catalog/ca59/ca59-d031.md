# 'Plan para convertir en pueblos las Misiones de las dos Californias' — THE SECULARIZATION BLUEPRINT: all Alta Cal. missi

**C-A 59 (Legislative Records), scan n134–n148** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 132–147 · ~1828–1830 · Savage abstract · 

'Plan para convertir en pueblos las Misiones de las dos Californias' — THE SECULARIZATION BLUEPRINT: all Alta Cal. missions (except San Rafael & San Francisco Solano, north of the SF river) to be converted into pueblos within 5 years, one at a time, per the Padres' & Presidente's reports + the Diputación's parecer; mission lands & goods distributed to families (each empadronado); the pueblos keep the mission name; the mission buildings repurposed for ayuntamiento/jail/barracks/schools/hospital; approved via the Diputación → the Cámaras. *The Diputación's own secularization plan, years before the 1834 acts*
