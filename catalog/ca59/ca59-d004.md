# the founding session — incl. a 1822 census: California = 24,990 habitantes, of whom 21,196 'nuevos ciudadanos' (neophyte

**C-A 59 (Legislative Records), scan n10–n12** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 9–11 · 1822-04-11 · Savage abstract · 

the founding session — incl. a 1822 census: California = 24,990 habitantes, of whom 21,196 'nuevos ciudadanos' (neophytes) in the 20 Misiones and 2,994 = the 4 presidial companies + their women/children/invalids (*corroborates C-A 46 Doc 99's '3000 almas' of gente de razón*); commissioner Fernández de San Vicente insists on an 'orden expresa' to install despite the 'absoluta falta de personas.' Founding vocales roster (Solá pres.; José Arus, Francisco Ortega, Francisco Castro, José Palomares, Carlos Castro, Antonio Castro, José Castro, Antonio Navarrete; Fran.co Haro secretary)
