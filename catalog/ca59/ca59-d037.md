# the 1831 anti-Victoria revolt, in the Diputación's record: the *pronunciamiento* to deny obedience to Gov. Manuel Victor

**C-A 59 (Legislative Records), scan n177–n188** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 174–183 · 1831–1832 · Savage abstract · 

the 1831 anti-Victoria revolt, in the Diputación's record: the *pronunciamiento* to deny obedience to Gov. Manuel Victoria 'por recobrar la representación popular y seguridad individual'; Victoria wounded ('estado moribundo') in the unexpected encounter; the Diputación convenes at LA to name interim office-holders per the plan; signed by José Pico, Mariano G. Vallejo, Antonio Mª Osio, José A. Ortega, Santiago Argüello, Juan B. Alvarado — the young Californio elite driving it
