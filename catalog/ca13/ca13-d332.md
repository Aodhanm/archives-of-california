# ⭐⭐ Manuel Gómez → Gov. Solá — the 1820 Fort San Joaquín collapse report (= expanded Document 7): only 8 cannons, 2 unser

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n260–n262** · tomo p. i.1110–1113 · Sav p. 261–263 · 1820-03-20 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Manuel Gómez → Gov. Solá — the 1820 Fort San Joaquín collapse report (= expanded Document 7): only 8 cannons, 2 unserviceable (12-pdr's mounting & pitted bore; 8-pdr's ball jams for lack of a *rasador*); gunner's tools broken; 2 cannons at the landing-point unusable for lack of platform planking, emplaced "in sand"; not a single veteran artilleryman (the rest too ailing to drill); had to destroy cartridge-boxes to sew sacking; little grapeshot, "only round shot"; a ship sighted near the port at 6 PM as he wrote
