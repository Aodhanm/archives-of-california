# ⭐⭐ Seizure inventory of Tarasov's & the Aleuts' effects — from Sgt. Carlos Carrillo, captured at the Ensenada de San Ped

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n217–n218** · tomo p. xx.885–886 · Sav p. 218–219 · 1817 · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Seizure inventory of Tarasov's & the Aleuts' effects — from Sgt. Carlos Carrillo, captured at the Ensenada de San Pedro from "the diver called Boris, with twelve cayucos": 3 sacks rice, 2 tobacco, *bayetón*, purple China ribbon, blue cloth; ⭐ 2 small bags + 40 otter pelts (*pieles de Nutria*), 5 sea-lion skins, 12 gut-shirts (kamleikas), fishing gear, lead, 9 badly-damaged muskets (one barrel burst, most locks useless), powder, a field-tent, Boris's notebook, hammer/tongs/augers, an empty bottle. (capture-side list; cf. Doc 273; Mathes "Tarasov effects-lists")
