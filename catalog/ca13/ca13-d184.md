# ⭐ Gov. Solá → José de la Cruz (long, emotional): congratulates him on the rumored Viceroyalty and the surrender of "the 

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n146–n150** · tomo p. i.654–655 · Sav p. 147–151 · 1817-06-04 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐ Gov. Solá → José de la Cruz (long, emotional): congratulates him on the rumored Viceroyalty and the surrender of "the Island" [Mezcala/Chapala] (pardon to Vargas & Salgado); then pleads that the garrison has not received its accustomed pay in 7 years, officers & families "en la indigencia," the troops naked — ⭐ "he tenido que despojarme de la mayor parte de mi ropa para cubrirles" (Solá has given away most of his own clothing) — while campaigning continuously against the "Indios Gentiles," guarding mission detachments & carrying mail; happier the last soldier than the governor; hopes relief after his 5-yr term
