# ⭐ The Indian woman captured on the "Mercurio" with Capt. [George] Ayres and their daughter: María Antonia de la Ascensió

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n108–n109** · tomo p. i.437 · Sav p. 109–110 · 1816-08 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ The Indian woman captured on the "Mercurio" with Capt. [George] Ayres and their daughter: María Antonia de la Ascensión Stuard sailed as Ayres's woman 4 years; they had a daughter; both taken aboard when the *Mercurio* was seized; when Ayres was sent on to San Blas, mother & daughter remained in Guerra's house at Santa Bárbara — both baptized, with Guerra as godfather
