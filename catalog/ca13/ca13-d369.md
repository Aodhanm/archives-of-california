# ⭐ José M.ª Estudillo → Solá: on the sale of aguardiente — Oliva arrived w/ brandy; proposes a regulated public *cantina*

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n277–n278** · tomo p. xx.1151–1154 · Sav p. 278–279 · 1820-02-13 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐ José M.ª Estudillo → Solá: on the sale of aguardiente — Oliva arrived w/ brandy; proposes a regulated public *cantina* (a trustworthy seller) under rules: ⭐ never sell to Indians; drink on-site only (unless medicinal, w/ a chit); per-person limits; no credit, no pawning soldiers' clothing/mounts/saddles; hours 6 AM–7 PM; no sale during Mass; the tavern within the presidio to keep order; seller takes 1 real per peso, $6/barrel
