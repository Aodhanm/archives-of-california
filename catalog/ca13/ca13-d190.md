# ⭐ Fermín de Yenza y Aguirre[?] → Gov. Solá: on the new tithe/duty on tallow (*sebo*) & import duties — agrees to pay the

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n153–n155** · tomo p. xx.675–689 · Sav p. 154–156 · 1817-08-26 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Fermín de Yenza y Aguirre[?] → Gov. Solá: on the new tithe/duty on tallow (*sebo*) & import duties — agrees to pay the two import duties to equip the troops, has served the King in Peru (once advancing 38,000 in value), but the governor's 17% tallow duty would hurt him (fixed prices, tallow-for-goods); argues (Chile practice) the friars/missions, as the tallow producers, should pay — notes 19 missions trade in tallow; offers his entire cargo to clothe the troops
