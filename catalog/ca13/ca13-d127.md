# ⭐⭐ Estado of men fit to bear arms in the Santa Bárbara jurisdiction (excl. the artillery militia, sent to San Diego), by

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n111–n113** · tomo p. i.452–455 · Sav p. 112–114 · 1816-07-06 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Estado of men fit to bear arms in the Santa Bárbara jurisdiction (excl. the artillery militia, sent to San Diego), by class & residence: Pueblo de Los Ángeles (4 sgts, 1 cabo, 15 soldiers-invalids, 70 vecinos), missions San Fernando, San Buenaventura, Purísima, ranchos Simí & del Refugio, + the presidio invalids & 17 vecinos. Total 135 persons — but only 32 muskets, 22 swords, 8 pistols, 6 lances for the whole jurisdiction. Signed Guerra y Noriega. = expanded Document 18
