# ⭐ Spanish passport/safe-conduct for the *Rurik*, issued by Don Carlos Gutiérrez de los Ríos, Conde de Fernán Núñez, duqu

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n137–n139** · tomo p. i.~631 · Sav p. 138–140 · 1815-09-16 (copied SF 1816-10-02) · verbatim transcription · ⭐

⭐ Spanish passport/safe-conduct for the *Rurik*, issued by Don Carlos Gutiérrez de los Ríos, Conde de Fernán Núñez, duque de Montellano (Spanish ambassador in London, *signed at London 16 Sep 1815, "gratis y vale para este viaje"*), at the request of the Russian ambassador to Britain — for the frigate *Rurik*, Capt. Kotzebue, property of the Chancellor of the Russian Empire, Prince "Romanoff" [Rumyantsev], on a round-the-world scientific voyage; binds all Spanish civil/military authorities in Asia, Africa & both Americas to aid it, invoking the "estrecha alianza" Spain–Russia. Certified copy by Luis Argüello, SF 2 Oct 1816
