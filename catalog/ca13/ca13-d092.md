⭐ Pablo de la Portilla → Gov. Solá: the "Cosak" anchored after 23 days from Mazatlán with his 100 men; not the contracted port, but advanced season and "la decadencia de treinta y siete individuos en estado de muerte" (37 men in near-death condition) forced landing here; will march to approach the governor, bringing 10,000 pesos + a *tercio* of sealed paper
