# ⭐ Fort Ross reconnaissance: detailed report on the Russian establishment ~7 leagues N of Bodega — 130-vara square timber

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n26–n28** · tomo p. xx.104–108 · Sav p. 27–29 · 1816-10-21 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Fort Ross reconnaissance: detailed report on the Russian establishment ~7 leagues N of Bodega — 130-vara square timber wall (~3 varas high) topped by a 6-point palisade, 2 octagonal bastions with gun-ports, 6 houses (37 rooms); 25 Russians + commander + ~80 Kodiak (*Codiaque*) Indians (living outside, hunting otter in cayucos); shipbuilding: a 24-vara *bergantín*, war-launches, 47 leather cayucos; Russians make no local-Indian conquests, some local women married to Kodiaks; encloses the Russian commander's *pliego*
