# ⭐ Camille de Roquefeuil → Gov. Solá (French): transmits a letter from M. Carvencia of Lima; forced to put in at San Fran

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n210–n211** · tomo p. xx.871–872 · Sav p. 211–212 · 1817-08-08 · verbatim transcription · ⭐

⭐ Camille de Roquefeuil → Gov. Solá (French): transmits a letter from M. Carvencia of Lima; forced to put in at San Francisco; asks Solá's protection for his ship; bases his confidence on the friendship between the two nations and "the proofs the French gave of their sentiments toward the Spanish in the recent events in Chile"; Luis Argüello to forward two cases of French products as a gift. Signed "De Roquefeuil, lieut. des vaisseaux du Roi"
