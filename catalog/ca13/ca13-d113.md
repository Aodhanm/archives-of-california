Aires expedition — the 2 cannon he has are useless, the militia artillerymen inept ("ni siquiera saben el ejercicio del arma"); asks for matériel & munitions; proposes making 500–600 bows and requesting the missions' best archers (*flecheros*), making machetes, and arming the vaqueros and Indians against the pirates
