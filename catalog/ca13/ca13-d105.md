# José de la Cruz → Gov. Solá: thanks for congratulations on the Gran Cruz conferred on him; denies the rumor he'll be pro

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n100–n101** · tomo p. xx.374–375 · Sav p. 101–102 · 1816-02-16 · Savage abstract · 

José de la Cruz → Gov. Solá: thanks for congratulations on the Gran Cruz conferred on him; denies the rumor he'll be promoted to Viceroy ("in these times best to have a corner and a little pot to enjoy"); reports Cartagena surrendered to General Morillo and the 1st division of Spanish forces is about to reach Veracruz
