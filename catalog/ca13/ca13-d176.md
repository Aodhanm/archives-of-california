# Ramón de Murcia → Gov. Solá: the habilitado Gervasio Argüello (2 months there seeking funds) will credit Solá's account 

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n142** · tomo p. i.637–640 · Sav p. 143 · 1817-05-02 · Savage abstract · ⭐

Ramón de Murcia → Gov. Solá: the habilitado Gervasio Argüello (2 months there seeking funds) will credit Solá's account once paid from the Real Cajas; the general [José de la Cruz] returned from Mexico (conferred w/ the Viceroy, received the Gran Cruz de Isabel); ⭐ no news from Spain "a causa de los muchos piratas que hay en el golfo de México"
