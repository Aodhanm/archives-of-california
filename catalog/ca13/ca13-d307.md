# ⭐ Instructions for the relief-expedition ships "San Carlos" (war-brig) & "Reina de los Ángeles" (armed merchant frigate)

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n240–n245** · tomo p. xx.1086–1099 · Sav p. 241–246 · 1819-06-08 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐ Instructions for the relief-expedition ships "San Carlos" (war-brig) & "Reina de los Ángeles" (armed merchant frigate) — to relieve Alta Calif. (attacked Nov 1818 by 2 rebel frigates) w/ 100 troops + artillery; ⭐ detailed sailing orders (route via the Socorro Islands, approach San Francisco to avoid falling leeward of Monterey, avoid any encounter with [rebel] ships, anchor at Monterey unless taken); ⭐ the King's order to keep "toda armonía con el pabellón anglo-americano y el de la Gran Bretaña" — the only maritime enemy being the insurgent colonies; intercept any ship aiding the rebels; before returning, urge the missions to ship hemp (*cáñamo*) for the San Blas Arsenal. Cmdr. Gonzalo Gómez de Ulloa, Capt. José Bandini (cf. Doc 87)
