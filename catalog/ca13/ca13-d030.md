# ⭐ Solá–Kotzebue–Kuskov conference at SF Presidio. Kotzebue (*Rurik*) mediates; Spain demands Kuskov withdraw from Ross (

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n29–n33** · tomo p. xx.109–110,117 · Sav p. 30–33 · 1816-10-23 · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Solá–Kotzebue–Kuskov conference at SF Presidio. Kotzebue (*Rurik*) mediates; Spain demands Kuskov withdraw from Ross (8 leagues N of Bodega, est. ~5 yrs by Baranov); Kuskov admits residence, will vacate only on Baranov's order, will write the Emperor ("íntima alianza" Russia–Spain). Chamisso interpreter; Estudillo & Luis Argüello witnesses. Prior Viceroy/Arrillaga/J. Argüello demands went unanswered (forwarded to St. Petersburg). ⚠ internal date wrinkle (Kuskov arrives "25th," doc concludes "23 Oct")
