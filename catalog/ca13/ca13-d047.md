# Viceroy Apodaca free-trade compilation (now COMPLETE, ends n54). A long bundle reproducing earlier royal orders granting

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n48–n54** · tomo p. i.169–172 · Sav p. 49–55 · 1820-08-21 (compilation 1790–1820) · unspecified · 

Viceroy Apodaca free-trade compilation (now COMPLETE, ends n54). A long bundle reproducing earlier royal orders granting duty-free privileges to merchant expeditions Cádiz→San Blas/California via Callao/Acapulco/Guayaquil: Ramón Márquez (Cinco Gremios Mayores, R.O. 1 Oct 1793); José Ignacio de Iturbe (fragata *Neptuno*, Acapulco scale, R.O. Aranjuez 3 Feb 1795); Fernando Sierra (fragata *Rosalía*, Guayaquil return cacao/cascarilla, then again re San Blas/Tepic/Guadalajara *alcabala eventual* abuse — R.O. Madrid 3 Jul 1818 & 8 May 1820, *Salmón*; foreign goods to pay 7% *almojarifazgo* at fixed Veracruz tariffs). Es copia México 21 Aug 1820 (*Humara*); Monterey 10 Apr 1821; circulated for archival copying — Guerra y Noriega (Sta Bárbara 18 Apr 1821), Estudillo (San Diego 22 Apr 1821)
