# ⭐ Manuel Gómez → Gov. Solá: the best site for the armory & magazine is Monterey or Mission Santa Clara — Monterey has ab

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n254** · tomo p. xx.1100–1101 · Sav p. 255 · 1819-09-24 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Manuel Gómez → Gov. Solá: the best site for the armory & magazine is Monterey or Mission Santa Clara — Monterey has abundant timber in the Carmelo sierra (6 lgs) & is under the governor's watch; Sta Clara has oaks; SF's timber (Contra Costa) is hard/costly to extract; Sta Bárbara lacks timber; SD's is remote; decide before the *maestranza* arrives
