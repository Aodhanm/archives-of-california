# Santiago Argüello → Gov. Solá (long rebuttal): answers the San Juan Capistrano fathers' charges over goods lost from the

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n90–n92** · tomo p. i.355–357 · Sav p. 91–93 · 1819-04-07 · Savage abstract · 

Santiago Argüello → Gov. Solá (long rebuttal): answers the San Juan Capistrano fathers' charges over goods lost from the mission *bodega* during their flight at the insurgents' [Bouchard] arrival — says the bodega was empty (they'd moved things), that he helped save items & returned what he found strewn on the beach, that the fathers abandoned the mission and fled to the woods and locked the doors, insulted him with foul words; quotes their dramatic complaint; notes Fr. José Barona had earlier said the insurgents' damage "valía nada"; praises the San Luis fathers' help
