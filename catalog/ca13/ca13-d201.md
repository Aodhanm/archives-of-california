# ⭐ Wilcocks → Gov. Solá: voyage narrative — broke rigging on the mainmast, put into San Quintín to repair; watered at San

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n160–n161** · tomo p. i.712–715 · Sav p. 161–162 · 1817-06-11 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐ Wilcocks → Gov. Solá: voyage narrative — broke rigging on the mainmast, put into San Quintín to repair; watered at San José del Cabo, then Loreto (delivered provisions, which lacked seeds, at Gov. Argüello's request); the delays cost him a voyage to China; carried Fr. Pablo M.ª Zárate & the sick Fr. Antonio Menéndez (per the Father President); ⭐ asks license to buy otters (*nutrias*) "the most important [venture] of my life"; Cmdt. Estudillo refused him >3 days at Monterey & ordered him out without bread/beans
