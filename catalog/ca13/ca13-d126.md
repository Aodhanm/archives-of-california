# ⭐ Guerra y Noriega → Gov. Solá: has sent for the 3 cannons from San Buenaventura, the two 2-pdrs from Santa Inés & Purís

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n111** · tomo p. xx.448–451 & 456 · Sav p. 112 · 1816-07-06 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐ Guerra y Noriega → Gov. Solá: has sent for the 3 cannons from San Buenaventura, the two 2-pdrs from Santa Inés & Purísima — which have no shot (*bala*); the added artillery needs more men, esp. lacking gun-platforms; the local militia should help; scarcity prevented sending matériel to San Diego; ⭐ approves "bala roja" (red-hot shot) against the pirates "pero desgraciadamente no hay allí quien sepa aplicarla" (no one knows how to use it)
