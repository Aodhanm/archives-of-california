# ⭐⭐ Conde del Venadito → Solá — the COLONEL-PROMOTION letter (= expanded Document 3 below; its page cite is corrected her

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n70–n72** · tomo p. xx.210–216 & 244–249 (en duplicado) · Sav p. 71–73 · 1819-03-22 · unspecified · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Conde del Venadito → Solá — the COLONEL-PROMOTION letter (= expanded Document 3 below; its page cite is corrected here from i.192–197). Acknowledges Solá's reports nº 174 (4 Nov) & nº 177 (12 Dec 1818) warning of two insurgent frigates preparing in the Sandwich Islands [Hawaii] to invade, and the attack on Monterey presidio after 18 Nov; approves the defense; thanks all in the King's name; confers on Solá the grade of *Coronel de Milicias Provinciales* (provisional despacho enclosed, published in extraordinary gazette). Recounts ordering the "San Carlos" (San Blas, ~100 men) and a Sonora ship; Occidente replied (13 Feb) the brig "Cosak" was readying at Mazatlán with 100 picked men under Capt. Eduardo García + 6–8,000 pesos & munitions; San Blas departure delayed, José de la Cruz ordered to expedite. ⭐ Immediate promotions to Alférez Manuel Gómez & José Estrada, who commanded the battery [Bouchard defense]
