# ⭐⭐⭐ RAC diplomatic protest — Alexander Baranov (Gov.-Gen. of the Russian American Possessions, Collegiate Counsellor, Kn

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n206–n208** · tomo p. i.866–870 · Sav p. 207–209 · 1817-02-02 · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ RAC diplomatic protest — Alexander Baranov (Gov.-Gen. of the Russian American Possessions, Collegiate Counsellor, Knight of St. Anne 2nd Cl.) → Gov. Solá: protests "insults to the Russian flag and the capture and keeping in cruel bondage of Russians and other people subject to our august Monarch… in the waters and coasts of California," invoking the treaty of 1812 (arts. 3 & 4); has petitioned St. Petersburg / the Emperor; sends Lt. Yakov Podushkin aboard the *Chirikoff* as a *parlamentario*; closes "The Holy Protector of California preserve you." Translated by Ivan Petroff (i.e. for the Bancroft project, not 1817). = expanded Document 20
