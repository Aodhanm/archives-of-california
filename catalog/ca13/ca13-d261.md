# ⭐ Certificate by José Darío Argüello (Gov. Baja Calif.): Santiago Smith Wilcocks, capt./owner of the schooner "Caminante

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n203** · tomo p. xx.864 · Sav p. 204 · 1817-05-06 · verbatim transcription · ⭐

⭐ Certificate by José Darío Argüello (Gov. Baja Calif.): Santiago Smith Wilcocks, capt./owner of the schooner "Caminante," appeared at Loreto 3 Apr w/ a passport from the Gov. of Alta Calif. (25 Feb), and provisioned the starving Loreto presidio — 126 fanegas maize, 20 of beans (+ other goods), for which he was paid. Certified copy by José M.ª Estudillo, Monterey 12 Jun 1817
