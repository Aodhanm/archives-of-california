# Guerra y Noriega → Gov. Solá: argues against drawing the *situados* from Guadalajara rather than Mexico City — Guadalaja

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n118–n119** · tomo p. i.496–498 · Sav p. 119–120 · 1816-12-30 · Savage abstract · 

Guerra y Noriega → Gov. Solá: argues against drawing the *situados* from Guadalajara rather than Mexico City — Guadalajara's goods are inferior to Puebla's, the troops benefit from receiving effects at capital prices; "más debe temerse el ser socorridos por Guadalajara si fracasa de un año a dos, que el carecer de memorias"
