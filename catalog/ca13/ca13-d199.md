# ⭐ Solá → Wilcocks: thanks him for loading timber for Sta Bárbara, but cannot grant permission to meet overland at San Bu

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n159** · tomo p. xx.709 · Sav p. 160 · 1817-07-27 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Solá → Wilcocks: thanks him for loading timber for Sta Bárbara, but cannot grant permission to meet overland at San Buenaventura/San Gabriel — he leaves for San Diego tomorrow, and letting a foreigner travel overland is new & against his orders (and his predecessors'); says the franchise he offers is "hija del afecto"
