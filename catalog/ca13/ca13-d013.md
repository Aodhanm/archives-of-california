# Long royal order on military uniform/dress (spans 9 scans): all ranks & cadets must wear rigorous uniform/insignia/arms,

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n9–n17** · tomo p. i.39–57 · Sav p. 10–18 · 1816-04-26 · Savage abstract · 

Long royal order on military uniform/dress (spans 9 scans): all ranks & cadets must wear rigorous uniform/insignia/arms, penalties, mustache/beard rules by corps; reproduces the foundational royal decrees of 17 Mar 1785 & 31 May 1785 on officer dress (Royal Guards, infantry, cavalry, dragoons, artillery, engineers); ends with the King's call for moderation against costly dress
