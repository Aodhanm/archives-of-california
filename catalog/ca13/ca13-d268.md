# ⭐⭐ Gov. Solá → Luis Argüello: re buying from the RAC frigate "Kutusoff" (cmdr. Hagemeister) — it has many goods; deal to

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n211–n212** · tomo p. i.884 · Sav p. 212–213 · 1817 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Gov. Solá → Luis Argüello: re buying from the RAC frigate "Kutusoff" (cmdr. Hagemeister) — it has many goods; deal to buy ~$25,000[?] on draft against the *habilitación general*, goods for the troops & families of the four presidios at fair prices; ⭐ but the Kodiaks the frigate wants to take back are only four (some at Sta Bárbara, others at San Gabriel), some now Christian, too distant to arrive before it sails
