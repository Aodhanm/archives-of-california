# Luis Argüello → Solá (omnibus): American schooner arrived w/ tools (requests axes); Sgt. Herrera to rejoin wife in N. Ga

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n41–n42** · tomo p. xx.150–153 · Sav p. 42 · 1816-02-29 · unspecified · 

Luis Argüello → Solá (omnibus): American schooner arrived w/ tools (requests axes); Sgt. Herrera to rejoin wife in N. Galicia; Fr. Ramón Abella proposes selling 200 mission cattle — Argüello urges each mission add 200 (→600) to found a Real Hacienda ranch to supply the presidio weekly; presidio launch destroyed in a storm (needs new one to haul timber); iron & steel scarce
