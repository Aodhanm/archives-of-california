# ⭐⭐ Santiago Smith Wilcocks → Gov. Solá: his schooner was seized & he is a prisoner at Guaymas; "el malvado Ramírez" robb

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n162–n164** · tomo p. i.~716 · Sav p. 163–165 · 1817-12-12 · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Santiago Smith Wilcocks → Gov. Solá: his schooner was seized & he is a prisoner at Guaymas; "el malvado Ramírez" robbed a large part of his cargo to condemn the ship "by licit means"; ⭐ denounces the slander that he meant "levantarme con las Californias para integrarlas a los anglo-americanos mis paisanos" (to seize the Californias and annex them to the Anglo-Americans) — believes the lie reached the governor; he & his sailors held like prisoners on 2–4 reales; encloses copies of all his letters to prove there are no secrets. = expanded Document 19
