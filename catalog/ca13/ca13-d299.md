# ⭐ José M.ª Estudillo → Gov. Solá: Buitrón arrived with "la presa" (the captives) — 37 women & children held at El Carmel

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n236** · tomo p. xx.1065–1066 · Sav p. 237 · 1819-05-24 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ José M.ª Estudillo → Gov. Solá: Buitrón arrived with "la presa" (the captives) — 37 women & children held at El Carmelo (+ some orphans given to live there); the captives kept from the presidio so the Indian men, seeing their wives & families, won't flee; he is building the furnaces for red-hot shot (*hornillos para la bala roja*)
