# ⭐ Conde del Venadito → Gov. Solá: emergency response to the Bouchard seizure of Monterey. Has learned — via San Blas, fr

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n66–n67** · tomo p. i.198–203 · Sav p. 67–68 · 1819-01-20 (re 24 Nov 1818) · unspecified · ⭐

⭐ Conde del Venadito → Gov. Solá: emergency response to the Bouchard seizure of Monterey. Has learned — via San Blas, from Don Manuel Varela[?], captain of the private brig "El Aventurero" (reached port the 30th prev. month) — that on 24 Nov [1818] two large ships + seven insurgent gunboat-launches seized the Monterey presidio, Solá withdrawing to a rancho with all his troops. Has urgently ordered the Cmdt. Gen. of Provincias Internas de Occidente to send from Sonora a ship (the "San Carlos" or another) with troops & munitions to San Antonio / Concepción, and alerted the Cmdt. Gen. of Nueva Galicia; expects Solá to dislodge the *facciosos* with the reinforcements + his troops + armed *paisanage*
