# ⭐⭐ Inventory of the effects of Don Boris Tarasov & the "Russian Indians" [Aleuts], taken at the Ensenada de San Pedro an

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n216–n217** · tomo p. i.884 · Sav p. 217–218 · 1817-05-05 · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Inventory of the effects of Don Boris Tarasov & the "Russian Indians" [Aleuts], taken at the Ensenada de San Pedro and returned by the governor's order to Lt. Jacobo (Yakov) Podushkin: a sack of rice, 10½ v. purple ribbon, 6 small bags of otter [pelts], 9 sea-lion skins, 12 gut-shirts (*camisas de tripa* = Aleut kamleikas), fishing instruments, lead, 9 badly-damaged muskets, Boris's notes, tools; note that tobacco/*bayetón*/a field-tent were consumed feeding the returning Aleuts; signed Monterey 5 May 1817 "(aquí hay varias palabras en idioma ruso)." [= the Mathes "Tarasov effects-lists"; cf. Doc 99, Document 20]
