# ⭐ Gov. Solá → the mission fathers (circular): watch for the relief ships the Viceroy is sending — keep lookouts (*vigías

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n81** · tomo p. i.298–299 · Sav p. 82 · 1819-03-30 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Gov. Solá → the mission fathers (circular): watch for the relief ships the Viceroy is sending — keep lookouts (*vigías*) and a *cayuco* ready to assist; ships sighted San Juan Capistrano→Santa Bárbara go to the Sta Bárbara roadstead, those further north to Monterey or San Francisco; provide outfitting, food & pack-animals; return the circular once read
