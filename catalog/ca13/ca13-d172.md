# ⭐ The royal order behind the *Rurik* hospitality (Lardizábal, Madrid 27 Jun 1815): Spanish authorities in both Americas 

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n139–n140** · tomo p. xx.631 · Sav p. 140–141 · 1815-06-27 (copied SF 1816-10-02) · verbatim transcription · ⭐

⭐ The royal order behind the *Rurik* hospitality (Lardizábal, Madrid 27 Jun 1815): Spanish authorities in both Americas to receive & assist the *Rurik* (Kotzebue) on its round-the-world voyage. Routed Viceroy of Peru → Marqués de la Concordia → Pres. of Chile (*Judas Tadeo Reyes*) → Talcahuano (*Miguel M.ª de Atero*); certified-copied by Luis Argüello at SF, sent to the Viceroy 10 Oct 1816. ⚠ cf. expanded Document 6 — note this R.O. is dated 27 Jun 1815, not 1816
