**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n133–n134** · tomo p. i.616–617 · Sav p. 134–135 · 1816-04-30 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Gov. Solá → the presidio commandants (circular): the Emperor of Russia notified that the ship "Rurik," Capt. Kotzebue, would sail on a scientific expedition — aid & attend him if he reaches Spanish dominions (per R.O. 27 Jun 1815); report his arrival at once. Commandants' acknowledgments follow. (= advance order to the Kotzebue visit, cf. Docs 29–30, Document 6)
