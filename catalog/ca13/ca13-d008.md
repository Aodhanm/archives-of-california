# Approves the permit given to Kuskov (capt. of Russian schooner *Chirikov*) to put in at SF and barter goods for provisio

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n5** · tomo p. i.29–31 · Sav p. 6 · 1816-04-16 · Savage abstract · 

Approves the permit given to Kuskov (capt. of Russian schooner *Chirikov*) to put in at SF and barter goods for provisions — he carried the Bodega Russian-colony commandant's reply about leaving Spanish dominions; orders no communication with that colony and not to repeat the concession; presidio *memorias* held at Acapulco
