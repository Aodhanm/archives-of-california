# ⭐ José M.ª Estudillo → Gov. Solá: on arms-making & fortification + the rebel [Bouchard] destruction at Monterey — repair

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n234–n235** · tomo p. i.1060–1061 · Sav p. 235–236 · 1819-05 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐ José M.ª Estudillo → Gov. Solá: on arms-making & fortification + the rebel [Bouchard] destruction at Monterey — repairing the muskets; approves making machetes to arm the company + sword-scabbards/belts; has mounted the 3 cannons & the esplanade, about to make grapeshot bags (*sacos de metralla*); on the Sta Cruz events sent the Alférez, Sergeant & others; ⭐ "da compasión ver la madera y la teja" — and a wrecked house (only the oven-rooms left, orchard fence smashed, peach trees stripped)
