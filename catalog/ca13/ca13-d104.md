**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n99–n100** · tomo p. xx.370 · Sav p. 100–101 · 1802-08-10 (copied 1814-04-15) · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ R.O. of 10 Aug 1802 (Pedro Cevallos). Foreign vessels in the Mar del Sur that "so color de la pesca de la Ballena hacen un crecido contrabando" along Spanish Pacific coasts: officials to bar foreigners from landing & confiscate per the rules; cites two ships caught smuggling, "Eliothin" & "Tigaty"; notes Spain is warning the U.S. govt to proceed against those who land at the colonies; "este punto convenido entre todas las potencias que tienen colonias." Signed Cevallos, Madrid 10 Aug 1802; *Es copia* México 15 Apr 1814 (Humara). (cf. the 1803 Cevallos whaler R.O. in [[ca54-state-papers-sacramento-i-v-survey]])
