**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n68–n69** · tomo p. i.206–207 · Sav p. 69–70 · 1819-03-20 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Conde del Venadito → Gov. Calif.: war-matériel shipments — orders José de la Cruz to send 4 field-pieces (cal. 4) w/ munitions & powder, the 400 carbines if possible, 3 garrison flags, and the 15–20 cannon (cal. 18/8/6/4) Solá requested; the 300 swords ordered remitted to San Blas via the Artillery inspection; orders the Cmdt. Gen. of Occidente to send funds to repair the fortifications in bad state; artillerymen + an officer + workers leaving immediately to put the artillery service in best state
