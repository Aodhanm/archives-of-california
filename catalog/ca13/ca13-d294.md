# Solá → the commandants of Sta Bárbara & San Diego: the season being advanced (less risk the rebel ships will strike), wi

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n232–n233** · tomo p. xx.1052–1054 · Sav p. 233–234 · 1818-11-12 · Savage abstract · 

Solá → the commandants of Sta Bárbara & San Diego: the season being advanced (less risk the rebel ships will strike), withdraw the invalids & vecinos to their planting/homes, cover the points with the regular garrisons; keep vigilance; missions to post vaqueros. (Dated before the raid actually hit Monterey ~20–24 Nov)
