# Guerra y Noriega → Gov. Solá: re the 1,474 pesos of the 1807 tithe (*diezmo*) charged to habilitado Argüello but never e

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n113–n114** · tomo p. i.458–461 · Sav p. 114–115 · 1816-06-12 · Savage abstract · 

Guerra y Noriega → Gov. Solá: re the 1,474 pesos of the 1807 tithe (*diezmo*) charged to habilitado Argüello but never entered; argues tithes are due 5 yrs after a business yields products, not 5 yrs after establishment — citing a Felipe de Neve order exempting soldiers (escort, serving, discharged-settlers) and *pobladores* from tithes for their first 5 years of farming/stock-raising
