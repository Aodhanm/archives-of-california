# ⭐⭐⭐ Reserved instructions (Conde de Floridablanca) accompanying the NOOTKA CONVENTION of 1790 with England. Orders the V

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n97–n99** · tomo p. i.368–369 · Sav p. 98–100 · 1790-10-25 (copied 1814-04-15) · unspecified · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ Reserved instructions (Conde de Floridablanca) accompanying the NOOTKA CONVENTION of 1790 with England. Orders the Viceroy to ensure British subjects "no se excedan de su literal contexto"; the convention does not alter the prior Costa de Honduras logwood/mahogany (*palo de tinte / caoba*) arrangement with the British "colonos"; arts. 3, 4 & 7 do not bar prosecuting contraband / illicit trade by any nation (incl. England) on Spanish coasts per Spanish law & custom. Signed Floridablanca, Madrid 25 Oct 1790; *Es copia* México 15 Apr 1814 (Humara) — recopied as legal backing for the *Mercurio* prize. → expand; Bodega/Nootka "Open Door"
