# ⭐ Relación of war matériel sent to Alta California on the warship "San Carlos" (cmdr. Tte. de fragata Silvestre Madrazo)

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n132–n133** · tomo p. xx.612–613 · Sav p. 133–134 · 1816-08-17 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Relación of war matériel sent to Alta California on the warship "San Carlos" (cmdr. Tte. de fragata Silvestre Madrazo): 20,000 musket cartridges + 20,000 loose balls (16-adarme), 5,000 country + 5,000 Castilian flints, 20 boxes of powder (1½ quintals each), 100 new English muskets w/ bayonets, 8 eight-pounder cannons w/ naval carriages + 800 balls. Signed Cmdt. of San Blas
