# Luis Argüello → Gov. Solá: the King's cattle now at Las Pulgas — asks whether to ear-mark them as royal (so missions nee

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n135–n136** · tomo p. i.628–630 · Sav p. 136–137 · 1816-05-31 · Savage abstract · ⭐

Luis Argüello → Gov. Solá: the King's cattle now at Las Pulgas — asks whether to ear-mark them as royal (so missions need a distinct mark); needs tar/pitch for the esplanade but "no hay un solo pino" to extract it; esplanade done ~5–6 June (delayed by the nail shortage); ⭐ a bitter personal grievance — his reputation damaged, his honor "anda volando de boca en boca"
