# ⭐ Conde del Venadito → Gov.: orders a *sumaria* on the four Buenos Aires insurgent deserters who crossed over (with arms

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n62** · tomo p. xx.189–190 · Sav p. 63 · 1819-10-28 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Conde del Venadito → Gov.: orders a *sumaria* on the four Buenos Aires insurgent deserters who crossed over (with arms) to the Californians at San Juan Capistrano, Oct/Nov 1818, and that they be watched. Names + filiations: Pedro Saldívar (Buenos Aires, printer, 22), Juan de la Rosa (labrador, 27, served as drummer), Nicolás Echeverría (Santa Fe, labrador, 24), José Pascual (African, servant, 28)
