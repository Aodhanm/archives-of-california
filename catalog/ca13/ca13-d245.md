# ⭐ Gervasio Argüello → Gov. Solá: encloses his oficio to the Viceroy; ⭐ the King approved promotions — Luis Argüello Capt

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n193–n194** · tomo p. xx.818–819 & 827 · Sav p. 194–195 · 1817-12-11 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐ Gervasio Argüello → Gov. Solá: encloses his oficio to the Viceroy; ⭐ the King approved promotions — Luis Argüello Capt. of San Francisco, Gabriel [Moraga] lieutenant, Santiago Argüello Alférez of Sta Bárbara, José Noriega & Ignacio Martínez lieutenants, and himself (Gervasio Argüello) Alférez of San Diego; asks a posting other than SD; a soldier's place for Magín Bernal; notes he (a cadet, the ex-gov.'s son) hasn't been paid in 6 years
