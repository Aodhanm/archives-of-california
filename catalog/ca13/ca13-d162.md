# Alejandro Arana → Gov. Solá: (most of the letter "carece de interés") postscript — Guadalajara is to supply California s

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n130–n131** · tomo p. i.604–605 · Sav p. 131–132 · 1816-11-06 · Savage abstract · 

Alejandro Arana → Gov. Solá: (most of the letter "carece de interés") postscript — Guadalajara is to supply California since Mexico City cannot; the Superintendency ordered the presidios supplied on Guadalajara's account; if unpaid, "clame con energía al Virrey"
