# ⭐ Luis Argüello → Gov. Solá: sends the 4 Christian Indians of Mission San Francisco — Teocoy[?], Félix, Nájara, Crescenc

⭐ Luis Argüello → Gov. Solá: sends the 4 Christian Indians of Mission San Francisco — Teocoy[?], Félix, Nájara, Crescencio — culprits in the murder of Tobías & Norato; a revenge killing (the victims had slain Crescencio's brother); the two were surprised sleeping 4 leagues from San José — one beaten to death, one hanged, bound, stones at the neck, thrown in a deep pool; divers recovered the body
