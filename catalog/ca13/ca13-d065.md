# ⭐ Conde del Venadito → Gov. Solá: instructions on the war matériel, provisions & 200 troops sent to Calif. aboard the fr

**C-A 13 (Prov. St. Pap. XX, 1816–22), scan n62–n65** · tomo p. i.192–197 · Sav p. 63–66 · 1819-12-11 · unspecified · ⭐⭐

⭐ Conde del Venadito → Gov. Solá: instructions on the war matériel, provisions & 200 troops sent to Calif. aboard the fragata "Reina," King's brigantine "San Carlos," brig "Cosak"; rebukes Solá for complaining (his 6 Sep letter to the Cmdt. of Provincias Internas de Occidente) that he got only 300 poor sword-blades "fit only for sickles," none of the 500 carbines requested, no situado cash, no officers, no artillery. Viceroy retorts: scabbards are abundant locally; carbines unavailable; troops use muskets like dragoons; the artillery officer + men + munitions came overland to Guaymas (arrived Oct); the complaint is unfounded; California is "rico de perlas, rico de peces" and rich-soiled — settlers & idle troops should apply themselves and aid the sovereign; orders Guadalajara & Occidente to aid with cash and to ready the *San Carlos* to carry 8 missionaries in May; ⭐ "los 200 hombres de tropa… no los necesita V. ahí, devuélvalos a sus respectivos puestos" (return the 200 reinforcement troops). = expanded Document 16 below
