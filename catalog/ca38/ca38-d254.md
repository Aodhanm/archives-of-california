# A debt-execution / vaquillas case against a Sepúlveda — the Juez decreed payment of vaquillas/caballos/costas within 15 

**C-A 38 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n410–n419** · Sav p. 410–419 · tomo p. 13–23 · 1841 · verbatim transcription · 

A debt-execution / vaquillas case against a Sepúlveda — the Juez decreed payment of vaquillas/caballos/costas within 15 days on pain of ejecución sobre bienes; Sepúlveda pleaded the bienes were his father's ('él es hijo de familia'); the fiador José Mª Aguilar cited ('por haber sido fiador de un sujeto inhábil para contratos'); appeals through the Prefect; Juan Gallardo appealed on Sepúlveda's behalf
