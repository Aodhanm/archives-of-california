# Narrative of the Los Angeles revolution: learned the 6th that Sonorenses were gathering at the rancho de los Nietos led 

**C-A 38 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n147–n148** · Sav p. 147–148 · tomo p. 460–463 · 1835-03-10 · Savage abstract · 

Narrative of the Los Angeles revolution: learned the 6th that Sonorenses were gathering at the rancho de los Nietos led by Apalátegui & Juan Gallardo; on the 7th they entered LA, took the artillery, arms; the alcalde reported Gallardo & others repentant surrendered prisoners Torres & Apalátegui; commissioned Juan Pérez to observe; Juan Pérez, Guillermo (William) Richardson & José Osorno presented on the rebels' behalf, submitting to govt, asking the ayuntamiento's promise of the 7th be fulfilled; the revolution has ended
