# 'Sobre nombramiento de gefe militar' — transcribes the Comandante genl's 6 Oct. communication naming Vallejo gefe de la 

**C-A 38 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n157–n158** · Sav p. 157–158 · tomo p. 1055–1056 · 1842-10-8 · Savage abstract · 

'Sobre nombramiento de gefe militar' — transcribes the Comandante genl's 6 Oct. communication naming Vallejo gefe de la línea militar desde Sonoma hasta San José, to preserve order & prevent any foreign aggression, in accord with E.S. Gob. interino Juan B. Alvarado; Vallejo asks the auxilios (armament, munitions, armed men) he may need, marching soon to Monterey before the rains, to arrange all relating to the national service & remedy the troops' ills
