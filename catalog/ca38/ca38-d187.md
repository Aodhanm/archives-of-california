# Full ayuntamiento session on the Sonorenses alarm — a partida of armed Sonorenses seized the juzgado, took the key from 

**C-A 38 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n140–n141** · Sav p. 140–141 · tomo p. 444–449 · 1835-03-7 · verbatim transcription · 

Full ayuntamiento session on the Sonorenses alarm — a partida of armed Sonorenses seized the juzgado, took the key from regidor Juan de Dios Brabo, took the cannon & munitions from D. Luis Arenas's house; headed by Juan Gallardo & Antonio Apalátegui; read an oficio + plan aiming 'á quitar el mando político y militar al Gral. José Figueroa' & halt the secularización de las misiones; ayuntamiento named commissioners Manuel Requena, Antonio Mª Osio & Vicente de la Osa to treat; the plan-bearers withdrew peacefully; the corporation declared the Gallardo plan 'fuera de toda ley'
