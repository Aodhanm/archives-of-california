# Ordered Antonio Mª Osio to proceed aboard the Barca Americana *Moscow* anchored at San Pedro & accompany it to Monterey 

**C-A 38 (Departmental State Papers, Benicia), scan n310** · Sav p. 310 · tomo p. 333–336 · 1846-01-21→22 · Savage abstract · 

Ordered Antonio Mª Osio to proceed aboard the Barca Americana *Moscow* anchored at San Pedro & accompany it to Monterey to aforo its cargamento, watching no merchandise disembarked en route; + transfer of the Tesorería Departamental de Monterey (Abrego's resignation, Ignacio del Valle takes over)
