# THE PORTS-OF-ENTRY / FOREIGN-TRADE RULEBOOK — only Monterey & S.Diego are ports of entry; ships at S.Fran/Sta Bárbara/S.

**C-A 47 (Departmental Records), scan n300** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 102 (or p.293) · 1828 · Savage abstract · 

THE PORTS-OF-ENTRY / FOREIGN-TRADE RULEBOOK — only Monterey & S.Diego are ports of entry; ships at S.Fran/Sta Bárbara/S.Pedro solely to extract *esquilmos* must first leave all cargo at Monterey/S.Diego; when empty may collect esquilmos for export; at the secondary ports 'no se desembarcará efecto alguno, ni jente, ni tampoco se embarcará ninguna moneda ni ganado hembro'; liquidate accounts before departure
