# *Informe sobre el temperamento* of the SB jurisdiction 1797 — a climate-&-health report: winter drought + desiccating wi

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n237** · Sav p. 238–239 · tomo p. ii.600–601 · 1797-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

*Informe sobre el temperamento* of the SB jurisdiction 1797 — a climate-&-health report: winter drought + desiccating winds to April; summer fogs then harsh sun causing 'puntas de costado' (pleurisy), eye disease, 'especialmente en los Indios'; many die of *ética/tísica* (consumption/TB), 'muy frecuente especialmente en los Indios'; SE wind brings fog & colds; rains returned in October
