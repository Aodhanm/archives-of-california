# *Conversión de Indios Gentiles* — forwards Goycoechea's survey of the still-independent Chumash rancherías of the Sta Bá

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n225** · Sav p. 226–229 · tomo p. ii.545–46, 557–58 · 1796 · Savage abstract · 

*Conversión de Indios Gentiles* — forwards Goycoechea's survey of the still-independent Chumash rancherías of the Sta Bárbara Channel & recommends congregating them as pueblos in their own lands (not missions) since they subsist by fishing & are 'los más vivos é industriosos de ambas Californias… comparados á los de las Islas de Sandwich y [O]tahiti' (see Expanded Entry 11)
