# 1827 SD *padrón* (7,981 / 7,279 Indians; ~5 ministers, + retired Fr. José Barona at San Luis Rey). *Notas*: neophytes su

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n370** · Sav p. 371 · tomo p. v.156–59 · 1827-06-07 · Savage abstract · 

1827 SD *padrón* (7,981 / 7,279 Indians; ~5 ministers, + retired Fr. José Barona at San Luis Rey). *Notas*: neophytes subsist by cultivating nearby lands + the estancias (wheat/corn/beans/barley 'en comunidad'), help feed the presidio garrison; meat rationed every 8 days (livestock can't support daily); cloth issued 'cuando hay proporción de expender los cueros, sebos y esquilmos en los buques que suelen arribar' (hide-tallow sales to visiting ships)
