# *Noticia de los indios bautizados en las 19 Misiones… en el quinquenio de 1811 á 1815* — a five-year demographic summary

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n348** · Sav p. 349 · tomo p. iv.389–94 · 1815-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

*Noticia de los indios bautizados en las 19 Misiones… en el quinquenio de 1811 á 1815* — a five-year demographic summary: 13,447 Indians baptized (8,385 mission-born + gentiles / 5,062 gentiles & their children) vs. 7,394 Indian deaths, net −4,071 Indians; españoles/other castes 615 baptized / 267 died (+348). ⭐ hard demographic-sink evidence: 55% Indian mortality-to-baptism over the quinquennium
