# *Informe sobre los estragos de un terremoto* — the 21 Dec 1812 earthquake ('día del glorioso Apóstol Santo Tomás') entir

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n329–n330** · Sav p. 330–331 · tomo p. iv.208–10 · 1812-12-31 · verbatim transcription · 

*Informe sobre los estragos de un terremoto* — the 21 Dec 1812 earthquake ('día del glorioso Apóstol Santo Tomás') entirely ruined the church & most adornments; buildings collapsed; 100 neophyte adobe houses rendered useless; heavy rains followed; the friars dug out valuables & built a *jacalón* + huts, delaying rebuilding 'hasta que se aquiete la tierra' (until the aftershocks stop) — La Purísima's destruction (→ its 1813 relocation)
