# 1833 cumulative *Cuadro Estadístico* of all Alta California missions — living neophyte population down to 10,482, agains

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n398** · Sav p. 399 · tomo p. v.304–5 · 1833-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

1833 cumulative *Cuadro Estadístico* of all Alta California missions — living neophyte population down to 10,482, against 82,882 baptized and 58,417 died since founding. The demographic sink at the eve of secularization (cf. Doc 387's 1817 figures: 64,675/41,767/20,238 — the living population roughly halved in 16 years). ganado/cosecha data in a separate estadística
