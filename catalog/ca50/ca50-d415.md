# 1827 Alta California general *estado* (no castas): 24,010 souls (4 jurisdictions); livestock — 186,227 cattle, 180,668 s

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n385–n387** · Sav p. 386–388 · tomo p. v.223–25 · 1827-08-26 · Savage abstract · 

1827 Alta California general *estado* (no castas): 24,010 souls (4 jurisdictions); livestock — 186,227 cattle, 180,668 sheep, 16,766 horses, 1,926 mules, 515 goats, 1,431 hogs; harvests 114,461 fanegas (wheat/corn/beans/barley). Notes: the horse-slaughter order (keep only herds needed for missions/pueblos/presidios + troop remounts); ⭐ the 1827 measles (*sarampión*) killing mostly *párvulos*, worse among Indians, 'no… facultativo que socorra á sus habitantes'
