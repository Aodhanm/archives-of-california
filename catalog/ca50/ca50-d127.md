# *Estado de las Misiones de la [Antigua/Baja] California* — the Baja parallel to Doc 124: 18 missions + Loreto presidio +

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n166** · Sav p. 167–169 · tomo p. ii.117–24[?] · 1795-04-08 · Savage abstract · 

*Estado de las Misiones de la [Antigua/Baja] California* — the Baja parallel to Doc 124: 18 missions + Loreto presidio + Real de Sta Ana; total almas to end-1794 ≈ 4,255; + Notas: ⭐ San Pedro Mártir founded May 1794 (last Dominican mission); ⭐ the obras-pías endowment history (Caballero, Nicolás de Arteaga, Marqués de Villapuente, Doña Ana María de Borja, Luis de Velasco, P. Juan Mª Luyando — funding the 11 oldest missions' sínodos); the Real de Sta Ana served by a Todos Santos friar; ⭐ Baja nations: Pericú, Guaycura, Cochimí + 'del Mar' (the last two dominate, the others 'ya extinguiéndose'); ends n169 with notes urging 1–2 new missions pushed east of Sto Tomás/San Vicente toward the Río Colorado to block gentile communication
