# 1828 Alta California general *estado*: 23,105 souls; livestock — 235,081 cattle, 183,203 sheep, 33,252 horses, 2,964 mul

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n387–n389** · Sav p. 388–390 · tomo p. v.227 · 1829-06-19 · Savage abstract · 

1828 Alta California general *estado*: 23,105 souls; livestock — 235,081 cattle, 183,203 sheep, 33,252 horses, 2,964 mules, 1,944 goats, 1,828 hogs; harvests only 53,527 fanegas (a poor year vs. 1827's 114,461). Note: seasonal fevers hitting mission *párvulos* + 'absoluta falta… de un Misionero ó Cirujano' in both territories; neophyte counts at Guadalupe (18) / Monterey presidio (51) / SD presidio (131)
