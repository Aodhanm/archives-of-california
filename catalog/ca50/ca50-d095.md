# *Resumen general de la propiedad pecuaria de California 1793* — province-wide livestock table, EVERY settlement Baja+Alt

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n128** · Sav p. 125 · tomo p. i.828 · 1794-09-09 · Savage abstract · 

*Resumen general de la propiedad pecuaria de California 1793* — province-wide livestock table, EVERY settlement Baja+Alta (Loreto→San Gabriel): cols vacuno/caballar/mular/cerval + lanar/pelo/cerda + total. Grand totals: ganado mayor vacuno 40,796; caballar 10,541; mular 1,630; lanar 46,534. ⭐ *Notas* (Arrillaga): warns some missions suffer *enfermedades que causan bastante estrago* [epidemics]; both Californias 'temperamentos sanos' but *falta de agua* limits fertility; flags binding errors hiding San Luis/San José totals
