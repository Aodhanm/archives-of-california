# *Informe de los Misioneros… en 1795* (Alta California) — the 1795 friar roster by mission (SF: Landaeta; Sta Clara: Magí

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n210** · Sav p. 211–213 · tomo p. ii.345–63 · 1795-12 · Savage abstract · 

*Informe de los Misioneros… en 1795* (Alta California) — the 1795 friar roster by mission (SF: Landaeta; Sta Clara: Magín Catalá & Miguel Sánchez; Sta Cruz: Baldomero López & Manuel Fernández; San Carlos: Lasuén & Pascual Martínez de Arenaza; La Soledad: José Manuel Martiarena & José de la Cruz; San Antonio: Buenaventura Sitjar & Marcelino Cíprés; San Luis: Gregorio Fernández & Miguel Giribet; Purísima: Arroita & Calzada; Sta Bárbara: Estevan Tápis & José de Miguel; San Buenaventura: Dumetz & Vicente de Sta María; San Gabriel: Antonio Cruzado & Miguel Sánchez; San Juan Cap.: Fuster & Juan N. de Santiago; San Diego: Mariner & Hilario Torrent) + per-mission new items/edificios (SF re-roofed in tile + 90-v. office bldg; Sta Clara church +8 v. & a ½-legua *vallado*; Sta Cruz cuadro & mill finished; San Diego digging a deep new *aguaje*) — the prosopographical key for the 1794–95 mission section (⚠ confirms 'Miguel Sánchez' appears at BOTH Sta Clara & San Gabriel = two friars or a roster slip; ✎ San Antonio's 2nd friar = Cíprés, correcting Doc 116's tentative 'Martiarena[?]' — Martiarena was at La Soledad)
