# *Instrucción* for the commander of the escort assigned to found Mission San Juan Bautista — a 19-article conduct code (s

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n177** · Sav p. 178–185 · tomo p. ii.222–228 · 1797-05-19 · Savage abstract · 

*Instrucción* for the commander of the escort assigned to found Mission San Juan Bautista — a 19-article conduct code (see Expanded Entry 7): friars pick the site + defensibility; treat as if facing enemy Indians; ramada guardhouse + day/night sentinel; gentiles may not approach armed; ⭐ gentle dealing mandated — punish 'luego luego' any soldier who harms an Indian & compensate the Indian; ⭐ no trading arms/knives/sword-pieces/barrel-hoops to Indians; ⭐ soldiers forbidden to visit rancherías alone; no runaway-hunting parties; ⭐ the troop must labor building the interim church/friars' quarters/guardhouse/houses; venerate the friars; send a certification of the *toma de posesión* + first Mass. = the founding instrument for site #4 of Doc 124's five-mission proposal (SJB founded 24 Jun 1797)
