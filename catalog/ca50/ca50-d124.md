**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n161** · Sav p. 162–166 · tomo p. ii.90–92 · 1795-07-13 · Savage abstract · 

*Estado de las Misiones… en los años de 1793 y 1794* — a 13-mission comparative census table (ministros / sínodos / indios H-M / gente de razón / almas), end-1794 vs end-1793 = +932 Indians; + 9 Notas (see Expanded Entry 6): mission-industry survey, '17 o 18 idiomas de SF a SD,' no gentile hostility, ⭐⭐⭐ proposal to found 5 new missions 'en los claros' (SD–SJC, SG–SB, SL–SA, Monterey–Sta Clara, Sta Clara–SF; surveys to run in 1795) + ⭐⭐ a demographic-causation passage blaming stagnant growth on maternal abortion/infanticide + gálico + the shift to hard mission labor/diet
