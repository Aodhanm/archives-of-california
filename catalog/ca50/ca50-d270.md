# reports (via the Tte. Comandante of Monterey, 11 Mar) the death of the Teniente Coronel, Captain of the 1.ª Compañía de 

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n281** · Sav p. 282 · tomo p. iii.373–74 · 1802-03-02 · Savage abstract · 

reports (via the Tte. Comandante of Monterey, 11 Mar) the death of the Teniente Coronel, Captain of the 1.ª Compañía de Voluntarios de Cataluña & Commander of the four Alta-CA presidios (almost certainly Pedro de Alberni — the Catalan Volunteers' commander & Nootka veteran, whose death at Monterey is dated 11 Mar 1802; the abstract gives titles not name, so inferred); Lt. José Font ordered to take charge of the company & execute the predecessor's will; Sgt. J. Joaquín Pico had held the company's interests
