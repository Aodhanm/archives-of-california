# *Informe sobre lugares a propósito para fundar nuevas Misiones* (see Expanded Entry 8) — the definitive site-selection s

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n191** · Sav p. 192–194 · tomo p. ii.267–69 · 1796-01-13 · Savage abstract · 

*Informe sobre lugares a propósito para fundar nuevas Misiones* (see Expanded Entry 8) — the definitive site-selection synthesis of the five 1795 reconnaissances: (1) SD–SJC = 'Palú' [→ San Luis Rey]; (2) SG–SB = still undecided (candidate far from firewood) [→ San Fernando]; (3) SL–SA = 'las Pozas' [→ San Miguel]; (4) Monterey–Sta Clara = two sites, near 'San Bernardino' (N) or 'San Benito' (S) [→ San Juan Bautista]; (5) N of Sta Clara toward SF = the estuary site the explorers called 'San Francisco Solano' [→ San José]; all 'con bastante Gentilidad'
