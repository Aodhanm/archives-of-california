# *Informes anuales sobre las Misiones… en 1794* (Baja) — Savage abstracts only the qualitative additions (pop/ag data fol

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n169** · Sav p. 170–174 · tomo p. ii.125–147 · 1794 · Savage abstract · 

*Informes anuales sobre las Misiones… en 1794* (Baja) — Savage abstracts only the qualitative additions (pop/ag data folded into the tables): a 1794 Dominican friar roster (San Miguel: Frs. Mariano Apolinario & Mariano Yoldi[?]; Sto Tomás: Fr. José Florente[?]; San Vicente: Fr. Tomás Valdellón; … Loreto: Fr. Cayetano Pallás, Presidente; San Javier: Frs. Miguel Gallego & Gerónimo Soldevilla; Todos Santos: Fr. Mariano Fernández) + per-mission lists of new liturgical items, tools & *edificios*. ⭐ Santo Tomás relocated to a new site June 1794 by superior order (buildings rebuilt); San José del Cabo has a 30-vol library + an *alambique* (still); La Purísima wine gear (23 tolvas, botijas, a *cal-y-canto* winepress). ⚠ friar surnames read at scale=2
