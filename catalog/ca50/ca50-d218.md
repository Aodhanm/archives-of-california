# *Certificado de la fundación de Sta Catalina* — the frontier-commander's act-of-possession certificate: full founding ce

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n249–n251** · Sav p. 250–252 · tomo p. ii.682 · 1797-11-12 · verbatim transcription · 

*Certificado de la fundación de Sta Catalina* — the frontier-commander's act-of-possession certificate: full founding ceremony (blessed water, cross adored & raised, Litany of the Saints, sung Mass + sermon, solemn Te-Deum, baptismal font blessed, one Gentile girl offered herself & was baptized), possession taken in the King's name with a *salva* by his troop — ⭐ 'se ministró en este sitio, no por usurparles sus tierras' (established here not to usurp their lands); dedicated to Sta Catalina Virgen y Mártir
