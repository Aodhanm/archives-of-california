# *Estragos del terremoto de 1812* — the Great Stone Church (cal y canto, blessed 8 Sep 1806) collapsed in one minute duri

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n333–n334** · Sav p. 334–335 · tomo p. iv.220–21 · 1812-12-31 · verbatim transcription · 

*Estragos del terremoto de 1812* — the Great Stone Church (cal y canto, blessed 8 Sep 1806) collapsed in one minute during the first Mass, 8 Dec 1812: the tower swayed twice, fell onto the façade & the congregation, bringing down the vault to the crossing — 40 Indians killed (38 adults, 2 children), only 6 escaped 'como por milagro'; no gente de razón died (cf. the church begun at Doc 213)
