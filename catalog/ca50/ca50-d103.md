# *Notas* on the Alta-CA missions — a rich qualitative report (see Expanded Entry 5): 1794 totals (1,746 baptisms, 502 mar

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n137** · Sav p. 4 (T.II) · tomo p. ii.4–8 · 1795-03-01 · Savage abstract · 

*Notas* on the Alta-CA missions — a rich qualitative report (see Expanded Entry 5): 1794 totals (1,746 baptisms, 502 marriages, 736 deaths, net +1,015); notes 26 sínodo-friars + 4 supernumerarios for 13 missions; a striking passage attributing infant deaths to mothers' *aborto/sofocar*; 9 numbered notes on churches, sínodos (ex-Jesuit obras pías), doctrina, mission industry (wool textiles, cotton at San Luis/San Gabriel, San Carlos artisans in carpentry/masonry, SF blacksmiths, tanning), crops; ⭐ '17 or 18 languages from San Francisco to San Diego' (Channel [Chumash] more inclined to Spanish); ⭐⭐ proposal to found 3 new missions (SD–San Juan Cap.; San Gabriel–San Buenaventura; San Luis–San Antonio) — the rationale for the 1797–98 second-wave foundings
