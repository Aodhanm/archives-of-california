# 1828 SB jurisdiction *estado* (2,854 souls / ~2,348 Indians; 4 ministers) — names the ranchos with distances/directions 

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n391–n393** · Sav p. 392–394 · tomo p. v.235 · 1828-12-31 · Savage abstract · 

1828 SB jurisdiction *estado* (2,854 souls / ~2,348 Indians; 4 ministers) — names the ranchos with distances/directions from the cabecera: Rancho del Refugio (10 lg NO), San Julián (15 NO), El Cuyo (17 E), Calaguaza (2 N), San Marcos (7 NO), Dos Pueblos (6 W), Sta Paula (7 NE), Cahuenga (6 SE), San Ramón (5 N) + Misiones Sta Inés/Sta Bárbara/San Buenaventura/San Fernando. Note: the few ex-mission & gentile Indians 'sirviendo á particulares… por los ranchos ó dentro de la población'
