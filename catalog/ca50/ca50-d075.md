# *Inventarios [físicos] de las misiones de la Baja California* — mission-by-mission building/material survey of the entir

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n105** · Sav p. 106–111 · tomo p. i.562–671 · 1793 · Savage abstract · 

*Inventarios [físicos] de las misiones de la Baja California* — mission-by-mission building/material survey of the entire Dominican peninsula: Loreto (church 56×7 v., cal-y-canto, 5 altars, 22 sculpture images, 2 gilt-silver custodias, 9 chalices, library 466 books); San José del Cabo (86 souls, chapel collapsed in floods); Todos Santos (church 24 v., *fragua*, cane-mill, 5 calderas + 2 alambiques for panocha & aguardiente); San Francisco Javier (church 41 v., dome); *[n107–111]* Comondú (3-nave stone church 30×13 v., 3 altars, 25 paintings, library 126 vols + 'una historia de California en 3 tomos'); La Purísima (25 v.; padres' house library 200 vols); Nª Sª de Guadalupe (32 v. adobe); ⭐ San Ignacio (single nave w/ *crucero*, 141×7½ v. = largest; stone-vaulted, 4 altars, rich plate, rooms lodging travelers/widows/orphans; 800 fig trees, 150 cattle); Santa Gertrudis (adobe/tule, poor); San Francisco Borja (32 v.); San Fernando [Velicatá] (33 v.); Rosario (46 v.); Santo Domingo (18 v.); San Vicente Ferrer (32 v.); Santo Tomás de Aquino (12 v.); San Miguel (26 v.) — church dimensions, roofing (bóveda vs. jacal/tule), plate, ornaments, page-refs per mission
