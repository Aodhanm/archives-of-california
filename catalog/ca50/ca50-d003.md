# Informe on the state of the ex-Jesuit ('regulares extinguidos') missions of Baja California (*copia*) — numbered ¶ surve

**C-A 50 (State Papers, Missions), scan n9** · Sav p. 10–18 · tomo p. i.31–33 · 1786-08 [⚠ header says '1770-10-20'] · Savage abstract · 

Informe on the state of the ex-Jesuit ('regulares extinguidos') missions of Baja California (*copia*) — numbered ¶ survey of ~16 missions (Santiago, San José del Cabo, Todos Santos, Loreto, San Javier, Comondú, Cadegomó, Guadalupe, Mulegé, San Ignacio, Sta Gertrudis, San Borja, Sta María→San Fernando Velicatá); catastrophic demographic collapse (5 nations ~22,000 souls '40 yrs ago' → near-extinct; Pericú <⅕ remain); *viruela* of 1781 devastated Sta Gertrudis (Cochimí); ¶44 the syphilis (*mal gálico*) so pervasive 'las madres no conciben… el feto sale con poca esperanza de vida,' some missions >1 yr w/o a baptism, 'triples los muertos que los nacidos'; mission economy, communal property, $350 *sínodo*/missionary, Indian *gobernadores*; blunt: 'los indios de California todos son igualmente perezosos, ineptos y estúpidos.' Savage closes: 'fechado en Agosto de 1786'
