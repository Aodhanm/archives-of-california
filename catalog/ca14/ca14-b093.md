# VILLA DE BRANCIFORTE FOUNDING: Borica to Viceroy (Aug 4, 1796): SF site rejected — Alberni + Córdoba confirmed terrain u

**C-A 14 (Prov. St. Pap. XXII), scan n259–262** · orig. p. ~xxii.641–5 · 1797-05-15 · Savage abstract · detail E · [block B #93]

VILLA DE BRANCIFORTE FOUNDING: Borica to Viceroy (Aug 4, 1796): SF site rejected — Alberni + Córdoba confirmed terrain unsuitable. Santa Cruz proposed: "best site from Cabo San Lucas to SF" — water, irrigable land, pasture, timber, stone, lime. Mission has 507 neophytes, 530 cattle. Settlers: retired/invalid soldiers. King to build each settler an adobe house (tiled roof, locked doors, ≤$100). Provision: 2 mares, 2 cows + calves, 6 sheep, 2 goats, 1 yoke oxen, plow, hoe, axe, sickle, escopeta, adarga, 2 horses + $10/month 1st year. SJ (18 yrs) and LA (15 yrs) residents still in tule huts. Córdoba to survey, plan villa incl. Church, Casa Real, Hospital + budget
