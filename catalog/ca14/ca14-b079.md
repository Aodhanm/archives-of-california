# VICEROY ORDERS FORT SJ REPAIRED (Oct 27, 1796): "única defensa del Pto de S.n Franc.o," one of most exposed ports. Trans

**C-A 14 (Prov. St. Pap. XXII), scan n252–253** · orig. p. ~xxii.629–30 · 1797-04-04 · Savage abstract · detail S · [block B #79]

VICEROY ORDERS FORT SJ REPAIRED (Oct 27, 1796): "única defensa del Pto de S.n Franc.o," one of most exposed ports. Transfer SD coastal guns if needed. Córdoba to SF immediately after Monterey merlons. Place longest-range cannons at channel front (4 east, 2 west). Then build SECOND BATTERY to prevent hostile anchoring; use surplus Fort SJ cannons + Saavedra's. Alberni (Arms Comdte) and presidial company to provide resources
