# CÓRDOBA SURVEY RESULTS: Will forward plans to Viceroy. SD battery shouldn't have been circular (excess cost). Córdoba's 

**C-A 14 (Prov. St. Pap. XXII), scan n249** · orig. p. ~xxii.620+ · 1797-01 · Savage abstract · detail E · [block B #68]

CÓRDOBA SURVEY RESULTS: Will forward plans to Viceroy. SD battery shouldn't have been circular (excess cost). Córdoba's opinion: "en toda la Costa no hay punto qe se pueda fortificar ventajosamente" — no point on the entire coast can be advantageously fortified; must increase troops. Will continue reconnaissances on return to Monterey
