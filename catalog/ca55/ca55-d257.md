# ⭐⭐⭐ Do NOT aid the two English ships at Bodega (those of his letter of the 2nd) who sought to resupply / harbored desert

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n219** · tomo p. vii.579+ · Sav p. 220 · 1793-03-27 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ Do NOT aid the two English ships at Bodega (those of his letter of the 2nd) who sought to resupply / harbored deserters — wait for the Viceroy's decision, "á no ser que sucediese antes peligro de muerte." Arrillaga's foreign-exclusion policy applied directly to Bodega, the policy follow-up to the Jan 1793 sighting (Doc 164/Entry 13)
