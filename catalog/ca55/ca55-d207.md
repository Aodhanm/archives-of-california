# ⭐ Bundle incl.: *factura* of San Blas provisions (via comisario Fran.º Hijosa) for the SF troop, carried on the frigate 

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n152–n156** · tomo p. vii.34–47 · Sav p. 153–157 · 1791-10-01 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐ Bundle incl.: *factura* of San Blas provisions (via comisario Fran.º Hijosa) for the SF troop, carried on the frigate *Aránzazu*; ⭐ founding of Mission Santa Cruz (1791) — aided P. Pres. Lasuén (at Sta Clara) to scout the Santa Cruz site; 5 sailors assigned as the new mission's servants; Tte. José Darío Argüello sent 12 men + a sergeant, well armed, to join a Monterey party at San Juan Bautista; (Nº8) ⭐ a foreign-ship signal — ~10 Sept, Lasuén at the San Felipe ranchería (≈6 leguas from SF mission) heard 6–7 cannon-shots near midnight, "como entre cañones," seeming a ship off the port to leeward; asks the SF presidio's help to verify the new Santa Cruz *reducción* before the rains close the roads
