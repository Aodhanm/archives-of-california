# On weaving works: 180 varas of cloth for the church (yellow/green/blue/purple *bayetas*, jaspered white warp); 80 blanke

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n102–n104** · tomo p. i.563–564 · Sav p. 103–105 · 1797-04-01 · Savage abstract · 

On weaving works: 180 varas of cloth for the church (yellow/green/blue/purple *bayetas*, jaspered white warp); 80 blankets on the broad loom; 150 *pastoras* for Sta Bárbara; the weaver's wife teaches Indian girls to spin; carpet dyed with *palo campeche, brasil, zacatlaxcal* & a *grana* (cochineal) stone (short of lemons, used vinegar); colors poor — the Indians get better ones; needs a *batán* & press; instruction given by "Enrique"
