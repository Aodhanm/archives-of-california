⭐ Royalist reaction to Mexican independence: received his *papeletas e impresos*; scorns the *"destinos"* being printed since the independence; praises *"la inmortal, incomparable nación Española"* with great resources to make itself respected; Gervasio [Argüello] "no ha querido jurar la detestada independencia" and may return to his family on the means given for his good conduct
