# ⭐⭐ Fortification of San Francisco & Monterey — see expanded entry. Branciforte acknowledges engineer Alberto de Córdoba'

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n169–n172** · tomo p. B.32–35 (abs. pp.~78–85) · Sav p. 170–173 · 1801-07-18 (copy of 1796-12-29) · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Fortification of San Francisco & Monterey — see expanded entry. Branciforte acknowledges engineer Alberto de Córdoba's 3 plans (SF presidio; Monterey battery; Santa Cruz); the Batería de San Joaquín, "única defensa" of SF port, is poorly laid out & defective — orders it strengthened; Tte. Col. Pedro de Alberni; two 24-pdr cannons at the port mouth; arms/provisions sent via the prepared frigates & paquebot *San Carlos*; received in Cal. 15 Mar 1797
