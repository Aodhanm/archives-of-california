# ⭐⭐⭐ English-translation letter proposing Russo-Spanish commerce & joint fur-hunting — see Entry 16. Hagemeister left ill

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n284–n286** · tomo p. viii.579–622 · Sav p. 285–287 · ~1820 (Savage "1810") · verbatim transcription · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ English-translation letter proposing Russo-Spanish commerce & joint fur-hunting — see Entry 16. Hagemeister left ill; the RAC brig *Ilmena* is bound for "Port Ross" with St-Petersburg papers & goods; sends agent Khlebnikov ("Clebnicoff") to arrange "a commercial intercourse"; offers goods for grain; recalls Hagemeister's petition to the Russian ambassador at Madrid for leave to hunt furs on the California coast "in company with His Catholic Majesty's subjects, on equal shares"; gift of a Russian glass
