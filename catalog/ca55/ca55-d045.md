# The only ship carrying the mail to the *otra banda* is in deplorable state — beg the Viceroy for another; the $30,000 fo

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n30** · tomo p. i.108–109 · Sav p. 31 · 1821-01-25 · Savage abstract · 

The only ship carrying the mail to the *otra banda* is in deplorable state — beg the Viceroy for another; the $30,000 for the 4 presidios barely began to pay them, so he has imposed duties on goods entering & leaving the Peninsula; same scarcity expected for B. Cal.
