**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n147–n151** · tomo p. vii.26–33 · Sav p. 148–152 · 1776-11-23 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Report (per Ortega's 16 Nov order): reached the new mission the 17th; confirms via Cabo Orlas Beltrán the "Trabuco" incident — armed gentiles tried to stop the cattle & cargo for the mission (the P. Presidente [Serra] & 3 soldiers escorting); the gentile-captains of nearby rancherías, wishing to be Christian, calmed via the San Gabriel interpreter; on the deserters (incl. Manuel Robles) & their effects; leaves soldier Armenta there pending the lieutenant's decision
