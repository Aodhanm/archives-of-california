# Report (per Fiscal Hernández de Alba) on Borica's request that the King annually send a *memoria* of goods to the pueblo

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n323+** · tomo p. ix.~50+ · Sav p. 324+ · 1797-05-24 · Savage abstract · ⭐

Report (per Fiscal Hernández de Alba) on Borica's request that the King annually send a *memoria* of goods to the pueblos — a LONG economic *dictamen* running n323→n345 (tomo pp.~50–62): critiques the mission economy (Indians clothed/fed but "no se conoce industria ni comercio"); argues for California fisheries — salmon "de los 35 grados al Norte," salt-cod/*bacalao*, anchoa, *salmones salpresos* — as a branch of industry & commerce; the 6,000-peso *memoria*, administrators vs. *habilitados*, the cost of San-Blas shipping. ⭐ (economic-history interest)
