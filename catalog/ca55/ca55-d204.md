# ⭐⭐ On a tumult of Indians at "Pala" & resistance near the new Mission San Juan Capistrano: gentiles tried (arms in hand)

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n142–n147** · tomo p. vii.~15–? · Sav p. 143–148 · 1776-11-29 (cert. of 13 Nov *providencias*) · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ On a tumult of Indians at "Pala" & resistance near the new Mission San Juan Capistrano: gentiles tried (arms in hand) to ambush soldiers (Ant. Peña; Dolores Domínguez & Juan Acevedo) driving cattle from San Gabriel to the mission, meaning to rob it — averted by the San Gabriel interpreter's parley; orders Sgt. Mariano Carrillo + 4 soldiers to the mission to investigate the gentiles' intent, overawe them, and inventory deserters' effects found there
