**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n165–n168** · tomo p. vii.28–31 (abs. pp.~–72) · Sav p. 166–169 · 1797-05-05 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Observations on the difficulties of founding the Villa de Branciforte (per Viceroy Branciforte's order, via P. Pres. Pedro Callejas) next to Mission Santa Cruz — Lasuén thinks founding a *villa* so near the mission all but "imposible," yet will obey; lists mission livestock issued to settlers (13 tame horses, 21 mares + 2 studs, 13 ox-yokes, 160 sheep, 232 ewes, 56 young bulls…); on Santa Cruz's poverty, ruined church & dwellings, grain shortage *(corrected — earlier mis-attributed to "Borica→Viceroy"; read mid-document)*
