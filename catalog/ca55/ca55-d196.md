# ⭐⭐ Dictamen on the seizure of the American frigate *Cromo[?]* — repeats the Viceroy's 19 Feb order: if the *Cromo* reach

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n134–n136** · tomo p. i.~737–774 · Sav p. 135–137 · 1808-12-07 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐

⭐⭐ Dictamen on the seizure of the American frigate *Cromo[?]* — repeats the Viceroy's 19 Feb order: if the *Cromo* reaches their districts, secure its cargo in deposit, make a formal inventory, hold the ship in the bay, arrest the captain/master/supercargo & crew, seize the navigation papers, take their depositions promptly & separately so they cannot collude — to establish the voyage's true provenance & objects and whether they violated the King's orders & the treaties of Peace, Limits & Navigation with the United States or meant to run contraband; remit all to the Viceroy with a report. A detailed foreign-ship interdiction protocol
