# ⭐ On swearing the Constitución Política de las Cortes Españolas (1820 Trienio re-swearing); Comte. de Frontera José Manu

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n19** · tomo p. i.77–79 · Sav p. 20 · 1820-09-27 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ On swearing the Constitución Política de las Cortes Españolas (1820 Trienio re-swearing); Comte. de Frontera José Manuel Ruiz fears Indian attack, no means to defend, will withdraw to San Diego; English ships arriving "con objeto de pescar" (to fish) will leave some revenue to the Real Hacienda for the duties they incur; 3 San Blas ships + 1 from Callao at México with goods but "sin un centavo de dinero"; everyone from the last soldier up is unpaid; waters scarce, everything costly
