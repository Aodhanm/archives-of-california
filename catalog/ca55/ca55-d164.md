# ⭐⭐⭐ "Buques ingleses en Bodega" (2nd report; see Entry 13) — two English ships anchored at Bodega 15–17 Jan 1793; cannon

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n97** · tomo p. i.545–546 · Sav p. 98 · 1793-01-30 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ "Buques ingleses en Bodega" (2nd report; see Entry 13) — two English ships anchored at Bodega 15–17 Jan 1793; cannon-signals heard ("5 cañonazos," answered with one); fog hid them near the harbor mouth; Vancouver sailed from Monterey the 13th, and Sal does not doubt it was Vancouver who fired the shots to call those ships — presumed bound for Bodega ever since he was at San Francisco; will watch & take reports from the *indios isleros*
