# Sworn relation of *mermas* (losses) of San Blas goods, $640; will not permit introduction of foreign goods except those 

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n6–n7** · tomo p. i.35–40 · Sav p. 7–8 · 1796-10-22 (+ Jul entries) · Savage abstract · 

Sworn relation of *mermas* (losses) of San Blas goods, $640; will not permit introduction of foreign goods except those under the Habilitado Gral's facturas, confiscating any others (Jul 9); received 614 ewes (100 wethers) from Tte. Antonio Grajera, wool at San Gabriel 20 rs/arroba (Jul 28); must economize on the presidio church works, dismiss the 10 Indian workers (Jul 21)
