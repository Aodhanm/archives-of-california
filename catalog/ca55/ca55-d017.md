# ⭐ Long verbatim *dictamen* on the pearl-fishery leases (*buceo de perlas*) of the Tepoca coast & the Californias — see e

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n10–n18** · tomo p. i.51–67 · Sav p. 11–19 · 1783-07-18 · verbatim transcription · ⭐

⭐ Long verbatim *dictamen* on the pearl-fishery leases (*buceo de perlas*) of the Tepoca coast & the Californias — see expanded entry. Three questions: (1) whether to auction the lease; (2) whether divers owe a *licencia* right beyond the royal fifth; (3) into which caja it's paid. Cites *Recopilación* lib. 4 tít. 25 leyes 29–30 (free pearl-fishing for vassals & *naturales* with permit, paying the fifth), R.O. 10 Apr 1777, Junta de México 1 Apr 1776, José de Gálvez's *instrucción* at La Paz 14 Apr 1769 (arts. 6, 14), the $13,000/yr offer of militia capt. Manuel García Morales, Fiscal of the Audiencia de México (8 Aug 1780), and a 1755 Panama-fishery cédula precedent
