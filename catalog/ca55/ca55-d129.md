# ⭐⭐⭐ "Buques ingleses en Bodega" — see Entry 9. An Indian "de las Islas" before the presidio reports two ships in the Pue

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n66–n67** · tomo p. i.296 · Sav p. 67–68 · 1792-10-08 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐ "Buques ingleses en Bodega" — see Entry 9. An Indian "de las Islas" before the presidio reports two ships in the Puerto de la Bodega; the foreigners' speech not understood; sailors wore clothes "con nuestras vueltas," believed to say they were English; ashore 4 days & still there, taken water, firewood, hunted many deer, asked the Indians for cattle, showing them otters beforehand
