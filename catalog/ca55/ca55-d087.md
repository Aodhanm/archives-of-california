**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n46–n47** · tomo p. i.155–156 · Sav p. 47–48 · 1822-05-14 · Savage abstract · 

San José pueblo discipline: jail the mother of militiaman Santiago Vázquez 15 days in the *monjerío* for insulting Sgt. Hernández; warn Encarnación Altamirano, [Perea] Galarza[?] & Ignacio Pacheco's wife (2 months' jail) for throwing filth in the *acequias* & cursing authorities; bachelors/widowers without houses to sleep at the guard; a third of the year's tallow from the *correría* to fund building a jail & casa real
