# ⭐⭐⭐⭐ "Diario de novedades" — his inspection tour of the escorts under SF/Monterey jurisdiction, ordered by Hermenegildo 

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n83–n90** · tomo p. i.~404–437 (rep. 426) · Sav p. 84–91 · 1796-05 → 1798-03 · Savage abstract · ⭐⭐⭐⭐

⭐⭐⭐⭐ "Diario de novedades" — his inspection tour of the escorts under SF/Monterey jurisdiction, ordered by Hermenegildo Sal, kept from 22 Dec 1796 (but folding in 1796 events) — see Entry 12. Highlights: a neophyte woman from Nootka; Borica's Order of Santiago investiture; H.M.S. *Providence* under Broughton, 4–18 June 1796 (11-gun salute); a Boston/American frigate (Capt. "Juan Gare[?]", 26 persons, 6 guns) at Carmel/Monterey Oct–Nov 1796, with English sailors (+ a woman) put ashore at pistol-point & rounded up (10 set to presidio work); 18 ships sighted off San Diego, Oct 1797 (alarm relayed up the coast); Indians reporting "cañones en la mar" near San Antonio; detailed arms/munitions inspections (Dec 1796 & Jan 1797); a 1798 sentinel wounding the guard corporal
