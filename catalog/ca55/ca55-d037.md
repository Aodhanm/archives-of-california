# ⭐ Cannot supply the Province's troops, lacking means even for his own; situation very bad — no commerce in the Peninsula

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n25–n26** · tomo p. i.96–98 · Sav p. 26–27 · 1820-12-15 · Savage abstract · ⭐

⭐ Cannot supply the Province's troops, lacking means even for his own; situation very bad — no commerce in the Peninsula; only non-Indians paying *diezmo* yield anything; has begged the Viceroy for *gente industriosa*; urges moving the Apostadero de San Blas to the Province, rich in shipbuilding timber & mines left "vírgenes" for want of skilled hands — "en 54 años que está en poder de España, no se han disfrutado ninguna de sus riquezas"
