# Depredations by Indians of the Arroyo del Pájaro: soldier Pablo Acebes, escorting a servant + an Indian of Sta Cruz with

**C-A 55 (St. Pap. Sacramento VI–IX), scan n70–n71** · tomo p. i.306–308 · Sav p. 71–72 · 1792-09-20 · Savage abstract · 

Depredations by Indians of the Arroyo del Pájaro: soldier Pablo Acebes, escorting a servant + an Indian of Sta Cruz with *Memoria* cargo, was halted, the *naturales* unloaded the cargo & threatened them with arms; Acebes mounted & fled; the Mont.→Sta Cruz road is unsafe (only passable Jun–Aug); few troops, most occupied conveying *memorias* & serving the frigates *Concepción* & *Gertrudis*
