# Original English sworn statement: impossible to proceed after a 'boisterous passage of 45 days' without provisions & wat

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n5** · Sav p. 6 · tomo p. i.12 · 1816-01-17 · Savage abstract · 

Original English sworn statement: impossible to proceed after a 'boisterous passage of 45 days' without provisions & water, the schr. 'having had her broadside stove in'; anchored 'in the first place where we discover.d houses.' Signed Master H. Gyzelaar, C. Off. Jacob Smith, 2nd Off. Wm. Owen Ines, sailmaker Archi. Bean, seamen Heft/Rowe/Benderfeldt/Dennison (some by mark).
