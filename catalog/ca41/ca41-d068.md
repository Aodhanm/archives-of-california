# Opposes export duties & burdensome alcabalas ('esto sería hostilizar demasiado al Comercio'); suggests appointing José M

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n62–n63** · Sav p. 62–63 · tomo p. II.23–24 (orig. 509–11) · 1833-06-05 · Savage abstract · 

Opposes export duties & burdensome alcabalas ('esto sería hostilizar demasiado al Comercio'); suggests appointing José M.ª Maldonado to the civil-treasury branch; recommends consulting the Supreme Govt on creating the land customs.
