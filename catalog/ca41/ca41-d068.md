# Opines against export duties — Monterey is too small to bear them, and taxing purchases made without the aduana's knowle

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n62** · Sav p. 62–63 · tomo p. ii.509–11 · 1833-06-05 · Savage abstract · 

Opines against export duties — Monterey is too small to bear them, and taxing purchases made without the aduana's knowledge would 'hostilizar demasiado al Comercio'; suggests José M.ª Maldonado for the hacienda post; consult the Sup. Govt. on the *aduana terrestre*.
