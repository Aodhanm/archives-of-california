# The incoming Comandante Gen. / Jefe Político of Alta Cal. reports the lack of resources and the 28 Aug 1832 mutiny of hi

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n34** · Sav p. 34–38 · tomo p. i.33–37; 487–92 · 1832-09-24 · Savage abstract · 

The incoming Comandante Gen. / Jefe Político of Alta Cal. reports the lack of resources and the 28 Aug 1832 mutiny of his troops that wrecked his voyage — embarked Acapulco 17 Jul, landed Cabo San Lucas while the ship went to San Blas/Mazatlán for troops, religiosos & funds; the troops rose, seized arms/ship/cargo/artillery, fired on the shore party, sailed off (see Entry 6). Asks Guaymas to negotiate a $15,000 draft / a $10,000 loan (1–5% premium, repayable in Sinaloa or Alta Cal. 'con frutos del país') and supply 25 muskets, powder & 570 lb lead via the national brig *Facio*; grants full power to contract; also wrote José Antonio Aguirre.
