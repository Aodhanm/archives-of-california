# Long account of his delayed voyage to Cal. as comandante gral./jefe político: embarked Acapulco 17 July, disembarked Cab

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n34–n38** · Sav p. 34–38 · tomo p. 33–37 (orig. 487–92) · 1832-09-24 · Savage abstract · 

Long account of his delayed voyage to Cal. as comandante gral./jefe político: embarked Acapulco 17 July, disembarked Cabo San Lucas; 28 Aug troop mutiny — soldiers seized the ship's arms, munitions, cargo, funds, pay, artillery (of Nation, officers & merchants) & sailed off at midnight (dest. San Blas). Asks Guaymas to honor an $18,000 draft + supply 25 muskets, powder & lead; authorizes a $10,000 loan (repayable in Sinaloa or Alta Cal. 'con frutos del país').
