# Demands the *Lydia* be sent to San Blas so he can claim justice there; protests the 'excesos' committed aboard and the b

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n15** · Sav p. 15–16 · tomo p. i.41, 45–6 · 1816-03-05 · Savage abstract · 

Demands the *Lydia* be sent to San Blas so he can claim justice there; protests the 'excesos' committed aboard and the boat given him; cargo was landed without sealing the hatches 'como es costumbre, con sellos'; his crew has lived on fishing for 3 weeks and is now barred from fishing — 'tratan de matarle de hambre.' (English original at tomo pp. 45–6.)
