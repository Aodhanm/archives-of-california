# Agrees to collect alcabalas without oppressive burdens that would 'aniquilar la naciente y única industria del país' (th

Agrees to collect alcabalas without oppressive burdens that would 'aniquilar la naciente y única industria del país' (the cattle trade); charge by value; uniformize across the Territory; consult Mexico.
