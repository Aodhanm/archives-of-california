# Roster of 'Prisioneros americanos' from the Am. frigate *Albatross* (Capt. William Smith) and schooner *Lydia*. *Albatro

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n3** · Sav p. 3 · tomo p. 3 · 1816-02-03 · Savage abstract · 

Roster of 'Prisioneros americanos' from the Am. frigate *Albatross* (Capt. William Smith) and schooner *Lydia*. *Albatross*: incl. Thomas Doak, Nathaniel Davis (deserters, shipped on the goleta to Monterey), William Stagg, Henry Sephem(?), Repian, Alrary(?), Bab (held at presidio); carpenter Dolibes(?) also shipped to Monterey. *Lydia*: Capt. Henry Gyzelaar (Holandés, shipped to Monterey), 1st pilot Jacob Smith, 2nd William Owen Ines(?), sailmaker Archi Bean, seamen George Heft, James Rowe, Johan Bunderfeldt, Nathaniel Dennison, Thomas Bennet (held at presidio), carpenter William Moore (shipped to Monterey).
