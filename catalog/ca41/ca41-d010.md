# English. Demands the *Lydia* be sent to San Blas to claim justice; protests the 'excesos' aboard & the poor boat given h

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n15–n16** · Sav p. 15–16 · tomo p. 14–15 (orig. 41, 45–6) · 1816-03-05 · verbatim transcription · 

English. Demands the *Lydia* be sent to San Blas to claim justice; protests the 'excesos' aboard & the poor boat given him; cargo taken ashore though hatches were not sealed 'as is customary'; complains he has nothing to eat — lived on fishing 3 weeks & is now barred from fishing, 'tratan de matarle de hambre.'
