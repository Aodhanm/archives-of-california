# Smuggling: an Am. frigate anchored in Sta Bárbara roadstead against the rule that ships first come to Monterey to nation

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n52** · Sav p. 52–53 · tomo p. ii.292–3 · 1833-05-07 · Savage abstract · 

Smuggling: an Am. frigate anchored in Sta Bárbara roadstead against the rule that ships first come to Monterey to nationalize cargo; 'Santa Bárbara y San Francisco son el criadero' of contraband; a newly-discovered overland route Los Ángeles → Presidio del Altar → Sonora feeding New-Mexican trade; needs guards even sent 'de México,' 'porque… como los de este país son todos de una familia, todos se cubren con un manto'; recommends Pedro del Castillo as guard.
