# Illegalities & contraband: an Am. frigate anchored at the SB roadstead against the law. 'Santa Barb.a y San Fran.co son 

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n52–n53** · Sav p. 52–53 · tomo p. II.13–14 (orig. 292–3) · 1833-05-07 · Savage abstract · 

Illegalities & contraband: an Am. frigate anchored at the SB roadstead against the law. 'Santa Barb.a y San Fran.co son el criadero' of contraband; an overland smuggling route LA→Presidio del Altar→Sonora (5 days) carrying New Mexico goods; needs a receiver at SB + corporal/guard at San Pedro 'porque… como los de este país son todos de una familia, todos se cubren con un manto.' Recommends Pedro del Castillo.
