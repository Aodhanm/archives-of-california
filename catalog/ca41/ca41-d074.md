# The LA town council resists Osio's collection of the matanza alcabala as an 'excessive' duty misapplied 'según el arance

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n66** · Sav p. 66–67 · tomo p. ii.548–71 · 1833-05-24 · Savage abstract · 

The LA town council resists Osio's collection of the matanza alcabala as an 'excessive' duty misapplied 'según el arancel del distrito federal'; resolves collection be suspended pending the Jefe Político's ruling — cannot compel citizens under 'una ley hecha exclusivam.te p.a el distrito federal.' Council: Felipe Lugo (reg. decano, for the ill alcalde), Ignacio M.ª Alvarado, José Sepúlveda, Antonio Machado, Tiburcio Tapia, Vicente Moraga (sec.); Alcalde José Antonio Carrillo tells Osio to desist.
