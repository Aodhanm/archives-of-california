# Municipal act opposing Receiver Osio's excessive alcabala on cattle-slaughter (a federal-district tariff misapplied); or

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n66–n67** · Sav p. 66–67 · tomo p. II.26–27 (orig. 568–71) · 1833-05-24 · Savage abstract · 

Municipal act opposing Receiver Osio's excessive alcabala on cattle-slaughter (a federal-district tariff misapplied); ordered him to suspend collection pending the jefe político's ruling. Regidores: Felipe Lugo (decano, acting for the ill alcalde), Ignacio M.ª Alvarado, José Sepúlveda, Antonio Machado, Tiburcio Tapia; sec. Vicente Moraga; alcalde José A. Carrillo.
