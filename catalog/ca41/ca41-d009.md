# Reports the *Lydia* sent to Monterey under Gyzelaar (Fr. Luis Martínez having interested himself in her, but refusing re

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n9** · Sav p. 9–10 · tomo p. i.— · 1816-02-03 · Savage abstract · 

Reports the *Lydia* sent to Monterey under Gyzelaar (Fr. Luis Martínez having interested himself in her, but refusing responsibility); the *Albatross*'s escape/disappearance; withdrew the Refugio detachment; put Sgt. Carlos Carrillo + 6 armed men aboard the *Lydia*, to reconnoiter the near islands (esp. Santa Rosa) en route.
