# Long explanatory report acknowledging the 18th's order approving the *Lydia*'s capture. Confesses his own 'poquísima int

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n10–n15** · Sav p. 10–15 · tomo p. 9–14 (orig. 25–40) · 1816-01-22 · Savage abstract · 

Long explanatory report acknowledging the 18th's order approving the *Lydia*'s capture. Confesses his own 'poquísima inteligencia en asuntos navales'; on Fr. Luis Martínez's pilotage offer ('no sé qué hacer'); the *Albatross* will return (passport ashore, no food, only 7 men, no pilot); Smith self-described as 'el mas pobre de los que han venido á esta costa'; encloses the *Lydia*'s passports, register, and 'la proclama de paz entre su Gob. y el de Inglaterra' (Treaty of Ghent, ending the War of 1812), which the captain asks returned.
