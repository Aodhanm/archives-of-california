# The Russian ship *Elena* (Helena, RAC) anchored at San Francisco (29th) though Monterey was the only port authorized for

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n78–n79** · Sav p. 78–79 · tomo p. IV.6–7 (orig. 467–9) · 1838-02-12 · Savage abstract · 

The Russian ship *Elena* (Helena, RAC) anchored at San Francisco (29th) though Monterey was the only port authorized for foreign trade; questions Vallejo's conduct in permitting it (and an arrangement the two had made at Santa Bárbara); Osio, with 'varios enemigos,' fears for his customs post.
