# Alcabala on the cattle *matanza* — proposed rates: 10% on cattle/bulls/oxen, 6% yearling calves, 4% 2–3 yr calves, 11% o

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n63** · Sav p. 63–64 · tomo p. ii.512–3, 516–7 · 1833-06-17 · Savage abstract · 

Alcabala on the cattle *matanza* — proposed rates: 10% on cattle/bulls/oxen, 6% yearling calves, 4% 2–3 yr calves, 11% on slaughter exports, 8% on peltry; the ranchers protested 'primero dejarían de matar que pagar impuesto tan[to],' a hide being barely worth 12 reales; González concedes they are right, urges charging by value pending Congress.
