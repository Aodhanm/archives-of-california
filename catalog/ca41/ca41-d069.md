# Matanza (cattle-slaughter) tax rates: 10% on cattle, 6% on yearling calves, 4% on 3–4-yr calves, 11% on matanza exports,

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n63–n64** · Sav p. 63–64 · tomo p. II.24–25 (orig. 512–3, 516–7) · 1833-06-17 · Savage abstract · 

Matanza (cattle-slaughter) tax rates: 10% on cattle, 6% on yearling calves, 4% on 3–4-yr calves, 11% on matanza exports, 8% on tanning (curtiduría). Ranchers protested they'd 'primero dejarían de matar que pagar'; slaughter done only for the hide 'que apenas vale 12 reales.' González sides with the ganaderos — charge by value for now.
