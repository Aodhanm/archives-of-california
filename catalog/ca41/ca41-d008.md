# *Lydia* left for Monterey with cargo under Capt. Gyzelaar's custody/responsibility because Fr. Luis Martínez interested 

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n9** · Sav p. 9 · tomo p. 8 · 1816-02-03 · Savage abstract · 

*Lydia* left for Monterey with cargo under Capt. Gyzelaar's custody/responsibility because Fr. Luis Martínez interested himself in it but refused all responsibility; granted the captain 2 of his own sailors + the 2 arrested from the *Albatross*; sent detachment to scout the near islands (esp. Santa Rosa) for the missing *Albatross*; Sgt. Carlos Carrillo + 6 armed soldiers put aboard the *Lydia* at Fr. Martínez's request.
