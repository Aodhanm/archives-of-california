# Original English letter: sailed from New Archangel [Sitka] ('Russian possessions on the NW coast, of which Alex. Baranof

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n8** · Sav p. 8–9 · tomo p. i.16–17 · 1816-01 · Savage abstract · 

Original English letter: sailed from New Archangel [Sitka] ('Russian possessions on the NW coast, of which Alex. Baranoff is Gov. & Commd.r in chief') 1 Dec 1815 for the Sandwich Is.; driven onto the CA coast, near starvation; anchored off Sta Bárbara 15 Jan, went ashore in his leaky pinnace, arrested with 5 men incl. '2 Sandwich Islanders & 1 negro'; 'I did not come here to smuggle… merely to get provisions'; refused to give the ship up — she carries 6 guns (6- & 12-pounders); asks that his men & himself be released.
