# English. Object of voyage & capture: sailed from New Archangel [Sitka], 'possession of Russia... Alex Baranoff Gov. & Co

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n8–n9** · Sav p. 8–9 · tomo p. 7–8 (orig. 16–17) · 1816-01 · verbatim transcription · 

English. Object of voyage & capture: sailed from New Archangel [Sitka], 'possession of Russia... Alex Baranoff Gov. & Comm.d in chief,' 1 Dec 1815, bound for the Sandwich Islands; driven onto Cal. coast, near starvation; anchored, went ashore in leaky pinnace, arrested by a sergeant. Crew of 5: 2 sailors, 2 Sandwich Islanders & 1 negro. Refused to give the ship up: 'I did not come here to smuggle, but... merely to get provisions'; notes she has 6 guns (6- & 12-pounders).
