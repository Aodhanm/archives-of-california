# English letter on the motives for anchoring at Refugio: 45-day gale passage, vessel 'leaking like a wreck,' broadside st

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n6** · Sav p. 7 · tomo p. i.15, 18 · 1816-01-22 · Savage abstract · 

English letter on the motives for anchoring at Refugio: 45-day gale passage, vessel 'leaking like a wreck,' broadside stove in, no provisions 'at Norfolk,' only 1 officer + 1 man aboard & danger of going ashore; if she is not a prize he must insist on staying until condemned in a Court of Enquiry; an 'utter stranger on the coast.'
