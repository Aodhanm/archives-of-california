# Arrival & detention of the Am. schooner *Lydia*, Capt. Henry Gyzelaar, from Sitka (45-day voyage); capt. came ashore 'mu

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n1** · Sav p. 1–2 · tomo p. 1–2 (orig. 5–6) · 1816-01-16 · Savage abstract · 

Arrival & detention of the Am. schooner *Lydia*, Capt. Henry Gyzelaar, from Sitka (45-day voyage); capt. came ashore 'muy confiado'; Luis Ortega sent overland; crew seized by Sgt. Carlos Carrillo + Cadet Santiago Argüello + 7 men; Gyzelaar protests, claims right of 'hospitalidad,' 1st visit to these seas, was coasting the 3 Californias for supplies. (Copy; orig. sent to Viceroy by Solá 5 Oct 1816.)
