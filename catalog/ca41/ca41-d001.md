# Arribada & detention of the Am. schooner *Lydia* at Refugio: Capt. Henry Gyzelaar, from Sitka, 45-day voyage; sought to 

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n1** · Sav p. 2–3 · tomo p. i.5–6 · 1814-01-16 (fwd. 1816-10-05) · Savage abstract · 

Arribada & detention of the Am. schooner *Lydia* at Refugio: Capt. Henry Gyzelaar, from Sitka, 45-day voyage; sought to buy meat/wood, bound for Canton; Guerra detained her with Sgt. Carlos Carrillo, Cadet Santiago Argüello + 7 men; captain claimed the 'right of hospitality… como es costumbre en todo reino á un navegante menesteroso.' Copy; orig. sent to Viceroy by Solá 5 Oct 1816.
