# On the whalers & the wealth they bring — the tonnage duty drove them off to the Sandwich Is. (only ½ real/ton there); ea

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n47** · Sav p. 47 · tomo p. ii.280–1 · 1833-02-15 · Savage abstract · 

On the whalers & the wealth they bring — the tonnage duty drove them off to the Sandwich Is. (only ½ real/ton there); each ship spent $400–500 (plus crews on aguardiente, horse rentals &c); 'algunos años vinieron hasta 60 balleneros' leaving $35–40,000; now that they stay away 'se experimenta mucha pobreza.' Recommends Congress set just 1 real/ton for whalers, the proceeds applied to building the customs house & wharf.
