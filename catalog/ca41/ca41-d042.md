# Anti-smuggling instructions targeting the Russian port of Bodega [Ross]: having learned that 'desde el puerto de Bodega 

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n40** · Sav p. 40–42 · tomo p. ii.2–3, 68–9 · 1834-08-21 · Savage abstract · 

Anti-smuggling instructions targeting the Russian port of Bodega [Ross]: having learned that 'desde el puerto de Bodega se introducían allí mercancías sin satisfacer los derechos nacionales' (at San Francisco), orders that no such introductions be permitted on any pretext; sets *visita*/manifiesto rules — every foreign ship must show its cargo manifest or proof of duties paid at Monterey before unloading; smaller river landings must clear customs too, on pain of *decomiso*.
