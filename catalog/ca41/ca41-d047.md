# The need for a customs house & wharf — took possession 14 Jan, swore before the Alcalde w/ resguardo cadet Fran.co Pache

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n45** · Sav p. 45–46 · tomo p. ii.276–9 · 1833-02-08 · Savage abstract · 

The need for a customs house & wharf — took possession 14 Jan, swore before the Alcalde w/ resguardo cadet Fran.co Pacheco; the presidio is ruinous ('no hay una habitación que no esté cayendo'); for lack of a building & wharf less than ⅓ of duties are collected; a well-run aduana 'debe rendir de 30 á 40 mil pesos anuales' unless 'la revolución acabe con los ganados que son la base fundamental del comercio'; smuggling 'no se puede contener' for want of coast-watchers; needs 2 guards + 2 auxiliaries + boatswain + 4 sailors at Monterey and a receptor each at SB/SF/SD — 'aunque se gasten $5,000… se sacará mucha utilidad.'
