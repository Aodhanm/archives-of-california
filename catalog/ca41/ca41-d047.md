# On the need for a custom-house & wharf. Presidio buildings collapsing ('no hay una habitación que no se esté cayendo'); 

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n45–n46** · Sav p. 45–46 · tomo p. II.6–7 (orig. 276–9) · 1833-02-08 · Savage abstract · 

On the need for a custom-house & wharf. Presidio buildings collapsing ('no hay una habitación que no se esté cayendo'); w/o a customs house/muelle less than ⅓ of duties are collected; a well-run aduana should yield 30–40,000 pesos/yr 'á no ser que la revolución acabe con los ganados que son la base fundamental del comercio.' Foreign commerce must be tolerated but foreign ships forced to pay tonnage & cargo duties; contraband uncontrollable for lack of guards; requests staff (praises Comand. Francisco Pacheco) + receivers at SB/SF/SD.
