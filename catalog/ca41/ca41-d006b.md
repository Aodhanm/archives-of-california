# English. On motives for anchoring at Refugio: necessity forced a Cal. port for wood & to repair gale damage; 'found no p

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n6** · Sav p. 7 · tomo p. 6 (orig. 15 & 18) · 1816-01-22 · verbatim transcription · 

English. On motives for anchoring at Refugio: necessity forced a Cal. port for wood & to repair gale damage; 'found no provisions at Norfolk [Sound = Sitka]'; vessel 'leaking like a wreck,' boats breaking up, only 1 officer + 1 man aboard; if not a prize, insists on staying by her until condemned 'in a Court of Justice'; 'an utter stranger on the coast.'
