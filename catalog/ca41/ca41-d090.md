# 'Contrabando entre Ross y San Francisco.' Considerable merchandise introduced from Fort Ross to SF duty-free, 'por tierr

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n81–n82** · Sav p. 81–82 · tomo p. V.1–2 (orig. 4–6) · 1839-11-16 · Savage abstract · 

'Contrabando entre Ross y San Francisco.' Considerable merchandise introduced from Fort Ross to SF duty-free, 'por tierra y mar'; the SF receiver who once watched this is long gone; ships entering SF unload without duties. Per the latest reglamento a 'Aduana fronteriza' (frontier customs house) should be set up; meanwhile place an administrator there. Recommends Jacob (Jacobo) Leese as 'persona única para el puesto.' ⭐ Marginal note in Gov. Alvarado's hand: 'Contestado, y que se debe prevenir la Recept.a en la frontera al S.mo Gob.o.'
