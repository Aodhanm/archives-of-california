# Antonio M.ª Osio presented an Echeandía-era appointment as Contador of the aduana; González, lacking power to confirm po

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n49** · Sav p. 49 · tomo p. ii.284 · 1833-04-10 · Savage abstract · 

Antonio M.ª Osio presented an Echeandía-era appointment as Contador of the aduana; González, lacking power to confirm posts not named by the Sup. Govt., referred it to Figueroa, who resolved that Osio be posted to Los Ángeles.
