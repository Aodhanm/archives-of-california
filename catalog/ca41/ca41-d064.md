# On Russian ships & the exemptions they seek: William A. Gale, 'á nombre del Gob. de Sitka' [the RAC], requests a Russian

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n58** · Sav p. 58–59 · tomo p. ii.476–7 · 1833-07-27 · Savage abstract · 

On Russian ships & the exemptions they seek: William A. Gale, 'á nombre del Gob. de Sitka' [the RAC], requests a Russian ship enter San Francisco to load salt and salt meat (*saladero*) free of tonnage duty; González refused per the arancel; warns that letting ships run *saladeros* drains 'los recursos á los hijos del país'; whalers are about to winter & the Russians to buy wheat, and these ships 'nunca se les ha cobrado'; opinions divided — many hold whalers & Russians 'útiles al territorio porque consumen sus esquilmos.'
