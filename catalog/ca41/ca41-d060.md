# Urges orders that district habilitados at SD/SB/SF not let ships load/unload without proof of duties — 'ni mucho menos e

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n56** · Sav p. 56 · tomo p. ii.302–3 · 1833-02-16 · Savage abstract · 

Urges orders that district habilitados at SD/SB/SF not let ships load/unload without proof of duties — 'ni mucho menos en las radas y ensenadas que tienen las misiones'; proof of malversation: a ship (against law) loaded horses at SF & sold $10,000 of goods with no customs knowledge.
