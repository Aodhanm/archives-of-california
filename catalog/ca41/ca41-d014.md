# Import-duty computation on the English frigate *Thomas Nowlan*: on $3,634 goods at 25% = $909; 15% internación $681.6; 2

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n18** · Sav p. 18 · tomo p. i.136–8 · 1826-08-19 · Savage abstract · 

Import-duty computation on the English frigate *Thomas Nowlan*: on $3,634 goods at 25% = $909; 15% internación $681.6; 2¾% avería $99.7; 201¼ tons @ $2½; commission ⅓% $65 — net to the Nation $2,120.5.
