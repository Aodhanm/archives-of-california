# Investigation of the Cooper/*Rover* otter contract: the contract Gov. L. A. Argüello made (9 Nov 1824, w/ Diputación aut

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n19** · Sav p. 19–22 · tomo p. i.19–20; 141–7 · 1826-08-01 · Savage abstract · 

Investigation of the Cooper/*Rover* otter contract: the contract Gov. L. A. Argüello made (9 Nov 1824, w/ Diputación authorization) with Capt. John B. R. Cooper of the Am. schr. *Rover* for a government trading voyage to Canton; convened at the Jefe Político's house w/ Juan Bandini & interpreter W. A. Richardson; Echeandía found the Canton sale of the *nutrias* (otter skins) & the cost of goods not fully justified but left responsibility to Cooper pending the Sup. Govt.'s ruling. Contract clauses transcribed (see Entry 4).
