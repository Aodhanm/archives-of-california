# '1843 Julio 21 Mont, Guillermo E. Hartnell… á Juan B. Alvarado'; 'naufragó en Punta de Pinos la goleta inglesa Estrella 

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n70–n71** · Sav p. 70–71 · tomo p. III.1–2 (orig. 836, 848–50) · 1843-07-21 · Savage abstract · 

'1843 Julio 21 Mont, Guillermo E. Hartnell… á Juan B. Alvarado'; 'naufragó en Punta de Pinos la goleta inglesa Estrella de Occidente… su carguamento… á cargo de Dn Tomás O. Larkin, cónsul de los [E.]Unidos'; the auction commission (Díaz/Spence/Osio/Munras/Wilson) confirmed. Wreck of the English schooner *Estrella de Occidente* (Star of the West) at Point Pinos (night of 7th); captain & crew abandoned her; cargo consigned to Thomas O. Larkin (U.S. merchant, future consul). No law covering shipwreck salvage — asks whether to charge duties on the salvaged effects; salvage sold at public auction by a commission incl. Manuel Díaz, David E. Spence, A.M. Osio, Santiago Wilson.
