# Shipwreck salvage: the Eng. schr. *Estrella de Occidente* (Star of the West) wrecked at Punta de Lobos 7 Jul, abandoned 

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n70** · Sav p. 70–71 · tomo p. iii.836, 848–50 · 1843-07-21 · Savage abstract · 

Shipwreck salvage: the Eng. schr. *Estrella de Occidente* (Star of the West) wrecked at Punta de Lobos 7 Jul, abandoned by her captain; US consul Thomas O. Larkin undertook to salvage the cargo; the aduana asks whether to charge duties on the rescued goods, which were sold at public auction before a commission of Manuel Díaz, David Spence, Antonio M.ª Osio, Salvador Munrás & James (Santiago) Watson.
