# Vessels entered in October [1826]: Eng. brig *Olive Branch* (Capt. Wm Henderson, from Callao, 13 men, sobrecargo Diego S

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n17** · Sav p. 17 · tomo p. 16 (orig. 134–5) · 1826-11-02 · Savage abstract · 

Vessels entered in October [1826]: Eng. brig *Olive Branch* (Capt. Wm Henderson, from Callao, 13 men, sobrecargo Diego Scott, 204 t); then a fleet of Nantucket whalers 'á refrescar víveres' — *Cyrus* (David Harris, 22 men), *Ma Teresa* (Wm Gardner, 24 men, 291 t), *Franklin* (Wm Coffin, 294 t), *Octavian* (Alex Macy, 22 men, 331 t), *Paragon* (Davis Edwards, 23 men, 309 t), *Charles* (Capt. David, 21 men, 301 t), *Triton* (Capt. Th. Albet, 22 men, 300 t).
