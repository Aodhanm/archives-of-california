# Answers González's consultation on whalers & Russian ships. Institutional memory: the barca *Baikal* once came seeking s

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n59–n61** · Sav p. 59–61 · tomo p. II.20–22 (orig. 478–9) · 1833-08-17 · Savage abstract · 

Answers González's consultation on whalers & Russian ships. Institutional memory: the barca *Baikal* once came seeking salt & Echeandía sent it to San Quintín (paid tonnage + salt duty); whalers paid only anchorage until the new arancel; Gov. Victoria had consulted the Supreme Govt to exempt Russian ships from tonnage (rationale: they 'extraer los frutos del país,' which could boost local agriculture) — no answer received.
