# Government's reply on the whalers/Russian-ships duties: no record Russian ships were ever exempted from tonnage duty — r

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n59** · Sav p. 59–61 · tomo p. ii.478–9 · 1833-08-17 · Savage abstract · 

Government's reply on the whalers/Russian-ships duties: no record Russian ships were ever exempted from tonnage duty — recalls the barca *Baikal* came seeking salt under Echeandía (sent to San Quintín, paid tonnage + salt duty); whalers paid only anchorage until the current arancel; ex-Gov. Victoria had consulted the Sup. Govt. about not charging Russian ships, since 'su objeto es extraer los frutos del país' and this could spur agriculture.
