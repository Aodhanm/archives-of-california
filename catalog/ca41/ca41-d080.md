# Collecting ship duties amid the cash shortage — duties taken in goods at inflated prices; merchants prefer a private *pa

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n92** · Sav p. 92 · tomo p. v.12 · 1839-04-09 · Savage abstract · 

Collecting ship duties amid the cash shortage — duties taken in goods at inflated prices; merchants prefer a private *pagaré* to an official aduana order; sets a coin-vs-goods payment schedule by duty size ($3,000 in coin; $3,001–6,000 ⅓; $6,001–12,000 ½; >$12,001 1/16).
