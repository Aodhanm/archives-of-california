# 'Tiranías y excesos de los buques nacionales': foreign ships are the only ones paying duties and selling cheap; national

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n53–n54** · Sav p. 53–54 · tomo p. II.14–15 (orig. 294–5) · 1833-05-31 · Savage abstract · 

'Tiranías y excesos de los buques nacionales': foreign ships are the only ones paying duties and selling cheap; national ships tyrannize the people with dear goods and fake Mexican-port guías (never paying a real, no coast guard). Enforcing the law turns both people (who lack the prohibited goods) and the unpaid troops — who have before 'echársele encima al comisario… queriéndolos matar porq.e les ha faltado el socorro' — against him.
