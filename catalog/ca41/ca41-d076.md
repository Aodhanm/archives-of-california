# Pending the Sup. Govt.'s ruling on the whalers' tonnage exemption (they are 'solo tolerados por la costumbre'), resolves

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n68** · Sav p. 68 · tomo p. ii.732–3 · 1833-11-18 · Savage abstract · 

Pending the Sup. Govt.'s ruling on the whalers' tonnage exemption (they are 'solo tolerados por la costumbre'), resolves all whalers pay a fixed $10-4-reales port duty, to be invested in building a wharf at Monterey and the other ports — resolving Docs 48/64/65.
