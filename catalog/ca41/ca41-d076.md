# Sets a fixed $10-real port duty on all whalers (pending the Supreme Govt's ruling on their tonnage exemption) — 'solo to

**C-A 41 (Dep. State Papers, Benicia (Custom House)), scan n68** · Sav p. 68 · tomo p. II.28 (orig. 732–3) · 1833-11-18 · Savage abstract · 

Sets a fixed $10-real port duty on all whalers (pending the Supreme Govt's ruling on their tonnage exemption) — 'solo tolerados por la costumbre' — earmarked to build the Monterey wharf.
