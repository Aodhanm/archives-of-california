# the RAC manager's petition to build a warehouse & wharf at the Yerba Buena beach (future SF) for the Company's Californi

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n238–240** · Sav p. 240–242 · tomo p. 326–328 (Sav. pp. 388, 529–31) · 1839-05-10 · Savage abstract · 

the RAC manager's petition to build a warehouse & wharf at the Yerba Buena beach (future SF) for the Company's California grain trade — 5 articles: buy/build there (no store or customs house exists); learn the storage-duty tariff; cut timber in the S. Rafael woods for construction & keep 2 Company workers to guard the *almacén y muelle*; cede it gratis to the Departmental government after 10 years; the Company's grain-only ships to be inspected & pay tonnage at Yerba Buena. ⚠ date reads 1839 (Rotchev's tenure c.1836–41), though bound amid 1833–35 material
