# the President resolves to populate the desert islands adjacent to the Department (part of the National Territory) to sto

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n305–306** · Sav p. 306–307 · tomo p. 387–388 (Sav. pp. 646–7) · 1838-07-20 · Savage abstract · 

the President resolves to populate the desert islands adjacent to the Department (part of the National Territory) to stop foreign adventurers exploiting the fisheries — directs the government & Junta Departamental to distribute the islands to Nationals who request them, recommending Antonio & Carlos Carrillo for preference (the Channel Islands grants — e.g. Sta Rosa I. to C. Carrillo)
