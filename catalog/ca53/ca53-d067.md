# transmits a 17-May-1832 suprema orden on taxing the otter/beaver (nutria/castor) pelt-export trade — a percentage impost

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n133–134** · Sav p. 134–135 · tomo p. 134–135 (pp. 323–25) · 1834-01-17 (encloses a 1832-05-17 orden) · Savage abstract · 

transmits a 17-May-1832 suprema orden on taxing the otter/beaver (nutria/castor) pelt-export trade — a percentage impost on otter pelts 'previo el correspondiente aforo' + other articles — referring it to the Diputación's revenue committee for 'un proyecto útil y equitativo' to fund the treasury 'que no cuenta con fondo' → otter-trade/revenue (cf. C-A 44 otter-tariff)
