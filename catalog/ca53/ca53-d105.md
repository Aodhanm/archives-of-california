# reports founding the strong detachment at Mission San Rafael + another, and the Russian-threat rationale: the hidden age

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n212–218** · Sav p. 214–220 · tomo p. 302–306 (Sav. p. 462+) · 1833-04-12 · Savage abstract · 

reports founding the strong detachment at Mission San Rafael + another, and the Russian-threat rationale: the hidden agents' 'miras… á apoderarse de esta parte de la República son ya bien conocidas'; cites SF-commander reports (English/Europeans seeking provisions; the Sitka governor waiting at Ross to found a new establishment at Santa Rosa near S. Rafael); presses to 'asegurar el Puerto de Bodega que había querido apropiarse los Rusos' and requests a stationed warship (the corvette *Morelos*) from S. Blas, rotated every 6 months
