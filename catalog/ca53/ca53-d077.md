# ⚠ the Diputación-side re-engrossing and July-1834 enactment of the Monterey propios-y-arbitrios ordinance (dedup vs Docs

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n148–162** · Sav p. 150–164 · tomo p. 238–252 (Sav. pp. 338–362) · 1834-07-10 · Savage abstract · 

⚠ the Diputación-side re-engrossing and July-1834 enactment of the Monterey propios-y-arbitrios ordinance (dedup vs Docs 69–76): the 12-article municipal tax schedule copied out & decreed by Figueroa 10 Jul 1834, then extended — an additional art. 6 (15 Jul): 1 real/*bulto* municipal landing duty at Monterey, S. Francisco, Sta Bárbara, S. Pedro & S. Diego; the 24 Jul liquor tax (aguardiente 3 pesos/barrel, angélica 2, wine 12 reales) + jail-release 12 r & branding-iron-license 12 r fees; and a voluntary contribution on ships toward a Monterey-bay wharf (*muelle*) fund invoking art. 5 of the 1827 Manual de Aduanas Marítimas
