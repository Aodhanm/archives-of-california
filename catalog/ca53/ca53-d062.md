# Híjar's formal PROTEST & rebuttal — denies ever conceding the Diputación's authority ('Nunca he tenido tal presunción; l

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n125–129** · Sav p. 126–130 · tomo p. 126–130 (doc's own pp. ~217–313) · 1834-11-06 · Savage abstract · 

Híjar's formal PROTEST & rebuttal — denies ever conceding the Diputación's authority ('Nunca he tenido tal presunción; lo que he hecho es negarles la facultad de subsanarse á las Leyes'); protests Figueroa's disposition; declares he will still establish the Colony, on 3 grounds: the families' committed fate; that destroying it would discredit the Government (families uprooted from their homeland); and ⭐⭐⭐ that it would leave undefended 'la importante frontera del norte, amagada por los Rusos y Anglo-americanos' — the Híjar–Padrés colony's explicit anti-Russian + anti-Anglo-American strategic rationale (Fort Ross counter-colonization) → [[russian-california]], [[bodega-primary-sources]], [[project_solano_county_future]], [[californio-article]]. ⚠ rough paleography + confusing internal pagination — transcribe verbatim at scale 4 before quoting
