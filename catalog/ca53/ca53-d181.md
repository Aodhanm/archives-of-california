# the central government's intervention in the Pico mission sales: the President, having learned the Department 'ha dispue

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n322–323** · Sav p. 323–324 · tomo p. 404–405 (Sav. p. 683) · 1845-11-14 · Savage abstract · 

the central government's intervention in the Pico mission sales: the President, having learned the Department 'ha dispuesto sacar á pública subasta todos los bienes… de las misiones' (which the predecessor Micheltorena had returned to the friars), orders the government to report and SUSPEND all proceedings on the alienation of mission property until the Supreme Govt resolves
