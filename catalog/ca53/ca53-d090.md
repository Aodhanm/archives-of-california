# cover letter transmitting the colony roster (persons with ages, occupation & status) + 3 budgets (Nº 1 = the colonists' 

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n184** · Sav p. 186 · tomo p. 274 (Sav. pp. 387–8) · 1834-11-08 · Savage abstract · 

cover letter transmitting the colony roster (persons with ages, occupation & status) + 3 budgets (Nº 1 = the colonists' 30-day stipends, 2,895 pesos; Nº 2 = the animals & tools owed the colonists 'por una sola vez' per the Supreme Govt's offer; Nº 3 = provisions & tools); asks Figueroa to supply the colony against the colonists' daily wages, the Territory being 'tan escaso de monedas'
