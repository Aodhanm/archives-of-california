# petition NOT to expel the Spanish friar-missionaries (nor the Spanish heads-of-family) under the 20 Mar 1829 expulsion l

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n7–10** · Sav p. 8–11 · tomo p. 15–18 · 1829-09-22 · Savage abstract · 

petition NOT to expel the Spanish friar-missionaries (nor the Spanish heads-of-family) under the 20 Mar 1829 expulsion law — 25 of the mission ministers are Spaniards serving the 21 missions + 4 presidios + 3 pueblos + many ranchos over 200 leguas; 3 Mexican friars could never replace them; praises Echeandía for not yet enforcing it; asks replacements be sent first. Signed José Tiburcio Castro, Francisco Soria, Feliciano Soberanes, Santiago Moreno (secr. José Mª Gijila[?])
