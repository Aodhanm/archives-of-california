# 'Reglamento Provisional para la secularización de las Misiones de la Alta California' (*Doc. impreso*) — the foundationa

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n163–167** · Sav p. 165–169 · tomo p. 253–257 · 1834-08-09 · verbatim transcription · 

'Reglamento Provisional para la secularización de las Misiones de la Alta California' (*Doc. impreso*) — the foundational secularization decree: per the spirit of the 17 Aug 1833 law & the Supremo Gobierno's instructions, the missions partially converted to pueblos beginning Aug (10 first, then the rest); the padres relieved of temporalities & confined to spiritual ministry until formal parish division; territorial govt reassumes temporalities. Distribución de intereses y terrenos: each family head & everyone over 20 (even without family) gets from the missions' common lands a solar ≤ 400 varas + watering commons, with ejidos & propios per pueblo; half the livestock (*semovientes*) & up to half the tools distributed proportionally; the surplus held under a Gefe-Político-named administrator (approved by the Sup. Gob. Federal); provision for padres' subsistence, mayordomo pay, worship, schools & police from the common mass. Gobierno Político de los Pueblos: ayuntamientos formed, elections held; economic govt to the ayuntamientos, contentious justice to the nearest 1ª-instancia judges; emancipados owe communal labor on the still-undivided vineyards/orchards & personal service to the padre. Prevenciones — Art. 18 anti-alienation: emancipated Indians may not sell, encumber or alienate granted lands or cattle; violating sales are void, the govt reclaims, buyers lose their money; heirless lands revert to the emancipated body (art. 19). Art. 22 bars padres from large-scale cattle slaughter before enactment; passive mission debts preferred (art. 23)
