# decree erecting Mission San Luis Obispo into a Pueblo (per the 11 Feb 1842 mission-arreglo faculties): having no goods &

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n317–319** · Sav p. 318–320 · tomo p. 399–401 (Sav. pp. 679–80) · 1844-07-16 · Savage abstract · 

decree erecting Mission San Luis Obispo into a Pueblo (per the 11 Feb 1842 mission-arreglo faculties): having no goods & few 'Cristianos viejos' who support themselves, it becomes a pueblo with the vacant nearby lands as ejidos; the neophytes given 'entera libertad,' owing only limited personal service to the cura; the ex-mission building becomes the Casa Cural (its rooms for schools/court/jail); an interim juez de paz; solar grants only via the government for symmetric streets; the cura to manage water rights; art. 7 the emancipados may not sell their lands (revert to the Nation if heirless) + art. 8 communal-labor duties
