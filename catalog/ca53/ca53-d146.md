# the Diputación's own dictamen endorsing Monterey (the ayuntamiento's expediente having been referred to it via Castro / 

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n273–277** · Sav p. 274–278 · tomo p. 355–359 · 1835-10-13 · Savage abstract · 

the Diputación's own dictamen endorsing Monterey (the ayuntamiento's expediente having been referred to it via Castro / Manuel Jimeno srío) — largely re-states the case (Monterey central by latitude, a superior port merchants prefer for the Aduana, Los Ángeles timber-poor); proposes to approve the ayuntamiento's petition & forward the expediente; 'la Diputación… no se ve llevada de la pasión de sus Diputados'
