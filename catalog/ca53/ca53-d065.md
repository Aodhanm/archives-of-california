# on the undefined jurisdiction of San Francisco — no old document fixes the límites; describes the military jurisdiction 

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n130–131** · Sav p. 131–132 · tomo p. 131–132 (pp. 318–19) · 1834-06-12 · Savage abstract · 

on the undefined jurisdiction of San Francisco — no old document fixes the límites; describes the military jurisdiction (missions S. José, Sta Clara, Sta Cruz + those by the Puerto) and the civil/political jurisdiction (Pueblo of San José from the Laguna to Sta Clara, the ranchos to the estrecho de Carquines, the Villa de Branciforte) (⚠ copyist marks a variant: 'aquí dice el original 'la población'')
