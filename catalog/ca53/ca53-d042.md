# pro-secularization report on the northern missions — the missions of San Francisco Solano, San Rafael, Santa Clara, San 

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n96** · Sav p. 97 · tomo p. 97 (pp. 208–10) · 1833-10-19 · Savage abstract · 

pro-secularization report on the northern missions — the missions of San Francisco Solano, San Rafael, Santa Clara, San Francisco de Asís & San José are ready to be secularized under the 13 Sept 1813 law; the established Indians 'tienen la necesaria aptitud,' could equal the other classes in rights & liberty, freeing them from 'el duro tratamiento que se les dá.' ⚠ 'Esta Comunicación está mutilada en su conclusión.' The PRO pole answering Durán's gradualism (Doc 40)
