# Figueroa's comprehensive report against TOTAL / foreign-favoring secularization — (a) general secularization is 'inmatur

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n70–74** · Sav p. 71–75 · tomo p. 70–75 · 1833-10-05 · Savage abstract · 

Figueroa's comprehensive report against TOTAL / foreign-favoring secularization — (a) general secularization is 'inmatura'; do it 'parcialmente' & 'insensiblemente' ('el remedio peor que el mal'; the neophytes 'tan salvajes como si acabaran de salir de los bosques,' 'like children' to lead by the hand); (b) ⭐⭐⭐ his emancipation EXPERIMENT — offered ~60 San Diego + ~40 San Luis Rey families freedom + land/water/livestock, but most REFUSED, 'por permanecer en la servidumbre en que vivían' (only 10 SD + 4 SLR accepted; now trying San Juan Capistrano); (c) ⭐⭐⭐ the mission property is 'del fruto exclusivo del trabajo de los Neófitos… propios únicamente de los Neófitos' — objects to handing it to settlers/foreigners (the Híjar–Padrés colonists, 'tres gentes estrañas que nada les han [dado]'); (d) objects the colonization law favors foreigners over the Mexican military; forwards Durán's & García Diego's replies
