# decree (11 arts.) regulating the grape & wine trade to protect vineyard owners from harvest-time theft: grape sales over

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n320–322** · Sav p. 321–323 · tomo p. 402–404 (Sav. p. 682) · 1845-09-06 · Savage abstract · 

decree (11 arts.) regulating the grape & wine trade to protect vineyard owners from harvest-time theft: grape sales over 15 lbs require a document; buying grapes from Indians or huerta servants without a certificate is prohibited; wine-fermenting needs an alcalde's license; daily inspection of the lagares of 'la ranchería de los Indios á orillas de esta Ciudad' + tabernas; fines/public-works penalties (LA viticulture + Indian-labor control)
