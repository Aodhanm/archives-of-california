# the 1827 supreme order (the dossier's legal root): on the Russians of Ross violating 'el derecho común de las Naciones' 

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n219–220** · Sav p. 221–222 · tomo p. 307–310 (Sav. pp. 478–80) · 1827-06-06 · Savage abstract · 

the 1827 supreme order (the dossier's legal root): on the Russians of Ross violating 'el derecho común de las Naciones' along the territory's demarcation line & coasts, drawing away (voluntarily or forcibly) the Mexican Indians of Mission San Rafael, and occupying the Farallon Islands of S. Francisco for sea-lion hunting — orders the reconnaissance & siting of a Casa fuerte with a garrison under a capable officer (mission-supplied labor) + a rotating stationed warship from S. Blas
