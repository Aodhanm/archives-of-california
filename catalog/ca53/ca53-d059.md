# report on the Híjar directorate — the Diputación Territorial's 22-Oct-1834 resolution that Híjar may exercise the Direct

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n123–124** · Sav p. 124–125 · tomo p. 124–125 (p. 276+) · 1834-11-09 · Savage abstract · 

report on the Híjar directorate — the Diputación Territorial's 22-Oct-1834 resolution that Híjar may exercise the Director-of-Colonization commission only 'con sujeción al Gobierno Político del Territorio, y á las leyes y reglamentos sobre la materia,' and its demand that Híjar state in writing whether he conforms (the constitutional crux of the Figueroa–Híjar affair — the territorial govt subordinating the Mexico-appointed director)
