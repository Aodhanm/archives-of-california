**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n263** · Sav p. 264 · tomo p. 345 (Sav. pp. 573–6) · 1835-08-02 · Savage abstract · 

the S. Francisco Solano friar's bitter complaint (the doc Doc 139 flagged): Mariano G. Vallejo, Salvador Vallejo & the mayordomo Antonio Ortega barred his passage, closed the door on him, denied him a cow for food; accuses Ortega of being 'inmoral, insolente y lujurioso, con escándalo' (cites Sgt. Pablo Pacheco & other soldiers as witnesses); threatens that unless Ortega is removed as mayordomo he will not return to Solano 'ni por la fuerza'
