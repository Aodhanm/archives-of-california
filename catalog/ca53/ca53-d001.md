# the 17-article general regulations implementing the 18 Aug 1824 Colonization Law — Jefes Políticos grant *terrenos baldí

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n1–5** · Sav p. 2–6 · tomo p. 1–5 · 1828-11-21 · Savage abstract · 

the 17-article general regulations implementing the 18 Aug 1824 Colonization Law — Jefes Políticos grant *terrenos baldíos* to empresarios/families (Mexican or foreign); petition w/ a diseño (map); grants need Diputación Territorial consent (else → Supreme Govt); empresario colonies (≥12 families) need Supreme-Govt approval; a registry-book of grants + diseños sent to México quarterly; ⭐ minimum grant sizes (regadío 200 v², temporal 800 v², abrevadero 1200 v, *solar* 100 v); ⭐⭐⭐ Art. 17 — mission lands may NOT be colonized 'hasta que se resuelva si deben considerarse como propiedad de las reducciones de los neófitos' (the neophyte-property question)
