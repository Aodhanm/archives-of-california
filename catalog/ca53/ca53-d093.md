# the consolidated colony budget — subsistence 2,895 p/month / 35,222.4 p/year (182 adults + 22 minors) + a once-only outf

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n190** · Sav p. 192 · tomo p. 280 (Sav. p. 413) · 1834-11-09 · Savage abstract · 

the consolidated colony budget — subsistence 2,895 p/month / 35,222.4 p/year (182 adults + 22 minors) + a once-only outfitting of 9,984 pesos for 42 families + 54 men (per family 4 cows, 2 ox-teams, 2 tame horses, 4 colts, 4 fillies, sheep & 2 fitted plows) = grand total 45,206.4 pesos; quantifies the colony at ~204 colonists & its full cost to the treasury
