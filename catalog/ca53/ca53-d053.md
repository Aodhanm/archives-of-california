# names Híjar Director of the colonization establishments — to direct 'las familias que deben ir a la Alta California con 

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n117–118** · Sav p. 118–119 · tomo p. 118–119 (p. 263+) · 1833-07-16 · Savage abstract · 

names Híjar Director of the colonization establishments — to direct 'las familias que deben ir a la Alta California con el objeto… de colonizar aquel Territorio' and the distribution of lands to the colonos (reconciling their private interest with the general) — the Híjar–Padrés colony directorate mandate. Financial/logistical terms (n118, doc pp.263–65): an 800-peso/yr *secretario* for the commission; colonists paid 3 reales/día per person for the overland journey, sea passage at government cost, and land parcels per family
