# forest-conservation dictamen (29/31 Oct & 3 Nov): invoking art. 8, ch. 1 of the 23 June 1813 decree, the ayuntamientos t

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n176–178** · Sav p. 178–180 · tomo p. 266–268 (Sav. pp. 375–7) · 1834-11-03 · Savage abstract · 

forest-conservation dictamen (29/31 Oct & 3 Nov): invoking art. 8, ch. 1 of the 23 June 1813 decree, the ayuntamientos to issue measures for conserving & repopulating the woodlands and punishing offenders; timber export from the Territory prohibited, interior transport only by written ayuntamiento permit specifying piece-counts (else deemed fraud, fined at the wood's appraised value to municipal funds); national/foreign ships may take wood for ordinary use & averías with port-captain & alcalde leave — approved by the Diputación
