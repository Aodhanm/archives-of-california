# the territory-wide 'Plan de propios y arbitrios y fondos municipales de los Ayuntamientos de la Alta California' — the g

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n242–246** · Sav p. 244–248 · tomo p. 329–333 · 1834-08-06 · Savage abstract · 

the territory-wide 'Plan de propios y arbitrios y fondos municipales de los Ayuntamientos de la Alta California' — the general municipal-finance ordinance every Monterey arbitrios doc (69–77) cites as 'el plan… acordado en sesión de 6 de Agosto': ayuntamientos to petition ejidos & propios for each pueblo; propios subdivided & leased in *censo enfitéutico* by public auction (existing holders pay an annual *censo*); graded solar/commerce/liquor/entertainment/hide/timber duties (arts. invoking Constitution art. 335); the Aduana to report ships' liquor to curb fraud; published for observance (arts. 1–21)
