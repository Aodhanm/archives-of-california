# Instrucciones / Prevenciones para los Comisionados de secularización (12 *prevenciones*, companion to Doc 78): each Comi

**C-A 53 (State Papers, Missions and Colonization), scan n168–172** · Sav p. 170–174 · tomo p. 258–262 (Sav. p. 363) · 1834-08-09 · Savage abstract · 

Instrucciones / Prevenciones para los Comisionados de secularización (12 *prevenciones*, companion to Doc 78): each Comisionado presents his credential to the mission's padre, takes the libros de caja & credit records, and forms the art.-13 general inventories — separating the *casco* (buildings, church, workshops, each office's tools/furniture) from the *campo* (real estate: vineyards & orchards with tree-counts, fences, then livestock); cut superfluous expense; explain secularization to the *indígenas* 'con dulzura y paciencia' (missions becoming pueblos, lands & goods to be distributed, their houses given in property); dissolve the *monjerío* (girls returned to parents); propose *mayordomos* & their salaries (art. 8); rancherías of 25+ families may form separate pueblos (fewer become a *barrio* of the nearest pueblo); census each pueblo's souls to set municipal offices & call elections
