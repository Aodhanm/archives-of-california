# forwards the San Luis Rey administrator's 12 June letter — the mission Indians are fleeing en masse, the establishment '

**C-A 35 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n26–n27** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 26–27 · 1839-06-14 · Savage abstract · 

forwards the San Luis Rey administrator's 12 June letter — the mission Indians are fleeing en masse, the establishment 'caminando a su ruina,' he threatens to resign for want of support; the fugitives cause 'escándalos' in the LA pueblos on feast days — Hartnell asks LA to round up & return the fugitive S.L. Rey Indians & prevent future desertions — see E1 (doc pp.198-199)
