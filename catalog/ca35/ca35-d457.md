# ~11-doc SD frontier-war bundle — published the Graham-Affair circular; E. Fink offered arms & the Comandante de Frontera

**C-A 35 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n165–n167** · Sav p.  · tomo p. VI · 1841-06-07 · Savage abstract · 

~11-doc SD frontier-war bundle — published the Graham-Affair circular; E. Fink offered arms & the Comandante de Frontera lent muskets to the SD vecindario 'para defenderse de los indios' (2 fusiles accounted); a joint campaign vs the Jamul[Jacum] Indians with named Indian-captain allies (Marisiche, el Gato Prieto, el Chapo, + the Diegueños; Juan Padre watching the Gentiles); tavern-keepers refuse the $4/mo + $3/barrel duty; a settlement-at-S.J.-Capistrano-mission petition; otter-hunters landing without license (doc pp.290-309)
