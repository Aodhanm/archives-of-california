# the Indians of Rancho Pala demand removal of the mayordomo Santiago Ríos — he made them farm a crop for himself, offered

**C-A 35 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n175** · Sav p.  · tomo p. VI · 1841-07-14 · Savage abstract · 

the Indians of Rancho Pala demand removal of the mayordomo Santiago Ríos — he made them farm a crop for himself, offered a maize gratification they refused, kept the harvest in the granaries (not the mission); Ríos disobeyed his replacement before witnesses & 'multitud de indios' — asks justice (indigenous labor protest) (doc pp.377-80)
