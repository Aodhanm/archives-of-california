# the 'Pájaro' affair — the Gentile Indian 'Pájaro' presented a document from the Governor of Sonora naming him 'Capitán G

**C-A 35 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n94–n96** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 94–96 · 1839-11-22 · Savage abstract · 

the 'Pájaro' affair — the Gentile Indian 'Pájaro' presented a document from the Governor of Sonora naming him 'Capitán General de San Felipe'; Ortega annuls it — the San Felipe ranchería (this side of the Colorado) belongs to California, not Sonora, & Pájaro is a years-long deserter from the establishment; he reports to the Superior Govt via the Visitaduría, noting Pájaro's usefulness for the security of travelers at the Colorado river-crossing → candidate E2 (doc pp.706-717)
