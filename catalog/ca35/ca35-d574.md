# custody petition — her daughter Micaela died of her husband Manuel Rivera's abuse, leaving 4 children she raised; the fa

**C-A 35 (Departmental State Papers, Los Angeles), scan n212** · Sav p.  · tomo p. VI · 1842-01-18 · Savage abstract · 

custody petition — her daughter Micaela died of her husband Manuel Rivera's abuse, leaving 4 children she raised; the father took them to San Gabriel to live with his new Indian wife, beating/threatening them; asks custody (fears the older girls become 'víctimas del crimen') — resolved: grandmother keeps the 3 girls, father the boy (doc pp.672-7)
