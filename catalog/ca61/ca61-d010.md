# detailed denunciation of neophyte mistreatment (post-secularization): the indígenas 'levantan clamores á cada paso' over

**C-A 61 (Legislative Records), scan n7–n15** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 34–40 (+5–11) · 1835-12-28 · Savage abstract · 

detailed denunciation of neophyte mistreatment (post-secularization): the indígenas 'levantan clamores á cada paso' over the *sevicia* of the temporalities-administrator José M. Ramírez & mayordomo Juan Padilla; the old & sick 'muriendo de… hambre y frío'; the mission's wheat & wine sold for profit while 'nada entra en beneficio de los propietarios que son los Indios'; the freed neophytes ('proclamados libres') fleeing to distant ranchos (Alisos, 20 leguas) unsupported. *The secularization's betrayal of the neophytes' property rights, on the record* (pairs w/ C-A 20 Doc 242)
