# the Asamblea DISAVOWS Gov. Micheltorena: on José de la Guerra's report, the assembly 'desconoce la autoridad que ejerce 

**C-A 61 (Legislative Records), scan n136** · Sav p.  · tomo p. 132 · 1845 · Savage abstract · 

the Asamblea DISAVOWS Gov. Micheltorena: on José de la Guerra's report, the assembly 'desconoce la autoridad que ejerce en este Departamento la persona de M. Micheltorena,' the vacant offices to be filled 'con arreglo á las leyes' — the legislative deposition of the Mexican-appointed governor that installed Pío Pico ⚠ *(date reads '1845 Nov 11' but the Micheltorena overthrow was Feb 1845 — verify month/leaf order)*
